 <p>2  What ye know,<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> the same do I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> also:<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> am not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> inferior<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> you. </p>
 <p>3  Surely<sup><a href="h0199.htm">199</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> would speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the Almighty,<sup><a href="h7706.htm">7706</a></sup> and I desire<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> to reason<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> God.<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  But<sup><a href="h0199.htm">199</a></sup> ye<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> are forgers<sup><a href="h2950.htm">2950</a></sup> of lies,<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> ye are all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> physicians<sup><a href="h7495.htm">7495</a></sup> of no value.<sup><a href="h0457.htm">457</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  O that<sup><a href="h4130.htm">4130</a></sup> ye would<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> altogether hold your peace!<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> and it should be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> your wisdom.<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> my reasoning,<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> and hearken<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> to the pleadings<sup><a href="h7379.htm">7379</a></sup> of my lips.<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Will ye speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> wickedly<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> for God?<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> and talk<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> deceitfully<sup><a href="h7423.htm">7423</a></sup> for him? </p>
 <p>8  Will ye accept<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> his person?<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> will ye contend<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> for God?<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Is it good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he should search you out?<sup><a href="h2713.htm">2713</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> or<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> as one man<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> mocketh<sup><a href="h2048.htm">2048</a></sup> another, do ye so mock<sup><a href="h2048.htm">2048</a></sup> him? </p>
 <p>10  He will surely reprove<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> you, if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> ye do secretly<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> accept<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> persons.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> his excellency<sup><a href="h7613.htm">7613</a></sup> make you afraid?<sup><a href="h1204.htm">1204</a></sup> and his dread<sup><a href="h6343.htm">6343</a></sup> fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> you? </p>
 <p>12  Your remembrances<sup><a href="h2146.htm">2146</a></sup> are like<sup><a href="h4912.htm">4912</a></sup> unto ashes,<sup><a href="h0665.htm">665</a></sup> your bodies<sup><a href="h1354.htm">1354</a></sup> to bodies<sup><a href="h1354.htm">1354</a></sup> of clay.<sup><a href="h2563.htm">2563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Hold your peace,<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> let me alone,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> that I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> may speak,<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> and let come<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> will. </p>
 <p>14  Wherefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> do I take<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> my flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> in my teeth,<sup><a href="h8127.htm">8127</a></sup> and put<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> my life<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> in mine hand?<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Though<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> he slay<sup><a href="h6991.htm">6991</a></sup> me, yet will I trust<sup><a href="h3176.htm">3176</a></sup> in him: but<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> I will maintain<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> mine own ways<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>16  He<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> shall be my salvation:<sup><a href="h3444.htm">3444</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> a hypocrite<sup><a href="h2611.htm">2611</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>17  Hear diligently<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my speech,<sup><a href="h4405.htm">4405</a></sup> and my declaration<sup><a href="h0262.htm">262</a></sup> with your ears.<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Behold<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> now,<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> I have ordered<sup><a href="h6186.htm">6186</a></sup> my cause;<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> I know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> shall be justified.<sup><a href="h6663.htm">6663</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> is he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> that will plead<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5978.htm">5978</a></sup> me? for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> now,<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> if I hold my tongue,<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> I shall give up the ghost.<sup><a href="h1478.htm">1478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Only<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> things unto<sup><a href="h5978.htm">5978</a></sup> me: then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> will I not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hide myself<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>21  Withdraw<sup><a href="h7368.htm">7368</a></sup> thine hand<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> far from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me: and let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> thy dread<sup><a href="h0367.htm">367</a></sup> make me afraid.<sup><a href="h1204.htm">1204</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  Then call<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> thou, and I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> will answer:<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> or<sup><a href="h0176.htm">176</a></sup> let me speak,<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> and answer<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> thou me. </p>
 <p>23  How<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> many are mine iniquities<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and sins?<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> make me to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> my transgression<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> and my sin.<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  Wherefore<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> hidest<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> thou thy face,<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> and holdest<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> me for thine enemy?<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  Wilt thou break<sup><a href="h6206.htm">6206</a></sup> a leaf<sup><a href="h5929.htm">5929</a></sup> driven to and fro?<sup><a href="h5086.htm">5086</a></sup> and wilt thou pursue<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> the dry<sup><a href="h3002.htm">3002</a></sup> stubble?<sup><a href="h7179.htm">7179</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou writest<sup><a href="h3789.htm">3789</a></sup> bitter things<sup><a href="h4846.htm">4846</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me, and makest me to possess<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> the iniquities<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> of my youth.<sup><a href="h5271.htm">5271</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  Thou puttest<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> my feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> also in the stocks,<sup><a href="h5465.htm">5465</a></sup> and lookest narrowly<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> unto all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> my paths;<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> thou settest a print<sup><a href="h2707.htm">2707</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the heels<sup><a href="h8328.htm">8328</a></sup> of my feet.<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 καὶ οἶδα ὅσα καὶ ὑμεῖς ἐπίστασθε καὶ οὐκ ἀσυνετώτερός εἰμι ὑμῶν </p>
 <p>3 οὐ μὴν δὲ ἀλλ᾽ ἐγὼ πρὸς κύριον λαλήσω ἐλέγξω δὲ ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ ἐὰν βούληται </p>
 <p>4 ὑμεῖς δέ ἐστε ἰατροὶ ἄδικοι καὶ ἰαταὶ κακῶν πάντες </p>
 <p>5 εἴη δὲ ὑμῖν κωφεῦσαι καὶ ἀποβήσεται ὑμῖν εἰς σοφίαν </p>
 <p>6 ἀκούσατε ἔλεγχον στόματός μου κρίσιν δὲ χειλέων μου προσέχετε </p>
 <p>7 πότερον οὐκ ἔναντι κυρίου λαλεῖτε ἔναντι δὲ αὐτοῦ φθέγγεσθε δόλον </p>
 <p>8 ἦ ὑποστελεῖσθε ὑμεῖς δὲ αὐτοὶ κριταὶ γένεσθε </p>
 <p>9 καλόν γε ἐὰν ἐξιχνιάσῃ ὑμᾶς εἰ γὰρ τὰ πάντα ποιοῦντες προστεθήσεσθε αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>10 οὐθὲν ἧττον ἐλέγξει ὑμᾶς εἰ δὲ καὶ κρυφῇ πρόσωπα θαυμάσετε </p>
 <p>11 πότερον οὐχὶ δεινὰ αὐτοῦ στροβήσει ὑμᾶς φόβος δὲ παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἐπιπεσεῖται ὑμῖν </p>
 <p>12 ἀποβήσεται δὲ ὑμῶν τὸ ἀγαυρίαμα ἴσα σποδῷ τὸ δὲ σῶμα πήλινον </p>
 <p>13 κωφεύσατε ἵνα λαλήσω καὶ ἀναπαύσωμαι θυμοῦ </p>
 <p>14 ἀναλαβὼν τὰς σάρκας μου τοῖς ὀδοῦσιν ψυχὴν δέ μου θήσω ἐν χειρί </p>
 <p>15 ἐάν με χειρώσηται ὁ δυνάστης ἐπεὶ καὶ ἦρκται ἦ μὴν λαλήσω καὶ ἐλέγξω ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>16 καὶ τοῦτό μοι ἀποβήσεται εἰς σωτηρίαν οὐ γὰρ ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ δόλος εἰσελεύσεται </p>
 <p>17 ἀκούσατε ἀκούσατε τὰ ῥήματά μου ἀναγγελῶ γὰρ ὑμῶν ἀκουόντων </p>
 <p>18 ἰδοὺ ἐγὼ ἐγγύς εἰμι τοῦ κρίματός μου οἶδα ἐγὼ ὅτι δίκαιος ἀναφανοῦμαι </p>
 <p>19 τίς γάρ ἐστιν ὁ κριθησόμενός μοι ὅτι νῦν κωφεύσω καὶ ἐκλείψω </p>
 <p>20 δυεῖν δέ μοι χρήσῃ τότε ἀπὸ τοῦ προσώπου σου οὐ κρυβήσομαι </p>
 <p>21 τὴν χεῖρα ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ ἀπέχου καὶ ὁ φόβος σου μή με καταπλησσέτω </p>
 <p>22 εἶτα καλέσεις ἐγὼ δέ σοι ὑπακούσομαι ἢ λαλήσεις ἐγὼ δέ σοι δώσω ἀνταπόκρισιν </p>
 <p>23 πόσαι εἰσὶν αἱ ἁμαρτίαι μου καὶ αἱ ἀνομίαι μου δίδαξόν με τίνες εἰσίν </p>
 <p>24 διὰ τί ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ κρύπτῃ ἥγησαι δέ με ὑπεναντίον σοι </p>
 <p>25 ἦ ὡς φύλλον κινούμενον ὑπὸ ἀνέμου εὐλαβηθήσῃ ἢ ὡς χόρτῳ φερομένῳ ὑπὸ πνεύματος ἀντίκεισαί μοι </p>
 <p>26 ὅτι κατέγραψας κατ᾽ ἐμοῦ κακά περιέθηκας δέ μοι νεότητος ἁμαρτίας </p>
 <p>27 ἔθου δέ μου τὸν πόδα ἐν κωλύματι ἐφύλαξας δέ μου πάντα τὰ ἔργα εἰς δὲ ῥίζας τῶν ποδῶν μου ἀφίκου </p>
 <p>28 ὃ παλαιοῦται ἴσα ἀσκῷ ἢ ὥσπερ ἱμάτιον σητόβρωτον </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/job013.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Ecce omnia hæc vidit oculus meus,<br> et audivit auris mea, et intellexi singula.<br></p>
 <p>2 Secundum scientiam vestram et ego novi:<br> nec inferior vestri sum.<br></p>
 <p>3 Sed tamen ad Omnipotentem loquar,<br> et disputare cum Deo cupio:<br></p>
 <p>4 prius vos ostendens fabricatores mendacii,<br> et cultores perversorum dogmatum.<br></p>
 <p>5 Atque utinam taceretis,<br> ut putaremini esse sapientes.<br></p>
 <p>6 Audite ergo correptionem meam,<br> et judicium labiorum meorum attendite.<br></p>
 <p>7 Numquid Deus indiget vestro mendacio,<br> ut pro illo loquamini dolos?<br></p>
 <p>8 numquid faciem ejus accipitis,<br> et pro Deo judicare nitimini?<br></p>
 <p>9 aut placebit ei quem celare nihil potest?<br> aut decipietur, ut homo, vestris fraudulentiis?<br></p>
 <p>10 Ipse vos arguet,<br> quoniam in abscondito faciem ejus accipitis.<br></p>
 <p>11 Statim ut se commoverit, turbabit vos,<br> et terror ejus irruet super vos.<br></p>
 <p>12 Memoria vestra comparabitur cineri,<br> et redigentur in lutum cervices vestræ.<br></p>
 <p>13 Tacete paulisper, ut loquar<br> quodcumque mihi mens suggesserit.<br></p>
 <p>14 Quare lacero carnes meas dentibus meis,<br> et animam meam porto in manibus meis?<br></p>
 <p>15 Etiam si occiderit me, in ipso sperabo:<br> verumtamen vias meas in conspectu ejus arguam.<br></p>
 <p>16 Et ipse erit salvator meus:<br> non enim veniet in conspectu ejus omnis hypocrita.<br></p>
 <p>17 Audite sermonem meum,<br> et ænigmata percipite auribus vestris.<br></p>
 <p>18 Si fuero judicatus,<br> scio quod justus inveniar.<br></p>
 <p>19 Quis est qui judicetur mecum?<br> veniat: quare tacens consumor?<br></p>
 <p>20 Duo tantum ne facias mihi,<br> et tunc a facie tua non abscondar:<br></p>
 <p>21 manum tuam longe fac a me,<br> et formido tua non me terreat.<br></p>
 <p>22 Voca me, et ego respondebo tibi:<br> aut certe loquar, et tu responde mihi.<br></p>
 <p>23 Quantas habeo iniquitates et peccata?<br> scelera mea et delicta ostende mihi.<br></p>
 <p>24 Cur faciem tuam abscondis,<br> et arbitraris me inimicum tuum?<br></p>
 <p>25 Contra folium, quod vento rapitur, ostendis potentiam tuam,<br> et stipulam siccam persequeris:<br></p>
 <p>26 scribis enim contra me amaritudines,<br> et consumere me vis peccatis adolescentiæ meæ.<br></p>
 <p>27 Posuisti in nervo pedem meum,<br> et observasti omnes semitas meas,<br> et vestigia pedum meorum considerasti:<br></p>
 <p>28 qui quasi putredo consumendus sum,<br> et quasi vestimentum quod comeditur a tinea.]</p>
