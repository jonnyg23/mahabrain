 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/hos006.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Come,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> and let us return<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hath torn,<sup><a href="h2963.htm">2963</a></sup> and he will heal<sup><a href="h7495.htm">7495</a></sup> us; he hath smitten,<sup><a href="h5221.htm">5221</a></sup> and he will bind us up.<sup><a href="h2280.htm">2280</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  After two days<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> will he revive<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> us: in the third<sup><a href="h7992.htm">7992</a></sup> day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> he will raise us up,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> and we shall live<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> in his sight.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Then shall we know,<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> if we follow on<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> his going forth<sup><a href="h4161.htm">4161</a></sup> is prepared<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> as the morning;<sup><a href="h7837.htm">7837</a></sup> and he shall come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> unto us as the rain,<sup><a href="h1653.htm">1653</a></sup> as the latter<sup><a href="h4456.htm">4456</a></sup> and former rain<sup><a href="h3138.htm">3138</a></sup> unto the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  O Ephraim,<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> shall I do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> unto thee? O Judah,<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> shall I do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> unto thee? for your goodness<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> is as a morning<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> cloud,<sup><a href="h6051.htm">6051</a></sup> and as the early<sup><a href="h7925.htm">7925</a></sup> dew<sup><a href="h2919.htm">2919</a></sup> it goeth away.<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> have I hewed<sup><a href="h2672.htm">2672</a></sup> them by the prophets;<sup><a href="h5030.htm">5030</a></sup> I have slain<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> them by the words<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of my mouth:<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and thy judgments<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> are as the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> that goeth forth.<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I desired<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> mercy,<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> and not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> sacrifice;<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> and the knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> more than burnt offerings.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5930.htm">5930</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  But they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> like men<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> have transgressed<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> the covenant:<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> have they dealt treacherously<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> against me. </p>
 <p>8  Gilead<sup><a href="h1568.htm">1568</a></sup> is a city<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> of them that work<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> iniquity,<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> and is polluted<sup><a href="h6121.htm">6121</a></sup> with blood.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  And as troops of robbers<sup><a href="h1416.htm">1416</a></sup> wait<sup><a href="h2442.htm">2442</a></sup> for a man,<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> so the company<sup><a href="h2267.htm">2267</a></sup> of priests<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> murder<sup><a href="h7523.htm">7523</a></sup> in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> by consent:<sup><a href="h7926.htm">7926</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they commit<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> lewdness.<sup><a href="h2154.htm">2154</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  I have seen<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> a horrible thing<sup><a href="h8186.htm">8186</a></sup> in the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of Israel:<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> is the whoredom<sup><a href="h2184.htm">2184</a></sup> of Ephraim,<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> is defiled.<sup><a href="h2930.htm">2930</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Also,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> O Judah,<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> he hath set<sup><a href="h7896.htm">7896</a></sup> a harvest<sup><a href="h7105.htm">7105</a></sup> for thee, when I returned<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> the captivity<sup><a href="h7622.htm">7622</a></sup> of my people.<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 ὑγιάσει ἡμᾶς μετὰ δύο ἡμέρας ἐν τῇ ἡμέρᾳ τῇ τρίτῃ ἀναστησόμεθα καὶ ζησόμεθα ἐνώπιον αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>3 καὶ γνωσόμεθα διώξομεν τοῦ γνῶναι τὸν κύριον ὡς ὄρθρον ἕτοιμον εὑρήσομεν αὐτόν καὶ ἥξει ὡς ὑετὸς ἡμῖν πρόιμος καὶ ὄψιμος τῇ γῇ </p>
 <p>4 τί σοι ποιήσω Εφραιμ τί σοι ποιήσω Ιουδα τὸ δὲ ἔλεος ὑμῶν ὡς νεφέλη πρωινὴ καὶ ὡς δρόσος ὀρθρινὴ πορευομένη </p>
 <p>5 διὰ τοῦτο ἀπεθέρισα τοὺς προφήτας ὑμῶν ἀπέκτεινα αὐτοὺς ἐν ῥήμασιν στόματός μου καὶ τὸ κρίμα μου ὡς φῶς ἐξελεύσεται </p>
 <p>6 διότι ἔλεος θέλω καὶ οὐ θυσίαν καὶ ἐπίγνωσιν θεοῦ ἢ ὁλοκαυτώματα </p>
 <p>7 αὐτοὶ δέ εἰσιν ὡς ἄνθρωπος παραβαίνων διαθήκην ἐκεῖ κατεφρόνησέν μου </p>
 <p>8 Γαλααδ πόλις ἐργαζομένη μάταια ταράσσουσα ὕδωρ </p>
 <p>9 καὶ ἡ ἰσχύς σου ἀνδρὸς πειρατοῦ ἔκρυψαν ἱερεῖς ὁδὸν κυρίου ἐφόνευσαν Σικιμα ὅτι ἀνομίαν ἐποίησαν </p>
 <p>10 ἐν τῷ οἴκῳ Ισραηλ εἶδον φρικώδη ἐκεῖ πορνείαν τοῦ Εφραιμ ἐμιάνθη Ισραηλ καὶ Ιουδα </p>
 <p>11 ἄρχου τρυγᾶν σεαυτῷ ἐν τῷ ἐπιστρέφειν με τὴν αἰχμαλωσίαν τοῦ λαοῦ μου </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hos006.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [In tribulatione sua mane consurgent ad me:<br> Venite, et revertamur ad Dominum,<br></p>
 <p>2 quia ipse cepit, et sanabit nos;<br> percutiet, et curabit nos.<br></p>
 <p>3 Vivificabit nos post duos dies;<br> in die tertia suscitabit nos,<br> et vivemus in conspectu ejus.<br> Sciemus, sequemurque ut cognoscamus Dominum:<br> quasi diluculum præparatus est egressus ejus,<br> et veniet quasi imber nobis temporaneus et serotinus terræ.<br></p>
 <p>4 Quid faciam tibi, Ephraim?<br> quid faciam tibi, Juda?<br> misericordia vestra quasi nubes matutina,<br> et quasi ros mane pertransiens.<br></p>
 <p>5 Propter hoc dolavi in prophetis;<br> occidi eos in verbis oris mei:<br> et judicia tua quasi lux egredientur.<br></p>
 <p>6 Quia misericordiam volui, et non sacrificium;<br> et scientiam Dei plus quam holocausta.<br></p>
 <p>7 Ipsi autem sicut Adam transgressi sunt pactum:<br> ibi prævaricati sunt in me.<br></p>
 <p>8 Galaad civitas operantium idolum,<br> supplantata sanguine.<br></p>
 <p>9 Et quasi fauces virorum latronum,<br> particeps sacerdotum, in via interficientium pergentes de Sichem:<br> quia scelus operati sunt.<br></p>
 <p>10 In domo Israël vidi horrendum:<br> ibi fornicationes Ephraim,<br> contaminatus est Israël.<br></p>
 <p>11 Sed et Juda, pone messem tibi,<br> cum convertero captivitatem populi mei.]</p>
