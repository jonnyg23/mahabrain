 <p>5  As<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou knowest<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> is the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of the spirit,<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> nor how the bones<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> do grow in the womb<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> of her that is with child:<sup><a href="h4392.htm">4392</a></sup> even so<sup><a href="h3602.htm">3602</a></sup> thou knowest<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the works<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> maketh<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all.<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  Truly the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> is sweet,<sup><a href="h4966.htm">4966</a></sup> and a pleasant<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> thing it is for the eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> to behold<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the sun:<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  But<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> live<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> many<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> years,<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> and rejoice<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> in them all;<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> yet let him remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of darkness;<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> many.<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that cometh<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> is vanity.<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> </p>

 <p>10  Therefore remove<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> sorrow<sup><a href="h3708.htm">3708</a></sup> from thy heart,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> and put away<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> from thy flesh:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> childhood<sup><a href="h3208.htm">3208</a></sup> and youth<sup><a href="h7839.htm">7839</a></sup> are vanity.<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/ecc011.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἀπόστειλον τὸν ἄρτον σου ἐπὶ πρόσωπον τοῦ ὕδατος ὅτι ἐν πλήθει τῶν ἡμερῶν εὑρήσεις αὐτόν </p>
 <p>2 δὸς μερίδα τοῖς ἑπτὰ καί γε τοῖς ὀκτώ ὅτι οὐ γινώσκεις τί ἔσται πονηρὸν ἐπὶ τὴν γῆν </p>
 <p>3 ἐὰν πληρωθῶσιν τὰ νέφη ὑετοῦ ἐπὶ τὴν γῆν ἐκχέουσιν καὶ ἐὰν πέσῃ ξύλον ἐν τῷ νότῳ καὶ ἐὰν ἐν τῷ βορρᾷ τόπῳ οὗ πεσεῖται τὸ ξύλον ἐκεῖ ἔσται </p>
 <p>4 τηρῶν ἄνεμον οὐ σπερεῖ καὶ βλέπων ἐν ταῖς νεφέλαις οὐ θερίσει </p>
 <p>5 ἐν οἷς οὐκ ἔστιν γινώσκων τίς ἡ ὁδὸς τοῦ πνεύματος ὡς ὀστᾶ ἐν γαστρὶ τῆς κυοφορούσης οὕτως οὐ γνώσῃ τὰ ποιήματα τοῦ θεοῦ ὅσα ποιήσει σὺν τὰ πάντα </p>
 <p>6 ἐν πρωίᾳ σπεῖρον τὸ σπέρμα σου καὶ εἰς ἑσπέραν μὴ ἀφέτω ἡ χείρ σου ὅτι οὐ γινώσκεις ποῖον στοιχήσει ἢ τοῦτο ἢ τοῦτο καὶ ἐὰν τὰ δύο ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτὸ ἀγαθά </p>
 <p>7 καὶ γλυκὺ τὸ φῶς καὶ ἀγαθὸν τοῖς ὀφθαλμοῖς τοῦ βλέπειν σὺν τὸν ἥλιον </p>
 <p>8 ὅτι καὶ ἐὰν ἔτη πολλὰ ζήσεται ὁ ἄνθρωπος ἐν πᾶσιν αὐτοῖς εὐφρανθήσεται καὶ μνησθήσεται τὰς ἡμέρας τοῦ σκότους ὅτι πολλαὶ ἔσονται πᾶν τὸ ἐρχόμενον ματαιότης </p>
 <p>9 εὐφραίνου νεανίσκε ἐν νεότητί σου καὶ ἀγαθυνάτω σε ἡ καρδία σου ἐν ἡμέραις νεότητός σου καὶ περιπάτει ἐν ὁδοῖς καρδίας σου καὶ ἐν ὁράσει ὀφθαλμῶν σου καὶ γνῶθι ὅτι ἐπὶ πᾶσι τούτοις ἄξει σε ὁ θεὸς ἐν κρίσει </p>
 <p>10 καὶ ἀπόστησον θυμὸν ἀπὸ καρδίας σου καὶ παράγαγε πονηρίαν ἀπὸ σαρκός σου ὅτι ἡ νεότης καὶ ἡ ἄνοια ματαιότης </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/ecc011.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Mitte panem tuum super transeuntes aquas,<br> quia post tempora multa invenies illum.<br></p>
 <p>2 Da partem septem necnon et octo,<br> quia ignoras quid futurum sit mali super terram.<br></p>
 <p>3 Si repletæ fuerint nubes,<br> imbrem super terram effundent.<br> Si ceciderit lignum ad austrum aut ad aquilonem,<br> in quocumque loco ceciderit, ibi erit.<br></p>
 <p>4 Qui observat ventum non seminat;<br> et qui considerat nubes numquam metet.<br></p>
 <p>5 Quomodo ignoras quæ sit via spiritus,<br> et qua ratione compingantur ossa in ventre prægnantis,<br> sic nescis opera Dei,<br> qui fabricator est omnium.<br></p>
 <p>6 Mane semina semen tuum,<br> et vespere ne cesset manus tua:<br> quia nescis quid magis oriatur, hoc aut illud;<br> et si utrumque simul, melius erit.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>7 [Dulce lumen,<br> et delectabile est oculis videre solem.<br></p>
 <p>8 Si annis multis vixerit homo,<br> et in his omnibus lætatus fuerit,<br> meminisse debet tenebrosi temporis, et dierum multorum,<br> qui cum venerint, vanitatis arguentur præterita.<br></p>
 <p>9 Lætare ergo, juvenis, in adolescentia tua,<br> et in bono sit cor tuum in diebus juventutis tuæ:<br> et ambula in viis cordis tui,<br> et in intuitu oculorum tuorum,<br> et scito quod pro omnibus his adducet te Deus in judicium.<br></p>
 <p>10 Aufer iram a corde tuo,<br> et amove malitiam a carne tua:<br> adolescentia enim et voluptas vana sunt.]</p>
