 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/est005.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Now it came to pass<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> on the third<sup><a href="h7992.htm">7992</a></sup> day,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> that Esther<sup><a href="h0635.htm">635</a></sup> put on<sup><a href="h3847.htm">3847</a></sup> her royal<sup><a href="h4438.htm">4438</a></sup> apparel, and stood<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> in the inner<sup><a href="h6442.htm">6442</a></sup> court<sup><a href="h2691.htm">2691</a></sup> of the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> over against<sup><a href="h5227.htm">5227</a></sup> the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> house:<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> and the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> sat<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his royal<sup><a href="h4438.htm">4438</a></sup> throne<sup><a href="h3678.htm">3678</a></sup> in the royal<sup><a href="h4438.htm">4438</a></sup> house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> over against<sup><a href="h5227.htm">5227</a></sup> the gate<sup><a href="h6607.htm">6607</a></sup> of the house.<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> </p>

 <p>3  Then said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> unto her, What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> wilt thou, queen<sup><a href="h4436.htm">4436</a></sup> Esther?<sup><a href="h0635.htm">635</a></sup> and what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> is thy request?<sup><a href="h1246.htm">1246</a></sup> it shall be even given<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> thee to<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the half<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> of the kingdom.<sup><a href="h4438.htm">4438</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  And Esther<sup><a href="h0635.htm">635</a></sup> answered,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it seem good<sup><a href="h2895.htm">2895</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> let the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> and Haman<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> this day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the banquet<sup><a href="h4960.htm">4960</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I have prepared<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> for him. </p>

 <p>6  And the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto Esther<sup><a href="h0635.htm">635</a></sup> at the banquet<sup><a href="h4960.htm">4960</a></sup> of wine,<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> is thy petition?<sup><a href="h7596.htm">7596</a></sup> and it shall be granted<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> thee: and what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> is thy request?<sup><a href="h1246.htm">1246</a></sup> even to<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the half<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> of the kingdom<sup><a href="h4438.htm">4438</a></sup> it shall be performed.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Then answered<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> Esther,<sup><a href="h0635.htm">635</a></sup> and said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> My petition<sup><a href="h7596.htm">7596</a></sup> and my request<sup><a href="h1246.htm">1246</a></sup> is; </p>
 <p>8  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> I have found<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> favor<sup><a href="h2580.htm">2580</a></sup> in the sight<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> of the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> and if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it please<sup><a href="h2895.htm">2895</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> to grant<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> my petition,<sup><a href="h7596.htm">7596</a></sup> and to perform<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> my request,<sup><a href="h1246.htm">1246</a></sup> let the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> and Haman<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the banquet<sup><a href="h4960.htm">4960</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I shall prepare<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> for them, and I will do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> tomorrow<sup><a href="h4279.htm">4279</a></sup> as the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> hath said.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Then went Haman forth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> joyful<sup><a href="h8056.htm">8056</a></sup> and with a glad<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> heart:<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> but when Haman<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> saw<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Mordecai<sup><a href="h4782.htm">4782</a></sup> in the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> gate,<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> that he stood not up,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> moved<sup><a href="h2111.htm">2111</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> him,<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> was full<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of indignation<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> Mordecai.<sup><a href="h4782.htm">4782</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Nevertheless Haman<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> refrained himself:<sup><a href="h0662.htm">662</a></sup> and when he came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> home,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> he sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> and called<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> for <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> his friends,<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> and Zeresh<sup><a href="h2238.htm">2238</a></sup> his wife.<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  And Haman<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> told<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> them of <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> of his riches,<sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> and the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of his children,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the things wherein<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> had promoted<sup><a href="h1431.htm">1431</a></sup> him, and how<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he had advanced<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> him above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the princes<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> and servants<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> of the king.<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Haman<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> moreover,<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> Yea, Esther<sup><a href="h0635.htm">635</a></sup> the queen<sup><a href="h4436.htm">4436</a></sup> did let no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> man come in<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the banquet<sup><a href="h4960.htm">4960</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> she had prepared<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> myself; and tomorrow<sup><a href="h4279.htm">4279</a></sup> am I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> invited<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> unto her also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the king.<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Yet all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> availeth<sup><a href="h7737.htm">7737</a></sup> me nothing,<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> so long<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> as I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Mordecai<sup><a href="h4782.htm">4782</a></sup> the Jew<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> sitting<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> at the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> gate.<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Then said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Zeresh<sup><a href="h2238.htm">2238</a></sup> his wife<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his friends<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> unto him, Let a gallows<sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> be made<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> of fifty<sup><a href="h2572.htm">2572</a></sup> cubits<sup><a href="h0520.htm">520</a></sup> high,<sup><a href="h1364.htm">1364</a></sup> and tomorrow<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> speak<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> thou unto the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> that <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Mordecai<sup><a href="h4782.htm">4782</a></sup> may be hanged<sup><a href="h8518.htm">8518</a></sup> thereon:<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> then go<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> thou in merrily<sup><a href="h8056.htm">8056</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the banquet.<sup><a href="h4960.htm">4960</a></sup> And the thing<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> pleased<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> Haman;<sup><a href="h2001.htm">2001</a></sup> and he caused the gallows<sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> to be made.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/est005.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 καὶ ἐγενήθη ἐν τῇ ἡμέρᾳ τῇ τρίτῃ ὡς ἐπαύσατο προσευχομένη ἐξεδύσατο τὰ ἱμάτια τῆς θεραπείας καὶ περιεβάλετο τὴν δόξαν αὐτῆς  <br>
  καὶ γενηθεῖσα ἐπιφανὴς ἐπικαλεσαμένη τὸν πάντων ἐπόπτην θεὸν καὶ σωτῆρα παρέλαβεν τὰς δύο ἅβρας καὶ τῇ μὲν μιᾷ ἐπηρείδετο ὡς τρυφερευομένη ἡ δὲ ἑτέρα ἐπηκολούθει κουφίζουσα τὴν ἔνδυσιν αὐτῆς  <br>
  καὶ αὐτὴ ἐρυθριῶσα ἀκμῇ κάλλους αὐτῆς καὶ τὸ πρόσωπον αὐτῆς ἱλαρὸν ὡς προσφιλές ἡ δὲ καρδία αὐτῆς ἀπεστενωμένη ἀπὸ τοῦ φόβου  <br>
  καὶ εἰσελθοῦσα πάσας τὰς θύρας κατέστη ἐνώπιον τοῦ βασιλέως καὶ αὐτὸς ἐκάθητο ἐπὶ τοῦ θρόνου τῆς βασιλείας αὐτοῦ καὶ πᾶσαν στολὴν τῆς ἐπιφανείας αὐτοῦ ἐνεδεδύκει ὅλος διὰ χρυσοῦ καὶ λίθων πολυτελῶν καὶ ἦν φοβερὸς σφόδρα  <br>
  καὶ ἄρας τὸ πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ πεπυρωμένον δόξῃ ἐν ἀκμῇ θυμοῦ ἔβλεψεν καὶ ἔπεσεν ἡ βασίλισσα καὶ μετέβαλεν τὸ χρῶμα αὐτῆς ἐν ἐκλύσει καὶ κατεπέκυψεν ἐπὶ τὴν κεφαλὴν τῆς ἅβρας τῆς προπορευομένης  <br>
  καὶ μετέβαλεν ὁ θεὸς τὸ πνεῦμα τοῦ βασιλέως εἰς πραΰτητα καὶ ἀγωνιάσας ἀνεπήδησεν ἀπὸ τοῦ θρόνου αὐτοῦ καὶ ἀνέλαβεν αὐτὴν ἐπὶ τὰς ἀγκάλας αὐτοῦ μέχρις οὗ κατέστη καὶ παρεκάλει αὐτὴν λόγοις εἰρηνικοῖς καὶ εἶπεν αὐτῇ  <br>
  τί ἐστιν Εσθηρ ἐγὼ ὁ ἀδελφός σου θάρσει οὐ μὴ ἀποθάνῃς ὅτι κοινὸν τὸ πρόσταγμα ἡμῶν ἐστιν πρόσελθε </p>
 <p>2 καὶ ἄρας τὴν χρυσῆν ῥάβδον ἐπέθηκεν ἐπὶ τὸν τράχηλον αὐτῆς καὶ ἠσπάσατο αὐτὴν καὶ εἶπεν λάλησόν μοι  <br>
  καὶ εἶπεν αὐτῷ εἶδόν σε κύριε ὡς ἄγγελον θεοῦ καὶ ἐταράχθη ἡ καρδία μου ἀπὸ φόβου τῆς δόξης σου ὅτι θαυμαστὸς εἶ κύριε καὶ τὸ πρόσωπόν σου χαρίτων μεστόν  <br>
  ἐν δὲ τῷ διαλέγεσθαι αὐτὴν ἔπεσεν ἀπὸ ἐκλύσεως αὐτῆς καὶ ὁ βασιλεὺς ἐταράσσετο καὶ πᾶσα ἡ θεραπεία αὐτοῦ παρεκάλει αὐτήν </p>
 <p>3 καὶ εἶπεν ὁ βασιλεύς τί θέλεις Εσθηρ καὶ τί σού ἐστιν τὸ ἀξίωμα ἕως τοῦ ἡμίσους τῆς βασιλείας μου καὶ ἔσται σοι </p>
 <p>4 εἶπεν δὲ Εσθηρ ἡμέρα μου ἐπίσημος σήμερόν ἐστιν εἰ οὖν δοκεῖ τῷ βασιλεῖ ἐλθάτω καὶ αὐτὸς καὶ Αμαν εἰς τὴν δοχήν ἣν ποιήσω σήμερον </p>
 <p>5 καὶ εἶπεν ὁ βασιλεύς κατασπεύσατε Αμαν ὅπως ποιήσωμεν τὸν λόγον Εσθηρ καὶ παραγίνονται ἀμφότεροι εἰς τὴν δοχήν ἣν εἶπεν Εσθηρ </p>
 <p>6 ἐν δὲ τῷ πότῳ εἶπεν ὁ βασιλεὺς πρὸς Εσθηρ τί ἐστιν βασίλισσα Εσθηρ καὶ ἔσται σοι ὅσα ἀξιοῖς </p>
 <p>7 καὶ εἶπεν τὸ αἴτημά μου καὶ τὸ ἀξίωμά μου </p>
 <p>8 εἰ εὗρον χάριν ἐνώπιον τοῦ βασιλέως ἐλθάτω ὁ βασιλεὺς καὶ Αμαν ἐπὶ τὴν αὔριον εἰς τὴν δοχήν ἣν ποιήσω αὐτοῖς καὶ αὔριον ποιήσω τὰ αὐτά </p>
 <p>9 καὶ ἐξῆλθεν ὁ Αμαν ἀπὸ τοῦ βασιλέως ὑπερχαρὴς εὐφραινόμενος ἐν δὲ τῷ ἰδεῖν Αμαν Μαρδοχαῖον τὸν Ιουδαῖον ἐν τῇ αὐλῇ ἐθυμώθη σφόδρα </p>
 <p>10 καὶ εἰσελθὼν εἰς τὰ ἴδια ἐκάλεσεν τοὺς φίλους καὶ Ζωσαραν τὴν γυναῖκα αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>11 καὶ ὑπέδειξεν αὐτοῖς τὸν πλοῦτον αὐτοῦ καὶ τὴν δόξαν ἣν ὁ βασιλεὺς αὐτῷ περιέθηκεν καὶ ὡς ἐποίησεν αὐτὸν πρωτεύειν καὶ ἡγεῖσθαι τῆς βασιλείας </p>
 <p>12 καὶ εἶπεν Αμαν οὐ κέκληκεν ἡ βασίλισσα μετὰ τοῦ βασιλέως οὐδένα εἰς τὴν δοχὴν ἀλλ᾽ ἢ ἐμέ καὶ εἰς τὴν αὔριον κέκλημαι </p>
 <p>13 καὶ ταῦτά μοι οὐκ ἀρέσκει ὅταν ἴδω Μαρδοχαῖον τὸν Ιουδαῖον ἐν τῇ αὐλῇ </p>
 <p>14 καὶ εἶπεν πρὸς αὐτὸν Ζωσαρα ἡ γυνὴ αὐτοῦ καὶ οἱ φίλοι κοπήτω σοι ξύλον πηχῶν πεντήκοντα ὄρθρου δὲ εἰπὸν τῷ βασιλεῖ καὶ κρεμασθήτω Μαρδοχαῖος ἐπὶ τοῦ ξύλου σὺ δὲ εἴσελθε εἰς τὴν δοχὴν σὺν τῷ βασιλεῖ καὶ εὐφραίνου καὶ ἤρεσεν τὸ ῥῆμα τῷ Αμαν καὶ ἡτοιμάσθη τὸ ξύλον </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/est005.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Die autem tertio induta est Esther regalibus vestimentis, et stetit in atrio domus regiæ, quod erat interius, contra basilicam regis: at ille sedebat super solium suum in consistorio palatii contra ostium domus.</p>
 <p>2 Cumque vidisset Esther reginam stantem, placuit oculis ejus, et extendit contra eam virgam auream, quam tenebat manu: quæ accedens, osculata est summitatem virgæ ejus.</p>
 <p>3 Dixitque ad eam rex: Quid vis, Esther regina? quæ est petitio tua? etiam si dimidiam partem regni petieris, dabitur tibi.</p>
 <p>4 At illa respondit: Si regi placet, obsecro ut venias ad me hodie, et Aman tecum, ad convivium quod paravi.</p>
 <p>5 Statimque rex: Vocate, inquit, cito Aman ut Esther obediat voluntati. Venerunt itaque rex et Aman ad convivium, quod eis regina paraverat.</p>
 <p>6 Dixitque ei rex, postquam vinum biberat abundanter: Quid petis ut detur tibi? et pro qua re postulas? etiam si dimidiam partem regni mei petieris, impetrabis.</p>
 <p>7 Cui respondit Esther: Petitio mea, et preces sunt istæ:</p>
 <p>8 si inveni in conspectu regis gratiam, et si regi placet ut det mihi quod postulo, et meam impleat petitionem: veniat rex et Aman ad convivium quod paravi eis, et cras aperiam regi voluntatem meam.<br>
 </p>
 <p>9 Egressus est itaque illo die Aman lætus et alacer. Cumque vidisset Mardochæum sedentem ante fores palatii, et non solum non assurrexisse sibi, sed nec motum quidem de loco sessionis suæ, indignatus est valde:</p>
 <p>10 et dissimulata ira reversus in domum suam, convocavit ad se amicos suos, et Zares uxorem suam,</p>
 <p>11 et exposuit illis magnitudinem divitiarum suarum, filiorumque turbam, et quanta eum gloria super omnes principes et servos suos rex elevasset.</p>
 <p>12 Et post hæc ait: Regina quoque Esther nullum alium vocavit ad convivium cum rege præter me: apud quam etiam cras cum rege pransurus sum.</p>
 <p>13 Et cum hæc omnia habeam, nihil me habere puto, quamdiu videro Mardochæum Judæum sedentem ante fores regias.</p>
 <p>14 Responderuntque ei Zares uxor ejus, et ceteri amici: Jube parari excelsam trabem, habentem altitudinis quinquaginta cubitos, et dic mane regi ut appendatur super eam Mardochæus, et sic ibis cum rege lætus ad convivium. Placuit ei consilium, et jussit excelsam parari crucem.</p>
