 <p>2  Thou hast made the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> to tremble;<sup><a href="h7493.htm">7493</a></sup> thou hast broken<sup><a href="h6480.htm">6480</a></sup> it: heal<sup><a href="h7495.htm">7495</a></sup> the breaches<sup><a href="h7667.htm">7667</a></sup> thereof; for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it shaketh.<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Thou hast showed<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> thy people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> hard<sup><a href="h7186.htm">7186</a></sup> things: thou hast made us to drink<sup><a href="h8248.htm">8248</a></sup> the wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> of astonishment.<sup><a href="h8653.htm">8653</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Thou hast given<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> a banner<sup><a href="h5251.htm">5251</a></sup> to them that fear<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> thee, that it may be displayed<sup><a href="h5127.htm">5127</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of the truth.<sup><a href="h7189.htm">7189</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  That<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> thy beloved<sup><a href="h3039.htm">3039</a></sup> may be delivered;<sup><a href="h2502.htm">2502</a></sup> save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> with thy right hand,<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> and hear<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>6  God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> hath spoken<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> in his holiness;<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> I will rejoice,<sup><a href="h5937.htm">5937</a></sup> I will divide<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> Shechem,<sup><a href="h7927.htm">7927</a></sup> and mete out<sup><a href="h4058.htm">4058</a></sup> the valley<sup><a href="h6010.htm">6010</a></sup> of Succoth.<sup><a href="h5523.htm">5523</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Gilead<sup><a href="h1568.htm">1568</a></sup> is mine, and Manasseh<sup><a href="h4519.htm">4519</a></sup> is mine; Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> also is the strength<sup><a href="h4581.htm">4581</a></sup> of mine head;<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> is my lawgiver;<sup><a href="h2710.htm">2710</a></sup> </p>

 <p>9  Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> will bring<sup><a href="h2986.htm">2986</a></sup> me into the strong<sup><a href="h4692.htm">4692</a></sup> city?<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> will lead<sup><a href="h5148.htm">5148</a></sup> me into<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> Edom?<sup><a href="h0123.htm">123</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Wilt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which hadst cast us off?<sup><a href="h2186.htm">2186</a></sup> and thou, O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which didst not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> go out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> with our armies?<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Give<sup><a href="h3051.htm">3051</a></sup> us help<sup><a href="h5833.htm">5833</a></sup> from trouble:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> for vain<sup><a href="h7723.htm">7723</a></sup> is the help<sup><a href="h8668.htm">8668</a></sup> of man.<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> </p>
