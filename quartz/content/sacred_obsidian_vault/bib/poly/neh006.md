 <p>2  That Sanballat<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> and Geshem<sup><a href="h1654.htm">1654</a></sup> sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me, saying,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Come,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> let us meet<sup><a href="h3259.htm">3259</a></sup> together<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> in some one of the villages<sup><a href="h3715.htm">3715</a></sup> in the plain<sup><a href="h1237.htm">1237</a></sup> of Ono.<sup><a href="h0207.htm">207</a></sup> But they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> thought<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> to do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> me mischief.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  And I sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> messengers<sup><a href="h4397.htm">4397</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them, saying,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> am doing<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> a great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> work,<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> so that I cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> come down:<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> why<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> should the work<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> cease,<sup><a href="h7673.htm">7673</a></sup> whilst<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I leave<sup><a href="h7503.htm">7503</a></sup> it, and come down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> you? </p>
 <p>4  Yet they sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me four<sup><a href="h0702.htm">702</a></sup> times<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> after this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> sort;<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> and I answered<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> them after the same<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> manner.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Then sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> Sanballat<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> his servant<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me in like<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> manner<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> the fifth<sup><a href="h2549.htm">2549</a></sup> time<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> with an open<sup><a href="h6605.htm">6605</a></sup> letter<sup><a href="h0107.htm">107</a></sup> in his hand;<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Wherein was written,<sup><a href="h3789.htm">3789</a></sup> It is reported<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> among the heathen,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> and Gashmu<sup><a href="h1654.htm">1654</a></sup> saith<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> it, that thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> and the Jews<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> think<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> to rebel:<sup><a href="h4775.htm">4775</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> which cause<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> buildest<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> that thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> mayest be<sup><a href="h1933.htm">1933</a></sup> their king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> according to these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> words.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  And thou hast also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> appointed<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> prophets<sup><a href="h5030.htm">5030</a></sup> to preach<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee at Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> saying,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> There is a king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> in Judah:<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> and now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> shall it be reported<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> to the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> according to these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> words.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> Come<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> therefore, and let us take counsel<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> together.<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Then I sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> him, saying,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> There are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> such<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> things<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> done as<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> sayest,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> feignest<sup><a href="h0908.htm">908</a></sup> them out of thine own heart.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> made us afraid,<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> saying,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Their hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> shall be weakened<sup><a href="h7503.htm">7503</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> the work,<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> that it be not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> done.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> Now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> therefore, O God, strengthen<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> my hands.<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Afterward I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> unto the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of Shemaiah<sup><a href="h8098.htm">8098</a></sup> the son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Delaiah<sup><a href="h1806.htm">1806</a></sup> the son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Mehetabeel,<sup><a href="h4105.htm">4105</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> was shut up;<sup><a href="h6113.htm">6113</a></sup> and he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Let us meet together<sup><a href="h3259.htm">3259</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> the temple,<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> and let us shut<sup><a href="h5462.htm">5462</a></sup> the doors<sup><a href="h1817.htm">1817</a></sup> of the temple:<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they will come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to slay<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> thee; yea, in the night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> will they come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to slay<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>11  And I said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Should such a man as I<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> flee?<sup><a href="h1272.htm">1272</a></sup> and who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> is there, that,<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> being as I<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> am, would go<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the temple<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> to save his life?<sup><a href="h2425.htm">2425</a></sup> I will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> go in.<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  And, lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> I perceived<sup><a href="h5234.htm">5234</a></sup> that God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> had not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> him; but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> that he pronounced<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> this prophecy<sup><a href="h5016.htm">5016</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me: for Tobiah<sup><a href="h2900.htm">2900</a></sup> and Sanballat<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> had hired<sup><a href="h7936.htm">7936</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>13  Therefore<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> was he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hired,<sup><a href="h7936.htm">7936</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> I should be afraid,<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> and do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> so,<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> and sin,<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> and that they might have<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> matter for an evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> report,<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> they might reproach<sup><a href="h2778.htm">2778</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>14  My God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> think<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> thou upon Tobiah<sup><a href="h2900.htm">2900</a></sup> and Sanballat<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> according to these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> their works,<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> and<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> on the prophetess<sup><a href="h5031.htm">5031</a></sup> Noadiah,<sup><a href="h5129.htm">5129</a></sup> and the rest<sup><a href="h3499.htm">3499</a></sup> of the prophets,<sup><a href="h5030.htm">5030</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> would have<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> put me in fear.<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  So the wall<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> was finished<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> in the twenty<sup><a href="h6242.htm">6242</a></sup> and fifth<sup><a href="h2568.htm">2568</a></sup> day of the month Elul,<sup><a href="h0435.htm">435</a></sup> in fifty<sup><a href="h2572.htm">2572</a></sup> and two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> days.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>

 <p>18  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> there were many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> in Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> sworn<sup><a href="h1167.htm">1167</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7621.htm">7621</a></sup> unto him, because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> was the son in law<sup><a href="h2860.htm">2860</a></sup> of Shechaniah<sup><a href="h7935.htm">7935</a></sup> the son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Arah;<sup><a href="h0733.htm">733</a></sup> and his son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> Johanan<sup><a href="h3076.htm">3076</a></sup> had taken<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the daughter<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> of Meshullam<sup><a href="h4918.htm">4918</a></sup> the son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Berechiah.<sup><a href="h1296.htm">1296</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/neh006.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Factum est autem, cum audisset Sanaballat, et Tobias, et Gossem Arabs, et ceteri inimici nostri, quod ædificassem ego murum, et non esset in ipso residua interruptio (usque ad tempus autem illud valvas non posueram in portis),</p>
 <p>2 miserunt Sanaballat et Gossem ad me, dicentes: Veni, et percutiamus fdus pariter in viculis in campo Ono. Ipsi autem cogitabant ut facerent mihi malum.</p>
 <p>3 Misi ergo ad eos nuntios, dicens: Opus grande ego facio, et non possum descendere, ne forte negligatur cum venero, et descendero ad vos.</p>
 <p>4 Miserunt autem ad me secundum verbum hoc per quatuor vices: et respondi eis juxta sermonem priorem.</p>
 <p>5 Et misit ad me Sanaballat juxta verbum prius quinta vice puerum suum, et epistolam habebat in manu sua scriptam hoc modo:</p>
 <p>6 In gentibus auditum est, et Gossem dixit, quod tu et Judæi cogitetis rebellare, et propterea ædifices murum, et levare te velis super eos regem: propter quam causam</p>
 <p>7 et prophetas posueris, qui prædicent de te in Jerusalem, dicentes: Rex in Judæa est. Auditurus est rex verba hæc: idcirco nunc veni, ut ineamus consilium pariter.</p>
 <p>8 Et misi ad eos, dicens: Non est factum secundum verba hæc, quæ tu loqueris: de corde enim tuo tu componis hæc.</p>
 <p>9 Omnes enim hi terrebant nos, cogitantes quod cessarent manus nostræ ab opere, et quiesceremus: quam ob causam magis confortavi manus meas.<br>
 </p>
 <p>10 Et ingressus sum domum Semaiæ filii Dalaiæ filii Metabeel secreto. Qui ait: Tractemus nobiscum in domo Dei in medio templi, et claudamus portas ædis: quia venturi sunt ut interficiant te, et nocte venturi sunt ad occidendum te.</p>
 <p>11 Et dixi: Num quisquam similis mei fugit? et quis ut ego ingredietur templum, et vivet? non ingrediar.</p>
 <p>12 Et intellexi quod Deus non misisset eum, sed quasi vaticinans locutus esset ad me, et Tobias et Sanaballat conduxissent eum.</p>
 <p>13 Acceperat enim pretium, ut territus facerem, et peccarem, et haberent malum quod exprobrarent mihi.</p>
 <p>14 Memento mei, Domine, pro Tobia et Sanaballat, juxta opera eorum talia: sed et Noadiæ prophetæ, et ceterorum prophetarum, qui terrebant me.<br>
 </p>
 <p>15 Completus est autem murus vigesimo quinto die mensis Elul, quinquaginta duobus diebus.</p>
 <p>16 Factum est ergo cum audissent omnes inimici nostri, ut timerent universæ gentes quæ erant in circuitu nostro, et conciderent intra semetipsos, et scirent quod a Deo factum esset opus hoc.</p>
 <p>17 Sed et in diebus illis multæ optimatum Judæorum epistolæ mittebantur ad Tobiam, et a Tobia veniebant ad eos.</p>
 <p>18 Multi enim erant in Judæa habentes juramentum ejus, quia gener erat Secheniæ filii Area, et Johanan filius ejus acceperat filiam Mosollam filii Barachiæ:</p>
 <p>19 sed et laudabant eum coram me, et verba mea nuntiabant ei: et Tobias mittebat epistolas ut terreret me.</p>
