 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/rut003.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Then Naomi<sup><a href="h5281.htm">5281</a></sup> her mother-in-law<sup><a href="h2545.htm">2545</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto her, My daughter,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> shall I not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> rest<sup><a href="h4494.htm">4494</a></sup> for thee, that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> it may be well<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> with thee? </p>
 <p>2  And now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> is not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> Boaz<sup><a href="h1162.htm">1162</a></sup> of our kindred,<sup><a href="h4130.htm">4130</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> whose<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> maidens<sup><a href="h5291.htm">5291</a></sup> thou wast?<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> Behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> winnoweth<sup><a href="h2219.htm">2219</a></sup> barley<sup><a href="h8184.htm">8184</a></sup> tonight<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> in <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the threshingfloor.<sup><a href="h1637.htm">1637</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Wash<sup><a href="h7364.htm">7364</a></sup> thyself therefore, and anoint<sup><a href="h5480.htm">5480</a></sup> thee, and put<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> thy raiment<sup><a href="h8071.htm">8071</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee, and get thee down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> to the floor:<sup><a href="h1637.htm">1637</a></sup> but make not thyself known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> unto the man,<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> he shall have done<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> eating<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> and drinking.<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  And it shall be,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> when he lieth down,<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> that thou shalt mark<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> where<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> he shall lie,<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> and thou shalt go in,<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> and uncover<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> his feet,<sup><a href="h4772.htm">4772</a></sup> and lay thee down;<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> and he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> will tell<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> thee <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou shalt do.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  And she said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> her, All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou sayest<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me I will do.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  And she went down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> unto the floor,<sup><a href="h1637.htm">1637</a></sup> and did<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> according to all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> her mother-in-law<sup><a href="h2545.htm">2545</a></sup> bade<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> her. </p>
 <p>7  And when Boaz<sup><a href="h1162.htm">1162</a></sup> had eaten<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> and drunk,<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> and his heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> was merry,<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> he went<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to lie down<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> at the end<sup><a href="h7097.htm">7097</a></sup> of the heap of corn:<sup><a href="h6194.htm">6194</a></sup> and she came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> softly,<sup><a href="h3909.htm">3909</a></sup> and uncovered<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> his feet,<sup><a href="h4772.htm">4772</a></sup> and laid her down.<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  And it came to pass<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> at midnight,<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> that the man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> was afraid,<sup><a href="h2729.htm">2729</a></sup> and turned himself:<sup><a href="h3943.htm">3943</a></sup> and, behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> a woman<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> lay<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> at his feet.<sup><a href="h4772.htm">4772</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  And he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> art thou?<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> And she answered,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> am Ruth<sup><a href="h7327.htm">7327</a></sup> thine handmaid:<sup><a href="h0519.htm">519</a></sup> spread<sup><a href="h6566.htm">6566</a></sup> therefore thy skirt<sup><a href="h3671.htm">3671</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thine handmaid;<sup><a href="h0519.htm">519</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art a near kinsman.<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  And he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Blessed<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> be thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> my daughter:<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> for thou hast showed more<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> kindness<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> in the latter end<sup><a href="h0314.htm">314</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> at the beginning,<sup><a href="h7223.htm">7223</a></sup> inasmuch as thou followedst<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0310.htm">310</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h1115.htm">1115</a></sup> young men,<sup><a href="h0970.htm">970</a></sup> whether<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> poor<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> or<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> rich.<sup><a href="h6223.htm">6223</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  And now,<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> my daughter,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> fear<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> not;<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> I will do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> to thee all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou requirest:<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the city<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> of my people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> doth know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou art a virtuous<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> woman.<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  And now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is true<sup><a href="h0551.htm">551</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> am thy near kinsman:<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> howbeit<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> there is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> a kinsman<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> nearer<sup><a href="h7138.htm">7138</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> I. </p>
 <p>13  Tarry<sup><a href="h3885.htm">3885</a></sup> this night,<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> and it shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> in the morning,<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> that if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> he will perform unto thee the part of a kinsman,<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> well;<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> let him do the kinsman's part:<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> but if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> he will<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> do the part of a kinsman<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> to thee, then will I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> do the part of a kinsman<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> to thee, as the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> liveth:<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> lie down<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the morning.<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  And she lay<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> at his feet<sup><a href="h4772.htm">4772</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the morning:<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> and she rose up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h2958.htm">2958</a></sup> one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> could know<sup><a href="h5234.htm">5234</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> another.<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> And he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Let it not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> be known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> a woman<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into the floor.<sup><a href="h1637.htm">1637</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Also he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Bring<sup><a href="h3051.htm">3051</a></sup> the veil<sup><a href="h4304.htm">4304</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou hast upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee, and hold<sup><a href="h0270.htm">270</a></sup> it.  <p>16  And when she came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> her mother-in-law,<sup><a href="h2545.htm">2545</a></sup> she said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> art thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> my daughter?<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> And she told<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> her <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> had done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> to her. </p>
 <p>17  And she said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> These<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> six<sup><a href="h8337.htm">8337</a></sup> measures of barley<sup><a href="h8184.htm">8184</a></sup> gave<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> he me; for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me, Go<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> empty<sup><a href="h7387.htm">7387</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thy mother-in-law.<sup><a href="h2545.htm">2545</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Then said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> she, Sit still,<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> my daughter,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> how<sup><a href="h0349.htm">349</a></sup> the matter<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> will fall:<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be in rest,<sup><a href="h8252.htm">8252</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> he have finished<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> the thing<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> this day.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/rut003.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἶπεν δὲ αὐτῇ Νωεμιν ἡ πενθερὰ αὐτῆς θύγατερ οὐ μὴ ζητήσω σοι ἀνάπαυσιν ἵνα εὖ γένηταί σοι </p>
 <p>2 καὶ νῦν οὐχὶ Βοος γνώριμος ἡμῶν οὗ ἦς μετὰ τῶν κορασίων αὐτοῦ ἰδοὺ αὐτὸς λικμᾷ τὸν ἅλωνα τῶν κριθῶν ταύτῃ τῇ νυκτί </p>
 <p>3 σὺ δὲ λούσῃ καὶ ἀλείψῃ καὶ περιθήσεις τὸν ἱματισμόν σου ἐπὶ σεαυτῇ καὶ ἀναβήσῃ ἐπὶ τὸν ἅλω μὴ γνωρισθῇς τῷ ἀνδρὶ ἕως οὗ συντελέσαι αὐτὸν πιεῖν καὶ φαγεῖν </p>
 <p>4 καὶ ἔσται ἐν τῷ κοιμηθῆναι αὐτόν καὶ γνώσῃ τὸν τόπον ὅπου κοιμᾶται ἐκεῖ καὶ ἐλεύσῃ καὶ ἀποκαλύψεις τὰ πρὸς ποδῶν αὐτοῦ καὶ κοιμηθήσῃ καὶ αὐτὸς ἀπαγγελεῖ σοι ἃ ποιήσεις </p>
 <p>5 εἶπεν δὲ Ρουθ πρὸς αὐτήν πάντα ὅσα ἐὰν εἴπῃς ποιήσω </p>
 <p>6 καὶ κατέβη εἰς τὸν ἅλω καὶ ἐποίησεν κατὰ πάντα ὅσα ἐνετείλατο αὐτῇ ἡ πενθερὰ αὐτῆς </p>
 <p>7 καὶ ἔφαγεν Βοος καὶ ἠγαθύνθη ἡ καρδία αὐτοῦ καὶ ἦλθεν κοιμηθῆναι ἐν μερίδι τῆς στοιβῆς ἡ δὲ ἦλθεν κρυφῇ καὶ ἀπεκάλυψεν τὰ πρὸς ποδῶν αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>8 ἐγένετο δὲ ἐν τῷ μεσονυκτίῳ καὶ ἐξέστη ὁ ἀνὴρ καὶ ἐταράχθη καὶ ἰδοὺ γυνὴ κοιμᾶται πρὸς ποδῶν αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>9 εἶπεν δέ τίς εἶ σύ ἡ δὲ εἶπεν ἐγώ εἰμι Ρουθ ἡ δούλη σου καὶ περιβαλεῖς τὸ πτερύγιόν σου ἐπὶ τὴν δούλην σου ὅτι ἀγχιστεὺς εἶ σύ </p>
 <p>10 καὶ εἶπεν Βοος εὐλογημένη σὺ τῷ κυρίῳ θεῷ θύγατερ ὅτι ἠγάθυνας τὸ ἔλεός σου τὸ ἔσχατον ὑπὲρ τὸ πρῶτον τὸ μὴ πορευθῆναί σε ὀπίσω νεανιῶν εἴτοι πτωχὸς εἴτοι πλούσιος </p>
 <p>11 καὶ νῦν θύγατερ μὴ φοβοῦ πάντα ὅσα ἐὰν εἴπῃς ποιήσω σοι οἶδεν γὰρ πᾶσα φυλὴ λαοῦ μου ὅτι γυνὴ δυνάμεως εἶ σύ </p>
 <p>12 καὶ ὅτι ἀληθῶς ἀγχιστεὺς ἐγώ εἰμι καί γε ἔστιν ἀγχιστεὺς ἐγγίων ὑπὲρ ἐμέ </p>
 <p>13 αὐλίσθητι τὴν νύκτα καὶ ἔσται τὸ πρωί ἐὰν ἀγχιστεύσῃ σε ἀγαθόν ἀγχιστευέτω ἐὰν δὲ μὴ βούληται ἀγχιστεῦσαί σε ἀγχιστεύσω σε ἐγώ ζῇ κύριος κοιμήθητι ἕως πρωί </p>
 <p>14 καὶ ἐκοιμήθη πρὸς ποδῶν αὐτοῦ ἕως πρωί ἡ δὲ ἀνέστη πρὸ τοῦ ἐπιγνῶναι ἄνδρα τὸν πλησίον αὐτοῦ καὶ εἶπεν Βοος μὴ γνωσθήτω ὅτι ἦλθεν γυνὴ εἰς τὸν ἅλωνα </p>
 <p>15 καὶ εἶπεν αὐτῇ φέρε τὸ περίζωμα τὸ ἐπάνω σου καὶ ἐκράτησεν αὐτό καὶ ἐμέτρησεν ἓξ κριθῶν καὶ ἐπέθηκεν ἐπ᾽ αὐτήν καὶ εἰσῆλθεν εἰς τὴν πόλιν </p>
 <p>16 καὶ Ρουθ εἰσῆλθεν πρὸς τὴν πενθερὰν αὐτῆς ἡ δὲ εἶπεν τίς εἶ θύγατερ καὶ εἶπεν αὐτῇ πάντα ὅσα ἐποίησεν αὐτῇ ὁ ἀνήρ </p>
 <p>17 καὶ εἶπεν αὐτῇ τὰ ἓξ τῶν κριθῶν ταῦτα ἔδωκέν μοι ὅτι εἶπεν πρός με μὴ εἰσέλθῃς κενὴ πρὸς τὴν πενθεράν σου </p>
 <p>18 ἡ δὲ εἶπεν κάθου θύγατερ ἕως τοῦ ἐπιγνῶναί σε πῶς οὐ πεσεῖται ῥῆμα οὐ γὰρ μὴ ἡσυχάσῃ ὁ ἀνήρ ἕως ἂν τελέσῃ τὸ ῥῆμα σήμερον </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/rut003.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Postquam autem reversa est ad socrum suam, audivit ab ea: Filia mea, quæram tibi requiem, et providebo ut bene sit tibi.</p>
 <p>2 Booz iste, cujus puellis in agro juncta es, propinquus noster est, et hac nocte aream hordei ventilat.</p>
 <p>3 Lavare igitur, et ungere, et induere cultioribus vestimentis, et descende in aream: non te videat homo, donec esum potumque finierit.</p>
 <p>4 Quando autem ierit ad dormiendum, nota locum in quo dormiat: veniesque et discooperies pallium, quo operitur a parte pedum, et projicies te, et ibi jacebis: ipse autem dicet quid agere debeas.</p>
 <p>5 Quæ respondit: Quidquid præceperis, faciam.</p>
 <p>6 Descenditque in aream, et fecit omnia quæ sibi imperaverat socrus.<br>
 </p>
 <p>7 Cumque comedisset Booz, et bibisset, et factus esset hilarior, issetque ad dormiendum juxta acervum manipulorum, venit abscondite, et discooperto pallio, a pedibus ejus se projecit.</p>
 <p>8 Et ecce, nocte jam media expavit homo, et conturbatus est: viditque mulierem jacentem ad pedes suos,</p>
 <p>9 et ait illi: Quæ es? Illaque respondit: Ego sum Ruth ancilla tua: expande pallium tuum super famulam tuam, quia propinquus es.</p>
 <p>10 Et ille: Benedicta, inquit, es a Domino, filia, et priorem misericordiam posteriore superasti: quia non est secuta juvenes, pauperes sive divites.</p>
 <p>11 Noli ergo metuere, sed quidquid dixeris mihi, faciam tibi. Scit enim omnis populus, qui habitat intra portas urbis meæ, mulierem te esse virtutis.</p>
 <p>12 Nec abnuo me propinquum, sed est alius me propinquior.</p>
 <p>13 Quiesce hac nocte: et facto mane, si te voluerit propinquitatis jure retinere, bene res acta est: sin autem ille noluerit, ego te absque ulla dubitatione suscipiam, vivit Dominus. Dormi usque mane.</p>
 <p>14 Dormivit itaque ad pedes ejus, usque ad noctis abscessum. Surrexit itaque antequam homines se cognoscerent mutuo, et dixit Booz: Cave ne quis noverit quod huc veneris.</p>
 <p>15 Et rursum: Expande, inquit, pallium tuum, quo operiris, et tene utraque manu. Qua extendente, et tenente, mensus est sex modios hordei, et posuit super eam. Quæ portans ingressa est civitatem,</p>
 <p>16 et venit ad socrum suam. Quæ dixit ei: Quid egisti, filia? Narravitque ei omnia, quæ sibi fecisset homo.</p>
 <p>17 Et ait: Ecce sex modios hordei dedit mihi, et ait: Nolo vacuam te reverti ad socrum tuam.</p>
 <p>18 Dixitque Noëmi: Expecta, filia, donec videamus quem res exitum habeat: neque enim cessabit homo, nisi compleverit quod locutus est.</p>
