 <p>2  For there were<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> We,<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> our sons,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and our daughters,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> are many:<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> therefore we take up<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> corn<sup><a href="h1715.htm">1715</a></sup> for them, that we may eat,<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> and live.<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Some also there were<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> We<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> have mortgaged<sup><a href="h6148.htm">6148</a></sup> our lands,<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> vineyards,<sup><a href="h3754.htm">3754</a></sup> and houses,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> that we might buy<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> corn,<sup><a href="h1715.htm">1715</a></sup> because of the dearth.<sup><a href="h7458.htm">7458</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  There were<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> also that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> We have borrowed<sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> money<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> for the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> tribute,<sup><a href="h4060.htm">4060</a></sup> and that upon our lands<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> and vineyards.<sup><a href="h3754.htm">3754</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Yet now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> our flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> is as the flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> of our brethren,<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> our children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> as their children:<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and, lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> bring into bondage<sup><a href="h3533.htm">3533</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> our sons<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and our daughters<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> to be servants,<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> and some of our daughters<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> are<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> brought unto bondage<sup><a href="h3533.htm">3533</a></sup> already: neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> is it in our power<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> to redeem them; for other<sup><a href="h0312.htm">312</a></sup> men have our lands<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> and vineyards.<sup><a href="h3754.htm">3754</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  And I was very<sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> angry<sup><a href="h2734.htm">2734</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> their cry<sup><a href="h2201.htm">2201</a></sup> and these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> words.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Then I consulted<sup><a href="h4427.htm">4427</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> myself,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> and I rebuked<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the nobles,<sup><a href="h2715.htm">2715</a></sup> and the rulers,<sup><a href="h5461.htm">5461</a></sup> and said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto them, Ye<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> exact<sup><a href="h5378.htm">5378</a></sup> usury,<sup><a href="h4855.htm">4855</a></sup> every one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of his brother.<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> And I set<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> a great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> assembly<sup><a href="h6952.htm">6952</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>8  And I said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto them, We<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> after our ability<sup><a href="h1767.htm">1767</a></sup> have redeemed<sup><a href="h7069.htm">7069</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> our brethren<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> the Jews,<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> which were sold<sup><a href="h4376.htm">4376</a></sup> unto the heathen;<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> and will ye<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> even<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> sell<sup><a href="h4376.htm">4376</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> your brethren?<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> or shall they be sold<sup><a href="h4376.htm">4376</a></sup> unto us?  <p>9  Also I said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> It is not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> ye<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> do:<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> ought ye not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> to walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> in the fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of our God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> because of the reproach<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> of the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> our enemies?<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> likewise,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> and my brethren,<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> and my servants,<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> might exact<sup><a href="h5383.htm">5383</a></sup> of them money<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> and corn:<sup><a href="h1715.htm">1715</a></sup> I pray<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> you, let us leave off<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> usury.<sup><a href="h4855.htm">4855</a></sup> </p>

 <p>12  Then said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> they, We will restore<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> them, and will require<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> nothing<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> them; so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> will we do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> sayest.<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Then I called<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the priests,<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> and took an oath<sup><a href="h7650.htm">7650</a></sup> of them, that they should do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> according to this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> promise.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>

 <p>14  Moreover<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> from the time<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I was appointed<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> to be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> their governor<sup><a href="h6346.htm">6346</a></sup> in the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of Judah,<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> from the twentieth<sup><a href="h6242.htm">6242</a></sup> year<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> even unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> and thirtieth<sup><a href="h7970.htm">7970</a></sup> year<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> of Artaxerxes<sup><a href="h0783.htm">783</a></sup> the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> that is, twelve<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6240.htm">6240</a></sup> years,<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> and my brethren<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> have not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> eaten<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> the bread<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> of the governor.<sup><a href="h6346.htm">6346</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/neh005.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Et factus est clamor populi et uxorum ejus magnus adversus fratres suos Judæos.</p>
 <p>2 Et erant qui dicerent: Filii nostri et filiæ nostræ multæ sunt nimis: accipiamus pro pretio eorum frumentum, et comedamus, et vivamus.</p>
 <p>3 Et erant qui dicerent: Agros nostros, et vineas, et domus nostras opponamus, et accipiamus frumentum in fame.</p>
 <p>4 Et alii dicebant: Mutuo sumamus pecunias in tributa regis, demusque agros nostros et vineas:</p>
 <p>5 et nunc sicut carnes fratrum nostrorum, sic carnes nostræ sunt: et sicut filii eorum, ita et filii nostri: ecce nos subjugamus filios nostros et filias nostras in servitutem, et de filiabus nostris sunt famulæ, nec habemus unde possint redimi: et agros nostros et vineas nostras alii possident.</p>
 <p>6 Et iratus sum nimis cum audissem clamorem eorum secundum verba hæc:</p>
 <p>7 cogitavitque cor meum mecum, et increpavi optimates et magistratus, et dixi eis: Usurasne singuli a fratribus vestris exigitis? Et congregavi adversum eos concionem magnam,</p>
 <p>8 et dixi eis: Nos, ut scitis, redemimus fratres nostros Judæos, qui venditi fuerant gentibus secundum possibilitatem nostram: et vos igitur vendetis fratres vestros, et redimemus eos? Et siluerunt, nec invenerunt quid responderent.</p>
 <p>9 Dixique ad eos: Non est bona res quam facitis: quare non in timore Dei nostri ambulastis, ne exprobretur nobis a gentibus inimicis nostris?</p>
 <p>10 Et ego, et fratres mei, et pueri mei commodavimus plurimis pecuniam et frumentum. Non repetamus in commune istud: æs alienum concedamus quod debetur nobis.</p>
 <p>11 Reddite eis hodie agros suos, et vineas suas, et oliveta sua, et domos suas: quin potius et centesimum pecuniæ, frumenti, vini et olei, quam exigere soletis ab eis, date pro illis.</p>
 <p>12 Et dixerunt: Reddemus, et ab eis nihil quæremus: sicque faciemus ut loqueris. Et vocavi sacerdotes, et adjuravi eos ut facerent juxta quod dixeram.</p>
 <p>13 Insuper excussi sinum meum, et dixi: Sic excutiat Deus omnem virum qui non compleverit verbum istud, de domo sua, et de laboribus suis: sic excutiatur, et vacuus fiat. Et dixit universa multitudo: Amen: et laudaverunt Deum. Fecit ergo populus sicut erat dictum.<br>
 </p>
 <p>14 A die autem illa, qua præceperat rex mihi ut essem dux in terra Juda, ab anno vigesimo usque ad annum trigesimum secundum Artaxerxis regis per annos duodecim, ego et fratres mei annonas quæ ducibus debebantur non comedimus.</p>
 <p>15 Duces autem primi, qui fuerant ante me, gravaverunt populum, et acceperunt ab eis in pane, et vino, et pecunia, quotidie siclos quadraginta: sed et ministri eorum depresserunt populum. Ego autem non feci ita propter timorem Dei:</p>
 <p>16 quin potius in opere muri ædificavi, et agrum non emi, et omnes pueri mei congregati ad opus erant.</p>
 <p>17 Judæi quoque et magistratus centum quinquaginta viri, et qui veniebant ad nos de gentibus quæ in circuitu nostro sunt, in mensa mea erant.</p>
 <p>18 Parabatur autem mihi per dies singulos bos unus, arietes sex electi, exceptis volatilibus, et inter dies decem vina diversa, et alia multa tribuebam: insuper et annonas ducatus mei non quæsivi: valde enim attenuatus erat populus.</p>
 <p>19 Memento mei, Deus meus, in bonum, secundum omnia quæ feci populo huic.</p>
