  <h3 align="center">Young's Literal Translation: Numbers</h3> 
 <p><a name="an_1:1">1:1</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai,  in the tent of meeting, on the first of the second month, in  the second year of their going out of the land of Egypt,  saying:</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:2">1:2</a> `Take ye up the sum of all the company of the sons of Israel  by their families, by the house of their fathers, in the number  of names -- every male by their polls;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:3">1:3</a> from a son of twenty years and upward, every one going out  to the host in Israel, ye do number them by their hosts, thou  and Aaron;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:4">1:4</a> and with you there is a man for a tribe, each is a head to  the house of his fathers.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:5">1:5</a> `And these [are] the names of the men who stand with you:  `For Reuben -- Elizur son of Shedeur.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:6">1:6</a> `For Simeon -- Shelumiel son of Zurishaddai.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:7">1:7</a> `For Judah -- Nahshon son of Amminadab.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:8">1:8</a> `For Issachar -- Nathaneel son of Zuar.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:9">1:9</a> `For Zebulun -- Eliab son of Helon.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:10">1:10</a> `For the sons of Joseph -- for Ephraim: Elishama son of  Ammihud: for Manasseh -- Gamaliel son of Pedahzur.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:11">1:11</a> `For Benjamin -- Abidan son of Gideoni.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:12">1:12</a> `For Dan -- Ahiezer son of Ammishaddai.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:13">1:13</a> `For Asher -- Pagiel son of Ocran.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:14">1:14</a> `For Gad -- Eliasaph son of Deuel.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:15">1:15</a> `For Naphtali -- Ahira son of Enan.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:16">1:16</a> These [are] those called of the company, princes of the  tribes of their fathers; they [are] heads of the thousands of  Israel.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:17">1:17</a> And Moses taketh -- Aaron also -- these men, who were  defined by name,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:18">1:18</a> and all the company they assembled on the first of the  second month, and they declare their births, by their families,  by the house of their fathers, in the number of names from a  son of twenty years and upward, by their polls,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:19">1:19</a> as Jehovah hath commanded Moses; and he numbereth them in  the wilderness of Sinai.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:20">1:20</a> And the sons of Reuben, Israel's first-born -- their  births, by their families, by the house of their fathers, in  the number of names, by their polls, every male from a son of  twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:21">1:21</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Reuben, are six and  forty thousand and five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:22">1:22</a> Of the sons of Simeon -- their births, by their families,  by the house of their fathers, its numbered ones in the number  of names, by their polls, every male from a son of twenty years  and upward, every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:23">1:23</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Simeon, [are] nine  and fifty thousand and three hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:24">1:24</a> Of the sons of Gad -- their births, by their families, by  the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a son  of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:25">1:25</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Gad, [are] five and  forty thousand and six hundred and fifty.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:26">1:26</a> Of the sons of Judah -- their births, by their families, by  the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a son  of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:27">1:27</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Judah, [are] four and  seventy thousand and six hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:28">1:28</a> Of the sons of Issachar -- their births, by their families,  by the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a  son of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host  --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:29">1:29</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Issachar, [are] four  and fifty thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:30">1:30</a> Of the sons of Zebulun -- their births, by their families,  by the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a  son of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host  --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:31">1:31</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Zebulun, [are] seven  and fifty thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:32">1:32</a> Of the sons of Joseph -- of the sons of Ephraim -- their  births, by their families, by the house of their fathers, in  the number of names, from a son of twenty years and upward,  every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:33">1:33</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Ephraim, [are] forty  thousand and five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:34">1:34</a> Of the sons of Manasseh -- their births, by their families,  by the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a  son of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host  --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:35">1:35</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Manasseh, [are] two  and thirty thousand and two hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:36">1:36</a> Of the sons of Benjamin -- their births, by their families,  by the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a  son of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host  --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:37">1:37</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Benjamin, [are] five  and thirty thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:38">1:38</a> Of the sons of Dan -- their births, by their families, by  the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a son  of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:39">1:39</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Dan, [are] two and  sixty thousand and seven hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:40">1:40</a> Of the sons of Asher -- their births, by their families, by  the house of their fathers, in the number of names, from a son  of twenty years and upward, every one going out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:41">1:41</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Asher, [are] one and  forty thousand and five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:42">1:42</a> [Of] the sons of Naphtali -- their births, by their  families, by the house of their fathers, in the number of  names, from a son of twenty years and upward, every one going  out to the host --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:43">1:43</a> their numbered ones, for the tribe of Naphtali, [are] three  and fifty thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:44">1:44</a> These [are] those numbered, whom Moses numbered -- Aaron  also, and the princes of Israel, twelve men -- each for the  house of his fathers, they have been.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:45">1:45</a> And they are, all those numbered of the sons of Israel, by  the house of their fathers, from a son of twenty years and  upward, every one going out to the host in Israel,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:46">1:46</a> yea, all those numbered are six hundred thousand, and three  thousand, and five hundred and fifty.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:47">1:47</a> And the Levites, for the tribe of their fathers, have not  numbered themselves in their midst,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:48">1:48</a> seeing Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:49">1:49</a> `Only, the tribe of Levi thou dost not number, and their  sum thou dost not take up in the midst of the sons of Israel;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:50">1:50</a> and thou, appoint the Levites over the tabernacle of the  testimony, and over all its vessels, and over all that it hath;  they bear the tabernacle, and all its vessels, and they serve  it; and round about the tabernacle they encamp.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:51">1:51</a> `And in the journeying of the tabernacle, the Levites take  it down, and in the encamping of the tabernacle, the Levites  raise it up; and the stranger who is coming near is put to  death.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:52">1:52</a> And the sons of Israel have encamped, each by his camp, and  each by his standard, by their hosts;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:53">1:53</a> and the Levites encamp round about the tabernacle of the  testimony; and there is no wrath on the company of the sons of  Israel, and the Levites have kept the charge of the tabernacle  of the testimony.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_1:54">1:54</a> And the sons of Israel do according to all that Jehovah  hath commanded Moses; so they have done.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:1">2:1</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, and unto Aaron, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:2">2:2</a> `Each by his standard, with ensigns of the house of their  fathers, do the sons of Israel encamp; over-against round about  the tent of meeting they encamp.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:3">2:3</a> And those encamping eastward towards the sun-rising, [are  of] the standard of the camp of Judah, by their hosts; and the  prince of the sons of Judah [is] Nahshon, son of Amminadab;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:4">2:4</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] four and  seventy thousand and six hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:5">2:5</a> And those encamping by him [are of] the tribe of Issachar;  and the prince of the sons of Issachar [is] Nethaneel son of  Zuar;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:6">2:6</a> and his host, and its numbered ones, [are] four and fifty  thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:7">2:7</a> The tribe of Zebulun; and the prince of the sons of Zebulun  [is] Eliab son of Helon;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:8">2:8</a> and his host, and its numbered ones, [are] seven and fifty  thousand and four hundred;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:9">2:9</a> all those numbered of the camp of Judah [are] a hundred  thousand, and eighty thousand, and six thousand, and four  hundred, by their hosts; they journey first.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:10">2:10</a> The standard of the camp of Reuben [is] southward, by their  hosts; and the prince of the sons of Reuben [is] Elizur son of  Shedeur;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:11">2:11</a> and his host, and its numbered ones, [are] six and forty  thousand and five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:12">2:12</a> And those encamping by him [are of] the tribe of Simeon;  and the prince of the sons of Simeon [is] Shelumiel son of  Zurishaddai;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:13">2:13</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] nine and fifty  thousand and three hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:14">2:14</a> And the tribe of Gad; and the prince of the sons of Gad  [is] Eliasaph son of Reuel;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:15">2:15</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] five and forty  thousand and six hundred and fifty.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:16">2:16</a> All those numbered of the camp of Reuben [are] a hundred  thousand, and one and fifty thousand, and four hundred and  fifty, by their hosts; and they journey second.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:17">2:17</a> And the tent of meeting -- the camp of the Levites -- hath  journeyed in the midst of the camps; as they encamp so they  journey, each at his station by their standards.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:18">2:18</a> The standard of the camp of Ephraim, by their hosts, [is]  westward; and the prince of the sons of Ephraim [is] Elishama  son of Ammihud;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:19">2:19</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] forty thousand  and five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:20">2:20</a> And by him [is] the tribe of Manasseh; and the prince of  the sons of Manasseh [is] Gamaliel son of Pedahzur;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:21">2:21</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] two and thirty  thousand, and two hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:22">2:22</a> And the tribe of Benjamin; and the prince of the sons of  Benjamin [is] Abidan son of Gideoni;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:23">2:23</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] five and  thirty thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:24">2:24</a> All those numbered of the camp of Ephraim [are] a hundred  thousand, and eight thousand, and a hundred, by their hosts;  and they journey third.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:25">2:25</a> The standard of the camp of Dan [is] northward, by their  hosts; and the prince of the sons of Dan [is] Ahiezer son of  Ammishaddai;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:26">2:26</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] two and sixty  thousand and seven hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:27">2:27</a> And those encamping by him [are of] the tribe of Asher; and  the prince of the sons of Asher [is] Pagiel son of Ocran;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:28">2:28</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] one and forty  thousand and five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:29">2:29</a> And the tribe of Naphtali; and the prince of the sons of  Naphtali [is] Ahira son of Enan;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:30">2:30</a> and his host, and their numbered ones, [are] three and  fifty thousand and four hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:31">2:31</a> All those numbered of the camp of Dan [are] a hundred  thousand, and seven and fifty thousand, and six hundred; at the  rear they journey, by their standards.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:32">2:32</a> These [are] those numbered of the sons of Israel by the  house of their fathers; all those numbered of the camps by  their hosts [are] six hundred thousand, and three thousand, and  five hundred and fifty.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:33">2:33</a> And the Levites have not numbered themselves in the midst  of the sons of Israel, as Jehovah hath commanded Moses.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_2:34">2:34</a> And the sons of Israel do according to all that Jehovah  hath commanded Moses; so they have encamped by their standards,  and so they have journeyed; each by his families, by the house  of his fathers.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:1">3:1</a> And these [are] births of Aaron and Moses, in the day of  Jehovah's speaking with Moses in mount Sinai.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:2">3:2</a> And these [are] the names of the sons of Aaron: the  first-born Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:3">3:3</a> these [are] the names of the sons of Aaron, the anointed  priests, whose hand he hath consecrated for acting as priest.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:4">3:4</a> And Nadab dieth -- Abihu also -- before Jehovah, in their  bringing near strange fire before Jehovah, in the wilderness  of Sinai, and sons they had not; and Eleazar -- Ithamar also --  acteth as priest in the presence of Aaron their father.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:5">3:5</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:6">3:6</a> `Bring near the tribe of Levi, and thou hast caused it to  stand before Aaron the priest, and they have served him,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:7">3:7</a> and kept his charge, and the charge of all the company  before the tent of meeting, to do the service of the  tabernacle;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:8">3:8</a> and they have kept all the vessels of the tent of meeting,  and the charge of the sons of Israel, to do the service of the  tabernacle;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:9">3:9</a> and thou hast given the Levites to Aaron and to his sons;  they are surely given to him out of the sons of Israel.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:10">3:10</a> `And Aaron and his sons thou dost appoint, and they have  kept their priesthood, and the stranger who cometh near is put  to death.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:11">3:11</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:12">3:12</a> `And I, lo, I have taken the Levites from the midst of the  sons of Israel instead of every first-born opening a womb among  the sons of Israel, and the Levites have been Mine;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:13">3:13</a> for Mine [is] every first-born, in the day of My smiting  every first-born in the land of Egypt I have sanctified to  Myself every first-born in Israel, from man unto beast; Mine  they are; I [am] Jehovah.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:14">3:14</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai,  saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:15">3:15</a> `Number the sons of Levi by the house of their fathers, by  their families; every male from a son of a month and upward  thou dost number them.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:16">3:16</a> And Moses numbereth them according to the command of  Jehovah, as he hath been commanded.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:17">3:17</a> And these are sons of Levi by their names: Gershon, and  Kohath, and Merari.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:18">3:18</a> And these [are] the names of the sons of Gershon by their  families: Libni and Shimei.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:19">3:19</a> And the sons of Kohath, by their families, [are] Amram and  Izhar, Hebron and Uzziel.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:20">3:20</a> And the sons of Merari by their families [are] Mahli and  Mushi; these are the families of the Levites, by the house of  their fathers.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:21">3:21</a> Of Gershon [is] the family of the Libnite, and the family  of the Shimite; these are the families of the Gershonite.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:22">3:22</a> Their numbered ones, in number, every male from a son of a  month and upward, their numbered ones [are] seven thousand and  five hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:23">3:23</a> The families of the Gershonite, behind the tabernacle, do  encamp westward.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:24">3:24</a> And the prince of a father's house for the Gershonite [is]  Eliasaph son of Lael.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:25">3:25</a> And the charge of the sons of Gershon in the tent of  meeting [is] the tabernacle, and the tent, its covering, and  the vail at the opening of the tent of meeting,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:26">3:26</a> and the hangings of the court, and the vail at the opening  of the court, which [is] by the tabernacle and by the altar  round about, and its cords, to all its service.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:27">3:27</a> And of Kohath [is] the family of the Amramite, and the  family of the Izharite, and the family of the Hebronite, and  the family of the Uzzielite; these are families of the  Kohathite.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:28">3:28</a> In number, all the males, from a son of a month and upward,  [are] eight thousand and six hundred, keeping the charge of the  sanctuary.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:29">3:29</a> The families of the sons of Kohath encamp by the side of  the tabernacle southward.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:30">3:30</a> And the prince of a father's house for the families of the  Kohathite [is] Elizaphan son of Uzziel.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:31">3:31</a> And their charge [is] the ark, and the table, and the  candlestick, and the altars, and the vessels of the sanctuary  with which they serve, and the vail, and all its service.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:32">3:32</a> And [to] the prince of the princes of the Levites, Eleazar  son of Aaron the priest, [is] the oversight of the keepers of  the charge of the sanctuary.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:33">3:33</a> Of Merari [is] the family of the Mahlite, and the family of  the Mushite; these [are] the families of Merari.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:34">3:34</a> And their numbered ones, in number, all the males from a  son of a month and upward, [are] six thousand and two hundred.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:35">3:35</a> And the prince of a father's house for the families of  Merari [is] Zuriel son of Abihail; by the side of the  tabernacle they encamp northward.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:36">3:36</a> And the oversight -- the charge of the sons of Merari --  [is] the boards of the tabernacle, and its bars, and its  pillars, and its sockets, and all its vessels, and all its  service,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:37">3:37</a> and the pillars of the court round about, and their  sockets, and their pins, and their cords.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:38">3:38</a> And those encamping before the tabernacle eastward, before  the tent of meeting, at the east, [are] Moses and Aaron, and  his sons, keeping the charge of the sanctuary for the charge of  the sons of Israel, and the stranger who cometh near is put to  death.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:39">3:39</a> All those numbered of the Levites whom Moses numbered --  Aaron also -- by the command of Jehovah, by their families,  every male from a son of a month and upward, [are] two and  twenty thousand.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:40">3:40</a> And Jehovah saith unto Moses, `Number every first-born male  of the sons of Israel from a son of a month and upward, and  take up the number of their names;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:41">3:41</a> and thou hast taken the Levites for Me (I [am] Jehovah),  instead of every first-born among the sons of Israel, and the  cattle of the Levites instead of every firstling among the  cattle of the sons of Israel.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:42">3:42</a> And Moses numbereth, as Jehovah hath commanded him, all the  first-born among the sons of Israel.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:43">3:43</a> And all the first-born -- male -- by the number of names,  from a son of a month and upward, of their numbered ones, are  two and twenty thousand two hundred and seventy and three.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:44">3:44</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:45">3:45</a> `Take the Levites instead of every first-born among the  sons of Israel, and the cattle of the Levites instead of their  cattle; and the Levites have been Mine; I [am] Jehovah.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:46">3:46</a> `And [from] those ransomed of the two hundred and seventy  and three (who are more than the Levites) of the first-born of  the sons of Israel,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:47">3:47</a> thou hast even taken five shekels a-piece by the poll -- by  the shekel of the sanctuary thou takest; twenty gerahs the  shekel [is];</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:48">3:48</a> and thou hast given the money to Aaron, and to his sons,  whereby those over and above are ransomed.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:49">3:49</a> And Moses taketh the ransom money from those over and above  those ransomed by the Levites;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:50">3:50</a> from the first-born of the sons of Israel he hath taken the  money, a thousand and three hundred and sixty and five -- by  the shekel of the sanctuary;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_3:51">3:51</a> and Moses giveth the money of those ransomed to Aaron, and  to his sons, according to the command of Jehovah, as Jehovah  hath commanded Moses.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_4:1">4:1</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, and unto Aaron, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_4:2">4:2</a> `Take up the sum of the sons of Kohath from the midst of the  sons of Levi, by their families, by the house of their fathers;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_4:3">4:3</a> from a son of thirty years and upward, even till a son of  fifty years, every one going in to the host, to do work in the  tent of meeting.</p>

 <p><a name="an_33:53">33:53</a> and ye have possessed the land, and dwelt in it, for to  you I have given the land -- to possess it.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_33:54">33:54</a> `And ye have inherited the land by lot, by your families;  to the many ye increase their inheritance, and to the few ye  diminish their inheritance; whither the lot goeth out to him,  it is his; by the tribes of your fathers ye inherit.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_33:55">33:55</a> `And if ye do not dispossess the inhabitants of the land  from before you, then it hath been, those whom ye let remain of  them, [are] for pricks in your eyes, and for thorns in your  sides, and they have distressed you on the land in which ye are  dwelling,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_33:56">33:56</a> and it hath come to pass, as I thought to do to them -- I  do to you.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:1">34:1</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:2">34:2</a> `Command the sons of Israel, and thou hast said unto them,  When ye are coming in unto the land of Canaan -- this [is] the  land which falleth to you by inheritance, the land of Canaan,  by its borders --</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:3">34:3</a> then hath the south quarter been to you from the wilderness  of Zin, by the sides of Edom, yea, the south border hath been  to you from the extremity of the Salt Sea, eastward;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:4">34:4</a> and the border hath turned round to you from the south to  the ascent of Akrabbim, and hath passed on to Zin, and its  outgoings have been from the south to Kadesh-Barnea, and it  hath gone out at Hazar-Addar, and hath passed on to Azmon;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:5">34:5</a> and the border hath turned round from Azmon to the brook of  Egypt, and its outgoings have been at the sea.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:6">34:6</a> `As to the west border, even the great sea hath been to you  a border; this is to you the west border.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:7">34:7</a> `And this is to you the north border: from the great sea ye  mark out for yourselves mount Hor;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:8">34:8</a> from mount Hor ye mark out to go in to Hamath, and the  outgoings of the border have been to Zedad;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:9">34:9</a> and the border hath gone out to Ziphron, and its outgoings  have been at Hazar-Enan; this is to you the north border.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:10">34:10</a> `And ye have marked out for yourselves for the border  eastward, from Hazar-Enan to Shepham;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:11">34:11</a> and the border hath gone down from Shepham to Riblah, on  the east of Ain, and the border hath gone down, and hath  smitten against the shoulder of the sea of Chinnereth eastward;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:12">34:12</a> and the border hath gone down to the Jordan, and its  outgoings have been at the Salt Sea; this is for you the land  by its borders round about.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:13">34:13</a> And Moses commandeth the sons of Israel, saying, `This  [is] the land which ye inherit by lot, which Jehovah hath  commanded to give to the nine tribes and the half of the tribe;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:14">34:14</a> for the tribe of the sons of Reuben have received, by the  house of their fathers; and the tribe of the children of Gad,  by the house of their fathers; and the half of the tribe of  Manasseh have received their inheritance;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:15">34:15</a> the two tribes and the half of the tribe have received  their inheritance beyond the Jordan, [near] Jericho, eastward,  at the [sun]-rising.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:16">34:16</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:17">34:17</a> `These [are] the names of the men who give to you the  inheritance of the land: Eleazar the priest, and Joshua son of  Nun,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:18">34:18</a> and one prince -- one prince -- for a tribe ye do take to  give the land by inheritance.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:19">34:19</a> `And these [are] the names of the men: of the tribe of  Judah, Caleb son of Jephunneh;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:20">34:20</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Simeon, Shemuel son of  Aminihud;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:21">34:21</a> of the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad son of Chislon;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:22">34:22</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Dan, the prince Bukki son  of Jogli;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:23">34:23</a> of the sons of Joseph, of the tribe of the sons of  Manasseh, the prince Hanniel son of Ephod;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:24">34:24</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Ephraim, the prince Kemuel  son of Shiphtan;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:25">34:25</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Zebulun, the prince  Elizaphan son of Parnach;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:26">34:26</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Issachar, the prince  Paltiel son of Azzan;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:27">34:27</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Asher, the prince Ahihud  son of Shelomi;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:28">34:28</a> and of the tribe of the sons of Naphtali, the prince  Pedahel son of Ammihud.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_34:29">34:29</a> These [are] those whom Jehovah hath commanded to give the  sons of Israel inheritance in the land of Canaan.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:1">35:1</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, in the plains of Moab, by  Jordan, [near] Jericho, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:2">35:2</a> `Command the sons of Israel, and they have given to the  Levites of the inheritance of their possession cities to  inhabit; also a suburb for the cities round about them ye do  give to the Levites.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:3">35:3</a> And the cities have been to them to inhabit, and their  suburbs are for their cattle, and for their goods, and for all  their beasts.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:4">35:4</a> `And the suburbs of the cities which ye give to the Levites  [are], from the wall of the city and without, a thousand cubits  round about.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:5">35:5</a> And ye have measured from the outside of the city, the east  quarter, two thousand by the cubit, and the south quarter, two  thousand by the cubit, and the west quarter, two thousand by  the cubit, and the north quarter, two thousand by the cubit;  and the city [is] in the midst; this is to them the suburbs of  the cities.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:6">35:6</a> `And the cities which ye give to the Levites [are] the six  cities of refuge, which ye give for the fleeing thither of the  man-slayer, and besides them ye give forty and two cities;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:7">35:7</a> all the cities which ye give to the Levites [are] forty and  eight cities, them and their suburbs.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:8">35:8</a> And the cities which ye give [are] of the possession of the  sons of Israel, from the many ye multiply, and from the few ye  diminish; each, according to his inheritance which they  inherit, doth give of his cities to the Levites.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:9">35:9</a> And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses, saying,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:10">35:10</a> `Speak unto the sons of Israel, and thou hast said unto  them, When ye are passing over the Jordan to the land of  Canaan,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:11">35:11</a> and have prepared to yourselves cities -- cities of refuge  they are to you -- then fled thither hath a man-slayer, smiting  a person unawares,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:12">35:12</a> and the cities have been to you for a refuge from the  redeemer, and the man-slayer doth not die till his standing  before the company for judgment.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:13">35:13</a> `As to the cities which ye give -- six [are] cities of  refuge to you;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:14">35:14</a> the three of the cities ye give beyond the Jordan, and the  three of the cities ye give in the land of Canaan; cities of  refuge they are.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:15">35:15</a> To sons of Israel, and to a sojourner, and to a settler in  their midst, are these six cities for a refuge, for the fleeing  thither of any one smiting a person unawares.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:16">35:16</a> `And if with an instrument of iron he hath smitten him,  and he dieth, he [is] a murderer: the murderer is certainly put  to death.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:17">35:17</a> `And if with a stone [in] the hand, wherewith he dieth, he  hath smitten him, and he dieth, he [is] a murderer: the  murderer is certainly put to death.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:18">35:18</a> `Or with a wooden instrument [in] the hand, wherewith he  dieth, he hath smitten him, and he dieth, he [is] a murderer:  the murderer is certainly put to death.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:19">35:19</a> `The redeemer of blood himself doth put the murderer to  death; in his coming against him he doth put him to death.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:20">35:20</a> `And if in hatred he thrust him through, or hath cast  [anything] at him by lying in wait, and he dieth;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:21">35:21</a> or in enmity he hath smitten him with his hand, and he  dieth; the smiter is certainly put to death; he [is] a  murderer; the redeemer of blood doth put the murderer to death  in his coming against him.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:22">35:22</a> `And if, in an instant, without enmity, he hath thrust him  through, or hath cast at him any instrument, without lying in  wait;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:23">35:23</a> or with any stone wherewith he dieth, without seeing, and  causeth [it] to fall upon him, and he dieth, and he [is] not  his enemy, nor seeking his evil;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:24">35:24</a> then have the company judged between the smiter and the  redeemer of blood, by these judgments.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:25">35:25</a> `And the company have delivered the man-slayer out of the  hand of the redeemer of blood, and the company have caused him  to turn back unto the city of his refuge, whither he hath fled,  and he hath dwelt in it till the death of the chief priest, who  hath been anointed with the holy oil.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:26">35:26</a> `And if the man-slayer at all go out [from] the border of  the city of his refuge whither he fleeth,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:27">35:27</a> and the redeemer of blood hath found him at the outside of  the border of the city of his refuge, and the redeemer of blood  hath slain the man-slayer, blood is not for him;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:28">35:28</a> for in the city of his refuge he doth dwell till the death  of the chief priest; and after the death of the chief priest  doth the man-slayer turn back unto the city of his possession.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:29">35:29</a> `And these things have been to you for a statute of  judgment to your generations, in all your dwellings:</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:30">35:30</a> whoso smiteth a person, by the mouth of witnesses doth  [one] slay the murderer; and one witness doth not testify  against a person -- to die.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:31">35:31</a> `And ye take no atonement for the life of a murderer who  [is] condemned -- to die, for he is certainly put to death;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:32">35:32</a> and ye take no atonement for him to flee unto the city of  his refuge, to turn back to dwell in the land, until the death  of the priest.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:33">35:33</a> `And ye profane not the land which ye [are] in, for blood  profaneth the land; as to the land, it is not pardoned for  blood which is shed in it except by the blood of him who  sheddeth it;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_35:34">35:34</a> and ye defile not the land in which ye are dwelling, in  the midst of which I do tabernacle, for I Jehovah do tabernacle  in the midst of the sons of Israel.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:1">36:1</a> And the heads of the fathers of the families of the sons of  Gilead, son of Machir, son of Manasseh, of the families of the  sons of Joseph, come near, and speak before Moses, and before  the princes, heads of the fathers of the sons of Israel,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:2">36:2</a> and say, Jehovah commanded my lord to give the land for  inheritance by lot to the sons of Israel, and my lord hath been  commanded by Jehovah to give the inheritance of Zelophehad our  brother to his daughters.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:3">36:3</a> `And -- they have been to one of the sons of the [other]  tribes of the sons of Israel for wives, and their inheritance  hath been withdrawn from the inheritance of our fathers, and  hath been added to the inheritance of the tribe which is  theirs, and from the lot of our inheritance it is withdrawn,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:4">36:4</a> and if it is the jubilee of the sons of Israel, then hath  their inheritance been added to the inheritance of the tribe  which is theirs, and from the inheritance of the tribe of our  fathers is their inheritance withdrawn.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:5">36:5</a> And Moses commandeth the sons of Israel, by the command of  Jehovah, saying, `Rightly are the tribe of the sons of Joseph  speaking;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:6">36:6</a> this [is] the thing which Jehovah hath commanded concerning  the daughters of Zelophehad, saying, To those good in their  eyes let them be for wives; only, to a family of the tribe of  their fathers let them be for wives;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:7">36:7</a> and the inheritance of the sons of Israel doth not turn  round from tribe unto tribe; for each to the inheritance of the  tribe of his fathers, do the sons of Israel cleave.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:8">36:8</a> `And every daughter possessing an inheritance, of the  tribes of the sons of Israel, is to one of the family of the  tribe of her father for a wife, so that the sons of Israel  possess each the inheritance of his fathers,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:9">36:9</a> and the inheritance doth not turn round from [one] tribe to  another tribe; for each to his inheritance do they cleave, the  tribes of the sons of Israel.'</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:10">36:10</a> As Jehovah hath commanded Moses, so have the daughters of  Zelophehad done,</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:11">36:11</a> and Mahlah, Tirzah, and Hoglah, and Milcah, and Noah,  daughters of Zelophehad, are to the sons of their fathers'  brethren for wives;</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:12">36:12</a> [to men] of the families of the sons of Manasseh, son of  Joseph, they have been for wives, and their inheritance is with  the tribe of the family of their father.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_36:13">36:13</a> These [are] the commands and the judgments which Jehovah  hath commanded, by the hand of Moses, concerning the sons of  Israel, in the plains of Moab, by Jordan, [near] Jericho. </p>
