 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#001">Psa 56:1</a>, David, praying to God in confidence of his word, complains of his enemies; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#009">Psa 56:9</a>, He professes his confidence in God's word, and promises to praise him.
 </p><p>rechokim.}} Or, as it may be rendered, "concerning the dumb dove (or oppressed band) in distant places," i.e., David, or his companions; though some consider it as the name of a tune, and others a musical instrument.
