 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#001">2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 23</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#001">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">sa2 23:1</a></font></span></p>Now these [be] the (a) last words of David. David the son of Jesse said, and the man [who was] raised up on high, the anointed of the God of Jacob, and the sweet psalmist of Israel, said, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Which he spoke after he had written the psalms. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#002">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">sa2 23:2</a></font></span></p>The Spirit of the LORD spake by me, and his word [was] in my (b) tongue. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Meaning, he spoke nothing but by the motion of God's Spirit. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#004">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">sa2 23:4</a></font></span></p>And [he shall be] as the light of the morning, [when] the sun riseth, [even] a morning without clouds; [as] the tender (c) grass [springing] out of the earth by clear shining after rain. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Which grows quickly, and fades soon. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#005">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">sa2 23:5</a></font></span></p>Although my house [be] not so with God; yet he hath made with me an everlasting covenant, ordered in all [things], and sure: for [this is] all my salvation, and all [my] desire, although he make [it] not to (d) grow. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) But that my kingdom may continue for ever according to his promise. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#008">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">sa2 23:8</a></font></span></p>These [be] the names of the mighty men whom David had: The Tachmonite that sat in the (e) seat, chief among the captains; the same [was] Adino the Eznite: [he lift up his spear] against eight hundred, whom he slew at one time. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) As one of the king's counsel. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#009">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">sa2 23:9</a></font></span></p>And after him [was] Eleazar the son of Dodo the Ahohite, [one] of the three mighty men with David, when they defied the Philistines [that] were there gathered together to battle, and the men of Israel were (f) gone away: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Meaning, fled from the battle. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#010">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">sa2 23:10</a></font></span></p>He arose, and smote the Philistines until his hand was weary, and his (g) hand clave unto the sword: and the LORD wrought a great victory that day; and the people returned after him only to spoil. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) By a cramp which came from weariness and fighting. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#012">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">sa2 23:12</a></font></span></p>But he stood in the midst of the ground, and defended it, and slew the Philistines: and the LORD wrought a (h) great victory. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Which has no respect for many or few, when he will show his power. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#015">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">sa2 23:15</a></font></span></p>And David (i) longed, and said, Oh that one would give me drink of the water of the well of Bethlehem, which [is] by the gate! 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Being overcome with weariness and thirst. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#016">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">sa2 23:16</a></font></span></p>And the three mighty men brake through the host of the Philistines, and drew water out of the well of Bethlehem, that [was] by the gate, and took [it], and brought [it] to David: nevertheless he would not drink thereof, but (k) poured it out unto the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Bridling his affection, and also desiring God not to be offended for that rash enterprise. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#021">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">sa2 23:21</a></font></span></p>And he slew an Egyptian, a goodly man: and the Egyptian had a (l) spear in his hand; but he went down to him with a staff, and plucked the spear out of the Egyptian's hand, and slew him with his own spear. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Which was as big as a weavers beam, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#023">Ch1 11:23</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#023">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">sa2 23:23</a></font></span></p>He was more honourable than the (m) thirty, but he attained not to the [first] three. And David set him over his guard. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) He was more valiant than the thirty that follow and not so valiant as the six before. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#027">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">sa2 23:27</a></font></span></p>Abiezer the Anethothite, (n) Mebunnai the Hushathite, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) Some of these had two names, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#029">Ch1 11:29</a>) and also many more are mentioned there. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#039">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 23:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">sa2 23:39</a></font></span></p>Uriah the Hittite: (o) thirty and seven in all. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) These came to David, and helped restore him to his kingdom.
