 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">PRAYER FOR RAIN</h3>  <h4 align="center">SIA</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">White floating clouds,<br> Clouds like the plains<br> Come and water the earth.<br> Sun embrace the earth<br> That she may be fruitful.<br> Moon, lion of the north,<br> Bear of the west,<br> Badger of the south,<br> Wolf of the east,</div>  
 <p><a name="page_63"><font size="1" color="green">p. 63</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Eagle of the heavens,<br> Shrew of the earth,<br> Elder war hero,<br> Warriors of the six mountains of the world,<br> Intercede with the cloud people for us,<br> That they may water the earth.<br> Medicine bowl, cloud bowl, and water vase<br> Give us your hearts,<br> That the earth may be watered.<br> I make the ancient road of meal,<br> That my song may pass over it—<br> The ancient road.<br> White shell bead woman<br> Who lives where the sun goes down,<br> Mother Whirlwind,<br> Father Sus’sistinnako,<br> Mother Ya’ya, creator of good thoughts.<br> Yellow woman of the north,<br> Blue woman of the west,<br> Red woman of the south,<br> White woman of the east,<br> Slightly yellow woman of the zenith,<br> And dark woman of the nadir,<br> I ask your intercession with the cloud people.</div>  <h3 align="center">RAIN SONG OF THE GIANT SOCIETY</h3>  <h4 align="center">SIA</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">We, the Ancient Ones,<br> Who ascended from the middle of the world below,<br> Our medicine is precious,<br> It is as our hearts precious to us,<br> Arrow of lightning<br> Come to us<br> Echo.<br> Spruce of the north</div>  
 <p><a name="page_64"><font size="1" color="green">p. 64</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">And all your people;<br> Your thoughts come to us.<br> Who is it?<br> White floating clouds.<br> May your thoughts come to us<br> And all your people,<br> May their thoughts come to us.<br> Who is it?<br> Clouds like the plains,<br> May your thoughts come to us.<br> Who is it?<br> Arrow of lightning,<br> May your thoughts come to us.<br> Who is it?<br> Earth horizon<br> And all your people,<br> May your thoughts come to us.</div>  <h3 align="center">INVOCATION TO THE U’WANNAMI</h3>  <h4 align="center">SIA</h4>  <h4 align="center">I</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come you, ascend the ladder; all come in; all sit down.<br> We were poor, poor, poor, poor, poor, poor,<br> When we came to this world through the poor place,<br> Where the body of water dried for our passing.<br> Banked up clouds cover the earth.<br> All come four times with your showers,<br> Descend to the base of the ladder and stand still;<br> Bring your showers and great rains.<br> All, all come, all ascend, all come in, all sit down.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I throw out to you my sacred meal that you may all. come.<br> Hold your gaming-stick; throw it forward; all come.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_65"><font size="1" color="green">p. 65</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">All come out and give us your showers and great rains; all come,<br> That the seeds may be strong and come up, that all seed plants may come up and be. strong.<br> Come you that all trees and seeds may come up and be strong.<br> Come you hither; all come.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Cover my earth mother four times with many flowers.<br> Let the heavens be covered with the banked up clouds.<br> Let the earth be covered with fog; cover the earth with rains.<br> Great waters, rains, cover the earth. Lightning cover the earth.<br> Let thunder be heard over the earth; let thunder be heard;<br> Let thunder be heard over the six regions of the earth.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Rain-makers, come out from all roads that great rivers may cover the earth;<br> That stones may be moved by the torrents;<br> That trees may be uprooted and moved by the torrents.<br> Great rain-makers, come out from all roads, carry the sands of our earth mother of the place.<br> Cover the earth with her heart, that all seeds may develop,<br> That my children may have all things to eat and be happy;<br> That the people of the outlying villages may all laugh and be happy;<br> That the growing children may all have things to eat and be happy.<br> This way our great father wishes you to come.<br> This way our great mother wishes you to come.<br> That we may have all kinds of seeds and all things good;<br> That we may inhale the sacred breath of life;<br> That our fathers and our mothers may bring us happy days.<br> Let our children live and be happy.<br> Send us the good south winds.<br> Send us your breath over the lakes that our great world may be made beautiful and our people may live.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_66"><font size="1" color="green">p. 66</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">There, far off, my Sun Father arises, ascends the ladder, comes forth from his place.<br> May all complete the road of life, may all grow old.<br> May the children inhale more of the sacred breath of life.<br> May all my children have corn that they may complete the road of life.<br> Here sit down; here remain; we give you our best thoughts.<br> Hasten over the meal road; we are jealous of you.<br> We inhale the sacred breath through our prayer plumes.</div>
