 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Science of Mind, by Ernest Shurtleff Holmes [1926], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HARMONY OF LIFE</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>BEAUTY</b></p>  
 <p>I behold the Beautiful and the Pleasant.<br> My eyes see only that which is beautiful to look upon.<br> I will not see anything else nor believe in anything else.<br> I know that beauty has entered into my life, and will always remain there.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I see only the beautiful</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>FRIENDSHIP OF THE SPIRIT AND OF MAN</b></p>  
 <p>The Friendship of the Spirit and of man is mine now and forever.<br> Even now I see the countless numbers of friends coming and going around me.<br> I enter into this friendship and this companionship with gladness and rejoicing.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I receive my friends</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_336"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 336</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>HE WILLS ME TO BE</b></p>  
 <p>It was God Himself who willed that I should be.<br> I have no cares nor burdens to bear.<br> I am reminded of the great command:<br> "Come unto Me all ye who labor and are heavy laden and I will give you rest."<br> I do come into the Light and I do know that all cares<br> And responsibilities fall from me as I let the<br> Light of Wisdom direct my ways.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>He wills me to be; therefore, I am</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I SERVE</b></p>  
 <p>I serve the world.<br> I wait upon the Lord within all men;<br> I call forth glory from On High through the minds of all people.<br> I obey the will of Him Who inhabits Eternity.<br> I do the works of Him Who dwelleth among the heavens.<br> My Lord within commands and I obey.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I do good to all people</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I SHALL NOT DOUBT NOR FEAR</b></p>  
 <p>I shall not doubt nor fear, for my salvation is from On High, and the day of its appearing is now at hand.<br> I shall not doubt nor fear, for my whole being responds to the realization of Life within and around me.<br> I shall not fear, for the Hosts of Heaven are waiting upon me and the Law of the Universe is my Salvation.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I shall not fear</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_337"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 337</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I WAS TOLD TO LIVE</b></p>  
 <p>I was told to live and to love, to laugh and to be glad.<br> I was told to be still and know of the One Almighty Power, in and through all.<br> I was told to let that Power work through and in me.<br> I believed that voice and I received my Good.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am healed--The joy of Life</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>LAW</b></p>  
 <p>I meditate upon the Law of God.<br> It is a Perfect Law and is now working for me and in and through me.<br> "The Law of the Lord is perfect."<br> I speak into that Law and it is done unto me.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Thy Law is in my heart</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>LOVE</b></p>  
 <p>The Love of the All Good is within me and through me.<br> That Love goes out to meet all who come into my atmosphere.<br> It radiates to all and flowing through all.<br> My Love within me is Perfect.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Thy Love within me is Perfect</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>LOVE DISSOLVES ALL FEAR</b></p>  
 <p>Greater than fear is Love.<br> Love dissolves all fear, casts out all doubt and sets the captive free.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_338"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 338</font></a></p>  
 <p>Love, like the River of Life, flows through me and refreshes me with its eternal blessings.<br> Love cannot be afraid; it is fearless and strong, and is mighty in its works.<br> It can accomplish all things through the Inner Light of that faith in the All Good,<br> Which fills my very Being with a Powerful Presence.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Love casts out all fear</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY AFFAIRS</b></p>  
 <p>My affairs are in the hands of Him<br> Who guides the planets in their course,<br> And Who causes the Sun to shine.<br> Divine Understanding attends me on the Way,<br> And I shall not be hindered in my work.<br> My affairs are controlled by Perfect Intelligence,<br> And cannot be hindered from expression.<br> I know that all that I do is done from the One Motive:<br> To express Life; and Life will be expressed<br> In and through me. I cannot hinder it.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am controlled by Intelligence</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY BUSINESS</b></p>  
 <p>My business is directed by Divine Intelligence.<br> The All-Knowing Mind knows what to do and how to do it.<br> I do not hinder, but let It operate in my affairs.<br> It prospers and directs me and controls my life.<br> My affairs are managed by Love, and directed by<br> Wisdom, and they cannot fail to prosper and expand.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My affairs are in His hands</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_339"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 339</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY PROFESSION</b></p>  
 <p>My profession is the Activity of the Great Mind working through me.<br> As such It is a Divine Activity and is constantly in touch with Reality.<br> I am inspired in my work from On High with lofty ideals,<br> And my thought is illumined by the All-Knowing One.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am inspired</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>NO DELAYS</b></p>  
 <p>There are no delays in the Divine Plan for me.<br> Nothing can hinder the operation of this Law unto my Life and Action.<br> Obstructions are removed from my path, and<br> I now enter into the realization and manifestation of complete fulfillment of right desires.<br> I do not have to wait, for the Law waits upon me at every turn in Life's road.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Now it is done unto me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>NO MISREPRESENTATIONS</b></p>  
 <p>No one can lie to me; none can mislead me.<br> I am free from the belief in all lies and untruths;<br> I know and speak only the Truth, and the Truth alone can be spoken to me.<br> I know the false and can understand the Real.<br> I cannot be fooled nor misled; I am guided by Truth alone.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>There is no lie nor liar</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><a name="page_340"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 340</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>NO OBSTRUCTIONS</b></p>  
 <p>There are no obstructions to Life's Path; no hinderance to man's endeavors.<br> Let my Word be the Law of elimination to all thought of hinderance or delay.<br> And let the thing that I speak come forth into manifestation at once.<br> I behold it and see that it is even now done, complete and perfect.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I receive now</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>NO OVER-ACTION NOR INACTION</b></p>  
 <p>There is no over-action nor inaction in Divine Law, for everything moves according to perfect harmony.<br> Every idea of my body functions in accordance with this Law of Perfect Life.<br> I now perceive that the action within me is perfect, complete and harmonious.<br> Peace be unto every part of me, and perfect Life to every member of my body.<br> I act in accordance with Divine Law.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am Perfect Life throughout my whole Being</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>ONE WITH PERFECT ACTION</b></p>  
 <p>I am One with Perfect Action. Everything that I do, say or think is quickened into action through this right understanding and this correct knowing.<br> The harmonious action of the Great Whole operates through me now and at all times.<br> I am carried along by this Right Action and am compelled to do the right thing at the right time.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_341"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 341</font></a></p>  
 <p>There is nothing in me that can hinder this action from flowing through me.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The action of God is the only action</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>PEACE, POISE AND POWER</b></p>  
 <p>Peace, Poise and Power are within me, for they are the witnesses of the Inner Spirit of all Truth, Love and Wisdom.<br> I am at peace within me, and all about responds to that Great Calm of the Inner Soul which knows its rightful place in the All Good.<br> Power is born from within me and passes into my experience without effort or labor.<br> I rest in Security and Peace, for the Inner Light shines forth and illumines the way.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I rest in Thee</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>STILLNESS AND RECEPTIVITY</b></p>  
 <p>I am still and receptive to Life.<br> I let Life flow through me into all that I do, say or think.<br> I shall let my Life be what it is, and shall not worry nor complain.<br> I am now entered into the Secret Place of the Soul where complete quiet reigns supreme and where God talks to me.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I receive</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THANKSGIVING AND PRAISE</b></p>  
 <p>I will give thanks to my Inner Life for all Its Marvelous Wonders, and for all Its Wonderful Works.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_342"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 342</font></a></p>  
 <p>I will sing and be glad, for I know that I am hid with Truth in a Perfect Life.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The fulness of Joy is mine</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE DIVINE PROMISES ARE KEPT</b></p>  
 <p>The Divine Promises are all kept; not one is changed.<br> God has told me that my Life is Perfect;<br> He will never desert or leave me to travel alone.<br> I know that from this day on and forevermore<br> I shall live under Divine Protection and Love.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I live under Divine Protection and Love.</i></p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE INNER LIGHT</b></p>  
 <p>The Light of Heaven shines through me and illumines my Path.<br> The Light Eternal is my guide and my protection.<br> In that Light there is no darkness at all.<br> It is a Perfect Light shining from the altar of a perfect Love.<br> O Light and Love within me, Thou art welcome.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Light shines through me and illumines the Way</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE NIGHT IS FILLED WITH PEACE</b></p>  
 <p>I wrap myself in the mantle of Love and fall asleep, filled with Peace.<br> Through the long night Peace remains with me, and at the breaking of the new day I shall still be filled with Life and Love.<br> I shall go forth into the new day confident and happy.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I rest in Thee</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_343"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 343</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SEAL OF APPROVAL</b></p>  
 <p>The Seal of Approval is upon me, and I am not condemned by the thought or act of man.<br> I will fear no evil, for I know that the Great Judge of all controls my every act.<br> Let every fear of man be removed from and let the Silence of my soul bear witness to the Truth.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>God approves of me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SECRET WAY</b></p>  
 <p>There is a Secret Way of the Soul which all may know.<br> It is the Way of Peace and Love.<br> This Secret Way leads into places of joy<br> And into the house of good.<br> It is the Way of the Spirit, and all may enter who will.<br> I tread the Secret Way of good, the Path of Peace,<br> And I enter into "The Secret Place of The Most High."</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Secret Place of The Most High is within me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SHINING PATH</b></p>  
 <p>The Pathway of Life shines before me unto the Perfect Day.<br> I walk the pathway of the Soul to the Gate of Good.<br> I enter into the fulfillment of my desires.<br> Nothing need be added and nothing can be taken from the All Good which is forever expressing Itself in me.<br> Daily shall I receive Its great blessings and my Soul shall rejoice forevermore.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am now entered into my good</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_344"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 344</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE THINGS I NEED COME TO ME</b></p>  
 <p>Whatever I need comes to me from the All Good.<br> Divine Intelligence working through me always knows just what I need and always supplies it when I need it.<br> This Law is unfailing and sure, and cannot be broken.<br> I receive my Good daily as I go along the pathway of Life, and I cannot be robbed of my birthright to freedom and happiness.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I receive my Good</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE WAY IS MADE CLEAR BEFORE ME</b></p>  
 <p>The Way is made clear before me; I do not falter nor fall.<br> The Way of the Spirit is my Way, and I am compelled to walk in it.<br> My feet are kept on the Path of Perfect Life.<br> The Way is prepared before me, and that Way is a Path of Peace, of Fulfillment and Joy.<br> The Way is bright with the light of Love and Kindness.<br> The Way I tread is a pleasant and a happy one.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I see the Way and I walk in It</i>.</p>
