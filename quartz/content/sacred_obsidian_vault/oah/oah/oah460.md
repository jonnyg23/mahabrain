 <h3 align="CENTER">ROTH.</h3>
 <p>1. PRAISE Jehovih, ye angels of heaven: In the countless places of the journeys of the world: Send forth a song to the Everlasting!</p>
 <p>2. Praise Jehovih, O Uklo and Gibrath! In the plains and High Arches <a name="page_656"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 656</font></a> of Etisia, in the etherean worlds: Sing loud and clear the song of Omaza.</p>
 <p>3. Praise Jehovih, ye angels of Wanwan: In the circuit of Hissa and the plains of Oat: Render unto the Creator, O Waukawauk!</p>
 <p>4. Praise Jehovih, ye angels of Howt: In the etherean heavens of Noe and Kaba-Se-o-nita: Sing praises, and shout unto the Almighty!</p>
 <p>5. Praise Jehovih, ye etherean hosts of Rax: In the heavenly Caverns of Wass and Bliathon: Sing in Hise and Nor to the Creator's praise.</p>
 <p>6. Praise Jehovih, ye angels of Mor: In the Seam of Wia-wis, in the labor of Pan: Sing a song of praise to the All One, Jehovih!</p>
 <p>7. Praise Jehovih, ye angels of Noe: In the arc of the journey of an uplifted world: Render Him praise for the labors of Aph, His Orian Son.</p>
