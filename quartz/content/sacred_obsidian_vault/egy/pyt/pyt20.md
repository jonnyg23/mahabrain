 <p align="center"><i>Utterance

 <p>665a. for thou art indeed the mysterious, the hidden, as the gods call thee,</p>
 
 <p>665b. because thou hast no legs, because thou hast no arms,</p>
 
 <p>665c. with which thou mayest go in the following of thy brothers, thy gods.</p>

 <p>666b. ye who make the <i>mti</i>-knot of
