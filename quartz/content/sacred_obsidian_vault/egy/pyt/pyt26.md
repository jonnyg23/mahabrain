 <h3 align="center">23. A SERIES OF FOOD TEXTS, UTTERANCES 487-502.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 487</i>.</p>
 
 <p>1046a. To say: O my father, Osiris N.,</p>
 
 <p>1046b. thou art spiritualized on the horizon, thou endurest in the <i><u>D</u>di.t</i>;</p>
 
 <p>1046c. thou commandest (with) words as he who is at the head of the living,
 eternally.</p>
 
 <p>1047a. Get (lit. stand up) from thy left side, put thyself on thy right
 side;</p>
 
 <p>1047b. take this thy bread, which I am giving thee; I am thy son and thine
 heir.</p>

 <p>1059a. To say: Greetings to you, who rule over abundance,</p>
 
 <p>1059b. who look after food, who reside as ruler of the green field,</p>
 
 <p>1059b + 1 (N. XIV 1055 + 47). near the lord of splendour.</p>
 
 <p>1059b + 2 (Nt. XXVII 701-702). Cause N. to eat of the corn which originates

 <p>1059b + 3 (N. 1055 + 48). like the equipment which was made in <i>Mḥt-wr.t</i></p>

 <p>1059c-1060a. Those who are attached to the offerings of the oldest gods--</p>
 
 <p>1060a-b. they introduce me to abundance, they introduce me to food,</p>

 separates <i>Wp-šn.wi</i> (the two tuffs

 <p>1061a (Nt. XXVII 704). and drop with my (or, his) anus like Śerḳet.</p>
 
 <p>1061a + 1 (Nt. 704). I give offerings and distribute food</p>
 
 <p>1061a + 2 (N. 1055 + 50). like him with the long wings who lives in the
 Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1061b. Wind is in my nose; seed is in my phallus,</p>
 
 <p>1061c. as (seed is in the phallus) of him of mysterious form, who lives in
 splendour.</p>
 
 <p>1061c + 1 (N. 1055 + 50. N. sees Nun,</p>

 six days' feast in <i><u>H</u>ri-ḥȝ</i>;</p>
 
 <p>1062. (Nt. 706). N. will eat of the pregnant cow like those who are in Heliopolis.</p>
