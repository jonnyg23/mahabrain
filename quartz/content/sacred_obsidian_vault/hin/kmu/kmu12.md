 <p>1. The Holy Mother.</p>  
 <p>2. The Great Queen.</p>  
 <p>3. The Ruler of the Highest Throne.</p>  
 <p>4. Thou art born from the Altar of Fire (dispeller of ignorance and darkness).</p>
