 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 14</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Walaw annan<u>a</u> nazzaln<u>a</u> ilayhimu almal<u>a</u>-ikata wakallamahumu almawt<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>asharn<u>a</u> AAalayhim kulla shay-in qubulan m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo liyu/minoo ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum yajhaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  Even if We did send<br> Unto them angels,<br> And the dead did speak<br> Unto them, and We gathered<br> Together all things before<br> Their very eyes, they are not<br> The ones to believe,<br> Unless it is in God's Plan.<br> But most of them<br> Ignore (the truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika jaAAaln<u>a</u> likulli nabiyyin AAaduwwan shay<u>at</u>eena al-insi wa<b>a</b>ljinni yoo<u>h</u>ee baAA<u>d</u>uhum il<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in zukhrufa alqawli ghurooran walaw sh<u>a</u>a rabbuka m<u>a</u> faAAaloohu fa<u>th</u>arhum wam<u>a</u> yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  Likewise did We make<br> For every Messenger<br> An enemy,—evil ones<br> Among men and Jinns,<br> Inspiring each other<br> With flowery discourses<br> By way of deception.<br> If thy Lord had so planned,<br> They would not have<br> Done it: so leave them<br> And their inventions alone.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Walita<u>s</u>gh<u>a</u> ilayhi af-idatu alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati waliyar<u>d</u>awhu waliyaqtarifoo m<u>a</u> hum muqtarifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  To such (deceit)<br> Let the hearts of those<br> Incline, who have no faith<br> In the Hereafter: let them<br> Delight in it, and let them<br> Earn from it what they may</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Afaghayra All<u>a</u>hi abtaghee <u>h</u>akaman wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee anzala ilaykumu alkit<u>a</u>ba mufa<u>ss</u>alan wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba yaAAlamoona annahu munazzalun min rabbika bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi fal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina almumtareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  Say: "Shall I seek<br> For judge other than God?—<br> When He it is<br> Who hath sent unto you<br> The Book, explained in detail."<br> They know full well,<br> To whom We have given<br> The Book, that it hath been<br> Sent down from thy Lord<br> In truth. Never be then<br> Of those who doubt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Watammat kalimatu rabbika <u>s</u>idqan waAAadlan l<u>a</u> mubaddila likalim<u>a</u>tihi wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  The Word of thy Lord<br> Doth find its fulfilment<br> In truth and in justice:<br> None can change His Words:<br> For He is the one Who<br> Heareth and knoweth all.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Wa-in tu<u>t</u>iAA akthara man fee al-ar<u>d</u>i yu<u>d</u>illooka AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi in yattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anna wa-in hum ill<u>a</u> yakhru<u>s</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  Wert thou to follow<br> The common run of those<br> On earth, they will lead<br> Thee away from the Way<br> Of God. They follow<br> Nothing but conjecture: they<br> Do nothing but lie.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Inna rabbaka huwa aAAlamu man ya<u>d</u>illu AAan sabeelihi wahuwa aAAlamu bi<b>a</b>lmuhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  Thy Lord knoweth best<br> Who strayeth from His Way:<br> He knoweth best<br> Who they are that receive<br> His guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Fakuloo mimm<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ukira ismu All<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi in kuntum bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  So eat of (meats)<br> On which God's name<br> Hath been pronounced,<br> If ye have faith<br> In His Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Wam<u>a</u> lakum all<u>a</u> ta/kuloo mimm<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ukira ismu All<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi waqad fa<u>ss</u>ala lakum m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama AAalaykum ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> i<u>dt</u>urirtum ilayhi wa-inna katheeran layu<u>d</u>illoona bi-ahw<u>a</u>-ihim bighayri AAilmin inna rabbaka huwa aAAlamu bi<b>a</b>lmuAAtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  Why should ye not<br> Eat of (meats) on which<br> God's name hath been<br> Pronounced, when He hath<br> Explained to you in detail<br> What is forbidden to you—<br>Except under compulsion<br> Of necessity?<br> But many do mislead (men)<br> By their appetites unchecked<br> By knowledge. Thy Lord<br> Knoweth best those who transgress</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Wa<u>th</u>aroo <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hira al-ithmi wab<u>at</u>inahu inna alla<u>th</u>eena yaksiboona al-ithma sayujzawna bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaqtarifoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>120.  Eschew all sin,<br> Open or secret:<br> Those who earn sin<br> Will get due recompense<br> For their "earnings."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Wal<u>a</u> ta/kuloo mimm<u>a</u> lam yu<u>th</u>kari ismu All<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi wa-innahu lafisqun wa-inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena layoo<u>h</u>oona il<u>a</u> awliy<u>a</u>-ihim liyuj<u>a</u>dilookum wa-in a<u>t</u>aAAtumoohum innakum lamushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Eat not of (meats)<br> On which God's name<br> Hath not been pronounced:<br> That would be impiety.<br> But the evil ones<br> Ever inspire their friends<br> To contend with you<br> If ye were to obey them,<br> Ye would indeed be Pagans.</p> <div class="filenav">
