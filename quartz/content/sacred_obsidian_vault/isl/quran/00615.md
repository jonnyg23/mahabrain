 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 15</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Awa man k<u>a</u>na maytan faa<u>h</u>yayn<u>a</u>hu wajaAAaln<u>a</u> lahu nooran yamshee bihi fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si kaman mathaluhu fee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti laysa bikh<u>a</u>rijin minh<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika zuyyina lilk<u>a</u>fireena m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  Can he who was dead,<br> To whom We gave life,<br> And a Light whereby<br> He can walk amongst men,<br> Be like him who is<br> In the depths of darkness,<br> From which he can<br> Never come out?<br> Thus to those without Faith<br> Their own deeds seem pleasing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika jaAAaln<u>a</u> fee kulli qaryatin ak<u>a</u>bira mujrimeeh<u>a</u> liyamkuroo feeh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yamkuroona ill<u>a</u> bi-anfusihim wam<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  Thus have We placed<br> Leaders in every town,<br>Its wicked men, to plot<br> (And burrow) therein:<br> But they only plot<br> Against their own souls,<br> And they perceive it not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>at-hum <u>a</u>yatun q<u>a</u>loo lan nu/mina <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> nu/t<u>a</u> mithla m<u>a</u> ootiya rusulu All<u>a</u>hi All<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu <u>h</u>aythu yajAAalu ris<u>a</u>latahu sayu<u>s</u>eebu alla<u>th</u>eena ajramoo <u>s</u>agh<u>a</u>run AAinda All<u>a</u>hi waAAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeedun bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yamkuroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>124.  When there comes to them<br> A Sign (from God),<br> They say: "We shall not<br> Believe until we receive<br> One (exactly) like those<br> Received by God's apostles."<br> God knoweth best where<br> (And how) to carry out<br> His mission. Soon<br> Will the wicked<br> Be overtaken by<br> Humiliation before God,<br> And a severe punishment,<br> For all their plots.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Faman yuridi All<u>a</u>hu an yahdiyahu yashra<u>h</u> <u>s</u>adrahu lil-isl<u>a</u>mi waman yurid an yu<u>d</u>illahu yajAAal <u>s</u>adrahu <u>d</u>ayyiqan <u>h</u>arajan kaannam<u>a</u> ya<u>ss</u>aAAAAadu fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i ka<u>tha</u>lika yajAAalu All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>rrijsa AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  Those whom God (in His Plan)<br> Willeth to guide,—He openeth<br> Their breast to Islam;<br> Those whom He willeth<br> To leave straying,—He maketh<br> Their breast close and constricted,<br> As if they had to climb<br> Up to the skies: thus<br> Doth God (heap) the penalty<br> On those who refuse to believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Wah<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>u rabbika mustaqeeman qad fa<u>ss</u>aln<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin ya<u>thth</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  This is the Way<br> Of thy Lord, leading straight:<br>We have detailed the Signs<br> For those who<br> Receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Lahum d<u>a</u>ru a<b>l</b>ssal<u>a</u>mi AAinda rabbihim wahuwa waliyyuhum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  For them will be a Home<br> Of Peace in the presence<br> Of their Lord: He will be<br> Their Friend, because<br> They practised (righteousness).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Wayawma ya<u>h</u>shuruhum jameeAAan y<u>a</u> maAAshara aljinni qadi istakthartum mina al-insi waq<u>a</u>la awliy<u>a</u>ohum mina al-insi rabban<u>a</u> istamtaAAa baAA<u>d</u>un<u>a</u> bibaAA<u>d</u>in wabalaghn<u>a</u> ajalan<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee ajjalta lan<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru mathw<u>a</u>kum kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu inna rabbaka <u>h</u>akeemun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  One day will He gather<br> Them all together, (and say):<br> "O ye assembly of Jinns!<br> Much (toll) did ye take<br> Of men." Their friends<br> Amongst men will say:<br> "Our Lord! we made profit no<br> From each other: but (alas!)<br> We reached our term—<br> Which Thou didst appoint<br> For us." He will say:<br> "The Fire be your dwelling-place:<br> You will dwell therein for ever,<br> Except as God willeth."<br> For thy Lord is full<br> Of wisdom and knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika nuwallee baAA<u>d</u>a a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena baAA<u>d</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  Thus do We make<br> The wrong-doers turn<br> To each other, because<br> Of what they earn.</p> <div class="filenav">
