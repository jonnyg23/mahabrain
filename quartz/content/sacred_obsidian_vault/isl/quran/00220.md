 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Inna fee khalqi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>i</b>khtil<u>a</u>fi allayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wa<b>a</b>lfulki allatee tajree fee alba<u>h</u>ri bim<u>a</u> yanfaAAu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa wam<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i min m<u>a</u>-in faa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> wabaththa feeh<u>a</u> min kulli d<u>a</u>bbatin wata<u>s</u>reefi a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>i wa<b>al</b>ssa<u>ha</u>bi almusakhkhari bayna a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  Behold! In the creation<br> Of the heavens and the earth;<br> In the alternation<br> Of the Night and the Day;<br> In the sailing of the ships<br> Through the Ocean<br> For the profit of mankind;<br> In the rain which God<br> Sends down from the skies,<br> And the life which He gives therewith<br> To an earth that is dead;<br> In the beasts of all kinds<br> That He scatters<br> Through the earth;<br> In the change of the winds,<br> And the clouds which they<br> Trail like their slaves<br> Between the sky and the earth;—<br> (Here) indeed are Signs<br> For a people that are wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yattakhi<u>th</u>u min dooni All<u>a</u>hi and<u>a</u>dan yu<u>h</u>ibboonahum ka<u>h</u>ubbi All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ashaddu <u>h</u>ubban lill<u>a</u>hi walaw yar<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo i<u>th</u> yarawna alAAa<u>tha</u>ba anna alquwwata lill<u>a</u>hi jameeAAan waanna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAa<u>tha</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  Yet there are men<br> Who take (for worship)<br> Others besides God,<br> As equal (with God):<br>They love them<br> As they should love God.<br> But those of Faith are<br> Overflowing in their love<br> For God. If only<br> The unrighteous could see,<br> Behold, they would see<br> The Penalty: that to God<br> Belongs all power, and God<br> Will strongly enforce<br> The Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_166.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>166.  I<u>th</u> tabarraa alla<u>th</u>eena ittubiAAoo mina alla<u>th</u>eena ittabaAAoo waraawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba wataqa<u>tt</u>aAAat bihimu al-asb<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>166.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_167.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>167.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ittabaAAoo law anna lan<u>a</u> karratan fanatabarraa minhum kam<u>a</u> tabarraoo minn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika yureehimu All<u>a</u>hu aAAm<u>a</u>lahum <u>h</u>asar<u>a</u>tin AAalayhim wam<u>a</u> hum bikh<u>a</u>rijeena mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>167.  And those who followed<br> Would say: "If only<br> We had one more chance,<br> We would clear ourselves<br> Of them, as they have<br> Cleared themselves of us."<br> Thus will God show them<br> (The fruits of) their deeds<br> As (nothing but) regrets.<br> Nor will there be a way<br> For them out of the Fire.</p> <div class="filenav">
