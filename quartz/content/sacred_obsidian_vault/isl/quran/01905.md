 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIX.: Maryam, or Mary.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Wayaqoolu al-ins<u>a</u>nu a-i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> mittu lasawfa okhraju <u>h</u>ayy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Man says: "What!<br> When I am dead, shall I<br> Then be raised up alive?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Awa l<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>kuru al-ins<u>a</u>nu ann<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>hu min qablu walam yaku shay-<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  But does not man<br> Call to mind that We<br> Created him before<br> Out of nothing?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Fawarabbika lana<u>h</u>shurannahum wa<b>al</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena thumma lanu<u>hd</u>irannahum <u>h</u>awla jahannama jithiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  So, by thy Lord,<br> Without doubt, We shall gather<br> Them together, and (also)<br> The Evil Ones (with them);<br> Then shall We bring them<br> Forth on their knees<br> Round about Hell;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Thumma lananziAAanna min kulli sheeAAatin ayyuhum ashaddu AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni AAitiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Then shall We certainly<br> Drag out from every sect<br> All those who were worst<br> In obstinate rebellion<br> Against (God) Most Gracious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Thumma lana<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum awl<u>a</u> bih<u>a</u> <u>s</u>iliyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  And certainly We know best<br> Those who are most worthy<br> Of being burned therein.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa-in minkum ill<u>a</u> w<u>a</u>riduh<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> rabbika <u>h</u>atman maq<u>d</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Not one of you but will<br> Pass over it: this is,<br> With thy Lord, a Decree<br> Which must be accomplished.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Thumma nunajjee alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw wana<u>th</u>aru a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena feeh<u>a</u> jithiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  But We shall save those<br> Who guarded against evil,<br> And We shall leave<br> The wrong-doers therein,<br> (Humbled) to their knees.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> bayyin<u>a</u>tin q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ayyu alfareeqayni khayrun maq<u>a</u>man waa<u>h</u>sanu nadiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  When Our Clear Signs<br> Are rehearsed to them,<br> The Unbelievers say to those<br> Who believe, "Which of the two<br> Sides is best in point of<br> Position? which makes the best<br> Show in Council?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wakam ahlakn<u>a</u> qablahum min qarnin hum a<u>h</u>sanu ath<u>a</u>than wari/y<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  But how many (countless)<br> Generations before them<br> Have We destroyed,<br> Who were even better<br> In equipment and in glitter<br> To the eye?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Qul man k<u>a</u>na fee a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lati falyamdud lahu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu maddan <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> raaw m<u>a</u> yooAAadoona imm<u>a</u> alAAa<u>tha</u>ba wa-imm<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata fasayaAAlamoona man huwa sharrun mak<u>a</u>nan waa<u>d</u>AAafu jund<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Say: "If any men go<br> Astray, (God) Most Gracious<br> Extends (the rope) to them,<br> Until, when they see<br> The warning of God (being<br> Fulfilled)—either in punishment<br> Or in (the approach of)<br> The Hour,—they will<br> At length realise who is<br> Worst in position, and (who)<br> Weakest in forces!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wayazeedu All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena ihtadaw hudan wa<b>a</b>lb<u>a</u>qiy<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>tu khayrun AAinda rabbika thaw<u>a</u>ban wakhayrun maradd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  "And God doth advance<br> In guidance those who seek<br> Guidance: and the things<br> That endure, Good Deeds,<br> Are best in the sight<br> Of thy Lord, as rewards,<br> And best in respect of<br> (Their) eventual returns."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Afaraayta alla<u>th</u>ee kafara bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>la laootayanna m<u>a</u>lan wawalad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Hast thou then seen<br> The (sort of) man who<br> Rejects Our Signs, yet<br> Says: "I shall certainly<br> Be given wealth and children?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  A<u>tt</u>alaAAa alghayba ami ittakha<u>th</u>a AAinda a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni AAahd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  Has he penetrated to<br> The Unseen, or has he<br> Taken a contract with<br> (God) Most Gracious?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Kall<u>a</u> sanaktubu m<u>a</u> yaqoolu wanamuddu lahu mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi madd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Nay! We shall record<br> What he says, and We<br> Shall add and add<br> To his punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Wanarithuhu m<u>a</u> yaqoolu waya/teen<u>a</u> fard<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  To Us shall return<br> All that he talks of,<br> And he shall appear<br> Before Us bare and alone.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi <u>a</u>lihatan liyakoonoo lahum AAizz<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  And they have taken<br> (For worship) gods other than<br> God, to give them<br> Power and glory!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Kall<u>a</u> sayakfuroona biAAib<u>a</u>datihim wayakoonoona AAalayhim <u>d</u>idd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Instead, they shall reject<br> Their worship, and become<br> Adversaries against them.</p> <div class="filenav">
