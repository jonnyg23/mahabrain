 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena qeela lahum kuffoo aydiyakum waaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta falamm<u>a</u> kutiba AAalayhimu alqit<u>a</u>lu i<u>tha</u> fareequn minhum yakhshawna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa kakhashyati All<u>a</u>hi aw ashadda khashyatan waq<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> lima katabta AAalayn<u>a</u> alqit<u>a</u>la lawl<u>a</u> akhkhartan<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ajalin qareebin qul mat<u>a</u>AAu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> qaleelun wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khiratu khayrun limani ittaq<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoona fateel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to those<br>Who were told to hold back<br> Their hands (from fight)<br> But establish regular prayers<br> And spend in regular Charity?<br> When (at length) the order<br> For fighting was issued to them,<br> Behold! a section of them<br> Feared men as<br> Or even more than<br> They should have feared God:<br> They said: "Our Lord!<br> Why hast Thou ordered us<br> To fight? Wouldst Thou not<br> Grant us respite<br> To our (natural) term,<br> Near (enough)?" Say: "Short<br> Is the enjoyment of this world:<br> The Hereafter is the best<br> For those who do right:<br> Never will ye be<br> Dealt with unjustly<br> In the very least!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Aynam<u>a</u> takoonoo yudrikkumu almawtu walaw kuntum fee buroojin mushayyadatin wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibhum <u>h</u>asanatun yaqooloo h<u>ath</u>ihi min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibhum sayyi-atun yaqooloo h<u>ath</u>ihi min AAindika qul kullun min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi fam<u>a</u>li h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i alqawmi l<u>a</u> yak<u>a</u>doona yafqahoona <u>h</u>adeeth<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  "Wherever ye are,<br> Death will find you out,<br> Even if ye are in towers<br> Built up strong and high!"<br>If some good befalls them,<br> They say, "This is from God";<br> But if evil, they say,<br> "This is from thee" (O Prophet).<br>Say: "All things are from God."<br> But what hath come<br> To these people,<br> That they fail<br> To understand<br> A single fact?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  M<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>baka min <u>h</u>asanatin famina All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>baka min sayyi-atin famin nafsika waarsaln<u>a</u>ka li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si rasoolan wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi shaheedan   </p>
 <p>79.  Whatever good, (O man!)<br> Happens to thee, is from God;<br> But whatever evil happens<br> To thee, is from thy (own) soul.<br> And We have sent thee<br> As an Apostle<br> To (instruct) mankind.<br> And enough is God<br> For a witness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Man yu<u>t</u>iAAi a<b>l</b>rrasoola faqad a<u>ta</u>AAa All<u>a</u>ha waman tawall<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka AAalayhim <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wayaqooloona <u>ta</u>AAatun fa-i<u>tha</u> barazoo min AAindika bayyata <u>ta</u>-ifatun minhum ghayra alla<u>th</u>ee taqoolu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaktubu m<u>a</u> yubayyitoona faaAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum watawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  They have "Obedience"<br> On their lips; but<br> When they leave thee,<br> A section of them<br> Meditate all night<br> On things very different<br> From what thou tellest them.<br> But God records<br> Their nightly (plots):<br> So keep clear of them,<br> And put thy trust in God,<br> And enough is God<br>As a disposer of affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Afal<u>a</u> yatadabbaroona alqur-<u>a</u>na walaw k<u>a</u>na min AAindi ghayri All<u>a</u>hi lawajadoo feehi ikhtil<u>a</u>fan katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Do they not consider<br> The Qur-ān (with care)?<br> Had it been from other<br> Than God, they would surely<br> Have found therein<br> Much discrepancy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ahum amrun mina al-amni awi alkhawfi a<u>tha</u>AAoo bihi walaw raddoohu il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli wa-il<u>a</u> olee al-amri minhum laAAalimahu alla<u>th</u>eena yastanbi<u>t</u>oonahu minhum walawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wara<u>h</u>matuhu la<b>i</b>ttabaAAtumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  When there comes to them<br> Some matter touching<br> (Public) safety or fear,<br> They divulge it.<br> If they had only referred it<br> To the Apostle, or to those<br> Charged with authority<br> Among them, the proper<br> Investigators would have<br> Tested it from them (direct).<br> Were it not for the Grace<br> And Mercy of God unto you,<br> All but a few of you<br> Would have fallen<br> Into the clutches of Satan.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Faq<u>a</u>til fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> tukallafu ill<u>a</u> nafsaka wa<u>h</u>arri<u>d</u>i almu/mineena AAas<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu an yakuffa ba/sa alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ashaddu ba/san waashaddu tankeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Then fight in God's cause—<br> Thou art held responsible<br> Only for thyself—<br> And rouse the Believers.<br> It may be that God<br> Will restrain the fury<br>Of the Unbelievers;<br> For God is the strongest<br> In might and in punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Man yashfaAA shaf<u>a</u>AAatan <u>h</u>asanatan yakun lahu na<u>s</u>eebun minh<u>a</u> waman yashfaAA shaf<u>a</u>AAatan sayyi-atan yakun lahu kiflun minh<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in muqeet<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  Whoever recommends<br> And helps a good cause<br> Becomes a partner therein:<br> And whoever recommends<br> And helps an evil cause,<br> Shares in its burden:<br> And God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>uyyeetum bita<u>h</u>iyyatin fa<u>h</u>ayyoo bi-a<u>h</u>sana minh<u>a</u> aw ruddooh<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in <u>h</u>aseeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  When a (courteous) greeting<br> Is offered you, meet it<br> With a greeting still more<br> Courteous, or (at least)<br> Of equal courtesy.<br> God takes careful account<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa layajmaAAannakum il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati l<u>a</u> rayba feehi waman a<u>s</u>daqu mina All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>adeeth<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  God! There is no god<br> But He: of a surety<br> He will gather you together<br> Against the Day of Judgment,<br> About which there is no doubt.<br> And whose word can be<br> Truer than God's?</p> <div class="filenav">
