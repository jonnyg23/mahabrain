 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 19</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_152.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>152.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo alAAijla sayan<u>a</u>luhum gha<u>d</u>abun min rabbihim wa<u>th</u>illatun fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> waka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almuftareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>152.  Those who took the calf<br> (For worship) will indeed<br> Be overwhelmed with wrath<br> From their Lord, and with<br> Shame in this life:<br> Thus do We recompense<br> Those who invent (falsehoods).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_153.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>153.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena AAamiloo a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti thumma t<u>a</u>boo min baAAdih<u>a</u> wa<u>a</u>manoo inna rabbaka min baAAdih<u>a</u> laghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>153.  But those who do wrong<br> But repent thereafter and<br> (Truly) believe,—verily<br> Thy Lord is thereafter<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_154.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>154.  Walamm<u>a</u> sakata AAan moos<u>a</u> algha<u>d</u>abu akha<u>th</u>a al-alw<u>ah</u>a wafee nuskhatih<u>a</u> hudan wara<u>h</u>matun lilla<u>th</u>eena hum lirabbihim yarhaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>154.  When the anger of Moses<br> Was appeased, he took up<br> The Tablets: in the writing<br> Thereon was Guidance and Mercy<br> For such as fear their Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Wa<b>i</b>kht<u>a</u>ra moos<u>a</u> qawmahu sabAAeena rajulan limeeq<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> falamm<u>a</u> akha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b>rrajfatu q<u>a</u>la rabbi law shi/ta ahlaktahum min qablu wa-iyy<u>a</u>ya atuhlikun<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> faAAala a<b>l</b>ssufah<u>a</u>o minn<u>a</u> in hiya ill<u>a</u> fitnatuka tu<u>d</u>illu bih<u>a</u> man tash<u>a</u>o watahdee man tash<u>a</u>o anta waliyyun<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>r<u>h</u>amn<u>a</u> waanta khayru algh<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  And Moses chose seventy<br> Of his people for Our place<br> Of meeting: when they<br> Were seized with violent quaking,<br> He prayed: "O my Lord!<br> If it had been Thy Will<br> Thou couldst have destroyed,<br> Long before, both them<br> And me: wouldst Thou<br> Destroy us for the deeds<br> Of the foolish ones among us?<br> This is no more than<br> Thy trial: by it Thou causest<br> Whom Thou wilt to stray,<br> And Thou leadest whom<br> Thou wilt into the right path.<br> Thou art our Protector:<br> So forgive us and give us<br> Thy mercy; for Thou art<br> The Best of those who forgive.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  Wa<b>o</b>ktub lan<u>a</u> fee h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asanatan wafee al-<u>a</u>khirati inn<u>a</u> hudn<u>a</u> ilayka q<u>a</u>la AAa<u>tha</u>bee o<u>s</u>eebu bihi man ash<u>a</u>o wara<u>h</u>matee wasiAAat kulla shay-in fasaaktubuh<u>a</u> lilla<u>th</u>eena yattaqoona wayu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>156.  "And ordain for us<br> That which is good,<br> In this life<br> And in the Hereafter:<br> For we have turned unto Thee."<br> He said: "With My Punishment<br>I visit whom I will;<br> But My Mercy extendeth<br> To all things. That (Mercy)<br> I shall ordain for those<br> Who do right, and practise<br> Regular charity, and those<br> Who believe in Our Signs;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yattabiAAoona a<b>l</b>rrasoola a<b>l</b>nnabiyya al-ommiyya alla<u>th</u>ee yajidoonahu maktooban AAindahum fee a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-injeeli ya/muruhum bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wayanh<u>a</u>hum AAani almunkari wayu<u>h</u>illu lahumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti wayu<u>h</u>arrimu AAalayhimu alkhab<u>a</u>-itha waya<u>d</u>aAAu AAanhum i<u>s</u>rahum wa<b>a</b>l-aghl<u>a</u>la allatee k<u>a</u>nat AAalayhim fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bihi waAAazzaroohu wana<u>s</u>aroohu wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo a<b>l</b>nnoora alla<u>th</u>ee onzila maAAahu ol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  "Those who follow the Apostle,<br> The unlettered Prophet,<br> Whom they find mentioned<br> In their own (Scriptures),—<br> In the Law and the Gospel;—<br> For he commands them<br> What is just and forbids them<br> What is evil; he allows<br> Them as lawful what is good<br> (And pure) and prohibits them<br> From what is bad (and impure);<br> He releases them<br> From their heavy burdens<br> And from the yokes<br> That are upon them.<br> So it is those who believe<br> In him, honour him,<br> Help him, and follow the Light<br> Which is sent down with him,—<br>It is they who will prosper."</p> <div class="filenav">
