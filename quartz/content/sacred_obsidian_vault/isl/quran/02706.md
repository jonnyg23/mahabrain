 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo a-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u> tur<u>a</u>ban wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> a-inn<u>a</u> lamukhrajoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  The Unbelievers say: "What!<br> When we become dust,<br> We and our fathers,—shall we<br> Really be raised (from the dead)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Laqad wuAAidn<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> na<u>h</u>nu wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> min qablu in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  "It is true we were promised<br> This,—we and our fathers<br> Before (us): these are nothing<br> But tales of the ancients."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Qul seeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Say: "Go ye through the earth<br> And see what has been<br> The end of those guilty<br> (Of sin)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zan AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> takun fee <u>d</u>ayqin mimm<u>a</u> yamkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  But grieve not over them,<br> Nor distress thyself<br> Because of their plots.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  They also say: "When will<br> This promise (come to pass)?<br> (Say) if ye are truthful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Qul AAas<u>a</u> an yakoona radifa lakum baAA<u>d</u>u alla<u>th</u>ee tastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Say: "It may be that<br> Some of the events which<br> Ye wish to hasten on<br> May be (close) in your pursuit!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa-inna rabbaka la<u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  But verily thy Lord is<br> Full of grace to mankind:<br> Yet most of them are ungrateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa-inna rabbaka layaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tukinnu <u>s</u>udooruhum wam<u>a</u> yuAAlinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  And verily thy Lord knoweth<br> All that their hearts do hide,<br> As well as all that<br> They reveal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Wam<u>a</u> min gh<u>a</u>-ibatin fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> fee kit<u>a</u>bin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Nor is there aught<br> Of the Unseen, in heaven<br> Or earth, but is (recorded)<br> In a clear record.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Inna h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na yaqu<u>ss</u>u AAal<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela akthara alla<u>th</u>ee hum feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Verily this Qur-ān doth explain<br> To the Children of Israel<br> Most of the matters<br> In which they disagree.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Wa-innahu lahudan wara<u>h</u>matun lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  And it certainly is<br> A Guide and a Mercy<br> To those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Inna rabbaka yaq<u>d</u>ee baynahum bi<u>h</u>ukmihi wahuwa alAAazeezu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  Verily thy Lord will decide<br> Between them by His Decree:<br> And He is Exalted in Might,<br> All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Fatawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innaka AAal<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqi almubeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  So put thy trust in God:<br> For thou art on (the Path<br> Of) manifest Truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Innaka l<u>a</u> tusmiAAu almawt<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tusmiAAu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>umma a<b>l</b>dduAA<u>a</u>a i<u>tha</u> wallaw mudbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Truly thou canst not cause<br> The Dead to listen, nor<br> Canst thou cause the Deaf<br> To hear the call,<br> (Especially) when they<br> Turn back in retreat.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wam<u>a</u> anta bih<u>a</u>dee alAAumyi AAan <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>latihim in tusmiAAu ill<u>a</u> man yu/minu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fahum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Nor canst thou be a guide<br> To the Blind, (to prevent them)<br> From straying: only those<br> Wilt thou get to listen<br> Who believe in Our Signs,<br> And they will bow in Islam.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> waqaAAa alqawlu AAalayhim akhrajn<u>a</u> lahum d<u>a</u>bbatan mina al-ar<u>d</u>i tukallimuhum anna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  And when the Word is<br> Fulfilled against them (the unjust),<br> We shall produce from the earth<br> A Beast to (face) them:<br> He will speak to them,<br> For that mankind did not<br> Believe with assurance<br> In Our Signs.</p> <div class="filenav">
