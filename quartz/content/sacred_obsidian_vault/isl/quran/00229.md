 <p><a></a>229.  A<b>l</b><u>tt</u>al<u>a</u>qu marrat<u>a</u>ni fa-ims<u>a</u>kun bimaAAroofin aw tasree<u>h</u>un bi-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>nin wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>illu lakum an ta/khu<u>th</u>oo mimm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytumoohunna shay-an ill<u>a</u> an yakh<u>a</u>f<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> yuqeem<u>a</u> <u>h</u>udooda All<u>a</u>hi fa-in khiftum all<u>a</u> yuqeem<u>a</u> <u>h</u>udooda All<u>a</u>hi fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayhim<u>a</u> feem<u>a</u> iftadat bihi tilka <u>h</u>udoodu All<u>a</u>hi fal<u>a</u> taAAtadooh<u>a</u> waman yataAAadda <u>h</u>udooda All<u>a</u>hi faol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>229.  A divorce is only<br> Permissible twice: after that,<br> The parties should either hold<br> Together on equitable terms,<br> Or separate with kindness.<br> It is not lawful for you,<br> (Men), to take back<br> Any of your gifts (from your wives)<br> Except when both parties<br> Fear that they would be<br> Unable to keep the limits<br>Ordained by God.<br> If ye (judges) do indeed<br> Fear that they would be<br> Unable to keep the limits<br> Ordained by God,<br> There is no blame on either<br> Of them if she give<br> Something for her freedom.<br> These are the limits<br> Ordained by God;<br> So do not transgress them<br> If any do transgress<br> The limits ordained by God,<br> Such persons wrong<br> (Themselves as well as others).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_230.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>230.  Fa-in <u>t</u>allaqah<u>a</u> fal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>illu lahu min baAAdu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tanki<u>h</u>a zawjan ghayrahu fa-in <u>t</u>allaqah<u>a</u> fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayhim<u>a</u> an yatar<u>a</u>jaAA<u>a</u> in <i><u>th</u></i>ann<u>a</u> an yuqeem<u>a</u> <u>h</u>udooda All<u>a</u>hi watilka <u>h</u>udoodu All<u>a</u>hi yubayyinuh<u>a</u> liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>230.  So if a husband<br> Divorces his wife (irrevocably),<br> He cannot, after that,<br> Re-marry her until<br> After she has married<br> Another husband and<br> He has divorced her.<br> In that case there is<br> No blame on either of them<br> If they re-unite, provided<br> They feel that they<br> Can keep the limits<br> Ordained by God.<br> Such are the limits<br> Ordained by God,<br> Which He makes plain<br> To those who understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_231.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>231.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>t</u>allaqtumu a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a fabalaghna ajalahunna faamsikoohunna bimaAAroofin aw sarri<u>h</u>oohunna bimaAAroofin wal<u>a</u> tumsikoohunna <u>d</u>ir<u>a</u>ran litaAAtadoo waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika faqad <i><u>th</u></i>alama nafsahu wal<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi huzuwan wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wam<u>a</u> anzala AAalaykum mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmati yaAAi<i><u>th</u></i>ukum bihi wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>231.  When ye divorce<br> Women, and they fulfil<br>The term of their (’Iddat),<br> Either take them back<br> On equitable terms<br> Or set them free<br> On equitable terms;<br> But do not take them back<br> To injure them, (or) to take<br> Undue advantage;<br> If any one does that,<br> He wrongs his own soul.<br> Do not treat God's Signs<br> As a jest,<br> But solemnly rehearse<br> God's favours on you,<br> And the fact that He<br> Sent down to you<br> The Book<br> And Wisdom,<br> For your instruction.<br> And fear God,<br> And know that God<br> Is well acquainted<br> With all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
