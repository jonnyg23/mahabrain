 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa<b>o</b>tlu AAalayhim nabaa noo<u>h</u>in i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi y<u>a</u> qawmi in k<u>a</u>na kabura AAalaykum maq<u>a</u>mee wata<u>th</u>keeree bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi faAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi tawakkaltu faajmiAAoo amrakum washurak<u>a</u>akum thumma l<u>a</u> yakun amrukum AAalaykum ghummatan thumma iq<u>d</u>oo ilayya wal<u>a</u> tun<i><u>th</u></i>iroon<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>71.  Relate to them the story<br> Of Noah. Behold! he said<br> To his People: "O my People,<br> If it be hard on your (mind)<br>That I should stay (with you)<br> And commemorate the Signs<br> Of God,—yet I put<br> My trust in God.<br> Get ye then an agreement<br> About your plan and among<br> Your Partners, so your plan<br> Be not to you dark and dubious.<br> Then pass your sentence on me,<br> And give me no respite.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Fa-in tawallaytum fam<u>a</u> saaltukum min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi waomirtu an akoona mina almuslimeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>72.  "But if ye turn back, (consider):<br> No reward have I asked<br> Of you: my reward is only<br> Due from God, and I<br> Have been commanded to be<br> Of those who submit<br> To God's Will (in Islam)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu fanajjayn<u>a</u>hu waman maAAahu fee alfulki wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum khal<u>a</u>-ifa waaghraqn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almun<u>th</u>areen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>73.  They rejected him,<br> But We delivered him,<br> And those with him,<br> In the Ark, and We made<br> Them inherit (the earth),<br> While We overwhelmed<br> In the Flood those<br> Who rejected Our Signs.<br> Then see what was the end<br> Of those who were warned<br> (But heeded not)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Thumma baAAathn<u>a</u> min baAAdihi rusulan il<u>a</u> qawmihim faj<u>a</u>oohum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo liyu/minoo bim<u>a</u> ka<u>thth</u>aboo bihi min qablu ka<u>tha</u>lika na<u>t</u>baAAu AAal<u>a</u> quloobi almuAAtadeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>74.  Then after him We sent<br> (Many) apostles to their Peoples:<br> They brought them Clear Signs,<br> But they would not believe<br> What they had already rejected<br>Beforehand. Thus do We seal<br> The hearts of the transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Thumma baAAathn<u>a</u> min baAAdihim moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roona il<u>a</u> firAAawna wamala-ihi bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>stakbaroo wak<u>a</u>noo qawman mujrimeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>75.  Then after them sent We<br> Moses and Aaron to Pharaoh<br> And his chiefs with Our Signs.<br> But they were arrogant:<br> They were a people in sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu min AAindin<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo inna h<u>atha</u> lasi<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un </b>   </p>
 <p>76.  When the Truth did come<br> To them from Us, they said:<br> "This is indeed evident sorcery!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> ataqooloona lil<u>h</u>aqqi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>akum asi<u>h</u>run h<u>atha</u> wal<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u a<b>l</b>ss<u>ah</u>iroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>77.  Said Moses: "Say ye (this)<br> About the Truth when<br> It hath (actually) reached you?<br> Is sorcery (like) this?<br> But sorcerers will not prosper."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Q<u>a</u>loo aji/tan<u>a</u> litalfitan<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> watakoona lakum<u>a</u> alkibriy<u>a</u>o fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu lakum<u>a</u> bimu/mineen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>78.  They said: "Hast thou<br> Come to us to turn us<br> Away from the ways<br> We found our fathers following,—<br> In order that thou and thy brother<br> May have greatness in the land?<br> But not we shall believe in you!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Waq<u>a</u>la firAAawnu i/toonee bikulli s<u>ah</u>irin AAaleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Said Pharaoh: "Bring me<br> Every sorcerer well versed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu q<u>a</u>la lahum moos<u>a</u> alqoo m<u>a</u> antum mulqoon<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>80.  When the sorcerers came,<br> Moses said to them:<br> "Throw ye what ye (wish)<br> To throw!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Falamm<u>a</u> alqaw q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ji/tum bihi a<b>l</b>ssi<u>h</u>ru inna All<u>a</u>ha sayub<u>t</u>iluhu inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>u AAamala almufsideen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>81.  When they had had their throw,<br> Moses said: "What ye<br> Have brought is sorcery:<br> God will surely make it<br> Of no effect: for God<br> Prospereth not the work<br> Of those who make mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wayu<u>h</u>iqqu All<u>a</u>hu al<u>h</u>aqqa bikalim<u>a</u>tihi walaw kariha almujrimoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>82.  "And God by His Words<br> Doth prove and establish<br> His Truth, however much<br> The Sinners may hate it!"</p> <div class="filenav">
