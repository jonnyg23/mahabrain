 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  I<u>th</u> yughashsheekumu a<b>l</b>nnuAA<u>a</u>sa amanatan minhu wayunazzilu AAalaykum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an liyu<u>t</u>ahhirakum bihi wayu<u>th</u>hiba AAankum rijza a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni waliyarbi<u>t</u>a AAal<u>a</u> quloobikum wayuthabbita bihi al-aqd<u>a</u>m<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Remember He covered you<br> With a sort of drowsiness,<br> To give you calm as from<br> Himself, and he caused<br> Rain to descend on you<br> From heaven, to clean you<br> Therewith, to remove from you<br> The stain of Satan,<br> To strengthen your hearts,<br> And to plant your feet<br> Firmly therewith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  I<u>th</u> yoo<u>h</u>ee rabbuka il<u>a</u> almal<u>a</u>-ikati annee maAAakum fathabbitoo alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo saolqee fee quloobi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo a<b>l</b>rruAAba fa<b>i</b><u>d</u>riboo fawqa al-aAAn<u>a</u>qi wa<b>i</b><u>d</u>riboo minhum kulla ban<u>a</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Remember thy Lord inspired<br> The angels (with the message):<br> "I am with you: give<br> Firmness to the Believers:<br>I will instil terror<br> Into the hearts of the Unbelievers:<br> Smite ye above their necks<br> And smite all their<br> Finger-tips off them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahum sh<u>a</u>qqoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu waman yush<u>a</u>qiqi All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  This because they contended<br> Against God and His Apostle:<br> If any contend against God<br> And His Apostle, God<br> Is strict in punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  <u>Tha</u>likum fa<u>th</u>ooqoohu waanna lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Thus (will it be said): "Taste ye<br> Then of the (punishment):<br> For those who resist God,<br> Is the penalty of the Fire."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> laqeetumu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo za<u>h</u>fan fal<u>a</u> tuwalloohumu al-adb<u>a</u>r<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye meet<br> The Unbelievers<br> In hostile array,<br> Never turn your backs<br> To them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waman yuwallihim yawma-i<u>th</u>in duburahu ill<u>a</u> muta<u>h</u>arrifan liqit<u>a</u>lin aw muta<u>h</u>ayyizan il<u>a</u> fi-atin faqad b<u>a</u>a bigha<u>d</u>abin mina All<u>a</u>hi wama/w<u>a</u>hu jahannamu wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  If any do turn his back<br> To them on such a day—<br> Unless it be in a stratagem<br> Of war, or to retreat<br> To a troop (of his own)—<br> He draws on himself<br> The wrath of God,<br> And his abode is Hell,—<br> An evil refuge (indeed)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Falam taqtuloohum wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha qatalahum wam<u>a</u> ramayta i<u>th</u> ramayta wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha ram<u>a</u> waliyubliya almu/mineena minhu bal<u>a</u>an <u>h</u>asanan inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  It is not ye who<br> Slew them; it was God:<br>When thou threwest (a handful<br> Of dust), it was not<br> Thy act, but God's:<br> In order that He might<br> Test the Believers<br> By a gracious trial<br> From Himself: for God<br> Is He Who heareth<br> And knoweth (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  <u>Tha</u>likum waanna All<u>a</u>ha moohinu kaydi alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  That, and also because<br> God is He Who makes feeble<br> The plans and stratagems<br> Of the Unbelievers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  In tastafti<u>h</u>oo faqad j<u>a</u>akumu alfat<u>h</u>u wa-in tantahoo fahuwa khayrun lakum wa-in taAAoodoo naAAud walan tughniya AAankum fi-atukum shay-an walaw kathurat waanna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  (O Unbelievers!) if ye prayed<br> For victory and judgment,<br> Now hath the judgment<br> Come to you: if ye desist<br> (From wrong), it will be<br> Best for you: if ye return<br> (To the attack), so shall We.<br> Not the least good<br> Will your forces be to you<br> Even if they were multiplied:<br> For verily God<br> Is with those who believe!</p> <div class="filenav">
