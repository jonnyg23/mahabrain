 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo m<u>a</u> lakum i<u>tha</u> qeela lakumu infiroo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi ithth<u>a</u>qaltum il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i ara<u>d</u>eetum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> mina al-<u>a</u>khirati fam<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> fee al-<u>a</u>khirati ill<u>a</u> qaleel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  O ye who believe! what<br> Is the matter with you,<br> That, when ye are asked<br> To go forth in the Cause of God,<br> Ye cling heavily to the earth?<br> Do ye prefer the life<br> Of this world to the Hereafter?<br> But little is the comfort<br> Of this life, as compared<br> With the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Ill<u>a</u> tanfiroo yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibkum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleeman wayastabdil qawman ghayrakum wal<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>urroohu shay-an wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Unless ye go forth,<br> He will punish you<br> With a grievous penalty,<br> And put others in your place;<br> But Him ye would not harm<br> In the least. For God<br> Hath power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Ill<u>a</u> tan<u>s</u>uroohu faqad na<u>s</u>arahu All<u>a</u>hu i<u>th</u> akhrajahu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo th<u>a</u>niya ithnayni i<u>th</u> hum<u>a</u> fee algh<u>a</u>ri i<u>th</u> yaqoolu li<u>sah</u>ibihi l<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zan inna All<u>a</u>ha maAAan<u>a</u> faanzala All<u>a</u>hu sakeenatahu AAalayhi waayyadahu bijunoodin lam tarawh<u>a</u> wajaAAala kalimata alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo a<b>l</b>ssufl<u>a</u> wakalimatu All<u>a</u>hi hiya alAAuly<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  If ye help not (your Leader),<br> (it is no matter): for God<br> Did indeed help him,<br> When the Unbelievers<br> Drove him out: he had<br> No more than one companion:<br> They two were in the Cave,<br> And he said to his companion,<br> "Have no fear, for God<br> Is with us": then God<br> Sent down His peace upon him,<br> And strengthened him with forces<br> Which ye saw not, and humbled<br> To the depths the word<br> Of the Unbelievers.<br> But the word of God<br> Is exalted to the heights:<br> For God is Exalted in might, Wise</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Infiroo khif<u>a</u>fan wathiq<u>a</u>lan waj<u>a</u>hidoo bi-amw<u>a</u>likum waanfusikum fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Go ye forth, (whether equipped)<br> Lightly or heavily, and strive<br> And struggle, with your goods<br> And your persons, in the Cause<br> Of God. That is best<br> For you, if ye (but) knew.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Law k<u>a</u>na AAara<u>d</u>an qareeban wasafaran q<u>as</u>idan la<b>i</b>ttabaAAooka wal<u>a</u>kin baAAudat AAalayhimu a<b>l</b>shshuqqatu wasaya<u>h</u>lifoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi lawi ista<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> lakharajn<u>a</u> maAAakum yuhlikoona anfusahum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  If there had been<br> Immediate gain (in sight),<br> And the journey easy,<br> They would (all) without doubt<br> Have followed thee, but<br> The distance was long,<br> (And weighed) on them.<br> They would indeed swear<br> By God, "If we only could,<br> We should certainly<br> Have come out with you:"<br> They would destroy their own souls;<br> For God doth know<br> That they are certainly lying.</p> <div class="filenav">
