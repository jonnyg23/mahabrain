 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 14</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> baddaln<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan mak<u>a</u>na <u>a</u>yatin wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yunazzilu q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> anta muftarin bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  When We substitute one revelation<br> For another,—and God knows best<br> What He reveals (in stages),—<br> They say, "Thou art but a forger":<br> But most of them understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Qul nazzalahu roo<u>h</u>u alqudusi min rabbika bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi liyuthabbita alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wahudan wabushr<u>a</u> lilmuslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Say, the Holy Spirit has brought<br> The revelation from thy Lord<br> In Truth, in order to strengthen<br> Those who believe, and as a Guide<br> And Glad Tidings to Muslims.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Walaqad naAAlamu annahum yaqooloona innam<u>a</u> yuAAallimuhu basharun lis<u>a</u>nu alla<u>th</u>ee yul<u>h</u>idoona ilayhi aAAjamiyyun wah<u>atha</u> lis<u>a</u>nun AAarabiyyun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  We know indeed that they<br> Say, "It is a man that.<br> Teaches him." The tongue<br> Of him they wickedly point to<br> Is notably foreign, while this<br> Is Arabic, pure and clear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yahdeehimu All<u>a</u>hu walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  Those who believe not<br> In the Signs of God,—<br> God will not guide them,<br> And theirs will be<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Innam<u>a</u> yaftaree alka<u>th</u>iba alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi waol<u>a</u>-ika humu alk<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  It is those who believe not<br> In the Signs of God,<br> That forge falsehood:<br> It is they who lie!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Man kafara bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi min baAAdi eem<u>a</u>nihi ill<u>a</u> man okriha waqalbuhu mu<u>t</u>ma-innun bi<b>a</b>l-eem<u>a</u>ni wal<u>a</u>kin man shara<u>h</u>a bi<b>a</b>lkufri <u>s</u>adran faAAalayhim gha<u>d</u>abun mina All<u>a</u>hi walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  Any one who, after accepting<br> Faith in God, utters Unbelief,—<br> Except under compulsion,<br> His heart remaining firm<br> In Faith—but such as<br> Open their breast to Unbelief,—<br> On them is Wrath from God,<br> And theirs will be<br> A dreadful Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahumu ista<u>h</u>abboo al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khirati waanna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  This because they love<br> The life of this world<br> Better than the Hereafter:<br> And God will not guide<br> Those who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena <u>t</u>abaAAa All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim wasamAAihim waab<u>sa</u>rihim waol<u>a</u>-ika humu algh<u>a</u>filoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Those are they whose hearts,<br> Ears, and eyes God has sealed up<br> And they take no heed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  L<u>a</u> jarama annahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Without doubt, in the Hereafter<br> They will perish.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Thumma inna rabbaka lilla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>jaroo min baAAdi m<u>a</u> futinoo thumma j<u>a</u>hadoo wa<u>s</u>abaroo inna rabbaka min baAAdih<u>a</u> laghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  But verily thy Lord,—<br> To those who leave their homes<br> After trials and persecutions,—<br> And who thereafter strive<br> And fight for the Faith<br> And patiently persevere,<br> Thy Lord, after all this<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
