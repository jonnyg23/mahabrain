 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo in tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yaruddookum AAal<u>a</u> aAAq<u>a</u>bikum fatanqaliboo kh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  O ye who believe!<br> If ye obey the Unbelievers,<br> They will drive you back<br> On your heels, and ye<br> Will turn back (from Faith)<br> To your own loss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Bali All<u>a</u>hu mawl<u>a</u>kum wahuwa khayru a<b>l</b>nn<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  Nay, God is your Protector,<br> And He is the best of helpers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_151.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>151.  Sanulqee fee quloobi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo a<b>l</b>rruAAba bim<u>a</u> ashrakoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> lam yunazzil bihi sul<u>ta</u>nan wama/w<u>a</u>humu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wabi/sa mathw<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>151.  Soon shall We cast terror<br> Into the hearts of the Unbelievers<br> For that they joined companions<br> With God, for which He had sew<br> No authority: their abode<br> Will be the Fire: and evil<br> Is the home of the wrong-doers!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_152.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>152.  Walaqad <u>s</u>adaqakumu All<u>a</u>hu waAAdahu i<u>th</u> ta<u>h</u>ussoonahum bi-i<u>th</u>nihi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> fashiltum watan<u>a</u>zaAAtum fee al-amri waAAa<u>s</u>aytum min baAAdi m<u>a</u> ar<u>a</u>kum m<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>ibboona minkum man yureedu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> waminkum man yureedu al-<u>a</u>khirata thumma <u>s</u>arafakum AAanhum liyabtaliyakum walaqad AAaf<u>a</u> AAankum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>152.  God did indeed fulfil<br> His promise to you<br> When ye with His permission<br> Were about to annihilate<br> Your enemy,—until ye flinched<br> And fell to disputing<br> About the order,<br> And disobeyed it<br> After He brought you in sight<br> (Of the Booty) which ye covet.<br> Among you are some<br> That hanker after this world<br> And some that desire<br> The Hereafter. Then did He<br>Divert you from your foes<br> In order to test you.<br> But He forgave you:<br> For God is full of grace<br> To those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_153.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>153.  I<u>th</u> tu<u>s</u>AAidoona wal<u>a</u> talwoona AAal<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>adin wa<b>al</b>rrasoolu yadAAookum fee okhr<u>a</u>kum faath<u>a</u>bakum ghamman bighammin likay l<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanoo AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>takum wal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>153.  Behold! ye were climbing up<br> The high ground, without even<br> Casting a side glance<br> At any one, and the Apostle<br> In your rear was calling you<br> Back. There did God give you<br> One distress after another<br> By way of requital,<br> To teach you not to grieve<br> For (the booty) that had escaped you<br> And for (the ill) that had befallen you.<br> For God is well aware<br> Of all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_154.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>154.  Thumma anzala AAalaykum min baAAdi alghammi amanatan nuAA<u>a</u>san yaghsh<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-ifatan minkum wa<u>ta</u>-ifatun qad ahammat-hum anfusuhum ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi ghayra al<u>h</u>aqqi <i><u>th</u></i>anna alj<u>a</u>hiliyyati yaqooloona hal lan<u>a</u> mina al-amri min shay-in qul inna al-amra kullahu lill<u>a</u>hi yukhfoona fee anfusihim m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yubdoona laka yaqooloona law k<u>a</u>na lan<u>a</u> mina al-amri shay-on m<u>a</u> qutiln<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>hun<u>a</u> qul law kuntum fee buyootikum labaraza alla<u>th</u>eena kutiba AAalayhimu alqatlu il<u>a</u> ma<u>da</u>jiAAihim waliyabtaliya All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>udoorikum waliyuma<u>hh</u>i<u>s</u>a m<u>a</u> fee quloobikum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>154.  After (the excitement)<br> Of the distress, He sent down<br> Calm on a band of you<br> Overcome with slumber,<br> While another band<br> Was stirred to anxiety<br> By their own feelings,<br> Moved by wrong suspicions<br> Of God—suspicions due<br> To Ignorance. They said:<br> "What affair is this of ours?"<br> Say thou: "Indeed, this affair<br>Is wholly God's." They hide<br> In their minds what they<br> Dare not reveal to thee.<br> They say (to themselves):<br> "If we had had anything<br> To do with this affair,<br> We should not have been<br> In the slaughter here."<br> Say: "Even if you had remained<br> In your homes, those<br> For whom death was decreed<br> Would certainly have gone forth<br> To the place of their death";<br> But (all this was)<br> That God might test<br> What is in your breasts<br> And purge what is<br> In your hearts.<br> For God knoweth well<br> The secrets of your hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena tawallaw minkum yawma iltaq<u>a</u> aljamAA<u>a</u>ni innam<u>a</u> istazallahumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu bibaAA<u>d</u>i m<u>a</u> kasaboo walaqad AAaf<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAanhum inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun <u>h</u>aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  Those of you<br> Who turned back<br> On the day the two hosts<br> Met,—it was Satan<br> Who caused them to fail,<br> Because of some (evil)<br> They had done. But God<br> Has blotted out (their fault):<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Forbearing.</p> <div class="filenav">
