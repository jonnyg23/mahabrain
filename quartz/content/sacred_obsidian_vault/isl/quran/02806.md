 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Walaqad wa<u>ss</u>aln<u>a</u> lahumu alqawla laAAallahum yata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Now have We caused<br> The Word to reach them<br> Themselves, in order that<br> They may receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablihi hum bihi yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Those to whom We sent<br> The Book before this,—they<br> Do believe in this (Revelation);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> yutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bihi innahu al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbin<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> min qablihi muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  And when it is recited<br> To them, they say: "We<br> Believe therein, for it is<br> The Truth from our Lord:<br> Indeed we have been Muslims<br> (Bowing to God's Will)<br> From before this.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika yu/tawna ajrahum marratayni bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo wayadraoona bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>asanati a<b>l</b>ssayyi-ata wamimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum yunfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Twice will they be given<br> Their reward, for that they<br> Have persevered, that they avert<br> Evil with Good, and that<br> They spend (in charity) out of<br> What We have given them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> samiAAoo allaghwa aAAra<u>d</u>oo AAanhu waq<u>a</u>loo lan<u>a</u> aAAm<u>a</u>lun<u>a</u> walakum aAAm<u>a</u>lukum sal<u>a</u>mun AAalaykum l<u>a</u> nabtaghee alj<u>a</u>hileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  And when they hear vain talk,<br> They turn away therefrom<br> And say: "To us our deeds,<br> And to you yours;<br>Peace be to you: we<br> Seek not the ignorant."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Innaka l<u>a</u> tahdee man a<u>h</u>babta wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha yahdee man yash<u>a</u>o wahuwa aAAlamu bi<b>a</b>lmuhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  It is true thou wilt not<br> Be able to guide every one;<br> Whom thou lowest; but God<br> Guides those whom He will.<br> And He knows best those<br> Who receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Waq<u>a</u>loo in nattabiAAi alhud<u>a</u> maAAaka nutakha<u>tt</u>af min ar<u>d</u>in<u>a</u> awa lam numakkin lahum <u>h</u>araman <u>a</u>minan yujb<u>a</u> ilayhi thamar<u>a</u>tu kulli shay-in rizqan min ladunn<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  They say: "If we were<br> To follow the guidance with thee,<br> We should be snatched away<br> From our land." Have We not<br> Established for them a secure<br> Sanctuary, to which are brought<br> As tribute fruits of all kinds,—<br> A provision from Ourselves?<br> But most of them understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wakam ahlakn<u>a</u> min qaryatin ba<u>t</u>irat maAAeeshatah<u>a</u> fatilka mas<u>a</u>kinuhum lam tuskan min baAAdihim ill<u>a</u> qaleelan wakunn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu alw<u>a</u>ritheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And how many populations<br> We destroyed, which exulted<br> In their life (of ease and plenty)!<br> Now those habitations of theirs,<br> After them, are deserted,—<br> All but a (miserable) few!<br> And We are their heirs!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na rabbuka muhlika alqur<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yabAAatha fee ommih<u>a</u> rasoolan yatloo AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> muhlikee alqur<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> waahluh<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Nor was thy Lord the one<br> To destroy a population until<br> He had sent to its Centre<br> An apostle, rehearsing to them<br> Our Signs; nor are We<br> Going to destroy a population<br> Except when its members<br> Practise iniquity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wam<u>a</u> ooteetum min shay-in famat<u>a</u>AAu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wazeenatuh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi khayrun waabq<u>a</u> afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  The (material) things which<br> Ye are given are but<br> The conveniences of this life<br> And the glitter thereof;<br> But that which is with God<br> Is better and more enduring:<br> Will ye not then be wise?</p> <div class="filenav">
