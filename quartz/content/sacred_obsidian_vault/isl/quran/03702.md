 <hr>
 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVII.: Ṣāffāt, or Those Ranged in Ranks.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  O<u>h</u>shuroo alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo waazw<u>a</u>jahum wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  "Bring ye up",<br> It shall be said,<br>"The wrong-doers<br> And their wives,<br> And the things they worshipped—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Min dooni All<u>a</u>hi fa<b>i</b>hdoohum il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>i alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "Besides God,<br> And lead them to the Way<br> To the (Fierce) Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Waqifoohum innahum masooloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "But stop them,<br> For they must be asked:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  M<u>a</u> lakum l<u>a</u> tan<u>as</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "What is the matter<br> With you that ye<br> Help not each other?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Bal humu alyawma mustaslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Nay, but that day they<br> Shall submit (to judgment);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Waaqbala baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And they will turn to<br> One another, and question<br> One <a href="errata.htm#5">another</a>.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Q<u>a</u>loo innakum kuntum ta/toonan<u>a</u> AAani alyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  They will say: "It was ye<br> Who used to come to us<br> From the right hand<br> (Of power and authority)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Q<u>a</u>loo bal lam takoonoo mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  They will reply: "Nay, ye<br> Yourselves had no Faith!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lan<u>a</u> AAalaykum min <u>s</u>ul<u>ta</u>nin bal kuntum qawman <u>ta</u>gheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  "Nor had we any authority<br> Over you. Nay, it was<br> Ye who were a people<br> In obstinate rebellion!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Fa<u>h</u>aqqa AAalayn<u>a</u> qawlu rabbin<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> la<u>tha</u>-iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "So now has been proved true,<br> Against us, the Word<br> Of our Lord that we<br> Shall indeed (have to) taste<br> (The punishment of our sins).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Faaghwayn<u>a</u>kum inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>ween<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "We led you astray: for truly<br> We were ourselves astray."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Fa-innahum yawma-i<u>th</u>in fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi mushtarikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Truly, that Day, they will<br> (All) share in the Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika nafAAalu bi<b>a</b>lmujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Verily that is how We<br> Shall deal with Sinners.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Innahum k<u>a</u>noo i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu yastakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  For they, when they were<br> Told that there is<br> No god except God, would<br> Puff themselves up with Pride,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wayaqooloona a-inn<u>a</u> lat<u>a</u>rikoo <u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u> lish<u>a</u>AAirin majnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  And say: "What! Shall we<br> Give up our gods.<br> For the sake of<br> A Poet possessed?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Bal j<u>a</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa<u>s</u>addaqa almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Nay! he has come<br> With the (very) Truth,<br> And he confirms (the Message<br> Of) the apostles (before him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Innakum la<u>tha</u>-iqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>bi al-aleem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Ye shall indeed taste<br> Of the Grievous Penalty;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wam<u>a</u> tujzawna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  But it will be no more<br> Than the retribution<br> Of (the Evil) that ye<br> Have wrought;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  But the sincere (and devoted)<br> Servants of God,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum rizqun maAAloom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  For them is a Sustenance<br> Determined,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Faw<u>a</u>kihu wahum mukramoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Fruits (Delights); and they<br> (Shall enjoy) honour and dignity,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Fee jann<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  In Gardens of Felicity,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  AAal<u>a</u> sururin mutaq<u>a</u>bileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Facing each other<br> On Thrones (of dignity):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Yu<u>ta</u>fu AAalayhim bika/sin min maAAeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Round will be passed<br> To them a Cup<br> From a clear-flowing fountain,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Bay<u>da</u>a la<u>thth</u>atin li<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>ribeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Crystal-white, of a taste<br> Delicious to those<br> Who drink (thereof),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  L<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> ghawlun wal<u>a</u> hum AAanh<u>a</u> yunzafoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Free from headiness;<br> Nor will they suffer<br> Intoxication therefrom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  WaAAindahum q<u>as</u>ir<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>arfi AAeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And besides them will be<br> Chaste women, restraining<br> Their glances, with big eyes<br> (Of wonder and beauty).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Kaannahunna bay<u>d</u>un maknoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  As if they were<br> (Delicate) eggs closely guarded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Faaqbala baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  When they will turn to<br> One another and question<br> One another.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Q<u>a</u>la q<u>a</u>-ilun minhum innee k<u>a</u>na lee qareen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  One of them will start<br> The talk and say:<br> "I had an intimate<br> Companion (on the earth),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Yaqoolu a-innaka lamina almu<u>s</u>addiqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "Who used to say,<br> "What! art thou amongst those<br> Who bear witness to<br> The truth (of the Message)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  A-i<u>tha</u> mitn<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> tur<u>a</u>ban waAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man a-inn<u>a</u> lamadeenoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  "When we die and become<br> Dust and bones, shall we<br> Indeed receive rewards<br> And punishments?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Q<u>a</u>la hal antum mu<u>tt</u>aliAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  (A voice) said: "Would ye<br> Like to look down?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fa<b>i</b><u>tt</u>alaAAa fara<u>a</u>hu fee saw<u>a</u>-i alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  He looked down<br> And saw him<br> In the midst of the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Q<u>a</u>la ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi in kidta laturdeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  He said: "By God!<br> Thou wast little short<br> Of bringing me to perdition!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Walawl<u>a</u> niAAmatu rabbee lakuntu mina almu<u>hd</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  "Had it not been for<br> The Grace of my Lord,<br> I should certainly have been<br> Among those brought (there)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Afam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimayyiteen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  "Is it (the case) that<br> We shall not die,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Ill<u>a</u> mawtatan<u>a</u> al-ool<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimuAAa<u>thth</u>abeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  "Except our first death,<br> And that we<br> Shall not be punished?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Inna h<u>atha</u> lahuwa alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Verily this is<br> The supreme achievement!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Limithli h<u>atha</u> falyaAAmali alAA<u>a</u>miloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  For the like of this<br> Let all strive,<br> Who wish to strive.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  A<u>tha</u>lika khayrun nuzulan am shajaratu a<b>l</b>zzaqqoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Is that the better entertainment<br> Or the Tree of Zaqqūm?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> fitnatan li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  For We have truly<br> Made it (as) a trial<br> For the wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Innah<u>a</u> shajaratun takhruju fee a<u>s</u>li alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  For it is a tree<br> That springs out<br> Of the bottom of Hell-fire:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  <u>T</u>alAAuh<u>a</u> kaannahu ruoosu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>een<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  The shoots of its fruit-stalks<br> Are like the heads<br> Of devils:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Fa-innahum la<u>a</u>kiloona minh<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u>li-oona minh<u>a</u> albu<u>t</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Truly they will eat thereof<br> And fill their bellies therewith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Thumma inna lahum AAalayh<u>a</u> lashawban min <u>h</u>ameem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Then on top of that<br> They will be given<br> A mixture made of<br> Boiling water.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Thumma inna marjiAAahum la-il<u>a</u> alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Then shall their return<br> Be to the (Blazing) Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Innahum alfaw <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum <u>da</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Truly they found their fathers<br> On the wrong Path;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Fahum AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim yuhraAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  So they (too) were rushed<br> Down on their footsteps!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Walaqad <u>d</u>alla qablahum aktharu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  And truly before them,<br> Many of the ancients<br> Went astray;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> feehim mun<u>th</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  But We sent aforetime,<br> Among them, (apostles)<br> To admonish them;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almun<u>th</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  Then see what was<br> The End of those who<br> Were admonished (but heeded not),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Except the sincere (and devoted)<br> Servants of God.</p> <div class="filenav">
