 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXV.: Fāṭir, or The Originator of Creation; or Malāïka, or The Angels.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha AA<u>a</u>limu ghaybi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i innahu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Verily God knows<br> (All) the hidden things<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br> Verily He has full knowledge<br> Of all that is<br> In (men's) hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAalakum khal<u>a</u>-ifa fee al-ar<u>d</u>i faman kafara faAAalayhi kufruhu wal<u>a</u> yazeedu alk<u>a</u>fireena kufruhum AAinda rabbihim ill<u>a</u> maqtan wal<u>a</u> yazeedu alk<u>a</u>fireena kufruhum ill<u>a</u> khas<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  He it is that has made<br> You inheritors in the earth:<br>If, then, any do reject<br> (God), their rejection (works)<br> Against themselves: their rejection<br> But adds to the odium<br> For the Unbelievers<br> In the sight of their Lord:<br> Their rejection but adds<br> To (their own) undoing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Qul araaytum shurak<u>a</u>akumu alla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi aroonee m<u>atha</u> khalaqoo mina al-ar<u>d</u>i am lahum shirkun fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti am <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum kit<u>a</u>ban fahum AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin minhu bal in yaAAidu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona baAA<u>d</u>uhum baAA<u>d</u>an ill<u>a</u> ghuroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Say: "Have ye seen<br> (These) "Partners" of yours<br> Whom ye call upon<br> Besides God? Show me<br> What it is they have created<br> In the (wide) earth.<br> Or have they a share<br> In the heavens? Or<br> Have We given them a Book<br> From which they (can derive)<br> Clear (evidence)?—Nay,<br> The wrong-doers promise<br> Each other nothing but delusions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yumsiku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a an tazool<u>a</u> wala-in z<u>a</u>lat<u>a</u> in amsakahum<u>a</u> min a<u>h</u>adin min baAAdihi innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>aleeman ghafoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  It is God Who sustains<br> The heavens and the earth,<br> Lest they cease (to function):<br> And if they should fail,<br> There is none—not one—<br> Can sustain them thereafter:<br>Verily He is Most Forbearing,<br> Oft-Forgiving.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Waaqsamoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi jahda aym<u>a</u>nihim la-in j<u>a</u>ahum na<u>th</u>eerun layakoonunna ahd<u>a</u> min i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u> al-omami falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum na<u>th</u>eerun m<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>dahum ill<u>a</u> nufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  They swore their strongest oaths<br> By God that if a warner<br> Came to them, they would<br> Follow his guidance better<br> Than any (other) of the Peoples:<br> But when a warner came<br> To them, it has only<br> Increased their flight<br> (From righteousness),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Istikb<u>a</u>ran fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wamakra a<b>l</b>ssayyi-i wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>eequ almakru a<b>l</b>ssayyi-o ill<u>a</u> bi-ahlihi fahal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> sunnata al-awwaleena falan tajida lisunnati All<u>a</u>hi tabdeelan walan tajida lisunnati All<u>a</u>hi ta<u>h</u>weel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  On account of their arrogance<br> In the land and their<br> Plotting of Evil.<br> But the plotting of Evil<br> Will hem in only<br> The authors thereof. Now<br> Are they but looking for<br> The way the ancients<br> Were dealt with? But<br> No change wilt thou find<br> In God's way (of dealing):<br> No turning off wilt thou<br> Find in God's way (of dealing).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Awa lam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim wak<u>a</u>noo ashadda minhum quwwatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liyuAAjizahu min shay-in fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wal<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i innahu k<u>a</u>na AAaleeman qadeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Do they not travel<br> Through the earth, and see.<br> What was the End<br> Of those before them,<br> Though they were superior<br> To them in strength?<br> Nor is God to be frustrated<br> By anything whatever<br> In the heavens<br> Or on earth: for He<br> Is All-Knowing, All-Powerful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Walaw yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>u All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa bim<u>a</u> kasaboo m<u>a</u> taraka AAal<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ahrih<u>a</u> min d<u>a</u>bbatin wal<u>a</u>kin yu-akhkhiruhum il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a ajaluhum fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na biAAib<u>a</u>dihi ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  If God were to punish<br> Men according to what<br> They deserve, He would not<br> Leave on the back<br> Of the (earth) a single<br> Living creature: but He<br> Gives them respite<br> For a stated Term:<br> When their Term expires,<br> Verily God has in His sight<br> All His servants.</p>  <div class="filenav">
