 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Walamm<u>a</u> fa<u>s</u>alati alAAeeru q<u>a</u>la aboohum innee laajidu ree<u>h</u>a yoosufa lawl<u>a</u> an tufannidoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  When the Caravan left (Egypt),<br> Their father said: "I do indeed<br> Scent the presence of Joseph:<br> Nay, think me not a dotard."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Q<u>a</u>loo ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi innaka lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lika alqadeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  They said: "By God!<br> Truly thou art in<br> Thine old wandering mind."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Falamm<u>a</u> an j<u>a</u>a albasheeru alq<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> wajhihi fa<b>i</b>rtadda ba<u>s</u>eeran q<u>a</u>la alam aqul lakum innee aAAlamu mina All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Then when the bearer<br> Of the good news came,<br> He cast (the shirt)<br> Over his face, and he<br> Forthwith regained clear sight.<br> He said: "Did I not say<br> To you, "I know from God<br> That which ye know not?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ab<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> istaghfir lan<u>a</u> <u>th</u>unooban<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> kh<u>at</u>i-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  They said: "O our father!<br> Ask for us forgiveness<br> For our sins, for we<br> Were truly at fault."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Q<u>a</u>la sawfa astaghfiru lakum rabbee innahu huwa alghafooru a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  He said: "Soon will I<br> Ask my Lord for forgiveness<br> For you: for He is indeed<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Falamm<u>a</u> dakhaloo AAal<u>a</u> yoosufa <u>a</u>w<u>a</u> ilayhi abawayhi waq<u>a</u>la odkhuloo mi<u>s</u>ra in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  Then when they entered<br> The presence of Joseph,<br> He provided a home<br> For his parents with himself,<br> And said: "Enter ye<br> Egypt (all) in safety<br> If it please God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  WarafaAAa abawayhi AAal<u>a</u> alAAarshi wakharroo lahu sujjadan waq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> abati h<u>atha</u> ta/weelu ru/y<u>a</u>ya min qablu qad jaAAalah<u>a</u> rabbee <u>h</u>aqqan waqad a<u>h</u>sana bee i<u>th</u> akhrajanee mina a<b>l</b>ssijni waj<u>a</u>a bikum mina albadwi min baAAdi an nazagha a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu baynee wabayna ikhwatee inna rabbee la<u>t</u>eefun lim<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o innahu huwa alAAaleemu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  And he raised his parents<br> High on the throne (of dignity),<br> And they fell down in prostration,<br> (All) before him. He said:<br> "O my father! this is<br> The fulfilment of my vision<br> Of old! God hath made it<br> Come true! He was indeed<br> Good to me when He<br> Took me out of prison<br>And brought you (all here)<br> Out of the desert,<br> (Even) after Satan had sown<br> Enmity between me and my brothers.<br> Verily my Lord understandeth<br> Best the mysteries of all<br> that He planneth to do.<br> For verily He is full<br> Of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Rabbi qad <u>a</u>taytanee mina almulki waAAallamtanee min ta/weeli al-a<u>ha</u>deethi f<u>at</u>ira a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i anta waliyyee fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati tawaffanee musliman waal<u>h</u>iqnee bi<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  "O my Lord! Thou hast<br> Indeed bestowed on me<br> Some power, and taught me<br> Something of the interpretation<br> Of dreams and events,—O Thou<br> Creator of the heavens<br> And the earth! Thou art<br> My Protector in this world<br> And in the Hereafter.<br> Take Thou my soul (at death)<br> As one submitting to Thy Will<br> (As a Muslim), and unite me<br> With the righteous."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  <u>Tha</u>lika min anb<u>a</u>-i alghaybi noo<u>h</u>eehi ilayka wam<u>a</u> kunta ladayhim i<u>th</u> ajmaAAoo amrahum wahum yamkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Such is one of the stories<br> Of what happened unseen,<br> Which We reveal by inspiration<br> Unto thee: nor wast thou<br> (Present) with them when they<br> Concerted their plans together<br> In the process of weaving their plots.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Wam<u>a</u> aktharu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si walaw <u>h</u>ara<u>s</u>ta bimu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  Yet no faith will<br> The greater part of mankind<br> Have, however ardently<br> Thou dost desire it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Wam<u>a</u> tas-aluhum AAalayhi min ajrin in huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikrun lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  And no reward dost thou ask<br> Of them for this: it is<br> No less than a Message<br> For all creatures.</p> <div class="filenav">
