 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XX.: Ṭā-Hā. (Mystic Letters, Ṭ. H.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Wa-i<u>th</u> quln<u>a</u> lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati osjudoo li-<u>a</u>dama fasajadoo ill<u>a</u> ibleesa ab<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>116.  When We said to the angels,<br> "Prostrate yourselves to Adam",<br> They prostrated themselves, but not<br> Iblīs: he refused.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Faquln<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> <u>a</u>damu inna h<u>atha</u> AAaduwwun laka walizawjika fal<u>a</u> yukhrijannakum<u>a</u> mina aljannati fatashq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>117.  Then We said: "O Adam!<br> Verily, this is an enemy<br> To thee and thy wife:<br> So let him not get you<br> Both out of the Garden,<br> So that thou art landed<br> In misery.''</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Inna laka all<u>a</u> tajooAAa feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> taAAr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>118.  "There is therein (enough provision)<br> For thee not to go hungry<br> Nor to go naked,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Waannaka l<u>a</u> ta<i><u>th</u></i>mao feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta<u>dha</u>   </p>
 <p>119.  "Nor to suffer from thirst,<br> Nor from the sun's heat."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Fawaswasa ilayhi a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> <u>a</u>damu hal adulluka AAal<u>a</u> shajarati alkhuldi wamulkin l<u>a</u> yabl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>120.  But Satan whispered evil<br> To him: he said, "O Adam!<br> Shall I lead thee to<br> The Tree of Eternity<br> And to a kingdom<br> That never decays?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Faakal<u>a</u> minh<u>a</u> fabadat lahum<u>a</u> saw-<u>a</u>tuhum<u>a</u> wa<u>t</u>afiq<u>a</u> yakh<u>s</u>if<u>a</u>ni AAalayhim<u>a</u> min waraqi aljannati waAAa<u>sa</u> <u>a</u>damu rabbahu faghaw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>121.  In the result, they both<br> Ate of the tree, and so<br> Their nakedness appeared<br> To them: they began to sew<br> Together, for their covering,<br> Leaves from the Garden:<br> Thus did Adam disobey<br> His Lord, and allow himself<br> To be seduced.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Thumma ijtab<u>a</u>hu rabbuhu fat<u>a</u>ba AAalayhi wahad<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>122.  But his Lord chose him<br> (For His Grace): He turned<br> To him, and gave him guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Q<u>a</u>la ihbi<u>ta</u> minh<u>a</u> jameeAAan baAA<u>d</u>ukum libaAA<u>d</u>in AAaduwwun fa-imm<u>a</u> ya/tiyannakum minnee hudan famani ittabaAAa hud<u>a</u>ya fal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>illu wal<u>a</u> yashq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>123.  He said: "Get ye down,<br> Both of you,—all together,<br> From the Garden, with enmity<br> One to another: but if,<br> As is sure, there comes to you<br> Guidance from Me, whosoever<br> Follows My guidance, will not<br> Lose his way, nor fall<br> Into misery.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Waman aAAra<u>d</u>a AAan <u>th</u>ikree fa-inna lahu maAAeeshatan <u>d</u>ank<u>a</u><b>n</b> wana<u>h</u>shuruhu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati aAAm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  "But whosoever turns away<br> From My Message, verily<br> For him is a life narrowed<br> Down, and We shall raise<br>Him up blind on the Day<br> Of Judgment."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi lima <u>h</u>ashartanee aAAm<u>a</u> waqad kuntu ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  He will say: "O my Lord!<br> Why hast thou raised me<br> Up blind, while I had<br> Sight (before)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Q<u>a</u>la ka<u>tha</u>lika atatka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> fanaseetah<u>a</u> waka<u>tha</u>lika alyawma tuns<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>126.  (God) will say: "Thus<br> Didst thou, when Our Signs<br> Came unto thee, disregard<br> Them: so wilt thou,<br> This day, be disregarded."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika najzee man asrafa walam yu/min bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihi walaAAa<u>tha</u>bu al-<u>a</u>khirati ashaddu waabq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>127.  And thus do We recompense<br> Him who transgresses beyond bounds<br> And believes not in the Signs<br> Of his Lord: and the Penalty<br> Of the Hereafter is far more<br> Grievous and more enduring.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Afalam yahdi lahum kam ahlakn<u>a</u> qablahum mina alqurooni yamshoona fee mas<u>a</u>kinihim inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin li-olee a<b>l</b>nnuh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>128.  Is it not a warning to such<br> Men (to call to mind)<br> How many generations before them<br> We destroyed, in whose haunts<br> They (now) move? Verily,<br> In this are Signs for men<br> Endued with understanding.</p> <div class="filenav">
