 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo q<u>a</u>tiloo alla<u>th</u>eena yaloonakum mina alkuff<u>a</u>ri walyajidoo feekum ghil<i><u>th</u></i>atan wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  O ye who believe! Fight<br> The Unbelievers who gird you about,<br> And let them find firmness<br> In you: and know that God<br> Is with those who fear Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> onzilat sooratun faminhum man yaqoolu ayyukum z<u>a</u>dat-hu h<u>ath</u>ihi eem<u>a</u>nan faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo faz<u>a</u>dat-hum eem<u>a</u>nan wahum yastabshiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  Whenever there cometh down'<br> A Sūra, some of them say:<br> "Which of you has had<br> His faith increased by it?"<br> Yea, those who believe,—<br> Their faith is increased,<br> And they do rejoice.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un faz<u>a</u>dat-hum rijsan il<u>a</u> rijsihim wam<u>a</u>too wahum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  But those in whose hearts<br> Is a disease,—it will add doubt<br> To their doubt, and they will die<br> In a state of Unbelief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Awa l<u>a</u> yarawna annahum yuftanoona fee kulli AA<u>a</u>min marratan aw marratayni thumma l<u>a</u> yatooboona wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>thth</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  See they not that they<br> Are tried every year<br> Once or twice? Yet they<br> Turn not in repentance,<br> And they take no heed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> onzilat sooratun na<i><u>th</u></i>ara baAA<u>d</u>uhum il<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in hal yar<u>a</u>kum min a<u>h</u>adin thumma in<u>s</u>arafoo <u>s</u>arafa All<u>a</u>hu quloobahum bi-annahum qawmun l<u>a</u> yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  Whenever there cometh down<br> A Sūra, they look at each other,<br> (Saying), "Doth anyone see you?"<br> Then they turn aside:<br> God hath turned their hearts<br> (From the light); for they<br> Are a people that understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Laqad j<u>a</u>akum rasoolun min anfusikum AAazeezun AAalayhi m<u>a </u>AAanittum <u>h</u>aree<u>s</u>un AAalaykum bi<b>a</b>lmu/mineena raoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  Now hath come unto you<br> An Apostle from amongst<br>Yourselves: it grieves him<br> That ye should perish:<br> Ardently anxious is he<br> Over you: to the Believers<br> Is he most kind and merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Fa-in tawallaw faqul <u>h</u>asbiya All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa AAalayhi tawakkaltu wahuwa rabbu alAAarshi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  But if they turn away,<br> Say: "God sufficeth me:<br> There is no god but He:<br> On Him is my trust,<br> He the Lord of the Throne<br> (Of Glory) Supreme!</p>  <div class="filenav">
