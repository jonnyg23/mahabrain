 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu fa<u>dd</u>ala baAA<u>d</u>akum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in fee a<b>l</b>rrizqi fam<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena fu<u>dd</u>iloo bir<u>a</u>ddee rizqihim AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nuhum fahum feehi saw<u>a</u>on afabiniAAmati All<u>a</u>hi yaj<u>h</u>adoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  God has bestowed His gifts<br> Of sustenance more freely on some<br> Of you than on others: those<br> More favoured are not going<br> To throw back their gifts<br> To those whom their right hands<br> Possess, so as to be equal<br> In that respect. Will they then<br> Deny the favours of God?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu jaAAala lakum min anfusikum azw<u>a</u>jan wajaAAala lakum min azw<u>a</u>jikum baneena wa<u>h</u>afadatan warazaqakum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti afabi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili yu/minoona wabiniAAmati All<u>a</u>hi hum yakfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  And God has made for you<br> Mates (and Companions) of your own nature,<br> And made for you, out of them,<br> Sons and daughters and grandchildren,<br> And provided for you sustenance<br> Of the best: will they<br> Then believe in vain things,<br> And be ungrateful for God's favours?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  WayaAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yamliku lahum rizqan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i shay-an wal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  And worship others than God,—<br> Such as have no power<br> Of providing them, for sustenance,<br>With anything in heavens or earth,<br> And cannot possibly have<br> Such power?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Fal<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>riboo lill<u>a</u>hi al-amth<u>a</u>la inna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu waantum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Invent not similitudes<br> For God: for God knoweth,<br> And ye know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  <u>D</u>araba All<u>a</u>hu mathalan AAabdan mamlookan l<u>a</u> yaqdiru AAal<u>a</u> shay-in waman razaqn<u>a</u>hu minn<u>a</u> rizqan <u>h</u>asanan fahuwa yunfiqu minhu sirran wajahran hal yastawoona al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  God sets forth the Parable<br> (Of two men: one) a slave<br> Under the dominion of another;<br> He has no power of any sort;<br> And (the other) a man<br> On whom We have bestowed<br> Goodly favours from Ourselves,<br> And he spends thereof (freely),<br> Privately and publicly:<br> Are the two equal?<br> (By no means;)<br> Praise be to God. But<br> Most of them understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wa<u>d</u>araba All<u>a</u>hu mathalan rajulayni a<u>h</u>aduhum<u>a</u> abkamu l<u>a</u> yaqdiru AAal<u>a</u> shay-in wahuwa kallun AAal<u>a</u> mawl<u>a</u>hu aynam<u>a</u> yuwajjihhu l<u>a</u> ya/ti bikhayrin hal yastawee huwa waman ya/muru bi<b>a</b>lAAadli wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  God sets forth (another) Parable<br> Of two men: one of them<br> Dumb, with no power<br> Of any sort; a wearisome burden<br> Is he to his master;<br> Whichever way he directs him,<br> He brings no good:<br> Is such a man equal<br> With one who commands<br>Justice, and is on<br> A Straight Way?</p> <div class="filenav">
