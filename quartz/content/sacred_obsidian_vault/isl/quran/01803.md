 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wata<u>h</u>sabuhum ayq<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>an wahum ruqoodun wanuqallibuhum <u>tha</u>ta alyameeni wa<u>tha</u>ta a<b>l</b>shshim<u>a</u>li wakalbuhum b<u>a</u>si<u>t</u>un <u>th</u>ir<u>a</u>AAayhi bi<b>a</b>lwa<u>s</u>eedi lawi i<u>tt</u>alaAAta AAalayhim lawallayta minhum fir<u>a</u>ran walamuli/ta minhum ruAAb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Thou wouldst have deemed them<br> Awake, whilst they were asleep,<br> And We turned them<br> On their right and on<br> Their left sides: their dog<br> Stretching forth his two fore-legs<br> On the threshold: if thou<br> Hadst come up on to them,<br> Thou wouldst have certainly<br> Turned back from them in flight,<br> And wouldst certainly have been<br> Filled with terror of them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika baAAathn<u>a</u>hum liyatas<u>a</u>aloo baynahum q<u>a</u>la q<u>a</u>-ilun minhum kam labithtum q<u>a</u>loo labithn<u>a</u> yawman aw baAA<u>d</u>a yawmin q<u>a</u>loo rabbukum aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> labithtum fa<b>i</b>bAAathoo a<u>h</u>adakum biwariqikum h<u>ath</u>ihi il<u>a</u> almadeenati falyan<i><u>th</u></i>ur ayyuh<u>a</u> azk<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>man falya/tikum birizqin minhu walyatala<u>tt</u>af wal<u>a</u> yushAAiranna bikum a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Such (being their state),<br> We raised them up (from sleep),<br> That they might question<br> Each other. Said one of them,<br>"How long have ye stayed (here)?"<br> They said, "We have stayed<br> (Perhaps) a day, or part<br> Of a day." (At length)<br> They (all) said, "God (alone)<br> Knows best how long<br> Ye have stayed here…<br> Now send ye then one of you<br> With this money of yours<br> To the town: let him<br> Find out which is the best<br> Food (to be had) and bring some<br> To you, that (ye may)<br> Satisfy your hunger therewith:<br> And let him behave<br> With care and courtesy,<br> And let him not inform<br> Any one about you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Innahum in ya<i><u>th</u></i>haroo AAalaykum yarjumookum aw yuAAeedookum fee millatihim walan tufli<u>h</u>oo i<u>th</u>an abad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  "For if they should<br> Come upon you, they would<br> Stone you or force you<br> To return to their cult,<br> And in that case ye would<br> Never attain prosperity."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika aAAtharn<u>a</u> AAalayhim liyaAAlamoo anna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun waanna a<b>l</b>s<u>a</u>AAata l<u>a</u> rayba feeh<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> yatan<u>a</u>zaAAoona baynahum amrahum faq<u>a</u>loo ibnoo AAalayhim buny<u>a</u>nan rabbuhum aAAlamu bihim q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ghalaboo AAal<u>a</u> amrihim lanattakhi<u>th</u>anna AAalayhim masjid<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Thus did We make<br> Their case known to the people,<br>That they might know<br> That the promise of God<br> Is true, and that there can<br> Be no doubt about the Hour<br> Of Judgment. Behold,<br> They dispute among themselves<br> As to their affair. (Some) said,<br> "Construct a building over them":<br> Their Lord knows best<br> About them: those who prevailed<br> Over their affair said,<br> "Let us surely build a place<br> Of worship over them."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Sayaqooloona thal<u>a</u>thatun r<u>a</u>biAAuhum kalbuhum wayaqooloona khamsatun s<u>a</u>disuhum kalbuhum rajman bi<b>a</b>lghaybi wayaqooloona sabAAatun wath<u>a</u>minuhum kalbuhum qul rabbee aAAlamu biAAiddatihim m<u>a</u> yaAAlamuhum ill<u>a</u> qaleelun fal<u>a</u> tum<u>a</u>ri feehim ill<u>a</u> mir<u>a</u>an <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiran wal<u>a</u> tastafti feehim minhum a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  (Some) say they were three,<br> The dog being the fourth<br> Among them; (others) say<br> They were five, the dog<br> Being the sixth,—doubtfully<br> Guessing at the unknown;<br> (Yet others) say they were<br> Seven, the dog being the eighth.<br> Say thou: "My Lord<br> Knoweth best their number;<br> It is but few that know<br> Their (real case)." Enter not,<br> Therefore, into controversies<br> Concerning them, except<br> On a matter that is clear,<br> Nor consult any of them<br> About (the affair of) the Sleepers.</p> <div class="filenav">
