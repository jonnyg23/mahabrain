 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Wakaayyin min <u>a</u>yatin fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yamurroona AAalayh<u>a</u> wahum AAanh<u>a</u> muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  And how many Signs<br> In the heavens and the earth<br>Do they pass by? Yet they<br> Turn (their faces) away from them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Wam<u>a</u> yu/minu aktharuhum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> wahum mushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  And most of them<br> Believe not in God<br> Without associating (others<br> As partners) with Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Afaaminoo an ta/tiyahum gh<u>a</u>shiyatun min AAa<u>tha</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi aw ta/tiyahumu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu baghtatan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  Do they then feel secure<br> From the coming against them<br> Of the covering veil<br> Of the wrath of God,<br> Or of the coming against them<br> Of the (final) Hour<br> All of a sudden<br> While they perceive not?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Qul h<u>ath</u>ihi sabeelee adAAoo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> ba<u>s</u>eeratin an<u>a</u> wamani ittabaAAanee wasub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Say thou: "This is my Way:<br> I do invite unto God,<br> On evidence clear as<br> The seeing with one's eyes,—<br>I and whoever follows me.<br> Glory to God! and never<br> Will I join gods with God!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika ill<u>a</u> rij<u>a</u>lan noo<u>h</u>ee ilayhim min ahli alqur<u>a</u> afalam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim walad<u>a</u>ru al-<u>a</u>khirati khayrun lilla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Nor did We send before thee<br> (As apostles) any but men,<br> Whom We did inspire,—<br> (Men) living in human habitations.<br> Do they not travel<br> Through the earth, and see<br> What was the end<br> Of those before them?<br> But the home of the Hereafter<br> Is best, for those who do right.<br> Will ye not then understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> istay-asa a<b>l</b>rrusulu wa<i><u>th</u></i>annoo annahum qad ku<u>th</u>iboo j<u>a</u>ahum na<u>s</u>run<u>a</u> fanujjiya man nash<u>a</u>o wal<u>a</u> yuraddu ba/sun<u>a</u> AAani alqawmi almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  (Respite will be granted)<br> Until, when the apostles<br> Give up hope (of their people)<br> And (come to) think that they<br> Were treated as liars,<br> There reaches them Our help,<br> And those whom We will<br> Are delivered into safety.<br> But never will be warded off<br> Our punishment from those<br> Who are in sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Laqad k<u>a</u>na fee qa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>ihim AAibratun li-olee al-alb<u>a</u>bi m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>adeethan yuftar<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin ta<u>s</u>deeqa alla<u>th</u>ee bayna yadayhi wataf<u>s</u>eela kulli shay-in wahudan wara<u>h</u>matan liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  There is, in their stories,<br> Instruction for men endued<br>With understanding. It is not<br> A tale invented, but a confirmation<br> Of what went before it,—<br> A detailed exposition<br> Of all things, and a Guide<br> And a Mercy to any such<br> As believe.</p>  <div class="filenav">
