 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLI.: Ḥā-mīm (Abbreviated Letters), or Ḥā-Mīm Sajda, or Fuṣṣilat</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo l<u>a</u> tasmaAAoo lih<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni wa<b>i</b>lghaw feehi laAAallakum taghliboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  The Unbelievers say<br> "Listen not to this Qur-ān,<br> But talk at random<br> In the midst<br> Of its (reading), that ye<br> May gain the upper hand!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Falanu<u>th</u>eeqanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan walanajziyannahum aswaa alla<u>th</u>ee k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  But We will certainly<br> Give the Unbelievers a taste<br> Of a severe Penalty,<br> And We will requite them<br> For the worst of their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  <u>Tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o aAAd<u>a</u>-i All<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru lahum feeh<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>ru alkhuldi jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Such is the requital<br> Of the enemies of God,—<br> The Fire: therein will be<br> For them the Eternal Home:<br> A (fit) requital, for<br> That they were wont<br> To reject Our Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo rabban<u>a</u> arin<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ayni a<u>d</u>all<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> mina aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi najAAalhum<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>ta aqd<u>a</u>min<u>a</u> liyakoon<u>a</u> mina al-asfaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And the Unbelievers will say:<br> "Our Lord! Show us those,<br> Among Jinns and men,<br> Who misled us: we shall<br> Crush them beneath our feet,<br> So that they become<br> The vilest (before all)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo rabbun<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu thumma istaq<u>a</u>moo tatanazzalu AAalayhimu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu all<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>foo wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanoo waabshiroo bi<b>a</b>ljannati allatee kuntum tooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  In the case of those<br> Who say, "Our Lord<br>Is God", and, further,<br> Stand straight and steadfast,<br> The angels descend on them.<br> (From time to time):<br> "Fear ye not!" (they suggest),<br> "Nor grieve! But receive<br> The Glad Tidings<br> Of the Garden (of Bliss),<br> The which ye were promised!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Na<u>h</u>nu awliy<u>a</u>okum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wafee al-<u>a</u>khirati walakum feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> tashtahee anfusukum walakum feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> taddaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "We are your protectors<br> In this life and<br> In the Hereafter:<br> Therein shall ye have<br> All that your souls<br> Shall desire; therein<br> Shall ye have all<br> That ye ask for!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Nuzulan min ghafoorin ra<u>h</u>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "A hospitable gift from One<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful!"</p> <div class="filenav">
