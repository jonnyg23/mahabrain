 <p><a></a>12.  Walaqad akha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu meeth<u>a</u>qa banee isr<u>a</u>-eela wabaAAathn<u>a</u> minhumu ithnay AAashara naqeeban waq<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu innee maAAakum la-in aqamtumu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>taytumu a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>mantum birusulee waAAazzartumoohum waaqra<u>d</u>tumu All<u>a</u>ha qar<u>d</u>an <u>h</u>asanan laokaffiranna AAankum sayyi-<u>a</u>tikum walaodkhilannakum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru faman kafara baAAda <u>tha</u>lika minkum faqad <u>d</u>alla saw<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">13</font></span> God did aforetime<br> Take a Covenant from<br> The Children of Israel,<br> And We appointed twelve<br> Captains among them.<br> And God said: "I am<br> With you: if ye (but)<br> Establish regular Prayers,<br>Practise regular Charity,<br> Believe in My apostles,<br> Honour and assist them,<br> And loan to God<br> A beautiful loan,<br> Verily I will wipe out<br> From you your evils,<br> And admit you to Gardens<br> With rivers flowing beneath;<br> But if any of you, after this,<br> Resisteth faith, he hath truly<br> Wandered from the path<br> Of rectitude."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fabim<u>a</u> naq<u>d</u>ihim meeth<u>a</u>qahum laAAann<u>a</u>hum wajaAAaln<u>a</u> quloobahum q<u>a</u>siyatan yu<u>h</u>arrifoona alkalima AAan maw<u>ad</u>iAAihi wanasoo <u>h</u>a<i><u>thth</u></i>an mimm<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo bihi wal<u>a</u> taz<u>a</u>lu ta<u>tt</u>aliAAu AAal<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>-inatin minhum ill<u>a</u> qaleelan minhum fa<b>o</b>AAfu AAanhum wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>fa<u>h</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">14</font></span> But because of their breach<br> Of their Covenant, We<br> Cursed them, and made<br> Their hearts grow hard:<br> They change the words<br> From their (right) places<br> And forget a good part<br> Of the Message that was<br> Sent them, nor wilt thou<br> Cease to find them<br> Barring a few—ever<br> Bent on (new) deceits:<br> But forgive them, and overlook<br> (Their misdeeds): for God<br> Loveth those who are kind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wamina alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> na<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> meeth<u>a</u>qahum fanasoo <u>h</u>a<i><u>thth</u></i>an mimm<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo bihi faaghrayn<u>a</u> baynahumu alAAad<u>a</u>wata wa<b>a</b>lbagh<u>da</u>a il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati wasawfa yunabbi-ohumu All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo ya<u>s</u>naAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">15</font></span> From those, too, who call<br> Themselves Christians,<br>We did take a Covenant,<br> But they forgot a good part<br> Of the Message that was<br> Sent them: so We estranged<br> Them, with enmity and hatred<br> Between the one and the other,<br> To the Day of Judgment.<br> And soon will God show<br> Them what it is<br> They have done.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi qad j<u>a</u>akum rasoolun<u>a</u> yubayyinu lakum katheeran mimm<u>a</u> kuntum tukhfoona mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wayaAAfoo AAan katheerin qad j<u>a</u>akum mina All<u>a</u>hi noorun wakit<u>a</u>bun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">16</font></span> O People of the Book!<br> There hath come to you<br> Our Apostle, revealing<br> To you much that ye<br> Used to hide in the Book,<br> And passing over much<br> (That is now unnecessary):<br> <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">17</font></span> There hath come to you<br> From God a (new) light<br> And a perspicuous Book,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Yahdee bihi All<u>a</u>hu mani ittabaAAa ri<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nahu subula a<b>l</b>ssal<u>a</u>mi wayukhrijuhum mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori bi-i<u>th</u>nihi wayahdeehim il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">18</font></span> Wherewith God guideth all<br> Who seek His good pleasure<br> To ways of peace and safety,<br> And leadeth them out<br> Of darkness, by His Will,<br> Unto the light,—guideth them<br> To a Path that is Straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Laqad kafara alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa almasee<u>h</u>u ibnu maryama qul faman yamliku mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an in ar<u>a</u>da an yuhlika almasee<u>h</u>a ibna maryama waommahu waman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> yakhluqu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">19</font></span> In blasphemy indeed<br> Are those that say<br> That God is Christ<br> The son of Mary.<br> Say: "Who then<br> Hath the least power<br>Against God, if His Will<br> Were to destroy Christ<br> The son of Mary, his mother,<br> And all—every one<br> That is on the earth?<br> For to God belongeth<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth, and all<br> That is between. He createth<br> What He pleaseth. For God<br> Hath power over all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Waq<u>a</u>lati alyahoodu wa<b>al</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu abn<u>a</u>o All<u>a</u>hi waa<u>h</u>ibb<u>a</u>ohu qul falima yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibukum bi<u>th</u>unoobikum bal antum basharun mimman khalaqa yaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o wayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> wa-ilayhi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">20</font></span> (Both) the Jews and the Christians<br> Say: "We are sons<br> Of God, and His beloved."<br> Say: "Why then doth He<br> Punish you for your sins?<br> Nay, ye are but men,<br> Of the men He hath created:<br> He forgiveth whom He pleaseth,<br> And He punisheth whom He pleaseth:<br> And to God belongeth<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth, and all<br> That is between:<br> And unto Him<br> Is the final goal (of all)"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi qad j<u>a</u>akum rasoolun<u>a</u> yubayyinu lakum AAal<u>a</u> fatratin mina a<b>l</b>rrusuli an taqooloo m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> min basheerin wal<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerin faqad j<u>a</u>akum basheerun wana<u>th</u>eerun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">21</font></span> O People of the Book!<br> Now hath come unto you,<br> Making (things) clear unto you,<br> Our Apostle, after the break<br>In (the series of) our apostles,<br> Lest ye should say:<br> "There came unto us<br> No bringer of glad tidings<br> And no warner (from evil)":<br> But now hath come<br> Unto you a bringer<br> Of glad tidings<br> And a warner (from evil).<br> And God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
