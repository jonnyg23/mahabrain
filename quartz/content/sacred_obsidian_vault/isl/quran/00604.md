 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Qad khasira alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo biliq<u>a</u>-i All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu baghtatan q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asratan<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> farra<u>t</u>n<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> wahum ya<u>h</u>miloona awz<u>a</u>rahum AAal<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorihim al<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> yaziroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Lost indeed are they<br> Who treat it as a falsehood<br> That they must meet God,<br> Until on a sudden<br> The hour is on them,<br> And they say: "Ah! woe<br> Unto us that we took<br> No thought of it";<br> For they bear their burdens<br> On their backs,<br>And evil indeed are<br> The burdens that they bear?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wam<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> laAAibun walahwun wala<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>ru al-<u>a</u>khirati khayrun lilla<u>th</u>eena yattaqoona afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  What is the life of this world<br> But play and amusement? ess<br> But best is the Home<br> In the Hereafter, for those<br> Who are righteous.<br> Will ye not then understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Qad naAAlamu innahu laya<u>h</u>zunuka alla<u>th</u>ee yaqooloona fa-innahum l<u>a</u> yuka<u>thth</u>iboonaka wal<u>a</u>kinna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi yaj<u>h</u>adoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  We know indeed the grief<br> Which their words do cause thee:<br> It is not thee they reject:<br> It is the Signs of God,<br> Which the wicked contemn.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Walaqad ku<u>thth</u>ibat rusulun min qablika fa<u>s</u>abaroo AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ku<u>thth</u>iboo waoo<u>th</u>oo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> at<u>a</u>hum na<u>s</u>run<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> mubaddila likalim<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi walaqad j<u>a</u>aka min naba-i almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Rejected were the Apostles<br> Before thee: with patience<br> And constancy they bore<br> Their rejection and their wrongs,<br> Until Our aid did reach<br> Them: there is none<br> That can alter the Words<br> (And Decrees) of God.<br> Already hast thou received<br> Some account of those Apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>na kabura AAalayka iAAr<u>ad</u>uhum fa-ini ista<u>t</u>aAAta an tabtaghiya nafaqan fee al-ar<u>d</u>i aw sullaman fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i fata/tiyahum bi-<u>a</u>yatin walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lajamaAAahum AAal<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> fal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina alj<u>a</u>hileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  If their spurning is hard<br> On thy mind, yet if<br> Thou wert able to seek<br> A tunnel in the ground<br> Or a ladder to the skies<br> And bring them a Sign,—<br> (What good?). If it were<br> God's Will, He could<br> Gather them together<br> Unto true guidance:<br>So be not thou<br> Amongst those who are swayed<br> By ignorance (and impatience)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Innam<u>a</u> yastajeebu alla<u>th</u>eena yasmaAAoona wa<b>a</b>lmawt<u>a</u> yabAAathuhumu All<u>a</u>hu thumma ilayhi yurjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Those who listen (in truth),<br> Be sure, will accept:<br> As to the dead, God will<br> Raise them up; then will they<br> Be turned unto Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> nuzzila AAalayhi <u>a</u>yatun min rabbihi qul inna All<u>a</u>ha q<u>a</u>dirun AAal<u>a</u> an yunazzila <u>a</u>yatan wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Whey say: "Why is not<br> A Sign sent down<br> To him from his Lord?"<br> Say: "God hath certainly<br> Power to send down a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Understand not."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wam<u>a</u> min d<u>a</u>bbatin fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-irin ya<u>t</u>eeru bijan<u>ah</u>ayhi ill<u>a</u> omamun amth<u>a</u>lukum m<u>a</u> farra<u>t</u>n<u>a</u> fee alkit<u>a</u>bi min shay-in thumma il<u>a</u> rabbihim yu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  There is not an animal<br> (That lives) on the earth,<br> Nor a being that flies<br> On its wings, but (forms<br> Part of) communities like you.<br> Nothing have we omitted<br> From the Book, and they (all)<br> Shall be gathered to their Lord<br> In the end.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ummun wabukmun fee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti man yasha-i All<u>a</u>hu yu<u>d</u>lilhu waman yasha/ yajAAalhu AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Those who reject our Signs<br> Are deaf and dumb,—<br>In the midst of darkness<br> Profound: whom God willeth,<br> He leaveth to wander:<br> Whom He willeth, He placeth<br> On the Way that is Straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Qul araaytakum in at<u>a</u>kum AAa<u>tha</u>bu All<u>a</u>hi aw atatkumu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu aghayra All<u>a</u>hi tadAAoona in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Say: "Think ye to yourselves,<br> If there come upon you<br> The Wrath of God,<br> Or the Hour (that ye dread),<br> Would ye then call upon<br> Other than God?—<br> (Reply) if ye are truthful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Bal iyy<u>a</u>hu tadAAoona fayakshifu m<u>a</u> tadAAoona ilayhi in sh<u>a</u>a watansawna m<u>a</u> tushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  "Nay,—On Him would ye<br> Call, and if it be<br> His Will, He would remove<br> (The distress) which occasioned<br> Your call upon Him,<br> And ye would forget<br> (The false gods) which ye<br> Join with Him!"</p> <div class="filenav">
