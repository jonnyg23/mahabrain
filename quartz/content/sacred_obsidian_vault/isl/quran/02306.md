 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Qul rabbi imm<u>a</u> turiyannee m<u>a</u> yooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  Say: "O my Lord!<br> If Thou wilt show me<br> (In my lifetime) that which<br> They are warned against,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Rabbi fal<u>a</u> tajAAalnee fee alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>94.  "Then, O my Lord! put me not<br> Amongst the people<br> Who do wrong!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> an nuriyaka m<u>a</u> naAAiduhum laq<u>a</u>diroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>95.  And We are certainly able<br> To show thee (in fulfilment)<br> That against which they are warned.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  IdfaAA bi<b>a</b>llatee hiya a<u>h</u>sanu a<b>l</b>ssayyi-ata na<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Repel evil with that'<br> Which is best: We are<br> Well acquainted with<br> The things they say.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Waqul rabbi aAAoo<u>th</u>u bika min hamaz<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>een<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  And say "O my Lord!<br> I seek refuge with Thee<br>From the suggestions<br> Of the Evil Ones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  WaaAAoo<u>th</u>u bika rabbi an ya<u>hd</u>uroon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  "And I seek refuge with Thee<br> O my Lord! lest they<br> Should come near me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a a<u>h</u>adahumu almawtu q<u>a</u>la rabbi irjiAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  (In Falsehood will they be)<br> Until, when death comes<br> To one of them, he says:<br> "O my Lord! send me back<br> (To life),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  LaAAallee aAAmalu <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an feem<u>a</u> taraktu kall<u>a</u> innah<u>a</u> kalimatun huwa q<u>a</u>-iluh<u>a</u> wamin war<u>a</u>-ihim barzakhun il<u>a</u> yawmi yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  "In order that I may<br> Work righteousness in the things<br> I neglected."—"By no means!<br> It is but a word he says."—<br> Before them is a Partition<br> Till the Day they are<br> Raised up.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> nufikha fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori fal<u>a</u> ans<u>a</u>ba baynahum yawma-i<u>th</u>in wal<u>a</u> yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>101.  When the Trumpet<br> Is blown, there will be<br> No more relationships<br> Between them that day,<br> Nor will one ask after another!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Faman thaqulat maw<u>a</u>zeenuhu faol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>102.  Then those whose balance<br> (Of good deeds) is heavy,—<br> They will attain salvation:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Waman khaffat maw<u>a</u>zeenuhu faol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum fee jahannama kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  But those whose balance<br> Is light, will be those<br> Who have lost their souls;<br> In Hell will they abide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Talfa<u>h</u>u wujoohahumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wahum feeh<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>li<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  The Fire will burn their faces,<br> And they will therein<br> Grin, with their lips displaced.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Alam takun <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee tutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum fakuntum bih<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  "Were not My Signs rehearsed<br> To you, and ye did but<br> Treat them as falsehoods?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> ghalabat AAalayn<u>a</u> shiqwatun<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> qawman <u>da</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  They will say: "Our Lord!<br> Our misfortune overwhelmed us,<br> And we became a people<br> Astray!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Rabban<u>a</u> akhrijn<u>a</u> minh<u>a</u> fa-in AAudn<u>a</u> fa-inn<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  "Our Lord! Bring us out<br> Of this: if ever we return<br> (To evil), then shall we be<br> Wrong-doers indeed!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Q<u>a</u>la ikhsaoo feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tukallimoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  He will say: "Be ye<br> Driven into it (with ignominy)!<br> And speak ye not to Me!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Innahu k<u>a</u>na fareequn min AAib<u>a</u>dee yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>r<u>h</u>amn<u>a</u> waanta khayru a<b>l</b>rr<u>ah</u>imeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  "A part of My servants<br> There was, who used to pray,<br>'Our Lord! we believe;<br> Then do Thou forgive us,<br> And have mercy upon us:<br> For Thou art the Best<br> Of those who show mercy!'</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Fa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>tumoohum sikhriyyan <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ansawkum <u>th</u>ikree wakuntum minhum ta<u>dh</u>akoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  "But ye treated them<br> With ridicule, so much so<br> That (ridicule of) them made you<br> Forget My Message while<br> Ye were laughing at them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Innee jazaytuhumu alyawma bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo annahum humu alf<u>a</u>-izoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  "I have rewarded them<br> This day for their patience<br> And constancy: they are indeed<br> The ones that have achieved Bliss:.."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Q<u>a</u>la kam labithtum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i AAadada sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  He will say: "What number<br> Of years did ye stay<br> On earth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Q<u>a</u>loo labithn<u>a</u> yawman aw baAA<u>d</u>a yawmin fa<b>i</b>s-ali alAA<u>a</u>ddeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  They will say: "We stayed<br> A day or part of a day:<br> But ask those who<br> Keep account."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Q<u>a</u>la in labithtum ill<u>a</u> qaleelan law annakum kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  He will say: "Ye stayed<br> Not but a little,—<br> If ye had only known!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Afa<u>h</u>asibtum annam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>kum AAabathan waannakum ilayn<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  "Did ye then think<br> That We had created you<br>In jest, and that ye<br> Would not be brought back<br> To Us (for account)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  FataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu almaliku al<u>h</u>aqqu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa rabbu alAAarshi alkareem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  Therefore exalted be God,<br> The King, the Reality:<br> There is no god but He,<br> The Lord of the Throne<br> Of Honour!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Waman yadAAu maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara l<u>a</u> burh<u>a</u>na lahu bihi fa-innam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>buhu AAinda rabbihi innahu l<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  If anyone invokes, besides God,<br> Any other god, he has<br> No authority therefor;<br> And his reckoning will be<br> Only with his Lord!<br> And verily the Unbelievers<br> Will fail to win through</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Waqul rabbi ighfir wa<b>i</b>r<u>h</u>am waanta khayru a<b>l</b>rr<u>ah</u>imeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  So say: "O my Lord!<br> Grant Thou forgiveness and mercy!<br> For Thou art the Best<br> Of those who show mercy!"</p>  <div class="filenav">
