 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wal<u>a</u> taqtuloo awl<u>a</u>dakum khashyata iml<u>a</u>qin na<u>h</u>nu narzuquhum wa-iyy<u>a</u>kum inna qatlahum k<u>a</u>na khi<u>t</u>-an kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Kill not your children<br> For fear of want: We shall<br> Provide sustenance for them<br> As well as for you.<br> Verily the killing of them<br> Is a great sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wal<u>a</u> taqraboo a<b>l</b>zzin<u>a</u> innahu k<u>a</u>na f<u>ah</u>ishatan was<u>a</u>a sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Nor come nigh to adultery:<br> For it is a shameful (deed)<br> And an evil, opening the road<br> (To other evils).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wal<u>a</u> taqtuloo a<b>l</b>nnafsa allatee <u>h</u>arrama All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waman qutila ma<i><u>th</u></i>looman faqad jaAAaln<u>a</u> liwaliyyihi sul<u>ta</u>nan fal<u>a</u> yusrif fee alqatli innahu k<u>a</u>na man<u>s</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Nor take life—which God<br> Has made sacred—except<br> For just cause. And if<br> Anyone is slain wrongfully,<br> We have given his heir<br> Authority (to demand Qiṣāṣ<br> Or to forgive): but let him<br> Not exceed bounds in the matter<br> Of taking life; for he<br> Is helped (by the Law).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wal<u>a</u> taqraboo m<u>a</u>la alyateemi ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>llatee hiya a<u>h</u>sanu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yablugha ashuddahu waawfoo bi<b>a</b>lAAahdi inna alAAahda k<u>a</u>na mas-ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Come not nigh<br> To the orphan's property<br> Except to improve it,<br>Until he attains the age<br> Of full strength; and fulfil<br> (Every) engagement,<br> For (every) engagement<br> Will be enquired into<br> (On the Day of Reckoning).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Waawfoo alkayla i<u>tha</u> kiltum wazinoo bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>ta</u>si almustaqeemi <u>tha</u>lika khayrun waa<u>h</u>sanu ta/weel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Give full measure when ye<br> Measure, and weigh<br> With a balance that is straight:<br> That is the most fitting<br> And the most advantageous<br> In the final determination:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wal<u>a</u> taqfu m<u>a</u> laysa laka bihi AAilmun inna a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wa<b>a</b>lba<u>s</u>ara wa<b>a</b>lfu-<u>a</u>da kullu ol<u>a</u>-ika k<u>a</u>na AAanhu mas-ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  And pursue not that<br> Of which thou hast<br> No knowledge; for<br> Every act of hearing,<br> Or of seeing<br> Or of (feeling in) the heart<br> Will be enquired into<br> (On the Day of Reckoning).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wal<u>a</u> tamshi fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mara<u>h</u>an innaka lan takhriqa al-ar<u>d</u>a walan tablugha aljib<u>a</u>la <u>t</u>ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Nor walk on the earth<br> With insolence: for thou<br>Canst not rend the earth<br> Asunder, nor reach<br> The mountains in height.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Kullu <u>tha</u>lika k<u>a</u>na sayyi-ohu AAinda rabbika makrooh<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Of all such things<br> The evil is hateful<br> In the sight of thy Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  <u>Tha</u>lika mimm<u>a</u> aw<u>ha</u> ilayka rabbuka mina al<u>h</u>ikmati wal<u>a</u> tajAAal maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara fatulq<u>a</u> fee jahannama malooman mad<u>h</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  These are among the (precepts<br> Of) wisdom, which thy Lord<br> Has revealed to thee.<br> Take not, with God,<br> Another object of worship,<br> Lest thou shouldst be thrown<br> Into Hell, blameworthy and rejected.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Afaa<u>s</u>f<u>a</u>kum rabbukum bi<b>a</b>lbaneena wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>a mina almal<u>a</u>-ikati in<u>a</u>than innakum lataqooloona qawlan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n </b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Has then your Lord,<br> (O Pagans!) preferred for you<br> Sons, and taken for Himself<br> Daughters among the angels?<br> Truly ye utter<br> A most dreadful saying!</p> <div class="filenav">
