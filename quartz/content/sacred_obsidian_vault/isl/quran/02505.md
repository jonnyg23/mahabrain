 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXV.: Furqān, or The Criterion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> rabbika kayfa madda a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>illa walaw sh<u>a</u>a lajaAAalahu s<u>a</u>kinan thumma jaAAaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshamsa AAalayhi daleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to thy Lord?—<br> How He doth prolong<br> The Shadow! If He willed,<br> He could make it stationary!<br> Then do We make<br> The sun its guide;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Thumma qaba<u>d</u>n<u>a</u>hu ilayn<u>a</u> qab<u>d</u>an yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Then We draw it in<br> Towards Ourselves,<br> A contraction by easy stages.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu allayla lib<u>a</u>san wa<b>al</b>nnawma sub<u>a</u>tan wajaAAala a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra nushoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And He it is Who makes<br> The Night as a Robe<br> For you, and Sleep as Repose,<br> And makes the Day<br> (As it were) a Resurrection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee arsala a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>a bushran bayna yaday ra<u>h</u>matihi waanzaln<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an <u>t</u>ahoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And He it is Who sends<br> The Winds as heralds<br> Of glad tidings, going before<br> His Mercy, and We send down<br>Pure water from the sky,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Linu<u>h</u>yiya bihi baldatan maytan wanusqiyahu mimm<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> anAA<u>a</u>man waan<u>a</u>siyya katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  That with it We may give<br> Life to a dead land,<br> And slake the thirst<br> Of things We have created,—<br> Cattle and men in great numbers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Walaqad <u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u>hu baynahum liya<u>thth</u>akkaroo faab<u>a</u> aktharu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si ill<u>a</u> kufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And We have distributed<br> The (water) amongst them, in order<br> That they may celebrate<br> (Our) praises, but most men<br> Are averse (to aught) but<br> (Rank) ingratitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Walaw shi/n<u>a</u> labaAAathn<u>a</u> fee kulli qaryatin na<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Had it been Our Will,<br> We could have sent<br> A warner to every centre<br> Of population.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Fal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAi alk<u>a</u>fireena waj<u>a</u>hidhum bihi jih<u>a</u>dan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Therefore listen not<br> To the Unbelievers, but strive<br>Against them with the utmost<br> Strenuousness, with the (Qur-ān).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee maraja alba<u>h</u>rayni h<u>atha</u> AAa<u>th</u>bun fur<u>a</u>tun wah<u>atha</u> mil<u>h</u>un oj<u>a</u>jun wajaAAala baynahum<u>a</u> barzakhan wa<u>h</u>ijran ma<u>h</u>joor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  It is He Who has<br> Let free the two bodies<br> Of flowing water:<br> One palatable and sweet,<br> And the other salt<br> And hitter; yet has He<br> Made a barrier between them,<br> A partition that is forbidden<br> To be passed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa mina alm<u>a</u>-i basharan fajaAAalahu nasaban wa<u>s</u>ihran wak<u>a</u>na rabbuka qadeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  It is He Who has<br> Created man from water:<br> Then has He established<br> Relationships of lineage<br>And marriage: for thy Lord<br> Has power (over all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  WayaAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yanfaAAuhum wal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urruhum wak<u>a</u>na alk<u>a</u>firu AAal<u>a</u> rabbihi <i><u>th</u></i>aheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Yet do they worship,<br> Besides God, things that can<br> Neither profit them nor<br> Harm them: and the Misbeliever<br> Is a helper (of Evil),<br> Against his own Lord!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka ill<u>a</u> mubashshiran wana<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  But thee We only sent<br> To give glad tidings<br> And admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Qul m<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin ill<u>a</u> man sh<u>a</u>a an yattakhi<u>th</u>a il<u>a</u> rabbihi sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Say: "No reward do I<br> Ask of you for it but this:<br> That each one who will<br> May take a (straight) Path<br> To his Lord."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Watawakkal AAal<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ayyi alla<u>th</u>ee l<u>a</u> yamootu wasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdihi wakaf<u>a</u> bihi bi<u>th</u>unoobi AAib<u>a</u>dihi khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And put thy trust<br> In Him Who lives<br> And dies not; and celebrate<br> His praise; and enough is He<br> To be acquainted with<br> The faults of His servants;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> fee sittati ayy<u>a</u>min thumma istaw<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alAAarshi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu fa<b>i</b>s-al bihi khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  He Who created the heavens<br> And the earth and all<br> That is between, in six days,<br> And is firmly established<br> On the Throne (of authority):<br> God Most Gracious:<br> Ask thou, then, about Him<br> Of any acquainted (with such things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu osjudoo li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni q<u>a</u>loo wam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu anasjudu lim<u>a</u> ta/murun<u>a</u> waz<u>a</u>dahum nufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  When it is said to them,<br> "Adore ye (God) Most Gracious!",<br> They say, "And what is (God)<br> Most Gracious? Shall we adore<br> That which thou commandest us?"<br> And it increases their flight<br> (From the Truth).</p> <div class="filenav">
