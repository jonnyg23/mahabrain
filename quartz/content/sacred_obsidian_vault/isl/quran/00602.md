 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Qul seeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i thumma on<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Say: "Travel through the earth<br> And see what was the end<br> Of those who rejected Truth".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Qul liman m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i qul lill<u>a</u>hi kataba AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>mata layajmaAAannakum il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati l<u>a</u> rayba feehi alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum fahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Say: "To whom I elongeth<br> All that is in the heavens<br>And on earth?" Say:<br> "To God. He hath inscribed<br> For Himself (the rule of) Mercy.<br> That He will gather you<br> Together for the Day of Judgment,<br> There is no doubt whatever.<br> It is they who have lost<br> Their own souls, that will<br> Not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Walahu m<u>a</u> sakana fee allayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  To him belongeth all<br> That dwelleth (or lurketh)<br> In the Night and the Day.<br> For He is the One<br> Who heareth and knoweth<br> All things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Qul aghayra All<u>a</u>hi attakhi<u>th</u>u waliyyan f<u>at</u>iri a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa yu<u>t</u>AAimu wal<u>a</u> yu<u>t</u>AAamu qul innee omirtu an akoona awwala man aslama wal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Say: "Shall I take<br> For my protector<br> Any other than God,<br> The Maker of the heavens<br> And the earth?<br> And He it is that<br> Feedeth but is not fed."<br> Say: "Nay! but I am<br> Commanded to be the first<br> Of those who bow<br> To God (in Islam),<br> And be not thou<br> Of the company of those<br> Who join gods with God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Qul innee akh<u>a</u>fu in AAa<u>s</u>aytu rabbee AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Say: "I would, if I<br> Disobeyed my Lord,<br> Indeed have fear<br> Of the Penalty<br> Of a Mighty Day.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Man yu<u>s</u>raf AAanhu yawma-i<u>th</u>in faqad ra<u>h</u>imahu wa<u>tha</u>lika alfawzu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "On that day, if the Penalty<br> Is averted from any,<br> It is due to God's Mercy;<br> And that would be (Salvation),<br> The obvious fulfilment<br> Of all desire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa-in yamsaska All<u>a</u>hu bi<u>d</u>urrin fal<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>shifa lahu ill<u>a</u> huwa wa-in yamsaska bikhayrin fahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "If God touch thee<br> With affliction, none<br> Can remove it but He;<br> If He touch thee with happiness,<br> He hath power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wahuwa alq<u>a</u>hiru fawqa AAib<u>a</u>dihi wahuwa al<u>h</u>akeemu alkhabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  "He is the Irresistible, (watching)<br> From above over His worshippers;<br> And He is the Wise,<br> Acquainted with all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Qul ayyu shay-in akbaru shah<u>a</u>datan quli All<u>a</u>hu shaheedun baynee wabaynakum waoo<u>h</u>iya ilayya h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>nu li-on<u>th</u>irakum bihi waman balagha a-innakum latashhadoona anna maAAa All<u>a</u>hi <u>a</u>lihatan okhr<u>a</u> qul l<u>a</u> ashhadu qul innam<u>a</u> huwa il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun wa-innanee baree-on mimm<u>a</u> tushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Say: "What thing is most<br> Weighty in evidence?"<br> Say: "God is witness<br> Between me and you;<br> This Qur-ān hath been<br> Revealed to me by inspiration,<br> That I may warn you<br> And all whom it reaches.<br> Can ye possibly bear witness<br> That besides God there is<br> Another God?" Say:<br> "Nay! I cannot bear witness!"<br> Say: But in truth<br> He is the One God,<br>And I truly am innocent<br> Of (your blasphemy of) joining<br> Others with Him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba yaAArifoonahu kam<u>a</u> yaAArifoona abn<u>a</u>ahum alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum fahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Those to whom<br> We have given the Book<br> Know this as they know<br> Their own sons.<br> Those who have lost<br> Their own souls<br> Refuse therefore to believe.</p> <div class="filenav">
