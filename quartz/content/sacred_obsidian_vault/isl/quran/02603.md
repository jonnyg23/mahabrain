 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Q<u>a</u>la lilmala-i <u>h</u>awlahu inna h<u>atha</u> las<u>ah</u>irun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  (Pharaoh) said to the Chiefs<br> Around him: "This is indeed<br> A sorcerer well-versed:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Yureedu an yukhrijakum min ar<u>d</u>ikum bisi<u>h</u>rihi fam<u>atha</u> ta/muroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "His plan is to get you out<br> Of your land by his sorcery;<br> Then what is it ye counsel?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Q<u>a</u>loo arjih waakh<u>a</u>hu wa<b>i</b>bAAath fee almad<u>a</u>-ini <u>ha</u>shireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  They said: "Keep him<br> And his brother in suspense<br> (For a while), and dispatch<br> To the Cities heralds to collect—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Ya/tooka bikulli sa<u>hha</u>rin AAaleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "And bring up to thee<br> All (our) sorcerers well-versed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  FajumiAAa a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu limeeq<u>a</u>ti yawmin maAAloom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  So the sorcerers were got<br> Together for the appointment<br> Of a day well-known,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Waqeela li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si hal antum mujtamiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  And the people were told:<br> "Are ye (now) assembled?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  LaAAallan<u>a</u> nattabiAAu a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>arata in k<u>a</u>noo humu algh<u>a</u>libeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  "That we may follow<br> The sorcerers (in religion)<br> If they win?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu q<u>a</u>loo lifirAAawna a-inna lan<u>a</u> laajran in kunn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu algh<u>a</u>libeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  So when the sorcerers arrived,<br> They said to Pharaoh:<br> "Of course—shall we have<br> A (suitable) reward<br> If we win?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Q<u>a</u>la naAAam wa-innakum i<u>th</u>an lamina almuqarrabeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  He said: "Yea, (and more),—<br> For ye shall in that case<br> Be (raised to posts)<br> Nearest (to my person)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Q<u>a</u>la lahum moos<u>a</u> alqoo m<u>a</u> antum mulqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Moses said to them:<br> "Throw ye—that which<br> Ye are about to throw!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Faalqaw <u>h</u>ib<u>a</u>lahum waAAi<u>s</u>iyyahum waq<u>a</u>loo biAAizzati firAAawna inn<u>a</u> lana<u>h</u>nu algh<u>a</u>liboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  So they threw their ropes<br> And their rods, and said:<br> "By the might of Pharaoh,<br> It is we who will<br> Certainly win!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Faalq<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> AAa<u>sa</u>hu fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya talqafu m<u>a</u> ya/fikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Then Moses threw his rod,<br> When, behold, it straightway<br> Swallows up all<br> The falsehoods which they fake!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Faolqiya a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu s<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  When did the sorcerers<br> Fall down, prostrate in adoration,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> birabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Saying: "We believe<br> In the Lord of the Worlds,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Rabbi moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  "The Lord of Moses and Aaron."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Q<u>a</u>la <u>a</u>mantum lahu qabla an <u>ath</u>ana lakum innahu lakabeerukumu alla<u>th</u>ee AAallamakumu a<b>l</b>ssi<u>h</u>ra falasawfa taAAlamoona laoqa<u>tt</u>iAAanna aydiyakum waarjulakum min khil<u>a</u>fin walao<u>s</u>allibannakum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Said (Pharaoh): "Believe ye<br> In Him before I give<br> You permission? Surely he<br> Is your leader, who has<br> Taught you sorcery!<br> But soon shall ye know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> <u>d</u>ayra inn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> rabbin<u>a</u> munqaliboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  "Be sure I will cut off<br> Your hands and your feet<br> On opposite sides, and I<br> Will cause you all<br> To die on the cross!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Inn<u>a</u> na<u>t</u>maAAu an yaghfira lan<u>a</u> rabbun<u>a</u> kha<u>ta</u>y<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> an kunn<u>a</u> awwala almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  They said: "No matter!<br> For us, we shall but<br> Return to our Lord!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> an asri biAAib<u>a</u>dee innakum muttabaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "Only, our desire is<br> That our Lord will<br> Forgive us our faults,<br> That we may become<br> Foremost among the Believers!"</p> <div class="filenav">
