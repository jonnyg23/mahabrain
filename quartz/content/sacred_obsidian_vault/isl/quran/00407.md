 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> taqraboo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta waantum suk<u>a</u>r<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> taAAlamoo m<u>a</u> taqooloona wal<u>a</u> junuban ill<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>biree sabeelin <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> taghtasiloo wa-in kuntum mar<u>da</u> aw AAal<u>a</u> safarin aw j<u>a</u>a a<u>h</u>adun minkum mina algh<u>a</u>-i<u>t</u>i aw l<u>a</u>mastumu a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a falam tajidoo m<u>a</u>an fatayammamoo <u>s</u>aAAeedan <u>t</u>ayyiban fa<b>i</b>msa<u>h</u>oo biwujoohikum waaydeekum inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAafuwwan ghafoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  O ye who believe!<br> Approach not prayers<br> With a mind befogged,<br> Until ye can understand<br> All that ye say,<br> Nor in a state<br> Of ceremonial impurity<br> (Except when travelling on the road),<br> Until after washing<br> Your whole body.<br> If ye are ill,<br> Or on a journey,<br> Or one of you cometh<br> From offices of nature,<br>Or ye have been<br> In contact with women,<br> And ye find no water,<br> Then take for yourselves<br> Clean sand or earth,<br> And rub therewith<br> Your faces and hands.<br> For God doth blot out sins<br> And forgive again and again.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo na<u>s</u>eeban mina alkit<u>a</u>bi yashtaroona a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lata wayureedoona an ta<u>d</u>illoo a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to those<br> Who were given a portion<br> Of the Book? They traffic<br> In error, and wish that ye<br> Should lose the right path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bi-aAAd<u>a</u>-ikum wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi waliyyan wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  But God hath full knowledge<br> Of your enemies:<br> God is enough for a Protector,<br> And God is enough for a Helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Mina alla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo yu<u>h</u>arrifoona alkalima AAan maw<u>ad</u>iAAihi wayaqooloona samiAAn<u>a</u> waAAa<u>s</u>ayn<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>smaAA ghayra musmaAAin war<u>a</u>AAin<u>a</u> layyan bi-alsinatihim wa<u>t</u>aAAnan fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni walaw annahum q<u>a</u>loo samiAAn<u>a</u> waa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>smaAA wa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>urn<u>a</u> lak<u>a</u>na khayran lahum waaqwama wal<u>a</u>kin laAAanahumu All<u>a</u>hu bikufrihim fal<u>a</u> yu/minoona ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Of the Jews there are those<br> Who displace words<br> From their (right) places,<br> And say: "We hear<br> And we disobey";<br> And "Hear what is not<br> Heard"; and "Rā’inā";—<br> With a twist of their tongues<br> And a slander to Faith.<br> If only they had said:<br> "We hear and we obey;<br> And "Do hear";<br>And "Do look at us":<br> It would have been better<br> For them, and more proper;<br> But God hath cursed them<br> For their Unbelief; and but few<br> Of them will believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba <u>a</u>minoo bim<u>a</u> nazzaln<u>a</u> mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> maAAakum min qabli an na<u>t</u>misa wujoohan fanaruddah<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> adb<u>a</u>rih<u>a</u> aw nalAAanahum kam<u>a</u> laAAann<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba a<b>l</b>ssabti wak<u>a</u>na amru All<u>a</u>hi mafAAool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  O ye People of the Book!<br> Believe in what We<br> Have (now) revealed, confirming<br> What was (already) with you,<br> Before We change the face and fame<br> Of some (of you) beyond all recognition,<br> And turn them hindwards,<br> Or curse them as We cursed<br> The Sabbath-breakers,<br> For the decision of God<br> Must be carried out.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yaghfiru an yushraka bihi wayaghfiru m<u>a</u> doona <u>tha</u>lika liman yash<u>a</u>o waman yushrik bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi faqadi iftar<u>a</u> ithman AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  God forgiveth not<br> That partners should be set up<br> With Him; but He forgiveth<br> Anything else, to whom<br> He pleaseth; to set up<br> Partners with God<br> Is to devise a sin<br> Most heinous indeed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yuzakkoona anfusahum bali All<u>a</u>hu yuzakkee man yash<u>a</u>o wal<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoona fateel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to those<br> Who claim sanctity<br> For themselves?<br>Nay—but God<br> Doth sanctify<br> Whom He pleaseth.<br> But never will they<br> Fail to receive justice<br> In the least little thing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  On<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa yaftaroona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba wakaf<u>a</u> bihi ithman mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Behold! how they invent<br> A lie against God!<br> But that by itself<br> Is a manifest sin!</p> <div class="filenav">
