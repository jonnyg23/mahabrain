 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Y<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmatiya allatee anAAamtu AAalaykum waannee fa<u>dd</u>altukum AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  O Children of Israel! call to mind<br> The (special) favour which I bestowed<br> Upon you, and that I preferred you<br> To all others (for My Message).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo yawman l<u>a</u> tajzee nafsun AAan nafsin shay-an wal<u>a</u> yuqbalu minh<u>a</u> shaf<u>a</u>AAatun wal<u>a</u> yu/kha<u>th</u>u minh<u>a</u> AAadlun wal<u>a</u> hum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Then guard yourselves against a day<br> When one soul shall not avail another<br> Nor shall intercession be accepted for her,<br> Nor shall compensation be taken from her,<br> Nor shall anyone be helped (from outside).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wa-i<u>th</u> najjayn<u>a</u>kum min <u>a</u>li firAAawna yasoomoonakum soo-a alAAa<u>tha</u>bi yu<u>th</u>abbi<u>h</u>oona abn<u>a</u>akum wayasta<u>h</u>yoona nis<u>a</u>akum wafee <u>tha</u>likum bal<u>a</u>on min rabbikum AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And remember, We delivered you<br> From the people of Pharaoh: they set you<br> Hard tasks and punishments, slaughtered<br>Your sons and let your women-folk live;<br> Therein was a tremendous trial from your Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wa-i<u>th</u> faraqn<u>a</u> bikumu alba<u>h</u>ra faanjayn<u>a</u>kum waaghraqn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>la firAAawna waantum tan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And remember We divided<br> The Sea for you and saved you<br> And drowned Pharaoh's people<br> Within your very sight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wa-i<u>th</u> w<u>a</u>AAadn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> arbaAAeena laylatan thumma ittakha<u>th</u>tumu alAAijla min baAAdihi waantum <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And remember We appointed<br> Forty nights for Moses,<br> And in his absence ye took<br> The calf (for worship),<br> And ye did grievous wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Thumma AAafawn<u>a</u> AAankum min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Even then We did forgive you;<br> There was a chance for you<br> To be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wa-i<u>th</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>lfurq<u>a</u>na laAAallakum tahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  And remember We gave<br> Moses the Scripture and the Criterion<br>(Between right and wrong): there was<br> A chance for you to be guided aright.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> liqawmihi y<u>a</u> qawmi innakum <i><u>th</u></i>alamtum anfusakum bi<b>i</b>ttikh<u>ath</u>ikumu alAAijla fatooboo il<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>ri-ikum fa<b>o</b>qtuloo anfusakum <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum AAinda b<u>a</u>ri-ikum fat<u>a</u>ba AAalaykum innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ttaww<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  And remember Moses said<br> To his people: "O my people!<br> Ye have indeed wronged<br> Yourselves by your worship of the calf:<br> So turn (in repentance) to your Maker,<br> And slay yourselves (the wrong-doers);<br> That will be better for you<br> In the sight of your Maker."<br> Then He turned towards you (in forgiveness):<br> For He is Oft-Returning, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wa-i<u>th</u> qultum y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> lan nu/mina laka <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha jahratan faakha<u>th</u>atkumu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>AAiqatu waantum tan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  And remember ye said: "O Moses!<br> We shall never believe in thee<br> Until we see God manifestly,"<br> But ye were dazed<br> With thunder and lightning<br> Even as ye looked on.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Thumma baAAathn<u>a</u>kum min baAAdi mawtikum laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Then We raised you up<br> After your death:<br> Ye had the chance<br> To be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Wa<i><u>th</u></i>allaln<u>a</u> AAalaykumu algham<u>a</u>ma waanzaln<u>a</u> AAalaykumu almanna wa<b>al</b>ssalw<u>a</u> kuloo min <u>t</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamoon<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  And We gave you the shade of clouds<br>And sent down to you<br> Manna and quails, saying:<br> "Eat of the good things<br> We have provided fcr you:"<br> (But they rebelled);<br> To Us they did no harm,<br> But they harmed their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa-i<u>th</u> quln<u>a</u> odkhuloo h<u>ath</u>ihi alqaryata fakuloo minh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>aythu shi/tum raghadan wa<b>o</b>dkhuloo alb<u>a</u>ba sujjadan waqooloo <u>h</u>i<u>tt</u>atun naghfir lakum kha<u>ta</u>y<u>a</u>kum wasanazeedu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And remember We said:<br> "Enter this town, and eat<br> Of the plenty therein<br> As ye wish; but enter<br> The gate with humility,<br> In posture and in words,<br> And We shall forgive you your faults<br> And increase (the portion of)<br> Those who do good."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Fabaddala alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo qawlan ghayra alla<u>th</u>ee qeela lahum faanzaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo rijzan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafsuqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  But the transgressors<br> Changed the word from that<br> Which had been given them;<br> So We sent on the transgt essors<br> A plague from heaven,<br> For that they infringed<br> (Our command) repeatedly.</p> <div class="filenav">
