 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>lati almal<u>a</u>-ikatu y<u>a</u> maryamu inna All<u>a</u>ha i<u>st</u>af<u>a</u>ki wa<u>t</u>ahharaki wa<b>i</b><u>st</u>af<u>a</u>ki AAal<u>a</u> nis<u>a</u>-i alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Behold! the angels said:<br> "O Mary! God hath chosen thee<br> And purified thee—chosen thee<br> Above the women of all nations.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Y<u>a</u> maryamu oqnutee lirabbiki wa<b>o</b>sjudee wa<b>i</b>rkaAAee maAAa a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>kiAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  "O Mary! worship<br> Thy Lord devoutly:<br> Prostrate thyself,<br> And bow down (in prayer)<br> With those who bow down."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  <u>Tha</u>lika min anb<u>a</u>-i alghaybi noo<u>h</u>eehi ilayka wam<u>a</u> kunta ladayhim i<u>th</u> yulqoona aql<u>a</u>mahum ayyuhum yakfulu maryama wam<u>a</u> kunta ladayhim i<u>th</u> yakhta<u>s</u>imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  This is part of the tidings<br> Of the things unseen,<br> Which We reveal unto thee<br> (O Apostle!) by inspiration:<br> Thou wast not with them<br> When they cast lots<br> With arrows, as to which<br> Of them should be charged<br> With the care of Mary:<br> Nor wast thou with them<br> When they disputed (the point).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>lati almal<u>a</u>-ikatu y<u>a</u> maryamu inna All<u>a</u>ha yubashshiruki bikalimatin minhu ismuhu almasee<u>h</u>u AAees<u>a</u> ibnu maryama wajeehan fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wamina almuqarrabeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Behold! the angels said:<br> "O Mary! God giveth thee<br> Glad tidings of a Word<br> From Him: his name<br> Will be Christ Jesus,<br> The son of Mary, held in honour<br> In this world and the Hereafter<br> And of (the company of) those<br> Nearest to God;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wayukallimu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa fee almahdi wakahlan wamina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  "He shall speak to the people<br> In childhood and in maturity.<br> And he shall be (of the company)<br> Of the righteous."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Q<u>a</u>lat rabbi ann<u>a</u> yakoonu lee waladun walam yamsasnee basharun q<u>a</u>la ka<u>tha</u>liki All<u>a</u>hu yakhluqu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o i<u>tha</u> qa<u>da</u> amran fa-innam<u>a</u> yaqoolu lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  She said: "O my Lord!<br> How shall I have a son<br> When no man hath touched me?"<br> He said: "Even so:<br> God createth<br> What He willeth:<br> When He hath decreed<br> A Plan, He but saith<br> To it, 'Be,' and it is!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  WayuAAallimuhu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wa<b>al</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>l-injeel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  "And God will teach him<br> The Book and Wisdom,<br> The Law and the Gospel,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Warasoolan il<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela annee qad ji/tukum bi-<u>a</u>yatin min rabbikum annee akhluqu lakum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>eeni kahay-ati a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayri faanfukhu feehi fayakoonu <u>t</u>ayran bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waobri-o al-akmaha wa<b>a</b>l-abra<u>s</u>a wao<u>h</u>yee almawt<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waonabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> ta/kuloona wam<u>a</u> taddakhiroona fee buyootikum inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan lakum in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  "And (appoint him)<br> An apostle to the Children<br> Of Israel, (with this message):<br> "'I have come to you,<br> With a Sign from your Lord,<br> In that I make for you<br> Out of clay, as it were,<br> The figure of a bird,<br> And breathe into it,<br> And it becomes a bird<br> By God's leave:<br> And I heal those<br> Born blind, and the lepers,<br> And I quicken the dead,<br> By God's leave;<br> And I declare to you<br> What ye eat, and what ye store<br> In your houses. Surely<br> Therein is a Sign for you<br> If ye did believe;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wamu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayya mina a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti wali-o<u>h</u>illa lakum baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee <u>h</u>urrima AAalaykum waji/tukum bi-<u>a</u>yatin min rabbikum fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  "'(I have come to you),<br> To attest the Law<br> Which was before me.<br> And to make lawful<br> To you part of what was<br> (Before) forbidden to you;<br> I have come to you<br> With a Sign from your Lord.<br> So fear God,<br> And obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha rabbee warabbukum fa<b>o</b>AAbudoohu h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un mustaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "'It is God<br> Who is my Lord<br> And your Lord;<br> Then worship Him.<br> This is a Way<br> That is straight.'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Falamm<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>assa AAees<u>a</u> minhumu alkufra q<u>a</u>la man an<u>sa</u>ree il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>la al<u>h</u>aw<u>a</u>riyyoona na<u>h</u>nu an<u>sa</u>ru All<u>a</u>hi <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>i</b>shhad bi-ann<u>a</u> muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  When Jesus found<br> Unbelief on their part<br> He said: "Who will be<br> My helpers to (the work<br> Of) God?" Said the Disciples:<br> "We are God's helpers:<br> We believe in God,<br> And do thou bear witness<br> That we are Muslims.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> anzalta wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasoola fa<b>o</b>ktubn<u>a</u> maAAa a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  "Our Lord! we believe<br> In what Thou hast revealed,<br> And we follow the Apostle;<br> Then write us down<br> Among those who bear witness."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wamakaroo wamakara All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khayru alm<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  And (the unbelievers)<br> Plotted and planned,<br> And God too planned,<br> And the best of planners<br> Is God.</p> <div class="filenav">
