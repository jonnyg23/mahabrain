 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LI.: Zāriyāt, or the Winds That Scatter.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b><u>ththa</u>riy<u>a</u>ti <u>th</u>arw<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the (Winds)<br> That scatter broadcast;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fa<b>a</b>l<u>ha</u>mil<u>a</u>ti wiqr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And those that<br> Lift and bear away<br> Heavy weights;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Fa<b>a</b>lj<u>a</u>riy<u>a</u>ti yusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And those that<br> Flow with ease<br> And gentleness;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Fa<b>a</b>lmuqassim<u>a</u>ti amr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And those that<br> Distribute and apportion<br> By Command;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Innam<u>a</u> tooAAadoona la<u>sa</u>diq<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Verily that which ye<br> Are promised is true;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-inna a<b>l</b>ddeena law<u>a</u>qiAA<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And verily Judgment<br> And Justice must<br> Indeed come to pass.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i <u>tha</u>ti al<u>h</u>ubuk<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  By the Sky<br> With (its) numerous Paths,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Innakum lafee qawlin mukhtalif<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Truly ye are in<br> A doctrine discordant,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Yu/faku AAanhu man ofik<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Through which are deluded (away<br> From the Truth) such<br> As would be deluded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Qutila alkharr<u>as</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Woe to the falsehood-mongers,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Alla<u>th</u>eena hum fee ghamratin s<u>a</u>hoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Those who (flounder) heedless<br> In a flood of confusion:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Yas-aloona ayy<u>a</u>na yawmu a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  They ask, "When will be<br> The Day of Judgment<br> And Justice?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Yawma hum AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri yuftanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  (It will be) a Day<br> When they will be tried<br> (And tested) over the Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  <u>Th</u>ooqoo fitnatakum h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee kuntum bihi tastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "Taste ye your trial!<br> This is what ye used<br> To ask to be hastened!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Inna almuttaqeena fee jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  As to the Righteous,<br> They will be in the midst<br> Of Gardens and Springs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  <u>A</u>khi<u>th</u>eena m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum rabbuhum innahum k<u>a</u>noo qabla <u>tha</u>lika mu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Taking joy in the things<br> Which their Lord gives them,<br> Because, before then, they<br> Lived a good life.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  K<u>a</u>noo qaleelan mina allayli m<u>a</u> yahjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  They were in the habit<br> Of sleeping but little<br> By night,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wabi<b>a</b>l-as<u>ha</u>ri hum yastaghfiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  And in the hours<br> Of early dawn,<br>They (were found) praying<br> For Forgiveness;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wafee amw<u>a</u>lihim <u>h</u>aqqun li<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>-ili wa<b>a</b>lma<u>h</u>room<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And in their wealth<br> And possessions (was remembered)<br> The right of the (needy,)<br> Him who asked, and him<br> Who (for some reason) was<br> Prevented (from asking).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wafee al-ar<u>d</u>i <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun lilmooqineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  On the earth<br> Are Signs for those<br> Of assured Faith,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wafee anfusikum afal<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  As also in your, own<br> Selves: will ye not<br> Then see?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wafee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i rizqukum wam<u>a</u> tooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And in heaven is<br> Your Sustenance, as (also)<br> That which ye are promised.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Fawarabbi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i innahu la<u>h</u>aqqun mithla m<u>a</u> annakum tan<u>t</u>iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Then, by the Lord<br> Of heaven and earth,<br> This is the very Truth,<br> As much as the fact<br> That ye can speak<br> Intelligently to each other.</p> <div class="filenav">
