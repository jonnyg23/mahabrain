 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Iqtaraba li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>buhum wahum fee ghaflatin muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Closer and closer to mankind<br> Comes their Reckoning: yet they<br> Heed not and they turn away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> ya/teehim min <u>th</u>ikrin min rabbihim mu<u>h</u>dathin ill<u>a</u> istamaAAoohu wahum yalAAaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Never comes (aught) to them<br> Of a renewed Message<br> From their Lord, but they<br> Listen to it as in jest,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  L<u>a</u>hiyatan quloobuhum waasarroo a<b>l</b>nnajw<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo hal h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum afata/toona a<b>l</b>ssi<u>h</u>ra waantum tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Their hearts toying as with<br> Trifles. The wrong-doers conceal<br> Their private counsels, (saying),<br> "Is this (one) more than<br> A man like yourselves?<br> Will ye go to witchcraft<br> With your eyes open?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbee yaAAlamu alqawla fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Say: "My Lord<br> Knoweth (every) word (spoken)<br> In the heavens and on earth:<br> He is the One that heareth<br> And knoweth (all things)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Bal q<u>a</u>loo a<u>d</u>gh<u>a</u>thu a<u>h</u>l<u>a</u>min bali iftar<u>a</u>hu bal huwa sh<u>a</u>AAirun falya/tin<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>yatin kam<u>a</u> orsila al-awwaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  "Nay," they say, "(these are)<br> Medleys of dreams!—Nay,<br> He forged it!—Nay,<br> He is (but) a poet!<br> Let him then bring us<br> A Sign like the ones<br> That were sent to<br> (Prophets) of old!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  M<u>a</u> <u>a</u>manat qablahum min qaryatin ahlakn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> afahum yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  (As to those) before them,<br> Not one of the populations<br> Which We destroyed believed:<br> Will these believe?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> qablaka ill<u>a</u> rij<u>a</u>lan noo<u>h</u>ee ilayhim fa<b>i</b>s-aloo ahla a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri in kuntum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Before thee, also, the apostles<br> We sent were but men,<br> To whom We granted inspiration:<br> If ye realise this not, ask<br> Of those who possess the Message.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wam<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u>hum jasadan l<u>a</u> ya/kuloona a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>aAA<u>a</u>ma wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo kh<u>a</u>lideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Nor did We give them<br> Bodies that ate no food,<br> Nor were they exempt from death.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Thumma <u>s</u>adaqn<u>a</u>humu alwaAAda faanjayn<u>a</u>hum waman nash<u>a</u>o waahlakn<u>a</u> almusrifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  In the end We fulfilled<br> To them Our promise,<br> And We saved them<br> And those whom We pleased,<br>But We destroyed those<br> Who transgressed beyond bounds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Laqad anzaln<u>a</u> ilaykum kit<u>a</u>ban feehi <u>th</u>ikrukum afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  We have revealed for you<br> (O men!) a book in which<br> Is a Message for you:<br> Will ye not then understand?</p> <div class="filenav">
