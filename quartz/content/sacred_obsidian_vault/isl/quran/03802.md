 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVIII.: Ṣād (being one of the Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wam<u>a</u> yan<i><u>th</u></i>uru h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i ill<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atan w<u>ah</u>idatan m<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> min faw<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  These (to-day) only wait<br> For a single mighty Blast,<br> Which (when it comes)<br> Will brook no delay.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waq<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> AAajjil lan<u>a</u> qi<u>tt</u>an<u>a</u> qabla yawmi al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  They say: "Our Lord!<br> Hasten to us our sentence<br> (Even) before the Day<br> Of Account!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  I<u>s</u>bir AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yaqooloona wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur AAabdan<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>wooda <u>tha</u> al-aydi innahu aww<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Have patience at what they<br> Say, and remember Our Servant<br> David, the man of strength:<br> For he ever turned (to God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Inn<u>a</u> sakhkharn<u>a</u> aljib<u>a</u>la maAAahu yusabbi<u>h</u>na bi<b>a</b>lAAashiyyi wa<b>a</b>l-ishr<u>a</u>q<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  It was We that made<br> The hills declare,<br> In unison with him,<br> Our Praises, at eventide<br> And at break of day,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayra ma<u>h</u>shooratan kullun lahu aww<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And the birds gathered<br> (In assemblies): all with him<br> Did turn (to God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Washadadn<u>a</u> mulkahu wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu al<u>h</u>ikmata wafa<u>s</u>la alkhi<u>ta</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  We strengthened his kingdom,<br> And gave him wisdom<br> And sound judgment<br> In speech and decision.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wahal at<u>a</u>ka nabao alkha<u>s</u>mi i<u>th</u> tasawwaroo almi<u>h</u>r<u>a</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Has the Story of<br> The Disputants reached thee?<br> Behold, they climbed over<br> The wall of the private chamber;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Ith dakhaloo AAal<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>wooda fafaziAAa minhum q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> takhaf kha<u>s</u>m<u>a</u>ni bagh<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>un<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in fa<b>o</b><u>h</u>kum baynan<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u> tush<u>t</u>i<u>t</u> wa<b>i</b>hdin<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> saw<u>a</u>-i a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u><b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  When they entered<br> The presence of David,<br> And he was terrified<br> Of them, they said:<br> "Fear not: we are two<br> Disputants, one of whom<br> Has wronged the other:<br> Decide now between us<br> With truth, and treat us not<br> With unjustice, but guide us<br> To the even Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Inna h<u>atha</u> akhee lahu tisAAun watisAAoona naAAjatan waliya naAAjatun w<u>ah</u>idatun faq<u>a</u>la akfilneeh<u>a</u> waAAazzanee fee alkhi<u>ta</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "This man is my brother:<br> He has nine and ninety<br> Ewes, and I have (but) one:<br> Yet he says, "Commit her<br> To my care," and is (moreover)<br> Harsh to me in speech."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Q<u>a</u>la laqad <i><u>th</u></i>alamaka bisu-<u>a</u>li naAAjatika il<u>a</u> niAA<u>a</u>jihi wa-inna katheeran mina alkhula<u>ta</u>-i layabghee baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti waqaleelun m<u>a</u> hum wa<i><u>th</u></i>anna d<u>a</u>woodu annam<u>a</u> fatann<u>a</u>hu fa<b>i</b>staghfara rabbahu wakharra r<u>a</u>kiAAan waan<u>a</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  (David) said: "He has<br> Undoubtedly wronged thee<br> In demanding thy (single) ewe<br> To be added to his (flock<br> Of) ewes: truly many<br> Are the Partners (in business)<br> Who wrong each other:<br> Not so do those who believe<br> And work deeds of righteousness,<br> And how few are they?"…<br> And David gathered that we<br> Had tried him: he asked<br> Forgiveness of his Lord,<br> Fell down, bowing<br> (In prostration), and turned<br> (To God in repentance).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Faghafarn<u>a</u> lahu <u>tha</u>lika wa-inna lahu AAindan<u>a</u> lazulf<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>usna ma<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  So We forgave him<br> This (lapse): he enjoyed,<br> Indeed, a Near Approach to Us,<br> And a beautiful Place<br> Of (final) Return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Y<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>woodu inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u>ka khaleefatan fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b><u>h</u>kum bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u> tattabiAAi alhaw<u>a</u> fayu<u>d</u>illaka AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi inna alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>d</u>illoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeedun bim<u>a</u> nasoo yawma al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
