 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Tilka a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>ru al-<u>a</u>khiratu najAAaluh<u>a</u> lilla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yureedoona AAuluwwan fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> fas<u>a</u>dan wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>qibatu lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  What Home of the Hereafter<br> We shall give to those<br> Who intend not high-handedness<br> Or mischief on earth:<br>And the End is (best)<br> For the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Man j<u>a</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>asanati falahu khayrun minh<u>a</u> waman j<u>a</u>a bi<b>al</b>ssayyi-ati fal<u>a</u> yujz<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena AAamiloo a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  If any does good, the reward<br> To him is better than<br> His deed; but if any<br> Does evil, the doers of evil<br> Are only punished (to the extent)<br> Of their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Inna alla<u>th</u>ee fara<u>d</u>a AAalayka alqur-<u>a</u>na lar<u>a</u>dduka il<u>a</u> maAA<u>a</u>din qul rabbee aAAlamu man j<u>a</u>a bi<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> waman huwa fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  Verily He Who ordained<br> The Qur-ān for thee, will bring<br> Thee back to the Place<br> Of Return. Say: "My Lord<br> Knows best who it is<br> That brings true guidance,<br> And who is in manifest error."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wam<u>a</u> kunta tarjoo an yulq<u>a</u> ilayka alkit<u>a</u>bu ill<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan min rabbika fal<u>a</u> takoonanna <i><u>th</u></i>aheeran lilk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  And thou hadst not expected<br> That the Book would be<br> Sent to thee except as<br> A Mercy from thy Lord:<br> Therefore lend not thou support<br> In any way to those<br>Who reject (God's Message).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>uddunnaka AAan <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi baAAda i<u>th</u> onzilat ilayka wa<b>o</b>dAAu il<u>a</u> rabbika wal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  And let nothing keep thee<br> Back from the Signs of God<br> After they have been revealed<br> To thee: and invite (men)<br> To thy Lord, and be not<br> Of the company of those<br> Who join gods with God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Wal<u>a</u> tadAAu maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa kullu shay-in h<u>a</u>likun ill<u>a</u> wajhahu lahu al<u>h</u>ukmu wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>88.  And call not, besides God,<br> On another god. There is<br> No god but He. Everything<br> (That exists) will perish<br> Except His own Face<br> To Him belongs the Command,<br> And to Him will ye<br> (All) be brought back.</p>  <div class="filenav">
