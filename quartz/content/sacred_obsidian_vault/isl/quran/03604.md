 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVI.: Yā-Sīn (being Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wanufikha fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori fa-i<u>tha</u> hum mina al-ajd<u>a</u>thi il<u>a</u> rabbihim yansiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  The trumpet shall be<br> Sounded, when behold!<br> From the sepulchres (men)<br> Will rush forth<br> To their Lord!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> waylan<u>a</u> man baAAathan<u>a</u> min marqadin<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> waAAada a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu wa<u>s</u>adaqa almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  They will say: "Ah!<br> Woe unto us! Who<br> Hath raised us up<br> From our beds of repose?"<br> (A voice will say:)<br> "This is what (God)<br> Most Gracious had promised.<br> And true was the word<br> Of the apostles!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  In k<u>a</u>nat ill<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atan w<u>ah</u>idatan fa-i<u>tha</u> hum jameeAAun ladayn<u>a</u> mu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  It will be no more<br> Than a single Blast,<br> When to! they will all<br> Be brought up before Us!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Fa<b>a</b>lyawma l<u>a</u> tu<i><u>th</u></i>lamu nafsun shay-an wal<u>a</u> tujzawna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Then, on that Day,<br> Not a soul will be<br> Wronged in the least,<br> And ye shall but<br> Be repaid the meeds<br> Of your past Deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Inna a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba aljannati alyawma fee shughulin f<u>a</u>kihoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Verily the Companions<br> Of the Garden shall<br> That Day have joy<br> In all that they do:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Hum waazw<u>a</u>juhum fee <i><u>th</u></i>il<u>a</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>a</u>-iki muttaki-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  They and their associates<br> Will be in groves<br> Of (cool) shade, reclining<br> On Thrones (of dignity);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Lahum feeh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun walahum m<u>a</u> yaddaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  (Every) fruit (enjoyment)<br> Will be there for them;<br> They shall have whatever<br> They call for;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Sal<u>a</u>mun qawlan min rabbin ra<u>h</u>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  "Peace!"—a Word<br> (Of salutation) from a Lord<br> Most Merciful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Wa<b>i</b>mt<u>a</u>zoo alyawma ayyuh<u>a</u> almujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  "And O ye in sin!<br> Get ye apart this Day!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Alam aAAhad ilaykum y<u>a</u> banee <u>a</u>dama an l<u>a</u> taAAbudoo a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na innahu lakum AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  "Did I not enjoin<br> On you, O ye children<br>Of Adam, that ye<br> Should not worship Satan;<br> For that he was to you<br> An enemy avowed?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Waani oAAbudoonee h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un mustaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  "And that ye should<br> Worship Me, (for that) this<br> Was the Straight Way?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Walaqad a<u>d</u>alla minkum jibillan katheeran afalam takoonoo taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  "But he did lead astray<br> A great multitude of you.<br> Did ye not, then, understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  H<u>ath</u>ihi jahannamu allatee kuntum tooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  "This is the Hell<br> Of which ye were<br> (Repeatedly) warned!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  I<u>s</u>lawh<u>a</u> alyawma bim<u>a</u> kuntum takfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  "Embrace ye the (Fire)<br> This Day, for that ye<br> (Persistently) rejected (Truth)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Alyawma nakhtimu AAal<u>a</u> afw<u>a</u>hihim watukallimun<u>a</u> aydeehim watashhadu arjuluhum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  That Day shall We set<br> A seal on their mouths.<br> But their hands will speak<br> To Us, and their feet<br> Bear witness, to all<br> That they did.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Walaw nash<u>a</u>o la<u>t</u>amasn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> aAAyunihim fa<b>i</b>stabaqoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>a faann<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  If it had been Our Will,<br> We could surely have<br> Blotted out their eyes;<br> Then should they have<br> Run about groping for the Path,<br> But how could they have seen?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Walaw nash<u>a</u>o lamasakhn<u>a</u>hum AAal<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>natihim fam<u>a</u> ista<u>ta</u>AAoo mu<u>d</u>iyyan wal<u>a</u> yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  And if it had been<br> Our Will, We could<br> Have transformed them<br> (To remain) in their places;<br> Then should they have been<br> Unable to move about,<br> Nor could they have returned<br> (After error).</p> <div class="filenav">
