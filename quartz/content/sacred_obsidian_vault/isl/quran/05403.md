 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIV.: Qamar, or the Moon.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Walaqad j<u>a</u>a <u>a</u>la firAAawna a<b>l</b>nnu<u>th</u>ur<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  To the People<br> Of Pharaoh, too, aforetime,<br> Came Warners (from God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> kullih<u>a</u> faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum akh<u>th</u>a AAazeezin muqtadir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  The (people) rejected all<br> Our Signs; but We<br> Seized them with such Penalty<br> (As comes) from One<br> Exalted in Power,<br> Able to carry out His Will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Akuff<u>a</u>rukum khayrun min ol<u>a</u>-ikum am lakum bar<u>a</u>atun fee a<b>l</b>zzubur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Are your Unbelievers,<br> (O Quraish), better than they?<br> Or have ye an immunity<br> In the Sacred Books?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Am yaqooloona na<u>h</u>nu jameeAAun munta<u>s</u>ir<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Or do they say:<br> "We acting together<br> Can defend ourselves"?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Sayuhzamu aljamAAu wayuwalloona a<b>l</b>ddubur<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Soon will their multitude<br> Be put to flight,<br> And they will show<br> Their backs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Bali a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu mawAAiduhum wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu adh<u>a</u> waamar<b>ru</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Nay, the Hour (of Judgment)<br> Is the time promised them<br> (For their full recompense):<br> And that Hour will be<br> Most grievous and most bitter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Inna almujrimeena fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin wasuAAur<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Truly those in sin<br> Are the ones<br> Straying in mind, and mad.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Yawma yus<u>h</u>aboona fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri AAal<u>a</u> wujoohihim <u>th</u>ooqoo massa saqar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  The Day they will be<br> Dragged through the Fire<br> On their faces, (they<br> Will hear:) "Taste ye<br> The touch of Hell!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Inn<u>a</u> kulla shay-in khalaqn<u>a</u>hu biqadar<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Verily, all things<br> Have We created<br> In proportion and measure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wam<u>a</u> amrun<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> w<u>ah</u>idatun kalam<u>h</u>in bi<b>a</b>lba<u>s</u>ar<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And Our Command<br> Is but a single (Act),—<br> Like the twinkling<br> Of an eye.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Walaqad ahlakn<u>a</u> ashy<u>a</u>AAakum fahal min muddakir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And (oft) in the past,<br> Have We destroyed gangs<br> Like unto you: then<br> Is there any that<br> Will receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wakullu shay-in faAAaloohu fee a<b>l</b>zzubur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  All that they do<br> Is noted in (their)<br> Books (of Deeds):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wakullu <u>s</u>agheerin wakabeerin musta<u>t</u>ar<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Every matter, small and great,<br> Is on record.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Inna almuttaqeena fee jann<u>a</u>tin wanahar<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  As to the Righteous,<br> They will be in the midst<br> Of Gardens and Rivers,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fee maqAAadi <u>s</u>idqin AAinda maleekin muqtadir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  In an Assembly of Truth,<br> In the Presence of<br> A Sovereign Omnipotent.</p>  <div class="filenav">
