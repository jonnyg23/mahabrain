 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Qul lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in yantahoo yughfar lahum m<u>a</u> qad salafa wa-in yaAAoodoo faqad ma<u>d</u>at sunnatu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Say to the Unbelievers,<br> If (now) they desist (from Unbelief),<br> Their past would be forgiven them;<br> But if they persist, the punishment<br> Of those before them is already<br> (A matter of warning for them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Waq<u>a</u>tiloohum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> takoona fitnatun wayakoona a<b>l</b>ddeenu kulluhu lill<u>a</u>hi fa-ini intahaw fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  And fight them on<br> Until there is no <a href="errata.htm#2">more</a><br> Tumult or oppression,<br> And there prevail<br> Justice and faith in God<br> Altogether and everywhere;<br> But if they cease, verily God<br> Doth see all that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wa-in tawallaw fa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha mawl<u>a</u>kum niAAma almawl<u>a</u> waniAAma a<b>l</b>nna<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  If they refuse, be sure<br> That God is your Protector—<br> The Best to protect<br> And the Best to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annam<u>a</u> ghanimtum min shay-in faanna lill<u>a</u>hi khumusahu wali<b>l</b>rrasooli wali<u>th</u>ee alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keeni wa<b>i</b>bni a<b>l</b>ssabeeli in kuntum <u>a</u>mantum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> AAabdin<u>a</u> yawma alfurq<u>a</u>ni yawma iltaq<u>a</u> aljamAA<u>a</u>ni wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  And know that out of<br> All the booty that ye<br> May acquire (in war),<br> A fifth share is assigned<br> To God,—and to the Apostle,<br> And to near relatives,<br> Orphans, the needy,<br> And the wayfarer,<br> If ye do believe in God<br> And in the revelation<br> We sent down to Our Servant<br> On the Day of Testing,—<br> The Day of the meeting<br> Of the two forces.<br> For God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  I<u>th</u> antum bi<b>a</b>lAAudwati a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wahum bi<b>a</b>lAAudwati alqu<u>s</u>w<u>a</u> wa<b>al</b>rrakbu asfala minkum walaw taw<u>a</u>AAadtum la<b>i</b>khtalaftum fee almeeAA<u>a</u>di wal<u>a</u>kin liyaq<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu amran k<u>a</u>na mafAAoolan liyahlika man halaka AAan bayyinatin waya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> man <u>h</u>ayya AAan bayyinatin wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha lasameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Remember ye were<br> On the hither side<br> Of the valley, and they<br> On the farther side,<br> And the caravan<br> On lower ground than ye.<br> Even if ye had made<br> A mutual appointment<br> To meet, ye would certainly:<br> Have failed in the appointment:<br>But (thus ye met),<br> That God might accomplish<br> A matter already enacted;<br> That those who died might<br> Die after a clear Sign<br> (Had been given), and those who lived<br> Might live after a Clear Sign<br> (Had been given). And verily<br> God is He who heareth<br> And knoweth (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  I<u>th</u> yureekahumu All<u>a</u>hu fee man<u>a</u>mika qaleelan walaw ar<u>a</u>kahum katheeran lafashiltum walatan<u>a</u>zaAAtum fee al-amri wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha sallama innahu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Remember in thy dream<br> God showed them to thee<br> As few: if He had shown<br> Them to thee as many,<br> Ye would surely have been<br> Discouraged, and ye would<br> Surely have disputed<br> In (your) decision: but God<br> Saved (you): for He knoweth<br> Well the (secrets) of (all) hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wa-i<u>th</u> yureekumoohum i<u>th</u>i iltaqaytum fee aAAyunikum qaleelan wayuqallilukum fee aAAyunihim liyaq<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu amran k<u>a</u>na mafAAoolan wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi turjaAAu al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  And remember when ye met,<br> He showed them to you<br> As few in your eyes,<br> And He made you appear<br> As contemptible in their eyes:<br> That God might accomplish<br> A matter already enacted.<br> For to God do all questions<br> Go back (for decision).</p> <div class="filenav">
