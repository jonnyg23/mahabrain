 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Waq<u>a</u>la almaliku i/toonee bihi falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahu a<b>l</b>rrasoolu q<u>a</u>la irjiAA il<u>a</u> rabbika fa<b>i</b>s-alhu m<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>lu a<b>l</b>nniswati all<u>a</u>tee qa<u>tt</u>aAAna aydiyahunna inna rabbee bikaydihinna AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  So the king said:<br> "Bring ye him unto me."<br> But when the messenger<br> Came to him, (Joseph) said:<br> "Go thou back to thy lord,<br> And ask him, "What is<br> The state of mind<br> Of the ladies<br> Who cut their hands?"<br> For my Lord is<br> Certainly well aware<br> Of their snare."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Q<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>bukunna i<u>th</u> r<u>a</u>wadtunna yoosufa AAan nafsihi qulna <u>ha</u>sha lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> AAalimn<u>a</u> AAalayhi min soo-in q<u>a</u>lati imraatu alAAazeezi al-<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>a<u>sh</u>a<u>s</u>a al<u>h</u>aqqu an<u>a</u> r<u>a</u>wadtuhu AAan nafsihi wa-innahu lamina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  (The king) said (to the ladies):<br> "What was your affair<br> When ye did seek to seduce<br> Joseph from his (true) self?"<br> The ladies said: "God<br> Preserve us! no evil<br>Know we against him!"<br> Said the ’Azīz's wife:<br> "Now is the truth manifest<br> (To all): it was I<br> Who sought to seduce him<br> From his (true) self:<br> He is indeed of those<br> Who are (ever) true (and virtuous).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  <u>Tha</u>lika liyaAAlama annee lam akhunhu bi<b>a</b>lghaybi waanna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee kayda alkh<u>a</u>-ineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "This (say I), in order that<br> He may know that I<br> Have never been false<br> To him in his absence,<br> And that God will never<br> Guide the snare of the false ones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wam<u>a</u> obarri-o nafsee inna a<b>l</b>nnafsa laamm<u>a</u>ratun bi<b>al</b>ssoo-i ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>ima rabbee inna rabbee ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un </b>   </p>
 <p>53.  "Nor do I absolve my own self<br> (Of blame): the (human) soul<br> Is certainly prone to evil,<br> Unless my Lord do bestow<br> His Mercy: but surely<br> My Lord is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Waq<u>a</u>la almaliku i/toonee bihi astakhli<u>s</u>hu linafsee falamm<u>a</u> kallamahu q<u>a</u>la innaka alyawma ladayn<u>a</u> makeenun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  So the king said:<br> "Bring him unto me;<br> I will take him specially<br> To serve about my own person."<br> Therefore when he had spoken<br> To him, he said:<br> "Be assured this day,<br> Thou art, before our own Presence,<br> With rank firmly established,<br> And fidelity fully proved!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Q<u>a</u>la ijAAalnee AAal<u>a</u> khaz<u>a</u>-ini al-ar<u>d</u>i innee <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i>un AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  (Joseph) said: "Set me<br> Over the store-houses<br> Of the land: I will<br> Indeed guard them,<br>As one that knows<br> (Their importance)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika makann<u>a</u> liyoosufa fee al-ar<u>d</u>i yatabawwao minh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>aythu yash<u>a</u>o nu<u>s</u>eebu bira<u>h</u>matin<u>a</u> man nash<u>a</u>o wal<u>a</u> nu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  "Thus did we give<br> Established power to Joseph<br> In the land, to take possession<br> Therein as, when, or where<br> He pleased. We bestow<br> Of Our mercy on whom<br> We please, and We suffer not,<br> To be lost, the reward<br> Of those who do good.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Walaajru al-<u>a</u>khirati khayrun lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wak<u>a</u>noo yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  But verily the reward<br> Of the Hereafter<br> Is the best, for those<br> Who believe, and are constant<br> In righteousness.</p> <div class="filenav">
