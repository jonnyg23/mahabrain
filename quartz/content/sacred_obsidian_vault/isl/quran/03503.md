 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXV.: Fāṭir, or The Originator of Creation; or Malāïka, or The Angels.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su antumu alfuqar<u>a</u>o il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu huwa alghaniyyu al<u>h</u>ameed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  O ye men! It is<br> Ye that have need<br>Of God: but God is<br> The One Free of all wants,<br> Worthy of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  In yasha/ yu<u>th</u>hibkum waya/ti bikhalqin jadeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  If He so pleased, He<br> Could blot you out<br> And bring in<br> A New Creation.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wam<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi biAAazeez<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Nor is that (at all)<br> Difficult for God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wal<u>a</u> taziru w<u>a</u>ziratun wizra okhr<u>a</u> wa-in tadAAu muthqalatun il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>imlih<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>mal minhu shay-on walaw k<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u> qurb<u>a</u> innam<u>a</u> tun<u>th</u>iru alla<u>th</u>eena yakhshawna rabbahum bi<b>a</b>lghaybi waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta waman tazakk<u>a</u> fa-innam<u>a</u> yatazakk<u>a</u> linafsihi wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Nor can a bearer of burdens<br> Bear another's burden.<br> If one heavily laden should<br> Call another to (bear) his load,<br> Not the least portion of it<br> Can be carried (by the other),<br> Even though he be nearly<br> Related. Thou canst but<br> Admonish such as fear<br> Their Lord unseen<br> And establish regular Prayer.<br> And whoever purifies himself<br>Does so for the benefit<br> Of his own soul; and<br> The destination (of all)<br> Is to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wam<u>a</u> yastawee al-aAAm<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lba<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  The blind and the seeing<br> Are not alike;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>tu wal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Nor are the depths<br> Of Darkness and the Light;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>illu wal<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aroor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Nor are the (chilly) shade<br> And the (genial) heat of the sun:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wam<u>a</u> yastawee al-a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>o wal<u>a</u> al-amw<u>a</u>tu inna All<u>a</u>ha yusmiAAu man yash<u>a</u>o wam<u>a</u> anta bimusmiAAin man fee alquboor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Nor are alike those<br> That are living and those<br> That are dead. God can<br> Make any that He wills<br> To hear; but thou<br> Canst not make those<br> To hear who are<br> (Buried) in graves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  In anta ill<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Thou art no other<br> Than a warner.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi basheeran wana<u>th</u>eeran wa-in min ommatin ill<u>a</u> khal<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Verily We have sent thee<br> In truth, as a bearer<br> Of glad tidings,<br> And as a warner:<br>And there never was<br> A people, without a warner<br> Having lived among them<br> (In the past).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa-in yuka<u>thth</u>ibooka faqad ka<u>thth</u>aba alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim j<u>a</u>at-hum rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti wabi<b>al</b>zzuburi wabi<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi almuneer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And if they reject thee,<br> So did their predecessors,<br> To whom came their apostles<br> With Clear Signs, Books<br> Of dark prophecies,<br> And the Book<br> Of Enlightenment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Thumma akha<u>th</u>tu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fakayfa k<u>a</u>na nakeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  In the end did I<br> Punish those who rejected<br> Faith: and how (terrible)<br> Was My rejection (of them)!</p> <div class="filenav">
