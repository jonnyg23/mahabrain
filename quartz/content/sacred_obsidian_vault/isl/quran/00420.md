 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 20</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo koonoo qaww<u>a</u>meena bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i shuhad<u>a</u>a lill<u>a</u>hi walaw AAal<u>a</u> anfusikum awi alw<u>a</u>lidayni wa<b>a</b>l-aqrabeena in yakun ghaniyyan aw faqeeran fa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu awl<u>a</u> bihim<u>a</u> fal<u>a</u> tattabiAAoo alhaw<u>a</u> an taAAdiloo wa-in talwoo aw tuAAri<u>d</u>oo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  O ye who believe!<br> Stand out firmly<br> For justice, as witnesses<br> To God, even as against<br> Yourselves, or your parents,<br> Or your kin, and whether<br> It be (against) rich or poor:<br> For God can best protect both.<br> Follow not the lusts<br> (Of your hearts), lest ye<br> Swerve, and if ye<br> Distort (justice) or decline<br> To do justice, verily<br> God is well-acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo <u>a</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi alla<u>th</u>ee nazzala AAal<u>a</u> rasoolihi wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi alla<u>th</u>ee anzala min qablu waman yakfur bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wamal<u>a</u>-ikatihi wakutubihi warusulihi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri faqad <u>d</u>alla <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lan baAAeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  O ye who believe!<br> Believe in God<br> And His Apostle,<br> And the scripture which He<br> Hath sent to His Apostle<br> And the scripture which He sent<br> To those before (him)<br> Any who denieth God,<br> His angels, His Books,<br> His Apostles, and the Day<br> Of Judgment, hath gone<br> Far, far astray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo thumma kafaroo thumma <u>a</u>manoo thumma kafaroo thumma izd<u>a</u>doo kufran lam yakuni All<u>a</u>hu liyaghfira lahum wal<u>a</u> liyahdiyahum sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  Those who believe,<br> Then reject Faith,<br> Then believe (again)<br> And (again) reject Faith,<br> And go on increasing<br> In Unbelief,—God<br> Will not forgive them<br> Nor guide them on the Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  Bashshiri almun<u>a</u>fiqeena bi-anna lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>138.  To the Hypocrites give<br> The glad tidings that<br> There is for them<br> (But) a grievous Penalty;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yattakhi<u>th</u>oona alk<u>a</u>fireena awliy<u>a</u>a min dooni almu/mineena ayabtaghoona AAindahumu alAAizzata fa-inna alAAizzata lill<u>a</u>hi jameeAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  Yea, to those who take<br> For friends Unbelievers<br> Rather than Believers:<br> Is it honour they seek<br> Among them? Nay,—<br> All honour is with God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  Waqad nazzala AAalaykum fee alkit<u>a</u>bi an i<u>tha</u> samiAAtum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi yukfaru bih<u>a</u> wayustahzao bih<u>a</u> fal<u>a</u> taqAAudoo maAAahum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yakhoo<u>d</u>oo fee <u>h</u>adeethin ghayrihi innakum i<u>th</u>an mithluhum inna All<u>a</u>ha j<u>a</u>miAAu almun<u>a</u>fiqeena wa<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u>fireena fee jahannama jameeAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  Already has He sent you<br> Word in the Book, that when<br> Ye hear the Signs of God<br> Held in defiance and ridicule,<br> Ye are not to sit with them<br> Unless they turn to a different<br> Theme: if ye did, ye would be<br> Like them. For God will<br> Collect the Hypocrites and those<br> Who defy Faith—all in Hell;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yatarabba<u>s</u>oona bikum fa-in k<u>a</u>na lakum fat<u>h</u>un mina All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>loo alam nakun maAAakum wa-in k<u>a</u>na lilk<u>a</u>fireena na<u>s</u>eebun q<u>a</u>loo alam nasta<u>h</u>wi<u>th</u> AAalaykum wanamnaAAkum mina almu/mineena fa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ya<u>h</u>kumu baynakum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati walan yajAAala All<u>a</u>hu lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>141.  (These are) the ones who<br> Wait and watch about you:<br> If ye do gain<br> A victory from God,<br> They say: "Were we not<br> With you?"—but if<br> The Unbelievers gain<br> A success, they say<br> (To them): "Did we not<br> Gain an advantage over you,<br> And did we not guard<br> You from the Believers?"<br> But God will judge<br> Betwixt you on the Day<br> Of Judgment. And never<br> Will God grant<br> To the Unbelievers<br> A way (to Triumph)<br> Over the Believers.</p> <div class="filenav">
