 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Walaqad akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> <u>a</u>la firAAawna bi<b>al</b>ssineena wanaq<u>s</u>in mina a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti laAAallahum ya<u>thth</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  We punished the people<br> Of Pharaoh with years<br>(Of drought) and shortness<br> Of crops; that they might<br> Receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu al<u>h</u>asanatu q<u>a</u>loo lan<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibhum sayyi-atun ya<u>tt</u>ayyaroo bimoos<u>a</u> waman maAAahu al<u>a</u> innam<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-iruhum AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  But when good (times) came,<br> They said, "This is due<br> To us;" when gripped<br> By calamity, they ascribed it<br> To evil omens connected<br> With Moses and those with him!<br> Behold! in truth the omens<br> Of evil are theirs<br> In God's sight, but most<br> Of them do not understand!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Waq<u>a</u>loo mahm<u>a</u> ta/tin<u>a</u> bihi min <u>a</u>yatin litas<u>h</u>aran<u>a</u> bih<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu laka bimu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  They said (to Moses):<br> "Whatever be the Signs<br> Thou bringest, to work<br> Therewith thy sorcery on us,"<br> We shall never believe<br> In thee."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Faarsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhimu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oof<u>a</u>na wa<b>a</b>ljar<u>a</u>da wa<b>a</b>lqummala wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>af<u>a</u>diAAa wa<b>al</b>ddama <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin mufa<u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>tin fa<b>i</b>stakbaroo wak<u>a</u>noo qawman mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  So We sent (plagues) on them:<br> Wholesale Death,<br> Locusts, Lice, Frogs,<br> And Blood: Signs openly<br> Self-explained: but they<br> Were steeped in arrogance,—<br> A people given to sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Walamm<u>a</u> waqaAAa AAalayhimu a<b>l</b>rrijzu q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> odAAu lan<u>a</u> rabbaka bim<u>a</u> AAahida AAindaka la-in kashafta AAann<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrijza lanu/minanna laka walanursilanna maAAaka banee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  Every time the Penalty<br> Fell on them, they said:<br>"O Moses! on our behalf<br> Call on thy Lord in virtue<br> Of his promise to thee:<br> If thou wilt remove<br> The Penalty from us,<br> We shall truly believe in thee,<br> And we shall send away<br> The Children of Israel<br> With thee."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Falamm<u>a</u> kashafn<u>a</u> AAanhumu a<b>l</b>rrijza il<u>a</u> ajalin hum b<u>a</u>lighoohu i<u>tha</u> hum yankuthoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  But every tune We removed<br> The Penalty from them<br> According to a fixed term<br> Which they had to fulfil,—<br> Behold! they broke their word!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  Fa<b>i</b>ntaqamn<u>a</u> minhum faaghraqn<u>a</u>hum fee alyammi bi-annahum ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>noo AAanh<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  So We exacted retribution<br> From them: We drowned them<br> In the sea, because they<br> Rejected Our Signs, and failed<br> To take warning from them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  Waawrathn<u>a</u> alqawma alla<u>th</u>eena k<u>a</u>noo yusta<u>d</u>AAafoona mash<u>a</u>riqa al-ar<u>d</u>i wamagh<u>a</u>ribah<u>a</u> allatee b<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> watammat kalimatu rabbika al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo wadammarn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na ya<u>s</u>naAAu firAAawnu waqawmuhu wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAArishoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  And We made a people,<br> Considered weak (and of no account),<br> Inheritors of lands<br> In both East and West,—<br>Lands whereon We sent<br> Down Our blessings.<br> The fair promise of thy Lord<br> Was fulfilled for the Children<br> Of Israel, because they had<br> Patience and constancy,<br> And We levelled to the ground<br> The great Works and fine Buildings<br> Which Pharaoh and his people<br> Erected (with such pride).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  Waj<u>a</u>wazn<u>a</u> bibanee isr<u>a</u>-eela alba<u>h</u>ra faataw AAal<u>a</u> qawmin yaAAkufoona AAal<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>min lahum q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> ijAAal lan<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>han kam<u>a</u> lahum <u>a</u>lihatun q<u>a</u>la innakum qawmun tajhaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>138.  We took the Children of Israel<br> (With safety) across the sea.<br> They came upon a people<br> Devoted entirely to some idols<br> They had. They said:<br> "O Moses! fashion for us<br> A god like unto the gods<br> They have." He said:<br>"Surely ye are a people<br> Without knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i mutabbarun m<u>a</u> hum feehi wab<u>at</u>ilun m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  "As to these folk,—<br> The cult they are in<br> Is (but) a fragment of a ruin,<br> And vain is the (worship)<br> Which they practise."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  Q<u>a</u>la aghayra All<u>a</u>hi abgheekum il<u>a</u>han wahuwa fa<u>dd</u>alakum AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  He said: "Shall I seek for you<br> A god other than the (true)<br> God, when it is God<br> Who hath endowed you<br> With gifts above the nations?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Wa-i<u>th</u> anjayn<u>a</u>kum min <u>a</u>li firAAawna yasoomoonakum soo-a alAAa<u>tha</u>bi yuqattiloona abn<u>a</u>akum wayasta<u>h</u>yoona nis<u>a</u>akum wafee <u>tha</u>likum bal<u>a</u>on min rabbikum AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>141.  And remember We rescued you<br> From Pharaoh's people,<br> Who afflicted you with<br> The worst of penalties,<br> Who slew your male children<br> And saved alive your females:<br> In that was a momentous<br> Trial from your Lord.</p> <div class="filenav">
