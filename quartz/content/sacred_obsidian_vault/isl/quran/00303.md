 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yakfuroona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wayaqtuloona a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena bighayri <u>h</u>aqqin wayaqtuloona alla<u>th</u>eena ya/muroona bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fabashshirhum biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>21.  As to those who deny<br> The Signs of God, and in defiance<br>Of right, slay the prophets,<br> And slay those who teach<br> Just dealing whith mankind,<br> Announce to them a grievous penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wam<u>a</u> lahum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>22.  They are those whose works<br> Will bear no fruit<br> In this world<br> And in the Hereafter,<br> Nor will they have<br> Anyone to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo na<u>s</u>eeban mina alkit<u>a</u>bi yudAAawna il<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi liya<u>h</u>kuma baynahum thumma yatawall<u>a</u> fareequn minhum wahum muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to those<br> Who have been given a portion<br> Of the Book? They are<br> Invited to the Book of God,<br> To settle their dispute,<br> But a party of them<br> Turn back and decline<br> (The arbitration).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahum q<u>a</u>loo lan tamassan<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru ill<u>a</u> ayy<u>a</u>man maAAdood<u>a</u>tin wagharrahum fee deenihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>24.  This because they say:<br> "The Fire shall not touch us<br> But for a few numbered days":<br> For their forgeries deceive them<br> As to their own religion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Fakayfa i<u>tha</u> jamaAAn<u>a</u>hum liyawmin l<u>a</u> rayba feehi wawuffiyat kullu nafsin m<u>a</u> kasabat wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>25.  But how (will they fare)<br> When We gather them together<br>Against a Day about which<br> There is no doubt,<br> And each soul will be paid out<br> Just what it has earned,<br> Without (favour or) injustice?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Quli all<u>a</u>humma m<u>a</u>lika almulki tu/tee almulka man tash<u>a</u>o watanziAAu almulka mimman tash<u>a</u>o watuAAizzu man tash<u>a</u>o watu<u>th</u>illu man tash<u>a</u>o biyadika alkhayru innaka AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Say: "O God!<br> Lord of Power (and Rule),<br> Thou givest Power<br> To whom Thou pleasest,<br> And Thou strippest off Power<br> From whom Thou pleasest:<br> Thou enduest with honour<br> Whom Thou pleasest,<br> And Thou bringest low<br> Whom Thou pleasest:<br> In Thy hand is all Good."<br> Verily, over all things<br> Thou hast power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Tooliju allayla fee a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri watooliju a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra fee allayli watukhriju al<u>h</u>ayya mina almayyiti watukhriju almayyita mina al<u>h</u>ayyi watarzuqu man tash<u>a</u>o bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>27.  "Thou causest the Night<br> To gain on the Day,<br> And Thou causest the Day<br> To gain on the Night;<br> Thou bringest the Living<br> Out of the Dead,<br> And Thou bringest the Dead<br> Out of the Living;<br> And Thou givest sustenance<br> To whom Thou pleasest,<br> Without measure."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  L<u>a</u> yattakhi<u>th</u>i almu/minoona alk<u>a</u>fireena awliy<u>a</u>a min dooni almu/mineena waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika falaysa mina All<u>a</u>hi fee shay-in ill<u>a</u> an tattaqoo minhum tuq<u>a</u>tan wayu<u>h</u>a<u>thth</u>irukumu All<u>a</u>hu nafsahu wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  let not the Believers<br> Take for friends or helpers<br>Unbelievers rather than<br> Believers: if any do that,<br> In nothing will there be help<br> From God: except by way<br> Of precaution, that ye may<br> Guard yourselves from them,<br> But God cautions you<br> (To remember) Himself;<br> For the final goal<br> Is to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Qul in tukhfoo m<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>udoorikum aw tubdoohu yaAAlamhu All<u>a</u>hu wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Say: "Whether ye hide<br> What is in your hearts<br> Or reveal it,<br> God knows it all:<br> He knows what is<br> In the heavens,<br> And what is on earth.<br> And God has power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Yawma tajidu kullu nafsin m<u>a</u> AAamilat min khayrin mu<u>hd</u>aran wam<u>a</u> AAamilat min soo-in tawaddu law anna baynah<u>a</u> wabaynahu amadan baAAeedan wayu<u>h</u>a<u>thth</u>irukumu All<u>a</u>hu nafsahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu raoofun bi<b>a</b>lAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>30.  "On the Day when every soul<br> Will be confronted<br> With all the good it has done,<br> And all the evil it has done,<br> It will wish there were<br> A great distance<br> Between it and its evil.<br> But God cautions you<br> (To remember) Himself.<br> And God is full of kindness<br> To those that serve Him."</p> <div class="filenav">
