 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXI.: Luqmān (the Wise).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Alam taraw anna All<u>a</u>ha sakhkhara lakum m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i waasbagha AAalaykum niAAamahu <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiratan wab<u>at</u>inatan wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yuj<u>a</u>dilu fee All<u>a</u>hi bighayri AAilmin wal<u>a</u> hudan wal<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bin muneer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Do ye not see<br> That God has subjected<br> To your (use) all things<br> In the heavens and on earth,<br> And has made His bounties<br> Flow to you in exceeding<br> Measure, (both) seen and unseen?<br> Yet there are among men<br> Those who dispute about God,<br> Without knowledge and without<br> Guidance, and without a Book<br> To enlighten them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu ittabiAAoo m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>loo bal nattabiAAu m<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> awa law k<u>a</u>na a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu yadAAoohum il<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bi a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  When they are told to follow<br> The (Revelation) that God<br> Has sent down, they say:<br> "Nay, we shall follow<br> The ways that we found<br> Our fathers (following)."<br>What! even if it is<br> Satan beckoning them<br> To the Penalty<br> Of the (Blazing) Fire?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Waman yuslim wajhahu il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wahuwa mu<u>h</u>sinun faqadi istamsaka bi<b>a</b>lAAurwati alwuthq<u>a</u> wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi AA<u>a</u>qibatu al-omoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Whoever submits<br> His whole self to God,<br> And is a doer of good,<br> Has grasped indeed<br> The most trustworthy hand-hold<br> And with God rests the End<br> And Decision of (all) affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Waman kafara fal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>zunka kufruhu ilayn<u>a</u> marjiAAuhum fanunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  But if any reject Faith,<br> Let not his rejection<br> Grieve thee: to Us<br> Is their Return, and We<br> Shall tell them the truth<br> Of their deeds: for God<br> Knows well all that is<br> In (men's) hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  NumattiAAuhum qaleelan thumma na<u>dt</u>arruhum il<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bin ghalee<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  We grant them their pleasure<br> For a little while<br> In the end shall We<br> Drive them to<br> A chastisement unrelenting.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wala-in saaltahum man khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a layaqoolunna All<u>a</u>hu quli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  If thou ask them,<br> Who it is that created<br> The heavens and the earth.<br> They will certainly say,<br>"God". Say: "Praise be to God!"<br> But most of them<br> Understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa alghaniyyu al<u>h</u>ameed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  To God belong all things<br> In heaven and earth: verily<br> God is He (that is)<br> Free of all wants,<br> Worthy of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Walaw annam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i min shajaratin aql<u>a</u>mun wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ru yamudduhu min baAAdihi sabAAatu ab<u>h</u>urin m<u>a</u> nafidat kalim<u>a</u>tu All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And if all the trees<br> On earth were pens<br> And the Ocean (were ink),<br> With seven Oceans behind it<br> To add to its (supply),<br> Yet would not the Words<br> Of God be exhausted<br> (In the writing): for God<br> Is Exalted in power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  M<u>a</u> khalqukum wal<u>a</u> baAAthukum ill<u>a</u> kanafsin w<u>ah</u>idatin inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And your creation<br> Or your resurrection<br> Is in no wise but<br> As an individual soul:<br> For God is He Who<br> Hears and sees (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha yooliju allayla fee a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wayooliju a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra fee allayli wasakhkhara a<b>l</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara kullun yajree il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman waanna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Seest thou not that<br> God merges Night into Day<br> And He merges Day into Night;<br> That He has subjected the sun,<br> And the moon (to His Law),<br> Each running its course<br> For a term appointed; and<br> That God is well acquainted<br> With all that ye do?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha huwa al<u>h</u>aqqu waanna m<u>a</u> yadAAoona min doonihi alb<u>at</u>ilu waanna All<u>a</u>ha huwa alAAaliyyu alkabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  That is because God is<br> The (only) Reality, and because<br> Whatever else they invoke<br> Besides Him is Falsehood;<br> And because God,—He is<br> The Most High, Most Great.</p> <div class="filenav">
