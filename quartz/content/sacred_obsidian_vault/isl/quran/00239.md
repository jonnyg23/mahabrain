 <p><a></a>282.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> tad<u>a</u>yantum bidaynin il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman fa<b>o</b>ktuboohu walyaktub baynakum k<u>a</u>tibun bi<b>a</b>lAAadli wal<u>a</u> ya/ba k<u>a</u>tibun an yaktuba kam<u>a</u> AAallamahu All<u>a</u>hu falyaktub walyumlili alla<u>th</u>ee AAalayhi al<u>h</u>aqqu walyattaqi All<u>a</u>ha rabbahu wal<u>a</u> yabkhas minhu shay-an fa-in k<u>a</u>na alla<u>th</u>ee AAalayhi al<u>h</u>aqqu safeehan aw <u>d</u>aAAeefan aw l<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAu an yumilla huwa falyumlil waliyyuhu bi<b>a</b>lAAadli wa<b>i</b>stashhidoo shaheedayni min rij<u>a</u>likum fa-in lam yakoon<u>a</u> rajulayni farajulun wa<b>i</b>mraat<u>a</u>ni mimman tar<u>d</u>awna mina a<b>l</b>shshuhad<u>a</u>-i an ta<u>d</u>illa i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> fatu<u>th</u>akkira i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> al-okhr<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ya/ba a<b>l</b>shshuhad<u>a</u>o i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> duAAoo wal<u>a</u> tas-amoo an taktuboohu <u>s</u>agheeran aw kabeeran il<u>a</u> ajalihi <u>tha</u>likum aqsa<u>t</u>u AAinda All<u>a</u>hi waaqwamu li<b>l</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati waadn<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> tart<u>a</u>boo ill<u>a</u> an takoona tij<u>a</u>ratan <u>had</u>iratan tudeeroonah<u>a</u> baynakum falaysa AAalaykum jun<u>ah</u>un all<u>a</u> taktubooh<u>a</u> waashhidoo i<u>tha</u> tab<u>a</u>yaAAtum wal<u>a</u> yu<u>da</u>rra k<u>a</u>tibun wal<u>a</u> shaheedun wa-in tafAAaloo fa-innahu fusooqun bikum wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wayuAAallimukumu All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>282.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye deal with each other,<br> In transactions involving<br> Future obligations<br> In a fixed period of time,<br> Reduce them to writing<br> Let a scribe write down<br> Faithfully as between<br> The parties: let not the scribe<br> Refuse to write: as God<br> Has taught him,<br> So let him write.<br> Let him who incurs<br> The liability dictate,<br> But let him fear<br> His Lord God,<br> And not diminish<br> Aught of what he owes.<br> If the party liable<br> Is mentally deficient,<br>Or weak, or unable<br> Himself to dictate,<br> Let his guardian<br> Dictate faithfully.<br> And get two witnesses,<br> Out of your own men,<br> And if there are not two men,<br> Then a man and two women,<br> Such as ye choose,<br> For witnesses,<br> So that if one of them errs,<br> The other can remind her.<br> The witnesses<br> Should not refuse<br> When they are called on<br> (For evidence).<br> Disdain not to reduce<br> To writing (your contract)<br> For a future period,<br> Whether it be small<br> Or big: it is juster<br> In the sight of God,<br> More suitable as evidence,<br> And more convenient<br> To prevent doubts<br> Among yourselves<br> But if it be a transaction<br> Which ye carry out<br> On the spot among yourselves,<br> There is no blame on you<br> If ye reduce it not<br> To writing.<br> But take witnesses<br> Whenever ye make<br> A commercial contract;<br> And let neither scribe<br> Nor witness suffer harm.<br> If ye do (such harm),<br> It would be wickedness<br> In you. So fear God;<br> For it is God<br> That teaches you.<br> And God is well acquainted<br> With all things.</p> <hr>

 <p><a></a>283.  Wa-in kuntum AAal<u>a</u> safarin walam tajidoo k<u>a</u>tiban farih<u>a</u>nun maqboo<u>d</u>atun fa-in amina baAA<u>d</u>ukum baAA<u>d</u>an falyu-addi alla<u>th</u>ee<u> </u>i/tumina am<u>a</u>natahu walyattaqi All<u>a</u>ha rabbahu wal<u>a</u> taktumoo a<b>l</b>shshah<u>a</u>data waman yaktumh<u>a</u> fa-innahu <u>a</u>thimun qalbuhu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona AAaleem<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>283.  If ye are on a journey,<br> And cannot find<br> A scribe, a pledge<br> With possession (may serve<br> The purpose).<br> And if one of you<br> Deposits a thing<br> On trust with another,<br> Let the trustee<br> (Faithfully) discharge<br> His trust, and let him<br> Fear his Lord.<br> Conceal not evidence;<br> For whoever conceals it,—<br> His heart is tainted<br> With sin. And God<br> Knoweth all that ye do.</p> <div class="filenav">
