 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Wa-il<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>din akh<u>a</u>hum hoodan q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  To the ’Ad people,<br> (We sent) Hūd, one<br>Of their (own) brethren:<br> He said: "O my people!<br> Worship God! ye have<br> No other god but Him.<br> Will ye not fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Q<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qawmihi inn<u>a</u> lanar<u>a</u>ka fee saf<u>a</u>hatin wa-inn<u>a</u> lana<i><u>th</u></i>unnuka mina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  The leaders of the unbelievers<br> Among his people said:<br> "Ah! we see thou art<br> An imbecile!" and "We think<br> Thou art a liar!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi laysa bee saf<u>a</u>hatun wal<u>a</u>kinnee rasoolun min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  He said: "O my people!<br> I am no imbecile, but<br> (I am) an apostle from<br> The Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Worlds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Oballighukum ris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>ti rabbee waan<u>a</u> lakum n<u>as</u>i<u>h</u>un ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  "I but fulfil towards you<br> The duties of my Lord's mission:<br> I am to you a sincere<br> And trustworthy adviser.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Awa AAajibtum an j<u>a</u>akum <u>th</u>ikrun min rabbikum AAal<u>a</u> rajulin minkum liyun<u>th</u>irakum wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo i<u>th</u> jaAAalakum khulaf<u>a</u>a min baAAdi qawmi noo<u>h</u>in waz<u>a</u>dakum fee alkhalqi bas<u>t</u>atan fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hi laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  "Do ye wonder that<br> There hath come to you<br> A message from your Lord<br> Through a man of your own<br> People, to warn you?<br> Call in remembrance<br> That He made you<br> Inheritors after the people<br> Of Noah, and gave you<br> A stature tall among the nations.<br> Call in remembrance<br> The benefits (ye have received)<br> From God: that so<br> Ye may prosper."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Q<u>a</u>loo aji/tan<u>a</u> linaAAbuda All<u>a</u>ha wa<u>h</u>dahu wana<u>th</u>ara m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> fa/tin<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> taAAidun<u>a</u> in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  They said: "Comest thou<br> To us, that we may worship<br> God alone, and give up<br> The cult of our fathers?<br> Bring us what thou<br> Threatenest us with,<br> If so be that thou<br> Tellest the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Q<u>a</u>la qad waqaAAa AAalaykum min rabbikum rijsun wagha<u>d</u>abun atuj<u>a</u>diloonanee fee asm<u>a</u>-in sammaytumooh<u>a</u> antum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum m<u>a</u> nazzala All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>a</u> min sul<u>ta</u>nin fa<b>i</b>nta<i><u>th</u></i>iroo innee maAAakum mina almunta<i><u>th</u></i>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  He said: "Punishment<br> And wrath have already<br> Come upon you from your Lord<br> Dispute ye with me<br> Over names which ye<br> Have devised—ye<br> And your fathers,<br> Without authority from God?<br> Then wait: I am<br> Amongst you, also waiting."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Faanjayn<u>a</u>hu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena maAAahu bira<u>h</u>matin minn<u>a</u> waqa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>bira alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  We saved him and those<br> Who adhered to him,<br> By Our Mercy, and We<br> Cut off the roots of those<br> Who rejected Our Signs<br> And did not believe.</p> <div class="filenav">
