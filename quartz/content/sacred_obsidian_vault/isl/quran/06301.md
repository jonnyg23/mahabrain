 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXIII.: Munāfiqūn, or the Hypocrites.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  I<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>aka almun<u>a</u>fiqoona q<u>a</u>loo nashhadu innaka larasoolu All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu innaka larasooluhu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yashhadu inna almun<u>a</u>fiqeena lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  When the Hypocrites<br> Come to thee, they say,<br> "We bear witness that thou<br> Art indeed the Apostle<br> Of God." Yea, God<br> Knoweth that thou art<br> Indeed His Apostle,<br> And God beareth witness<br> That the Hypocrites are<br> Indeed liars.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Ittakha<u>th</u>oo aym<u>a</u>nahum junnatan fa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi innahum s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  They have made their oaths<br> A screen (for their misdeeds):<br> Thus they obstruct (men)<br> From the Path of God:<br> Truly evil are their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahum <u>a</u>manoo thumma kafaroo fa<u>t</u>ubiAAa AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim fahum l<u>a</u> yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  That is because they believed,<br> Then they rejected Faith:<br> So a seal was set<br> On their hearts: therefore<br> They understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> raaytahum tuAAjibuka ajs<u>a</u>muhum wa-in yaqooloo tasmaAA liqawlihim kaannahum khushubun musannadatun ya<u>h</u>saboona kulla <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atin AAalayhim humu alAAaduwwu fa<b>i</b><u>hth</u>arhum q<u>a</u>talahumu All<u>a</u>hu ann<u>a</u> yu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  When thou lookest<br> At them, their exteriors<br>Please thee; and when<br> They speak, thou listenest<br> To their words. They are<br> As (worthless as hollow)<br> Pieces of timber propped up,<br> (Unable to stand on their own).<br> They think that every<br> Cry is against them.<br> They are the enemies;<br> So beware of them.<br> The curse of God be<br> On them! How are they<br> Deluded (away from the Truth)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum taAA<u>a</u>law yastaghfir lakum rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi lawwaw ruoosahum waraaytahum ya<u>s</u>uddoona wahum mustakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And when it is said<br> To them, "Come, the Apostle<br> Of God will pray for your<br> Forgiveness" they turn aside<br> Their heads, and thou wouldst<br> See them turning away<br> Their faces in arrogance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Saw<u>a</u>on AAalayhim astaghfarta lahum am lam tastaghfir lahum lan yaghfira All<u>a</u>hu lahum inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  It is equal to them<br> Whether thou pray for<br> Their forgiveness or not.<br> God will not forgive them.<br> Truly God guides not<br> Rebellious transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Humu alla<u>th</u>eena yaqooloona l<u>a</u> tunfiqoo AAal<u>a</u> man AAinda rasooli All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yanfa<u>dd</u>oo walill<u>a</u>hi khaz<u>a</u>-inu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u>kinna almun<u>a</u>fiqeena l<u>a</u> yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  They are the ones who say,<br> "Spend nothing on those<br> Who are with God's Apostle,<br> To the end that they<br>May disperse (and quit Medina)."<br> But to God belong<br> The treasures of the heavens<br> And the earth; but<br> The Hypocrites understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Yaqooloona la-in rajaAAn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> almadeenati layukhrijanna al-aAAazzu minh<u>a</u> al-a<u>th</u>alla walill<u>a</u>hi alAAizzatu walirasoolihi walilmu/mineena wal<u>a</u>kinna almun<u>a</u>fiqeena l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>8.  They say, "If we<br> Return to Medina, surely<br> The more honourable (element)<br> Will expel therefrom the meaner".<br> But honour belongs to God<br> And His Apostle, and<br> To the Believers; but<br> The Hypocrites know not.</p> <div class="filenav">
