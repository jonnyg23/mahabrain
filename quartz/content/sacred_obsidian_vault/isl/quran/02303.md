 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Waq<u>a</u>la almalao min qawmihi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo biliq<u>a</u>-i al-<u>a</u>khirati waatrafn<u>a</u>hum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum ya/kulu mimm<u>a</u> ta/kuloona minhu wayashrabu mimm<u>a</u> tashraboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And the chiefs<br> Of his people, who disbelieved<br> And denied the Meeting<br> In the Hereafter, and on whom<br> We had bestowed the good things<br> Of this life, said: "He is<br> No more than a man<br> Like yourselves: he eats<br> Of that of which ye eat,<br> And drinks of what ye drink.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wala-in a<u>t</u>aAAtum basharan mithlakum innakum i<u>th</u>an lakh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "If ye obey a man<br> Like yourselves, behold,<br> It is certain ye will be lost.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  AyaAAidukum annakum i<u>tha</u> mittum wakuntum tur<u>a</u>ban waAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man annakum mukhrajoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "Does he promise that<br> When ye die and become dust<br> And bones, ye shall be<br> Brought forth (again)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Hayh<u>a</u>ta hayh<u>a</u>ta lim<u>a</u> tooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  "Far, very far is that<br> Which ye are promised!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  In hiya ill<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> namootu wana<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimabAAootheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "There is nothing but<br> Our life in this world!<br> We shall die and we live!<br> But we shall never<br> Be raised up again!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  In huwa ill<u>a</u> rajulun iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu lahu bimu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  "He is only a man<br> Who invents a lie<br> Against God, but we<br> Are not the ones<br> To believe in him!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi on<u>s</u>urnee bim<u>a</u> ka<u>thth</u>aboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  (The prophet) said:<br> "O my Lord! help me:<br> For that they accuse me<br> Of falsehood."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Q<u>a</u>la AAamm<u>a</u> qaleelin layu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>unna n<u>a</u>dimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  (God) said: "In but<br> A little while, they<br> Are sure to be sorry!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi fajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum ghuth<u>a</u>an fabuAAdan lilqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Then the Blast overtook them<br> With justice, and We made them<br> As rubbish of dead leaves<br> (Floating on the stream of Time)!<br> So away with the people<br> Who do wrong!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Thumma ansha/n<u>a</u> min baAAdihim quroonan <u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>42.  Then We raised after them<br> Other generations.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  M<u>a</u> tasbiqu min ommatin ajalah<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yasta/khiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  No people can hasten<br> Their term, nor can they<br> Delay (it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Thumma arsaln<u>a</u> rusulan<u>a</u> tatr<u>a</u> kulla m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a ommatan rasooluh<u>a</u> ka<u>thth</u>aboohu faatbaAAn<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ahum baAA<u>d</u>an wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum a<u>ha</u>deetha fabuAAdan liqawmin l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Then sent We Our apostles<br> In succession: every time<br> There came to a people<br> Their apostle, they accused him<br> Of falsehood: so We made<br> Them follow each other<br> (In punishment): We made them<br> As a tale (that is told):<br> So away with a people<br> That will not believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Thumma arsaln<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> waakh<u>a</u>hu h<u>a</u>roona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wasul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  When We sent Moses<br> And his brother Aaron,<br> With Our Signs and<br> Authority manifest,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Il<u>a</u> firAAawna wamala-ihi fa<b>i</b>stakbaroo wak<u>a</u>noo qawman AA<u>a</u>leen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  To Pharaoh and his Chiefs:<br> But these behaved insolently:<br> They were an arrogant people.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Faq<u>a</u>loo anu/minu libasharayni mithlin<u>a</u> waqawmuhum<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>bidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  They said: "Shall we believe<br> In two men like ourselves?<br> And their people are subject<br> To us!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohum<u>a</u> fak<u>a</u>noo mina almuhlakeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  So they accused them<br> Of falsehood, and they became<br> Of those who were destroyed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba laAAallahum yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And We gave Moses<br> The Book, in order that<br> They might receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> ibna maryama waommahu <u>a</u>yatan wa<u>a</u>wayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> rabwatin <u>tha</u>ti qar<u>a</u>rin wamaAAeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And We made<br> The son of Mary<br> And his mother<br> As a Sign:<br> We gave them both<br> Shelter on high ground,<br> Affording rest and security<br> And furnished with springs.</p> <div class="filenav">
