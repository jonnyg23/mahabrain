 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXIX.: Ḥāqqa, or the Sure Reality.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Fal<u>a</u> oqsimu bim<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  So I do<br> Call to witness<br> What ye see</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wam<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  And what ye see not,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Innahu laqawlu rasoolin kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  That this is<br> Verily the word<br> Of an honoured apostle;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wam<u>a</u> huwa biqawli sh<u>a</u>AAirin qaleelan m<u>a</u> tu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  It is not the word<br> Of a poet:<br> Little it is<br> Ye believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wal<u>a</u> biqawli k<u>a</u>hinin qaleelan m<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Nor is it the word<br> Of a soothsayer:<br> Little admonition it is<br> Ye receive.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Tanzeelun min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>43.  (This is) a Message<br> Sent down from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Walaw taqawwala AAalayn<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>a al-aq<u>a</u>weel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  And if the apostle<br> Were to invent<br> Any sayings in Our name,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Laakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> minhu bi<b>a</b>lyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  We should certainly seize him<br> By his right hand,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Thumma laqa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> minhu alwateen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And We should certainly<br> Then cut off the artery<br> Of his heart:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Fam<u>a</u> minkum min a<u>h</u>adin AAanhu <u>ha</u>jizeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Nor could any of you<br> Withhold him<br> (From Our wrath).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa-innahu lata<u>th</u>kiratun lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  But verily this<br> Is a Message for<br> The God-fearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> lanaAAlamu anna minkum muka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>49.  And We certainly know<br> That there are amongst you<br> Those that reject (it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wa-innahu la<u>h</u>asratun AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  But truly (Revelation)<br> Is a cause of sorrow<br> For the Unbelievers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wa-innahu la<u>h</u>aqqu alyaqeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  But verily it is Truth<br> Of assured certainty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Fasabbi<u>h</u> bi<b>i</b>smi rabbika alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  So glorify the name<br> Of thy Lord Most High.</p>  <div class="filenav">
