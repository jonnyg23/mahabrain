 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 33</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_249.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>249.  Falamm<u>a</u> fa<u>s</u>ala <u>ta</u>lootu bi<b>a</b>ljunoodi q<u>a</u>la inna All<u>a</u>ha mubtaleekum binaharin faman shariba minhu falaysa minnee waman lam ya<u>t</u>AAamhu fa-innahu minnee ill<u>a</u> mani ightarafa ghurfatan biyadihi fashariboo minhu ill<u>a</u> qaleelan minhum falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>wazahu huwa wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>qata lan<u>a</u> alyawma bij<u>a</u>loota wajunoodihi q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnoona annahum mul<u>a</u>qoo All<u>a</u>hi kam min fi-atin qaleelatin ghalabat fi-atan katheeratan bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu maAAa a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>249.  When Talūt set forth<br> With the armies, he said:<br> "God will test you<br> At the stream: if any<br> Drinks of its water,<br> He goes not with my army:<br> Only those who taste not<br> Of it go with me:<br> A mere sip out of the hand<br> Is excused." But they all<br> Drank of it, except a few.<br> When they crossed the river,—<br> He and the faithful ones with him,<br> They said: "This day<br> We cannot cope<br> With Goliath and his forces."<br>But those who were convinced<br> That they must meet God,<br> Said: "How oft, by God's will,<br> Hath a small force<br> Vanquished a big one?<br> God is with those<br> Who steadfastly persevere."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_250.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>250.  Walamm<u>a</u> barazoo lij<u>a</u>loota wajunoodihi q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> afrigh AAalayn<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abran wathabbit aqd<u>a</u>man<u>a</u> wa<b>o</b>n<u>s</u>urn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alqawmi alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>250.  When they advanced<br> To meet Goliath and his forces,<br> They prayed: "Our Lord<br> Pour out constancy on us<br> And make our steps firm:<br> Help us against those<br> That reject faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_251.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>251.  Fahazamoohum bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waqatala d<u>a</u>woodu j<u>a</u>loota wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hu All<u>a</u>hu almulka wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata waAAallamahu mimm<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o walawl<u>a</u> dafAAu All<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa baAA<u>d</u>ahum bibaAA<u>d</u>in lafasadati al-ar<u>d</u>u wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha <u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>251.  By God's will,<br> They routed them;<br> And David slew Goliath;<br> And God gave him<br> Power and wisdom<br> And taught him<br> Whatever (else) He willed,<br> And did not God<br> Check one set of people<br> By means of another,<br> The earth would indeed<br> Be full of mischief:<br> But God is full of bounty<br> To all the worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_252.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>252.  Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu All<u>a</u>hi natlooh<u>a</u> AAalayka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-innaka lamina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>252.  These are the Signs<br> Of God: we rehearse them<br> To thee in truth: verily<br> Thou art one of the Apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_253.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>253.  Tilka a<b>l</b>rrusulu fa<u>dd</u>aln<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ahum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in minhum man kallama All<u>a</u>hu warafaAAa baAA<u>d</u>ahum daraj<u>a</u>tin wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> ibna maryama albayyin<u>a</u>ti waayyadn<u>a</u>hu biroo<u>h</u>i alqudusi walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> iqtatala alla<u>th</u>eena min baAAdihim min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu albayyin<u>a</u>tu wal<u>a</u>kini ikhtalafoo faminhum man <u>a</u>mana waminhum man kafara walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> iqtataloo wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha yafAAalu m<u>a</u> yureed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>253.  Those apostles<br> Were endowed with gifts,<br> Some above others<br> To one of them God spoke;<br> Others He raised<br> To degrees (of honour);<br> To Jesus the son of Mary<br> We gave Clear (Signs),<br> And strengthened him<br> With the holy spirit.<br> If God had so willed,<br> Succeeding generations<br> Would not have fought<br> Among each other, after<br> Clear (Signs) had come to them,<br> But they (chose) to wrangle,<br> Some believing and Others<br> Rejecting. If God had so willed,<br> They would not have fought<br> Each other; but God<br> Fulfilleth His plan.</p> <div class="filenav">
