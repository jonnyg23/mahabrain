 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Faman yaAAmal mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti wahuwa mu/minun fal<u>a</u> kufr<u>a</u>na lisaAAyihi wa-inn<u>a</u> lahu k<u>a</u>tiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  Whoever works any act<br> Of Righteousness and has Faith,—<br> His endeavour will not<br> Be rejected: We shall<br> Record it in his favour.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Wa<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> qaryatin ahlakn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> annahum l<u>a</u> yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  But there is a ban<br> On any population which<br> We have destroyed: that they<br> Shall not return,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> futi<u>h</u>at ya/jooju wama/jooju wahum min kulli <u>h</u>adabin yansiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Until the Gog and Magog (people)<br> Are let through (their barrier),<br> And they swiftly swarm<br> From every hill.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Wa<b>i</b>qtaraba alwaAAdu al<u>h</u>aqqu fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya sh<u>a</u>khi<u>s</u>atun ab<u>sa</u>ru alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo y<u>a</u> waylan<u>a</u> qad kunn<u>a</u> fee ghaflatin min h<u>atha</u> bal kunn<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  Then will the True Promise<br> Draw nigh (of fulfilment):<br> Then behold! the eyes<br> Of the Unbelievers will<br> Fixedly stare in horror: "Ah!<br> Woe to us! we were indeed<br> Heedless of this; nay, we<br> Truly did wrong!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Innakum wam<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>a<u>s</u>abu jahannama antum lah<u>a</u> w<u>a</u>ridoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  Verily ye, (Unbelievers),<br> And the (false) gods that<br> Ye worship besides God,<br> Are (but) fuel for Hell!<br> To it will ye (surely) come!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Law k<u>a</u>na h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i <u>a</u>lihatan m<u>a</u> waradooh<u>a</u> wakullun feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  If these had been gods,<br> They would not have got there!<br> But each one will abide<br> Therein.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Lahum feeh<u>a</u> zafeerun wahum feeh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  There, sobbing will be<br> Their lot, nor will they<br> There hear (aught else).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena sabaqat lahum minn<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika AAanh<u>a </u>mubAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  Whose hose for whom<br> The Good (Record) from Us<br> Has gone before, will be<br> Removed far therefrom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  L<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona <u>h</u>aseesah<u>a</u> wahum fee m<u>a</u> ishtahat anfusuhum kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Not the slightest sound<br> Will they hear of Hell:<br> What their souls desired,<br> In that will they dwell.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  L<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>zunuhumu alfazaAAu al-akbaru watatalaqq<u>a</u>humu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu h<u>atha</u> yawmukumu alla<u>th</u>ee kuntum tooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  The Great Terror will<br> Bring them no grief:<br> But the angels will meet them<br> (With mutual greetings):<br> "This is your Day,—<br> (The Day) that ye were promised."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Yawma na<u>t</u>wee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a ka<u>t</u>ayyi a<b>l</b>ssijlli lilkutubi kam<u>a</u> bada/n<u>a</u> awwala khalqin nuAAeeduhu waAAdan AAalayn<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>AAileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  The Day that We roll up<br> The heavens like a scroll<br> Rolled up for books (completed),—<br> Even as We produced<br> The first Creation, so<br> Shall We produce<br> A new one: a promise<br> We have undertaken:<br> Truly shall We fulfil it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Walaqad katabn<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>zzaboori min baAAdi a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri anna al-ar<u>d</u>a yarithuh<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>diya a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  Before this We wrote<br> In the Psalms, after the Message<br> (Given to Moses): "My servants,<br> The righteous, shall inherit<br> The earth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Inna fee h<u>atha</u> labal<u>a</u>ghan liqawmin AA<u>a</u>bideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  Verily in this (Qur-ān)<br> Is a Message for people<br> Who would (truly) worship God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka ill<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  We sent thee not, but<br> As a Mercy for all creatures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Qul innam<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya annam<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hukum il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun fahal antum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Say: "What has come to me<br> By inspiration is that<br> Your God is One God:<br> Will ye therefore bow<br> To His Will (in Islām)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Fa-in tawallaw faqul <u>ath</u>antukum AAal<u>a</u> saw<u>a</u>-in wa-in adree aqareebun am baAAeedun m<u>a</u> tooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  But if they turn back,<br> Say: "I have proclaimed<br> The Message to you all alike<br> And in truth; but I<br> Know not whether that<br> Which ye are promised<br> Is near or far.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Innahu yaAAlamu aljahra mina alqawli wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> taktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  "It is He Who knows<br> What is open in speech<br> And what ye hide<br> (In your hearts).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Wa-in adree laAAallahu fitnatun lakum wamat<u>a</u>AAun il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  "I know not but that<br> It may be a trial<br> For you, and a grant<br> Of (worldly) livelihood<br> (To you) for a time."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi o<u>h</u>kum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi warabbun<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu almustaAA<u>a</u>nu AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  Say: "O my Lord!<br> Judge Thou in truth!<br> "Our Lord Most Gracious<br> Is the One Whose assistance<br> Should be sought against<br> The blasphemies ye utter!</p>  <div class="filenav">
