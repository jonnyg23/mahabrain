 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_192.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>192.  Wa-innahu latanzeelu rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>192.  Verily this is a Revelation<br> From the Lord of the Worlds:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_193.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>193.  Nazala bihi a<b>l</b>rroo<u>h</u>u al-ameen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>193.  With it came down<br> The Spirit of Faith and Truth—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_194.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>194.  AAal<u>a</u> qalbika litakoona mina almun<u>th</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>194.  To thy heart and mind,<br> That thou mayest admonish</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_195.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>195.  Bilis<u>a</u>nin AAarabiyyin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>195.  In the perspicuous<br> Arabic tongue.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_196.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>196.  Wa-innahu lafee zuburi al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>196.  Without doubt it is (announced)<br> In the mystic Book<br> Of former peoples.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_197.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>197.  Awa lam yakun lahum <u>a</u>yatan an yaAAlamahu AAulam<u>a</u>o banee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>197.  Is it not a Sign<br> To them that the Learned<br> Of the Children of Israel<br> Knew it (as true)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_198.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>198.  Walaw nazzaln<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>i al-aAAjameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>198.  Had We revealed it<br> To any of the non Arabs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_199.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>199.  Faqaraahu AAalayhim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>199.  And had he recited it<br> To them, they would not<br> Have believed in it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_200.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>200.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika salakn<u>a</u>hu fee quloobi almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>200.  Thus have We caused it<br> To enter the hearts<br> Of the Sinners.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_201.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>201.  L<u>a</u> yu/minoona bihi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yarawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba al-aleem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>201.  They will not believe<br> In it until they see<br> The grievous Penalty;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_202.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>202.  Faya/tiyahum baghtatan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>202.  But the (Penalty) will come<br> To them of a sudden,<br> While they preceive it not;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_203.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>203.  Fayaqooloo hal na<u>h</u>nu mun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>203.  Then they will say:<br> "Shall we be respited?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_204.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>204.  AfabiAAa<u>tha</u>bin<u>a</u> yastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>204.  Do they then ask<br> For Our Penalty to be<br> Hastened on?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_205.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>205.  Afaraayta in mattaAAn<u>a</u>hum sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>205.  Seest thou? If We do<br> Let them enjoy (this life)<br> For a few years,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_206.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>206.  Thumma j<u>a</u>ahum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>206.  Yet there comes to them<br> At length the (Punishment)<br> Which they were promised!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_207.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>207.  M<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yumattaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>207.  It will profit them not<br> That they enjoyed (this life)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_208.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>208.  Wam<u>a</u> ahlakn<u>a</u> min qaryatin ill<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> mun<u>th</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>208.  Never did We destroy<br> A population, but had<br> Its warners—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_209.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>209.  <u>Th</u>ikr<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>209.  By way of reminder;<br> And We never are unjust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_210.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>210.  Wam<u>a</u> tanazzalat bihi a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>een<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>210.  No evil ones have brought<br> Down this (Revelation):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_211.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>211.  Wam<u>a</u> yanbaghee lahum wam<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>211.  It would neither suit them<br> Nor would they be able<br> (To produce it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_212.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>212.  Innahum AAani a<b>l</b>ssamAAi lamaAAzooloona   </p>
 <p>212.  Indeed they have been removed<br> Far from even (a chance of)<br> Hearing it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_213.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>213.  Fal<u>a</u> tadAAu maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara fatakoona mina almuAAa<u>thth</u>abeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>213.  So call not on any<br> Other god with God,<br> Or thou wilt be among<br> Those under the Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_214.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>214.  Waan<u>th</u>ir AAasheerataka al-aqrabeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>214.  And admonish thy nearest<br> Kinsmen,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_215.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>215.  Wa<b>i</b>khfi<u>d</u> jan<u>ah</u>aka limani ittabaAAaka mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>215.  And lower thy wing<br> To the Believers who<br> Follow thee.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_216.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>216.  Fa-in AAa<u>s</u>awka faqul innee baree-on mimm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>216.  Then if they disobey thee,<br> Say: "I am free (of responsibility)<br> For what ye do!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_217.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>217.  Watawakkal AAal<u>a</u> alAAazeezi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>217.  And put thy trust<br> On the Exalted in Might,<br> The Merciful,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_218.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>218.  Alla<u>th</u>ee yar<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>eena taqoom<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>218.  Who seeth thee standing<br> Forth (in prayer),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_219.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>219.  Wataqallubaka fee a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>219.  And thy movements among<br> Those who prostrate themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_220.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>220.  Innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>220.  For it is He<br> Who heareth and knoweth<br> All things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_221.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>221.  Hal onabbi-okum AAal<u>a</u> man tanazzalu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>een<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>221.  Shall I inform you.<br> (O people!), on whom it is<br> That the evil ones descend?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_222.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>222.  Tanazzalu AAal<u>a</u> kulli aff<u>a</u>kin atheem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>222.  They descend on every<br> Lying, wicked person,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_223.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>223.  Yulqoona a<b>l</b>ssamAAa waaktharuhum k<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>223.  (Into whose ears) they pour<br> Hearsay vanities, and most<br> Of them are liars.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_224.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>224.  Wa<b>al</b>shshuAAar<u>a</u>o yattabiAAuhumu algh<u>a</u>woon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>224.  And the Poets,—<br> It is those straying in Evil,<br> Who follow them:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_225.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>225.  Alam tara annahum fee kulli w<u>a</u>din yaheemoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>225.  Seest thou not that they<br> Wander distracted in every<br> Valley?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_226.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>226.  Waannahum yaqooloona m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>226.  And that they say<br> What they practise not?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_227.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>227.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti wa<u>th</u>akaroo All<u>a</u>ha katheeran wa<b>i</b>nta<u>s</u>aroo min baAAdi m<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ulimoo wasayaAAlamu alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo ayya munqalabin yanqaliboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>227.  Except those who believe,<br> Work righteousness, engage much<br>In the remembrance of God,<br> And defend themselves only after<br> They are unjustly attacked.<br> And soon will the unjust'<br> Assailants know what vicissitudes<br> Their affairs will take!</p>  <div class="filenav">
