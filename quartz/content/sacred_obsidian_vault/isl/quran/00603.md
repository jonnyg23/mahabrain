 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban aw ka<u>thth</u>aba bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi innahu l<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Who doth more wrong<br> Than he who inventeth<br> A lie against God<br> Or rejecteth His Signs?<br> But verily the wrong-doers<br> Never shall prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wayawma na<u>h</u>shuruhum jameeAAan thumma naqoolu lilla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo ayna shurak<u>a</u>okumu alla<u>th</u>eena kuntum tazAAumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  One day shall We gather<br> Them all together: We<br> Shall say to those<br> Who ascribed partners (to Us):<br> "Where are the partners<br> Whom ye (invented<br> And) talked about?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Thumma lam takun fitnatuhum ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi rabbin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> mushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  There will then be (left)<br> No subterfuge for them<br> But to say: "By God<br> Our Lord, we were not<br> Those who joined gods<br> With God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  On<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa ka<u>th</u>aboo AAal<u>a</u> anfusihim wa<u>d</u>alla AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Behold! how they lie<br> Against their own souls!<br>But the (lie) which they<br> Invented will leave themes:<br> In the lurch.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waminhum man yastamiAAu ilayka wajaAAaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim akinnatan an yafqahoohu wafee <u>atha</u>nihim waqran wa-in yaraw kulla <u>a</u>yatin l<u>a</u> yu/minoo bih<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ooka yuj<u>a</u>diloonaka yaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Of them there are some<br> Who (pretend to) listen to thee;<br> But We have thrown<br> Veils on their hearts,<br> So they understand it not,<br> And deafness in their ears;<br> If they saw every one<br> Of the Signs, not they<br> Will believe in them;<br> In so much that<br> When they come to thee,<br> They (but) dispute with thee;<br> The Unbelievers say:<br> "These are nothing<br> But tales of the ancients."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wahum yanhawna AAanhu wayan-awna AAanhu wa-in yuhlikoona ill<u>a</u> anfusahum wam<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Others they keep away from it,<br> And themselves they keep away;<br> But they only destroy<br> Their own souls,<br> And they perceive it not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> wuqifoo AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri faq<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> laytan<u>a</u> nuraddu wal<u>a</u> nuka<u>thth</u>iba bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbin<u>a</u> wanakoona mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  If thou couldst but see<br> When they are confronted<br> With the Fire!<br> They will say:<br> "Would that we were<br> But sent back!<br> Then would we not reject<br> The Signs of our Lord,<br> But would be amongst those<br> Who believe!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Bal bad<u>a</u> lahum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yukhfoona min qablu walaw ruddoo laAA<u>a</u>doo lim<u>a</u> nuhoo AAanhu wa-innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Yea, in their own (eyes)<br> Will become manifest<br> What before they concealed.<br> But if they were returned,<br> They would certainly relapse<br> To the things they were forbidden,<br>For they are indeed liars.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Waq<u>a</u>loo in hiya ill<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimabAAootheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And they (sometimes) say:<br> "There is nothing except<br> Our life on this earth,<br> And never shall we be<br> Raised up again."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> wuqifoo AAal<u>a</u> rabbihim q<u>a</u>la alaysa h<u>atha</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> warabbin<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la fa<u>th</u>ooqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bim<u>a</u> kuntum takfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  If thou couldst but see<br> When they are confronted<br> With their Lord!<br> He will say:<br> "Is not this the truth?"<br> They will say:<br> "Yea, by our Lord!"<br> He will say:<br> "Taste ye then the Penalty,<br> Because ye rejected Faith."</p> <div class="filenav">
