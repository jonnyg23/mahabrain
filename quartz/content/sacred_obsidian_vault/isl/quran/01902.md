 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIX.: Maryam, or Mary.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur fee alkit<u>a</u>bi maryama i<u>th</u>i intaba<u>th</u>at min ahlih<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>nan sharqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Relate in the Book<br> (The story of) Mary,<br> When she withdrew<br> From her family<br> To a place in the East.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Fa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>at min doonihim <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ban faarsaln<u>a</u> ilayh<u>a</u> roo<u>h</u>an<u>a</u> fatamaththala lah<u>a</u> basharan sawiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  She placed a screen<br> (To screen herself) from them;<br> Then We sent to her<br> Our angel, and he appeared<br> Before her as a man<br> In all respects.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Q<u>a</u>lat innee aAAoo<u>th</u>u bi<b>al</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni minka in kunta taqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  She said: "I seek refuge<br> From thee to (God)<br> Most Gracious: (come not near)<br> If thou dost fear God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> rasoolu rabbiki li-ahaba laki ghul<u>a</u>man zakiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  He said: "Nay, I am only<br> A messenger from thy Lord,<br> (To announce) to thee<br> The gift of a holy son."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Q<u>a</u>lat ann<u>a</u> yakoonu lee ghul<u>a</u>mun walam yamsasnee basharun walam aku baghiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  She said: "How shall I<br> Have a son, seeing that<br> No man has touched me,<br> And I am not unchaste?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Q<u>a</u>la ka<u>tha</u>liki q<u>a</u>la rabbuki huwa AAalayya hayyinun walinajAAalahu <u>a</u>yatan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wara<u>h</u>matan minn<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na amran maq<u>d</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  He said: "So (it will be):<br> Thy Lord saith, "That is<br> Easy for Me: and (We<br> Wish) to appoint him<br> As a Sign unto men<br> And a Mercy from Us":<br>It is a matter<br> (So) decreed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Fa<u>h</u>amalat-hu fa<b>i</b>ntaba<u>th</u>at bihi mak<u>a</u>nan qa<u>s</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  So she conceived him,<br> And she retired with him<br> To a remote place.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Faaj<u>a</u>ah<u>a</u> almakh<u>ad</u>u il<u>a</u> ji<u>th</u>AAi a<b>l</b>nnakhlati q<u>a</u>lat y<u>a</u> laytanee mittu qabla h<u>atha</u> wakuntu nasyan mansiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  And the pains of childbirth<br> Drove her to the trunk<br> Of a palm-tree:<br> She cried (in her anguish):<br> "Ah! would that I had<br> Died before this! would that<br> I had been a thing<br> Forgotten and out of sight!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanee qad jaAAala rabbuki ta<u>h</u>taki sariyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  But (a voice) cried to her<br> From beneath the (palm-tree):<br> "Grieve not! for thy Lord<br> Hath provided a rivulet<br> Beneath thee;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wahuzzee ilayki biji<u>th</u>AAi a<b>l</b>nnakhlati tus<u>a</u>qi<u>t</u> AAalayki ru<u>t</u>aban janiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "And shake towards thyself<br> The trunk of the palm-tree:<br> It will let fall<br> Fresh ripe dates upon thee</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Fakulee wa<b>i</b>shrabee waqarree AAaynan fa-imm<u>a</u> tarayinna mina albashari a<u>h</u>adan faqoolee innee na<u>th</u>artu li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni <u>s</u>awman falan okallima alyawma insiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  "So eat and drink<br> And cool (thine) eye.<br> And if thou dost see<br> Any man, say, 'I have<br>Vowed a fast to (God)<br> Most Gracious, and this day<br> Will I enter into no talk<br> With any human being'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Faatat bihi qawmah<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>miluhu q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> maryamu laqad ji/ti shay-an fariyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  At length she brought<br> The (babe) to her people,<br> Carrying him (in her arms).<br> They said: "O Mary!<br> Truly an amazing thing<br> Hast thou brought!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Y<u>a</u> okhta h<u>a</u>roona m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na abooki imraa saw-in wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>nat ommuki baghiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "O sister of Aaron!<br> Thy father was not<br> A man of evil, nor thy<br> Mother a woman unchaste!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Faash<u>a</u>rat ilayhi q<u>a</u>loo kayfa nukallimu man k<u>a</u>na fee almahdi <u>s</u>abiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  But she pointed to the babe.<br> They said: "How can we<br> Talk to one who is<br> A child in the cradle?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Q<u>a</u>la innee AAabdu All<u>a</u>hi <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>niya alkit<u>a</u>ba wajaAAalanee nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  He said: "I am indeed<br> A servant of God:<br> He hath given me<br> Revelation and made me<br> A prophet;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  WajaAAalanee mub<u>a</u>rakan aynam<u>a</u> kuntu waaw<u>sa</u>nee bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b>zzak<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> dumtu <u>h</u>ayy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "And He hath made me<br> Blessed wheresoever I be,<br>And hath enjoined on me<br> Prayer and Charity as long<br> As I live;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wabarran biw<u>a</u>lidatee walam yajAAalnee jabb<u>a</u>ran shaqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "(He) hath made me kind<br> To my mother, and not<br> Overbearing or miserable;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa<b>al</b>ssal<u>a</u>mu AAalayya yawma wulidtu wayawma amootu wayawma obAAathu <u>h</u>ayy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "So Peace is on me<br> The day I was born,<br> The day that I die,<br> And the day that I<br> Shall be raised up<br> To life (again)"!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  <u>Tha</u>lika AAees<u>a</u> ibnu maryama qawla al<u>h</u>aqqi alla<u>th</u>ee feehi yamtaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Such (was) Jesus the son<br> Of Mary: (it is) a statement<br> Of truth, about which<br> They (vainly) dispute.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lill<u>a</u>hi an yattakhi<u>th</u>a min waladin sub<u>ha</u>nahu i<u>tha</u> qa<u>da</u> amran fa-innam<u>a</u> yaqoolu lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  It is not befitting<br> To (the majesty of) God<br> That He should beget<br> A son. Glory be to Him!<br> When He determines<br> A matter, He only says<br> To it, "Be", and it is.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha rabbee warabbukum fa<b>o</b>AAbudoohu h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un mustaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Verily God is my Lord<br> And your Lord: Him<br>Therefore serve ye: this is<br> A Way that is straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Fa<b>i</b>khtalafa al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bu min baynihim fawaylun lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min mashhadi yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  But the sects differ<br> Among themselves: and woe<br> To the Unbelievers because<br> Of the (coming) Judgment<br> Of a momentous Day!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  AsmiAA bihim waab<u>s</u>ir yawma ya/toonan<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>kini a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona alyawma fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  How plainly will they see<br> And hear, the Day that<br> They will appear before Us!<br> But the unjust to-day<br> Are in error manifest!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Waan<u>th</u>irhum yawma al<u>h</u>asrati i<u>th</u> qu<u>d</u>iya al-amru wahum fee ghaflatin wahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  But warn them of the Day<br> Of Distress, when<br> The matter will be determined:<br> For (behold,) they are negligent<br> And they do not believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Inn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu narithu al-ar<u>d</u>a waman AAalayh<u>a</u> wa-ilayn<u>a</u> yurjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  It is We Who will inherit<br> The earth, and all beings<br> Thereon: to Us will they<br> All be returned.</p> <div class="filenav">
