 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 21</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_168.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>168.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su kuloo mimm<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i <u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lan <u>t</u>ayyiban wal<u>a</u> tattabiAAoo khu<u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni innahu lakum AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>168.  O ye people!<br> Eat of what is on earth,<br> Lawful and good;<br> And do not follow<br> The footsteps of the Evil One,<br> For he is to you<br> An avowed enemy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_169.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>169.  Innam<u>a</u> ya/murukum bi<b>al</b>ssoo-i wa<b>a</b>lfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>-i waan taqooloo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>169.  For he commands you<br> What is evil<br> And shameful,<br> And that ye should say<br> Of God that of which<br> Ye have no knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_170.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>170.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu ittabiAAoo m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>loo bal nattabiAAu m<u>a</u> alfayn<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> awa law k<u>a</u>na <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ohum l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloona shay-an wal<u>a</u> yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>170.  When it is said to them:<br> "Follow what God hath revealed:"<br> They say: "Nay! we shall follow<br> The ways of our fathers."<br> What! even though their fathers<br> Were void of wisdom and guidance?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_171.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>171.  Wamathalu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo kamathali alla<u>th</u>ee yanAAiqu bim<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yasmaAAu ill<u>a</u> duAA<u>a</u>an wanid<u>a</u>an <u>s</u>ummun bukmun AAumyun fahum l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>171.  The parable of those<br> Who reject Faith is<br> As if one were to shout<br> Like a goat-herd, to things<br> That listen to nothing<br> But calls and cries:<br> Deaf, dumb, and blind,<br> They are void of wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_172.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>172.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo kuloo min <u>t</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum wa<b>o</b>shkuroo lill<u>a</u>hi in kuntum iyy<u>a</u>hu taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>172.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_173.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>173.  Innam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama AAalaykumu almaytata wa<b>al</b>ddama wala<u>h</u>ma alkhinzeeri wam<u>a</u> ohilla bihi lighayri All<u>a</u>hi famani i<u>dt</u>urra ghayra b<u>a</u>ghin wal<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>din fal<u>a</u> ithma AAalayhi inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>173.  He hath only forbidden you<br> Dead meat, and blood,<br> And the flesh of swine,<br> And that on which<br>Any other name hath been invoked<br> Besides that of God.<br> But if one is forced by necessity,<br> Without wilful disobedience,<br> Nor transgressing due limits,—<br> Then is he guiltless.<br> For God is Oft-forgiving<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_174.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>174.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yaktumoona m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wayashtaroona bihi thamanan qaleelan ol<u>a</u>-ika m<u>a</u> ya/kuloona fee bu<u>t</u>oonihim ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra wal<u>a</u> yukallimuhumu All<u>a</u>hu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wal<u>a</u> yuzakkeehim walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>174.  Those who conceal<br> God's revelations in the Book,<br> And purchase for them<br> A miserable profit,—<br> They swallow into themselves<br> Naught but Fire;<br> God will not address them<br> On the Day of Resurrection,<br> Nor purify them:<br> Grievous will be<br> Their Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_175.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>175.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena ishtarawoo a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lata bi<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lAAa<u>tha</u>ba bi<b>a</b>lmaghfirati fam<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>barahum AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>175.  They are the ones<br> Who buy Error<br> In place of Guidance<br> And Torment in place<br> Of Forgiveness.<br> Ah! what boldness<br> (They show) for the Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_176.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>176.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha nazzala alkit<u>a</u>ba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-inna alla<u>th</u>eena ikhtalafoo fee alkit<u>a</u>bi lafee shiq<u>a</u>qin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>176.  (Their doom is) because<br> God sent down the Book<br> In truth but those who seek<br> Causes of dispute in the Book<br>Are in a schism<br> Far (from the purpose).</p> <div class="filenav">
