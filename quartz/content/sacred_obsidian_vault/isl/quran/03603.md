 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVI.: Yā-Sīn (being Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa<u>a</u>yatun lahumu al-ar<u>d</u>u almaytatu a<u>h</u>yayn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> waakhrajn<u>a</u> minh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>abban faminhu ya/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  A Sign for them<br> Is the earth that is dead:<br> We do give it life,<br> And produce grain therefrom,<br> Of which ye do eat.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> jann<u>a</u>tin min nakheelin waaAAn<u>a</u>bin wafajjarn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> mina alAAuyoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And We produce therein<br> Orchards with date-palms<br> And vines, and We cause<br> Springs to gush forth therein:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Liya/kuloo min thamarihi wam<u>a</u> AAamilat-hu aydeehim afal<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  That they may enjoy<br> The fruits of this (artistry):<br> It was not their hands<br> That made this:<br> Will they not then give thanks?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Sub<u>ha</u>na alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa al-azw<u>a</u>ja kullah<u>a</u> mimm<u>a</u> tunbitu al-ar<u>d</u>u wamin anfusihim wamimm<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Glory to God, Who created<br> In pairs all things that<br> The earth produces, as well as<br> Their own (human) kind<br> And (other) things of which<br> They have no knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa<u>a</u>yatun lahumu allaylu naslakhu minhu a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra fa-i<u>tha</u> hum mu<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  And a Sign for them<br> Is the Night: We withdraw<br> Therefrom the Day, and behold<br> They are plunged in darkness;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<b>al</b>shshamsu tajree limustaqarrin lah<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika taqdeeru alAAazeezi alAAaleem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  And the Sun<br> Runs his course<br> For a period determined<br> For him: that is<br> The decree of (Him),<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> The All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wa<b>a</b>lqamara qaddarn<u>a</u>hu man<u>a</u>zila <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>da ka<b>a</b>lAAurjooni alqadeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  And the Moon,<br> We have measured for her<br> Mansions (to traverse)<br> Till she returns<br> Like the old (and withered)<br> Lower part of a date-stalk.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  L<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshamsu yanbaghee lah<u>a</u> an tudrika alqamara wal<u>a</u> allaylu s<u>a</u>biqu a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wakullun fee falakin yasba<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  It is not permitted<br> To the Sun to catch up<br> The Moon, nor can<br> The Night outstrip the Day:<br> Each (just) swims along (its own) orbit<br> (According to Law).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<u>a</u>yatun lahum ann<u>a</u> <u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u> <u>th</u>urriyyatahum fee alfulki almash<u>h</u>oon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  And a Sign for them<br> Is that We bore<br> Their race (through the Flood)<br> In the loaded Ark;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wakhalaqn<u>a</u> lahum min mithlihi m<u>a</u> yarkaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  And We have created<br> For them similar (vessels)<br> On which they ride.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wa-in nasha/ nughriqhum fal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>areekha lahum wal<u>a</u> hum yunqa<u>th</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  If it were Our Will,<br> We could drown them:<br> Then would there be<br> No helper (to hear<br> Their cry), nor could<br> They be delivered,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Ill<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan minn<u>a</u> wamat<u>a</u>AAan il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Except by way of Mercy<br> From Us, and by way<br> Of (worldly) convenience<br> (To serve them) for a time.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu ittaqoo m<u>a</u> bayna aydeekum wam<u>a</u> khalfakum laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  When they are told,<br> "Fear ye that which is<br> Before you and that which<br> Will be after you, in order<br> That ye may receive Mercy,"<br> (They turn back).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wam<u>a</u> ta/teehim min <u>a</u>yatin min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim ill<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo AAanh<u>a</u> muAAri<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Not a Sign comes to them<br> From among the Signs<br> Of their Lord, but they<br> Turn away therefrom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum anfiqoo mimm<u>a</u> razaqakumu All<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo anu<u>t</u>AAimu man law yash<u>a</u>o All<u>a</u>hu a<u>t</u>AAamahu in antum ill<u>a</u> fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And when they are told,<br> "Spend ye of (the bounties)<br> With which God<br> Has provided you," the Unbelievers<br> Say to those who believe:<br> "Shall we then feed those<br> Whom, if God had so willed,<br> He would have fed, (Himself)?—<br>Ye are in nothing<br> But manifest error."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Further, they say, "When<br> Will this promise (come to pass),<br> If what ye say is true?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  M<u>a</u> yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atan w<u>ah</u>idatan ta/khu<u>th</u>uhum wahum yakhi<u>ss</u>imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  They will not (have<br> To) wait for aught<br> But a single Blast:<br> It will seize them while<br> They are yet disputing<br> Among themselves!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona taw<u>s</u>iyatan wal<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ahlihim yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  No (chance) will they then<br> Have, by will, to dispose<br> (Of their affairs), nor<br> To return to their own people!</p> <div class="filenav">
