 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 19</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Wayastaftoonaka fee a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i quli All<u>a</u>hu yufteekum feehinna wam<u>a</u> yutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum fee alkit<u>a</u>bi fee yat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i all<u>a</u>tee l<u>a</u> tu/toonahunna m<u>a</u> kutiba lahunna watarghaboona an tanki<u>h</u>oohunna wa<b>a</b>lmusta<u>d</u>AAafeena mina alwild<u>a</u>ni waan taqoomoo lilyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i wam<u>a</u> tafAAaloo min khayrin fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bihi AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  They ask thy instruction<br> Concerning the Women<br> Say: God doth<br> Instruct you about them:<br> And (remember) what hath<br> Been rehearsed unto you<br> In the Book, concerning<br> The orphans of women to whom<br> Ye give not the portions<br> Prescribed, and yet whom ye<br> Desire to marry, as also<br> Concerning the children<br> Who are weak and oppressed:<br> That ye stand firm<br> For justice to orphans.<br> There is not a good deed<br> Which ye do, but God<br> Is well-acquainted therewith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Wa-ini imraatun kh<u>a</u>fat min baAAlih<u>a</u> nushoozan aw iAAr<u>ad</u>an fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayhim<u>a</u> an yu<u>s</u>li<u>ha</u> baynahum<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ul<u>h</u>an wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>ul<u>h</u>u khayrun wao<u>hd</u>irati al-anfusu a<b>l</b>shshu<u>hh</u>a wa-in tu<u>h</u>sinoo watattaqoo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  If a wife fears<br> Cruelty or desertion<br> On her husband's part,<br> There is no blame on them<br> If they arrange<br> An amicable settlement<br> Between themselves;<br> And such settlement is best;<br>Even though men's souls<br> Are swayed by greed.<br> But if ye do good<br> And practise self-restraint,<br> God is well-acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Walan tasta<u>t</u>eeAAoo an taAAdiloo bayna a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i walaw <u>h</u>ara<u>s</u>tum fal<u>a</u> tameeloo kulla almayli fata<u>th</u>arooh<u>a</u> ka<b>a</b>lmuAAallaqati wa-in tu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oo watattaqoo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  Ye are never able<br> To be fair and just<br> As between women,<br> Even if it is<br> Your ardent desire:<br> But turn not away<br> (From a woman) altogether,<br> So as to leave her (as it were)<br> Hanging (in the air).<br> If ye come to a friendly<br> Understanding, and practise<br> Self-restraint, God is<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Wa-in yatafarraq<u>a</u> yughni All<u>a</u>hu kullan min saAAatihi wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu w<u>a</u>siAAan <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  But if they disagree<br> (And must part), God<br> Will provide abundance<br> For all from His<br> All-reaching bounty:<br> For God is He<br> That careth for all<br> And is Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i walaqad wa<u>ss</u>ayn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablikum wa-iyy<u>a</u>kum ani ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa-in takfuroo fa-inna lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghaniyyan <u>h</u>ameed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  To God belong all things<br> In the heavens and on earth.<br>Verily We have directed<br> The People of the Book<br> Before you, and you (O Muslims).<br> To fear God. But if ye<br> Deny Him, to! unto God<br> Belong all things<br> In the heavens and on earth,<br> And God is free<br> Of all wants, worthy<br> Of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  Yea, unto God belong<br> All things in the heavens<br> And on earth, and enough<br> Is God to carry through<br> All affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  In yasha/ yu<u>th</u>hibkum ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su waya/ti bi-<u>a</u>khareena wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika qadeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  If it were His Will,<br> He could destroy you,<br> O mankind, and create<br> Another race; for He<br> Hath power this to do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Man k<u>a</u>na yureedu thaw<u>a</u>ba a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> faAAinda All<u>a</u>hi thaw<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu sameeAAan ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  If any one desires<br> A reward in this life,<br> In God's (gift) is the reward<br> (Both) of this life<br> And of the Hereafter:<br> For God is He that heareth<br> And seeth (all things).</p> <div class="filenav">
