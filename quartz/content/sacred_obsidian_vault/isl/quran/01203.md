 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>ee ishtar<u>a</u>hu min mi<u>s</u>ra li<b>i</b>mraatihi akrimee mathw<u>a</u>hu AAas<u>a</u> an yanfaAAan<u>a</u> aw nattakhi<u>th</u>ahu waladan waka<u>tha</u>lika makkann<u>a</u> liyoosufa fee al-ar<u>d</u>i walinuAAallimahu min ta/weeli al-a<u>ha</u>deethi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu gh<u>a</u>libun AAal<u>a</u> amrihi wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  The man in Egypt<br> Who bought him, said<br>To his wife: "Make his stay<br> (Among us) honourable:<br> Maybe he will bring us<br> Much good, or we shall<br> Adopt him as a son."<br> Thus did We establish<br> Joseph in the land,<br> That We might teach him<br> The interpretation of stories<br> (And events). And God<br> Hath full power and control<br> Over His affairs; but most<br> Among mankind know it not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Walamm<u>a</u> balagha ashuddahu <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>ukman waAAilman waka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  When Joseph attained<br> His full manhood, We gave him<br>Power and knowledge: thus do We<br> Reward those who do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  War<u>a</u>wadat-hu allatee huwa fee baytih<u>a</u> AAan nafsihi waghallaqati al-abw<u>a</u>ba waq<u>a</u>lat hayta laka q<u>a</u>la maAA<u>ath</u>a All<u>a</u>hi innahu rabbee a<u>h</u>sana mathw<u>a</u>ya innahu l<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  But she in whose house<br> He was, sought to seduce him<br> From his (true) self: she fastened<br> The doors, and said:<br> "Now come, thou (dear one)!"<br> He said: "God forbid!<br> Truly (thy husband) is<br> My lord! he made<br> My sojourn agreeable!<br> Truly to no good<br> Come those who do wrong!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Walaqad hammat bihi wahamma bih<u>a</u> lawl<u>a</u> an ra<u>a</u> burh<u>a</u>na rabbihi ka<u>tha</u>lika lina<u>s</u>rifa AAanhu a<b>l</b>ssoo-a wa<b>a</b>lfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>a innahu min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And (with passion) did she<br> Desire him, and he would<br> Have desired her, but that<br> He saw the evidence<br> Of his Lord: thus<br> (Did We order) that We<br> Might turn away from him<br> (All) evil and shameful deeds:<br>For he was one of Our servants,<br> Sincere and purified.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa<b>i</b>stabaq<u>a</u> alb<u>a</u>ba waqaddat qamee<u>s</u>ahu min duburin waalfay<u>a</u> sayyidah<u>a</u> lad<u>a</u> alb<u>a</u>bi q<u>a</u>lat m<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>o man ar<u>a</u>da bi-ahlika soo-an ill<u>a</u> an yusjana aw AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  So they both raced each other<br> To the door, and she<br> Tore his shirt from the back:<br> They both found her lord<br> Near the door. She said:<br> "What is the (fitting) punishment<br> For one who formed<br> An evil design against<br> Thy wife, but prison<br> Or a grievous chastisement?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Q<u>a</u>la hiya r<u>a</u>wadatnee AAan nafsee washahida sh<u>a</u>hidun min ahlih<u>a</u> in k<u>a</u>na qamee<u>s</u>uhu qudda min qubulin fa<u>s</u>adaqat wahuwa mina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  He said: "It was she<br> That sought to seduce me—<br> From my (true) self." And one<br> Of her household saw (this)<br> And bore witness, (thus):—<br> "If it be that his shirt<br> Is rent from the front, then<br> Is her tale true,<br> And he is a liar!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>na qamee<u>s</u>uhu qudda min duburin faka<u>th</u>abat wahuwa mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "But if it be that his shirt<br> Is torn from the back,<br>Then is she the liar,<br> And he is telling the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Falamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u> qamee<u>s</u>ahu qudda min duburin q<u>a</u>la innahu min kaydikunna inna kaydakunna AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  So when he saw his shirt,—<br> That it was torn at the back,<br> (Her husband) said: "Behold!<br> It is a snare of you women!<br> Truly, mighty is your snare!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Yoosufu aAAri<u>d</u> AAan h<u>atha</u> wa<b>i</b>staghfiree li<u>th</u>anbiki innaki kunti mina alkh<u>at</u>i-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "O Joseph, pass this over!<br> (O wife), ask forgiveness<br> For thy sin, for truly<br> Thou hast been at fault!"</p> <div class="filenav">
