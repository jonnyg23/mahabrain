 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo wa<b>al</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bi-eena man <u>a</u>mana biAll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an falahum ajruhum AAinda rabbihim wal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Those who believe (in the Qur-ān),<br> And those who follow the Jewish (scriptures),<br> And the Christians and the Sabians,—<br> Any who believe in God<br> And the Last Day,<br> And work righteousness,<br> Shall have their reward<br>With their Lord: on them<br> Shall be no fear, nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> meeth<u>a</u>qakum warafaAAn<u>a</u> fawqakumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oora khu<u>th</u>oo m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>kum biquwwatin wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo m<u>a</u> feehi laAAallakum tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  And remember We took<br> Your Covenant<br> And We raised above you<br> (The towering height)<br> Of Mount (Sinai):<br> (Saying): "Hold firmly<br> To what We have given you<br> And bring (ever) to remembrance<br> What is therein:<br> Perchance ye may fear God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Thumma tawallaytum min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika falawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wara<u>h</u>matuhu lakuntum mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  But ye turned back thereafter:<br> Had it not been for the Grace<br> And Mercy of God to you,<br> Ye had surely been<br> Among the lost.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Walaqad AAalimtumu alla<u>th</u>eena iAAtadaw minkum fee a<b>l</b>ssabti faquln<u>a</u> lahum koonoo qiradatan kh<u>a</u>si-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  And well ye knew<br> Those amongst you<br> Who transgressed<br> In the matter of the Sabbath:<br> We said to them:<br> "Be ye apes,<br> Despised and rejected."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  FajaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> nak<u>a</u>lan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> khalfah<u>a</u> wamawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atan lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  So We made it an example<br> To their own time<br>And to their posterity,<br> And a lesson<br> To those who fear God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> liqawmihi inna All<u>a</u>ha ya/murukum an ta<u>th</u>ba<u>h</u>oo baqaratan q<u>a</u>loo atattakhi<u>th</u>un<u>a</u> huzuwan q<u>a</u>la aAAoo<u>th</u>u biAll<u>a</u>hi an akoona mina alj<u>a</u>hileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  And remember Moses said<br> To his people: "God commands<br> That ye sacrifice a heifer."<br> They said: "Makest thou<br> A laughing-stock of us?"<br> He said: "God save me<br> From being an ignorant (fool)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Q<u>a</u>loo odAAu lan<u>a</u> rabbaka yubayyin lan<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> hiya q<u>a</u>la innahu yaqoolu innah<u>a</u> baqaratun l<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>ri<u>d</u>un wal<u>a</u> bikrun AAaw<u>a</u>nun bayna <u>tha</u>lika fa<b>i</b>fAAaloo m<u>a</u> tu/maroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  They said: "Beseech on our behalf<br> Thy Lord to make plain to us<br> What (heifer) it is!"<br> He said: "He says: the heifer<br> Should be neither too old<br> Nor too young, but of middling<br> Age: now do what ye are commanded!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Q<u>a</u>loo odAAu lan<u>a</u> rabbaka yubayyin lan<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lawnuh<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la innahu yaqoolu innah<u>a</u> baqaratun <u>s</u>afr<u>a</u>o f<u>a</u>qiAAun lawnuh<u>a</u> tasurru a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  They said: "Beseech on our behalf<br> Thy Lord to make plain to us<br> Her colour." He said: "He says:<br> A fawn-coloured heifer,<br> Pure and rich in tone,<br> The admiration of beholders!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Q<u>a</u>loo odAAu lan<u>a</u> rabbaka yubayyin lan<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> hiya inna albaqara tash<u>a</u>baha AAalayn<u>a</u> wa-inn<u>a</u> in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lamuhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  They said: "Beseech on our behalf<br> Thy Lord to make plain to us<br> What she is: to us are all heifers<br> Alike: we wish indeed for guidance,<br> If God wills."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Q<u>a</u>la innahu yaqoolu innah<u>a</u> baqaratun l<u>a</u> <u>th</u>aloolun tutheeru al-ar<u>d</u>a wal<u>a</u> tasqee al<u>h</u>artha musallamatun l<u>a</u> shiyata feeh<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo al-<u>a</u>na ji/ta bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi fa<u>th</u>aba<u>h</u>ooh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>doo yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  He said: "He says: a heifer<br> Not trained to till the soil<br> Or water the fields: sound<br> And without blemish." They said:<br> "Now hast thou brought<br> The truth." Then they offered<br> Her in sacrifice,<br> But not with good-will.</p> <div class="filenav">
