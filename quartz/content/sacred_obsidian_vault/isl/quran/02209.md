 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha sakhkhara lakum m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>a</b>lfulka tajree fee alba<u>h</u>ri bi-amrihi wayumsiku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a an taqaAAa AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>nihi inna All<u>a</u>ha bi<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si laraoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Seest thou not that God<br> Has made subject to you (men)<br> All that is on the earth,<br> And the ships that sail<br> Through the sea by His command?<br> He withholds the sky (rain)<br> From falling on the earth<br> Except by His leave:<br> For God is Most Kind<br> And Most Merciful to man.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>kum thumma yumeetukum thumma yu<u>h</u>yeekum inna al-ins<u>a</u>na lakafoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  It is He Who gave you life,<br> Will cause you to die,<br> And will again give you<br> Life: truly man is<br> A most ungrateful creature!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Likulli ommatin jaAAaln<u>a</u> mansakan hum n<u>a</u>sikoohu fal<u>a</u> yun<u>a</u>ziAAunnaka fee al-amri wa<b>o</b>dAAu il<u>a</u> rabbika innaka laAAal<u>a</u> hudan mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  To every People have We<br> Appointed rites and ceremonies<br> Which they must follow:<br> Let them not then dispute<br> With thee on the matter,<br> But do thou invite (them)<br> To thy Lord: for thou art<br> Assuredly on the Right Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Wa-in j<u>a</u>dalooka faquli All<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  If they do wrangle with thee,<br> Say, "God knows best<br> What it is ye are doing."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  All<u>a</u>hu ya<u>h</u>kumu baynakum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati feem<u>a</u> kuntum feehi takhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  "God will judge between you<br> On the Day of Judgment<br> Concerning the matters in which<br> Ye differ."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Alam taAAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i inna <u>tha</u>lika fee kit<u>a</u>bin inna <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Knowest thou not that<br> God knows all that is<br> In heaven and on earth?<br> Indeed it is all<br> In a record, and that<br> Is easy for God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  WayaAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> lam yunazzil bihi sul<u>ta</u>nan wam<u>a</u> laysa lahum bihi AAilmun wam<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena min na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Yet they worship, besides God,<br> Things for which no authority<br> Has been sent down to them,<br> And of which they have<br> (Really) no knowledge:<br> For those that do wrong<br> There is no helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> bayyin<u>a</u>tin taAArifu fee wujoohi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo almunkara yak<u>a</u>doona yas<u>t</u>oona bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yatloona AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> qul afaonabbi-okum bisharrin min <u>tha</u>likum a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru waAAadah<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  When Our Clear Signs<br> Are rehearsed to them,<br> Thou wilt notice a denial<br> On the faces of the Unbelievers!<br> They nearly attack with violence<br> Those who rehearse Our Signs<br> To them. Say, "Shall I<br> Tell you of something<br> (Far) worse than these Signs?<br> It is the Fire (of Hell)!<br>God has promised it<br> To the Unbelievers!<br> And evil is that destination!"</p> <div class="filenav">
