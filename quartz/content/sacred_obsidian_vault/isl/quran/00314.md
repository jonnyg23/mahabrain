 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 14</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> ta/kuloo a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> a<u>d</u>AA<u>a</u>fan mu<u>da</u>AAafatan wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  ye who believe!<br> Devour not Usury,<br> Doubled and multiplied;<br> But fear God; that<br> Ye may (really) prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra allatee oAAiddat lilk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  Fear the Fire, which is prepared<br> For those who reject Faith:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>al</b>rrasoola laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  And obey God<br> And the Apostle;<br> That ye may obtain mercy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Was<u>a</u>riAAoo il<u>a</u> maghfiratin min rabbikum wajannatin AAar<u>d</u>uh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u oAAiddat lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  Be quick in the race<br> For forgiveness from your Lord,<br> And for a Garden whose width<br> Is that (of the whole)<br> Of the heavens<br> And of the earth,<br> Prepared for the righteous,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yunfiqoona fee a<b>l</b>ssarr<u>a</u>-i wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>arr<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>imeena alghay<i><u>th</u></i>a wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>feena AAani a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>ibbu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  Those who spend (freely),<br> Whether in prosperity,<br> Or in adversity;<br> Who restrain anger,<br> And pardon (all) men;—<br> For God loves those<br> Who do good;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> faAAaloo f<u>ah</u>ishatan aw <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo anfusahum <u>th</u>akaroo All<u>a</u>ha fa<b>i</b>staghfaroo li<u>th</u>unoobihim waman yaghfiru a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>unooba ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu walam yu<u>s</u>irroo AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> faAAaloo wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  And those who,<br> Having done something<br> To be ashamed of,<br> Or wronged their own souls,<br> Earnestly bring God to mind,<br> And ask for forgiveness<br> For their sins,<br> And who can forgive<br> Sins except God?—<br> And are never obstinate<br> In persisting knowingly<br> In (the wrong) they have done</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika jaz<u>a</u>ohum maghfiratun min rabbihim wajann<u>a</u>tun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> waniAAma ajru alAA<u>a</u>mileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  For such the reward<br> Is forgiveness from their Lord,<br> And Gardens with rivers<br> Flowing underneath,—<br> An eternal dwelling:<br> How excellent a recompense<br> For those who work (and strive)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  Qad khalat min qablikum sunanun faseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  Many were the Ways of Life<br> That have passed away<br> Before you: travel through<br> The earth, and see what was<br> The end of those<br> Who rejected Truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  H<u>atha</u> bay<u>a</u>nun li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wahudan wamawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atun lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>138.  Here is a plain statement<br> To men, a guidance<br> And instruction to those<br> Who fear God!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Wal<u>a</u> tahinoo wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanoo waantumu al-aAAlawna in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  So lose not heart,<br> Nor fall into despair:<br> For ye must gain mastery<br> If ye are true in Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  In yamsaskum qar<u>h</u>un faqad massa alqawma qar<u>h</u>un mithluhu watilka al-ayy<u>a</u>mu nud<u>a</u>wiluh<u>a</u> bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waliyaAAlama All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wayattakhi<u>th</u>a minkum shuhad<u>a</u>a wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  If a wound hath touched you,<br> Be sure a similar wound<br> Hath touched the others.<br> Such days (of varying fortunes)<br> We give to men and men<br> By turns: that God may know<br> Those that believe,<br> And that He may take<br> To Himself from your ranks<br> Martyr-witnesses (to Truth).<br> And God loveth not<br> Those that do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Waliyuma<u>hh</u>i<u>s</u>a All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wayam<u>h</u>aqa alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>141.  God's object also is to purge<br> Those that are true in Faith<br> And to deprive of blessing<br> Those that resist Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  Am <u>h</u>asibtum an tadkhuloo aljannata walamm<u>a</u> yaAAlami All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena j<u>a</u>hadoo minkum wayaAAlama a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  Did ye think that ye<br> Would enter Heaven<br> Without God testing<br> Those of you who fought hard<br> (In His Cause) and<br> Remained steadfast?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Walaqad kuntum tamannawna almawta min qabli an talqawhu faqad raaytumoohu waantum tan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  Ye did indeed<br> Wish for Death<br> Before ye met him:<br> Now ye have seen him<br> With your own eyes,<br> (And ye flinch!)</p> <div class="filenav">
