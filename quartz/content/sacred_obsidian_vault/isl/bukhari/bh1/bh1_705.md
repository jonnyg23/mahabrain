 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:703</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:</i></p>
 I saw that whenever Allah's Apostle stood for the prayer, he used to 
 raise both his hands up to the shoulders, and used to do the same on 
 saying the Takbir for bowing and on raising his head from it and used 
 to say, "Sami a-l-lahu Liman hamida". But he did not do that (i.e. 
 raising his hands) in prostrations.
