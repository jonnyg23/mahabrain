   sub Figura XXVII</h1>

 <blockquote> 
   <blockquote> 
     <p><font size="2">Although the main body of this Liber is Class A, the attribution 
       of letters to the English Alphabet is not of that level. These letters were 
       added at a later date by Crowley, and with the later revisions of Liber 
       DCCLXXVII, form one approach toward an English Qabalah. These letters are 
       positioned to the left of the trigrams in this text. <em>--- Bill Heidrick</em></font></p>
   </blockquote>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center"><font size="5"><strong>Liber Trigrammaton</strong></font></p>
 <p align="center"><strong>sub figura<br>
   XXVII</strong></p>
 <p align="center">Being the Book of the Trigrams of the Mutations of the Tao with 
   the Yin and the Yang</p>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   *<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Here is Nothing under its three forms. It is not, yet informeth all things.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   *<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Now cometh the glory of the Single One, as an imperfection and stain.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   *<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>But by the Weak One the Mother was it equilibrated.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   ---<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Also the purity was divided by Strength, the force of the Demiurge.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   - -<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>And the Cross was formulated in the Universe that as yet was not.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   *<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>But now the Imperfection became manifest, presiding over the fading of perfection.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   *<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Also the Woman arose, and veiled the Upper Heaven with her body of stars.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   ---<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Now then a giant arose, of terrible strength; and asserted the Spirit in 
     a secret rite.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   ---<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>And the Master of the Temple balancing all things arose; his stature was 
     above the Heaven and below Earth and Hell.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">*<br>
   - -<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Against him the Brothers of the Left-hand Path, confusing the symbols. They 
     concealed their horror [in this symbol]; for in truth they were </p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center"><font size="2">- -<br>
   ---<br>
   *</font><br>
   <br>
   <br>
   *<br>
   - -<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>The master flamed forth as a star and set a guard of Water in every Abyss.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   *<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Also certain secret ones concealed the Light of Purity in themselves, protecting 
     it from the Persecutions.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   *<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Likewise also did certain sons and daughters of Hermes and of Aphrodite, 
     more openly</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   *<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>But the Enemy confused them. They pretended to conceal that Light, that they 
     might betray it, and profane it.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   *<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Yet certain holy nuns concealed the secret in songs upon the lyre.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   ---<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Now did the Horror of Time pervert all things, hiding the Purity with a loathsome 
     thing, a thing unnameable.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   - -<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Yea, and there arose sensualists upon the firmament, as a foul stain of storm 
     upon the sky.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   ---<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>And the Black Brothers raised their heads; yea, they unveiled themselves 
     without shame or fear.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   - -<br>
   *</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Also there rose up a soul of filth and of weakness, and it corrupted all 
     the rule of the Tao.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   ---<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Then only was Heaven established to bear sway; for only in the lowest corruption 
     is form manifest.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   ---<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Also did Heaven manifest in violent light,</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   - -<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>And in soft light.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   ---<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Then were the waters gathered together from the heaven,</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">---<br>
   - -<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>And a crust of earth concealed the core of flame.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   ---<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Around the globe gathered the wide air,</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   - -<br>
   ---</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>And men began to light fires upon the earth.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center">- -<br>
   - -<br>
   - -</p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>Therefore was the end of it sorrow; yet in that sorrow a sixfold star of 
     glory whereby they might see to return unto the stainless Abode; yea, unto 
     the Stainless Abode.</p>
 </blockquote>
