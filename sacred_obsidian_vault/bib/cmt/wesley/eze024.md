 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 24</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#001">Ezekiel 24:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">eze 24:1</a></font></span></p>In the ninth year - Of Zedekiah's reign. Came unto me - The prophet was now in Babylon. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#002">Ezekiel 24:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 24:2</a></font></span></p>Set himself - Sat down to besiege. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#004">Ezekiel 24:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 24:4</a></font></span></p>Every good piece - All the chief of the inhabitants of the land, the wealthiest, who will fly from their country - houses to live in safety in Jerusalem: the most war - like, who will betake themselves to Jerusalem for its defence. Fill it - With those pieces that are biggest, fullest of marrow, and which are divided according to the bones; these are the principal members of the state, the king, princes, priests, magistrates, and the most wealthy citizens. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#005">Ezekiel 24:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">eze 24:5</a></font></span></p>The bones - Not of the pieces to be boiled, but of the many innocents murdered in Jerusalem; for their blood crieth for vengeance, and their bones scattered on the face of the earth, will both make and maintain this fire. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#006">Ezekiel 24:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">eze 24:6</a></font></span></p>The bloody city - Jerusalem. Whose scum - Her wickedness is still within her. Piece by piece - One piece after another 'till all be consumed. No lot - Lots are for saving some, but here shall be no sparing any. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#007">Ezekiel 24:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">eze 24:7</a></font></span></p>The blood - Innocent blood which she hath shed. The top of a rock - Where it might be long seen. To cover it - These butchers of innocent ones leave their blood uncovered. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#008">Ezekiel 24:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">eze 24:8</a></font></span></p>I have set - I will openly punish, and in such a manner as shall not be soon forgotten. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#010">Ezekiel 24:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">eze 24:10</a></font></span></p>And spice it well - To express this justice, that is acceptable to God and men. The bones - The greatest, strongest, and firmest of the Jews shall perish in this fiery indignation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#011">Ezekiel 24:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">eze 24:11</a></font></span></p>The filthiness - A type of the unreformed sinfulness of the city. Molten - That their wickedness may be taken away with their persons, and city. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#012">Ezekiel 24:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">eze 24:12</a></font></span></p>She - Jerusalem. With lies - Her allies, their promises, their forces, and their idols, all prove a lie to the house of Judah. Her scum - Her unrepented sins shall be punished in the fire that burns their city. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#013">Ezekiel 24:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">eze 24:13</a></font></span></p>Lewdness - Or obstinacy and boldness. Purged thee - Used all means to purge thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#016">Ezekiel 24:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">eze 24:16</a></font></span></p>With a stroke - A sudden stroke, by my own immediate hand. We know not how soon the desire of our eyes may be removed from us. Death is a stroke, which the most pious, the most useful, the most amiable are not exempted from. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#017">Ezekiel 24:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">eze 24:17</a></font></span></p>Bind the tire - Adorn thy head, as thou wast used to do; go not bare - headed as a mourner. Thy shoes - ln great mournings the Jews went bare - footed. Cover not thy lips - It was a custom among them to cover the upper lip. Eat not - Of thy neighbours and friends, who were wont to visit their mourning friends, and send in choice provision to their houses. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#018">Ezekiel 24:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">eze 24:18</a></font></span></p>I spake - Told them what I expected would be. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#021">Ezekiel 24:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">eze 24:21</a></font></span></p>Profane - Cast off, and put into the hands of Heathens. The excellency of your strength - So it was while God's presence was there. The desire - As much your desire, as my wife was mine; most dear to you. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#022">Ezekiel 24:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">eze 24:22</a></font></span></p>Ye shall do - When you are in captivity, where you may not use your own customs. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#023">Ezekiel 24:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">eze 24:23</a></font></span></p>Pine away - You shall languish with secret sorrow, when you shall not dare to shew it openly. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#025">Ezekiel 24:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">eze 24:25</a></font></span></p>Their strength - Their walls and fortifications. The joy - All their public and private joys and hopes shall be destroyed in the destruction of the kingdom, and their children. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#026">Ezekiel 24:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">eze 24:26</a></font></span></p>To hear it - To give thee a narrative of all he had seen. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#027">Ezekiel 24:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">eze 24:27</a></font></span></p>No more dumb - From this prophecy for eighteen months during the siege, he does not prophesy of Israel, but of other nations. Thou shalt be a sign - Until the event shall convince the Jews, thou shalt by sign, signify to them, what is coming.
