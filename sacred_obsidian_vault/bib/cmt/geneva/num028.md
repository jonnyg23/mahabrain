 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 28</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#002">Numbers 28:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">num 28:2</a></font></span></p>Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, My offering, [and] (a) my bread for my sacrifices made by fire, [for] a sweet savour unto me, shall ye observe to offer unto me in their due season. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) By bread he means all manner of sacrifice.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#008">Numbers 28:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">num 28:8</a></font></span></p>And the other lamb shalt thou offer at even: as the meat offering of the morning, and as the drink offering thereof, thou shalt offer [it], (b) a sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) The meat offering and drink offering of the evening sacrifice.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#009">Numbers 28:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">num 28:9</a></font></span></p>And on the sabbath day two lambs of the first year without spot, and two (c) tenth deals of flour [for] a meat offering, mingled with oil, and the drink offering thereof: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Of the measure ephah.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#010">Numbers 28:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">num 28:10</a></font></span></p>[This is] the burnt offering of every sabbath, beside the (d) continual burnt offering, and his drink offering. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Which was offered every day at morning and evening.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#014">Numbers 28:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">num 28:14</a></font></span></p>And their (e) drink offerings shall be half an hin of wine unto a bullock, and the third [part] of an hin unto a ram, and a fourth [part] of an hin unto a lamb: this [is] the burnt offering of every month throughout the months of the year. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, the wine that will be poured on the sacrifice.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#018">Numbers 28:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">num 28:18</a></font></span></p>In the first day [shall be] an holy (f) convocation; ye shall do no manner of servile work [therein]: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Or, solemn assembly.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num028.htm#026">Numbers 28:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">num 28:26</a></font></span></p>Also in the day of the firstfruits, when ye bring a new meat offering unto the LORD, after your (g) weeks [be out], ye shall have an holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) In counting seven weeks from the Passover to Whit Sunday, as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev023.htm#015">Lev 23:15</a>).
