 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 63</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#001">Psalms 63:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 63:1</a></font></span></p>"A Psalm of David, when he was in the (a) wilderness of Judah." O God, thou [art] my God; early will I seek thee: my soul (b) thirsteth for thee, my flesh longeth for thee in a dry and thirsty land, where no water is; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) That is, of Ziph (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1023.htm#014">Sa1 23:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) Though he was both hungry and in great distress, yet he made God above all meat and drink.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#002">Psalms 63:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 63:2</a></font></span></p>To see thy power and thy glory, so [as] (c) I have seen thee in the sanctuary. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) In this misery I exercise myself in the contemplation of your power and glory, as if I were in the sanctuary.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#005">Psalms 63:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 63:5</a></font></span></p>My soul shall be satisfied as [with] (d) marrow and fatness; and my mouth shall praise [thee] with joyful lips: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) The remembrance of your favour is more sweet to me than all the pleasures and dainties of the world.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#008">Psalms 63:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 63:8</a></font></span></p>My soul (e) followeth hard after thee: thy right hand upholdeth me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) He assures himself by the Spirit of God to have the gift of constancy.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#010">Psalms 63:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 63:10</a></font></span></p>(f) They shall fall by the sword: they shall be a portion for foxes. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) He prophecies of the destruction of Saul and they who take his part, whose bodies will not be buried but be devoured with wild beasts.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#011">Psalms 63:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 63:11</a></font></span></p>But the king shall rejoice in God; every one that (g) sweareth by him shall glory: but the mouth of them that speak lies shall be stopped. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) All who swear by God correctly or profess him will rejoice in this worthy king.
