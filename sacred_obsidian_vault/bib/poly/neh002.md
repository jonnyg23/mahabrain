 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/neh002.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  And it came to pass<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> in the month<sup><a href="h2320.htm">2320</a></sup> Nisan,<sup><a href="h5212.htm">5212</a></sup> in the twentieth<sup><a href="h6242.htm">6242</a></sup> year<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> of Artaxerxes<sup><a href="h0783.htm">783</a></sup> the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> that wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> was before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him: and I took up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the wine,<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> and gave<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> it unto the king.<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> Now I had not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> been<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> formerly sad<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> in his presence.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Wherefore the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto me, Why<sup><a href="h4069.htm">4069</a></sup> is thy countenance<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> sad,<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> seeing thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> sick?<sup><a href="h2470.htm">2470</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> is nothing<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> else but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> sorrow<sup><a href="h7455.htm">7455</a></sup> of heart.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> Then I was very<sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> sore<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> afraid,<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  And said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> Let the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> live<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> why<sup><a href="h4069.htm">4069</a></sup> should not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> my countenance<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> be sad,<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the city,<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> the place<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of my fathers'<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> sepulchers,<sup><a href="h6913.htm">6913</a></sup> lieth waste,<sup><a href="h2720.htm">2720</a></sup> and the gates<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> thereof are consumed<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> with fire?<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Then the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto me, For<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> dost thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> make request?<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> So I prayed<sup><a href="h6419.htm">6419</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of heaven.<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  And I said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it please<sup><a href="h2895.htm">2895</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> and if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thy servant<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> have found favor<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> in thy sight,<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou wouldest send<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> me unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> Judah,<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the city<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> of my fathers'<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> sepulchers,<sup><a href="h6913.htm">6913</a></sup> that I may build<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>6  And the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto me, (the queen<sup><a href="h7694.htm">7694</a></sup> also sitting<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> by<sup><a href="h0681.htm">681</a></sup> him,) For<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> how long<sup><a href="h4970.htm">4970</a></sup> shall thy journey<sup><a href="h4109.htm">4109</a></sup> be?<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> and when<sup><a href="h4970.htm">4970</a></sup> wilt thou return?<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> So it pleased<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> to send<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> me; and I set<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> him a time.<sup><a href="h2165.htm">2165</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Moreover I said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it please<sup><a href="h2895.htm">2895</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> let letters<sup><a href="h0107.htm">107</a></sup> be given<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me to<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the governors<sup><a href="h6346.htm">6346</a></sup> beyond<sup><a href="h5676.htm">5676</a></sup> the river,<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they may convey me over<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> till<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> Judah;<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  And a letter<sup><a href="h0107.htm">107</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> Asaph<sup><a href="h0623.htm">623</a></sup> the keeper<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> of the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> forest,<sup><a href="h6508.htm">6508</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he may give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me timber<sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> to make beams<sup><a href="h7136.htm">7136</a></sup> for <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the gates<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> of the palace<sup><a href="h1002.htm">1002</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> appertained to the house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> and for the wall<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> of the city,<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> and for the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I shall enter<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into.<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> And the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> granted<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me, according to the good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of my God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>9  Then I came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the governors<sup><a href="h6346.htm">6346</a></sup> beyond<sup><a href="h5676.htm">5676</a></sup> the river,<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> and gave<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> them <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> letters.<sup><a href="h0107.htm">107</a></sup> Now the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> had sent<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> captains<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> of the army<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> and horsemen<sup><a href="h6571.htm">6571</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>10  When Sanballat<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> the Horonite,<sup><a href="h2772.htm">2772</a></sup> and Tobiah<sup><a href="h2900.htm">2900</a></sup> the servant,<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> the Ammonite,<sup><a href="h5984.htm">5984</a></sup> heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> of it, it grieved<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> them exceedingly<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> there was come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> to seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> the welfare<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> of the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Israel.<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  So I came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> and was<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> three<sup><a href="h7969.htm">7969</a></sup> days.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  And I arose<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> in the night,<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> and some few<sup><a href="h4592.htm">4592</a></sup> men<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> me; neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> told<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> I any man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> my God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> had put<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> to do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> at Jerusalem:<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> was there any beast<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> me, save<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> the beast<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> rode<sup><a href="h7392.htm">7392</a></sup> upon. </p>
 <p>13  And I went out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> by night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> by the gate<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> of the valley,<sup><a href="h1516.htm">1516</a></sup> even before<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> the dragon<sup><a href="h8577.htm">8577</a></sup> well,<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the dung<sup><a href="h0830.htm">830</a></sup> port,<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> and viewed<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7663.htm">7663</a></sup> the walls<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> of Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> were broken down,<sup><a href="h6555.htm">6555</a></sup> and the gates<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> thereof were consumed<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> with fire.<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Then I went on<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the gate<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> of the fountain,<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> pool:<sup><a href="h1295.htm">1295</a></sup> but there was no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> for the beast<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> that was under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> me to pass.<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Then went I up<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> in the night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> by the brook,<sup><a href="h5158.htm">5158</a></sup> and viewed<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7663.htm">7663</a></sup> the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> and turned back,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> and entered<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> by the gate<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> of the valley,<sup><a href="h1516.htm">1516</a></sup> and so returned.<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  And the rulers<sup><a href="h5461.htm">5461</a></sup> knew<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> whither<sup><a href="h0575.htm">575</a></sup> I went,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> or what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> did;<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> had I as<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> yet<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> told<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> it to the Jews,<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> nor to the priests,<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> nor to the nobles,<sup><a href="h2715.htm">2715</a></sup> nor to the rulers,<sup><a href="h5461.htm">5461</a></sup> nor to the rest<sup><a href="h3499.htm">3499</a></sup> that did<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> the work.<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Then said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> them, Ye<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the distress<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> are in, how<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> Jerusalem<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> lieth waste,<sup><a href="h2720.htm">2720</a></sup> and the gates<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> thereof are burned<sup><a href="h3341.htm">3341</a></sup> with fire:<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> come,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> and let us build up<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the wall<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> of Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> that we be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> more<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> a reproach.<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> </p>

 <p>20  Then answered<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> I them, and said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto them, The God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of heaven,<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> will prosper<sup><a href="h6743.htm">6743</a></sup> us; therefore we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> his servants<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> will arise<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> and build:<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> but ye have no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> portion,<sup><a href="h2506.htm">2506</a></sup> nor right,<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> nor memorial,<sup><a href="h2146.htm">2146</a></sup> in Jerusalem.<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/neh002.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Factum est autem in mense Nisan, anno vigesimo Artaxerxis regis: et vinum erat ante eum, et levavi vinum, et dedi regi: et eram quasi languidus ante faciem ejus.</p>
 <p>2 Dixitque mihi rex: Quare vultus tuus tristis est, cum te ægrotum non videam? non est hoc frustra, sed malum nescio quod in corde tuo est. Et timui valde, ac nimis:</p>
 <p>3 et dixi regi: Rex, in æternum vive: quare non mreat vultus meus, quia civitas domus sepulchrorum patris mei deserta est, et portæ ejus combustæ sunt igni?</p>
 <p>4 Et ait mihi rex: Pro qua re postulas? Et oravi Deum cæli,</p>
 <p>5 et dixi ad regem: Si videtur regi bonum, et si placet servus tuus ante faciem tuam, ut mittas me in Judæam ad civitatem sepulchri patris mei, et ædificabo eam.</p>
 <p>6 Dixitque mihi rex, et regina quæ sedebat juxta eum: Usque ad quod tempus erit iter tuum, et quando reverteris? Et placuit ante vultum regis, et misit me: et constitui ei tempus.</p>
 <p>7 Et dixi regi: Si regi videtur bonum, epistolas det mihi ad duces regionis trans flumen, ut traducant me, donec veniam in Judæam:</p>
 <p>8 et epistolam ad Asaph custodem saltus regis, ut det mihi ligna, ut tegere possim portas turris domus, et muros civitatis, et domum quam ingressus fuero. Et dedit mihi rex juxta manum Dei mei bonam mecum.</p>
 <p>9 Et veni ad duces regionis trans flumen, dedique eis epistolas regis. Miserat autem rex mecum principes militum, et equites.</p>
 <p>10 Et audierunt Sanaballat Horonites, et Tobias servus Ammanites: et contristati sunt afflictione magna, quod venisset homo qui quæreret prosperitatem filiorum Israël.<br>
 </p>
 <p>11 Et veni Jerusalem, et eram ibi tribus diebus.</p>
 <p>12 Et surrexi nocte ego, et viri pauci mecum, et non indicavi cuiquam quid Deus dedisset in corde meo ut facerem in Jerusalem: et jumentum non erat mecum, nisi animal cui sedebam.</p>
 <p>13 Et egressus sum per portam vallis nocte, et ante fontem draconis, et ad portam stercoris, et considerabam murum Jerusalem dissipatum, et portas ejus consumptas igni.</p>
 <p>14 Et transivi ad portam fontis, et ad aquæductum regis, et non erat locus jumento cui sedebam ut transiret.</p>
 <p>15 Et ascendi per torrentem nocte, et considerabam murum, et reversus veni ad portam vallis, et redii.</p>
 <p>16 Magistratus autem nesciebant quo abiissem, aut quid ego facerem: sed et Judæis, et sacerdotibus, et optimatibus, et magistratibus, et reliquis qui faciebant opus, usque ad id loci nihil indicaveram.</p>
 <p>17 Et dixi eis: Vos nostis afflictionem in qua sumus: quia Jerusalem deserta est, et portæ ejus consumptæ sunt igni: venite, et ædificemus muros Jerusalem, et non simus ultra opprobrium.</p>
 <p>18 Et indicavi eis manum Dei mei, quod esset bona mecum, et verba regis quæ locutus esset mihi, et aio: Surgamus, et ædificemus. Et confortatæ sunt manus eorum in bono.</p>
 <p>19 Audierunt autem Sanaballat Horonites, et Tobias servus Ammanites, et Gosem Arabs, et subsannaverunt nos, et despexerunt, dixeruntque: Quæ est hæc res quam facitis? numquid contra regem vos rebellatis?</p>
 <p>20 Et reddidi eis sermonem, dixique ad eos: Deus cæli ipse nos juvat, et nos servi ejus sumus: surgamus et ædificemus: vobis autem non est pars, et justitia, et memoria in Jerusalem.</p>
