 <p>2  The wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> in his pride<sup><a href="h1346.htm">1346</a></sup> doth persecute<sup><a href="h1814.htm">1814</a></sup> the poor:<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> let them be taken<sup><a href="h8610.htm">8610</a></sup> in the devices<sup><a href="h4209.htm">4209</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h2098.htm">2098</a></sup> they have imagined.<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> boasteth<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his heart's<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> desire,<sup><a href="h8378.htm">8378</a></sup> and blesseth<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> the covetous,<sup><a href="h1214.htm">1214</a></sup> whom the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> abhorreth.<sup><a href="h5006.htm">5006</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  The wicked,<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> through the pride<sup><a href="h1363.htm">1363</a></sup> of his countenance,<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> seek<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> after God: God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> is not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> in all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his thoughts.<sup><a href="h4209.htm">4209</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  His ways<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> are always<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> grievous;<sup><a href="h2342.htm">2342</a></sup> thy judgments<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> are far above<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> out of his sight:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> as for all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his enemies,<sup><a href="h6887.htm">6887</a></sup> he puffeth<sup><a href="h6315.htm">6315</a></sup> at them. </p>
 <p>6  He hath said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> in his heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> I shall not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> be moved:<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> for I shall never<sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be in adversity.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  His mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> is full<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of cursing<sup><a href="h0423.htm">423</a></sup> and deceit<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> and fraud:<sup><a href="h8496.htm">8496</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> his tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> is mischief<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> and vanity.<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  He sitteth<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in the lurking places<sup><a href="h3993.htm">3993</a></sup> of the villages:<sup><a href="h2691.htm">2691</a></sup> in the secret places<sup><a href="h4565.htm">4565</a></sup> doth he murder<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> the innocent:<sup><a href="h5355.htm">5355</a></sup> his eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> are privily set<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> against the poor.<sup><a href="h2489.htm">2489</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  He lieth in wait<sup><a href="h0693.htm">693</a></sup> secretly<sup><a href="h4565.htm">4565</a></sup> as a lion<sup><a href="h0738.htm">738</a></sup> in his den:<sup><a href="h5520.htm">5520</a></sup> he lieth in wait<sup><a href="h0693.htm">693</a></sup> to catch<sup><a href="h2414.htm">2414</a></sup> the poor:<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> he doth catch<sup><a href="h2414.htm">2414</a></sup> the poor,<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> when he draweth<sup><a href="h4900.htm">4900</a></sup> him into his net.<sup><a href="h7568.htm">7568</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  He croucheth,<sup><a href="h1794.htm">1794</a></sup> and humbleth<sup><a href="h7817.htm">7817</a></sup> himself, that the poor<sup><a href="h2489.htm">2489</a></sup> may fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> by his strong ones.<sup><a href="h6099.htm">6099</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  He hath said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> in his heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> hath forgotten:<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> he hideth<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> his face;<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> he will never<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>12  Arise,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> O LORD;<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> lift up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> thine hand:<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> forget<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the humble.<sup><a href="h6035.htm">6035</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Wherefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> doth the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> contemn<sup><a href="h5006.htm">5006</a></sup> God?<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> he hath said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> in his heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> Thou wilt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> require<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>14  Thou hast seen<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> it; for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> beholdest<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> mischief<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> and spite,<sup><a href="h3708.htm">3708</a></sup> to requite<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> it with thy hand:<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> the poor<sup><a href="h2489.htm">2489</a></sup> committeth<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> himself unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee; thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> the helper<sup><a href="h5826.htm">5826</a></sup> of the fatherless.<sup><a href="h3490.htm">3490</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Break<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> thou the arm<sup><a href="h2220.htm">2220</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> and the evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> man: seek out<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> his wickedness<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> till thou find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> none.<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> </p>

 <p>17  LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> thou hast heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> the desire<sup><a href="h8378.htm">8378</a></sup> of the humble:<sup><a href="h6035.htm">6035</a></sup> thou wilt prepare<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> their heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> thou wilt cause thine ear<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> to hear:<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  To judge<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> the fatherless<sup><a href="h3490.htm">3490</a></sup> and the oppressed,<sup><a href="h1790.htm">1790</a></sup> that the man<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> may no<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> more<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> oppress.<sup><a href="h6206.htm">6206</a></sup> </p>
