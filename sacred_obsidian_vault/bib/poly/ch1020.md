 <p>3  And he brought out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> were in it, and cut<sup><a href="h7787.htm">7787</a></sup> them with saws,<sup><a href="h4050.htm">4050</a></sup> and with harrows<sup><a href="h2757.htm">2757</a></sup> of iron,<sup><a href="h1270.htm">1270</a></sup> and with axes.<sup><a href="h4050.htm">4050</a></sup> Even so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> dealt<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> David<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> with all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the cities<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> of the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Ammon.<sup><a href="h5983.htm">5983</a></sup> And David<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> returned<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> to Jerusalem.<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  But when he defied<sup><a href="h2778.htm">2778</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Israel,<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> Jonathan<sup><a href="h3083.htm">3083</a></sup> the son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Shimea<sup><a href="h8092.htm">8092</a></sup> David's<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> brother<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> slew<sup><a href="h5221.htm">5221</a></sup> him. </p>

 <p>3 καὶ τὸν λαὸν τὸν ἐν αὐτῇ ἐξήγαγεν καὶ διέπρισεν πρίοσιν καὶ ἐν σκεπάρνοις σιδηροῖς καὶ οὕτως ἐποίησεν Δαυιδ τοῖς πᾶσιν υἱοῖς Αμμων καὶ ἀνέστρεψεν Δαυιδ καὶ πᾶς ὁ λαὸς αὐτοῦ εἰς Ιερουσαλημ </p>
 <p>4 καὶ ἐγένετο μετὰ ταῦτα καὶ ἐγένετο ἔτι πόλεμος ἐν Γαζερ μετὰ τῶν ἀλλοφύλων τότε ἐπάταξεν Σοβοχαι ὁ Ουσαθι τὸν Σαφου ἀπὸ τῶν υἱῶν τῶν γιγάντων καὶ ἐταπείνωσεν αὐτόν </p>
 <p>5 καὶ ἐγένετο ἔτι πόλεμος μετὰ τῶν ἀλλοφύλων καὶ ἐπάταξεν Ελλαναν υἱὸς Ιαϊρ τὸν Λεεμι ἀδελφὸν Γολιαθ τοῦ Γεθθαίου καὶ ξύλον δόρατος αὐτοῦ ὡς ἀντίον ὑφαινόντων </p>
 <p>6 καὶ ἐγένετο ἔτι πόλεμος ἐν Γεθ καὶ ἦν ἀνὴρ ὑπερμεγέθης καὶ δάκτυλοι αὐτοῦ ἓξ καὶ ἕξ εἴκοσι τέσσαρες καὶ οὗτος ἦν ἀπόγονος γιγάντων </p>
 <p>7 καὶ ὠνείδισεν τὸν Ισραηλ καὶ ἐπάταξεν αὐτὸν Ιωναθαν υἱὸς Σαμαα ἀδελφοῦ Δαυιδ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/ch1020.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Factum est autem post anni circulum, eo tempore quo solent reges ad bella procedere, congregavit Joab exercitum, et robur militiæ, et vastavit terram filiorum Ammon: perrexitque et obsedit Rabba. Porro David manebat in Jerusalem, quando Joab percussit Rabba et destruxit eam.</p>
 <p>2 Tulit autem David coronam Melchom de capite ejus, et invenit in ea auri pondo talentum, et pretiosissimas gemmas, fecitque sibi inde diadema: manubias quoque urbis plurimas tulit;</p>
 <p>3 populum autem, qui erat in ea, eduxit, et fecit super eos tribulas, et trahas, et ferrata carpenta transire, ita ut dissecarentur et contererentur. Sic fecit David cunctis urbibus filiorum Ammon: et reversus est cum omni populo suo in Jerusalem.</p>
 <p>4 Post hæc initum est bellum in Gazer adversum Philisthæos, in quo percussit Sobochai Husathites, Saphai de genere Raphaim, et humiliavit eos.</p>
 <p>5 Aliud quoque bellum gestum est adversus Philisthæos, in quo percussit Adeodatus filius Saltus Bethlehemites fratrem Goliath Gethæi, cujus hastæ lignum erat quasi liciatorium texentium.</p>
 <p>6 Sed et aliud bellum accidit in Geth, in quo fuit homo longissimus, senos habens digitos, id est, simul viginti quatuor: qui et ipse de Rapha fuerat stirpe generatus.</p>
 <p>7 Hic blasphemavit Israël: et percussit eum Jonathan filius Samaa fratris David. Hi sunt filii Rapha in Geth, qui ceciderunt in manu David et servorum ejus.</p>
