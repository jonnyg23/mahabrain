 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/isa024.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> maketh the earth empty,<sup><a href="h1238.htm">1238</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and maketh it waste,<sup><a href="h1110.htm">1110</a></sup> and turneth it upside down,<sup><a href="h5753.htm">5753</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> and scattereth abroad<sup><a href="h6327.htm">6327</a></sup> the inhabitants<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> thereof. </p>
 <p>2  And it shall be,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> as with the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> so with the priest;<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> as with the servant,<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> so with his master;<sup><a href="h0113.htm">113</a></sup> as with the maid,<sup><a href="h8198.htm">8198</a></sup> so with her mistress;<sup><a href="h1404.htm">1404</a></sup> as with the buyer,<sup><a href="h7069.htm">7069</a></sup> so with the seller;<sup><a href="h4376.htm">4376</a></sup> as with the lender,<sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> so with the borrower;<sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> as with the taker of usury,<sup><a href="h5383.htm">5383</a></sup> so<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> with the giver of usury<sup><a href="h5378.htm">5378</a></sup> to him. </p>
 <p>3  The land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall be utterly emptied,<sup><a href="h1238.htm">1238</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1238.htm">1238</a></sup> and utterly spoiled:<sup><a href="h0962.htm">962</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0962.htm">962</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hath spoken<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> word.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  The earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> mourneth<sup><a href="h0056.htm">56</a></sup> and fadeth away,<sup><a href="h5034.htm">5034</a></sup> the world<sup><a href="h8398.htm">8398</a></sup> languisheth<sup><a href="h0535.htm">535</a></sup> and fadeth away,<sup><a href="h5034.htm">5034</a></sup> the haughty<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> do languish.<sup><a href="h0535.htm">535</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> also is defiled<sup><a href="h2610.htm">2610</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the inhabitants<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> thereof; because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have transgressed<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> the laws,<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> changed<sup><a href="h2498.htm">2498</a></sup> the ordinance,<sup><a href="h2706.htm">2706</a></sup> broken<sup><a href="h6565.htm">6565</a></sup> the everlasting<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> covenant.<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> hath the curse<sup><a href="h0423.htm">423</a></sup> devoured<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> the earth,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and they that dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> therein are desolate:<sup><a href="h0816.htm">816</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> the inhabitants<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> are burned,<sup><a href="h2787.htm">2787</a></sup> and few<sup><a href="h4213.htm">4213</a></sup> men<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> left.<sup><a href="h7604.htm">7604</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  The new wine<sup><a href="h8492.htm">8492</a></sup> mourneth,<sup><a href="h0056.htm">56</a></sup> the vine<sup><a href="h1612.htm">1612</a></sup> languisheth,<sup><a href="h0535.htm">535</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the merryhearted<sup><a href="h8056.htm">8056</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> do sigh.<sup><a href="h0584.htm">584</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  The mirth<sup><a href="h4885.htm">4885</a></sup> of tabrets<sup><a href="h8596.htm">8596</a></sup> ceaseth,<sup><a href="h7673.htm">7673</a></sup> the noise<sup><a href="h7588.htm">7588</a></sup> of them that rejoice<sup><a href="h5947.htm">5947</a></sup> endeth,<sup><a href="h2308.htm">2308</a></sup> the joy<sup><a href="h4885.htm">4885</a></sup> of the harp<sup><a href="h3658.htm">3658</a></sup> ceaseth.<sup><a href="h7673.htm">7673</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  They shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> drink<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> with a song;<sup><a href="h7892.htm">7892</a></sup> strong drink<sup><a href="h7941.htm">7941</a></sup> shall be bitter<sup><a href="h4843.htm">4843</a></sup> to them that drink<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>10  The city<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> of confusion<sup><a href="h8414.htm">8414</a></sup> is broken down:<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> is shut up,<sup><a href="h5462.htm">5462</a></sup> that no man may come in.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  There is a crying<sup><a href="h6682.htm">6682</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> in the streets;<sup><a href="h2351.htm">2351</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> joy<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> is darkened,<sup><a href="h6150.htm">6150</a></sup> the mirth<sup><a href="h4885.htm">4885</a></sup> of the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> is gone.<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  In the city<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> is left<sup><a href="h7604.htm">7604</a></sup> desolation,<sup><a href="h8047.htm">8047</a></sup> and the gate<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> is smitten<sup><a href="h3807.htm">3807</a></sup> with destruction.<sup><a href="h7591.htm">7591</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  When<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thus<sup><a href="h3541.htm">3541</a></sup> it shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> in the midst<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> of the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> among<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> there shall be as the shaking<sup><a href="h5363.htm">5363</a></sup> of an olive tree,<sup><a href="h2132.htm">2132</a></sup> and as the gleaning grapes<sup><a href="h5955.htm">5955</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> the vintage<sup><a href="h1210.htm">1210</a></sup> is done.<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  They<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> shall lift up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> their voice,<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> they shall sing<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> for the majesty<sup><a href="h1347.htm">1347</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> they shall cry aloud<sup><a href="h6670.htm">6670</a></sup> from the sea.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Wherefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> glorify<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> ye the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> in the fires,<sup><a href="h0217.htm">217</a></sup> even the name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> in the isles<sup><a href="h0339.htm">339</a></sup> of the sea.<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  From the uttermost part<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3671.htm">3671</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> have we heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> songs,<sup><a href="h2158.htm">2158</a></sup> even glory<sup><a href="h6643.htm">6643</a></sup> to the righteous.<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> But I said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> My leanness,<sup><a href="h7334.htm">7334</a></sup> my leanness,<sup><a href="h7334.htm">7334</a></sup> woe<sup><a href="h0188.htm">188</a></sup> unto me! the treacherous dealers<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> have dealt treacherously;<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> yea, the treacherous dealers<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> have dealt very treacherously.<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0899.htm">899</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Fear,<sup><a href="h6343.htm">6343</a></sup> and the pit,<sup><a href="h6354.htm">6354</a></sup> and the snare,<sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> are upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee, O inhabitant<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> of the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  And it shall come to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> that he who fleeth<sup><a href="h5127.htm">5127</a></sup> from the noise<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of the fear<sup><a href="h6343.htm">6343</a></sup> shall fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> into<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the pit;<sup><a href="h6354.htm">6354</a></sup> and he that cometh up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> out of the midst<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> of the pit<sup><a href="h6354.htm">6354</a></sup> shall be taken<sup><a href="h3920.htm">3920</a></sup> in the snare:<sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the windows<sup><a href="h0699.htm">699</a></sup> from on high<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> are open,<sup><a href="h6605.htm">6605</a></sup> and the foundations<sup><a href="h4146.htm">4146</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> do shake.<sup><a href="h7493.htm">7493</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  The earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> is utterly broken down,<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> is clean dissolved,<sup><a href="h6565.htm">6565</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6565.htm">6565</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> is moved exceedingly.<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  The earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall reel to and fro<sup><a href="h5128.htm">5128</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5128.htm">5128</a></sup> like a drunkard,<sup><a href="h7910.htm">7910</a></sup> and shall be removed<sup><a href="h5110.htm">5110</a></sup> like a cottage;<sup><a href="h4412.htm">4412</a></sup> and the transgression<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> thereof shall be heavy<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it; and it shall fall,<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> and not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> rise<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> again.<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  And it shall come to pass<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> in that<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> day,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> that the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shall punish<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the host<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> of the high ones<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> that are on high,<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> and the kings<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0127.htm">127</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the earth.<sup><a href="h0127.htm">127</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  And they shall be gathered together,<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> as prisoners<sup><a href="h0616.htm">616</a></sup> are gathered<sup><a href="h0626.htm">626</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the pit,<sup><a href="h0953.htm">953</a></sup> and shall be shut up<sup><a href="h5462.htm">5462</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the prison,<sup><a href="h4525.htm">4525</a></sup> and after many<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> shall they be visited.<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  Then the moon<sup><a href="h3842.htm">3842</a></sup> shall be confounded,<sup><a href="h2659.htm">2659</a></sup> and the sun<sup><a href="h2535.htm">2535</a></sup> ashamed,<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> of hosts<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> shall reign<sup><a href="h4427.htm">4427</a></sup> in mount<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> Zion,<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> and in Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> and before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> his ancients<sup><a href="h2205.htm">2205</a></sup> gloriously.<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 καὶ ἔσται ὁ λαὸς ὡς ὁ ἱερεὺς καὶ ὁ παῖς ὡς ὁ κύριος καὶ ἡ θεράπαινα ὡς ἡ κυρία ἔσται ὁ ἀγοράζων ὡς ὁ πωλῶν καὶ ὁ δανείζων ὡς ὁ δανειζόμενος καὶ ὁ ὀφείλων ὡς ᾧ ὀφείλει </p>
 <p>3 φθορᾷ φθαρήσεται ἡ γῆ καὶ προνομῇ προνομευθήσεται ἡ γῆ τὸ γὰρ στόμα κυρίου ἐλάλησεν ταῦτα </p>
 <p>4 ἐπένθησεν ἡ γῆ καὶ ἐφθάρη ἡ οἰκουμένη ἐπένθησαν οἱ ὑψηλοὶ τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>5 ἡ δὲ γῆ ἠνόμησεν διὰ τοὺς κατοικοῦντας αὐτήν διότι παρέβησαν τὸν νόμον καὶ ἤλλαξαν τὰ προστάγματα διαθήκην αἰώνιον </p>
 <p>6 διὰ τοῦτο ἀρὰ ἔδεται τὴν γῆν ὅτι ἡμάρτοσαν οἱ κατοικοῦντες αὐτήν διὰ τοῦτο πτωχοὶ ἔσονται οἱ ἐνοικοῦντες ἐν τῇ γῇ καὶ καταλειφθήσονται ἄνθρωποι ὀλίγοι </p>
 <p>7 πενθήσει οἶνος πενθήσει ἄμπελος στενάξουσιν πάντες οἱ εὐφραινόμενοι τὴν ψυχήν </p>
 <p>8 πέπαυται εὐφροσύνη τυμπάνων πέπαυται αὐθάδεια καὶ πλοῦτος ἀσεβῶν πέπαυται φωνὴ κιθάρας </p>
 <p>9 ᾐσχύνθησαν οὐκ ἔπιον οἶνον πικρὸν ἐγένετο τὸ σικερα τοῖς πίνουσιν </p>
 <p>10 ἠρημώθη πᾶσα πόλις κλείσει οἰκίαν τοῦ μὴ εἰσελθεῖν </p>
 <p>11 ὀλολύζετε περὶ τοῦ οἴνου πανταχῇ πέπαυται πᾶσα εὐφροσύνη τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>12 καὶ καταλειφθήσονται πόλεις ἔρημοι καὶ οἶκοι ἐγκαταλελειμμένοι ἀπολοῦνται </p>
 <p>13 ταῦτα πάντα ἔσται ἐν τῇ γῇ ἐν μέσῳ τῶν ἐθνῶν ὃν τρόπον ἐάν τις καλαμήσηται ἐλαίαν οὕτως καλαμήσονται αὐτούς καὶ ἐὰν παύσηται ὁ τρύγητος </p>
 <p>14 οὗτοι φωνῇ βοήσονται οἱ δὲ καταλειφθέντες ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς εὐφρανθήσονται ἅμα τῇ δόξῃ κυρίου ταραχθήσεται τὸ ὕδωρ τῆς θαλάσσης </p>
 <p>15 διὰ τοῦτο ἡ δόξα κυρίου ἐν ταῖς νήσοις ἔσται τῆς θαλάσσης τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου ἔνδοξον ἔσται κύριε ὁ θεὸς Ισραηλ </p>
 <p>16 ἀπὸ τῶν πτερύγων τῆς γῆς τέρατα ἠκούσαμεν ἐλπὶς τῷ εὐσεβεῖ καὶ ἐροῦσιν οὐαὶ τοῖς ἀθετοῦσιν οἱ ἀθετοῦντες τὸν νόμον </p>
 <p>17 φόβος καὶ βόθυνος καὶ παγὶς ἐφ᾽ ὑμᾶς τοὺς ἐνοικοῦντας ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>18 καὶ ἔσται ὁ φεύγων τὸν φόβον ἐμπεσεῖται εἰς τὸν βόθυνον ὁ δὲ ἐκβαίνων ἐκ τοῦ βοθύνου ἁλώσεται ὑπὸ τῆς παγίδος ὅτι θυρίδες ἐκ τοῦ οὐρανοῦ ἠνεῴχθησαν καὶ σεισθήσεται τὰ θεμέλια τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>19 ταραχῇ ταραχθήσεται ἡ γῆ καὶ ἀπορίᾳ ἀπορηθήσεται ἡ γῆ </p>
 <p>20 ἔκλινεν καὶ σεισθήσεται ὡς ὀπωροφυλάκιον ἡ γῆ ὡς ὁ μεθύων καὶ κραιπαλῶν καὶ πεσεῖται καὶ οὐ μὴ δύνηται ἀναστῆναι κατίσχυσεν γὰρ ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς ἡ ἀνομία </p>
 <p>21 καὶ ἐπάξει ὁ θεὸς ἐπὶ τὸν κόσμον τοῦ οὐρανοῦ τὴν χεῖρα καὶ ἐπὶ τοὺς βασιλεῖς τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>22 καὶ συνάξουσιν καὶ ἀποκλείσουσιν εἰς ὀχύρωμα καὶ εἰς δεσμωτήριον διὰ πολλῶν γενεῶν ἐπισκοπὴ ἔσται αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>23 καὶ τακήσεται ἡ πλίνθος καὶ πεσεῖται τὸ τεῖχος ὅτι βασιλεύσει κύριος ἐν Σιων καὶ ἐν Ιερουσαλημ καὶ ἐνώπιον τῶν πρεσβυτέρων δοξασθήσεται </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/isa024.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Ecce Dominus dissipabit terram:<br> et nudabit eam, et affliget faciem ejus,<br> et disperget habitatores ejus.<br></p>
 <p>2 Et erit sicut populus, sic sacerdos;<br> et sicut servus, sic dominus ejus;<br> sicut ancilla, sic domina ejus;<br> sicut emens, sic ille qui vendit;<br> sicut fnerator, sic is qui mutuum accipit;<br> sicut qui repetit, sic qui debet.<br></p>
 <p>3 Dissipatione dissipabitur terra, et direptione prædabitur;<br> Dominus enim locutus est verbum hoc.<br></p>
 <p>4 Luxit, et defluxit terra, et infirmata est;<br> defluxit orbis,<br> infirmata est altitudo populi terræ.<br></p>
 <p>5 Et terra infecta est ab habitatoribus suis,<br> quia transgressi sunt leges,<br> mutaverunt jus,<br> dissipaverunt fdus sempiternum.<br></p>
 <p>6 Propter hoc maledictio vorabit terram,<br> et peccabunt habitatores ejus;<br> ideoque insanient cultores ejus,<br> et relinquentur homines pauci.<br></p>
 <p>7 Luxit vindemia, infirmata est vitis,<br> ingemuerunt omnes qui lætabantur corde;<br></p>
 <p>8 cessavit gaudium tympanorum,<br> quievit sonitus lætantium,<br> conticuit dulcedo citharæ.<br></p>
 <p>9 Cum cantico non bibent vinum;<br> amara erit potio bibentibus illam.<br></p>
 <p>10 Attrita est civitas vanitatis,<br> clausa est omnis domus, nullo introëunte.<br></p>
 <p>11 Clamor erit super vino in plateis,<br> deserta est omnia lætitia,<br> translatum est gaudium terræ.<br></p>
 <p>12 Relicta est in urbe solitudo,<br> et calamitas opprimet portas.<br></p>
 <p>13 Quia hæc erunt in medio terræ<br> in medio populorum,<br> quomodo si paucæ olivæ quæ remanserunt<br> excutiantur ex olea et racemi,<br> cum fuerit finita vindemia.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>14 [Hi levabunt vocem suam, atque laudabunt:<br> cum glorificatus fuerit Dominus, hinnient de mari.<br></p>
 <p>15 Propter hoc in doctrinis glorificate Dominum;<br> in insulis maris<br> nomen Domini Dei Israël.<br></p>
 <p>16 A finibus terræ laudes audivimus,<br> gloriam Justi.<br> Et dixi: Secretum meum mihi,<br> secretum meum mihi.<br> Væ mihi!<br> prævaricantes prævaricati sunt,<br> et prævaricatione transgressorum prævaricati sunt.<br></p>
 <p>17 Formido, et fovea, et laqueus<br> super te, qui habitator es terræ.<br></p>
 <p>18 Et erit: qui fugerit a voce formidinis cadet in foveam;<br> et qui se explicaverit de fovea tenebitur laqueo;<br> quia cataractæ de excelsis apertæ sunt<br> et concutientur fundamenta terræ.<br></p>
 <p>19 Confractione confringetur terra,<br> contritione conteretur terra,<br> commotione commovebitur terra;<br></p>
 <p>20 agitatione agitabitur terra sicut ebrius,<br> et auferetur quasi tabernaculum unius noctis;<br> et gravabit eam iniquitas sua,<br> et corruet, et non adjiciet ut resurgat.<br></p>
 <p>21 Et erit: in die illa visitabit Dominus<br> super militiam cæli in excelso,<br> et super reges terræ qui sunt super terram;<br></p>
 <p>22 et congregabuntur in congregatione unius fascis in lacum,<br> et claudentur ibi in carcere,<br> et post multos dies visitabuntur.<br></p>
 <p>23 Et erubescet luna, et confundetur sol,<br> cum regnaverit Dominus exercituum<br> in monte Sion et in Jerusalem<br> et in conspectu senum suorum fuerit glorificatus.]</p>
