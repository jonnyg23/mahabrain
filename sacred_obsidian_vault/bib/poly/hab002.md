 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/hab002.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  I will stand<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> my watch,<sup><a href="h4931.htm">4931</a></sup> and set<sup><a href="h3320.htm">3320</a></sup> me upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the tower,<sup><a href="h4692.htm">4692</a></sup> and will watch<sup><a href="h6822.htm">6822</a></sup> to see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> he will say<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> unto me, and what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> I shall answer<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> I am reproved.<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  And the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> answered<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me, and said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Write<sup><a href="h3789.htm">3789</a></sup> the vision,<sup><a href="h2377.htm">2377</a></sup> and make it plain<sup><a href="h0874.htm">874</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> tables,<sup><a href="h3871.htm">3871</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> he may run<sup><a href="h7323.htm">7323</a></sup> that readeth<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the vision<sup><a href="h2377.htm">2377</a></sup> is yet<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> for an appointed time,<sup><a href="h4150.htm">4150</a></sup> but at the end<sup><a href="h7093.htm">7093</a></sup> it shall speak,<sup><a href="h6315.htm">6315</a></sup> and not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> lie:<sup><a href="h3576.htm">3576</a></sup> though<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it tarry,<sup><a href="h4102.htm">4102</a></sup> wait<sup><a href="h2442.htm">2442</a></sup> for it; because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it will surely come,<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> it will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> tarry.<sup><a href="h0309.htm">309</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> his soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> which is lifted up<sup><a href="h6075.htm">6075</a></sup> is not upright<sup><a href="h3474.htm">3474</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> in him: but the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall live<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> by his faith.<sup><a href="h0530.htm">530</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Yea<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> also, because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he transgresseth<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> by wine,<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> he is a proud<sup><a href="h3093.htm">3093</a></sup> man,<sup><a href="h1397.htm">1397</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> keepeth at home,<sup><a href="h5115.htm">5115</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> enlargeth<sup><a href="h7337.htm">7337</a></sup> his desire<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> as hell,<sup><a href="h7585.htm">7585</a></sup> and is as death,<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> and cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be satisfied,<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> but gathereth<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> him all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> nations,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> and heapeth<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> him all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> people:<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> take up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> a parable<sup><a href="h4912.htm">4912</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> him, and a taunting<sup><a href="h4426.htm">4426</a></sup> proverb<sup><a href="h2420.htm">2420</a></sup> against him, and say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Woe<sup><a href="h1945.htm">1945</a></sup> to him that increaseth<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> that which is not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> his! how long?<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4970.htm">4970</a></sup> and to him that ladeth<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> himself with thick clay!<sup><a href="h5671.htm">5671</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Shall they not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> rise up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> suddenly<sup><a href="h6621.htm">6621</a></sup> that shall bite<sup><a href="h5391.htm">5391</a></sup> thee, and awake<sup><a href="h3364.htm">3364</a></sup> that shall vex<sup><a href="h2111.htm">2111</a></sup> thee, and thou shalt be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> for booties<sup><a href="h4933.htm">4933</a></sup> unto them? </p>
 <p>8  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> hast spoiled<sup><a href="h7997.htm">7997</a></sup> many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> nations,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the remnant<sup><a href="h3499.htm">3499</a></sup> of the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> shall spoil<sup><a href="h7997.htm">7997</a></sup> thee; because of men's<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> blood,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> and for the violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> of the land,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of the city,<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> and of all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> therein. </p>
 <p>9  Woe<sup><a href="h1945.htm">1945</a></sup> to him that coveteth<sup><a href="h1214.htm">1214</a></sup> an evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> covetousness<sup><a href="h1215.htm">1215</a></sup> to his house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> that he may set<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> his nest<sup><a href="h7064.htm">7064</a></sup> on high,<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> that he may be delivered<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> from the power<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> of evil!<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Thou hast consulted<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> shame<sup><a href="h1322.htm">1322</a></sup> to thy house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> by cutting off<sup><a href="h7096.htm">7096</a></sup> many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and hast sinned<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> against thy soul.<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the stone<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> shall cry out<sup><a href="h2199.htm">2199</a></sup> of the wall,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7023.htm">7023</a></sup> and the beam<sup><a href="h3714.htm">3714</a></sup> out of the timber<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> shall answer<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>12  Woe<sup><a href="h1945.htm">1945</a></sup> to him that buildeth<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> a town<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> with blood,<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> and establisheth<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> a city<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> by iniquity!<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> is it not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> of hosts<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> that the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> shall labor<sup><a href="h3021.htm">3021</a></sup> in the very<sup><a href="h1767.htm">1767</a></sup> fire,<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> and the people<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> shall weary<sup><a href="h3286.htm">3286</a></sup> themselves for very<sup><a href="h1767.htm">1767</a></sup> vanity?<sup><a href="h7385.htm">7385</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall be filled<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> with the knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> of the glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> as the waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> cover<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the sea.<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Woe<sup><a href="h1945.htm">1945</a></sup> unto him that giveth his neighbor drink,<sup><a href="h8248.htm">8248</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> that puttest<sup><a href="h5596.htm">5596</a></sup> thy bottle<sup><a href="h2573.htm">2573</a></sup> to him, and makest him drunken<sup><a href="h7937.htm">7937</a></sup> also,<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> thou mayest look<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> their nakedness!<sup><a href="h4589.htm">4589</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Thou art filled<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> with shame<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> for glory:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> drink<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> also,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> and let thy foreskin be uncovered:<sup><a href="h6188.htm">6188</a></sup> the cup<sup><a href="h3563.htm">3563</a></sup> of the LORD's<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> right hand<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> shall be turned<sup><a href="h5437.htm">5437</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee, and shameful spewing<sup><a href="h7022.htm">7022</a></sup> shall be on<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy glory.<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> of Lebanon<sup><a href="h3844.htm">3844</a></sup> shall cover<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> thee, and the spoil<sup><a href="h7701.htm">7701</a></sup> of beasts,<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> which made them afraid,<sup><a href="h2865.htm">2865</a></sup> because of men's<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> blood,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> and for the violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> of the land,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of the city,<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> and of all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> therein. </p>
 <p>18  What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> profiteth<sup><a href="h3276.htm">3276</a></sup> the graven image<sup><a href="h6459.htm">6459</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the maker<sup><a href="h3335.htm">3335</a></sup> thereof hath graven<sup><a href="h6458.htm">6458</a></sup> it; the molten image,<sup><a href="h4541.htm">4541</a></sup> and a teacher<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> of lies,<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the maker<sup><a href="h3335.htm">3335</a></sup> of his work<sup><a href="h3336.htm">3336</a></sup> trusteth<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> therein,<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> to make<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> dumb<sup><a href="h0483.htm">483</a></sup> idols?<sup><a href="h0457.htm">457</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  Woe<sup><a href="h1945.htm">1945</a></sup> unto him that saith<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> to the wood,<sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> Awake;<sup><a href="h6974.htm">6974</a></sup> to the dumb<sup><a href="h1748.htm">1748</a></sup> stone,<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> Arise,<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall teach!<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> Behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is laid over<sup><a href="h8610.htm">8610</a></sup> with gold<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> and silver,<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> and there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> breath<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> at all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> in the midst<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> of it. </p>
 <p>20  But the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is in his holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> temple:<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> let all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> keep silence<sup><a href="h2013.htm">2013</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/hab002.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἐπὶ τῆς φυλακῆς μου στήσομαι καὶ ἐπιβήσομαι ἐπὶ πέτραν καὶ ἀποσκοπεύσω τοῦ ἰδεῖν τί λαλήσει ἐν ἐμοὶ καὶ τί ἀποκριθῶ ἐπὶ τὸν ἔλεγχόν μου </p>
 <p>2 καὶ ἀπεκρίθη πρός με κύριος καὶ εἶπεν γράψον ὅρασιν καὶ σαφῶς ἐπὶ πυξίον ὅπως διώκῃ ὁ ἀναγινώσκων αὐτά </p>
 <p>3 διότι ἔτι ὅρασις εἰς καιρὸν καὶ ἀνατελεῖ εἰς πέρας καὶ οὐκ εἰς κενόν ἐὰν ὑστερήσῃ ὑπόμεινον αὐτόν ὅτι ἐρχόμενος ἥξει καὶ οὐ μὴ χρονίσῃ </p>
 <p>4 ἐὰν ὑποστείληται οὐκ εὐδοκεῖ ἡ ψυχή μου ἐν αὐτῷ ὁ δὲ δίκαιος ἐκ πίστεώς μου ζήσεται </p>
 <p>5 ὁ δὲ κατοινωμένος καὶ καταφρονητὴς ἀνὴρ ἀλαζών οὐδὲν μὴ περάνῃ ὃς ἐπλάτυνεν καθὼς ὁ ᾅδης τὴν ψυχὴν αὐτοῦ καὶ οὗτος ὡς θάνατος οὐκ ἐμπιπλάμενος καὶ ἐπισυνάξει ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν πάντα τὰ ἔθνη καὶ εἰσδέξεται πρὸς αὐτὸν πάντας τοὺς λαούς </p>
 <p>6 οὐχὶ ταῦτα πάντα παραβολὴν κατ᾽ αὐτοῦ λήμψονται καὶ πρόβλημα εἰς διήγησιν αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐροῦσιν οὐαὶ ὁ πληθύνων ἑαυτῷ τὰ οὐκ ὄντα αὐτοῦ ἕως τίνος καὶ βαρύνων τὸν κλοιὸν αὐτοῦ στιβαρῶς </p>
 <p>7 ὅτι ἐξαίφνης ἀναστήσονται δάκνοντες αὐτόν καὶ ἐκνήψουσιν οἱ ἐπίβουλοί σου καὶ ἔσῃ εἰς διαρπαγὴν αὐτοῖς </p>
 <p>8 διότι σὺ ἐσκύλευσας ἔθνη πολλά σκυλεύσουσίν σε πάντες οἱ ὑπολελειμμένοι λαοὶ δι᾽ αἵματα ἀνθρώπων καὶ ἀσεβείας γῆς καὶ πόλεως καὶ πάντων τῶν κατοικούντων αὐτήν </p>
 <p>9 ὦ ὁ πλεονεκτῶν πλεονεξίαν κακὴν τῷ οἴκῳ αὐτοῦ τοῦ τάξαι εἰς ὕψος νοσσιὰν αὐτοῦ τοῦ ἐκσπασθῆναι ἐκ χειρὸς κακῶν </p>
 <p>10 ἐβουλεύσω αἰσχύνην τῷ οἴκῳ σου συνεπέρανας λαοὺς πολλούς καὶ ἐξήμαρτεν ἡ ψυχή σου </p>
 <p>11 διότι λίθος ἐκ τοίχου βοήσεται καὶ κάνθαρος ἐκ ξύλου φθέγξεται αὐτά </p>
 <p>12 οὐαὶ ὁ οἰκοδομῶν πόλιν ἐν αἵμασιν καὶ ἑτοιμάζων πόλιν ἐν ἀδικίαις </p>
 <p>13 οὐ ταῦτά ἐστιν παρὰ κυρίου παντοκράτορος καὶ ἐξέλιπον λαοὶ ἱκανοὶ ἐν πυρί καὶ ἔθνη πολλὰ ὠλιγοψύχησαν </p>
 <p>14 ὅτι πλησθήσεται ἡ γῆ τοῦ γνῶναι τὴν δόξαν κυρίου ὡς ὕδωρ κατακαλύψει αὐτούς </p>
 <p>15 ὦ ὁ ποτίζων τὸν πλησίον αὐτοῦ ἀνατροπῇ θολερᾷ καὶ μεθύσκων ὅπως ἐπιβλέπῃ ἐπὶ τὰ σπήλαια αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>16 πλησμονὴν ἀτιμίας ἐκ δόξης πίε καὶ σὺ καὶ διασαλεύθητι καὶ σείσθητι ἐκύκλωσεν ἐπὶ σὲ ποτήριον δεξιᾶς κυρίου καὶ συνήχθη ἀτιμία ἐπὶ τὴν δόξαν σου </p>
 <p>17 διότι ἀσέβεια τοῦ Λιβάνου καλύψει σε καὶ ταλαιπωρία θηρίων πτοήσει σε διὰ αἵματα ἀνθρώπων καὶ ἀσεβείας γῆς καὶ πόλεως καὶ πάντων τῶν κατοικούντων αὐτήν </p>
 <p>18 τί ὠφελεῖ γλυπτόν ὅτι ἔγλυψαν αὐτό ἔπλασαν αὐτὸ χώνευμα φαντασίαν ψευδῆ ὅτι πέποιθεν ὁ πλάσας ἐπὶ τὸ πλάσμα αὐτοῦ τοῦ ποιῆσαι εἴδωλα κωφά </p>
 <p>19 οὐαὶ ὁ λέγων τῷ ξύλῳ ἔκνηψον ἐξεγέρθητι καὶ τῷ λίθῳ ὑψώθητι καὶ αὐτό ἐστιν φαντασία τοῦτο δέ ἐστιν ἔλασμα χρυσίου καὶ ἀργυρίου καὶ πᾶν πνεῦμα οὐκ ἔστιν ἐν αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>20 ὁ δὲ κύριος ἐν ναῷ ἁγίῳ αὐτοῦ εὐλαβείσθω ἀπὸ προσώπου αὐτοῦ πᾶσα ἡ γῆ </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎2 ‏וַיַּעֲנֵ֤נִי יְהוָה֙ וַיֹּ֔אמֶר כְּת֣וֹב חָז֔וֹן וּבָאֵ֖ר עַל־הַלֻּח֑וֹת לְמַ֥עַן יָר֖וּץ ק֥וֹרֵא בֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎3 ‏כִּ֣י ע֤וֹד חָזוֹן֙ לַמּוֹעֵ֔ד וְיָפֵ֥חַ לַקֵּ֖ץ וְלֹ֣א יְכַזֵּ֑ב אִם־יִתְמַהְמָהּ֙ חַכֵּה־ל֔וֹ כִּֽי־בֹ֥א יָבֹ֖א לֹ֥א יְאַחֵֽר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎4 ‏הִנֵּ֣ה עֻפְּלָ֔ה לֹא־יָשְׁרָ֥ה נַפְשׁ֖וֹ בּ֑וֹ וְצַדִּ֖יק בֶּאֱמוּנָת֥וֹ יִחְיֶֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎5 ‏וְאַף֙ כִּֽי־הַיַּ֣יִן בּוֹגֵ֔ד גֶּ֥בֶר יָהִ֖יר וְלֹ֣א יִנְוֶ֑ה אֲשֶׁר֩ הִרְחִ֨יב כִּשְׁא֜וֹל נַפְשׁ֗וֹ וְה֤וּא כַמָּ֙וֶת֙ וְלֹ֣א יִשְׂבָּ֔ע וַיֶּאֱסֹ֤ף אֵלָיו֙ כָּל־הַגּוֹיִ֔ם וַיִּקְבֹּ֥ץ אֵלָ֖יו כָּל־הָעַמִּֽים׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎6 ‏הֲלוֹא־אֵ֣לֶּה כֻלָּ֗ם עָלָיו֙ מָשָׁ֣ל יִשָּׂ֔אוּ וּמְלִיצָ֖ה חִיד֣וֹת ל֑וֹ וְיֹאמַ֗ר ה֚וֹי הַמַּרְבֶּ֣ה לֹּא־ל֔וֹ עַד־מָתַ֕י וּמַכְבִּ֥יד עָלָ֖יו עַבְטִֽיט׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏הֲל֣וֹא פֶ֗תַע יָק֙וּמוּ֙ נֹשְׁכֶ֔יךָ וְיִקְצ֖וּ מְזַעְזְעֶ֑יךָ וְהָיִ֥יתָ לִמְשִׁסּ֖וֹת לָֽמוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏כִּֽי אַתָּ֤ה שַׁלּ֙וֹתָ֙ גּוֹיִ֣ם רַבִּ֔ים יְשָׁלּ֖וּךָ כָּל־יֶ֣תֶר עַמִּ֑ים מִדְּמֵ֤י אָדָם֙ וַחֲמַס־אֶ֔רֶץ קִרְיָ֖ה וְכָל־יֹ֥שְׁבֵי בָֽהּ׃ פ</span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏ה֗וֹי בֹּצֵ֛עַ בֶּ֥צַע רָ֖ע לְבֵית֑וֹ לָשׂ֤וּם בַּמָּרוֹם֙ קִנּ֔וֹ לְהִנָּצֵ֖ל מִכַּף־רָֽע׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏יָעַ֥צְתָּ בֹּ֖שֶׁת לְבֵיתֶ֑ךָ קְצוֹת־עַמִּ֥ים רַבִּ֖ים וְחוֹטֵ֥א נַפְשֶֽׁךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏כִּי־אֶ֖בֶן מִקִּ֣יר תִּזְעָ֑ק וְכָפִ֖יס מֵעֵ֥ץ יַעֲנֶֽנָּה׃ פ</span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏ה֛וֹי בֹּנֶ֥ה עִ֖יר בְּדָמִ֑ים וְכוֹנֵ֥ן קִרְיָ֖ה בְּעַוְלָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏הֲל֣וֹא הִנֵּ֔ה מֵאֵ֖ת יְהוָ֣ה צְבָא֑וֹת וְיִֽיגְע֤וּ עַמִּים֙ בְּדֵי־אֵ֔שׁ וּלְאֻמִּ֖ים בְּדֵי־רִ֥יק יִעָֽפוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏כִּ֚י תִּמָּלֵ֣א הָאָ֔רֶץ לָדַ֖עַת אֶת־כְּב֣וֹד יְהוָ֑ה כַּמַּ֖יִם יְכַסּ֥וּ עַל־יָֽם׃ ס</span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏ה֚וֹי מַשְׁקֵ֣ה רֵעֵ֔הוּ מְסַפֵּ֥חַ חֲמָתְךָ֖ וְאַ֣ף שַׁכֵּ֑ר לְמַ֥עַן הַבִּ֖יט עַל־מְעוֹרֵיהֶֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏שָׂבַ֤עְתָּ קָלוֹן֙ מִכָּב֔וֹד שְׁתֵ֥ה גַם־אַ֖תָּה וְהֵֽעָרֵ֑ל תִּסּ֣וֹב עָלֶ֗יךָ כּ֚וֹס יְמִ֣ין יְהוָ֔ה וְקִיקָל֖וֹן עַל־כְּבוֹדֶֽךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏כִּ֣י חֲמַ֤ס לְבָנוֹן֙ יְכַסֶּ֔ךָּ וְשֹׁ֥ד בְּהֵמ֖וֹת יְחִיתַ֑ן מִדְּמֵ֤י אָדָם֙ וַחֲמַס־אֶ֔רֶץ קִרְיָ֖ה וְכָל־יֹ֥שְׁבֵי בָֽהּ׃ ס</span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏מָֽה־הוֹעִ֣יל פֶּ֗סֶל כִּ֤י פְסָלוֹ֙ יֹֽצְר֔וֹ מַסֵּכָ֖ה וּמ֣וֹרֶה שָּׁ֑קֶר כִּ֣י בָטַ֞ח יֹצֵ֤ר יִצְרוֹ֙ עָלָ֔יו לַעֲשׂ֖וֹת אֱלִילִ֥ים אִלְּמִֽים׃ ס</span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏ה֣וֹי אֹמֵ֤ר לָעֵץ֙ הָקִ֔יצָה ע֖וּרִי לְאֶ֣בֶן דּוּמָ֑ם ה֣וּא יוֹרֶ֔ה הִנֵּה־ה֗וּא תָּפוּשׂ֙ זָהָ֣ב וָכֶ֔סֶף וְכָל־ר֖וּחַ אֵ֥ין בְּקִרְבּֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏וַֽיהוָ֖ה בְּהֵיכַ֣ל קָדְשׁ֑וֹ הַ֥ס מִפָּנָ֖יו כָּל־הָאָֽרֶץ׃ פ</span></p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hab002.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Super custodiam meam stabo,<br> et figam gradum super munitionem:<br> et contemplabor ut videam quid dicatur mihi,<br> et quid respondeam ad arguentem me.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>2 Et respondit mihi Dominus, et dixit: [Scribe visum, et explana eum super tabulas,<br> ut percurrat qui legerit eum.<br></p>
 <p>3 Quia adhuc visus procul;<br> et apparebit in finem, et non mentietur:<br> si moram fecerit, exspecta illum,<br> quia veniens veniet, et non tardabit.<br></p>
 <p>4 Ecce qui incredulus est, non erit recta anima ejus in semetipso;<br> justus autem in fide sua vivet.<br></p>
 <p>5 Et quomodo vinum potantem decipit,<br> sic erit vir superbus, et non decorabitur:<br> qui dilatavit quasi infernus animam suam,<br> et ipse quasi mors, et non adimpletur:<br> et congregabit ad se omnes gentes,<br> et coacervabit ad se omnes populos.<br></p>
 <p>6 Numquid non omnes isti super eum parabolam sument,<br> et loquelam ænigmatum ejus, et dicetur:<br> Væ ei qui multiplicat non sua?<br> usquequo et aggravat contra se densum lutum?<br></p>
 <p>7 Numquid non repente consurgent qui mordeant te,<br> et suscitabuntur lacerantes te,<br> et eris in rapinam eis?<br></p>
 <p>8 Quia tu spoliasti gentes multas,<br> spoliabunt te omnes qui reliqui fuerint de populis,<br> propter sanguinem hominis,<br> et iniquitatem terræ, civitatis, et omnium habitantium in ea.<br></p>
 <p>9 Væ qui congregat avaritiam malam domui suæ,<br> ut sit in excelso nidus ejus,<br> et liberari se putat de manu mali!<br></p>
 <p>10 Cogitasti confusionem domui tuæ;<br> concidisti populos multos,<br> et peccavit anima tua.<br></p>
 <p>11 Quia lapis de pariete clamabit,<br> et lignum, quod inter juncturas ædificiorum est, respondebit.<br></p>
 <p>12 Væ qui ædificat civitatem in sanguinibus,<br> et præparat urbem in iniquitate!<br></p>
 <p>13 Numquid non hæc sunt a Domino exercituum?<br> laborabunt enim populi in multo igne,<br> et gentes in vacuum, et deficient.<br></p>
 <p>14 Quia replebitur terra, ut cognoscant gloriam Domini,<br> quasi aquæ operientes mare.<br></p>
 <p>15 Væ qui potum dat amico suo mittens fel suum,<br> et inebrians ut aspiciat nuditatem ejus!<br></p>
 <p>16 Repletus es ignominia pro gloria;<br> bibe tu quoque, et consopire.<br> Circumdabit te calix dexteræ Domini,<br> et vomitus ignominiæ super gloriam tuam.<br></p>
 <p>17 Quia iniquitas Libani operiet te,<br> et vastitas animalium deterrebit eos<br> de sanguinibus hominum,<br> et iniquitate terræ, et civitatis, et omnium habitantium in ea.<br></p>
 <p>18 Quid prodest sculptile, quia sculpsit illud fictor suus,<br> conflatile, et imaginem falsam?<br> quia speravit in figmento fictor ejus, ut faceret simulacra muta.<br></p>
 <p>19 Væ qui dicit ligno: Expergiscere;<br> Surge, lapidi tacenti!<br> Numquid ipse docere poterit?<br> ecce iste coopertus est auro et argento,<br> et omnis spiritus non est in visceribus ejus.<br></p>
 <p>20 Dominus autem in templo sancto suo:<br> sileat a facie ejus omnis terra!]</p>
