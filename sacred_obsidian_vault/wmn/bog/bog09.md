 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Book of the Goddess</i>, by Anna Livia Plurabelle, [© 2002 Anna Livia Plurabelle, All rights reserved], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">V.</h3>  
 <p>I believe, O Goddess of all Goddesses,<br> That Thou art the eternal Mother of life;<br> I believe, O Goddess of all Goddesses,<br> That Thou art the eternal Mother of love.<br> I believe, O Goddess of all Goddesses,<br> That Thou art the eternal Mother of the avatars;<br> I believe, O Goddess of all Goddesses,<br> That Thou art the eternal Mother of each one.<br> I believe, O Goddess of all Goddesses,<br> That Thou art the eternal Mother of all beings;<br> I believe, O Goddess of all Goddesses,<br> That Thou art the eternal Mother of the Universe.<br> I believe, O Goddess of the Universe,<br> That Thou art the creator of the high heavens,<br> That Thou art the creator of the skies above,<br> That Thou art the creator of the oceans below.<br> I believe, O Goddess of the Universe,<br> That Thou art She Who created my soul and set its warp,<br> Who created my body from dust and from ashes,<br> Who gave to my body breath, and to my soul its possession.<br> Mother, bless to me my body,<br> Mother, bless to me my soul,<br> Mother, bless to me my life,<br> Mother, bless to me my belief.<br> Mother eternal and Goddess of the Universe,<br> Mother eternal and Creatrix of life,<br> I believe that Thou didst sing thy song at the bringing of the light<br> Mother eternal and Goddess of the Universe,<br> Enwrap Thou my body and my soul beloved,<br> Safeguard me this night in the sanctuary of Thy love,<br> Shelter me this night in the shelter of the avatars.<br> Thou hast brought me up from last night<br> To the gracious light of this day,<br> Great joy to provide for my soul,<br> And to do excelling good to me.<br> Thanks be to Thee, Oh great Goddess,<br> For the many gifts Thou hast bestowed on me,<br> Each day and night, each sea and land,<br> Each weather fair, each calm, each wild.<br> I am giving Thee worship with my whole life,<br> I am giving Thee assent with my whole power,<br> I am giving Thee praise with my whole tongue,<br> I am giving Thee honor with my whole utterance.<br> I am giving Thee reverence with my whole understanding,<br> I am giving Thee offering with my whole thought,<br> I am giving Thee praise with my whole fervor,<br> I am giving Thee love with my whole devotion,<br> I am giving Thee pleasure with my whole desire,<br> I am giving Thee love with my whole heart,<br> I am giving Thee affection with my whole sense;<br> I am giving Thee my existence with my whole mind,<br> I am giving Thee my soul, O Goddess of the Universe.<br> My thought, my deed,<br> My word, my will,<br> My understanding, my intellect,<br> My way, my state.<br> I am beseeching Thee<br> To keep me from ill,<br> To keep me from hurt,<br> To keep me from harm;<br> To keep me from mischance,<br> To keep me from grief,<br> To keep me this night<br> In the nearness of Thy love.<br> May the Goddess shield me,<br> May the Goddess fill me,<br> May the Goddess keep me,<br> May the Goddess watch me.<br> May the Goddess bring me<br> To the land of peace,<br> To the grove of the Goddess,<br> To the peace of eternity.<br> Praise to the Nymph,<br> Praise to the Mother,<br> Praise to the Crone,<br> The Three in One.</p>
