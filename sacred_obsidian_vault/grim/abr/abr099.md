   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XXVII.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are only manifested by the Evil Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ORIENS, PAIMON, ARITON, and AMAIMON, execute the Operations hereof by the means of their common Ministers.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits can to an extent perform the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given by Abraham regarding this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 consists of 13 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. SELAC means "to cast down," "to cut down or fell" (as trees). Perhaps thus signifying the cut wood with which a trellis is made.</p>  
 <p>No. 2 consists of 13 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares. HESEB may mean the environs of a place.</p>  
 <p>No. 3 consists of 19 Squares from a Square of 49 Squares. AODONIA, from Hebrew root ODN = "Eden, a delightful place, etc.".</p>  
 <p>No. 4 is a Square of 49 Squares. ATSARAH = either "a store-house or treasury" or "to flow," according to its derivation.</p>  
 <p>No. 7 (the order of the numbering of the Squares is here changed) consists of 10 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. SOREK means "to wind about".</p>  
 <p>No. 8 consists of 19 Squares from a Square of 81 Squares. AKROPOLIS is a Greek word signifying "citadel".</p>  
 <p>No. 6 consists of 18 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. SELEG = "Snow," whence perhaps this should be numbered 19 instead of 6.</p>  
 <p>No. 5 consists of 15 Squares from a Square of 49. AGAMAGA = "pools of water," whence this should probably be numbered 4 instead of 5.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_242"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 242</font></a></p>  
 <p>No. 9 consists of 10 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares. CAIOT is probably from CHAIOTH = "Living creatures". It may also mean covert, where living creatures abide. Perhaps it should be numbered 20.</p>  
 <p>No. 10 consists of 11 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. IAQEB probably means a bird of the crane species.</p>  
 <p>No. 11 consists of 17 Squares taken from a Square of 49 Squares. MELUNAC = "Thy dwelling-place," and perhaps this should be numbered 21.</p>  
 <p>No. 12 consists of 11 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. PERAC may mean "flowering gardens". Perhaps this should be numbered 13.</p>  
 <p>No. 15 consists of 16 Squares taken from a Square of 49 Squares.</p>  
 <p>No. 14 is a Gnomon of 11 Squares from a Square of 36 Squares. OLELAH may mean "horned animals" or "tusked animals".</p>  
 <p>No. 13 consists of 14 Squares taken from a Square of 49 Squares. KIKAION = a place where gourds grow.</p>  
 <p>No. 16 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. MAKOR = "places digged".</p>  
 <p>No. 17 consists of 21 Squares taken from a Square of 49 Squares. MIGIRAS = "a place where productive plants grow".</p>  
 <p>No. 18 consists of 12 Squares from a Square of 36 Squares. ESAHEL = "rich".</p>  
 <p>No. 19 should probably be numbered 24. It is a Gnomon of 9 from a Square of 25 Squares. ARIEH = "a lion".</p>  
 <p>No. 20 consists of 19 from a Square of 49 Squares. LIMIKOS = "savage (animals)".</p>  
 <p>No. 21 consists of 13 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares. SASAS probably means "Horses," and this Square should probably be numbered 26 instead of 21.</p>  
 <p>No. 22 consists of 18 Squares taken from a Square of 49 Squares. KIKIMIS = "thistles," and also "some kinds of flowers".</p>  
 <p>No. 23 consists of 15 Squares from a Square of 49 Squares. NESIKER signifies fluids of various kinds.</p>  
 <p>No. 24 consists of 9 Squares from a Square of 25. DOBIH = "a bear," and should evidently be otherwise numbered.</p>  
 <p>No. 25 consists of 14 Squares from a Square of 49 Squares.</p>  
 <p>No. 26 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares. PARAH = "a heifer," but also "fruit, produce".</p>  
 <p>No. 27 consists of 15 Squares from a Square of 49.</p>  
 <p>No. 28 consists of 10 Squares from a Square of 25. FANIN is probably from BN, and meaning "Towns and Villages," whence this should probably be numbered 21.</p>  
 <p>No. 29 consists of 8 Squares from a Square of 16. REEM =</p>  
 <p><a name="page_243"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 243</font></a></p>  
 <p><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font> "Unicorns," and also beasts of the beeve kind, Buffaloes, etc. Perhaps this Square answers for 15 as well.</p>  
 <p>No. 30 consists of a Gnomon of 13 Squares from a Square of 2 5 Squares. AIIAH = rapacious birds.</p>  
 <p>No. 31 consists of 10 Squares from a Square of 25. SUHAL means "a blackish lion," whence this Square should be numbered 24 probably.</p>  
 <p>No. 32 consists of 18 Squares from a Square of 49. GIRIPES <i>may</i> mean "small beasts that run swiftly".</p>  
 <p>No. 33 consists of 10 Squares from a Square of 25. ARNEP should probably be ARNEB. It means "a Hare," whence this Square should perhaps be numbered 32.</p>  
 <p>No. 34 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares from a Square of 2 5. AIIAL probably means "wild goats".</p>  
 <p>No. 35 consists of 12 Squares from 25. KELEF = "a Dog," whence this Square should probably be numbered 33.</p>
