 <h1 class="title" align="center">LIBER XXI<br>
   KHING KANG KING <br>
   THE CLASSIC OF PURITY</h1>
 <h2 class="heading" align="center">first written down by me<br>
   KO YUEN<br>
   LAO-TZU)<br>
   in the Episode of the Dynasty of Wu <br>
   and now made into a Rime by me <br>
   ALEISTER CROWLEY</h2>
 <h2 align="center" class="heading">A.·. A.·.<br>
   PUBLICATION IN CLASS B<br>
   Imprimatur: O.S.V. 60= 5[] Imperator</h2>
 <p align="center"><font size="4"><strong>I</strong></font></p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>LAO KUN THE MASTER SAID:<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>TAO IS DEVOID OF FORM -<br>
     YET HEAVEN AND EARTH ARE BROUGHT TO BIRTH,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>AND NURTURED BY ITS NORM.</p>
   <p>TAO HATH NO WILL TO WORK;<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>YET BY ITS WAY OF HEAVEN<br>
     THE MOON AND SUN REJOICE TO RUN<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>AMONG THE STARRY SEVEN.</p>
   <p>TAO HATH NO NAME; ITS WORD<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> IS GROWTH, AND SUSTENANCE<br>
     TO ALL; I AIM TO GIVE IT NAME:<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>TAO (HEAVEN PROSPER CHANCE!)</p>
   <p>TAO HATH TWIN PHASE WITH TEH:<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THE SILENT AND THE STRESSED.<br>
     OF MOTION, THOSE; OF THESE, REPOSE<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>SUBLIMELY MANIFEST</p>
   <p>HEAVEN MOVES, PURE SILENCE HE;<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> EARTH RESTS BENEATH THE STRAIN;<br>
     SHUTTLE AND LOOM, AS WORD AND WOMB,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THEIR MYSTERY SUSTAIN</p>
 </blockquote>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>PURE MOTION MAKETH REST<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> AS SILENCE MAKETH STRESS.<br>
     IF MAN WERE STILL, THEN HEAVEN SHOULD THRILL<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>WITH EARTH TO NOTHINGNESS.</p>
   <p>SELF LOVETH SILENCE. YEA,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> BUT MIND DISTRACTETH IT.<br>
     MIND LOVETH REST; BUT PASSION'S PEST<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>ALLURES THE TREMBLING WIT.</p>
   <p>IF MAN RESTRAIN DESIRE,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>HIS MIND WILL CEASE TO ROLL,<br>
     AND MIND'S RELEASE ALLOW PURE PEACE<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>OF SILENCE TO THE SOUL.</p>
   <p>THE SENSES WILL NOT SOIL;<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> THE THOUGHT WILL NOT UPSTRESS;<br>
     NOR POISONS (GREED, WRATH, DULNESS) BREED<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THEIR TRIFORM DEADLINESS.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>MEN EARN NOT EASE OF TAO<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> FOR THEIR DESIRE'S DISEASE;<br>
     BECAUSE THEIR MIND IS NOT REFINED<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>OF THOUGHT BY KILLING THESE.</p>
   <p>IF ONE SHOULD SLAY DESIRES,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>HIS MIND AND BODY SEEM<br>
     NO LONGER HIS, BUT PHANTASIES<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>DANCED IN A WANTON'S DREAM.</p>
   <p>SLAY MIND, SLAY BODY, SLAY<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> THE EXTERNAL: MATTER GOES.<br>
     THEN SPACE REMAINS; RENEW THY PAINS!<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>UP! FRONT THE FINAL FOES!</p>
 </blockquote>
 <p align="center"><font size="4"><strong>II</strong></font></p>
 <blockquote> 
   <p>LAO KUN THE MASTER SAID:<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THE ADEPT IN SKILL OF SOUL<br>
     HATH NEVER AN AIM: THE BUNGLERS SHAME<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>IS THAT HE GROÿES A GOAL.</p>
   <p>WHO MOST POSSESS THE TEH<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> CONCEAL THEIR MAGICK POWER;<br>
     WHO LEAST POSSESS EXERT THEIR STRENGTH<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>SEVEN TIMES IN EVERY HOUR.</p>
   <p>THESE, WHO CLING FAST TO POWERS,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> WHO GUARD THEM, AND DISPLAY<br>
     THEIR MAGICK ART - THEY ARE NOT PART<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>OF TAO NOR YET OF TEH.</p>
   <p>MEN WIN NOT TRUTH OF TAO<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> BECAUSE THEIR MINDS ARE WRIED.<br>
     THE MIND UNCURBED, THE SELF'S PERTURBED,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>AND LOSES TUNE OF TIDE.</p>
   <p>LOST, THE EXTERNAL LURES;<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> THEY TURN TO SEEK IT: THEN<br>
     ALL THINGS PERPLEX, CONFUSE, AND VEX<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THOSE MISERABLE MEN.</p>
   <p>DISORDERED THOUGHTS ARISE;<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> BODY AND MIND GROW SICK.<br>
     DISGRACE AND FEAR GROW YEAR BY YEAR<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>TO THEIR CLIMACTERIC.</p>
   <p>WILD, THEY ARE TOSSED ABOUT<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THROUGH LIFE AND DEATH; THEY QUIVER,<br>
     SUNK IN SEA-STRESS OF BITTERNESS,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>AND LOSE THE TAO FOR EVER.</p>
   <p>THE TRUE, THE ABIDING TAO!<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> WHO UNDERSTANDETH HATH;<br>
     WHO HATH THE TAO IS HERE AND NOW<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>IN SILENCE OF THE PATH</p>
   <p>SLAY SPACE; THEN NAUGHT ABIDES.<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>HOLD NOT THY HOLY HAND!<br>
     WHEN NAUGHT GIVES BACK BEFORE THE ATTACK,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>SERENE THY SILENCE STAND!</p>
   <p>ALL'S REST, DEVOID OF MARK;<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>HOW SHOULD DESIRES FIX TOOTH?<br>
     WHEN THEY ARE PAST, THOU SURELY HAST<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THE SILENCE OF THE TRUTH.</p>
   <p>FLAWLESS THAT TRUTH AND FIXED,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> YET APT TO EACH APPEAL<br>
     NATURE AND SENSE TO INFLUENCE -<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THE MAGNET TO THE STEEL!</p>
   <p>OH! THIS TRUE TOUCH WITH ALL<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>ELASTIC AND EXACT<br>
     THAT YET ABOVE THEIR TIDES -<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THE SILENCE FREE FROM ACT!</p>
   <p>HE THAT HATH THIS SHALL COME<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> LITTLE BY LITTLE, A BREATH,<br>
     SO FLOWETH HE NOW, TO TRUTH OF TAO,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>WHEREIN HE VANISHETH.</p>
   <p>MEN STYLE HIM LORD OF TAO,<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> YET HE HATH NONE TO LORD.<br>
     HID MOTIVE HE OF ALL THAT BE:<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>ENOUGH FOR HIS REWARD!</p>
   <p>HE THAT CAN COMPREHEND<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font> THIS DOCTRINE MAY TRANSMIT<br>
     THIS SACRED TAO TO MEN THAT VOW<br>
     <font color="#C0C0C0">..........</font>THEMSELVES TO FATHOM IT.</p>
 </blockquote>
