 <br>
 1. Great Central Cross. This has 36 squares, for the decantes of the <br>

 <br>
 On the left side of the Pyramid, Linea Patris has the Cardinal signs, the <br>
 sign of the Element itself at the top, in the order of Tetragrammaton (Fire, <br>
 Water, Air, Earth) going upwards.<br>
 <br>
 Linea Filii has the Common signs in the same order.<br>
 <br>
 Linea S.S. has the Cherubic signs, that of the element on the left, in the <br>
 same order, right to left.<br>
 <br>
 But the order of the decans in each sign is reverse, and thus the planets <br>
 which fill the right-hand side of the Pyramids go in the first two cases <br>
 downwards, and in the third from left to right.<br>
 <br>
 The upper sides of the Pyramids are all attributed to the Element of <br>
 Spirit, the lower sides to the Element of the Tablet.<br>
 <br>
 Each square is also referred to the small card of the Tarot which <br>
 corresponds to the Decan (see 777).<br>
 <br>
 2. Calvary Crosses.<br>
 <br>
 Each has 10 squares.<br>
 <br>
 The upper sides of the Pyramids are uniformly given to Spirit, the <br>
 lower sides to the Sephiroth, in the order shewn. The left-hand sides are <br>
 attributed to the element of the Tablet, the right-hand sides to the <br>
 sub-element of the lesser angle.<br>
 <br>
 3. Kerubic Squares.<br>
 <br>
 The upper sides pertain to the element of the Tablet, the lower sides to <br>
 the sub-element. Right-and left-hand sides in this case correspond, according to a
 somewhat complex rule which it is unnecessary to give here. The attributions to the Court
 Cards of the Tarot naturally follow.<br>
 <br>
 4. Lesser Squares.<br>
 <br>
 The upper side of each pyramid is governed by the Kerub standing on the <br>
 pile above it. The lower side is governed by the Kerub also, but in order <br>
 descending as they are from right to left above. [See angle of Air of Water; <br>
 the Kerubs go Earth, Fire, Water, Air (from the square marked D, the fifth <br>
 from the left in the top rank of the Tablet), and downward the lower sides of the squares
 marked O, D, E, Z go Earth, Fire, Water, Air.]<br>
 <br>
 The left-hand side refers to the element of the Tablet, the right-hand side<br>
 to the sub-element of the lesser angle.<br>
 <br>
 5. The Black Cross of Central Tablet.<br>
 <br>
 The upper and lower sides are equally attributed to Spirit.<br>
 The left-hand sides to the element of the file, in this order from left to <br>
 right: Spirit, Air, Water, Earth, Fire.<br>
 The right-hand sides to the element of the rank in this order: Air, Water, <br>
 Earth, Fire.<br>
