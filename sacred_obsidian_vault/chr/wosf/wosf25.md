 <p><a name="fn_629"></a><a href="wosf25.htm#fr_629">195:1</a> Wadding's <i>Annales</i> appeared at Lyons in 8 vols. in fol. 1625-54. Fr. Jos. Man. Fonseca published a new edition and a continuation of the <i>Annales</i> in 19 vols. at Rome, 1731-45. The official Annalists of the Friars Minor have since added 6 vols. (tom. 20-25), which were issued at Naples, Ancona, and Quaracchi. The last vol. (t. 25) edited by Fr. Eusebius Fernandzin († 1899) extends to the year 2622. The Quaracchi Friars are now engaged on the 26th volume.</p><p></p><hr>
