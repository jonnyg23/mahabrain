 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../img/uni_t.jpg"></a><br><i>Teach Us to Pray</i>, by Charles Fillmore, [1941], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"> <br> Questions for Teach Us To Pray<br> By Charles and Cora Fillmore<br> </h3>  
 <p>The God to Whom We Pray </p> 
 <p>1. How is the spiritual character built? </p> 
 <p>2. Explain "I go to prepare a place for you." </p> 
 <p>3. How are we born anew through Christ? </p> 
 <p>4. How does man awaken the divine nature within him and bring about his union with God? </p> 
 <p>True Prayer </p> 
 <p>1. What is true prayer? </p> 
 <p>2. How does one enter the silence? </p> 
 <p>3. How are prayers fulfilled? </p> 
 <p>4. Why does God need man as an avenue of expression. </p> 
 <p>Intellectual Silence and Spiritual Silence </p> 
 <p>1. Why do we ask in the name of Jesus Christ? </p> 
 <p>2. Does salvation come by accepting Jesus as one's Saviour? Explain. </p> 
 <p>3. Did Jesus make individual effort unnecessary? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_192"><font size="1" color="green">p. 192</font></a></p> 
 <p>4. What is intellectual silence? </p> 
 <p>5. What is spiritual silence? </p> 
 <p>Healing through the Prayer of Faith </p> 
 <p>1. How is faith healing done? </p> 
 <p>2. How does man develop a deeper faith? </p> 
 <p>3. What is attention? </p> 
 <p>4. What is concentration? </p> 
 <p>Prosperity through Prayer </p> 
 <p>1. Give your own interpretation of the story of Elisha and the oil. </p> 
 <p>2. Give the metaphysical interpretation of Elisha, the widow, and the oil. </p> 
 <p>3. Why is praise so beneficial? </p> 
 <p>4. Why is daily prayer essential? </p> 
 <p>Contacting Spiritual Substance </p> 
 <p>1. What is Spirit substance? </p> 
 <p>2. How does man appropriate and manifest the invisible substance? </p> 
 <p>3. How do we gain control of Spirit substance? </p> 
 <p>4. Why are prosperity demonstrations delayed? </p> 
 <p>Joyous Prayer </p> 
 <p>1. How can a metaphysician contact the undiscovered quantities in space? </p> 
 <p>2. How is the Christ consciousness attained? What is the result? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_193"><font size="1" color="green">p. 193</font></a></p> 
 <p>3. Explain the effect of joy on the mind, body, and affairs. </p> 
 <p>4. Why must we pray with a purpose? </p> 
 <p>How to Handle the Psychic Forces of Consciousness </p> 
 <p>1. What is the only way to handle the psychic forces? </p> 
 <p>2. What are the three elements of the soul, and what is comprised in each? </p> 
 <p>3. What effect does the psychic realm have on the emotional nature? </p> 
 <p>4. What happens to souls who pass on? </p> 
 <p>5. Explain how Spirit gives messages in dreams and visions. </p> 
 <p>6. How is man being restored to the heavenly kingdom? </p> 
 <p>Spiritual Unfoldment Makes Man Master </p> 
 <p>1. Is man the equal of God? </p> 
 <p>2. How does man develop his innate abilities? </p> 
 <p>3. How did Jesus become a master? </p> 
 <p>4. Why is the unfoldment of love so important in gaining spiritual mastery? </p> 
 <p>Fulfillment </p> 
 <p>1. What is the "light of the world"? </p> 
 <p>2. How did the crucifixion of Jesus save us from sin, sickness, and death? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_194"><font size="1" color="green">p. 194</font></a></p> 
 <p>3. What is the grand fulfillment? </p> 
 <p>4. Explain the difference between "life" and "blood." </p> 
 <p>Unfoldment </p> 
 <p>1. What was the evolution of Jesus? </p> 
 <p>2. What is your understanding of the Logos? </p> 
 <p>3. How is the body transformed? </p> 
 <p>4. How do we partake of the Holy Communion? </p> 
 <p>Thought Images </p> 
 <p>1. What are thought images? </p> 
 <p>2. How do we project thought? </p> 
 <p>3. Can man have a perfect character? How? </p> 
 <p>4. Are there short cuts into the kingdom of the heaven? </p> 
 <p>The Spoken Word </p> 
 <p>1. Explain the process of creative thought. </p> 
 <p>2. How does the soul overcome death? </p> 
 <p>3. What is your understanding of the "ether"? </p> 
 <p>4. How do we receive a life transfusion? </p> 
 <p>Thou Shalt Decree </p> 
 <p>1. What is the effect of man's words? </p> 
 <p>2. Does an inferiority complex prevent one from expressing sonship? </p> 
 <p>3. Why do the organs of the body respond to our decrees? </p> 
 <p>4. Amplify Dr. Alexis Carrel's statement "The only thing that keeps men from living forever is the possession of a brain and nervous system." </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_195"><font size="1" color="green">p. 195</font></a></p> 
 <p>Be Strong in the Lord </p> 
 <p>1. How do we become strong in the Lord? </p> 
 <p>2. What was Jesus' instruction regarding prayer? </p> 
 <p>3. Name some helps to entering the silence. </p> 
 <p>4. Explain the effect of words of praise and failure. </p> 
 <p>Face to Face with God </p> 
 <p>1. Why should we speak direct to God? </p> 
 <p>2. How can we become established in the consciousness of oneness with God? </p> 
 <p>3. Explain the parable of the prodigal son. </p> 
 <p>4. Why must we study the life of Jesus? </p> 
 <p>Not Magic but Law </p> 
 <p>1. Where is the "kingdom of God"? </p> 
 <p>2. Was Jesus the only Son of God? Explain. </p> 
 <p>3. How do we attain the consciousness of eternal life? </p> 
 <p>4. Why should we look to God as our resource? </p> 
 <p>Spiritual Soul Therapy </p> 
 <p>1. What is Spirit psychoanalysis? </p> 
 <p>2. What determines the character of our soul? </p> 
 <p>3. Explain the effect of praise on the individual. </p> 
 <p>4. Does the mental attitude govern spiritual demonstration? </p> 
 <p>Health and Prosperity </p> 
 <p>1. How can Spirit manifest itself? </p> 
 <p>2. How is the immortal body formed? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_196"><font size="1" color="green">p. 196</font></a></p> 
 <p>3. Where is the substance of God? </p> 
 <p>4. Can we manipulate substance as Jesus did? Explain fully. </p> 
 <p>Thoughts Are Things </p> 
 <p>1. What are thoughts? </p> 
 <p>2. Compare the "ether" of science with the Garden of Eden and the "kingdom of the heavens" of Jesus. </p> 
 <p>3. Do you agree with Professor Jeans' statement that we live in a universe of waves? </p> 
 <p>4. How does man form his world? </p> 
 <p>5. Is there a panacea for the present world conditions? </p> 
 <p>The Supermind </p> 
 <p>1. Do all kinds of healers use the same force? Why? </p> 
 <p>2. What is heaven? </p> 
 <p>3. What is hell? </p> 
 <p>4. How did Jesus save us from destructive, discordant conditions? </p> 
 <p>5. Why do we pray in the name of Jesus Christ? </p> 
 <p>Cheerfulness Heals </p> 
 <p>1. How does cheerfulness aid healing? </p> 
 <p>2. Why does a cheerful attitude bring prosperity? </p> 
 <p>3. Why is proper diet conducive to health? </p> 
 <p>4. Why do we bless our money and affairs? </p> 
 <p>Love Harmonizes </p> 
 <p>1. How does love adjust man's discords? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_197"><font size="1" color="green">p. 197</font></a></p> 
 <p>2. Explain the law of gravitation. </p> 
 <p>3. How are great souls developed? </p> 
 <p>4. Why did Jesus use scientific methods? </p> 
 <p>Casting Out Fear </p> 
 <p>1. What is the effect of fear on the body? </p> 
 <p>2. How do we cast out fear? </p> 
 <p>3. How do we fulfill the law of our being? </p> 
 <p>4. What does perfect love mean to you? </p> 
 <p>Spiritual Hearing </p> 
 <p>1. How do we hear? </p> 
 <p>2. Can everyone hear with the "inner ear"? Explain. </p> 
 <p>3. Is excessive meditation advisable? Why not? </p> 
 <p>4. Explain the importance of spiritual receptivity. </p> 
 <p>Light of Life </p> 
 <p>1. What is the "true light"? </p> 
 <p>2. Why have the discoveries of modern science about light failed to aid man in his spiritual development? </p> 
 <p>3. Are light and intelligence one? Explain. </p> 
 <p>4. What should be the attitude of Truth students in regard to physical science? </p> 
 <p>Thought Substance </p> 
 <p>1. How are our prayers answered? </p> 
 <p>2. Does asking alone bring abundance into manifestation? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_198"><font size="1" color="green">p. 198</font></a></p> 
 <p>3. What is the Spirit of truth? </p> 
 <p>4. In what way are the laws of hygiene and diet beneficial? </p> 
 <p>Intensified Zeal </p> 
 <p>1. Explain the office of the faculty of zeal. </p> 
 <p>2. What is the cause of "old age"? </p> 
 <p>3. How did Jesus overcome greed? </p> 
 <p>4. How do we lay up treasures in the heavens? </p> 
 <p>The Unreality of Error </p> 
 <p>1. Why is error unreal? </p> 
 <p>2. What is the object of man's existence? </p> 
 <p>3. How is the truth revealed to us? </p> 
 <p>4. What is matter? </p> 
 <p>Joy Radiates Health </p> 
 <p>1. Explain the effect of laughter and joy on health. </p> 
 <p>2. Where did Solomon turn for judgment? </p> 
 <p>3. How is happiness attained? </p> 
 <p>4. How do we find lasting peace? </p> 
 <p>"Selah!" </p> 
 <p>1. What does "selah" mean? </p> 
 <p>2. Why is "God first" the only way to the consciousness of Truth? </p> 
 <p>3. How does man demonstrate prosperity? </p> 
 <p>4. What is the meaning of the word "Jehovah"? </p> 
 <p>
 </p><p><a name="page_199"><font size="1" color="green">p. 199</font></a></p> 
 <p>Spiritualizing the Intellect </p> 
 <p>1. How many minds are there? Explain. </p> 
 <p>2. How do we unify all our forces? </p> 
 <p>3. Explain the difference between the blessings bestowed by Isaac on Jacob and Esau. </p> 
 <p>4. What is the intellectual man? </p> 
 <p>5. What is the proper relationship between the mind and the heart? The mind and the body? </p> 
 <p>6. Explain the principle of tithing. </p> 
 <p>The Sevenfold Cleansing </p> 
 <p>1. Why is humility necessary in spiritual healing? </p> 
 <p>2. Why must personal egotism be overcome? </p> 
 <p>3. Name two important steps in spiritual healing. </p> 
 <p>4. What part did Elisha have in the healing of Naaman? </p> 
 <p>Prayer and Faith </p> 
 <p>1. Define prayer in your own words. </p> 
 <p>2. What does faith mean to you? </p> 
 <p>3. Why did Paul admonish us to pray without ceasing? </p> 
 <p>4. What is meant by true spiritual baptism? </p> 
 <p>The Healing Word </p> 
 <p>1. Does the "word" have power to heal? Why? </p> 
 <p>2. What is the "only Son of God"? </p> 
 <p>3. What does logos mean? </p> 
 <p>4. Can man forgive sin? How? </p>
