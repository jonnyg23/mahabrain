 <p>Polaa (sacred night) was born.<br> Then Storm was born, the Tide was born,<br> The Crash was born, and also bursts of bubbles.<br> Confusion was born, also rushing, rumbling, shaking earth.<br> The sea became calm and mountains rose;<br> Water became lakes and formed houses;<br> With great awe the posts rose,<br> And shouts ascended when the spear of Kauikaho was flung.<br> He wrestled with Kanaloa, Kanikahoe.<br> Second night was born in the presence of Wakea.<br> Stormy night was born.<br> Plenty was born.<br> A fowl was born on the back of Wakea.<br> Then Kupololiilialiimuaoloipo died;<br> The Aukaha Opikokahonua died; he was a warrior.<br> The leaves bore and disappeared,<br> Disappeared in the darkest night.</p>
