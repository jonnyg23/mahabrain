 <p>Oliaikuhonua was born in the night of Puukahonualani.<br> Then next in birth was</p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="327">   
 <p>Ohomaili   (beautiful hair).<br>   Kehaukea (white dews).<br>   Mohala (opening).<br>   Kahakuiaweaukelekele.<br>   Kahokuhookelemoana.<br>   Mulinaha (last of broken birth).</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="327">   
 <p>Honuakau   (earth hung).<br>   Kualeikahu (back that bore his follower).<br>   Luukaualani (showers that deluged heaven).<br>   Hinawainonolo (Hina of the gurgling waters).<br>   Hinawaioki (Hina of the cutting waters).<br>   Ipoi (held up).</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p>Then was born</p>  
 <p>Laumiha, the woman that dwelt with Kekahakualani.<br> Kahaula, the woman that dwelt with Kuhulihonua.<br> Kahakauakoko, the woman that dwelt with Kulaniehu.<br> Haumea who dwelt with Kanaloaakua.<br> Kukauakahi, the man who dwelt with Kuaimehana.<br> Kauahulihonua. <a name="page_68"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 68</font></a><br> Hinamanouluae.<br> Huhune.<br> Haunuu.<br> Haulani.</p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="410">   
 <p>Hikapuanaiea.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="410">   
 <p>Haumea   was discovered.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p>Haumea of mythical form, Haumea with eight different forms,<br> Haumea of several forms, Haumea in form of a shark.<br> Whose many forms took different shapes,<br> And at the birth of Hikapuanaiea her breasts were caught by the heavens.<br> This woman of Nuumea was discovered by a dog.<br> Nuumea was the land, Nuupapakini the earth,<br> Where Haumea's grandchildren increased.<br> In Kio sickness ended, the brains began to roll.<br> This woman that gave birth from her head,<br> Children were born from her brains.<br> This woman of the darkest night, of Nuumea,<br> And lived at Mulinaha,<br> Gave birth to Laumiha through the brain;<br> Gave birth to Kahaula, a woman, through the brain;<br> Gave birth to Kahakauakoko through the brain.<br> Haumea was the same woman<br> Who lived with Kanaloaakua.<br> Kauakahiakua was born from the brains;<br> Her children were mostly born from the brains;<br> With great slime was the birth from the brain<br> By "Papa who sought the earth" (people),<br> By "Papa who sought the heavens" (chief),<br> By Papa the great producer of lands,<br> By Papa who lived with Wakea.<br> Haalolo was born a woman.<br> Accompanying its birth were anger and jealousy.<br> Wakea became false to Papa.<br> Changed the days and months,<br> Ordered the nights of Kane towards the last of the month<br> And the nights of Hilo to be first; <a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 69</font></a><br> And established sacred tabus across his threshold.<br> Such was the house that Wakea lived in.<br> The food of the parent chief became sacred;<br> The Ape, so bitter, became sacred;<br> The Akia (sour) became sacred;<br> The Auhuhu (pungent and bitter) became sacred;<br> The Uhaloa for its life-giving properties became sacred;<br> The Laalo, so acid, became sacred;<br> The Haloa that grow by the edge of the patch became sacred.<br> Plant the Haloa, the leaves will grow tall;<br> So grew the sprout of Haloa in the day and<br>                             Thrived.</p>
