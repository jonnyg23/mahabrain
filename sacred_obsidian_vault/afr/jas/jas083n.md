 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">83. Andrew and his Sisters.</h3>
 <p>The story appears in Milne-Home, 114-120.</p>
 <p>Compare Chatelain, 145-151; 103-111; Renel 1:77-81; 2:261-265; 265-267; Ferrand, 119-122; Torrend, 159-163; Tremearne, 432-441; Callaway, 53; Theal, 124-126; Cronise and Ward, 178-186; Dennett, 52; Barker, 97-101; Junod, 144-148, Edwards, 92-93; Parsons, Andros Island, 44.</p>
 <p>See numbers 82 and 86. It is the imitative "Sharpen me razor" song which makes the story successful with an audience.</p>
