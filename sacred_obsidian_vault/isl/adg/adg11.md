 <p>The prince of believers  (puh) said:</p>  
 <p>1. Life is made for  others and not for itself.<br> 2. It is for the low level of this life that Allah is disobeyed only in it, and  nothing can be gotten from Him except by leaving it.</p>  
 <p>3. The bitterness of  this life is the sweetness of the after-life, and the sweetness of this life is  the bitterness of the after-life.</p>  
 <p>4. Life humiliates.<br> 5. Life is the market of losing.<br> 6. Life is the place of miserable ones.<br> 7. Life is full of troubles, frequent with adversities and disasters.<br> 8. The little of this life corrupts the faith.<br> 9. Life gives you up.<br> 10. Faith for the after-life is not useful with love for this life.<br> 11. Being happy about life is a stupidity.<br> 12. Life will end.<br> 13. Life do not protect who takes refuge unto her.<br> 14. Life is the deal of the tricked, and the human being is tricked by it.<br> 15. The little of this life is enough, and too much of it would kill.<br> 16. Life is divorced by the smart people.<br> 17. The companion of the life is a target for adversities and changes.<br> 18. Who turns away from life it shall come him by force.<br> 19. Who is not satisfied with Allah away from this life, then he has no  religion.<br> 20. Who was gave life away then he did not miss it.<br> 21. Who depends on this life, then he is a troublesome banned man.<br> 22. Who fights against life shall be dead.<br> 23. Who spends too much asking for life shall die poor.<br> 24. Who races against life it shall go before him.<br> 25. Who works for this life then had lost.<br> 26. Who sells his after-life for his life then he had lost both.<br> 27. If the people of this life understood, then this life would be destroyed.<br> 28. How many are there who were sure about this life and then got troubled.<br> 29. Who built his life then corrupted his after-life.<br> 30. How many are there of wise men who corrupted their ends.<br> 31. Who takes care of life is doomed.<br> 32. Everything in this life shall over.<br> 33. Who was tricked by this life, then shell be clogged with hopes.<br> 34. How many are there who were trusting this life until it killed them.<br> 35. Who was satisfied about this life, then missed the after-life.<br> 36. Troubles were linked to the love of this life.<br> 37. In leaving life, lies the path of success.<br> 38. The ego of life kills.<br> 39. In this life, the rest of the troubled ones.<br> 40. The goal of this life is to end.<br> 41. The divorce of this life is the dowry of paradise.<br> 42. The health of this life is full of sickness, and its lusts are full of  pains.<br> 43. Asking for life, that is the head of disturbance.<br> 44. The fixation of the after-life is to deny the life.<br> 45. The most devilish of disturbances, the love of life.<br> 46. The happiness of this life is ego, and its enjoyment is to end.<br> 47. The ornaments of life corrupts the weak minds.<br> 48. The reason for the corruption of mind is the love of this life.<br> 49. The head of beasts, falling in love with this life.<br> 50. Mentioning life is the sickest illness.<br> 51. Mixing up with the children of this life, that is the head of adversity and  the corruption of piety.<br> 52. The love of life is the head of every sin.<br> 53. The sweetness of this life is the bitterness of the after-life and the bad  end.<br> 54. The neighbour of this life is in war, and its abundance is troubled.<br> 55. The love of life is the head for every disturbance, and the origin for every  adversity.<br> 56. The love of life is the reason for disturbances.<br> 57. The love of life reveals the greed.<br> 58. Bad is the place of life.<br> 59. By death, life is ended.<br> 60. The greatest adversities and troublesome times is the love of this life.<br> 61. The smartest of people is he who refuses his life.<br> 62. The best garment for this life is to reject it.<br> 63. The greatest sin, the love of life.<br> 64. The best of worshipping is to give life up.<br> 65. Life is a place full of dangers.</p>
