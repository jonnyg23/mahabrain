 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:248</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Amr:</i></p>
 A man came to the Prophet  asking his permission to take part in 
 Jihad. The Prophet asked him, "Are your parents alive?" He replied in 
 the affirmative. The Prophet said to him, "Then exert yourself in 
 their service."
