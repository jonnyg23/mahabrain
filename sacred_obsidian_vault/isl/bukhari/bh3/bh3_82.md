 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:84</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha (mother of the faithful believers):</i></p>
 I said, "O Allah's Apostle! Shouldn't we participate in Holy battles 
 and Jihad along with you?" He replied, "The best and the most superior
 Jihad (for women) is Hajj which is accepted by Allah." 'Aisha added: 
 Ever since I heard that from Allah's Apostle I have determined not to 
 miss Hajj.
