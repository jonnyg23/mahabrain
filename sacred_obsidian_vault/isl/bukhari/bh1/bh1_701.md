 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:699</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle rode a horse and fell down and the right side of his 
 body was injured. On that day he prayed one of the prayers sitting and
 we also prayed behind him sitting. When the Prophet finished the 
 prayer with Taslim, he said, "The Imam is to be followed and if he 
 prays standing then pray standing, and bow when he bows, and raise 
 your heads when he raises his head; prostrate when he prostrates; and 
 if he says "Sami'a-l-lahu Liman hamida", you should say, "Rabbana 
 wa-laka-l hamd.:
