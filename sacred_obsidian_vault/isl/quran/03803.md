 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVIII.: Ṣād (being one of the Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> b<u>at</u>ilan <u>tha</u>lika <i><u>th</u></i>annu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fawaylun lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Not without purpose did We<br> Create heaven and earth<br> And all between! That<br> Were the thought of Unbelievers!<br> But woe to the Unbelievers<br> Because of the Fire (of Hell)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Am najAAalu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti ka<b>a</b>lmufsideena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i am najAAalu almuttaqeena ka<b>a</b>lfujj<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Shall We treat those<br> Who believe and work deeds<br> Of righteousness, the same<br> As those who do mischief<br> On earth? Shall We treat<br> Those who guard against evil,<br> The same as those who<br> Turn aside from the right?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Kit<u>a</u>bun anzaln<u>a</u>hu ilayka mub<u>a</u>rakun liyaddabbaroo <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi waliyata<u>th</u>akkara oloo al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  (Here is) a Book which<br> We have sent down<br> Unto thee, full of blessings,<br> That they may meditate<br> On its Signs, and that<br> Men of understanding may<br> Receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lid<u>a</u>wooda sulaym<u>a</u>na niAAma alAAabdu innahu aww<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  To David We gave<br> Solomon (for a son),—<br> How excellent in Our service!<br> Ever did he turn (to Us)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  I<u>th</u> AAuri<u>d</u>a AAalayhi bi<b>a</b>lAAashiyyi a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>fin<u>a</u>tu aljiy<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Behold, there were brought<br> Before him, at eventide,<br> Coursers of the highest breeding,<br> And swift of foot;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Faq<u>a</u>la innee a<u>h</u>babtu <u>h</u>ubba alkhayri AAan <u>th</u>ikri rabbee <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> taw<u>a</u>rat bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And he said, "Truly<br> Do I love the love<br> Of Good, with a view<br> To the glory of my Lord,"—<br> Until (the sun) was hidden<br> In the veil (of Night):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Ruddooh<u>a</u> AAalayya fa<u>t</u>afiqa mas<u>h</u>an bi<b>al</b>ssooqi wa<b>a</b>l-aAAn<u>a</u>q<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "Bring them back to me."<br> Then began he to pass<br> His hand over (their) legs<br> And their necks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Walaqad fatann<u>a</u> sulaym<u>a</u>na waalqayn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> kursiyyihi jasadan thumma an<u>a</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And We did try<br> Solomon: We placed<br> On his throne a body<br> (Without life): but he did turn<br> (To Us in true devotion):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ighfir lee wahab lee mulkan l<u>a</u> yanbaghee li-a<u>h</u>adin min baAAdee innaka anta alwahh<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  He said, "O my Lord!<br> Forgive me, and grant me<br>A Kingdom which,<br> (It may be), suits not<br> Another after me:<br> For Thou art the Grantor<br> Of Bounties (without measure).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fasakhkharn<u>a</u> lahu a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>a tajree bi-amrihi rukh<u>a</u>an <u>h</u>aythu a<u>sa</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Then We subjected the Wind<br> To his power, to flow<br> Gently to his order,<br> Whithersoever he willed,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa<b>al</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena kulla bann<u>a</u>-in waghaww<u>as</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  As also the evil ones,<br> (Including) every kind<br> Of builder and diver,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<u>a</u>khareena muqarraneena fee al-a<u>s</u>f<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  As also others bound<br> Together in fetters.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  H<u>atha</u> AAa<u>ta</u>on<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>mnun aw amsik bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "Such are Our Bounties:<br> Whether thou bestow them<br> (On others) or withhold them,<br> No account will be asked."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wa-inna lahu AAindan<u>a</u> lazulf<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>usna ma<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  And he enjoyed, indeed,<br> A Near Approach to Us,<br> And a beautiful Place<br> Of (final) Return.</p> <div class="filenav">
