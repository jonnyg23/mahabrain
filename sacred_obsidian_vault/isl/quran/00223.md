 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 23</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_183.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>183.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo kutiba AAalaykumu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>iy<u>a</u>mu kam<u>a</u> kutiba AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum laAAallakum tattaqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>183.  O ye who believe!<br> Fasting is prescribed to you<br> As it was prescribed<br> To those before you,<br> That ye may (learn)<br> Self-restraint,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_184.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>184.  Ayy<u>a</u>man maAAdood<u>a</u>tin faman k<u>a</u>na minkum maree<u>d</u>an aw AAal<u>a</u> safarin faAAiddatun min ayy<u>a</u>min okhara waAAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>t</u>eeqoonahu fidyatun <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mu miskeenin faman ta<u>t</u>awwaAAa khayran fahuwa khayrun lahu waan ta<u>s</u>oomoo khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>184.  (Fasting) for a fixed<br> Number of days;<br> But if any of you is ill,<br> Or on a journey,<br> The prescribed number<br> (Should be made up)<br> From days later.<br> For those who can do it<br> (With hardship), is a ransom,<br> The feeding of one<br> That is indigent.<br> But he that will give<br> More, of his own free will,—<br> It is better for him.<br> And it is better for you<br> That ye fast,<br> If ye only knew.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_185.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>185.  Shahru rama<u>da</u>na alla<u>th</u>ee onzila feehi alqur-<u>a</u>nu hudan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wabayyin<u>a</u>tin mina alhud<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lfurq<u>a</u>ni faman shahida minkumu a<b>l</b>shshahra falya<u>s</u>umhu waman k<u>a</u>na maree<u>d</u>an aw AAal<u>a</u> safarin faAAiddatun min ayy<u>a</u>min okhara yureedu All<u>a</u>hu bikumu alyusra wal<u>a</u> yureedu bikumu alAAusra walitukmiloo alAAiddata walitukabbiroo All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> had<u>a</u>kum walaAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>185.  Rama<u>dh</u>ān is the (month)<br> In which was sent down<br> The Qur-ān, as a guide<br> To mankind, also clear (Signs)<br> For guidance and judgment<br> (Between right and wrong).<br> So every one of you<br> Who is present (at his home)<br> During that month<br> Should spend it in fasting,<br> But if any one is ill,<br> Or on a journey,<br> The prescribed period<br> (Should be made up)<br> By days later.<br> God intends every facility<br> For you; He does not want<br> To put you to difficulties.<br> (He wants you) to complete<br> The prescribed period,<br> And to glorify Him<br> In that He has guided you;<br> And perchance ye shall be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_186.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>186.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> saalaka AAib<u>a</u>dee AAannee fa-innee qareebun ojeebu daAAwata a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>AAi i<u>tha</u> daAA<u>a</u>ni falyastajeeboo lee walyu/minoo bee laAAallahum yarshudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>186.  When My servants<br> Ask thee concerning Me,<br> I am indeed<br> Close (to them): I listen<br> To the prayer of every<br> Suppliant when he calleth on Me:<br> Let them also, with a will,<br> Listen to My call,<br> And believe in Me:<br> That they may walk<br> In the right way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_187.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>187.  O<u>h</u>illa lakum laylata a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>iy<u>a</u>mi a<b>l</b>rrafathu il<u>a</u> nis<u>a</u>-ikum hunna lib<u>a</u>sun lakum waantum lib<u>a</u>sun lahunna AAalima All<u>a</u>hu annakum kuntum takht<u>a</u>noona anfusakum fat<u>a</u>ba AAalaykum waAAaf<u>a</u> AAankum fa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>na b<u>a</u>shiroohunna wa<b>i</b>btaghoo m<u>a</u> kataba All<u>a</u>hu lakum wakuloo wa<b>i</b>shraboo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yatabayyana lakumu alkhay<u>t</u>u al-abya<u>d</u>u mina alkhay<u>t</u>i al-aswadi mina alfajri thumma atimmoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>iy<u>a</u>ma il<u>a</u> allayli wal<u>a</u> tub<u>a</u>shiroohunna waantum AA<u>a</u>kifoona fee almas<u>a</u>jidi tilka <u>h</u>udoodu All<u>a</u>hi fal<u>a</u> taqrabooh<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si laAAallahum yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>187.  Permitted to you,<br> On the night of the fasts,<br> Is the approach to your wives.<br> They are your garments<br>And ye are their garments.<br> God knoweth what ye<br> Used to do secretly among yourselves;<br> But He turned to you<br> And forgave you;<br> So now associate with them,<br> And seek what God<br> Hath ordained for you,<br> And eat and drink,<br> Until the white thread<br> Of dawn appear to you<br> Distinct from its black thread;<br> Then complete your fast<br> Till the night appears;<br> But do not associate<br> With your wives<br> While ye are in retreat<br> In the mosques. Those are<br> Limits (set by) God:<br> Approach not nigh thereto.<br> Thus doth God make clear<br> His Signs to men: that<br> They may learn self-restraint.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_188.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>188.  Wal<u>a</u> ta/kuloo amw<u>a</u>lakum baynakum bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili watudloo bih<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ukk<u>a</u>mi lita/kuloo fareeqan min amw<u>a</u>li a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi<b>a</b>l-ithmi waantum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>188.  And do not eat up<br> Your property among yourselves<br> For vanities, nor use it<br> As bait for the judges,<br> With intent that ye may<br> Eat up wrongfully and knowingly<br>A little of (other) people's property.</p> <div class="filenav">
