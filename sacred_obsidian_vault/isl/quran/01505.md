 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XV.: Al-Hijr, or The Rocky Tract.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a <u>a</u>la loo<u>t</u>in almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  At length when the messengers<br> Arrived among the adherents<br> Of Lūt,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Q<u>a</u>la innakum qawmun munkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  He said: "Ye appear<br> To be uncommon folk."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>loo bal ji/n<u>a</u>ka bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yamtaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  They said: "Yea,<br> We have come to thee<br> To accomplish that<br> Of which they doubt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Waatayn<u>a</u>ka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-inn<u>a</u> la<u>sa</u>diqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  "We have brought to thee<br> That which is inevitably<br> Due, and assuredly<br> We tell the truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Faasri bi-ahlika biqi<u>t</u>AAin mina allayli wa<b>i</b>ttabiAA adb<u>a</u>rahum wal<u>a</u> yaltafit minkum a<u>h</u>adun wa<b>i</b>m<u>d</u>oo <u>h</u>aythu tu/maroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  "Then travel by night<br> With thy household,<br> When a portion of the night<br> (Yet remains), and do thou<br>Bring up the rear:<br> Let no one amongst you<br> Look back, but pass on<br> Whither ye are ordered."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Waqa<u>d</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayhi <u>tha</u>lika al-amra anna d<u>a</u>bira h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i maq<u>t</u>ooAAun mu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  And We made known<br> This decree to him,<br> That the last remnants<br> Of those (sinners) should be<br> Cut off by the morning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Waj<u>a</u>a ahlu almadeenati yastabshiroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>67.  The inhabitants of the City<br> Came in (mad) joy<br> (At news of the young men).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Q<u>a</u>la inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i <u>d</u>ayfee fal<u>a</u> taf<u>d</u>a<u>h</u>oon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Lūt said: "These are<br> My guests: disgrace me not:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wal<u>a</u> tukhzoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  "But fear God,<br> And shame me not."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Q<u>a</u>loo awa lam nanhaka AAani alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  They said: "Did we not<br> Forbid thee (to speak)<br> For all and sundry?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Q<u>a</u>la h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i ban<u>a</u>tee in kuntum f<u>a</u>AAileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  He said: "There are<br> My daughters (to marry),<br> If ye must act (so)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  LaAAamruka innahum lafee sakratihim yaAAmahoona   </p>
 <p>72.  Verily, by thy life (O Prophet),<br> In their wild intoxication,<br>They wander in distraction,<br> To and fro.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu mushriqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  But the (mighty) Blast<br> Overtook them before morning;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  FajaAAaln<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>liyah<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>filah<u>a</u> waam<u>t</u>arn<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratan min sijjeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  And We turned (the Cities)<br> Upside down, and rained down<br> On them brimstones<br> Hard as baked clay.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin lilmutawassimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Behold! in this are Signs<br> For those who by tokens<br> Do understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wa-innah<u>a</u> labisabeelin muqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  And the (Cities were)<br> Right on the high-road.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Behold! in this<br> Is a Sign<br> For those who believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>na a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al-aykati la<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And the Companions of the Wood<br> Were also wrong-doers;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Fa<b>i</b>ntaqamn<u>a</u> minhum wa-innahum<u>a</u> labi-im<u>a</u>min mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  So We exacted retribution<br> From them. They were both<br> On an open highway,<br> Plain to see.</p> <div class="filenav">
