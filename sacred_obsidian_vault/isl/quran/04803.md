 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVIII.: Fat-ḥ or Victory.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Laqad ra<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu AAani almu/mineena i<u>th</u> yub<u>a</u>yiAAoonaka ta<u>h</u>ta a<b>l</b>shshajarati faAAalima m<u>a</u> fee quloobihim faanzala a<b>l</b>sakeenata AAalayhim waath<u>a</u>bahum fat<u>h</u>an qareeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  God's Good Pleasure<br> Was on the Believers<br> When they swore Fealty<br> To thee under the Tree:<br> He knew what was<br> In their hearts, and He<br> Sent down Tranquillity<br> To them; and He rewarded<br> Them with a speedy Victory;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wamagh<u>a</u>nima katheeratan ya/khu<u>th</u>oonah<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAazeezan <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And many gains will they<br> Acquire (besides): and God<br> Is Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  WaAAadakumu All<u>a</u>hu magh<u>a</u>nima katheeratan ta/khu<u>th</u>oonah<u>a</u> faAAajjala lakum h<u>ath</u>ihi wakaffa aydiya a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si AAankum walitakoona <u>a</u>yatan lilmu/mineena wayahdiyakum <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>an mustaqeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  God has promised you<br> Many gains that ye shall<br> Acquire, and He has given<br> You these beforehand; and<br> He has restrained the hands<br> Of men from you; that it<br> May be a Sign for<br> The Believers, and that<br> He may guide you<br> To a Straight Path;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waokhr<u>a</u> lam taqdiroo AAalayh<u>a</u> qad a<u>hat</u>a All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And other gains (there are),<br> Which are not within<br> Your power, but which<br> God has compassed: and God<br> Has power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Walaw q<u>a</u>talakumu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lawallawoo al-adb<u>a</u>ra thumma l<u>a</u> yajidoona waliyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  If the Unbelievers<br> Should fight you, they would<br> Certainly turn their backs;<br> Then would they find<br> Neither protector nor helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Sunnata All<u>a</u>hi allatee qad khalat min qablu walan tajida lisunnati All<u>a</u>hi tabdeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  (Such has been) the practice<br> (Approved) of God already<br> In the past: no change<br> Wilt thou find in<br> The practice (approved) of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee kaffa aydiyahum AAankum waaydiyakum AAanhum biba<u>t</u>ni makkata min baAAdi an a<i><u>th</u></i>farakum AAalayhim wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And it is He Who<br> Has restrained their hands<br>From you and your hands<br> From them in the midst<br> Of Mecca, after that He<br> Gave you the victory<br> Over them. And God sees<br> Well all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Humu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>s</u>addookum AAani almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa<b>a</b>lhadya maAAkoofan an yablugha ma<u>h</u>illahu walawl<u>a</u> rij<u>a</u>lun mu/minoona wanis<u>a</u>on mu/min<u>a</u>tun lam taAAlamoohum an ta<u>t</u>aoohum fatu<u>s</u>eebakum minhum maAAarratun bighayri AAilmin liyudkhila All<u>a</u>hu fee ra<u>h</u>matihi man yash<u>a</u>o law tazayyaloo laAAa<u>thth</u>abn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo minhum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  They are the ones who<br> Denied revelation and hindered you<br> From the Sacred Mosque<br> And the sacrificial animals,<br> Detained from reaching their<br> Place of sacrifice. Had there<br> Not been believing men<br> And believing women whom<br> Ye did not know that<br> Ye were trampling down<br> And on whose account<br> A crime would have accrued<br> To you without (your) knowledge,<br> (God would have allowed you<br> To force your way, but<br> He held back your hands)<br> That He may admit<br> To His Mercy whom He will.<br> If they had been<br> Apart, We should<br>Certainly have punished<br> The Unbelievers among them<br> With a grievous punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  I<u>th</u> jaAAala alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fee quloobihimu al<u>h</u>amiyyata <u>h</u>amiyyata alj<u>a</u>hiliyyati faanzala All<u>a</u>hu sakeenatahu AAal<u>a</u> rasoolihi waAAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena waalzamahum kalimata a<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>noo a<u>h</u>aqqa bih<u>a</u> waahlah<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  While the Unbelievers<br> Got up in their hearts<br> Heat and cant—the heat<br> And cant of Ignorance,—<br> God sent down His Tranquillity<br> To his Apostle and to<br> The Believers, and made them<br> Stick close to the command<br> Of self-restraint; and well<br> Were they entitled to it<br> And worthy of it.<br> And God has full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
