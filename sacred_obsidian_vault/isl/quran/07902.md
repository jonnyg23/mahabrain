 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sara LXXIX.: Nāzi’āt, or Those Who Tear Out.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Aantum ashaddu khalqan ami a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o ban<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>27.  What! Are ye the more<br> Difficult to create<br> Or the heaven (above)?<br> (God) hath constructed it:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  RafaAAa samkah<u>a</u> fasaww<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>28.  On high hath He raised<br> Its canopy, and He hath<br> Given it order and perfection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Waagh<u>t</u>asha laylah<u>a</u> waakhraja <u>d</u>u<u>ha</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>29.  Its night doth He<br> Endow with darkness,<br> And its splendour doth He<br> Bring out (with light).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda <u>tha</u>lika da<u>ha</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>30.  And the earth, moreover,<br> Hath He extended<br> (To a wide expanse);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Akhraja minh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>ah<u>a</u> wamarAA<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>31.  He draweth out<br> Therefrom its moisture<br> And its pasture;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>la ars<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>32.  And the mountains<br> Hath He firmly fixed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Mat<u>a</u>AAan lakum wali-anAA<u>a</u>mikum   </p>
 <p>33.  For use and convenience<br> To you and your cattle.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ati a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>mmatu alkubr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>34.  Therefore, when there comes<br> The great, overwhelming (Event),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Yawma yata<u>th</u>akkaru al-ins<u>a</u>nu m<u>a</u> saAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>35.  The Day when Man<br> Shall remember (all)<br> That he strove for,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Waburrizati alja<u>h</u>eemu liman yar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>36.  And Hell-Fire shall be<br> Placed in full view<br> For (all) to see,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Faamm<u>a</u> man <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>37.  Then, for such as had<br> Transgressed all bounds,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<u>a</u>thara al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>38.  And had preferred<br> The life of this world,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fa-inna alja<u>h</u>eema hiya alma/w<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>39.  The Abode will be<br> Hell-Fire;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Waam<u>a</u> man kh<u>a</u>fa maq<u>a</u>ma rabbihi wanah<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnafsa AAani alhaw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>40.  And for such as had<br> Entertained the fear<br> Of standing before<br> Their Lord's (tribunal)<br> And had restrained<br> (Their) soul from lower Desires,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Fa-inna aljannata hiya alma/w<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>41.  Their Abode will be<br> The Garden.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Yas-aloonaka AAani a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati ayy<u>a</u>na murs<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>42.  They ask thee<br> About the Hour,—"When<br> Will be its appointed time?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Feema anta min <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>43.  Wherein art thou (concerned)<br> With the declaration thereof?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Il<u>a</u> rabbika muntah<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>44.  With thy Lord is<br> The Limit fixed therefor.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Innam<u>a</u> anta mun<u>th</u>iru man yakhsh<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>45.  Thou art but a Warner<br> For such as fear it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Kaannahum yawma yarawnah<u>a</u> lam yalbathoo ill<u>a</u> AAashiyyatan aw <u>d</u>u<u>ha</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>46.  The Day they see it,<br> (It will be) as if they<br> Had tarried but a single<br> Evening, or (at most till)<br> The following morn!</p>  <div class="filenav">
