 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>stakbaroo AAanh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tufatta<u>h</u>u lahum abw<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wal<u>a</u> yadkhuloona aljannata <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yalija aljamalu fee sammi alkhiy<u>at</u>i waka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  To those who reject<br> Our Signs and treat them<br> With arrogance, no opening<br> Will there be of the gates<br>Of heaven, nor will they<br> Enter the Garden, until<br> The camel can pass<br> Through the eye of the needle:<br> Such is Our reward<br> For those in sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Lahum min jahannama mih<u>a</u>dun wamin fawqihim ghaw<u>a</u>shin waka<u>tha</u>lika najzee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>41.  For them there is<br> Hell, as a couch<br> (Below) and folds and folds<br> Of covering above: such<br> Is Our requital of those<br> Who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti l<u>a</u> nukallifu nafsan ill<u>a</u> wusAAah<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  But those who believe<br> And work righteousness,—<br> No burden do We place<br> On any soul, but that<br> Which it can bear,<br> They will be Companions<br> Of the Garden, therein<br> To dwell (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  WanazaAAn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>udoorihim min ghillin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tihimu al-anh<u>a</u>ru waq<u>a</u>loo al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee had<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> lih<u>atha</u> wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> linahtadiya lawl<u>a</u> an had<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu laqad j<u>a</u>at rusulu rabbin<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wanoodoo an tilkumu aljannatu oorithtumooh<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And We shall remove<br> From their hearts any<br> Lurking sense of injury;—<br> Beneath them will be<br> Rivers flowing;—and they<br> Shall say: "Praise be, to God,<br> Who hath guided us<br> To this (felicity): never<br> Could we have found<br> Guidance, had it not been<br> For the guidance of God:<br> Indeed it was the truth.<br> That the Apostles of our Lord<br> Brought unto us." And they<br> Shall hear the cry:<br> "Behold! the Garden before you!<br>Ye have been made<br> Its inheritors, for your<br> Deeds (of righteousness)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri an qad wajadn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> waAAadan<u>a</u> rabbun<u>a</u> <u>h</u>aqqan fahal wajadtum m<u>a</u> waAAada rabbukum <u>h</u>aqqan q<u>a</u>loo naAAam faa<u>thth</u>ana mu-a<u>thth</u>inun baynahum an laAAnatu All<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  The Companions of the Garden<br> Will call out to the Companions<br> Of the Fire: "We have<br> Indeed found the promises<br> Of our Lord to us true:<br> Have you also found<br> Your Lord's promises true?"<br> They shall say, "Yes"; but<br> A Crier shall proclaim<br> Between them: "The curse<br> Of God is on the wrong-doers;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wayabghoonah<u>a</u> AAiwajan wahum bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  "Those who would hinder (men)<br> From the path of God<br> And would seek in it<br> Something crooked:<br> They were those who<br> Denied the Hereafter."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wabaynahum<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>bun waAAal<u>a</u> al-aAAr<u>a</u>fi rij<u>a</u>lun yaAArifoona kullan biseem<u>a</u>hum wan<u>a</u>daw a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba aljannati an sal<u>a</u>mun AAalaykum lam yadkhulooh<u>a</u> wahum ya<u>t</u>maAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Between them shall be<br> A veil, and on the Heights<br>Will be men<br> Who would know every one<br> By his marks: they will call<br> Out to the Companions<br> Of the Garden, "Peace on you":<br> They will not have entered,<br> But they will have<br> An assurance (thereof.)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>s</u>urifat ab<u>sa</u>ruhum tilq<u>a</u>a a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tajAAaln<u>a</u> maAAa alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  When their eyes shall be turned<br> Towards the Companions<br> Of the Fire, they will say:<br> "Our Lord! send us not<br> To the company<br> Of the wrong-doers."</p> <div class="filenav">
