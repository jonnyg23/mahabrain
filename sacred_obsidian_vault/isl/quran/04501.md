 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLV.: Ja<u>th</u>iya, or Bowing the Knee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ha</u>-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Hā-Mīm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tanzeelu alkit<u>a</u>bi mina All<u>a</u>hi alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The revelation<br> Of the Book<br> Is from God<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Inna fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Verily in the heavens<br> And the earth, are Signs<br> For those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wafee khalqikum wam<u>a</u> yabuththu min d<u>a</u>bbatin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun liqawmin yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And in the creation<br> Of yourselves and the fact<br> That animals are scattered<br> (Through the earth), are Signs<br> For those of assured Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa<b>i</b>khtil<u>a</u>fi allayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wam<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i min rizqin faa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> wata<u>s</u>reefi a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>i <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun liqawmin yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And in the alternation<br> Of Night and Day,<br> And the fact that God<br> Sends down Sustenance from<br> The sky, and revives therewith<br> The earth after its death,<br>And in the change<br> Of the winds,—are Signs<br> For those that are wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu All<u>a</u>hi natlooh<u>a</u> AAalayka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi fabi-ayyi <u>h</u>adeethin baAAda All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Such are the Signs<br> Of God, which We rehearse to thee<br> In truth: then in what<br> Exposition will they believe<br> After (rejecting) God<br> And His Signs?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waylun likulli aff<u>a</u>kin atheem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Woe to each sinful<br> Dealer in Falsehoods:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  YasmaAAu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhi thumma yu<u>s</u>irru mustakbiran kaan lam yasmaAAh<u>a</u> fabashshirhu biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  He hears the Signs<br> Of God rehearsed to him,<br> Yet is obstinate and lofty,<br> As if he had not<br> Heard them: then announce<br> To him a Penalty Grievous!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> AAalima min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> shay-an ittakha<u>th</u>ah<u>a</u> huzuwan ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And when he learns<br> Something of Our Signs,<br> He takes them in jest:<br> For such there will be<br> A humiliating Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Min war<u>a</u>-ihim jahannamu wal<u>a</u> yughnee AAanhum m<u>a</u> kasaboo shay-an wal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ittakha<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi awliy<u>a</u>a walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  In front of them is<br> Hell: and of no profit<br> To them is anything<br> They may have earned,<br> Nor any protectors they<br> May have taken to themselves<br> Besides God: for them<br> Is a tremendous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  H<u>atha</u> hudan wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun min rijzin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  This is (true) Guidance:<br> And for those who reject<br> The Signs of their Lord,<br> Is a grievous Penalty<br> Of abomination.</p> <div class="filenav">
