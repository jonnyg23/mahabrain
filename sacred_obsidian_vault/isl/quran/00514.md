 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 14</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tas-aloo AAan ashy<u>a</u>a in tubda lakum tasu/kum wa-in tas-aloo AAanh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>eena yunazzalu alqur-<u>a</u>nu tubda lakum AAaf<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAanh<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun <u>h</u>aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">104</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Ask not questions<br> About things which,<br> If made plain to you,<br> May cause you trouble.<br> But if ye ask about things<br> When the Qur-ān is being<br> Revealed, they will be<br> Made plain to you,<br> God will forgive those:<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Forbearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Qad saalah<u>a</u> qawmun min qablikum thumma a<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo bih<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">105</font></span> Some people before you<br> Did ask such questions,<br>And on that account<br> Lost their faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  M<u>a</u> jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu min ba<u>h</u>eeratin wal<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>-ibatin wal<u>a</u> wa<u>s</u>eelatin wal<u>a</u> <u>ha</u>min wal<u>a</u>kinna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yaftaroona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba waaktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">106</font></span> It was not God<br> Who instituted (superstitions<br> Like those of) a slit-ear<br> She-camel, or a she-camel<br> Let loose for free pasture,<br> Or idol sacrifices for<br> Twin-births in animals,<br> Or stallion-camels<br> Freed from work<br> It is blasphemers<br> Who Invent a lie<br> Against God; but most<br> Of them lack wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum taAA<u>a</u>law il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu wa-il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli q<u>a</u>loo <u>h</u>asbun<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> awa law k<u>a</u>na <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ohum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona shay-an wal<u>a</u> yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">107</font></span> When it is said to them:<br> "Come to what God<br> Hath revealed; come<br> To the Apostle":<br> They say: "Enough for us<br> Are the ways we found<br> Our fathers following."<br> What! even though their fathers<br> Were void of knowledge<br> And guidance?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo AAalaykum anfusakum l<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urrukum man <u>d</u>alla i<u>th</u>a ihtadaytum il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi marjiAAukum jameeAAan fayunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">108</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Guard your own souls:<br> If ye follow (right) guidance,<br> No hurt can come to you<br> From those who stray.<br> The goal of you all<br> Is to God: it is He<br> That will show you<br> The truth of all<br> That ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo shah<u>a</u>datu baynikum i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>a<u>d</u>ara a<u>h</u>adakumu almawtu <u>h</u>eena alwa<u>s</u>iyyati ithn<u>a</u>ni <u>th</u>aw<u>a</u> AAadlin minkum aw <u>a</u>khar<u>a</u>ni min ghayrikum in antum <u>d</u>arabtum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i faa<u>sa</u>batkum mu<u>s</u>eebatu almawti ta<u>h</u>bisoonahum<u>a</u> min baAAdi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti fayuqsim<u>a</u>ni bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi ini irtabtum l<u>a</u> nashtaree bihi thamanan walaw k<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u> qurb<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> naktumu shah<u>a</u>data All<u>a</u>hi inn<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an lamina al-<u>a</u>thimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">109</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> When death approaches<br> Any of you, (take) witnesses<br> Among yourselves when making<br> Bequests,—two just men<br> Of your own (brotherhood)<br> Or others from outside<br> If ye are journeying<br> Through the earth,<br> And the chance of death<br> Befalls you (thus).<br> If ye doubt (their truth),<br> Detain them both<br> After prayer, and let them both<br> Swear by God:<br> "We wish not in this<br> For any worldly gain,<br> Even though the (beneficiary)<br> Be our near relation:<br> We shall hide not<br> The evidence before God:<br> If we do, then behold!<br> The sin be upon us!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Fa-in AAuthira AAal<u>a</u> annahum<u>a</u> ista<u>h</u>aqq<u>a</u> ithman fa<u>a</u>khara<u>n</u>i yaqoom<u>a</u>ni maq<u>a</u>mahum<u>a</u> mina alla<u>th</u>eena ista<u>h</u>aqqa AAalayhimu al-awlay<u>a</u>ni fayuqsim<u>a</u>ni bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi lashah<u>a</u>datun<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>aqqu min shah<u>a</u>datihim<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> iAAtadayn<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an lamina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">110</font></span> But if it gets known<br> That these two were guilty<br> Of the sin (of perjury),<br> Let two others stand forth<br> In their places,—nearest<br> In kin from among those<br> Who claim a lawful right:<br> Let them swear by God:<br> "We affirm that our witness<br> Is truer than that<br> Of those two, and that we<br> Have not trespassed (beyond<br> The truth): if we did,<br> Behold! the wrong be<br> Upon us!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  <u>Tha</u>lika adn<u>a</u> an ya/too bi<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati AAal<u>a</u> wajhih<u>a</u> aw yakh<u>a</u>foo an turadda aym<u>a</u>nun baAAda aym<u>a</u>nihim wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>smaAAoo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">111</font></span> That is most suitable:<br> That they may give the evidence<br>In its true nature and shape,<br> Or else they would fear<br> That other oaths would be<br> Taken after their oaths.<br> Hut fear God, and listen<br> (To His counsel): for God<br> Guideth not a rebellious people:</p> <div class="filenav">
