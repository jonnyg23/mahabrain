 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wal<u>a</u> taqoolanna lishay-in innee f<u>a</u>AAilun <u>tha</u>lika ghad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Nor say of anything,<br> "I shall be sure to do<br> So and so to-morrow"—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur rabbaka i<u>tha</u> naseeta waqul AAas<u>a</u> an yahdiyani rabbee li-aqraba min h<u>atha</u> rashad<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>24.  Without adding, "So please God!"<br> And call thy Lord to mind<br> When thou forgettest, and say,<br> "I hope that my Lord<br> Will guide me ever closer<br> (Even) than this<br> To the right road."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Walabithoo fee kahfihim thal<u>a</u>tha mi-atin sineena wa<b>i</b>zd<u>a</u>doo tisAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  So they stayed in their Cave<br> Three hundred years, and (some)<br> Add nine (more)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Quli All<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> labithoo lahu ghaybu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i ab<u>s</u>ir bihi waasmiAA m<u>a</u> lahum min doonihi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> yushriku fee <u>h</u>ukmihi a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Say: "God knows best<br> How long they stayed:<br> With Him is (the knowledge<br> Of) the secrets of the heavens<br> And the earth: how clearly<br> He sees, how finely He hears<br> (Everything)! They have no protector<br> Other than Him; nor does<br> He share His Command<br> With any person whatsoever.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa<b>o</b>tlu m<u>a</u> oo<u>h</u>iya ilayka min kit<u>a</u>bi rabbika l<u>a</u> mubaddila likalim<u>a</u>tihi walan tajida min doonihi multa<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And recite (and teach)<br> What has been revealed<br> To thee of the Book<br> Of thy Lord: none<br> Can change His Words,<br> And none wilt thou find<br> As a refuge other than Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir nafsaka maAAa alla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona rabbahum bi<b>a</b>lghad<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lAAashiyyi yureedoona wajhahu wal<u>a</u> taAAdu AAayn<u>a</u>ka AAanhum tureedu zeenata al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAA man aghfaln<u>a</u> qalbahu AAan <u>th</u>ikrin<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAa haw<u>a</u>hu wak<u>a</u>na amruhu furu<u>ta</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And keep thy soul content<br> With those who call<br> On their Lord morning<br> And evening, seeking<br> His Face; and let not<br> Thine eyes pass beyond them,<br> Seeking the pomp and glitter<br> Of this Life; nor obey<br> Any whose heart We<br> Have permitted to neglect<br> The remembrance of Us,<br> One who follows his own<br>Desires, whose case has<br> Gone beyond all bounds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Waquli al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbikum faman sh<u>a</u>a falyu/min waman sh<u>a</u>a falyakfur inn<u>a</u> aAAtadn<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena n<u>a</u>ran a<u>hat</u>a bihim sur<u>a</u>diquh<u>a</u> wa-in yastagheethoo yugh<u>a</u>thoo bim<u>a</u>-in ka<b>a</b>lmuhli yashwee alwujooha bi/sa a<b>l</b>shshar<u>a</u>bu was<u>a</u>at murtafaq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Say, "The Truth is<br> From your Lord":<br> Let him who will,<br> Believe, and let him<br> Who will, reject (it):<br> For the wrong-doers We<br> Have prepared a Fire<br> Whose (smoke and flames),<br> Like the walls and roof<br> Of a tent, will hem<br> Them in: if they implore<br> Relief they will be granted<br> Water like melted brass,<br> That will scald their faces.<br> How dreadful the drink!<br> How uncomfortable a couch<br> To recline on!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti inn<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra man a<u>h</u>sana AAamal<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  As to those who believe<br> And work righteousness,<br> Verily We shall not suffer<br> To perish the reward<br> Of any who do<br> A (single) righteous deed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum jann<u>a</u>tu AAadnin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tihimu al-anh<u>a</u>ru yu<u>h</u>allawna feeh<u>a</u> min as<u>a</u>wira min <u>th</u>ahabin way<b>a</b>lbasoona thiy<u>a</u>ban khu<u>d</u>ran min sundusin wa-istabraqin muttaki-eena feeh<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>a</u>-iki niAAma a<b>l</b>ththaw<u>a</u>bu wa<u>h</u>asunat murtafaq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  For them will be Gardens<br> Of Eternity; beneath them<br> Rivers will flow: they will<br> Be adorned therein<br> With bracelets of gold,<br> And they will wear<br>Green garments of fine silk<br> And heavy brocade;<br> They will recline therein<br> On raised thrones.<br> How good the recompense!<br> How beautiful a couch<br> To recline on!</p> <div class="filenav">
