 <hr>
 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVII.: Ṣāffāt, or Those Ranged in Ranks.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Wa-inna yoonusa lamina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  So also was Jonah<br> Among those sent (by Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  I<u>th</u> abaqa il<u>a</u> alfulki almash<u>h</u>oon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  When he ran away<br> (Like a slave from captivity)<br> To the ship (fully) laden,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Fas<u>a</u>hama fak<u>a</u>na mina almud<u>h</u>a<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>141.  He (agreed to) cast lots,<br> And he was condemned:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  Fa<b>i</b>ltaqamahu al<u>h</u>ootu wahuwa muleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  Then the big Fish<br> Did swallow him,<br> And he had done<br> Acts worthy of blame.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Falawl<u>a</u> annahu k<u>a</u>na mina almusabbi<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  Had it not been<br> That he (repented and)<br> Glorified God,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Lalabitha fee ba<u>t</u>nihi il<u>a</u> yawmi yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  He would certainly have<br> Remained inside the Fish<br> Till the Day of Resurrection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Fanaba<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hu bi<b>a</b>lAAar<u>a</u>-i wahuwa saqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  But We cast him forth<br> On the naked shore<br> In a state of sickness,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  Waanbatn<u>a</u> AAalayhi shajaratan min yaq<u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>146.  And We caused to grow,<br> Over him, a spreading plant<br> Of the Gourd kind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  Waarsaln<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> mi-ati alfin aw yazeedoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  And We sent him<br> (On a mission)<br> To a hundred thousand<br> (Men) or more.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  Fa<u>a</u>manoo famattaAAn<u>a</u>hum il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  And they believed;<br> So We permitted them<br> To enjoy (their life)<br> For a while.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  Fa<b>i</b>staftihim alirabbika alban<u>a</u>tu walahumu albanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  Now ask them their opinion:<br> Is it that thy Lord<br> Has (only) daughters, and they<br> Have sons?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Am khalaqn<u>a</u> almal<u>a</u>-ikata in<u>a</u>than wahum sh<u>a</u>hidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  Or that We created<br> The angels female, and they<br> Are witnesses (thereto)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_151.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>151.  Al<u>a</u> innahum min ifkihim layaqooloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>151.  Is it not that they<br> Say, from their own invention,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_152.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>152.  Walada All<u>a</u>hu wa-innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>152.  "God has begotten children"?<br> But they are liars!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_153.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>153.  A<u>st</u>af<u>a</u> alban<u>a</u>ti AAal<u>a</u> albaneen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>153.  Did He (then) choose<br> Daughters rather than sons?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_154.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>154.  M<u>a</u> lakum kayfa ta<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>154.  What is the matter<br> With you? How judge ye?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Afal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  Will ye not then<br> Receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  Am lakum sul<u>ta</u>nun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>156.  Or have ye<br> An authority manifest?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  Fa/too bikit<u>a</u>bikum in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  Then bring ye your Book<br> (Of authority) if ye be<br> Truthful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  WajaAAaloo baynahu wabayna aljinnati nasaban walaqad AAalimati aljinnatu innahum lamu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  And they have invented<br> A blood-relationship<br> Between Him and the Jinns:<br> But the Jinns know<br> (Quite well) that they<br> Have indeed to appear<br> (Before His Judgment-seat)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Sub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  Glory to God! (He is free)<br> From the things they ascribe<br> (To Him)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  Not (so do) the Servants<br> Of God, sincere and devoted.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  Fa-innakum wam<u>a</u> taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  For, verily, neither ye<br> Nor those ye worship—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  M<u>a</u> antum AAalayhi bif<u>a</u>tineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  Can lead (any)<br> Into temptation<br> Concerning God,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  Ill<u>a</u> man huwa <u>sa</u>li alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  Except such as are<br> (Themselves) going to<br> The blazing Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Wam<u>a</u> minn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> lahu maq<u>a</u>mun maAAloom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  (Those ranged in ranks say):<br> "Not one of us but has<br> A place appointed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> lana<u>h</u>nu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>ffoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  "And we are verily<br> Ranged in ranks (for service);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_166.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>166.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> lana<u>h</u>nu almusabbi<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>166.  "And we are verily those<br> Who declare (God's) glory!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_167.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>167.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>noo layaqooloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>167.  And there were those<br> Who said,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_168.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>168.  Law anna AAindan<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikran mina al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>168.  "If only we had had<br> Before us a Message<br> From those of old,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_169.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>169.  Lakunn<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>169.  "We should certainly have<br> Been Servants of God,<br> Sincere (and devoted)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_170.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>170.  Fakafaroo bihi fasawfa yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>170.  But (now that the Qur-ān<br> Has come), they reject it:<br> But soon will they know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_171.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>171.  Walaqad sabaqat kalimatun<u>a</u> liAAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>171.  Already has Our Word<br> Been passed before (this)<br> To Our Servants sent (by Us),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_172.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>172.  Innahum lahumu alman<u>s</u>ooroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>172.  That they would certainly<br> Be assisted,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_173.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>173.  Wa-inna jundan<u>a</u> lahumu algh<u>a</u>liboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>173.  And that Our forces,—<br> They surely must conquer.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_174.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>174.  Fatawalla AAanhum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>174.  So turn thou away<br> From them for a little while,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_175.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>175.  Waab<u>s</u>irhum fasawfa yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>175.  And watch them (how<br> They fare), and they soon<br> Shall see (how thou farest)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_176.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>176.  AfabiAAa<u>tha</u>bin<u>a</u> yastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>176.  Do they wish (indeed)<br> To hurry on our Punishment?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_177.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>177.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> nazala bis<u>ah</u>atihim fas<u>a</u>a <u>s</u>ab<u>ah</u>u almun<u>th</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>177.  But when it descends<br> Into the open space<br> Before them, evil will be<br> The morning for those who<br> Were warned (and heeded not)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_178.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>178.  Watawalla AAanhum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>178.  So turn thou away<br> From them for a little while,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_179.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>179.  Waab<u>s</u>ir fasawfa yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>179.  And watch (how they fare)<br> And they soon shall see<br> (How thou farest)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_180.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>180.  Sub<u>ha</u>na rabbika rabbi alAAizzati AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>180.  Glory to thy Lord,<br> The Lord of Honour<br> And Power! (He is free)<br> From what they ascribe<br> (To Him)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_181.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>181.  Wasal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>181.  And Peace on the apostles!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_182.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>182.  Wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>182.  And Praise to God,<br> The Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Worlds.</p>  <div class="filenav">
