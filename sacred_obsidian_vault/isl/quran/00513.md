 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo layabluwannakumu All<u>a</u>hu bishay-in mina a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>aydi tan<u>a</u>luhu aydeekum warim<u>ah</u>ukum liyaAAlama All<u>a</u>hu man yakh<u>a</u>fuhu bi<b>a</b>lghaybi famani iAAtad<u>a</u> baAAda <u>tha</u>lika falahu AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">97</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> God doth but make a trial of you<br> In a little matter<br> Of game well within reach<br> Of your hands and your lances,<br> That He may test<br> Who feareth Him unseen:<br> Any who transgress<br> Thereafter, will have<br> A grievous penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> taqtuloo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ayda waantum <u>h</u>urumun waman qatalahu minkum mutaAAammidan fajaz<u>a</u>on mithlu m<u>a</u> qatala mina a<b>l</b>nnaAAami ya<u>h</u>kumu bihi <u>th</u>aw<u>a</u> AAadlin minkum hadyan b<u>a</u>ligha alkaAAbati aw kaff<u>a</u>ratun <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mu mas<u>a</u>keena aw AAadlu <u>tha</u>lika <u>s</u>iy<u>a</u>man liya<u>th</u>ooqa wab<u>a</u>la amrihi AAaf<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAamm<u>a</u> salafa waman AA<u>a</u>da fayantaqimu All<u>a</u>hu minhu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>th</u>oo intiq<u>a</u>m<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">98</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Kill not game<br> While in the Sacred<br> Precincts or in pilgrim garb.<br> If any of you doth so<br> Intentionally, the compensation<br> Is an offering, brought<br> To the Ka’ba, of a domestic animal<br> Equivalent to the one he killed,<br>As adjudged by two just men<br> Among you; or by way<br> Of atonement, the feeding<br> Of the indigent; or its<br> Equivalent in fasts: that he<br> May taste of the penalty<br> Of his deed. God<br> Forgives what is past:<br> For repetition God will<br> Exact from him the penalty.<br> For God is Exalted,<br> And Lord of Retribution.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  O<u>h</u>illa lakum <u>s</u>aydu alba<u>h</u>ri wa<u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>muhu mat<u>a</u>AAan lakum wali<b>l</b>ssayy<u>a</u>rati wa<u>h</u>urrima AAalaykum <u>s</u>aydu albarri m<u>a</u> dumtum <u>h</u>uruman wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>96.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">99</font></span> Lawful to you is the pursuit<br> Of water-game and its use<br> For food,—for the benefit<br> Of yourselves and those who<br> Travel; but forbidden<br> Is the pursuit of land-game;—<br> As long as ye are<br> In the Sacred Precincts<br> Or in pilgrim garb.<br> And fear God, to Whom<br> Ye shall be gathered back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  JaAAala All<u>a</u>hu alkaAAbata albayta al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>ma qiy<u>a</u>man li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>al</b>shshahra al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>ma wa<b>a</b>lhadya wa<b>a</b>lqal<u>a</u>-ida <u>tha</u>lika litaAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i waanna All<u>a</u>ha bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">100</font></span> God made the Ka’ba,<br> The Sacred House, an asylum<br> Of security for men, as<br> Also the Sacred Months,<br> The animals for offerings,<br> And the garlands that mark them:<br> That ye may know<br> That God hath knowledge<br> Of what is in the heavens<br> And on earth and that God<br> Is well acquainted<br> With all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  IAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>bi waanna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">101</font></span> Know ye that God<br> Is strict in punishment<br>And that God is<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  M<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli ill<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tubdoona wam<u>a</u> taktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">102</font></span> The Apostle's duty is<br> But to proclaim (the Message).<br> But God knoweth all<br> That ye reveal and ye conceal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Qul l<u>a</u> yastawee alkhabeethu wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayyibu walaw aAAjabaka kathratu alkhabeethi fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha y<u>a</u> olee al-alb<u>a</u>bi laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">103</font></span> Say: "Not equal are things<br> That are bad and things<br> That are good, even though<br> The abundance of the bad<br> May dazzle thee;<br> So fear God, O ye<br> That understand;<br> That (so) ye may prosper."</p> <div class="filenav">
