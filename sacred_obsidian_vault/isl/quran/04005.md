 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>ee <u>a</u>mana y<u>a</u> qawmi ittabiAAooni ahdikum sabeela a<b>l</b>rrash<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  The man who believed said<br> Further: "O my People!<br> Follow me: I will lead<br> You to the Path of Right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Y<u>a</u> qawmi innam<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAun wa-inna al-<u>a</u>khirata hiya d<u>a</u>ru alqar<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "O my People! This life<br> Of the present is nothing<br> But (temporary) convenience:<br> It is the Hereafter<br>That is the Home<br> That will last.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Man AAamila sayyi-atan fal<u>a</u> yujz<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> mithlah<u>a</u> waman AAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an min <u>th</u>akarin aw onth<u>a</u> wahuwa mu/minun faol<u>a</u>-ika yadkhuloona aljannata yurzaqoona feeh<u>a</u> bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  "He that works evil<br> Will not be requited<br> But by the like thereof:<br> And he that works<br> A righteous deed—whether<br> Man or woman—and is<br> A Believer—such will enter<br> The Garden (of Bliss): therein<br> Will they have abundance<br> Without measure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi m<u>a</u>lee adAAookum il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnaj<u>a</u>ti watadAAoonanee il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  "And O my People!<br> How (strange) it is<br> For me to call you<br> To Salvation while ye<br> Call me to the Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  TadAAoonanee li-akfura bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi waoshrika bihi m<u>a</u> laysa lee bihi AAilmun waan<u>a</u> adAAookum il<u>a</u> alAAazeezi alghaff<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  "Ye do call upon me<br> To blaspheme against God,<br> And to join with Him<br> Partners of whom I have<br> No knowledge; and I<br> Call you to the Exalted<br> In Power, Who forgives<br> Again and again!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  L<u>a</u> jarama annam<u>a</u> tadAAoonanee ilayhi laysa lahu daAAwatun fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> fee al-<u>a</u>khirati waanna maraddan<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi waanna almusrifeena hum a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  "Without doubt ye do call<br> Me to one who is not<br> Fit to be called to,<br> Whether in this world,<br>Or in the Hereafter;<br> Our Return will be<br> To God; and the Transgressors<br> Will be Companions<br> Of the Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Fasata<u>th</u>kuroona m<u>a</u> aqoolu lakum waofawwi<u>d</u>u amree il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha ba<u>s</u>eerun bi<b>a</b>lAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  "Soon will ye remember<br> What I say to you (now).<br> My (own) affair I commit<br> To God: for God (ever)<br> Watches over His Servants."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Fawaq<u>a</u>hu All<u>a</u>hu sayyi-<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> makaroo wa<u>ha</u>qa bi-<u>a</u>li firAAawna soo-o alAAa<u>tha</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Then God saved him<br> From (every) ill that they<br> Plotted (against him),<br> But the brunt of the Penalty<br> Encompassed on all sides<br> The People of Pharaoh.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  A<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru yuAAra<u>d</u>oona AAalayh<u>a</u> ghuduwwan waAAashiyyan wayawma taqoomu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu adkhiloo <u>a</u>la firAAawna ashadda alAAa<u>tha</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  In front of the Fire<br> Will they be brought,<br> Morning and evening:<br> And (the Sentence will be)<br> On the Day that<br> Judgment will be established:<br> "Cast ye the People<br> Of Pharaoh into<br> The severest Penalty!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wa-i<u>th</u> yata<u>ha</u>jjoona fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri fayaqoolu a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>uAAaf<u>a</u>o lilla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> lakum tabaAAan fahal antum mughnoona AAann<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eeban mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Behold, they will dispute<br> With each other in the Fire!<br> The weak ones (who followed)<br> Will say to those who<br> Had been arrogant, "We but<br> Followed you: can ye then<br>Take (on yourselves) from us<br> Some share of the Fire?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo inn<u>a</u> kullun feeh<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha qad <u>h</u>akama bayna alAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Those who had been arrogant<br> Will say: "We are all<br> In this (Fire)! Truly,<br> God has judged<br> Between (His) Servants!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri likhazanati jahannama odAAoo rabbakum yukhaffif AAann<u>a</u> yawman mina alAAa<u>tha</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Those in the Fire will say<br> To the Keepers of Hell:<br> "Pray to your Lord<br> To lighten us the Penalty<br> For a Day (at least)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Q<u>a</u>loo awa lam taku ta/teekum rusulukum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo fa<b>o</b>dAAoo wam<u>a</u> duAA<u>a</u>o alk<u>a</u>fireena ill<u>a</u> fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>l<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  They will say: "Did there<br> Not come to you<br> Your apostles with Clear Signs?"<br> They will say, "Yes".<br> They will reply, "Then<br> Pray (as ye like)! But<br> The Prayer of those<br> Without Faith is nothing<br> But (futile wandering)<br> In (mazes of) error!"</p> <div class="filenav">
