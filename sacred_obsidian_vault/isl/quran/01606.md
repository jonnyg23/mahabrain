 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>jaroo fee All<u>a</u>hi min baAAdi m<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ulimoo lanubawwi-annahum fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asanatan walaajru al-<u>a</u>khirati akbaru law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  To those who leave<br> Their homes in the cause<br> Of God, after suffering<br> oppression,—<br> We will assuredly give<br> A goodly home in this world;<br> But truly the reward<br> Of the Hereafter will be greater.<br> If they only realised (this)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>abaroo waAAal<u>a</u> rabbihim yatawakkaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>42.  (They are) those who persevere<br> In patience, and put<br> Their trust on their Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika ill<u>a</u> rij<u>a</u>lan noo<u>h</u>ee ilayhim fa<b>i</b>s-aloo ahla a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri in kuntum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And before thee also<br> The apostles We sent<br> Were but men, to whom<br> We granted inspiration: if ye<br> Realise this not, ask of those<br> Who possess the Message.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b>zzuburi waanzaln<u>a</u> ilayka a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikra litubayyina li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si m<u>a</u> nuzzila ilayhim walaAAallahum yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  (We sent them) with Clear Signs<br> And Books of dark prophecies;<br> And We have sent down<br> Unto thee (also) the Message;<br> That thou mayest explain clearly<br> To men what is sent<br> For them, and that they<br> May give thought.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Afaamina alla<u>th</u>eena makaroo a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti an yakhsifa All<u>a</u>hu bihimu al-ar<u>d</u>a aw ya/tiyahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu min <u>h</u>aythu l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Do then those who devise<br> Evil (plots) feel secure<br> That God will not cause<br> the earth to swallow them up,<br> Or that the Wrath will not<br> Seize them from directions<br> They little perceive?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Aw ya/khu<u>th</u>ahum fee taqallubihim fam<u>a</u> hum bimuAAjizeen<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Or that He may not<br> Call them to account<br>In the midst of their goings<br> To and fro, without a chance<br> Of their frustrating Him?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Aw ya/khu<u>th</u>ahum AAal<u>a</u> takhawwufin fa-inna rabbakum laraoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Or that He may not<br> Call them to account<br> By a process of slow wastage—<br> For thy Lord is indeed<br> Full of kindness and mercy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Awa lam yaraw il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> khalaqa All<u>a</u>hu min shay-in yatafayyao <i><u>th</u></i>il<u>a</u>luhu AAani alyameeni wa<b>al</b>shsham<u>a</u>-ili sujjadan lill<u>a</u>hi wahum d<u>a</u>khiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Do they not look<br> At God's creation, (even)<br> Among (inanimate) things,—<br> How their (very) shadows<br> Turn round, from the right<br> And the left, prostrating<br> Themselves to God, and that<br> In the humblest manner?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Walill<u>a</u>hi yasjudu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i min d<u>a</u>bbatin wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu wahum l<u>a</u> yastakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And to God doth obeisance<br> All that is in the heavens<br> And on earth, whether<br> Moving (living) creatures<br> Or the angels: for none<br> Are arrogant (before their Lord).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Yakh<u>a</u>foona rabbahum min fawqihim wayafAAaloona m<u>a</u> yu/maroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  They all revere their Lord,<br> High above them, and they do<br> All that they are commanded.</p> <div class="filenav">
