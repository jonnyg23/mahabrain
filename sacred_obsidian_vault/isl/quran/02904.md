 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at rusulun<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema bi<b>a</b>lbushr<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> muhlikoo ahli h<u>ath</u>ihi alqaryati inna ahlah<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  When Our Messengers came<br> To Abraham with the good news,<br> They said: "We are indeed<br> Going to destroy the people<br> Of this township: for truly<br> They are (addicted to) crime."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>la inna feeh<u>a</u> loo<u>t</u>an q<u>a</u>loo na<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu biman feeh<u>a</u> lanunajjiyannahu waahlahu ill<u>a</u> imraatahu k<u>a</u>nat mina algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  He said: "But there is<br> Lūṭ there." They said:<br> "Well do we know who<br> Is there: we will certainly<br> Save him and his following,—<br> Except his wife: she is<br> Of those who lag behind!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Walamm<u>a</u> an j<u>a</u>at rusulun<u>a</u> loo<u>t</u>an see-a bihim wa<u>da</u>qa bihim <u>th</u>arAAan waq<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> takhaf wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zan inn<u>a</u> munajjooka waahlaka ill<u>a</u> imraataka k<u>a</u>nat mina algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And when Our Messengers<br> Came to Lūṭ, he was<br> Grieved on their account,<br> And felt himself powerless<br> (To protect) them: but they said:<br> "Fear thou not, nor grieve:<br> We are (here) to save thee<br> And thy following, except<br> Thy wife: she is<br> Of those who lag behind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inn<u>a</u> munziloona AAal<u>a</u> ahli h<u>ath</u>ihi alqaryati rijzan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafsuqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "For we are going to<br> Bring down on the people<br> Of this township a Punishment<br> From heaven, because they<br> Have been wickedly rebellious."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Walaqad tarakn<u>a</u> minh<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan bayyinatan liqawmin yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  And We have left thereof<br> An evident Sign,<br> For any people who<br> (Care to) understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa-il<u>a</u> madyana akh<u>a</u>hum shuAAayban faq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>o</b>rjoo alyawma al-<u>a</u>khira wal<u>a</u> taAAthaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  To the Madyan (people)<br> (We sent) their brother Shu‘aib.<br> Then he said: "O my people!<br> Serve God, and fear the Last<br> Day: nor commit evil<br> On the earth, with intent<br> To do mischief."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b>rrajfatu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo fee d<u>a</u>rihim j<u>a</u>thimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  But they rejected him:<br> Then the mighty Blast<br> Seized them, and they lay<br> Prostrate in their homes<br> By the morning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  WaAA<u>a</u>dan wathamooda waqad tabayyana lakum min mas<u>a</u>kinihim wazayyana lahumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu aAAm<u>a</u>lahum fa<u>s</u>addahum AAani a<b>l</b>ssabeeli wak<u>a</u>noo mustab<u>s</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  (Remember also) the ’Ad<br> And the <u>Th</u>amūd (people):<br> Clearly will appear to you<br> From (the traces) of their buildings<br> (Their fate): the Evil One<br> Made their deeds alluring<br> To them, and kept them hack<br> From the Path, though they<br> Were gifted with Intelligence<br> And Skill.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Waq<u>a</u>roona wafirAAawna wah<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>na walaqad j<u>a</u>ahum moos<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fa<b>i</b>stakbaroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo s<u>a</u>biqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  (Remember also) Qārūn,<br> Pharaoh, and Haman: there came<br> To them Moses with Clear Signs,<br> But they behaved with insolence<br> On the earth; yet they<br> Could not overreach (Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Fakullan akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> bi<u>th</u>anbihi faminhum man arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>has</u>iban waminhum man akha<u>th</u>at-hu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu waminhum man khasafn<u>a</u> bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a waminhum man aghraqn<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liya<i><u>th</u></i>limahum wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Each one of them We seized<br> For his crime: of them,<br> Against some We sent<br> A violent tornado (with showers<br> Of stones); some were caught<br> By a (mighty) Blast; some<br> We caused the earth<br> To swallow up; and some<br> We drowned (in the waters):<br> It was net God Who<br> Injured (or oppressed) them:<br> They injured (and oppressed)<br> Their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Mathalu alla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi awliy<u>a</u>a kamathali alAAankabooti ittakha<u>th</u>at baytan wa-inna awhana albuyooti labaytu alAAankabooti law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  The parable of those who<br> Take protectors other than God<br> Is that of the Spider,<br> Who builds (to itself)<br>A house; but truly<br> The flimsiest of houses<br> Is the Spider's house;—<br> If they but knew.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> yadAAoona min doonihi min shay-in wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Verily God doth know<br> Of (every thing) whatever<br> That they call upon<br> Besides Him: and He is<br> Exalted (in power), Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Watilka al-amth<u>a</u>lu na<u>d</u>ribuh<u>a</u> li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wam<u>a</u> yaAAqiluh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And such are the Parables<br> We set forth for mankind,<br> But only those understand them<br> Who have Knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Khalaqa All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan lilmu-mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  God created the heavens<br> And the earth in true (proportions):<br> Verily in that is a Sign<br> For those who believe.</p> <div class="filenav">
