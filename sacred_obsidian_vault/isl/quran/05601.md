 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVI.: Wāqi‘a, or The Inevitable Event.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  I<u>tha</u> waqaAAati alw<u>a</u>qiAAat<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  When the Event Inevitable<br> Cometh to pass,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Laysa liwaqAAatih<u>a</u> k<u>ath</u>iba<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Then will no (soul)<br> Entertain falsehood<br> Concerning its coming.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Kh<u>a</u>fi<u>d</u>atun r<u>a</u>fiAAa<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  (Many) will it bring low;<br> (Many) will it exalt;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  I<u>tha</u> rujjati al-ar<u>d</u>u rajj<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  When the earth shall be<br> Shaken to its depths,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wabussati aljib<u>a</u>lu bass<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And the mountains shall<br> Be crumbled to atoms,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Fak<u>a</u>nat hab<u>a</u>an munbathth<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Becoming dust scattered abroad,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wakuntum azw<u>a</u>jan thal<u>a</u>tha<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And ye shall be sorted out<br> Into three classes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Faa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almaymanati m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almaymanat<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Then (there will be)<br> The Companions of<br> The Right Hand;—<br> What will be<br> The Companions of<br> The Right Hand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almash-amati m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almash-amat<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And the Companions of<br> The Left Hand,—<br> What will be<br> The Companions of<br> The Left Hand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>biqoona a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>biqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And those Foremost<br> (In Faith) will be<br> Foremost (in the Hereafter).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika almuqarraboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  These will be<br> Those Nearest to God:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Fee jann<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  In Gardens of Bliss:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Thullatun mina al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  A number of people<br> From those of old,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waqaleelun mina al-<u>a</u>khireena   </p>
 <p>14.  And a few from those<br> Of later times.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  AAal<u>a</u> sururin maw<u>d</u>oona<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  (They will be) on Thrones<br> Encrusted (with gold<br> And precious stones),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Muttaki-eena AAalayh<u>a</u> mutaq<u>a</u>bileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Reclining on them,<br> Facing each other.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Ya<u>t</u>oofu AAalayhim wild<u>a</u>nun mukhalladoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Round about them will (serve)<br> Youths of perpetual (freshness),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Bi-akw<u>a</u>bin waab<u>a</u>reeqa waka/sin min maAAeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  With goblets, (shining) beakers,<br> And cups (filled) out of<br> Clear-flowing fountains:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  L<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>addaAAoona AAanh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> yunzifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  No after-ache will they<br> Receive therefrom, nor will they<br> Suffer intoxication:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Waf<u>a</u>kihatin mimm<u>a</u> yatakhayyaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And with fruits,<br> Any that they may select;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wala<u>h</u>mi <u>t</u>ayrin mimm<u>a</u> yashtahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And the flesh of fowls,<br> Any that they may desire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wa<u>h</u>oorun AAeen<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>22.  And (there will be) Companions<br> With beautiful, big,<br> And lustrous eyes,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Kaamth<u>a</u>li allu/lui almaknoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Like unto Pearls<br> Well-guarded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  A Reward for the Deeds<br> Of their past (Life).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  L<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> laghwan wal<u>a</u> ta/theem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  No frivolity will they<br> Hear therein, nor any<br> Taint of ill,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Ill<u>a</u> qeelan sal<u>a</u>man sal<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Only the saying,<br> "Peace! Peace".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alyameeni m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  The Companions of<br> The Right Hand,—<br> What will be<br> The Companions of<br> The Right Hand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Fee sidrin makh<u>d</u>ood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  (They will be) among<br> Lote-trees without thorns,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wa<u>t</u>al<u>h</u>in man<u>d</u>ood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Among Ṭalḥ trees<br> With flowers (or fruits)<br> Piled one above another,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wa<i><u>th</u></i>illin mamdood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  In shade long-extended,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wam<u>a</u>-in maskoob<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  By water flowing constantly,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Waf<u>a</u>kihatin katheera<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And fruit in abundance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  L<u>a</u> maq<u>t</u>ooAAatin wal<u>a</u> mamnooAAa<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Whose season is not limited,<br> Nor (supply) forbidden,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wafurushin marfooAAa<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And on Thrones (of Dignity),<br> Raised high.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Inn<u>a</u> ansha/n<u>a</u>hunna insh<u>aa</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  We have created<br> (their Companions)<br> Of special creation.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  FajaAAaln<u>a</u>hunna abk<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>36.  And made them<br> Virgin-pure (and undefiled),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  AAuruban atr<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Beloved (by nature),<br> Equal in age,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Li-a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  For the Companions<br> Of the Right Hand.</p> <div class="filenav">
