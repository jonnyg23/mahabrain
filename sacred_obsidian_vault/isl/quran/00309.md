 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu meeth<u>a</u>qa a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena lam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytukum min kit<u>a</u>bin wa<u>h</u>ikmatin thumma j<u>a</u>akum rasoolun mu<u>s</u>addiqun lim<u>a</u> maAAakum latu/minunna bihi walatan<u>s</u>urunnahu q<u>a</u>la aaqrartum waakha<u>th</u>tum AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum i<u>s</u>ree q<u>a</u>loo aqrarn<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la fa<b>i</b>shhadoo waan<u>a</u> maAAakum mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Behold! God took<br> The Covenant of the Prophets,<br> Saying: "I give you<br> A Book and Wisdom;<br> Then comes to you<br> An Apostle, confirming<br> What is with you;<br> Do ye believe in him<br> And render him help."<br> God said: "Do ye agree,<br> And take this my Covenant<br> As binding on you?"<br> They said: "We agree."<br> He said: "Then bear witness,<br> And I am with you<br> Among the witnesses."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Faman tawall<u>a</u> baAAda <u>tha</u>lika faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  If any turn back<br> After this, they are<br> Perverted transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Afaghayra deeni All<u>a</u>hi yabghoona walahu aslama man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i <u>t</u>awAAan wakarhan wa-ilayhi yurjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Do they seek<br> For other than the Religion<br> Of God?—while all creatures<br> In the heavens and on earth<br> Have, willing or unwilling,<br> Bowed to His Will<br> (Accepted Islam),<br> And to Him shall they<br> All be brought back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Qul <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> onzila AAalayn<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> onzila AAal<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba wa<b>a</b>l-asb<u>at</u>i wam<u>a</u> ootiya moos<u>a</u> waAAees<u>a</u> wa<b>al</b>nnabiyyoona min rabbihim l<u>a</u> nufarriqu bayna a<u>h</u>adin minhum wana<u>h</u>nu lahu muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Say: "We believe<br> In God, and in what<br> Has been revealed to us<br> And what was revealed<br> To Abraham, Ismā’īl;<br> Isaac, Jacob, and the Tribes,<br> And in (the Books)<br> Given to Moses, Jesus,<br> And the Prophets,<br> From their Lord:<br> We make no distinction<br> Between one and another<br> Among them, and to God do we<br> Bow our will (in Islam)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Waman yabtaghi ghayra al-isl<u>a</u>mi deenan falan yuqbala minhu wahuwa fee al-<u>a</u>khirati mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  If anyone desires<br> A religion other than<br> Islam (submission to God),<br> Never will it be accepted<br> Of him; and in the Hereafter<br> He will be in the ranks<br> Of those who have lost<br> (All spiritual good).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Kayfa yahdee All<u>a</u>hu qawman kafaroo baAAda eem<u>a</u>nihim washahidoo anna a<b>l</b>rrasoola <u>h</u>aqqun waj<u>a</u>ahumu albayyin<u>a</u>tu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  How shall God<br> Guide those who reject<br> Faith after they accepted it<br> And bore witness<br> That the Apostle was true<br> And that Clear Signs<br> Had come unto them?<br> But God guides not<br> A people unjust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika jaz<u>a</u>ohum anna AAalayhim laAAnata All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikati wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  Of such the reward<br> Is that on them (rests)<br>The curse of God,<br> Of His angels,<br> And of all mankind;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yukhaffafu AAanhumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu wal<u>a</u> hum yun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  In that will they dwell;<br> Nor will their penalty<br> Be lightened, nor respite<br> Be their (lot);—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena t<u>a</u>boo min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>oo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>89.  Except for those that repent<br> (Even) after that,<br> And make amends;<br> For verily God<br> Is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo baAAda eem<u>a</u>nihim thumma izd<u>a</u>doo kufran lan tuqbala tawbatuhum waol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  But those who reject<br> Faith after they accepted it,<br> And then go on adding<br> To their defiance of Faith,—<br> Never will their repentance<br> Be accepted; for they<br> Are those who have<br> (Of set purpose) gone astray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wam<u>a</u>too wahum kuff<u>a</u>run falan yuqbala min a<u>h</u>adihim milo al-ar<u>d</u>i <u>th</u>ahaban walawi iftad<u>a</u> bihi ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleemun wam<u>a</u> lahum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  As to those who reject<br> Faith, and die rejecting,—<br> Never would be accepted<br> From any such as much<br> Gold as the earth contains,<br> Though they should offer it<br> For ransom. For such<br> Is (in store) a penalty grievous,<br> And they will find no helpers.</p> <div class="filenav">
