 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Walamm<u>a</u> tawajjaha tilq<u>a</u>a madyana q<u>a</u>la AAas<u>a</u> rabbee an yahdiyanee saw<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  When, when he turned his face<br> Towards (the land of) Madyan,<br> He said: "I do hope<br> That my Lord will show me<br> The smooth and straight Path."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Walamm<u>a</u> warada m<u>a</u>a madyana wajada AAalayhi ommatan mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si yasqoona wawajada min doonihimu imraatayni ta<u>th</u>ood<u>a</u>ni q<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>bukum<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>lat<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nasqee <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>dira a<b>l</b>rriAA<u>a</u>o waaboon<u>a</u> shaykhun kabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  And when he arrived at<br> The watering (place) in Madyan;<br> He found there a group<br> Of men watering (their flocks),<br> And besides them he found<br> Two women who were keeping<br>Back (their flocks). He said:<br> "What is the matter with you?"<br> They said: "We cannot water<br> (Our flocks) until the shepherds<br> Take back (their flocks):<br> And our father is<br> A very old man."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fasaq<u>a</u> lahum<u>a</u> thumma tawall<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>illi faq<u>a</u>la rabbi innee lim<u>a</u> anzalta ilayya min khayrin faqeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  So he watered (their flocks)<br> For them; then he turned back<br> To the shade, and said:<br> "O my Lord!<br> Truly am I<br> In (desperate) need<br> Of any good<br> That Thou dost send me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Faj<u>a</u>at-hu i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> tamshee AAal<u>a</u> isti<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>-in q<u>a</u>lat inna abee yadAAooka liyajziyaka ajra m<u>a</u> saqayta lan<u>a</u> falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahu waqa<u>ss</u>a AAalayhi alqa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>a q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> takhaf najawta mina alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Afterwards one of the (damsels)<br> Came (back) to him, walking<br> Bashfully. She said: "My father<br> Invites thee that he may<br> Reward thee for having watered<br> (Our flocks) for us." So when<br> He came to him and narrated<br> The story, he said:<br>"Fear thou not: (well) hast thou<br> Escaped from unjust people."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Q<u>a</u>lat i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> abati ista/jirhu inna khayra mani ista/jarta alqawiyyu al-ameen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Said one of the (damsels):<br> "O my (dear) father! engage<br> Him on wages: truly the best<br> Of men for thee to employ is<br> The (man) who is strong and trusty"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Q<u>a</u>la innee oreedu an onki<u>h</u>aka i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u> ibnatayya h<u>a</u>tayni AAal<u>a</u> an ta/juranee tham<u>a</u>niya <u>h</u>ijajin fa-in atmamta AAashran famin AAindika wam<u>a</u> oreedu an ashuqqa AAalayka satajidunee in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  He said: "I intend to wed<br> One of these my daughters<br> To thee, on, condition that<br> Thou serve me for eight years;<br> But if thou complete ten years,<br> It will be (grace) from thee.<br> But I intend not to place<br> Thee under a difficulty:<br>Thou wilt find me,<br> Indeed, if God wills,<br> One of the righteous."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Q<u>a</u>la <u>tha</u>lika baynee wabaynaka ayyam<u>a</u> al-ajalayni qa<u>d</u>aytu fal<u>a</u> AAudw<u>a</u>na AAalayya wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> naqoolu wakeel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  He said: "Be that (the agreement)<br> Between me and thee:<br> Whichever of the two terms<br> I fulfil, let there be<br> No ill-will to me.<br> Be God a witness<br> To what we say."</p> <div class="filenav">
