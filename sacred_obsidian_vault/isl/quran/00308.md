 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Waq<u>a</u>lat <u>ta</u>-ifatun min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi <u>a</u>minoo bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee onzila AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wajha a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wa<b>o</b>kfuroo <u>a</u>khirahu laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  A section of the People<br> Of the Book say:<br> "Believe in the morning<br> What is revealed<br> To the Believers,<br> But reject it at the end<br> Of the day; perchance<br> They may (themselves)<br> Turn back;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wal<u>a</u> tu/minoo ill<u>a</u> liman tabiAAa deenakum qul inna alhud<u>a</u> hud<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi an yu/t<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>adun mithla m<u>a</u> ooteetum aw yu<u>ha</u>jjookum AAinda rabbikum qul inna alfa<u>d</u>la biyadi All<u>a</u>hi yu/teehi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu w<u>a</u>siAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  "And believe no one<br> Unless he follows<br> Your religion."<br> Say: "True guidance<br> Is the guidance of God:<br> (Fear ye) lest a revelation<br> Be sent to someone (else)<br> Like unto that which was sent<br> Unto you? Or that those<br> (Receiving such revelation)<br> Should engage you in argument<br> Before your Lord?"<br> Say: "All bounties<br> Are in the hand of God:<br> He granteth them<br> To whom He pleaseth:<br>And God careth for all,<br> And He knoweth all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Yakhta<u>ss</u>u bira<u>h</u>matihi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  For His Mercy He specially chooseth<br> Whom He pleaseth;<br> For God is the Lord<br> Of bounties unbounded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Wamin ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi man in ta/manhu biqin<u>ta</u>rin yu-addihi ilayka waminhum man in ta/manhu bideen<u>a</u>rin l<u>a</u> yu-addihi ilayka ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> dumta AAalayhi q<u>a</u>-iman <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum q<u>a</u>loo laysa AAalayn<u>a</u> fee al-ommiyyeena sabeelun wayaqooloona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Among the People of the Book<br> Are some who, if entrusted<br> With a hoard of gold,<br> Will (readily) pay it back;<br> Others, who, if entrusted<br> With a single silver coin,<br> Will not repay it unless<br> Thou constantly stoodest<br> Demanding, because,<br> They say, "there is no call<br> On us (to keep faith)<br> With these ignorant (Pagans)."<br> But they tell a lie against God,<br> And (well) they know it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Bal<u>a</u> man awf<u>a</u> biAAahdihi wa<b>i</b>ttaq<u>a</u> fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Nay.—Those that keep<br> Their plighted faith<br> And act aright,—verily<br> God loves those<br> Who act aright.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yashtaroona biAAahdi All<u>a</u>hi waaym<u>a</u>nihim thamanan qaleelan ol<u>a</u>-ika l<u>a</u> khal<u>a</u>qa lahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati wal<u>a</u> yukallimuhumu All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u> yan<i><u>th</u></i>uru ilayhim yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wal<u>a</u> yuzakkeehim walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  As for those who sell<br> The faith they owe to God<br> And their own plighted word<br> For a small price,<br> They shall have no portion<br> In the Hereafter:<br>Nor will God<br> (Deign to) speak to them<br> Or look at them<br> On the Day of Judgment,<br> Nor will He cleanse them<br> (Of sin): they shall have<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wa-inna minhum lafareeqan yalwoona alsinatahum bi<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi lita<u>h</u>saboohu mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wam<u>a</u> huwa mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wayaqooloona huwa min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> huwa min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi wayaqooloona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  There is among them<br> A section who distort<br> The Book with their tongues<br> (As they read) you would think<br> It is a part of the Book,<br> But it is no part<br> Of the Book; and they say,<br> That is from God,"<br> But it is not from God:<br> It is they who tell<br> A lie against God,<br> And (well) they know it!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na libasharin an yu/tiyahu All<u>a</u>hu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ukma wa<b>al</b>nnubuwwata thumma yaqoola li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si koonoo AAib<u>a</u>dan lee min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u>kin koonoo rabb<u>a</u>niyyeena bim<u>a</u> kuntum tuAAallimoona alkit<u>a</u>ba wabim<u>a</u> kuntum tadrusoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  It is not (possible)<br> That a man, to whom<br> Is given the Book,<br> And Wisdom,<br> And the Prophetic Office,<br> Should say to people:<br> "Be ye my worshippers<br> Rather than God's":<br> On the contrary<br> (He would say):<br> "Be ye worshippers<br> Of Him Who is truly<br> The Cherisher of all:<br> For ye have taught<br> The Book and ye<br> Have studied it earnestly."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Wal<u>a</u> ya/murakum an tattakhi<u>th</u>oo almal<u>a</u>-ikata wa<b>al</b>nnabiyyeena arb<u>a</u>ban aya/murukum bi<b>a</b>lkufri baAAda i<u>th</u> antum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Nor would he instruct you<br> To take angels and prophets<br> For Lords and Patrons.<br> What! would he bid you<br>To unbelief after ye have<br> Bowed your will<br> (To God in Islam)?</p> <div class="filenav">
