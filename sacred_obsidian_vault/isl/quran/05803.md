 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVIII.: Mujādila, or The Woman who Pleads.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena tawallaw qawman gha<u>d</u>iba All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim m<u>a</u> hum minkum wal<u>a</u> minhum waya<u>h</u>lifoona AAal<u>a</u> alka<u>th</u>ibi wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Turnest thou not<br> Thy attention to those<br> Who turn (in friendship)<br> To such as have the Wrath<br> Of God upon them?<br> They are neither of you<br> Nor of them, and they<br> Swear to falsehood knowingly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  aAAadda All<u>a</u>hu lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan innahum s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  God has prepared for them<br> A severe Penalty: evil<br> Indeed are their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Ittakha<u>th</u>oo aym<u>a</u>nahum junnatan fa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi falahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  They have made their oaths<br> A screen (for their misdeeds):<br> Thus they obstruct (men)<br> From the Path of God:<br> Therefore shall they have<br> A humiliating Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Lan tughniya AAanhum amw<u>a</u>luhum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>duhum mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Of no profit whatever<br> To them, against God,<br> Will be their riches<br> Nor their sons:<br>They will be Companions<br> Of the Fire, to dwell<br> Therein (for aye)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Yawma yabAAathuhumu All<u>a</u>hu jameeAAan faya<u>h</u>lifoona lahu kam<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>lifoona lakum waya<u>h</u>saboona annahum AAal<u>a</u> shay-in al<u>a</u> innahum humu alk<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  One Day will God<br> Raise them all up<br> (For Judgment): then<br> Will they swear to Him<br> As they swear to you:<br> And they think that they<br> Have something (to stand upon).<br> No, indeed! they are<br> But liars!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Ista<u>h</u>wa<u>th</u>a AAalayhimu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu faans<u>a</u>hum <u>th</u>ikra All<u>a</u>hi ol<u>a</u>-ika <u>h</u>izbu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni al<u>a</u> inna <u>h</u>izba a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  The Evil One has<br> Got the better of them:<br> So he has made them<br> Lose the remembrance<br> Of God. They are the Party<br> Of the Evil One. Truly,<br> It is the Party<br> Of the Evil One<br> That will perish!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>ha</u>ddoona All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu ol<u>a</u>-ika fee al-a<u>th</u>alleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Those who resist<br> God and His Apostle<br> Will be among those<br> Most humiliated.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Kataba All<u>a</u>hu laaghlibanna an<u>a</u> warusulee inna All<u>a</u>ha qawiyyun AAazeez<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  God has decreed:<br> "It is I and My apostles<br> Who must prevail":<br> For God is One<br> Full of strength,<br> Able to enforce His Will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  L<u>a</u> tajidu qawman yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri yuw<u>a</u>ddoona man <u>ha</u>dda All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu walaw k<u>a</u>noo <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum aw abn<u>a</u>ahum aw ikhw<u>a</u>nahum aw AAasheeratahum ol<u>a</u>-ika kataba fee quloobihimu al-eem<u>a</u>na waayyadahum biroo<u>h</u>in minhu wayudkhiluhum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> ra<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu AAanhum wara<u>d</u>oo AAanhu ol<u>a</u>-ika <u>h</u>izbu All<u>a</u>hi al<u>a</u> inna <u>h</u>izba All<u>a</u>hi humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Thou wilt not find<br> Any people who believe<br> In God and the Last Day,<br> Loving those who resist<br> God and His Apostle,<br> Even though they were<br> Their fathers or their sons,<br> Or their brothers, or<br> Their kindred. For such<br> He has written Faiths<br> In their hearts, and strengthened<br> Them with a spirit<br> From Himself. And He<br> Will admit them to Gardens<br> Beneath which Rivers flow,<br> To dwell therein (for ever).<br> God will be well pleased<br> With them, and they with Him.<br> They are the Party<br> Of God. Truly it is<br> The Party of God that<br> Will achieve Felicity.</p>  <div class="filenav">
