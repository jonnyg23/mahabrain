 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XX.: Ṭā-Hā. (Mystic Letters, Ṭ. H.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Minh<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>kum wafeeh<u>a</u> nuAAeedukum waminh<u>a</u> nukhrijukum t<u>a</u>ratan okhr<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>55.  From the (earth) did We<br> Create you, and into it<br> Shall We return you,<br> And from it shall We<br> Bring you out once again"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Walaqad arayn<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> kullah<u>a</u> faka<u>thth</u>aba waab<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>56.  And We showed Pharaoh<br> All Our Signs, but he<br> Did reject and refuse.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Q<u>a</u>la aji/tan<u>a</u> litukhrijan<u>a</u> min ar<u>d</u>in<u>a</u> bisi<u>h</u>rika y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>57.  He said: "Hast thou come<br> To drive us out<br> Of our land with thy magic,<br> O Moses?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Falana/tiyannaka bisi<u>h</u>rin mithlihi fa<b>i</b>jAAal baynan<u>a</u> wabaynaka mawAAidan l<u>a</u> nukhlifuhu na<u>h</u>nu wal<u>a</u> anta mak<u>a</u>nan suw<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  "But we can surely produce<br> Magic to match thine!<br> So make a tryst<br> Between us and thee,<br> Which we shall not fail<br> To keep—neither we nor thou—<br> In a place where both<br> Shall have even chances."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Q<u>a</u>la mawAAidukum yawmu a<b>l</b>zzeenati waan yu<u>h</u>shara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su <u>d</u>u<u>ha</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Moses said: "Your tryst<br> Is the Day of the Festival,<br> And let the people be assembled<br> When the sun is well up."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Fatawall<u>a</u> firAAawnu fajamaAAa kaydahu thumma at<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>60.  So Pharaoh withdrew:<br> He concerted his plan,<br> And then came (back).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Q<u>a</u>la lahum moos<u>a</u> waylakum l<u>a</u> taftaroo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban fayus<u>h</u>itakum biAAa<u>tha</u>bin waqad kh<u>a</u>ba mani iftar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>61.  Moses said to him:<br> Woe to you! Forge not<br> Ye a lie against God,<br> Lest He destroy you (at once)<br>Utterly by chastisement:<br> The forger must suffer<br> Frustration!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Fatan<u>a</u>zaAAoo amrahum baynahum waasarroo a<b>l</b>nnajw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>62.  So they disputed, one with<br> Another, over their affair,<br> But they kept their talk secret.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>loo in h<u>atha</u>ni las<u>ah</u>ir<u>a</u>ni yureed<u>a</u>ni an yukhrij<u>a</u>kum min ar<u>d</u>ikum bisi<u>h</u>rihim<u>a</u> waya<u>th</u>hab<u>a</u> bi<u>t</u>areeqatikumu almuthl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>63.  They said: "These two<br> Are certainly (expert) magicians:<br> Their object is to drive you<br> Out from your land<br> With their magic, and<br> To do away with your<br> Most cherished institutions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  FaajmiAAoo kaydakum thumma i/too <u>s</u>affan waqad afla<u>h</u>a alyawma mani istaAAl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>64.  "Therefore concert your plan,<br> And then assemble<br> In (serried) ranks:<br> He wins (all along) to-day<br> Who gains the upper hand"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> imm<u>a</u> an tulqiya wa-imm<u>a</u> an nakoona awwala man alq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>65.  They said: "O Moses!<br> Whether wilt thou<br> That thou throw (first)<br> Or that we be the first<br> To throw?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Q<u>a</u>la bal alqoo fa-i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>ib<u>a</u>luhum waAAi<u>s</u>iyyuhum yukhayyalu ilayhi min si<u>h</u>rihim annah<u>a</u> tasAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>66.  He said, "Nay, throw ye<br> First!" Then behold<br>Their ropes and their rods—<br> So it seemed to him<br> On account of their magic—<br> Began to be in lively motion!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Faawjasa fee nafsihi kheefatan moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>67.  So Moses conceived<br> In his mind<br> A (sort of) fear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Quln<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> takhaf innaka anta al-aAAl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>68.  We said: "Fear not!<br> For thou hast indeed<br> The upper hand:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Waalqi m<u>a</u> fee yameenika talqaf m<u>a</u> <u>s</u>anaAAoo innam<u>a</u> <u>s</u>anaAAoo kaydu s<u>ah</u>irin wal<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u a<b>l</b>ss<u>ah</u>iru <u>h</u>aythu at<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>69.  "Throw that which is<br> In thy right hand:<br> Quickly will it swallow up<br> That which they have faked<br> What they have faked<br> Is but a magician's trick:<br> And the magician thrives not,<br> (No matter) where he goes."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Faolqiya a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu sujjadan q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> birabbi h<u>a</u>roona wamoos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>70.  So the magicians were<br> Thrown down to prostration:<br> They said, "We believe<br> In the Lord of Aaron and Moses".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Q<u>a</u>la <u>a</u>mantum lahu qabla an <u>ath</u>ana lakum innahu lakabeerukumu alla<u>th</u>ee AAallamakumu a<b>l</b>ssi<u>h</u>ra falaoqa<u>tt</u>iAAanna aydiyakum waarjulakum min khil<u>a</u>fin walao<u>s</u>allibannakum fee ju<u>th</u>ooAAi a<b>l</b>nnakhli walataAAlamunna ayyun<u>a</u> ashaddu AAa<u>tha</u>ban waabq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>71.  (Pharaoh) said: "Believe ye<br> In Him before I give<br> You permission? Surely<br> This must be your leader,<br> Who has taught you magic!<br> Be sure I will cut off<br>Your hands and feet<br> On opposite sides, and I<br> Will have you crucified<br> On trunks of palm-trees:<br> So shall ye know for certain,<br> Which of us can give<br> The more severe and the more<br> Lasting Punishment!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Q<u>a</u>loo lan nu/thiraka AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> mina albayyin<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>aran<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>q<u>d</u>i m<u>a</u> anta q<u>ad</u>in innam<u>a</u> taq<u>d</u>ee h<u>ath</u>ihi al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>72.  They said: "Never shall we<br> Regard thee as more than<br> The Clear Signs that have<br> Come to us; or than<br> Him Who created us!<br> So decree whatever thou<br> Desirest to decree: for thou<br> Canst only decree (touching)<br> The life of this world.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Inn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> birabbin<u>a</u> liyaghfira lan<u>a</u> kha<u>ta</u>y<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> akrahtan<u>a</u> AAalayhi mina a<b>l</b>ssi<u>h</u>ri wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khayrun waabq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>73.  "For us, we have believed<br> In our Lord: may He<br> Forgive us our faults,<br> And the magic to which<br> Thou didst compel us:<br> For God is Best<br> And Most Abiding."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Innahu man ya/ti rabbahu mujriman fa-inna lahu jahannama l<u>a</u> yamootu feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>74.  Verily he who comes<br> To his Lord as a sinner<br> (At Judgment),—for him<br> Is Hell: therein shall he<br> Neither die nor live.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Waman ya/tihi mu/minan qad AAamila a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti faol<u>a</u>-ika lahumu a<b>l</b>ddaraj<u>a</u>tu alAAul<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>75.  But such as come<br> To Him as Believers<br> Who have worked righteous deeds,—<br> For them are ranks exalted,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Jann<u>a</u>tu AA<u>a</u>dnin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> wa<u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o man tazakk<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>76.  Gardens of Eternity,<br> Beneath which flow rivers:<br> They will dwell therein<br> For aye: such is the reward<br> Of those who purify<br> Themselves (from evil).</p> <div class="filenav">
