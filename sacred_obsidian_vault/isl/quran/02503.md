 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXV.: Furqān, or The Criterion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yarjoona liq<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> lawl<u>a</u> onzila AAalayn<u>a</u> almal<u>a</u>-ikatu aw nar<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> laqadi istakbaroo fee anfusihim waAAataw AAutuwwan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Such as fear not<br> The meeting with Us<br> (For Judgment) say:<br> "Why are not the angels<br> Sent down to us, or<br> (Why) do we not see<br> Our Lord?" Indeed they<br> Have an arrogant conceit<br> Of themselves, and mighty<br> Is the insolence of their impiety!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Yawma yarawna almal<u>a</u>-ikata l<u>a</u> bushr<u>a</u> yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmujrimeena wayaqooloona <u>h</u>ijran ma<u>h</u>joor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  The Day they see the angels,—<br> No joy will there be<br> To the sinners that Day:<br> The (angels) will say:<br> "There is a barrier<br> Forbidden (to you) altogether!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Waqadimn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> AAamiloo min AAamalin fajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu hab<u>a</u>an manthoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  A<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati yawma-i<u>th</u>in khayrun mustaqarran waa<u>h</u>sanu maqeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  The Companions of the Garden<br> Will be well, that Day,<br> In their abode, and have<br> The fairest of places for repose:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wayawma tashaqqaqu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o bi<b>a</b>lgham<u>a</u>mi wanuzzila almal<u>a</u>-ikatu tanzeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  The Day the heaven shall be<br> Rent asunder with clouds,<br> And angels shall be sent down,<br> Descending (in ranks),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Almulku yawma-i<u>th</u>in al<u>h</u>aqqu li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni wak<u>a</u>na yawman AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireena AAaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  That Day, the dominion<br> As of right and truth,<br> Shall be (wholly) for (God)<br> Most Merciful: it will be<br> A Day of dire difficulty<br> For the Misbelievers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wayawma yaAAa<u>dd</u>u a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limu AAal<u>a</u> yadayhi yaqoolu y<u>a</u> laytanee ittakha<u>th</u>tu maAAa a<b>l</b>rrasooli sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  I the Day that the wrong-doer<br> Will bite at his hands,<br> He will say, "Oh! would that<br> I had taken a (straight) path<br> With the Apostle!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Y<u>a</u> waylat<u>a</u> laytanee lam attakhi<u>th</u> ful<u>a</u>nan khaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "Ah! woe is me!<br> Would that I had never<br> Taken such a one<br> For a friend!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Laqad a<u>d</u>allanee AAani a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri baAAda i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>anee wak<u>a</u>na a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu lil-ins<u>a</u>ni kha<u>th</u>ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "He did lead me astray<br> From the Message (of God)<br> After it had come to me!<br> Ah! the Evil One is<br> But a traitor to man!<br>Then the Apostle will say:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Waq<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b>rrasoolu y<u>a</u> rabbi inna qawmee ittakha<u>th</u>oo h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na mahjoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  "</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika jaAAaln<u>a</u> likulli nabiyyin AAaduwwan mina almujrimeena wakaf<u>a</u> birabbika h<u>a</u>diyan wana<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Thus have We made<br> For every prophet an enemy<br> Among the sinners: but enough<br> Is thy Lord to guide<br> And to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lawl<u>a</u> nuzzila AAalayhi alqur-<u>a</u>nu jumlatan w<u>ah</u>idatan ka<u>tha</u>lika linuthabbita bihi fu-<u>a</u>daka warattaln<u>a</u>hu tarteel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Whose who reject Faith<br> Say: "Why is not the Qur-ān<br> Revealed to him all at once?<br> Thus (is it revealed), that We<br> May strengthen thy heart<br> Thereby, and We have<br> Rehearsed it to thee in slow,<br> Well-arranged stages, gradually.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wal<u>a</u> ya/toonaka bimathalin ill<u>a</u> ji/n<u>a</u>ka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waa<u>h</u>sana tafseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And no question do they<br> Bring to thee but We<br> Reveal to thee the truth<br> And the best explanation (thereof),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>h</u>sharoona AAal<u>a</u> wujoohihim il<u>a</u> jahannama ol<u>a</u>-ika sharrun mak<u>a</u>nan waa<u>d</u>allu sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Whose who will be gathered<br> To Hell (prone) on their faces,—<br> They will be in an evil<br> Plight, and, as to Path,<br> Most astray.</p> <div class="filenav">
