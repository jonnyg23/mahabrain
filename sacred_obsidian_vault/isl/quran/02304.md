 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrusulu kuloo mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti wa<b>i</b>AAmaloo <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an innee bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  O ye apostles! enjoy<br> (All) things good and pure,<br> And work righteousness:<br> For I am well-acquainted<br> With (all) that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wa-inna h<u>ath</u>ihi ommatukum ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan waan<u>a</u> rabbukum fa<b>i</b>ttaqoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  And verily this Brotherhood<br> Of yours is a single Brotherhood,<br> And I am your Lord<br> And Cherisher: therefore<br> Fear Me (and no other).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Fataqa<u>tt</u>aAAoo amrahum baynahum zuburan kullu <u>h</u>izbin bim<u>a</u> ladayhim fari<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  But people have cut off<br> Their affair (of unity),<br> Between them, into sects:<br> Each party rejoices in that<br> Which is with itself.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Fa<u>th</u>arhum fee ghamratihim <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  But leave them<br> In their confused ignorance<br> For a time.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Aya<u>h</u>saboona annam<u>a</u> numidduhum bihi min m<u>a</u>lin wabaneen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Do they think that because<br> We have granted them abundance<br> Of wealth and sons,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Nus<u>a</u>riAAu lahum fee alkhayr<u>a</u>ti bal l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  We would hasten them<br> On in every good? Nay,<br> They do not understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena hum min khashyati rabbihim mushfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Verily those who live<br> In awe for fear of their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Those who believe<br> In the Signs of their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum birabbihim l<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Those who join not (in worship)<br> Partners with their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu/toona m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taw waquloobuhum wajilatun annahum il<u>a</u> rabbihim r<u>a</u>jiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  And those who dispense<br> Their charity with their hearts<br> Full of fear, because<br> They will return to their Lord;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika yus<u>a</u>riAAoona fee alkhayr<u>a</u>ti wahum lah<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>biqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  It is these who hasten<br> In every good work,<br> And these who are<br> Foremost in them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Wal<u>a</u> nukallifu nafsan ill<u>a</u> wusAAah<u>a</u> waladayn<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bun yan<u>t</u>iqu bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  On no soul do We<br> Place a burden greater<br> Than it can bear:<br> Before Us is a record<br> Which clearly shows the truth:<br> They will never be wronged.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Bal quloobuhum fee ghamratin min h<u>atha</u> walahum aAAm<u>a</u>lun min dooni <u>tha</u>lika hum lah<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>miloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  But their hearts are<br> In confused ignorance<br> Of this; and there are,<br> Besides that, deeds of theirs,<br> Which they will (continue)<br> To do,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> mutrafeehim bi<b>a</b>lAAa<u>tha</u>bi i<u>tha</u> hum yaj-aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Until, when We seize<br> In Punishment those of them<br> Who received the good things<br> Of this world, behold,<br> They will groan in supplication!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  L<u>a</u> taj-aroo alyawma innakum minn<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  (It will be said):<br> "Groan not in supplication<br> This day; for ye shall<br> Certainly not be helped by Us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Qad k<u>a</u>nat <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee tutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum fakuntum AAal<u>a</u> aAAq<u>a</u>bikum tanki<u>s</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  "My Signs used to be<br> Rehearsed to you, but ye<br> Used to turn back<br> On your heels—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Mustakbireena bihi s<u>a</u>miran tahjuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  "In arrogance: talking nonsense<br> About the (Qur-ān), like one<br> Telling fables by night."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Afalam yaddabbaroo alqawla am j<u>a</u>ahum m<u>a</u> lam ya/ti <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahumu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Do they not ponder over<br> The Word (of God), or<br> Has anything (new) come<br> To them that did not<br> Come to their fathers of old?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Am lam yaAArifoo rasoolahum fahum lahu munkiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Or do they not recognise<br> Their Apostle, that they<br> Deny him?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Am yaqooloona bihi jinnatun bal j<u>a</u>ahum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waaktharuhum lil<u>h</u>aqqi k<u>a</u>rihoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Or do they say, "he is<br> Possessed"? Nay, he has<br> Brought them the Truth,<br> But most of them<br> Hate the Truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Walawi ittabaAAa al<u>h</u>aqqu ahw<u>a</u>ahum lafasadati a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u waman feehinna bal atayn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>th</u>ikrihim fahum AAan <u>th</u>ikrihim muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  If the Truth had been<br> In accord with their desires,<br> Truly the heavens and the earth,<br> And all beings therein<br> Would have been in confusion<br> And corruption! Nay, We<br> Have sent them their admonition,<br> But they turn away<br> From their admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Am tas-aluhum kharjan fakhar<u>a</u>ju rabbika khayrun wahuwa khayru a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>ziqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Or is it that thou<br> Askest them for some<br> Recompense? But the recompense<br> Of thy Lord is best:<br> He is the Best of those<br> Who give sustenance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa-innaka latadAAoohum il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  But verily thou callest them<br> To the Straight Way;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa-inna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati AAani a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>i lan<u>a</u>kiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  And verily those who<br> Believe not in the Hereafter<br> Are deviating from that Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Walaw ra<u>h</u>imn<u>a</u>hum wakashafn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> bihim min <u>d</u>urrin lalajjoo fee <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nihim yaAAmahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  If We had mercy on them<br> And removed the distress<br> Which is on them, they<br> Would obstinately persist<br> In their transgression,<br> Wandering in distraction<br> To and fro.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Walaqad akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum bi<b>a</b>lAAa<u>tha</u>bi fam<u>a</u> istak<u>a</u>noo lirabbihim wam<u>a</u> yata<u>d</u>arraAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  We inflicted Punishment<br> On them, but they<br> Humbled not themselves<br> To their Lord, nor do they<br> Submissively entreat (Him)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> fata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayhim b<u>a</u>ban <u>tha</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bin shadeedin i<u>tha</u> hum feehi mublisoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Until We open on them<br> A gate leading to<br> A severe Punishment: then<br> Lo! they will be plunged<br> In despair therein!</p> <div class="filenav">
