 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wasul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  And we sent Moses,<br> With our Clear (signs)<br> And an authority manifest,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Il<u>a</u> firAAawna wamala-ihi fa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo amra firAAawna wam<u>a</u> amru firAAawna birasheed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  Unto Pharaoh and his Chiefs:<br> But they followed the command<br> Of Pharaoh, and the command<br> Of Pharaoh was no right (guide).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Yaqdumu qawmahu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati faawradahumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra wabi/sa alwirdu almawrood<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  He will go before his people<br> On the Day of Judgment,<br> And lead them into the Fire<br> (As cattle are led to water):<br> But woeful indeed will be<br> The place to which they are led!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  WaotbiAAoo fee h<u>ath</u>ihi laAAnatan wayawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati bi/sa a<b>l</b>rrifdu almarfood<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  And they are followed<br> By a curse in this (life)<br> And on the Day of Judgment:<br> And woeful is the gift<br> Which shall be given<br> (Unto them)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  <u>Tha</u>lika min anb<u>a</u>-i alqur<u>a</u> naqu<u>ss</u>uhu AAalayka minh<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>-imun wa<u>h</u>a<u>s</u>eed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  These are some of the stories<br> Of communities which We<br> Relate unto thee: of them<br> Some are standing, and some<br>Have been mown down<br> (By the sickle of time.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamn<u>a</u>hum wal<u>a</u>kin <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo anfusahum fam<u>a</u> aghnat AAanhum <u>a</u>lihatuhumu allatee yadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min shay-in lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amru rabbika wam<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>doohum ghayra tatbeeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  It was not We that wronged them:<br> They wronged their own souls:<br> The deities, other than God,<br> Whom they invoked, profited them<br> No whit when there issued<br> The decree of thy Lord:<br> Nor did they add aught<br> (To their lot) but perdition!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika akh<u>th</u>u rabbika i<u>tha</u> akha<u>th</u>a alqur<u>a</u> wahiya <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limatun inna akh<u>th</u>ahu aleemun shadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Such is the chastisement<br> Of thy Lord when He chastises<br> Communities in the midst of<br> Their wrong: grievous, indeed,<br> And severe is His chastisement.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan liman kh<u>a</u>fa AAa<u>tha</u>ba al-<u>a</u>khirati <u>tha</u>lika yawmun majmooAAun lahu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su wa<u>tha</u>lika yawmun mashhoodun   </p>
 <p>103.  In that is a Sign<br> For those who fear<br> The Penalty of the Hereafter:<br> That is a Day for which mankind<br> Will be gathered together:<br> That will be a Day<br> Of Testimony.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Wam<u>a</u> nu-akhkhiruhu ill<u>a</u> li-ajalin maAAdood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  Nor shall We delay it<br> But for a term appointed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Yawma ya/ti l<u>a</u> takallamu nafsun ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>nihi faminhum shaqiyyun wasaAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  The day it arrives,<br> No soul shall speak<br>Except by His leave:<br> Of those (gathered) some<br> Will be wretched and some<br> Will be blessed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena shaqoo fafee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri lahum feeh<u>a</u> zafeerun washaheeq<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  Those who are wretched<br> Shall be in the Fire:<br> There will be for them<br> Therein (nothing but) the heaving<br> Of sighs and sobs:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>mati a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a rabbuka inna rabbaka faAAAA<u>a</u>lun lim<u>a</u> yureed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  They will dwell therein<br> For all the time that<br> The heavens and the earth<br> Endure, except as thy Lord<br> Willeth: for thy Lord<br> Is the (sure) Accomplisher<br> Of what He planneth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena suAAidoo fafee aljannati kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>mati a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a rabbuka AAa<u>ta</u>an ghayra maj<u>th</u>oo<u>th</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  And those who are blessed<br> Shall be in the Garden:<br> They will dwell therein<br> For all the time that<br> The heavens and the earth<br> Endure, except as thy Lord<br> Willeth: a gift without break.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Fal<u>a</u> taku fee miryatin mimm<u>a</u> yaAAbudu h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u> yaAAbudoona ill<u>a</u> kam<u>a</u> yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ohum min qablu wa-inn<u>a</u> lamuwaffoohum na<u>s</u>eebahum ghayra manqoo<u>s</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Be not then in doubt<br> As to what these men<br> Worship. They worship nothing<br> But what their fathers worshipped<br> Before (them): but verily<br> We shall pay them back<br> (In full) their portion<br> Without (the least) abatement.</p> <div class="filenav">
