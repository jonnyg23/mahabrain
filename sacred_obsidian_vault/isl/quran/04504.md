 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLV.: Ja<u>th</u>iya, or Bowing the Knee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wayawma taqoomu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu yawma-i<u>th</u>in yakhsaru almub<u>t</u>iloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  To God belongs<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth, and<br> The Day that the Hour<br>Of Judgment is established,—<br> That Day will the dealers<br> In Falsehood perish!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Watar<u>a</u> kulla ommatin j<u>a</u>thiyatan kullu ommatin tudAA<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bih<u>a</u> alyawma tujzawna m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And thou wilt see<br> Every sect bowing the knee:<br> Every sect will be called<br> To its Record: "This Day<br> Shall ye be recompensed<br> For all that ye did!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  H<u>atha</u> kit<u>a</u>bun<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>iqu AAalaykum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> nastansikhu m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "This Our Record speaks<br> About you with truth:<br> For We were wont<br> To put on record<br> All that ye did."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fayudkhiluhum rabbuhum fee ra<u>h</u>matihi <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfawzu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Then, as to those who<br> Believed and did righteous<br> Deeds, their Lord will<br> Admit them to His Mercy:<br> That will be the Achievement's<br> For all to see.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo afalam takun <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee tutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum fa<b>i</b>stakbartum wakuntum qawman mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  But as to those who<br> Rejected God, (to them<br> Will be said): "Were not<br> Our Signs rehearsed to you?<br> But ye were arrogant,<br> And were a people<br> Given to sin!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu l<u>a</u> rayba feeh<u>a</u> qultum m<u>a</u> nadree m<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu in na<i><u>th</u></i>unnu ill<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>annan wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimustayqineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "And when it was said<br> That the promise of God<br> Was true, and that the Hour—<br> There was no doubt<br> About its (coming), ye<br> Used to say, "We<br> Know not what is<br> The Hour: we only think<br> It is an idea, and we<br> Have no firm assurance."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wabad<u>a</u> lahum sayyi-<u>a</u>tu m<u>a</u> AAamiloo wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Then will appear to them<br> The evil (fruits) of what<br> They did, and they will be<br> Completely encircled by that<br> Which they used to mock at!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Waqeela alyawma nans<u>a</u>kum kam<u>a</u> naseetum liq<u>a</u>a yawmikum h<u>atha</u> wama/w<u>a</u>kumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wam<u>a</u> lakum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  It will also be said:<br> "This Day We will forget<br> You as ye forgot<br> The meeting of this Day<br> Of yours! And your<br> Abode is the Fire, and<br> No helpers have ye!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  <u>Tha</u>likum bi-annakumu ittakha<u>th</u>tum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi huzuwan wagharratkumu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> fa<b>a</b>lyawma l<u>a</u> yukhrajoona minh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> hum yustaAAtaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "This, because ye used<br> To take the Signs of God<br> In jest, and the life<br> Of the world deceived you:<br> (From) that Day, therefore,<br> They shall not be taken out<br> Thence, nor shall they be<br> Received into Grace.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Falill<u>a</u>hi al<u>h</u>amdu rabbi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti warabbi al-ar<u>d</u>i rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Then Praise be to God,<br> Lord of the heavens<br> And Lord of the earth,—<br> Lord and Cherisher<br> Of all the worlds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Walahu alkibriy<u>a</u>o fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  To Him be Glory<br> Throughout the heavens<br> And the earth: and He<br> Is Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom!</p>  <div class="filenav">
