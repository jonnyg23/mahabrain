 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Qul man yaklaokum bi<b>a</b>llayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri mina a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni bal hum AAan <u>th</u>ikri rabbihim muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Say, "Who can keep<br> You safe by night and by day<br> From (the Wrath of) (God)<br> Most Gracious?" Yet they<br> Turn away from the mention<br> Of their Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Am lahum <u>a</u>lihatun tamnaAAuhum min doonin<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona na<u>s</u>ra anfusihim wal<u>a</u> hum minn<u>a</u> yu<u>sh</u>aboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Or have they gods that<br> Can guard them from Us?<br> They have no power to aid<br> Themselves, nor can they<br> Be defended from Us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Bal mattaAAn<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>la AAalayhimu alAAumuru afal<u>a</u> yarawna ann<u>a</u> na/tee al-ar<u>d</u>a nanqu<u>s</u>uh<u>a</u> min a<u>t</u>r<u>a</u>fih<u>a</u> afahumu algh<u>a</u>liboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Nay, We gave the good things<br> Of this life to these men<br> And their fathers until<br> The period grew long for them;<br> See they not that We<br> Gradually reduce the land<br> (In their control) from<br> Its outlying borders? Is it<br> Then they who will win?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Qul innam<u>a</u> on<u>th</u>irukum bi<b>a</b>lwa<u>h</u>yi wal<u>a</u> yasmaAAu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ummu a<b>l</b>dduAA<u>a</u>a i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> yun<u>th</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Say, "I do but warn you<br> According to revelation":<br> But the deaf will not hear<br> The call, (even) when<br> They are warned!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wala-in massat-hum naf<u>h</u>atun min AAa<u>tha</u>bi rabbika layaqoolunna y<u>a </u>waylan<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  If but a breath of the Wrath<br> Of thy Lord do touch them,<br> They will then say, "Woe<br> To us! we did wrong indeed!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wana<u>d</u>aAAu almaw<u>a</u>zeena alqis<u>t</u>a liyawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati fal<u>a</u> tu<i><u>th</u></i>lamu nafsun shay-an wa-in k<u>a</u>na mithq<u>a</u>la <u>h</u>abbatin min khardalin atayn<u>a</u> bih<u>a</u> wakaf<u>a</u> bin<u>a</u> <u>ha</u>sibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  We shall set up scales<br> Of justice for the day<br> Of Judgment, so that<br> Not a soul will be dealt with<br> Unjustly in the least.<br> And if there be<br> (No more than) the weight<br> Of a mustard seed,<br> We will bring it (to account):<br> And enough are We<br> To take account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roona alfurq<u>a</u>na wa<u>d</u>iy<u>a</u>an wa<u>th</u>ikran lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  In the past We granted<br> To Moses and Aaron<br>The Criterion (for judgment),<br> And a Light and a Message<br> For those who would do right,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yakhshawna rabbahum bi<b>a</b>lghaybi wahum mina a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati mushfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Those who fear their Lord<br> In their most secret thoughts,<br> And who hold the Hour<br> (Of Judgment) in awe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wah<u>atha</u> <u>th</u>ikrun mub<u>a</u>rakun anzaln<u>a</u>hu afaantum lahu munkiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And this is a blessed<br> Message which We have<br> Sent down: will ye then<br> Reject it?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema rushdahu min qablu wakunn<u>a</u> bihi AA<u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  We bestowed aforetime<br> On Abraham his rectitude<br> Of conduct, and well were we<br> Acquainted with him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la li-abeehi waqawmihi m<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>ttam<u>a</u>theelu allatee antum lah<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>kifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Behold! he said<br> To his father and his people<br> "What are these images,<br> To which ye are<br> (So assiduously) devoted?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Q<u>a</u>loo wajadn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>bideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  They said, "We found<br> Our fathers worshipping them."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Q<u>a</u>la laqad kuntum antum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  He said, "Indeed ye<br> Have been made in manifest<br> Error—ye and your fathers."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Q<u>a</u>loo aji/tan<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi am anta mina all<u>a</u>AAibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  They said, "Have you<br> Brought us the Truth,<br> Or are you one<br> Of those who jest?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Q<u>a</u>la bal rabbukum rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>arahunna waan<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  He said, "Nay, your Lord<br> Is the Lord of the heavens<br> And the earth, He Who<br> Created them (from nothing):<br> And I am a witness<br> To this (truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Wata<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi laakeedanna a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>makum baAAda an tuwalloo mudbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  "And by god, I have<br> A plan for your idols—<br> After ye go away<br> And turn your backs"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  FajaAAalahum ju<u>thath</u>an ill<u>a</u> kabeeran lahum laAAallahum ilayhi yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  So he broke them to pieces,<br> (All) but the biggest of them,<br> That they might turn<br> (And address themselves) to it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Q<u>a</u>loo man faAAala h<u>atha</u> bi-<u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u> innahu lamina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  They said, "Who has<br> Done this to our gods?<br> He must indeed be<br> Some man of impiety!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Q<u>a</u>loo samiAAn<u>a</u> fatan ya<u>th</u>kuruhum yuq<u>a</u>lu lahu ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  They said, "We heard<br> A youth talk of them:<br> He is called Abraham."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Q<u>a</u>loo fa/too bihi AAal<u>a</u> aAAyuni a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si laAAallahum yashhadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  They said, "Then bring him<br> Before the eyes of the people,<br> That they may bear witness:"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Q<u>a</u>loo aanta faAAalta h<u>atha</u> bi-<u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  They said, "Art thou<br> The one that did this<br> With our gods, O Abraham?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>la bal faAAalahu kabeeruhum h<u>atha</u> fa<b>i</b>s-aloohum in k<u>a</u>noo yan<u>t</u>iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  He said: "Nay, this<br> Was done by —<br> This is their biggest one!<br> Ask them, if they<br> Can speak intelligently!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  FarajaAAoo il<u>a</u> anfusihim faq<u>a</u>loo innakum antumu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  So they turned to themselves<br> And said, "Surely ye<br> Are the ones in the wrong!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Thumma nukisoo AAal<u>a</u> ruoosihim laqad AAalimta m<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i yan<u>t</u>iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Then were they confounded<br> With shame: (they said),<br> "Thou knowest full well that<br> These (idols) do not speak!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Q<u>a</u>la afataAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yanfaAAukum shay-an wal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urrukum   </p>
 <p>66.  (Abraham) said, "Do ye then<br> Worship, besides God,<br> Things that can neither<br> Be of any good to you<br> Nor do you harm?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Offin lakum walim<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  "Fie upon you, and upon<br> The things that ye worship<br> Besides God! Have ye<br> No sense?"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Q<u>a</u>loo <u>h</u>arriqoohu wa<b>o</b>n<u>s</u>uroo <u>a</u>lihatakum in kuntum f<u>a</u>AAileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  They said, "Burn him<br> And protect your gods,<br> If ye do (anything at all)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Quln<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ru koonee bardan wasal<u>a</u>man AAal<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  We said, "O Fire!<br> Be thou cool,<br> And (a means of) safety<br> For Abraham!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Waar<u>a</u>doo bihi kaydan fajaAAaln<u>a</u>humu al-akhsareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Then they sought a stratagem<br> Against him: but We<br> Made them the ones<br> That lost most!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wanajjayn<u>a</u>hu waloo<u>t</u>an il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i allatee b<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  But We delivered him<br> And (his nephew) Lūt<br> (And directed them) to the land<br> Which We have blessed<br> For the nations.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba n<u>a</u>filatan wakullan jaAAaln<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  And We bestowed on him Isaac<br>And, as an additional gift,<br> (A grandson), Jacob, and We<br> Made righteous men of every one<br> (Of them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum a-immatan yahdoona bi-amrin<u>a</u> waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayhim fiAAla alkhayr<u>a</u>ti wa-iq<u>a</u>ma a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa-eet<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ti wak<u>a</u>noo lan<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>bideen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>73.  And We made them<br> Leaders, guiding (men) by<br> Our Command, and We<br> Sent them inspiration<br> To do good deeds,<br> To establish regular prayers,<br> And to practise regular charity;<br> And they constantly served<br> Us (and Us only).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Waloo<u>t</u>an <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>ukman waAAilman wanajjayn<u>a</u>hu mina alqaryati allatee k<u>a</u>nat taAAmalu alkhab<u>a</u>-itha innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawma saw-in f<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  And to Lūt, too,<br> We gave Judgment and Knowledge,<br> And We saved him<br> From the town which practised<br> Abominations: truly they were<br> A people given to Evil,<br> A rebellious people.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Waadkhaln<u>a</u>hu fee ra<u>h</u>matin<u>a</u> innahu mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  And We admitted him<br> To Our Mercy: for he<br> Was one of the Righteous.</p> <div class="filenav">
