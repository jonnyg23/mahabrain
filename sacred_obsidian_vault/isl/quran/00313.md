 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Wa-i<u>th</u> ghadawta min ahlika tubawwi-o almu/mineena maq<u>a</u>AAida lilqit<u>a</u>li wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Remember that morning<br> Thou didst leave<br> Thy household (early)<br> To post the Faithful<br> At their stations for battle:<br> And God heareth<br> And knoweth all things:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  I<u>th</u> hammat <u>ta</u>-ifat<u>a</u>ni minkum an tafshal<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu waliyyuhum<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  Rememer two of your parties<br> Meditated cowardice;<br> But God was their protector,<br> And in God should the Faithful<br> (Ever) put their trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Walaqad na<u>s</u>arakumu All<u>a</u>hu bibadrin waantum a<u>th</u>illatun fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  God had helped you<br> At Badr, when ye were<br> A contemptible little force;<br> Then fear God; thus<br> May ye show your gratitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  I<u>th</u> taqoolu lilmu/mineena alan yakfiyakum an yumiddakum rabbukum bithal<u>a</u>thati <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>fin mina almal<u>a</u>-ikati munzaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  Remember thou saidst<br> To the Faithful: "Is it not enough<br> For you that God should help you<br> With three thousand angels<br> (Specially) sent down?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Bal<u>a</u> in ta<u>s</u>biroo watattaqoo waya/tookum min fawrihim h<u>atha</u> yumdidkum rabbukum bikhamsati <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>fin mina almal<u>a</u>-ikati musawwimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  "Yea,—if ye remain firm,<br> And act aright, even if<br> The enemy should rush here<br> On you in hot haste,<br> Your Lord would help you<br> With five thousand angels<br> Making a terrific onslaught."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Wam<u>a</u> jaAAalahu All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> bushr<u>a</u> lakum walita<u>t</u>ma-inna quloobukum bihi wam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nna<u>s</u>ru ill<u>a</u> min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  God made it but a message<br> Of hope for you, and an assurance<br> To your hearts: (in any case)<br> There is no help<br> Except from God,<br> The Exalted, the Wise:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Liyaq<u>t</u>aAAa <u>t</u>arafan mina alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo aw yakbitahum fayanqaliboo kh<u>a</u>-ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  That He might cut off<br> A fringe of the Unbelievers<br> Or expose them to infamy,<br> And they should then<br> Be turned back,<br> Frustrated of their purpose.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Laysa laka mina al-amri shay-on aw yatooba AAalayhim aw yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibahum fa-innahum <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>128.  Not for thee, (but for God),<br> Is the decision:<br> Whether He turn in mercy<br> To them, or punish them;<br> For they are indeed wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i yaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o wayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  To God belongeth all<br> That is in the heavens<br> And on earth.<br> He forgiveth whom He pleaseth<br> And punisheth whom He pleaseth<br> But God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
