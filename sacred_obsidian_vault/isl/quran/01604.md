 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Qad makara alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim faat<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu buny<u>a</u>nahum mina alqaw<u>a</u>AAidi fakharra AAalayhimu a<b>l</b>ssaqfu min fawqihim waat<u>a</u>humu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu min <u>h</u>aythu l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Those before them did also<br> Plot (against God's Way):<br> But God took their structures<br> From their foundations, and the roof<br> Fell down on them from above;<br> And the Wrath seized them<br> From directions they did not perceive.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Thumma yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati yukhzeehim wayaqoolu ayna shurak<u>a</u>-iya alla<u>th</u>eena kuntum tush<u>a</u>qqoona feehim q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma inna alkhizya alyawma wa<b>al</b>ssoo-a AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Then, on the Day of Judgment,<br> He will cover them<br>With shame, and say:<br> "Where are My "partners"<br> Concerning whom ye used<br> To dispute (with the godly)?"<br> Those endued with knowledge<br> Will say: "This Day, indeed,<br> Are the Unbelievers covered<br> With Shame and Misery,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Alla<u>th</u>eena tatawaff<u>a</u>humu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limee anfusihim faalqawoo a<b>l</b>ssalama m<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> naAAmalu min soo-in bal<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "(Namely) those whose lives the angels<br> Take in a state of wrong-doing<br> To their own souls."<br> Then would they offer submission<br> (With the pretence), "We did<br> No evil (knowingly)." (The angels<br> Will reply), "Nay, but verily<br> God knoweth all that ye did;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Fa<b>o</b>dkhuloo abw<u>a</u>ba jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> falabi/sa mathw<u>a</u> almutakabbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "So enter the gates of Hell,<br> To dwell therein.<br> Thus evil indeed<br> Is the abode of the arrogant."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Waqeela lilla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw m<u>atha</u> anzala rabbukum q<u>a</u>loo khayran lilla<u>th</u>eena a<u>h</u>sanoo fee h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asanatun walad<u>a</u>ru al-<u>a</u>khirati khayrun walaniAAma d<u>a</u>ru almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  To the righteous<br> (When) it is said, "What<br> Is it that your Lord<br> Has revealed?" they say,<br> "All that is good." To those<br> Who do good, there is good<br> In this world, and-the Home<br> Of the Hereafter is even better<br>And excellent indeed is the Home<br> Of the righteous,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Jann<u>a</u>tu AAadnin yadkhuloonah<u>a</u> tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru lahum feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>oona ka<u>tha</u>lika yajzee All<u>a</u>hu almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Gardens of Eternity which they<br> Will enter: beneath them<br> Flow (pleasant) rivers: they<br> Will have therein all<br> That they wish: thus doth<br> God reward the righteous,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Alla<u>th</u>eena tatawaff<u>a</u>humu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu <u>t</u>ayyibeena yaqooloona sal<u>a</u>mun AAalaykumu odkhuloo aljannata bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  (Namely) those whose lives<br> The angels take in a state<br> Of purity, saying (to them),<br> "Peace be on you; enter ye<br> The Garden, because of (the good)<br> Which ye did (in the world)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Hal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> an ta/tiyahumu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu aw ya/tiya amru rabbika ka<u>tha</u>lika faAAala alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamahumu All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Do the (ungodly) wait until<br> The angels come to them,<br> Or there comes the Command<br> Of thy Lord (for their doom)?<br> So did those who went<br> Before them. But God<br> Wronged them not: nay,<br> They wronged their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Faa<u>sa</u>bahum sayyi-<u>a</u>tu m<u>a</u> AAamiloo wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  But the evil results<br> Of their deeds overtook them,<br> And that very (Wrath)<br> At which they had scoffed<br> Hemmed them in.</p> <div class="filenav">
