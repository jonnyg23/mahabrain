 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Qul innee nuheetu an aAAbuda alla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi qul l<u>a</u> attabiAAu ahw<u>a</u>akum qad <u>d</u>alaltu i<u>th</u>an wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> mina almuhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Say: "I am forbidden<br> To worship those—others<br> Than God—whom ye<br> Call upon." Say: "I will<br> Not follow your vain desires:<br> If I did, I would stray<br> From the path, and be not<br> Of the company of those<br> Who receive guidance."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Qul innee AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin min rabbee waka<u>thth</u>abtum bihi m<u>a</u> AAindee m<u>a</u> tastaAAjiloona bihi ini al<u>h</u>ukmu ill<u>a</u> lill<u>a</u>hi yaqu<u>ss</u>u al<u>h</u>aqqa wahuwa khayru alf<u>as</u>ileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Say; "For me, I (work)<br> On a clear Sign from my Lord,<br> But ye reject Him. What ye<br> Would see hastened, is not<br> In my power. The Command<br> Rests with none but God:<br> He declares the Truth,<br> And He is the best of judges."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Qul law anna AAindee m<u>a</u> tastaAAjiloona bihi laqu<u>d</u>iya al-amru baynee wabaynakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bi<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Say: "If what ye would see<br> Hastened were in my power,<br> The matter would be settled<br> At once between you and me.<br>But God knoweth best<br> Those who do wrong."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  WaAAindahu maf<u>a</u>ti<u>h</u>u alghaybi l<u>a</u> yaAAlamuh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> huwa wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee albarri wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri wam<u>a</u> tasqu<u>t</u>u min waraqatin ill<u>a</u> yaAAlamuh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>abbatin fee <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> ra<u>t</u>bin wal<u>a</u> y<u>a</u>bisin ill<u>a</u> fee kit<u>a</u>bin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  With Him are the keys<br> Of the Unseen, the treasures<br> That none knoweth but He.<br> He knoweth whatever there is<br> On the earth and in the sea.<br> Not a leaf doth fall<br> But with His knowledge:<br> There is not a grain<br> In the darkness (or depths)<br> Of the earth, nor anything<br> Fresh or dry (green or withered),<br> But is (inscribed) in a Record<br> Clear (to those who can read).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee yatawaff<u>a</u>kum bi<b>a</b>llayli wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> jara<u>h</u>tum bi<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri thumma yabAAathukum feehi liyuq<u>da</u> ajalun musamman thumma ilayhi marjiAAukum thumma yunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  It is He Who doth take<br> Your souls by night,<br> And hath knowledge of all<br> That ye have done by day:<br> By day doth He raise<br> You up again; that a term<br> Appointed be fulfilled;<br> In the end unto Him<br> Will be your return;<br> Then will He show you<br> The truth of all<br> That ye did.</p> <div class="filenav">
