 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Walaqad <u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> fee h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni liya<u>thth</u>akkaroo wam<u>a</u> yazeeduhum ill<u>a</u> nufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  We have explained (things)<br> In various (ways) in this Qur-ān,<br> In order that they may receive<br> Admonition, but it only increases<br> Their flight (from the Truth)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Qul law k<u>a</u>na maAAahu <u>a</u>lihatun kam<u>a</u> yaqooloona i<u>th</u>an la<b>i</b>btaghaw il<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ee alAAarshi sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Say: if there had been<br> (Other) gods with Him,—<br> As they say,—behold,<br> They would certainly have<br> Sought out a way<br> To the Lord of the Throne!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Sub<u>ha</u>nahu wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yaqooloona AAuluwwan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Glory to Him! He is high<br> Above all that they say!—<br> Exalted and Great (beyond measure)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Tusabbi<u>h</u>u lahu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>ssabAAu wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u waman feehinna wa-in min shay-in ill<u>a</u> yusabbi<u>h</u>u bi<u>h</u>amdihi wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> tafqahoona tasbee<u>h</u>ahum innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>aleeman ghafoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  The seven heavens and the earth,<br> And all beings therein,<br> Declare His glory:<br> There is not a thing<br> But celebrates His praise;<br> And yet ye understand not<br> How they declare His glory!<br> Verily He is Oft-Forbearing,<br> Most Forgiving!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qara/ta alqur-<u>a</u>na jaAAaln<u>a</u> baynaka wabayna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ban mastoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>45.  When thou dost recite<br> The Qur-ān, We put,<br>Between thee and those who<br> Believe not to the Hereafter,<br> A veil invisible:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim akinnatan an yafqahoohu wafee <u>atha</u>nihim waqran wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>akarta rabbaka fee alqur-<u>a</u>ni wa<u>h</u>dahu wallaw AAal<u>a</u> adb<u>a</u>rihim nufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And We put coverings<br> Over their hearts (and minds)<br> Lest they should understand<br> The Our-ān, and deafness<br> Into their ears: when thou<br> Dost commemorate thy Lord—<br> And Him alone—in the Qur-ān,<br> They turn on their backs,<br> Fleeing (from the Truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Na<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yastamiAAoona bihi i<u>th</u> yastamiAAoona ilayka wa-i<u>th</u> hum najw<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> yaqoolu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona in tattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> rajulan mas<u>h</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  We know best why it is<br> They listen, when they listen<br> To thee; and when they<br> Meet in private conference,<br> Behold, the wicked say,<br> "Ye follow none other than<br> A man bewitched!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  On<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa <u>d</u>araboo laka al-amth<u>a</u>la fa<u>d</u>alloo fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  See what similes they strike<br> For thee: but they have gone<br> Astray, and never can they<br> Find a way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waq<u>a</u>loo a-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man waruf<u>a</u>tan a-inn<u>a</u> lamabAAoothoona khalqan jadeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  They say: "What!<br> When we are reduced<br>To bones and dust,<br> Should we really be raised up<br> (To be) a new creation?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Qul koonoo <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratan aw <u>h</u>adeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Say: "(Nay!) be ye<br> Stones or iron,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Aw khalqan mimm<u>a</u> yakburu fee <u>s</u>udoorikum fasayaqooloona man yuAAeedun<u>a</u> quli alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>arakum awwala marratin fasayunghi<u>d</u>oona ilayka ruoosahum wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> huwa qul AAas<u>a</u> an yakoona qareeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "Or created matter<br> Which, in your minds,<br> Is hardest (to be raised up),<br> (Yet shall ye be raised up)!"<br> Then will they say:<br> "Who will cause us<br> To return?" Say: "He<br> Who created you first!"<br> Then will they wag<br> Their heads towards thee,<br> And say, "When will<br> That be?" Say, "May be<br> It will be quite soon!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Yawma yadAAookum fatastajeeboona bi<u>h</u>amdihi wata<i><u>th</u></i>unnoona in labithtum ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "It will be on a Day<br> When He will call you,<br> And ye will answer<br> (His call) with (words<br> Of) His praise, and ye<br> Will think that ye tarried<br> But a little while!</p> <div class="filenav">
