 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 18</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_172.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>172.  Alla<u>th</u>eena istaj<u>a</u>boo lill<u>a</u>hi wa<b>al</b>rrasooli min baAAdi m<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bahumu alqar<u>h</u>u lilla<u>th</u>eena a<u>h</u>sanoo minhum wa<b>i</b>ttaqaw ajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>172.  Of those who answered<br> The call of God<br> And the Apostle,<br> Even after being wounded,<br> Those who do right<br> And refrain from wrong<br> Have a great reward;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_173.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>173.  Alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>la lahumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su inna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa qad jamaAAoo lakum fa<b>i</b>khshawhum faz<u>a</u>dahum eem<u>a</u>nan waq<u>a</u>loo <u>h</u>asbun<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu waniAAma alwakeel<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>173.  Men said to them:<br> "A great army is gathering<br> Against you":<br> And frightened them:<br> But it (only) increased<br> Their Faith: they said:<br> "For us God sufficeth,<br> And He is the best<br> Disposer of affairs."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_174.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>174.  Fa<b>i</b>nqalaboo biniAAmatin mina All<u>a</u>hi wafa<u>d</u>lin lam yamsas-hum soo-on wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo ri<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>174.  And they returned<br> With Grace and Bounty<br> From God: no harm<br> Ever touched them:<br> For they followed<br> The good pleasure of God:<br> And God is the Lord<br> Of bounties unbounded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_175.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>175.  Innam<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu yukhawwifu awliy<u>a</u>ahu fal<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>foohum wakh<u>a</u>fooni in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>175.  It is only the Evil One<br> That suggests to you<br> The fear of his votaries:<br> Be ye not afraid<br> Of them, but fear Me,<br> If ye have Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_176.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>176.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>zunka alla<u>th</u>eena yus<u>a</u>riAAoona fee alkufri innahum lan ya<u>d</u>urroo All<u>a</u>ha shay-an yureedu All<u>a</u>hu all<u>a</u> yajAAala lahum <u>h</u>a<i><u>thth</u></i>an fee al-<u>a</u>khirati walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>176.  Let not those grieve thee<br> Who rush headlong<br> Into Unbelief:<br> Not the least harm<br> Will they do to God:<br> God's Plan is that He<br>Will give them no portion<br> In the Hereafter,<br> But a severe punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_177.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>177.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena ishtarawoo alkufra bi<b>a</b>l-eem<u>a</u>ni lan ya<u>d</u>urroo All<u>a</u>ha shay-an walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>177.  Those who purchase<br> Unbelief at the price<br> Of faith,<br> Not the least harm<br> Will they do to God,<br> But they will have<br> A grievous punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_178.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>178.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>sabanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo annam<u>a</u> numlee lahum khayrun li-anfusihim innam<u>a</u> numlee lahum liyazd<u>a</u>doo ithman walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>178.  Let not the Unbelievers<br> Think that Our respite<br> To them is good for themselves:<br> We grant them respite<br> That they may grow<br> In their iniquity:<br> But they will have<br> A shameful punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_179.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>179.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liya<u>th</u>ara almu/mineena AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> antum AAalayhi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yameeza alkhabeetha mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyibi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liyu<u>t</u>liAAakum AAal<u>a</u> alghaybi wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha yajtabee min rusulihi man yash<u>a</u>o fa<u>a</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warusulihi wa-in tu/minoo watattaqoo falakum ajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>179.  God will not leave<br> The Believers in the state<br> In which ye are now,<br> Until He separates<br> What is evil<br> From what is good.<br> Nor will He disclose<br> To you the secrets<br> Of the Unseen,<br> But He chooses<br> Of His Apostles<br> (For the purpose)<br> Whom He pleases.<br> So believe in God<br> And His Apostles:<br> And if ye believe<br> And do right,<br> Ye have a reward<br> Without measure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_180.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>180.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>sabanna alla<u>th</u>eena yabkhaloona bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hummu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi huwa khayran lahum bal huwa sharrun lahum sayu<u>t</u>awwaqoona m<u>a</u> bakhiloo bihi yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati walill<u>a</u>hi meer<u>a</u>thu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>180.  And let not those<br> Who covetously withhold<br> Of the gifts which God<br> Hath given them of His Grace,<br> Think that it is good for them:<br> Nay, it will be the worse<br> For them: soon shall the things<br> Which they covetously withheld<br> Be tied to their necks<br> Like a twisted collar,<br> On the Day of Judgment.<br> To God belongs the heritage<br> Of the heavens and the earth;<br> And God is well-acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <div class="filenav">
