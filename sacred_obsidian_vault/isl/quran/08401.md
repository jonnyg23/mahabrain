 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXIV.: Inshiqāq, or The Rending Asunder.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  I<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o inshaqqat   </p>
 <p>1.  When the Sky is<br> Rent asunder,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Waa<u>th</u>inat lirabbih<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>uqqat   </p>
 <p>2.  And hearkens to<br> (The Command of) its Lord,—<br> And it must needs<br> (Do so);—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> al-ar<u>d</u>u muddat   </p>
 <p>3.  And when the Earth<br> Is flattened out,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Waalqat m<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> watakhallat   </p>
 <p>4.  And casts forth<br> What is within it<br> And becomes (clean) empty,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Waa<u>th</u>inat lirabbih<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>uqqat   </p>
 <p>5.  And hearkens to<br> (The Command of) its Lord,—<br> And it must needs<br> (Do so);—(then will come<br> Home the full Reality)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu innaka k<u>a</u>di<u>h</u>un il<u>a</u> rabbika kad<u>h</u>an famul<u>a</u>qeeh<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  O thou man!<br> Verily thou art ever<br> Toiling on towards thy Lord—<br> Painfully toiling,—but thou<br> Shalt meet Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Faam<u>a</u> man ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu biyameenih<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Then he who is given<br> His Record in his<br> Right hand,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fasawfa yu<u>ha</u>sabu <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>ban yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Soon will his account<br> Be taken by an easy reckoning,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wayanqalibu il<u>a</u> ahlihi masroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And he will turn<br> To his people, rejoicing!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waamm<u>a</u> man ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu war<u>a</u>a <i><u>th</u></i>ahrih<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  But he who is given<br> His Record behind his back,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fasawfa yadAAoo thuboor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Soon will he cry<br> For Perdition,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Waya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u> saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>12.  And he will enter<br> A Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Innahu k<u>a</u>na fee ahlihi masroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Truly, did he go about<br> Among his people, rejoicing!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Innahu <i><u>th</u></i>anna an lan ya<u>h</u>oor<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Truly, did he think<br> That he would not<br> Have to return (to Us)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Bal<u>a</u> inna rabbahu k<u>a</u>na bihi ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Nay, nay! for his Lord<br> Was (ever) watchful of him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Fal<u>a</u> oqsimu bi<b>al</b>shshafaq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  So I do call<br> To witness the ruddy glow<br> Of Sunset;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli wam<u>a</u> wasaq<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  The Night and its Homing;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wa<b>a</b>lqamari i<u>tha</u> ittasaq<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  And the Moon<br> In her Fulness:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Latarkabunna <u>t</u>abaqan AAan <u>t</u>abaq<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Ye shall surely travel<br> From stage to stage</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Fam<u>a</u> lahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  What then is the matter<br> With them, that they<br> Believe not?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> quri-a AAalayhimu alqur-<u>a</u>nu l<u>a</u> yasjudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And when the Qur-ān<br> Is read to them, they<br> Fall not prostrate,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Bali alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  But on the contrary<br> The Unbelievers reject (it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yooAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  But God has full Knowledge<br> Of what they secrete<br> (In their breasts).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fabashshirhum biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  So announce to them<br> A Penalty Grievous,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/084/084_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum ajrun ghayru mamnoon<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>25.  Except to those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds:<br> For them is a Reward<br> That will never fail.</p>  <div class="filenav">
