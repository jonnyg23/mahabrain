 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  BadeeAAu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i ann<u>a</u> yakoonu lahu waladun walam takun lahu <u>sah</u>ibatun wakhalaqa kulla shay-in wahuwa bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  To Him is due<br> The primal origin<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br> How can He have a son<br> When He hath no consort?<br> He created all things,<br> And He hath full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  <u>Tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu rabbukum l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa kh<u>a</u>liqu kulli shay-in fa<b>o</b>AAbudoohu wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in wakeel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  That is God, your Lord!<br> There is no god but He,<br> The Creator of all things:<br> Then worship ye Him:<br> And He hath power<br> To dispose of all affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  L<u>a</u> tudrikuhu al-ab<u>sa</u>ru wahuwa yudriku al-ab<u>sa</u>ra wahuwa alla<u>t</u>eefu alkhabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  No vision can grasp Him,<br> But His grasp is over<br>All vision: He is<br> Above all comprehension,<br> Yet is acquainted with all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Qad j<u>a</u>akum ba<u>sa</u>-iru min rabbikum faman ab<u>s</u>ara falinafsihi waman AAamiya faAAalayh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> AAalaykum bi<u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  "Now have come to you,<br> From your Lord, proofs<br> (To open your eyes):<br> If any will see,<br> It will be for (the good<br> Of) his own soul;<br> If any will be blind,<br> It will be to his own<br> (Harm): I am not (here)<br> To watch over your doings.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika nu<u>s</u>arrifu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti waliyaqooloo darasta walinubayyinahu liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  Thus do We explain<br> The Signs by various (symbols):<br> That they may say,<br> "Thou hast taught (us) diligently,"<br> And that We may make<br> The matter clear<br> To those who know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  IttabiAA m<u>a</u> oo<u>h</u>iya ilayka min rabbika l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa waaAAri<u>d</u> AAani almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  Follow what thou art taught<br> By inspiration from thy Lord:<br> There is no god but He:<br> And turn aside from those<br> Who join gods with God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> ashrakoo wam<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u>ka AAalayhim <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i>an wam<u>a</u> anta AAalayhim biwakeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  If it had been God's Plan,<br> They would not have taken<br> False gods: but We<br> Made thee not one<br> To watch over their doings,<br> Nor art thou set<br> Over them to dispose<br> Of their affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Wal<u>a</u> tasubboo alla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi fayasubboo All<u>a</u>ha AAadwan bighayri AAilmin ka<u>tha</u>lika zayyann<u>a</u> likulli ommatin AAamalahum thumma il<u>a</u> rabbihim marjiAAuhum fayunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Revile not ye<br> Those whom they call upon<br> Besides God, lest<br> They out of spite<br> Revile God<br> In their ignorance.<br> Thus have We made<br> Alluring to each people'<br> Its own doings.<br> In the end will they<br> Return to their Lord,<br> And We shall then<br>Tell them the truth<br> Of all that they did.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Waaqsamoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi jahda aym<u>a</u>nihim la-in j<u>a</u>at-hum <u>a</u>yatun layu/minunna bih<u>a</u> qul innam<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> yushAAirukum annah<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>at l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  They swear their strongest<br> Oaths by God, that if<br> A (special) Sign came<br> To them, by it they would<br> Believe. Say: "Certainly<br> (All) Signs are in the power<br> Of God: but what will<br> Make you (Muslims) realise<br> That (even) if (special) Signs<br> Came, they will not believe."?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Wanuqallibu af-idatahum waab<u>sa</u>rahum kam<u>a</u> lam yu/minoo bihi awwala marratin wana<u>th</u>aruhum fee <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nihim yaAAmahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  We (too) shall turn<br> To (confusion) their hearts<br> And their eyes, even as they<br> Refused to believe in this<br> In the first instance:<br> We shall leave them<br> In their trespasses,<br> To wander in distraction.</p> <div class="filenav">
