 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Faarsala firAAawnu fee almad<u>a</u>-ini <u>ha</u>shireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  By inspiration We told Moses:<br> "Travel by night with<br> My servants; for surely<br> Ye shall be pursued."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i lashir<u>th</u>imatun qaleeloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Then Pharaoh sent heralds<br> To (all) the Cities,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wa-innahum lan<u>a</u> lagh<u>a</u>-i<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  (Saying): "These (Israelites)<br> Are but a small band,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> lajameeAAun <u>hath</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  "And they are raging<br> Furiously against us;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Faakhrajn<u>a</u>hum min jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  "But we are a multitude<br> Amply fore-warned."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wakunoozin wamaq<u>a</u>min kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  So We expelled them<br> From gardens, springs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika waawrathn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Treasures, and every kind<br> Of honourable position;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  FaatbaAAoohum mushriqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Thus it was, but<br> We made the Children<br> Of Israel inheritors<br> Of such things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Falamm<u>a</u> tar<u>a</u>a aljamAA<u>a</u>ni q<u>a</u>la a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu moos<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> lamudrakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  So they pursued them<br> At sunrise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Q<u>a</u>la kall<u>a</u> inna maAAiya rabbee sayahdeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  And when the two bodies<br> Saw each other, the people<br> Of Moses said: "We are<br> Sure to be overtaken."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Faaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> ani i<u>d</u>rib biAAa<u>sa</u>ka alba<u>h</u>ra fa<b>i</b>nfalaqa fak<u>a</u>na kullu firqin ka<b>al</b><u>tt</u>awdi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  (Moses) said: "By no means!<br> My Lord is with me!<br> Soon will He guide me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Waazlafn<u>a</u> thamma al-<u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Then We told Moses<br> By inspiration: "Strike<br> The sea with thy rod."<br> So it divided, and each<br> Separate part became<br> Like the huge, firm mass<br> Of a mountain.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Waanjayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> waman maAAahu ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  And We made the other<br> Party approach thither.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Thumma aghraqn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  We delivered Moses and all<br> Who were with him;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  But We drowned the others.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Verily in this is a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Wa<b>o</b>tlu AAalayhim nabaa ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
