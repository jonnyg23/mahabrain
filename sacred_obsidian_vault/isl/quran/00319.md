 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_181.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>181.  Laqad samiAAa All<u>a</u>hu qawla alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inna All<u>a</u>ha faqeerun wana<u>h</u>nu aghniy<u>a</u>on sanaktubu m<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo waqatlahumu al-anbiy<u>a</u>a bighayri <u>h</u>aqqin wanaqoolu <u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba al<u>h</u>areeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>181.  God hath heard<br> The taunt of those<br> Who say: "Truly, God<br> Is indigent and we<br> Are rich!"—We shall<br> Certainly record their word<br>And (their act) of slaying<br> The Prophets in defiance<br> Of right, and We shall say:<br> "Taste ye the Penalty<br> Of the Scorching Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_182.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>182.  <u>Tha</u>lika bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeekum waanna All<u>a</u>ha laysa bi<i><u>th</u></i>all<u>a</u>min lilAAabeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>182.  "This is because<br> Of the (unrighteous deeds)<br> Which your hands<br> Sent on before ye:<br> For God never harms<br> Those who serve Him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_183.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>183.  Alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inna All<u>a</u>ha AAahida ilayn<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> nu/mina lirasoolin <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/tiyan<u>a</u> biqurb<u>a</u>nin ta/kuluhu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru qul qad j<u>a</u>akum rusulun min qablee bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti wabi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee qultum falima qataltumoohum in kutum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>183.  They (also) said: "God took<br> Our promise not to believe<br> In an apostle unless<br> He showed us a sacrifice<br> Consumed by fire<br> (From heaven)." Say:<br> "There came to you<br> Apostles before me,<br> With Clear Signs<br> And even with what<br> Ye ask for: why then<br> Did ye slay them,<br> If ye speak the truth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_184.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>184.  Fa-in ka<u>thth</u>abooka faqad ku<u>thth</u>iba rusulun min qablika j<u>a</u>oo bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b>zzuburi wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi almuneer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>184.  Then if they reject thee,<br> So were rejected apostles<br> Before thee, who came<br> With Clear Signs,<br> Books of dark prophecies,<br> And the Book of Enlightenment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_185.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>185.  Kullu nafsin <u>tha</u>-iqatu almawti wa-innam<u>a</u> tuwaffawna ojoorakum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati faman zu<u>h</u>zi<u>h</u>a AAani a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri waodkhila aljannata faqad f<u>a</u>za wam<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAu alghuroor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>185.  Every soul shall have<br> A taste of death:<br> And only on the Day<br> Of Judgment shall you<br> Be paid your full recompense.<br> Only he who is saved<br> Far from the Fire<br> And admitted to the Garden<br> Will have attained<br> The object (of Life):<br> For the life of this world<br> Is but goods and chattels<br> Of deception.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_186.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>186.  Latublawunna fee amw<u>a</u>likum waanfusikum walatasmaAAunna mina alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablikum wamina alla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo a<u>th</u>an katheeran wa-in ta<u>s</u>biroo watattaqoo fa-inna <u>tha</u>lika min AAazmi al-omoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>186.  Ye shall certainly<br> Be tried and tested<br> In your possessions<br> And in your personal selves;<br> And ye shall certainly<br> Hear much that will grieve you,<br> From those who received<br> The Book before yot:<br> And from those who<br> Worship many gods.<br> But if ye persevere<br> Patiently, and guard<br> Against evil,—then<br> That will be<br> A determining factor<br> In all affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_187.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>187.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu meeth<u>a</u>qa alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba latubayyinunnahu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u> taktumoonahu fanaba<u>th</u>oohu war<u>a</u>a <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorihim wa<b>i</b>shtaraw bihi thamanan qaleelan fabi/sa m<u>a</u> yashtaroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>187.  And remember<br> God took a Covenant<br> From the People of the Book,<br> To make it known<br> And clear to mankind,<br> And not to hide it;<br>But they threw it away<br> Behind their backs,<br> And purchased with it<br> Some miserable gain!<br> And vile was the bargain<br> They made!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_188.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>188.  L<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>sabanna alla<u>th</u>eena yafra<u>h</u>oona bim<u>a</u> ataw wayu<u>h</u>ibboona an yu<u>h</u>madoo bim<u>a</u> lam yafAAaloo fal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>sabannahum bimaf<u>a</u>zatin mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>188.  Think not that those<br> Who exult in what they<br> Have brought about, and love<br> To be praised for what<br> They have not done,—<br> Think not that they<br> Can escape the Penalty.<br> For them is a Penalty<br> Grievous indeed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_189.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>189.  Walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>189.  To God belongeth<br> The dominion<br> Of the heavens<br> And the earth;<br> And God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
