 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 26</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_211.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>211.  Sal banee isr<u>a</u>-eela kam <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum min <u>a</u>yatin bayyinatin waman yubaddil niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-hu fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>211.  Ask the Children of Israel<br> How many Clear (Signs)<br>We have sent them.<br> But if any one,<br> After God's favour<br> Has come to him,<br> Substitutes (something else),<br> God is strict in punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_212.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>212.  Zuyyina lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wayaskharoona mina alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw fawqahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yarzuqu man yash<u>a</u>o bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>212.  The life of this world<br> Is alluring to those<br> Who reject faith,<br> And they scoff at those<br> Who believe.<br> But the righteous<br> Will be above them<br> On the Day of Resurrection;<br> For God bestows His abundance<br> Without measure<br> On whom He will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_213.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>213.  K<u>a</u>na a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan fabaAAatha All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena mubashshireena wamun<u>th</u>ireena waanzala maAAahumu alkit<u>a</u>ba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi liya<u>h</u>kuma bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si feem<u>a</u> ikhtalafoo feehi wam<u>a</u> ikhtalafa feehi ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoohu min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu albayyin<u>a</u>tu baghyan baynahum fahad<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo lim<u>a</u> ikhtalafoo feehi mina al<u>h</u>aqqi bi-i<u>th</u>nihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yahdee man yash<u>a</u>o il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>213.  Mankind was one single nation,<br> And God sent Messengers<br> With glad tidings and warnings;<br> And with them He sent<br> The Book in truth,<br> To judge between people<br> In matters wherein<br> They differed;<br> But the People of the Book,<br> After the clear Signs<br> Came to them, did not differ<br> Among themselves,<br> Except through selfish contumacy.<br> God by His Grace<br> Guided the Believers<br> To the Truth,<br> Concerning that<br> Wherein they differed.<br> For God guides<br> Whom He will<br> To a path<br> That is straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_214.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>214.  Am <u>h</u>asibtum an tadkhuloo aljannata walamm<u>a</u> ya/tikum mathalu alla<u>th</u>eena khalaw min qablikum massat-humu alba/s<u>a</u>o wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>arr<u>a</u>o wazulziloo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yaqoola a<b>l</b>rrasoolu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu mat<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>ru All<u>a</u>hi al<u>a</u> inna na<u>s</u>ra All<u>a</u>hi qareeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>214.  Or do ye think<br> That ye shall enter<br>The Garden (of Bliss)<br> Without such (trials)<br> As came to those<br> Who passed away<br> Before you?<br> They encountered<br> Suffering and adversity,<br> And were so shaken in spirit<br> That even the Apostle<br> And those of faith<br> Who were with him<br> Cried: "When (will come)<br> The help of God?"<br> Ah! Verily, the help of God<br> Is (always) near!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_215.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>215.  Yas-aloonaka m<u>atha</u> yunfiqoona qul m<u>a</u> anfaqtum min khayrin falilw<u>a</u>lidayni wa<b>a</b>l-aqrabeena wa<b>a</b>lyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keeni wa<b>i</b>bni a<b>l</b>ssabeeli wam<u>a</u> tafAAaloo min khayrin fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha bihi AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>215.  They ask thee<br> What they should spend<br> (In charity). Say: Whatever<br> Ye spend that is good,<br> Is for parents and kindred<br> And orphans<br> And those in want<br> And for wayfarers.<br> And whatever ye do<br> That is good,—God<br> Knoweth it well.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_216.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>216.  Kutiba AAalaykumu alqit<u>a</u>lu wahuwa kurhun lakum waAAas<u>a</u> an takrahoo shay-an wahuwa khayrun lakum waAAas<u>a</u> an tu<u>h</u>ibboo shay-an wahuwa sharrun lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu waantum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>216.  Fighting is prescribed<br> For you, and ye dislike it.<br> But it is possible<br> That ye dislike a thing<br> Which is good for you,<br> And that ye love a thing<br> Which is bad for you.<br> But God knoweth,<br> And ye know not.</p> <div class="filenav">
