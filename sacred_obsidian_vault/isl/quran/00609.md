 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Qul anadAAoo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yanfaAAun<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urrun<u>a</u> wanuraddu AAal<u>a</u> aAAq<u>a</u>bin<u>a</u> baAAda i<u>th</u> had<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee<u> </u>istahwat-hu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eenu fee al-ar<u>d</u>i <u>h</u>ayr<u>a</u>na lahu a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bun yadAAoonahu il<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> i/tin<u>a</u> qul inna hud<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi huwa alhud<u>a</u> waomirn<u>a</u> linuslima lirabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>71.  Say: "Shall we indeed<br> Call on others besides God,<br> Things that can do us<br> Neither good nor harm,—<br> And turn on our heels<br> After receiving guidance<br> From God?—like one<br> Whom the evil ones<br> Have made into a fool,<br> Wandering bewildered<br> Through the earth, his friends<br> Calling 'Come to us',<br> (Vainly) guiding him to the Path."<br>Say: "God's guidance<br> Is the (only) guidance,<br> And we have been directed<br> To submit ourselves<br> To the Lord of the worlds;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Waan aqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<b>i</b>ttaqoohu wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  "To establish regular prayers<br> And to fear God:<br> For it is to Him<br> That we shall be<br> Gathered together."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wayawma yaqoolu kun fayakoonu qawluhu al<u>h</u>aqqu walahu almulku yawma yunfakhu fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori AA<u>a</u>limu alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati wahuwa al<u>h</u>akeemu alkhabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  It is He Who created<br> The heavens and the earth<br> In true (proportions):<br> The day He saith, "Be,"<br>Behold! it is. His Word<br> Is the Truth. His will be<br> The dominion the day<br> The trumpet will be blown.<br> He knoweth the Unseen<br> As well as that which is<br> Open. For He<br> Is the Wise, well acquainted<br> (With all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu li-abeehi <u>a</u>zara atattakhi<u>th</u>u a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>man <u>a</u>lihatan innee ar<u>a</u>ka waqawmaka fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Lo! Abraham said<br> To his father Āzar:<br> "Takest thou idols for gods?<br> For I see thee<br> And thy people<br> In manifest error."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika nuree ibr<u>a</u>heema malakoota a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i waliyakoona mina almooqineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  So also did We show<br> Abraham the power<br> And the laws of the heavens<br> And the earth, that he<br> Might (with understanding)<br> Have certitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Falamm<u>a</u> janna AAalayhi allaylu ra<u>a</u> kawkaban q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> rabbee falamm<u>a</u> afala q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> o<u>h</u>ibbu al-<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>76.  When the night<br> Covered him over,<br> He saw a star:<br> He said: "This is my Lord."<br> But when it set,<br> He said: "I love not<br> Those that set."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Falamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u> alqamara b<u>a</u>zighan q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> rabbee falamm<u>a</u> afala q<u>a</u>la la-in lam yahdinee rabbee laakoonanna mina alqawmi a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  When he saw the moon<br> Rising in splendour,<br> He said: "This is my Lord."<br> But when the moon set,<br>He said: "Unless my Lord<br> Guide me, I shall surely<br> Be among those<br> Who go astray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Falamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshamsa b<u>a</u>zighatan q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> rabbee h<u>atha</u> akbaru falamm<u>a</u> afalat q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi innee baree-on mimm<u>a</u> tushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  When he saw the sun<br> Rising in splendour,<br> He said: "This is my Lord;<br> This is the greatest (of all)."<br> But when the sun set,<br> He said: "O my people!<br> I am indeed free<br> From your (guilt)<br> Of giving partners to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Innee wajjahtu wajhiya lilla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>ara a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a <u>h</u>aneefan wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  "For me, I have set<br> My face, firmly and truly,<br> Towards Him Who created<br> The heavens and the earth,<br> And never shall I give<br> Partners to God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Wa<u>ha</u>jjahu qawmuhu q<u>a</u>la atu<u>ha</u>jjoonnee fee All<u>a</u>hi waqad had<u>a</u>ni wal<u>a</u> akh<u>a</u>fu m<u>a</u> tushrikoona bihi ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a rabbee shay-an wasiAAa rabbee kulla shay-in AAilman afal<u>a</u> tata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  His people disputed<br> With him. He said:<br> "(Come) ye to dispute<br> With me, about God,<br> When He (Himself)<br> Hath guided me?<br> I fear not (the beings)<br> Ye associate with God:<br>Unless my Lord willeth,<br> (Nothing can happen).<br> My Lord comprehendeth<br> In His knowledge all things.<br> Will ye not (yourselves)<br> Be admonished?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wakayfa akh<u>a</u>fu m<u>a</u> ashraktum wal<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>foona annakum ashraktum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> lam yunazzil bihi AAalaykum sul<u>ta</u>nan faayyu alfareeqayni a<u>h</u>aqqu bi<b>a</b>l-amni in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "How should I fear<br> (The beings) ye associate<br> With God, when ye<br> Fear not to give partners<br> To God without any warrant<br> Having been given to you?<br> Which of (us) two parties<br> Hath more right to security?<br> (Tell me) if ye know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo walam yalbisoo eem<u>a</u>nahum bi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmin ol<u>a</u>-ika lahumu al-amnu wahum muhtadoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>82.  "It is those who believe<br> And confuse not their beliefs<br> With wrong—that are<br> (Truly) in security, for they<br> Are on (right) guidance."</p> <div class="filenav">
