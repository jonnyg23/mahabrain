 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wakam qa<u>s</u>amn<u>a</u> min qaryatin k<u>a</u>nat <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limatan waansha/n<u>a</u> baAAdah<u>a</u> qawman <u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  How many were the populations<br> We utterly destroyed because<br> Of their iniquities, setting up<br> In their places other peoples?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Falamm<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>assoo ba/san<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> hum minh<u>a</u> yarku<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Yet, when they felt<br> Our Punishment (coming),<br> Behold, they (tried to) flee<br> From it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  L<u>a</u> tarku<u>d</u>oo wa<b>i</b>rjiAAoo il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> otriftum feehi wamas<u>a</u>kinikum laAAallakum tus-aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Flee not, but return to<br> The good things of this life<br> Which were given you,<br> And to your homes,<br> In order that ye may<br> Be called to account</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> waylan<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  They said: "Ah! woe to us!<br> We were indeed wrong-doers!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Fam<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>lat tilka daAAw<u>a</u>hum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u>hum <u>h</u>a<u>s</u>eedan kh<u>a</u>mideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  And that cry of theirs<br> Ceased not, till we made<br>Them as a field<br> That is mown, as ashes<br> Silent and quenched.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>AAibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Not for (idle) sport did We<br> Create the heavens and the earth<br> And all that is between!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Law aradn<u>a</u> an nattakhi<u>th</u>a lahwan la<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hu min ladunn<u>a</u> in kunn<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>AAileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  If it had been Our wish<br> To take (just) a pastime,<br> We should surely have taken<br> It from the things nearest<br> To Us, if We would<br> Do (such a thing)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Bal naq<u>th</u>ifu bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi AAal<u>a</u> alb<u>at</u>ili fayadmaghuhu fa-i<u>tha</u> huwa z<u>a</u>hiqun walakumu alwaylu mimm<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Nay, We hurl the Truth<br> Against falsehood, and it knocks<br> Out its brain, and behold,<br> Falsehood doth perish!<br> Ah! woe be to you<br> For the (false) things<br> Ye ascribe (to Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Walahu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i waman AAindahu l<u>a</u> yastakbiroona AAan AAib<u>a</u>datihi wal<u>a</u> yasta<u>h</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  To Him belong all (creatures)<br> In the heavens and on earth:<br> Even those who are in His<br> (Very) Presence are not<br> Too proud to serve Him,<br> Nor are they (ever) weary<br> (Of His service):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Yusabbi<u>h</u>oona allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra l<u>a</u> yafturoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  They celebrate His praises<br> Night and day, nor do they<br> Ever flag or intermit.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Ami ittakha<u>th</u>oo <u>a</u>lihatan mina al-ar<u>d</u>i hum yunshiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Or have they taken<br> (For worship) gods from the earth<br> Who can raise (the dead)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Law k<u>a</u>na feehim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>lihatun ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu lafasadat<u>a</u> fasub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAAarshi AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  If there were, in the heavens<br> And the earth, other gods<br> Besides God, there would<br> Have been confusion in both!<br> But glory to God,<br> The Lord of the Throne:<br> (High is He) above<br> What they attribute to Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  L<u>a</u> yus-alu AAamm<u>a</u> yafAAalu wahum yus-aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  He cannot be questioned<br> For His acts, but they<br> Will be questioned (for theirs).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Ami ittakha<u>th</u>oo min doonihi <u>a</u>lihatan qul h<u>a</u>too burh<u>a</u>nakum h<u>atha</u> <u>th</u>ikru man maAAiya wa<u>th</u>ikru man qablee bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona al<u>h</u>aqqa fahum muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Or have they taken<br> For worship (other) gods<br> Besides him? Say, "Bring<br> Your convincing proof: this<br> Is the Message of those<br> With me and the Message<br> Of those before me."<br> But most of them know not<br> The Truth, and so turn away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>ee ilayhi annahu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>AAbudoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Not an apostle did We<br> Send before thee without<br> This inspiration sent by Us<br> To him: that there is<br> No god but I; therefore<br> Worship and serve Me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Waq<u>a</u>loo ittakha<u>th</u>a a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu waladan sub<u>ha</u>nahu bal AAib<u>a</u>dun mukramoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And they say: "(God)<br> Most Gracious has begotten<br> Offspring." Glory to Him!<br> They are (but) servants raised<br> To honour.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  L<u>a</u> yasbiqoonahu bi<b>a</b>lqawli wahum bi-amrihi yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  They speak not before<br> He speaks, and they act<br> (In all things) by His command.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  YaAAlamu m<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wam<u>a</u> khalfahum wal<u>a</u> yashfaAAoona ill<u>a</u> limani irta<u>da</u> wahum min khashyatihi mushfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  He knows what is before them,<br> And what is behind them,<br> And they offer no intercession<br> Except for those who are<br> Acceptable, and they stand<br> In awe and reverence<br> Of His (glory).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Waman yaqul minhum innee il<u>a</u>hun min doonihi fa<u>tha</u>lika najzeehi jahannama ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  If any of them should say,<br> "I am a god besides Him",<br> Such a one We should<br> Reward with Hell: thus<br> Do We reward those<br> Who do wrong.</p> <div class="filenav">
