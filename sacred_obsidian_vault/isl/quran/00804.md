 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>m<u>a</u>noo in tattaqoo All<u>a</u>ha yajAAal lakum furq<u>a</u>nan wayukaffir AAankum sayyi-<u>a</u>tikum wayaghfir lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  O ye who believe!<br> If ye fear God,<br> He will grant you a Criterion<br> (To judge between right and wrong),<br> Remove from you (all) evil<br> (That may afflict) you,<br> And forgive you:<br> For God is the Lord<br> Of grace unbounded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wa-i<u>th</u> yamkuru bika alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo liyuthbitooka aw yaqtulooka aw yukhrijooka wayamkuroona wayamkuru All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khayru alm<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Remember how the Unbelievers<br> Plotted against thee, to keep<br> Thee in bonds, or slay thee,<br> Or get thee out (of thy home).<br> They plot and plan,<br> And God too plans,<br> But the best of planners<br> Is God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo qad samiAAn<u>a</u> law nash<u>a</u>o laquln<u>a</u> mithla h<u>atha</u> in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  When Our Signs are rehearsed<br> To them, they say: "We<br> Have heard this (before):<br> If we wished, we could<br> Say (words) like these:<br> These are nothing<br> But tales of the ancients."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>loo all<u>a</u>humma in k<u>a</u>na h<u>atha</u> huwa al<u>h</u>aqqa min AAindika faam<u>t</u>ir AAalayn<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i awi i/tin<u>a</u> biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Remember how they said:<br> "O God! if this is indeed<br> The Truth from Thee,<br> Rain down on as a shower<br> Of stones from the sky,<br> Or send us a grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liyuAAa<u>thth</u>ibahum waanta feehim wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu muAAa<u>thth</u>ibahum wahum yastaghfiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  But God was not going<br> To send them a Penalty<br> Whilst thou wast amongst them;<br> Nor was He going to send it<br> Whilst they could ask for pardon</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wam<u>a</u> lahum all<u>a</u> yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibahumu All<u>a</u>hu wahum ya<u>s</u>uddoona AAani almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo awliy<u>a</u>ahu in awliy<u>a</u>ohu ill<u>a</u> almuttaqoona wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  But what plea have they<br> That God should not punish<br> Them, when they keep out<br> (Men) from the Sacred Mosque—<br> And they are not its guardians?<br> No men can be its guardians<br> Except the righteous; but most<br> Of them do not understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tuhum AAinda albayti ill<u>a</u> muk<u>a</u>an wata<u>s</u>diyatan fa<u>th</u>ooqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bim<u>a</u> kuntum takfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Their prayer at the House<br> (Of God) is nothing but<br> Whistling and clapping of hands:<br> (Its only answer can be),<br> "Taste ye the Penalty<br> Because ye blasphemed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yunfiqoona amw<u>a</u>lahum liya<u>s</u>uddoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi fasayunfiqoonah<u>a</u> thumma takoonu AAalayhim <u>h</u>asratan thumma yughlaboona wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo il<u>a</u> jahannama yu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  The Unbelievers spend their wealth<br> To hinder (men) from the path<br> Of God, and so will they<br> Continue to spend; but<br> In the end they will have<br> (Only) regrets and sighs;<br>At length they will be overcome:<br> And the Unbelievers will be<br> Gathered together to Hell;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Liyameeza All<u>a</u>hu alkhabeetha mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyibi wayajAAala alkhabeetha baAA<u>d</u>ahu AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in fayarkumahu jameeAAan fayajAAalahu fee jahannama ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  In order that God may separate<br> The impure from the pure,<br> Put the impure, one on another,<br> Heap them together, and cast them<br> Into Hell. They will be<br> The ones to have lost.</p> <div class="filenav">
