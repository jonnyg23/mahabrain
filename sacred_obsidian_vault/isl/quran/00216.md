 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Waman yarghabu AAan millati ibr<u>a</u>heema ill<u>a</u> man safiha nafsahu walaqadi i<u>st</u>afayn<u>a</u>hu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa-innahu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati lamina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  And who turns away<br> From the religion of Abraham<br> But such as debase their souls<br> With folly? Him We chose<br> And rendered pure in this world:<br> And he will be in the Hereafter<br> In the ranks of the Righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahu rabbuhu aslim q<u>a</u>la aslamtu lirabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  Behold! his Lord said<br> To him: "Bow (thy will to Me):"<br> He said: "I bow (my will)<br> To the Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Universe."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Wawa<u>ssa</u> bih<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heemu baneehi wayaAAqoobu y<u>a</u> baniyya inna All<u>a</u>ha i<u>st</u>af<u>a</u> lakumu a<b>l</b>ddeena fal<u>a</u> tamootunna ill<u>a</u> waantum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  And this was the legacy<br> That Abraham left to his sons,<br> And so did Jacob;<br> "Oh my sons! God hath chosen<br> The Faith for you; then die not<br> Except in the Faith of Islam."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Am kuntum shuhad<u>a</u>a i<u>th</u> <u>h</u>a<u>d</u>ara yaAAqooba almawtu i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la libaneehi m<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min baAAdee q<u>a</u>loo naAAbudu il<u>a</u>haka wa-il<u>a</u>ha <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ika ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa il<u>a</u>han w<u>ah</u>idan wana<u>h</u>nu lahu muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  Were ye witnesses<br> When Death appeared before Jacob?<br> Behold, he said to his sons:<br> "What will ye worship after me?"<br> They said: "We shall worship<br>Thy God and the God of thy fathers,—<br> Of Abraham, Ismā'īl, and Isaac,—<br> The One (True) God:<br> To Him we bow (in Islam)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Tilka ommatun qad khalat lah<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kasabat walakum m<u>a</u> kasabtum wal<u>a</u> tus-aloona AAamm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>no yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  That was a People that hath<br> Passed away. They shall reap<br> The fruit of what they did,<br> And ye of what ye do!<br> Of their merits<br> There is no question in your case!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Waq<u>a</u>loo koonoo hoodan aw na<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> tahtadoo qul bal millata ibr<u>a</u>heema <u>h</u>aneefan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  They say: "Become Jews<br> Or Christians if ye would be guided<br> (To salvation)." Say thou:<br> "Nay! (I would rather) the Religion<br> Of Abraham the True,<br> And he joined not gods with God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  Qooloo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> onzila ilayn<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> onzila il<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba wa<b>a</b>l-asb<u>at</u>i wam<u>a</u> ootiya moos<u>a</u> waAAees<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> ootiya a<b>l</b>nnabiyyoona min rabbihim l<u>a</u> nufarriqu bayna a<u>h</u>adin minhum wana<u>h</u>nu lahu muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  Say ye: "We believe<br> In God, and the revelation<br> Given to us, and to Abraham,<br> Ismā'īl, Isaac, Jacob,<br> And the Tribes, and that given<br> To Moses and Jesus, and that given<br> To (all) Prophets from their Lord:<br> We make no difference<br> Between one and another of them:<br> And we bow to God (in Islam)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  Fa-in <u>a</u>manoo bimithli m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mantum bihi faqadi ihtadaw wa-in tawallaw fa-innam<u>a</u> hum fee shiq<u>a</u>qin fasayakfeekahumu All<u>a</u>hu wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  So if they believe<br> As ye believe, they are indeed<br> On the right path; but if<br> They turn back, it is they<br>Who are in schism; but God will<br> Suffice thee as against them,<br> And He is the All-Hearing,<br> The All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  <u>S</u>ibghata All<u>a</u>hi waman a<u>h</u>sanu mina All<u>a</u>hi <u>s</u>ibghatan wana<u>h</u>nu lahu AA<u>a</u>bidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>138.  (Our religion is)<br> The Baptism of God:<br> And who can baptize better<br> Than God? And it is He<br> Whom we worship.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Qul atu<u>ha</u>jjoonan<u>a</u> fee All<u>a</u>hi wahuwa rabbun<u>a</u> warabbukum walan<u>a</u> aAAm<u>a</u>lun<u>a</u> walakum aAAm<u>a</u>lukum wana<u>h</u>nu lahu mukhli<u>s</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  Say: Will ye dispute<br> With us about God, seeing<br> That He is our Lord<br> And your Lord; that we<br> Are responsible for our doings<br> And ye for yours; and that<br> We are sincere (in our faith) In him?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  Am taqooloona inna ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba wa<b>a</b>l-asb<u>at</u>a k<u>a</u>noo hoodan aw na<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> qul aantum aAAlamu ami All<u>a</u>hu waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimman katama shah<u>a</u>datan AAindahu mina All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  Or do ye say that<br> Abraham, Ismā'īl, Isaac,<br> Jacob and the Tribes were<br> Jews or Christians?<br> Say: Do ye know better<br> Than God? Ah! who<br> Is more unjust than those<br> Who conceal the testimony<br> They have from God?<br> But God is not unmindful<br> Of what ye do!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Tilka ommatun qad khalat lah<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kasabat walakum m<u>a</u> kasabtum wal<u>a</u> tus-aloona AAamm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>141.  That was a people that hath<br> Passed away. They shall reap<br> The fruit of what they did,<br> And ye of what ye do!<br> Of their merits<br> There is no question in your case:</p> <div class="filenav">
