 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Bar<u>a</u>atun mina All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena AA<u>a</u>hadtum mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  A (declaration) of immunity<br> From God and His Apostle,<br> To those of the Pagans<br> With whom ye have contracted<br> Mutual alliances:—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fasee<u>h</u>oo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i arbaAAata ashhurin wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annakum ghayru muAAjizee All<u>a</u>hi waanna All<u>a</u>ha mukhzee alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>2.  Go ye, then, for four months,<br> Backwards and forwards,<br> (As ye will), throughout the land,<br> But know ye that ye cannot<br> Frustrate God (lay your falsehood)<br> But that God will cover<br> With shame those who reject Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waa<u>tha</u>nun mina All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si yawma al<u>h</u>ajji al-akbari anna All<u>a</u>ha baree-on mina almushrikeena warasooluhu fa-in tubtum fahuwa khayrun lakum wa-in tawallaytum fa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annakum ghayru muAAjizee All<u>a</u>hi wabashshiri alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And an announcement from God<br> And His Apostle, to the people<br> (Assembled) on the day<br> Of the Great Pilgrimage,—<br> That God and His Apostle<br> Dissolve (treaty) obligations<br> With the Pagans.<br> If, then, ye repent,<br> It were best for you;<br> But if ye turn away,<br>Know ye that ye cannot<br> Frustrate God. And proclaim<br> A grievous penalty to those<br> Who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena AA<u>a</u>hadtum mina almushrikeena thumma lam yanqu<u>s</u>ookum shay-an walam yu<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiroo AAalaykum a<u>h</u>adan faatimmoo ilayhim AAahdahum il<u>a</u> muddatihim inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  (But the treaties are)not dissolved<br> With those Pagans with whom<br> Ye have entered into alliance<br> And who have not subsequently<br> Failed you in aught,<br> Nor aided any one against you.<br> So fulfil your engagements<br> With them to the end<br> Of their term: for God<br> Loveth the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> insalakha al-ashhuru al<u>h</u>urumu fa<b>o</b>qtuloo almushrikeena <u>h</u>aythu wajadtumoohum wakhu<u>th</u>oohum wa<b>o</b><u>hs</u>uroohum wa<b>o</b>qAAudoo lahum kulla mar<u>s</u>adin fa-in t<u>a</u>boo waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>tawoo a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta fakhalloo sabeelahum inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But when the forbidden months<br> Are past, then fight and slay<br> The Pagans wherever ye find them,<br> And seize them, beleaguer them,<br> And lie in wait for them<br> In every stratagem (of war);<br> But if they repent,<br> And establish regular prayers<br> And practise regular charity,<br> Then open the way for them:<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-in a<u>h</u>adun mina almushrikeena istaj<u>a</u>raka faajirhu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yasmaAAa kal<u>a</u>ma All<u>a</u>hi thumma ablighhu ma/manahu <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum qawmun l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  If one amongst the Pagans<br> Ask thee for asylum,<br> Grant it to him,<br> So that he may hear the Word<br> Of God; and then escort him<br> To where he can be secure.<br> That is because they are<br> Men without knowledge.</p> <div class="filenav">
