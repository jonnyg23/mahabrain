 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Falamm<u>a</u> istay-asoo minhu khala<u>s</u>oo najiyyan q<u>a</u>la kabeeruhum alam taAAlamoo anna ab<u>a</u>kum qad akha<u>th</u>a AAalaykum mawthiqan mina All<u>a</u>hi wamin qablu m<u>a</u> farra<u>t</u>tum fee yoosufa falan abra<u>h</u>a al-ar<u>d</u>a <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/<u>th</u>ana lee abee aw ya<u>h</u>kuma All<u>a</u>hu lee wahuwa khayru al<u>ha</u>kimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Now when they saw<br> No hope of his (yielding),<br> They held a conference in private.<br> The leader among them said:<br> "Know ye not that your father<br> Did take an oath from you<br> In God's name, and how,<br> Before this, ye did fail<br>In your duty with Joseph?<br> Therefore will I not leave<br> This land until my father<br> Permits me, or God<br> Commands me; and He<br> Is the best to command.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  IrjiAAoo il<u>a</u> abeekum faqooloo y<u>a</u> ab<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> inna ibnaka saraqa wam<u>a</u> shahidn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> AAalimn<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> lilghaybi <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "Turn ye back to your father,<br> And say, "O our father!<br> Behold! thy son committed theft!<br> We bear witness only to what<br> We know, and we could not<br> Well guard against the unseen!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wa<b>i</b>s-ali alqaryata allatee kunn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lAAeera allatee aqbaln<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> wa-inn<u>a</u> la<u>sa</u>diqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  "Ask at the town where<br> We have been and the caravan<br> In which we returned,<br> And (you will find) we are<br> Indeed telling the truth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Q<u>a</u>la bal sawwalat lakum anfusukum amran fa<u>s</u>abrun jameelun AAas<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu an ya/tiyanee bihim jameeAAan innahu huwa alAAaleemu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Jacob said: "Nay, but ye<br> Have yourselves contrived<br> A story (good enough) for you.<br> So patience is most fitting<br> (For me). Maybe God will<br> Bring them (back) all<br> To me (in the end).<br> For He is indeed full<br> Of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Watawall<u>a</u> AAanhum waq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> asaf<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> yoosufa wa<b>i</b>bya<u>dd</u>at AAayn<u>a</u>hu mina al<u>h</u>uzni fahuwa ka<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  And he turned away from them,<br> And said: "How great<br> Is my grief for Joseph!"<br> And his eyes became white<br> With sorrow, and he fell<br> Into silent melancholy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Q<u>a</u>loo ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi taftao ta<u>th</u>kuru yoosufa <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> takoona <u>h</u>ara<u>d</u>an aw takoona mina alh<u>a</u>likeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  They said: "By God!<br> (Never) wilt thou cease<br> To remember Joseph<br> Until thou reach the last<br> Extremity of illness,<br> Or until thou die!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> ashkoo baththee wa<u>h</u>uznee il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi waaAAlamu mina All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  He said: "I only complain<br> Of my distraction and anguish<br> To God, and I know from God<br> That which ye know not…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Y<u>a</u> baniyya i<u>th</u>haboo fata<u>h</u>assasoo min yoosufa waakheehi wal<u>a</u> tay-asoo min raw<u>h</u>i All<u>a</u>hi innahu l<u>a</u> yay-asu min raw<u>h</u>i All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> alqawmu alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  "O my sons! go ye<br> And enquire about Joseph<br> And his brother, and never<br> Give up hope of God's<br> Soothing Mercy: truly<br> No one despairs of God's<br>Soothing Mercy, except<br> Those who have no faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Falamm<u>a</u> dakhaloo AAalayhi q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alAAazeezu massan<u>a</u> waahlan<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>urru waji/n<u>a</u> bibi<u>da</u>AAatin muzj<u>a</u>tin faawfi lan<u>a</u> alkayla wata<u>s</u>addaq AAalayn<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha yajzee almuta<u>s</u>addiqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Then, when they came<br> (Back) into (Joseph's) presence<br> They said: "O exalted one!<br> Distress has seized us<br> And our family: we have<br> (Now) brought but scanty capital:<br> So pay us full measure,<br> (We pray thee), and treat it<br> As charity to us: for God<br> Doth reward the charitable."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Q<u>a</u>la hal AAalimtum m<u>a</u> faAAaltum biyoosufa waakheehi i<u>th</u> antum j<u>a</u>hiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  He said: "Know ye<br> How ye dealt with Joseph<br> And his brother, not knowing<br> (What ye were doing)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Q<u>a</u>loo a-innaka laanta yoosufa q<u>a</u>la an<u>a</u> yoosufu wah<u>atha</u> akhee qad manna All<u>a</u>hu AAalayn<u>a</u> innahu man yattaqi waya<u>s</u>bir fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  They said: "Art thou indeed<br> Joseph?" He said . "I am<br> Joseph, and this is my brother:<br> God has indeed been gracious<br>To us (all): behold, he that is<br> Righteous and patient,—never<br> Will God suffer the reward<br> To be lost, of those<br> Who do right."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Q<u>a</u>loo ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi laqad <u>a</u>tharaka All<u>a</u>hu AAalayn<u>a</u> wa-in kunn<u>a</u> lakh<u>at</u>i-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  They said: "By God! Indeed<br> Has God preferred thee<br> Above us, and we certainly<br> Have been guilty of sin!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> tathreeba AAalaykumu alyawma yaghfiru All<u>a</u>hu lakum wahuwa ar<u>h</u>amu a<b>l</b>rr<u>ah</u>imeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  He said: "This day<br> Let no reproach be (cast)<br> On you; God will forgive you,<br> And He is the Most Merciful<br> Of those who show mercy!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  I<u>th</u>haboo biqamee<u>s</u>ee h<u>atha</u> faalqoohu AAal<u>a</u> wajhi abee ya/ti ba<u>s</u>eeran wa/toonee bi-ahlikum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  "Go with this my shirt,<br> And cast it over the face<br> Of my father: he will<br> Come to see (clearly). Then come<br> Ye (here) to me together<br> With all your family."</p> <div class="filenav">
