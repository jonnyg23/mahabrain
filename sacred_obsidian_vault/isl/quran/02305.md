 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee anshaa lakumu a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>ra wa<b>a</b>l-af-idata qaleelan m<u>a</u> tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  It is He Who has created<br> For you (the faculties of)<br> Hearing, sight, feeling<br> And understanding: little thanks<br> It is ye give!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee <u>th</u>araakum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  And He Has multiplied you<br> Through the earth, and to Him<br> Shall ye be gathered back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu walahu ikhtil<u>a</u>fu allayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  It is He Who gives<br> Life and death, and to Him<br> (Is due) the alternation<br> Of Night and Day:<br> Will ye not then understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Bal q<u>a</u>loo mithla m<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la al-awwaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  On the contrary they say<br> Things similar to what<br> The ancients said.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Q<u>a</u>loo a-i<u>tha</u> mitn<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> tur<u>a</u>ban waAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man a-inn<u>a</u> lamabAAoothoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  They say: "What! When we<br> Die and become dust and bones,<br> Could we really be<br> Raised up again?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Laqad wuAAidn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> min qablu in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  "Such things have been promised<br> To us and to our fathers<br> Before! They are nothing<br> But tales of the ancients!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Qul limani al-ar<u>d</u>u waman feeh<u>a</u> in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Say: "To whom belong<br> The earth and all beings therein?<br> (Say) if ye know!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Sayaqooloona lill<u>a</u>hi qul afal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  They will say, "To God!"<br> Say: "Yet will ye not<br> Receive admonition?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Qul man rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>ssabAAi warabbu alAAarshi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  Say: "Who is the Lord<br> Of the seven heavens,<br> And the Lord of the Throne<br> (Of Glory) Supreme?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Sayaqooloona lill<u>a</u>hi qul afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  They will say, "(They belong)<br> To God." Say: "Will ye not<br> Then be filled with awe?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Qul man biyadihi malakootu kulli shay-in wahuwa yujeeru wal<u>a</u> yuj<u>a</u>ru AAalayhi in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Say: "Who is it in whose<br> Hands is the governance<br> Of all things,—who protects<br> (All), but is not protected<br> (Of any)? (Say) if ye know."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Sayaqooloona lill<u>a</u>hi qul faann<u>a</u> tus<u>h</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  They will say, "(It belongs)<br> To God." Say: "Then how<br> Are ye deluded?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Bal atayn<u>a</u>hum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>90.  We have sent them the Truth:<br> But they indeed practise Falsehood!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  M<u>a</u> ittakha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu min waladin wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na maAAahu min il<u>a</u>hin i<u>th</u>an la<u>th</u>ahaba kullu il<u>a</u>hin bim<u>a</u> khalaqa walaAAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in sub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  No son did God beget,<br> Nor is there any god<br> Along with Him: (if there were<br> Many gods), behold, each god<br> Would have taken away<br> What he had created,<br> And some would have<br> Lorded it over others!<br> Glory to God! (He is free)<br> From the (sort of) things<br> They attribute to Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  AA<u>a</u>limi alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati fataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  He knows what is hidden<br> And what is open: too high<br> Is He for the partners<br> They attribute to Him!</p> <div class="filenav">
