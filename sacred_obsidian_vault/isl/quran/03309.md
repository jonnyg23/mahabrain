 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>ath</u>aw moos<u>a</u> fabarraahu All<u>a</u>hu mimm<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo wak<u>a</u>na AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wajeeh<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  O ye who believe!<br> Be ye not like those<br> Who vexed and insulted Moses,<br> But God cleared him<br> Of the (calumnies) they<br> Had uttered: and he<br> Was honourable in God's sight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waqooloo qawlan sadeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  O ye who believe!<br> Fear God, and (always) say<br> A word directed to the Right:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Yu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u> lakum aAAm<u>a</u>lakum wayaghfir lakum <u>th</u>unoobakum waman yu<u>t</u>iAAi All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu faqad f<u>a</u>za fawzan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  That He may make<br> Your conduct whole and sound<br> And forgive you your sins:<br> He that obeys God<br> And His Apostle, has already<br> Attained the highest<br> Achievement.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Inn<u>a</u> AAara<u>d</u>n<u>a</u> al-am<u>a</u>nata AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>li faabayna an ya<u>h</u>milnah<u>a</u> waashfaqna minh<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>amalah<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu innahu k<u>a</u>na <i><u>th</u></i>alooman jahool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  We did indeed offer<br> The Trust to the Heavens<br>And the Earth<br> And the Mountains;<br> But they refused<br> To undertake it,<br> Being afraid thereof:<br> But man undertook it;—<br> He was indeed unjust<br> And foolish;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  LiyuAAa<u>thth</u>iba All<u>a</u>hu almun<u>a</u>fiqeena wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lmushrikeena wa<b>a</b>lmushrik<u>a</u>ti wayatooba All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  (With the result (that<br> God has to punish<br> The Hypocrites, men and women,<br> And the Unbelievers, men<br> And women, and God turns<br> In Mercy to the Believers,<br> Men and women: for God<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p>  <div class="filenav">
