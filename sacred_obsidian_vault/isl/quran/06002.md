 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LX.: Mumtaḥana, or the Woman to be Examined.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  AAas<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu an yajAAala baynakum wabayna alla<u>th</u>eena AA<u>a</u>daytum minhum mawaddatan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu qadeerun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  It may be that God<br> Will grant love (and friendship)<br> Between you and those whom<br> Ye (now) hold as enemies.<br> For God has power<br> (Over all things); And God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  L<u>a</u> yanh<u>a</u>kumu All<u>a</u>hu AAani alla<u>th</u>eena lam yuq<u>a</u>tilookum fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni walam yukhrijookum min diy<u>a</u>rikum an tabarroohum watuqsi<u>t</u>oo ilayhim inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuqsi<u>t</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  God forbids you not,<br> With regard to those who<br> Fight you not for (your) Faith<br> Nor drive you out<br> Of your homes,<br> From dealing kindly and justly<br> With them: For God loveth<br> Those who are just.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Innam<u>a</u> yanh<u>a</u>kumu All<u>a</u>hu AAani alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>talookum fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni waakhrajookum min diy<u>a</u>rikum wa<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>haroo AAal<u>a</u> ikhr<u>a</u>jikum an tawallawhum waman yatawallahum faol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  God only forbids you,<br> With regard to those who<br> Fight you for (your) Faith,<br> And drive you out<br> Of your homes, and support<br> (Others) in driving you out,<br> From turning to them<br> (For friendship and protection).<br> It is such as turn to them<br> (In these circumstances),<br> That do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>akumu almu/min<u>a</u>tu muh<u>a</u>jir<u>a</u>tin fa<b>i</b>mta<u>h</u>inoohunna All<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bi-eem<u>a</u>nihinna fa-in AAalimtumoohunna mu/min<u>a</u>tin fal<u>a</u> tarjiAAoohunna il<u>a</u> alkuff<u>a</u>ri l<u>a</u> hunna <u>h</u>illun lahum wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>illoona lahunna wa<u>a</u>toohum m<u>a</u> anfaqoo wal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum an tanki<u>h</u>oohunna i<u>tha</u> <u>a</u>taytumoohunna ojoorahunna wal<u>a</u> tumsikoo biAAi<u>s</u>ami alkaw<u>a</u>firi wa<b>i</b>s-aloo m<u>a</u> anfaqtum walyas-aloo m<u>a</u> anfaqoo <u>tha</u>likum <u>h</u>ukmu All<u>a</u>hi ya<u>h</u>kumu baynakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  O ye who believe!<br> When there come to you<br> Believing women refugees,<br> Examine (and test) them:<br> God knows best as to<br> Their Faith: if ye ascertain<br> That they are Believers,<br> Then send them not back<br> To the Unbelievers.<br> They are not lawful (wives)<br>For the Unbelievers, nor are<br> The (Unbelievers) lawful (husbands)<br> For them. But pay<br> The Unbelievers what they<br> Have spent (on their dower).<br> And there will be no blame<br> On you if ye marry them<br> On payment of their dower<br> To them. But hold not<br> To the guardianship of<br> Unbelieving women: ask<br> For what ye have spent<br> On their dowers, and let<br> The (Unbelievers) ask for<br> What they have spent<br> (On the dowers of women<br> Who come over to you).<br> Such is the command<br> Of God: He judges<br> (With justice) between you.<br> And God is Full of<br> Knowledge and Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa-in f<u>a</u>takum shay-on min azw<u>a</u>jikum il<u>a</u> alkuff<u>a</u>ri faAA<u>a</u>qabtum fa<u>a</u>too alla<u>th</u>eena <u>th</u>ahabat azw<u>a</u>juhum mithla m<u>a</u> anfaqoo wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee antum bihi mu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And if any<br> Of your wives deserts you<br> To the Unbelievers,<br> And ye have an accession<br> (By the coming over of<br> A woman from the other side),<br> Then pay to those<br> Whose wives have deserted<br>The equivalent of what they<br> Had spent (on their dower).<br> And fear God,<br> In Whom ye believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>aka almu/min<u>a</u>tu yub<u>a</u>yiAAnaka AAal<u>a</u> an l<u>a</u> yushrikna bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi shay-an wal<u>a</u> yasriqna wal<u>a</u> yazneena wal<u>a</u> yaqtulna awl<u>a</u>dahunna wal<u>a</u> ya/teena bibuht<u>a</u>nin yaftareenahu bayna aydeehinna waarjulihinna wal<u>a</u> yaAA<u>s</u>eenaka fee maAAroofin fab<u>a</u>yiAAhunna wa<b>i</b>staghfir lahunna All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  O Prophet!<br> When believing women come<br> To thee to take the oath<br> Of fealty to thee, that they<br> Will not associate in worship<br> Any other thing whatever<br> With God, that they<br> Will not steal, that they<br> Will not commit adultery<br> (Or fornication), that they<br> Will not kill their children,<br> That they will not utter<br> Slander, intentionally forging<br> Falsehood, and that they<br> Will not disobey thee<br> In any just matter,<br> Then do thou receive<br> Their fealty, and pray to God<br> For the forgiveness (of<br> Their sins): for God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tatawallaw qawman gha<u>d</u>iba All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim qad ya-isoo mina al-<u>a</u>khirati kam<u>a</u> ya-isa alkuff<u>a</u>ru min a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alquboor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  O ye who believe!<br> Turn not (for friendship)<br> To people on whom<br> Is the Wrath of God.<br>Of the Hereafter they are<br> Already in despair, just as<br> The Unbelievers are<br> In despair about those<br> (Buried) in graves.</p>  <div class="filenav">
