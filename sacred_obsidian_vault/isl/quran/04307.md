 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Y<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>di l<u>a</u> khawfun AAalaykumu alyawma wal<u>a</u> antum ta<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  My devotees<br> No fear shall be<br> On you that Day,<br> Nor shall ye grieve,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>noo muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  (Being) those who have believed<br> In Our Signs and bowed<br> (Their wills to Ours) in Islam.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Odkhuloo aljannata antum waazw<u>a</u>jukum tu<u>h</u>baroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Enter ye the Garden,<br> Ye and your wives,<br> In (beauty and) rejoicing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Yu<u>ta</u>fu AAalayhim bi<u>s</u>i<u>ha</u>fin min <u>th</u>ahabin waakw<u>a</u>bin wafeeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> tashtaheehi al-anfusu watala<u>thth</u>u al-aAAyunu waantum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  To them will be passed<br> Round, dishes and goblets<br> Of gold: there will be<br> There all that the souls<br> Could desire, all that<br> The eyes could delight in:<br> And ye shall abide<br> Therein (for aye).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Watilka aljannatu allatee oorithtumooh<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Such will be the Garden<br> Of which ye are made<br> Heirs for your (good) deeds<br> (In life).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Lakum feeh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun katheeratun minh<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  Ye shall have therein<br> Abundance of fruit, from which<br> Ye shall have satisfaction.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Inna almujrimeena fee AAa<u>tha</u>bi jahannama kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  The Sinners will be<br> In the Punishment of Hell,<br> To dwell therein (for aye):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  L<u>a</u> yufattaru AAanhum wahum feehi mublisoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Nowise will the (punishment)<br> Be lightened for them,<br> And in despair will they<br> Be there overwhelmed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamn<u>a</u>hum wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Nowise shall We<br> Be unjust to them:<br>But it is they who<br> Have been unjust themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Wan<u>a</u>daw y<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>liku liyaq<u>d</u>i AAalayn<u>a</u> rabbuka q<u>a</u>la innakum m<u>a</u>kithoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  They will cry: "O Mālik!<br> Would that thy Lord<br> Put an end to us!"<br> He will say, "Nay, but<br> Ye shall abide!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Laqad ji/n<u>a</u>kum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharakum lil<u>h</u>aqqi k<u>a</u>rihoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  Verily We have brought<br> The Truth to you:<br> But most of you<br> Have a hatred for Truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Am abramoo amran fa-inn<u>a</u> mubrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  What! Have they settled<br> Some Plan (among themselves)?<br> But it is We Who<br> Settle things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Am ya<u>h</u>saboona ann<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nasmaAAu sirrahum wanajw<u>a</u>hum bal<u>a</u> warusulun<u>a</u> ladayhim yaktuboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Or do they think<br> That We hear not<br> Their secrets and their<br> Private counsels? Indeed<br> (We do), and Our Messengers<br> Are by them, to record.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Qul in k<u>a</u>na li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni waladun faan<u>a</u> awwalu alAA<u>a</u>bideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Say: If (God) Most Gracious<br> Had a son, I would<br> Be the first to worship."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Sub<u>ha</u>na rabbi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i rabbi alAAarshi AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Glory to the Lord<br> Of the heavens and the earth,<br>The Lord of the Throne<br> (Of Authority)! (He is<br> Free) from the things<br> They attribute (to Him)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Fa<u>th</u>arhum yakhoo<u>d</u>oo wayalAAaboo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yul<u>a</u>qoo yawmahumu alla<u>th</u>ee yooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  So leave them to babble<br> And play (with vanities)<br> Until they meet that Day,<br> Of theirs, which they<br> Have been promised.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i il<u>a</u>hun wafee al-ar<u>d</u>i il<u>a</u>hun wahuwa al<u>h</u>akeemu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  It is He Who is God<br> In heaven and God on earth;<br> And He is Full<br> Of Wisdom and Knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Watab<u>a</u>raka alla<u>th</u>ee lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> waAAindahu AAilmu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  And blessed is He<br> To Whom belongs the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth,<br> And all between them:<br> With Him is the knowledge<br> Of the Hour (of Judgment):<br> And to Him shall ye<br> Be brought back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wal<u>a</u> yamliku alla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona min doonihi a<b>l</b>shshaf<u>a</u>AAata ill<u>a</u> man shahida bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  And those whom they invoke<br> Besides God have no power<br> Of intercession;—only he<br> Who bears witness to the Truth,<br> And they know (him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wala-in saaltahum man khalaqahum layaqoolunna All<u>a</u>hu faann<u>a</u> yu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  If thou ask them, Who<br> Created them, they will<br> Certainly say, God: how<br> Then are they deluded<br> Away (from the Truth)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Waqeelihi y<u>a</u> rabbi inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i qawmun l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  (God has knowledge)<br> Of the (Prophet's) cry,<br> "O my Lord! Truly<br> These are a people<br> Who will not believe!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>fa<u>h</u> AAanhum waqul sal<u>a</u>mun fasawfa yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  But turn away from them,<br> And say "Peace!"<br> But soon shall they know!</p>  <div class="filenav">
