 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Otlu m<u>a</u> oo<u>h</u>iya ilayka mina alkit<u>a</u>bi waaqimi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta inna a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta tanh<u>a</u> AAani alfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lmunkari wala<u>th</u>ikru All<u>a</u>hi akbaru wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>naAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Recite what is sent<br> Of the Book by inspiration<br> To thee, and establish<br> Regular Prayer: for Prayer<br> Restrains from shameful<br> And unjust deeds;<br> And remembrance of God<br> Is the greatest (thing in life)<br> Without doubt. And God knows<br> The (deeds) that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wal<u>a</u> tuj<u>a</u>diloo ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>llatee hiya a<u>h</u>sanu ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo minhum waqooloo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee onzila ilayn<u>a</u> waonzila ilaykum wa-il<u>a</u>hun<u>a</u> wa-il<u>a</u>hukum w<u>ah</u>idun wana<u>h</u>nu lahu muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And dispute ye not<br> With the People of the Book,<br> Except with means better<br> (Than mere disputation), unless<br> It be with those of them<br> Who inflict wrong (and injury):<br> But say, "We believe<br> In the Revelation which has<br> Come down to us and in that<br> Which came down to you;<br>Our God and your God<br> Is One; and it is to Him<br> We bow (in Islam)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika anzaln<u>a</u> ilayka alkit<u>a</u>ba fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba yu/minoona bihi wamin h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i man yu/minu bihi wam<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And thus (it is) that We<br> Have sent down the Book<br> To thee. So the People<br> Of the Book believe therein,<br> As also do some of these<br> (Pagan Arabs): and none<br> But Unbelievers reject Our Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wam<u>a</u> kunta tatloo min qablihi min kit<u>a</u>bin wal<u>a</u> takhu<u>tt</u>uhu biyameenika i<u>th</u>an la<b>i</b>rt<u>a</u>ba almub<u>t</u>iloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And thou vast not (able)<br> To recite a Book before<br> This (Book came), nor art thou<br> (Able) to transcribe it<br> With thy right hand:<br> In that case, indeed, would<br>The talkers of vanities<br> Have doubted.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Bal huwa <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun bayyin<u>a</u>tun fee <u>s</u>udoori alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma wam<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Nay, here are Signs<br> Self-evident in the hearts<br> Of those endowed with<br> knowledge:<br> And none but the unjust<br> Reject Our Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> onzila AAalayhi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun min rabbihi qul innam<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wa-innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Yet they say: "Why<br> Are not Signs sent down<br> To him from his Lord?"<br> Say: "The Signs are indeed<br> With God: and I am<br> Indeed a clear Warner.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Awa lam yakfihim ann<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba yutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim inna fee <u>tha</u>lika lara<u>h</u>matan wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And is it not enough<br> For them that We have<br> Sent down to thee<br> The Book which is rehearsed<br> To them? Verily, in it<br> Is Mercy and a Reminder<br> To those who believe.</p> <div class="filenav">
