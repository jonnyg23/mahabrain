 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXIX.: Ḥāqqa, or the Sure Reality.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Al<u>ha</u>qqa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  The Sure Reality!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> al<u>ha</u>qqa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  What is the Sure Reality?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> al<u>ha</u>qqa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And what will make<br> Thee realise what<br> The Sure Reality is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat thamoodu waAA<u>a</u>dun bi<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>riAAa<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  The <u>Th</u>amūd<br> And the ‘Ād people<br> (Branded) as false<br> The Stunning Calamity!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Faamm<u>a</u> thamoodu faohlikoo bi<b>al</b><u>tta</u>ghiya<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But the <u>Th</u>amūd,—<br> They were destroyed<br> By a terrible Storm<br> Of thunder and lightning!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Waamm<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>dun faohlikoo biree<u>h</u>in <u>s</u>ar<u>s</u>arin AA<u>a</u>tiya<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And the ‘Ād,—<br> They were destroyed<br> By a furious Wind,<br> Exceedingly violent;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Sakhkharah<u>a</u> AAalayhim sabAAa lay<u>a</u>lin watham<u>a</u>niyata ayy<u>a</u>min <u>h</u>usooman fatar<u>a</u> alqawma feeh<u>a</u> <u>s</u>arAA<u>a</u> kaannahum aAAj<u>a</u>zu nakhlin kh<u>a</u>wiya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  He made it rage<br> Against them seven nights<br> And eight days in succession:<br> So that thou couldst see<br> The (whole) people lying<br> Prostrate in its (path),<br> As if they had been<br> Roots of hollow palm-trees<br> Tumbled down!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fahal tar<u>a</u> lahum min b<u>a</u>qiya<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Then seest thou any<br> Of them left surviving?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waj<u>a</u>a firAAawnu waman qablahu wa<b>a</b>lmu/tafik<u>a</u>tu bi<b>a</b>lkh<u>at</u>i-a<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And Pharaoh,<br> And those before him,<br> And the Cities Overthrown,<br> Committed habitual Sin,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  FaAAa<u>s</u>aw rasoola rabbihim faakha<u>th</u>ahum akh<u>th</u>atan r<u>a</u>biya<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And disobeyed (each)<br> The apostle of their Lord;<br> So He punished them<br> With an abundant Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Inn<u>a</u> lamm<u>a</u> <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u> alm<u>a</u>o <u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u>kum fee alj<u>a</u>riya<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  We, when the water<br> (Of Noah's Flood) overflowed<br> Beyond its limits,<br> Carried you (mankind),<br> In the floating (Ark),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  LinajAAalah<u>a</u> lakum ta<u>th</u>kiratan wataAAiyah<u>a</u> o<u>th</u>unun w<u>a</u>AAiya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  That We might<br> Make it a Message<br> Unto you, and that ears<br> (That should hear the tale<br> And) retain its memory<br> Should bear its (lessons)<br> In remembrance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> nufikha fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori nafkhatun w<u>ah</u>ida<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Then, when one<br> Blast is sounded<br> On the Trumpet,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wa<u>h</u>umilati al-ar<u>d</u>u wa<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>lu fadukkat<u>a</u> dakkatan w<u>ah</u>ida<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And the earth is moved,<br> And its mountains,<br> And they are crushed to powder<br> At one stroke,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Fayawma-i<u>th</u>in waqaAAati alw<u>a</u>qiAAa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  On that Day<br> Shall the (Great) Event<br> Come to pass,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wa<b>i</b>nshaqqati a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o fahiya yawma-i<u>th</u>in w<u>a</u>hiya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And the sky will be<br> Rent asunder, for it will<br> That Day be flimsy,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>a</b>lmalaku AAal<u>a</u> arj<u>a</u>-ih<u>a</u> waya<u>h</u>milu AAarsha rabbika fawqahum yawma-i<u>th</u>in tham<u>a</u>niya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And the angels will be<br> On its sides,<br>And eight will, that Day,<br> Bear the Throne<br> Of thy Lord above them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in tuAAra<u>d</u>oona l<u>a</u> takhf<u>a</u> minkum kh<u>a</u>fiya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  That Day shall ye be<br> Brought to Judgment:<br> Not an act of yours<br> That ye hide will be hidden.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Faamm<u>a</u> man ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu biyameenihi fayaqoolu h<u>a</u>omu iqraoo kit<u>a</u>biyah   </p>
 <p>19.  Then he that will be<br> Given his Record<br> In his right hand<br> Will say: "Ah here!<br> Read ye my Record!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Innee <i><u>th</u></i>anantu annee mul<u>a</u>qin <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>biyah   </p>
 <p>20.  "I did really understand<br> That my Account would<br> (One Day) reach me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Fahuwa fee AAeeshatin r<u>ad</u>iya<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And he will be<br> In a life of Bliss,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Fee jannatin AA<u>a</u>liya<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  In a Garden on high,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Qu<u>t</u>oofuh<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>niya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  The Fruits whereof<br> (Will hang in bunches)<br> Low and near.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Kuloo wa<b>i</b>shraboo hanee-an bim<u>a</u> aslaftum fee al-ayy<u>a</u>mi alkh<u>a</u>liya<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "Eat ye and drink ye,<br> With full satisfaction;<br>Because of the (good)<br> That ye sent before you,<br> In the days that are gone!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waamm<u>a</u> man ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu bishim<u>a</u>lihi fayaqoolu y<u>a</u> laytanee lam oota kit<u>a</u>biyah   </p>
 <p>25.  And he that will<br> Be given his Record<br> In his left hand,<br> Will say: "Ah! would<br> That my record had not<br> Been given to me!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Walam adri m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>biyah   </p>
 <p>26.  "And that I had never<br> Realised how<br> My account (stood)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Y<u>a</u> laytah<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>nati alq<u>ad</u>iya<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "Ah! would that (Death)<br> Had made an end of me!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  M<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAannee m<u>a</u>liyah   </p>
 <p>28.  "Of no profit to me<br> Has been my wealth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Halaka AAannee sul<u>ta</u>niyah   </p>
 <p>29.  "My power has<br> Perished from me!"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Khu<u>th</u>oohu faghullooh<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  (The stern command will say):<br> "Seize ye him,<br> And bind ye him,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Thumma alja<u>h</u>eema <u>s</u>allooh<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "And burn ye him<br> In the Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Thumma fee silsilatin <u>th</u>arAAuh<u>a</u> sabAAoona <u>th</u>ir<u>a</u>AAan fa<b>o</b>slukooh<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "Further, make him march<br> In a chain, whereof<br> The length is seventy cubits!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Innahu k<u>a</u>na l<u>a</u> yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "This was he that<br> Would not believe<br> In God Most High,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>u<u>dd</u>u AAal<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mi almiskeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "And would not encourage<br> The feeding of the indigent!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Falaysa lahu alyawma h<u>a</u>hun<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ameem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "So no friend hath he<br> Here this Day.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wal<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mun ill<u>a</u> min ghisleen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  "Nor hath he any food<br> Except the corruption<br> From the washing of wounds,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/069/069_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  L<u>a</u> ya/kuluhu ill<u>a</u> alkh<u>at</u>i-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "Which none do eat<br> But those in sin."</p> <div class="filenav">
