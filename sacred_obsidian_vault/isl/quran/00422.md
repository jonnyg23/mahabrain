 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 22</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_153.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>153.  Yas-aluka ahlu alkit<u>a</u>bi an tunazzila AAalayhim kit<u>a</u>ban mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i faqad saaloo moos<u>a</u> akbara min <u>tha</u>lika faq<u>a</u>loo arin<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha jahratan faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>AAiqatu bi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmihim thumma ittakha<u>th</u>oo alAAijla min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu albayyin<u>a</u>tu faAAafawn<u>a</u> AAan <u>tha</u>lika wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> sul<u>ta</u>nan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>153.  The People of the Book<br> Ask thee to cause<br> A book to descend to them<br> From heaven: indeed<br> They asked Moses<br> For an even greater<br> (Miracle), for they said:<br> "Show us God in public,"<br> But they were dazed<br> For their presumption,<br> With thunder and lightning.<br> Yet they worshipped the calf<br> Even after Clear Signs<br> Had come to them;<br> Even so We forgave them;<br> And gave Moses manifest<br> Proofs of authority.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_154.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>154.  WarafaAAn<u>a</u> fawqahumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oora bimeeth<u>a</u>qihim waquln<u>a</u> lahumu odkhuloo alb<u>a</u>ba sujjadan waquln<u>a</u> lahum l<u>a</u> taAAdoo fee a<b>l</b>ssabti waakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> minhum meeth<u>a</u>qan ghalee<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>154.  And for their Covenant<br> We raised over them<br> (The towering height)<br> Of Mount (Sinai);<br> And (on another occasion)<br> We said: "Enter the gate<br> With humility"; and (once again)<br> We commanded them:<br> "Transgress not in the matter<br> Of the Sabbath."<br> And We took from them<br> A solemn Covenant.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Fabim<u>a</u> naq<u>d</u>ihim meeth<u>a</u>qahum wakufrihim bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi waqatlihimu al-anbiy<u>a</u>a bighayri <u>h</u>aqqin waqawlihim quloobun<u>a</u> ghulfun bal <u>t</u>abaAAa All<u>a</u>hu AAalayh<u>a</u> bikufrihim fal<u>a</u> yu/minoona ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  (They have incurred divine<br> Displeasure): in that they<br> Broke their Covenant;<br> That they rejected the Signs<br> Of God; that they slew<br> The Messengers in defiance<br> Of right; that they said,<br> "Our hearts are the wrappings<br> (Which preserve God's Word;<br> We need no more)";—nay,<br> God hath set the seal on their hearts<br> For their blasphemy,<br> And little is it they believe;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  Wabikufrihim waqawlihim AAal<u>a</u> maryama buht<u>a</u>nan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>156.  That they rejected Faith;<br> That they uttered against Mary<br>A grave false charge;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  Waqawlihim inn<u>a</u> qataln<u>a</u> almasee<u>h</u>a AAees<u>a</u> ibna maryama rasoola All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> qataloohu wam<u>a</u> <u>s</u>alaboohu wal<u>a</u>kin shubbiha lahum wa-inna alla<u>th</u>eena ikhtalafoo feehi lafee shakkin minhu m<u>a</u> lahum bihi min AAilmin ill<u>a</u> ittib<u>a</u>AAa a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anni wam<u>a</u> qataloohu yaqeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  That they said (in boast),<br> "We killed Christ Jesus<br> The son of Mary,<br> The Apostle of God";—<br> But they killed him not,<br> Nor crucified him,<br> But so it was made<br> To appear to them,<br> And those who differ<br> Therein are full of doubts,<br> With no (certain) knowledge,<br> But only conjecture to follow,<br> For of a surety<br> They killed him not:—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  Bal rafaAAahu All<u>a</u>hu ilayhi wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAazeezan <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  Nay, God raised him up<br> Unto Himself; and God<br> Is Exalted in Power, Wise;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Wa-in min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi ill<u>a</u> layu/minanna bihi qabla mawtihi wayawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati yakoonu AAalayhim shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  And there is none<br> Of the People of the Book<br>But must believe in him<br> Before his death;<br> And on the Day of Judgment<br> He will be a witness<br> Against them;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  Fabi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmin mina alla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo <u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>t</u>ayyib<u>a</u>tin o<u>h</u>illat lahum wabi<u>s</u>addihim AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  For the iniquity of the Jews<br> We made unlawful for them<br> Certain (foods) good and wholesome<br> Which had been lawful for them;—<br> In that they hindered many<br> From God's Way;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  Waakh<u>th</u>ihimu a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> waqad nuhoo AAanhu waaklihim amw<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili waaAAtadn<u>a</u> lilk<u>a</u>fireena minhum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  That they took usury,<br> Though they were forbidden;<br> And that they devoured<br> Men's substance wrongfully;—<br> We have prepared for those<br> Among them who reject Faith<br> A grievous punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  L<u>a</u>kini a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>sikhoona fee alAAilmi minhum wa<b>a</b>lmu/minoona yu/minoona bim<u>a</u> onzila ilayka wam<u>a</u> onzila min qablika wa<b>a</b>lmuqeemeena a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>lmu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>lmu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri ol<u>a</u>-ika sanu/teehim ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  But those among them<br> Who are well-grounded in knowledge,<br> And the Believers,<br> Believe in what hath been<br> Revealed to thee and what was<br> Revealed before thee:<br> And (especially) those<br> Who establish regular prayer<br> And practise regular charity<br> And believe in God<br> And in the Last Day:<br> To them shall We soon<br> Give a great reward.</p> <div class="filenav">
