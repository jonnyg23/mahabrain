 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LV.: Raḥmān, or (God) Most Gracious.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  A<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>n<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  (God) Most Gracious!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  AAallama alqur-<u>a</u>n<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  It is He Who has<br> Taught the Qur-ān.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>n<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  He has created man:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  AAallamahu albay<u>a</u>n<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  He has taught him speech<br> (And Intelligence).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  A<b>l</b>shshamsu wa<b>a</b>lqamaru bi<u>h</u>usb<u>a</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  The sun and the moon<br> Follow courses (exactly) computed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>al</b>nnajmu wa<b>al</b>shshajaru yasjud<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And the herbs and the trees—<br> Both (alike) bow in adoration.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>a rafaAAah<u>a</u> wawa<u>d</u>aAAa almeez<u>a</u>n<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And the Firmament has He<br> Raised high, and He has set up<br> The Balance (of Justice),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  All<u>a</u> ta<u>t</u>ghaw fee almeez<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  In order that ye may<br> Not transgress (due) balance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waaqeemoo alwazna bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i wal<u>a</u> tukhsiroo almeez<u>a</u>n<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  So establish weight with justice<br> And fall not short<br> In the balance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wa<u>d</u>aAAah<u>a</u> lil-an<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  It is He Who has<br> Spread out the earth<br> For (His) creatures:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Feeh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun wa<b>al</b>nnakhlu <u>tha</u>tu al-akm<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Therein is fruit<br> And date-palms, producing<br> Spathes (enclosing dates);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>abbu <u>th</u>oo alAAa<u>s</u>fi wa<b>al</b>rray<u>ha</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Also corn, with (its)<br> Leaves and stalk for fodder,<br> And sweet-smelling plants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>na min <u>s</u>al<u>sa</u>lin ka<b>a</b>lfakhkh<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  He created man<br> From sounding clay<br> Like unto pottery,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wakhalaqa alj<u>a</u>nna min m<u>a</u>rijin min n<u>a</u>r<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  And He created Jinns<br> From fire free of smoke:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Rabbu almashriqayni warabbu almaghribayn<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  (He is) Lord<br> Of the two Easts<br> And Lord<br> Of the two Wests:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Maraja alba<u>h</u>rayni yaltaqiy<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  He has let free<br> The two bodies<br> Of flowing water,<br> Meeting together:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Baynahum<u>a</u> barzakhun l<u>a</u> yabghiy<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Between them is a Barrier<br> Which they do not transgress:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Yakhruju minhum<u>a</u> allu/luo wa<b>a</b>lmarj<u>a</u>n<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Out of them come<br> Pearls and Coral:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>ni    </p>
 <p>23.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Walahu aljaw<u>a</u>ri almunsha<u>a</u>tu fee alba<u>h</u>ri ka<b>a</b>l-aAAl<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And His are the Ships<br> Sailing smoothly through the seas,<br> Lofty as mountains:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <div class="filenav">
