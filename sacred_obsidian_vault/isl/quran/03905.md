 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIX.: Zumar, or the Crowds.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  All<u>a</u>hu yatawaff<u>a</u> al-anfusa <u>h</u>eena mawtih<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>llatee lam tamut fee man<u>a</u>mih<u>a</u> fayumsiku allatee qa<u>da</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> almawta wayursilu al-okhr<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  It is God that takes<br> The souls (of men) at death;<br>And those that die not<br> (He takes) during their sleep:<br> Those on whom He<br> Has passed the decree<br> Of death, He keeps back<br> (From returning to life),<br> But the rest He sends<br> (To their bodies)<br> For a term appointed.<br> Verily in this are Signs<br> For those who reflect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Ami ittakha<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi shufaAA<u>a</u>a qul awa law k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yamlikoona shay-an wal<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  What! Do they take<br> For intercessors others<br> Besides God? Say: "Even if<br> They have no power whatever<br> And no intelligence?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Qul lill<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b>shshaf<u>a</u>AAatu jameeAAan lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i thumma ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Say: "To God belongs<br> Exclusively (the right<br>To grant) Intercession:<br> To Him belongs the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br> In the End, it is to Him<br> That ye shall be<br> Brought back."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ukira All<u>a</u>hu wa<u>h</u>dahu ishmaazzat quloobu alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ukira alla<u>th</u>eena min doonihi i<u>tha</u> hum yastabshiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  When God, the One and Only,<br> Is mentioned, the hearts<br> Of those who believe not<br> In the Hereafter are filled<br> With disgust and horror;<br> But when (gods) other than He<br> Are mentioned, behold,<br> They are filled with joy!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Quli all<u>a</u>humma f<u>at</u>ira a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i AA<u>a</u>lima alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati anta ta<u>h</u>kumu bayna AAib<u>a</u>dika fee m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Say: "O God!<br> Creator of the heavens<br> And the earth!<br> Knower of all that is<br> Hidden and open!<br> It is Thou that wilt<br> Judge between Thy Servants<br> In those matters about which<br> They have differed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Walaw anna lilla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan wamithlahu maAAahu la<b>i</b>ftadaw bihi min soo-i alAAa<u>tha</u>bi yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wabad<u>a</u> lahum mina All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> lam yakoonoo ya<u>h</u>tasiboon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>47.  Even if the wrong-doers<br> Had all that there is<br> On earth, and as much more,<br> (In vain) would they offer it<br>For ransom from the pain<br> Of the Penalty on the Day<br> Of Judgment: but something<br> Will confront them from God,<br> Which they could never<br> Have counted upon!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wabad<u>a</u> lahum sayyi-<u>a</u>tu m<u>a</u> kasaboo wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  For the evils of their Deeds<br> Will confront them,<br> And they will be (completely)<br> Encircled by that which<br> They used to mock at!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> massa al-ins<u>a</u>na <u>d</u>urrun daAA<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> thumma i<u>tha</u> khawwaln<u>a</u>hu niAAmatan minn<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> ooteetuhu AAal<u>a</u> AAilmin bal hiya fitnatun wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Now, when trouble touches man,<br> He cries to Us:<br> But when We bestow<br> A favour upon him<br> As from Ourselves,<br> He says, "This has been<br> Given to me because of<br> A certain knowledge (I have)!"<br> Nay, but this is<br> But a trial, but most<br> Of them understand not!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Qad q<u>a</u>lah<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim fam<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Thus did the (generations)<br> Before them say! But<br> All that they did<br> Was of no profit to them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Faa<u>sa</u>bahum sayyi-<u>a</u>tu m<u>a</u> kasaboo wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo min h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i sayu<u>s</u>eebuhum sayyi-<u>a</u>tu m<u>a</u> kasaboo wam<u>a</u> hum bimuAAjizeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Nay, the evil results<br> Of their deeds overtook them.<br> And the wrong-doers<br> Of this (generation)<br> The evil results of their deeds<br> Will soon overtake them (too),<br>And they will never be<br> Able to frustrate (Our Plan)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Awa lam yaAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o wayaqdiru inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Know they not that<br> God enlarges the provision<br> Or restricts it, for any<br> He pleases? Verily, in this are<br> Signs for those who believe!</p> <div class="filenav">
