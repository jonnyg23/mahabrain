 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu qul liman fee aydeekum mina al-asr<u>a</u> in yaAAlami All<u>a</u>hu fee quloobikum khayran yu/tikum khayran mimm<u>a</u> okhi<u>th</u>a minkum wayaghfir lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  "</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa-in yureedoo khiy<u>a</u>nataka faqad kh<u>a</u>noo All<u>a</u>ha min qablu faamkana minhum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  But if they have<br> Treacherous designs against thee,<br> (O Apostle!), they have already<br> Been in treason against God,<br> And so hath He given<br> (Thee) power over them.<br> And God is He who hath<br> (Full) knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wah<u>a</u>jaroo waj<u>a</u>hadoo bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>waw wana<u>s</u>aroo ol<u>a</u>-ika baAA<u>d</u>uhum awliy<u>a</u>o baAA<u>d</u>in wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo walam yuh<u>a</u>jiroo m<u>a</u> lakum min wal<u>a</u>yatihim min shay-in <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yuh<u>a</u>jiroo wa-ini istan<u>s</u>arookum fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni faAAalaykumu a<b>l</b>nna<u>s</u>ru ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> qawmin baynakum wabaynahum meeth<u>a</u>qun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Those who believed,<br> And adopted exile,<br>And fought for the Faith,<br> With their property<br> And their persons,<br> In the cause of God,<br> As well as those<br> Who gave (them) asylum<br> And aid,—these are (all)<br> Friends and protectors,<br> One of another.<br> As to those who believed<br> But came not into exile,<br> Ye owe no duty<br> Of protection to them<br> Until they come into exile;<br> But if they seek<br> Your aid in religion,<br> It is your duty<br> To help them,<br> Except against a people<br> With whom ye have<br> A treaty of mutual alliance.<br> And (remember) God<br> Seeth all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo baAA<u>d</u>uhum awliy<u>a</u>o baAA<u>d</u>in ill<u>a</u> tafAAaloohu takun fitnatun fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wafas<u>a</u>dun kabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  The Unbelievers are<br> Protectors, one of another:<br> Unless ye do this,<br> (Protect each other),<br> There would be<br> Tumult and oppression<br> On earth, and great mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wah<u>a</u>jaroo waj<u>a</u>hadoo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>waw wana<u>s</u>aroo ol<u>a</u>-ika humu almu/minoona <u>h</u>aqqan lahum maghfiratun warizqun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Those who believe,<br> And adopt exile,<br> And tight for the Faith,<br> In the cause of God,<br> As well as those<br> Who give (them) asylum<br> And aid,—these are (all)<br> In very truth the Believers:<br> For them is the forgiveness<br> Of sins and a provision<br> Most generous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo min baAAdu wah<u>a</u>jaroo waj<u>a</u>hadoo maAAakum faol<u>a</u>-ika minkum waoloo al-ar<u>ha</u>mi baAA<u>d</u>uhum awl<u>a</u> bibaAA<u>d</u>in fee kit<u>a</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  And those who<br> Accept Faith subsequently,<br> And adopt exile,<br> And fight for the Faith<br> In your company,<br> They are of you.<br> But kindred by blood<br> Have prior rights<br> Against each other<br> In the Book of God.<br> Verily God is well-acquainted<br> With all things.</p>  <div class="filenav">
