 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wala-in a<u>th</u>aqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na minn<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan thumma nazaAAn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> minhu innahu layaoosun kafoor<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>9.  If We give man a taste<br> Of Mercy from Ourselves,<br> And then withdraw it from him,<br> Behold! he is in despair<br> And (falls into) blasphemy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wala-in a<u>th</u>aqn<u>a</u>hu naAAm<u>a</u>a baAAda <u>d</u>arr<u>a</u>a massat-hu layaqoolanna <u>th</u>ahaba a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>tu AAannee innahu lafari<u>h</u>un fakhoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  But if We give hint a taste<br> Of (Our) favours after<br> Adversity hath touched him,<br> He is sure to say,<br> "All evil has departed from me:<br> Behold! he falls into exultation<br> And pride.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>abaroo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum maghfiratun waajrun kabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Not so do those who show<br> Patience and constancy, and work<br> Righteousness; for them<br> Is forgiveness (of sins)<br> And a great reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  FalaAAallaka t<u>a</u>rikun baAA<u>d</u>a m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayka wa<u>da</u>-iqun bihi <u>s</u>adruka an yaqooloo lawl<u>a</u> onzila AAalayhi kanzun aw j<u>a</u>a maAAahu malakun innam<u>a</u> anta na<u>th</u>eerun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in wakeel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Perchance thou mayest (feel<br> The inclination) to give up<br> A part of what is revealed<br> Unto thee, and thy heart<br>Feeleth straitened lest they say,<br> "Why is not a treasure sent down<br> Unto him, or why does not<br> An angel come down with him?"<br> But thou art there only to warn!<br> It is God that arrangeth<br> All affairs!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Am yaqooloona iftar<u>a</u>hu qul fa/too biAAashri suwarin mithlihi muftaray<u>a</u>tin wa<b>o</b>dAAoo mani ista<u>t</u>aAAtum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Or they may say, "He forged it."<br> Say, "Bring ye then ten Sūras<br> Forged, like unto it, and call<br> (To your aid) whomsoever<br> Ye can, other than God!—<br> If ye speak the truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Fa-illam yastajeeboo lakum fa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annam<u>a</u> onzila biAAilmi All<u>a</u>hi waan l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa fahal antum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "If then they (your false gods)<br> Answer not your (call),<br> Know ye that this Revelation<br> Is sent down (replete) with the knowledge<br> Of God, and that there is<br> No god but He! Will ye<br> Even then submit (to Islam)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Man k<u>a</u>na yureedu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wazeenatah<u>a</u> nuwaffi ilayhim aAAm<u>a</u>lahum feeh<u>a</u> wahum feeh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yubkhasoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Those who desire<br> The life of the Present<br> And its glitter,—to them<br> We shall pay (the price<br> Of) their deeds therein,—<br> Without diminution.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena laysa lahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wa<u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>a m<u>a</u> <u>s</u>anaAAoo feeh<u>a</u> wab<u>at</u>ilun m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  They are those for whom<br> There is nothing in the Hereafter<br> But the Fire: vain<br> Are the designs they frame therein,<br> And of no effect<br> Are the deeds that they do!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Afaman k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin min rabbihi wayatloohu sh<u>a</u>hidun minhu wamin qablihi kit<u>a</u>bu moos<u>a</u> im<u>a</u>man wara<u>h</u>matan ol<u>a</u>-ika yu/minoona bihi waman yakfur bihi mina al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bi fa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>ru mawAAiduhu fal<u>a</u> taku fee miryatin minhu innahu al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Can they be (like) those<br> Who accept a Clear (Sign)<br> From their Lord, and whom<br> A witness from Himself<br>Doth teach, as did the Book<br> Of Moses before it,—a guide<br> And a mercy? They believe<br> Therein; but those of the Sects<br> That reject it,—the Fire<br> Will be their promised<br> Meeting-place. Be not then<br> In doubt thereon: for it is<br> The Truth from thy Lord:<br> Yet many among men<br> Do not believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban ol<u>a</u>-ika yuAAra<u>d</u>oona AAal<u>a</u> rabbihim wayaqoolu al-ashh<u>a</u>du h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>th</u>aboo AAal<u>a</u> rabbihim al<u>a</u> laAAnatu All<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Who doth more wrong<br> Than those who invent a lie<br> Against God? They will be<br> Turned back to the presence<br> Of their Lord, and the witnesses<br> Will say, "These are the ones<br> Who lied against their Lord!<br> Behold! the Curse of God<br> Is on those who do wrong!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wayabghoonah<u>a</u> AAiwajan wahum bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati hum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "Those who would hinder (men)<br> From the path of God<br> And would seek in it<br> Something crooked: these were<br> They who denied the Hereafter!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika lam yakoonoo muAAjizeena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min awliy<u>a</u>a yu<u>da</u>AAafu lahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  They will in no wise<br> Frustrate (His design) on earth,<br> Nor have they protectors<br> Besides God! Their penalty<br> Will be doubled! They lost<br> The power to hear,<br> And they did not see!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum wa<u>d</u>alla AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  They are the ones who<br> Have lost their own souls:<br>And the (fancies) they invented<br> Have left them in the lurch!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  L<u>a</u> jarama annahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati humu al-akhsaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Without a doubt, these<br> Are the very ones who<br> Will lose most in the Hereafter!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>sa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti waakhbatoo il<u>a</u> rabbihim ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  But those who believe<br> And work righteousness,<br> And humble themselves<br> Before their Lord,—<br> They will be Companions<br> Of the Garden, to dwell<br> Therein for aye!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Mathalu alfareeqayni ka<b>a</b>l<b>-</b>aAAm<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-a<u>s</u>ammi wa<b>a</b>lba<u>s</u>eeri wa<b>al</b>ssameeAAi hal yastawiy<u>a</u>ni mathalan afal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  These two kinds (of men)<br> May be compared to<br> The blind and deaf,<br> And those who can see<br> And hear well. Are they<br> Equal when compared?<br> Will ye not then take heed?</p> <div class="filenav">
