 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIII.: Najm, or the Star.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Afaraayta alla<u>th</u>ee tawall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>33.  Seest thou one<br> Who turns back,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  WaaAA<u>ta</u> qaleelan waakd<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>34.  Gives a little,<br> Then hardens (his heart)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  aAAindahu AAilmu alghaybi fahuwa yar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>35.  What! Has he knowledge<br> Of the unseen<br> So that he can see?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Am lam yunabba/ bim<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>u<u>h</u>ufi moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>36.  Nay, is he not acquainted<br> With what is in the books<br> Of Moses—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa-ibr<u>a</u>heema alla<u>th</u>ee waff<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>37.  And of Abraham<br> Who fulfilled his<br> engagements;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  All<u>a</u> taziru w<u>a</u>ziratun wizra okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>38.  Namely, that no bearer<br> Of burdens can bear<br> The burden of another;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Waan laysa lil-ins<u>a</u>ni ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> saAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>39.  That man can have nothing<br> But what he strives for;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Waanna saAAyahu sawfa yur<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>40.  That (the fruit of) his striving<br> Will soon come in sight;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Thumma yujz<u>a</u>hu aljaz<u>a</u>a al-awf<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>41.  Then will he be rewarded<br> With a reward complete;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Waanna il<u>a</u> rabbika almuntah<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>42.  That to thy Lord<br> Is the final Goal;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Waannahu huwa a<u>dh</u>aka waabk<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>43.  That it is He Who<br> Granteth Laughter and Tears;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Waannahu huwa am<u>a</u>ta waa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>44.  That it is He Who<br> Granteth Death and Life;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Waannahu khalaqa a<b>l</b>zzawjayni a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akara wa<b>a</b>l-onth<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>45.  That He did create<br> In pairs,—male and female,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Min nu<u>t</u>fatin i<u>tha</u> tumn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>46.  From a seed when lodged<br> (In its place);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Waanna AAalayhi a<b>l</b>nnash-ata al-okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>47.  That He hath promised<br> A Second Creation<br> (Raising of the Dead);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Waannahu huwa aghn<u>a</u> waaqn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>48.  That it is He Who<br> Giveth wealth and satisfaction;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waannahu huwa rabbu a<b>l</b>shshiAAr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>49.  That He is the Lord<br> Of Sirius (the Mighty Star);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Waannahu ahlaka AA<u>a</u>dan al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>50.  And that it is He<br> Who destroyed the (powerful)<br> Ancient ‘Ād (people),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wathamooda fam<u>a</u> abq<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>51.  And the <u>Th</u>amūd,<br> Nor gave them a lease<br> Of perpetual life.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Waqawma noo<u>h</u>in min qablu innahum k<u>a</u>noo hum a<i><u>th</u></i>lama waa<u>t</u>gh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>52.  And before them,<br> The people of Noah,<br> For that they were (all)<br> Most unjust<br> And most insolent transgressors,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wa<b>a</b>lmu/tafikata ahw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>53.  And He destroyed<br> The Overthrown Cities<br> (Of Sodom and Gomorrah),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Faghashsh<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ghashsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>54.  So that (ruins unknown)<br> Have covered them up.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbika tatam<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>55.  Then which of the gifts<br> Of thy Lord, (O man,)<br> Wilt thou dispute about?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  H<u>atha </u>na<u>th</u>eerun mina a<b>l</b>nnu<u>th</u>uri al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>56.  This is a Warner,<br> Of the (series of) Warners<br> Of old!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Azifati al-<u>a</u>zifat<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  The (Judgment) ever approaching<br> Draws nigh:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Laysa lah<u>a</u> min dooni All<u>a</u>hi k<u>a</u>shifat<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  No (soul) but God<br> Can lay it bare.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Afamin h<u>atha</u> al<u>h</u>adeethi taAAjaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Do ye then wonder<br> At this recital?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wata<u>dh</u>akoona wal<u>a</u> tabkoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  And will ye laugh<br> And not weep,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Waantum s<u>a</u>midoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Wasting your time<br> In vanities?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Fa<b>o</b>sjudoo lill<u>a</u>hi wa<b>o</b>AAbudoo   </p>
 <p>62.  But fall ye down in prostration<br> To God, and adore (Him)!</p>  <div class="filenav">
