 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lawl<u>a</u> onzila AAalayhi <u>a</u>yatun min rabbihi qul inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>d</u>illu man yash<u>a</u>o wayahdee ilayhi man an<u>a</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  The Unbelievers say: "Why<br> Is not a Sign sent down<br> To him from his Lord?"<br> Say: "Truly God leaveth,<br> To stray, whom He will;<br> But He guideth to Himself<br> Those who turn to Him<br> In penitence,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wata<u>t</u>ma-innu quloobuhum bi<u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi al<u>a</u> bi<u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi ta<u>t</u>ma-innu alquloob<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "Those who believe, and whose hearts<br> Find satisfaction in the remembrance<br> Of God: for without doubt<br> In the remembrance of God<br> Do hearts find satisfaction.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti <u>t</u>oob<u>a</u> lahum wa<u>h</u>usnu ma<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>29.  "For those who believe<br> And work righteousness,<br> Is (every) blessedness,<br> And a beautiful place<br> Of (final) return."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika arsaln<u>a</u>ka fee ommatin qad khalat min qablih<u>a</u> omamun litatluwa AAalayhimu alla<u>th</u>ee aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka wahum yakfuroona bi<b>al</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni qul huwa rabbee l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa AAalayhi tawakkaltu wa-ilayhi mat<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Thus have We sent thee<br> Amongst a People before whom<br> (Long since) have (other) Peoples<br> (Gone and) passed away;<br> In order that thou mightest<br> Rehearse unto them what We<br> Send down unto thee by inspiration;<br>Yet do they reject (Him),<br> The Most Gracious!<br> Say: "He is my Lord!<br> There is no god but He!<br> On Him is my trust,<br> And to Him do I turn!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Walaw anna qur-<u>a</u>nan suyyirat bihi aljib<u>a</u>lu aw qu<u>tt</u>iAAat bihi al-ar<u>d</u>u aw kullima bihi almawt<u>a</u> bal lill<u>a</u>hi al-amru jameeAAan afalam yay-asi alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo an law yash<u>a</u>o All<u>a</u>hu lahad<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa jameeAAan wal<u>a</u> yaz<u>a</u>lu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo tu<u>s</u>eebuhum bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>anaAAoo q<u>a</u>riAAatun aw ta<u>h</u>ullu qareeban min d<u>a</u>rihim <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/tiya waAAdu All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yukhlifu almeeAA<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  If there were a Qur-ān<br> With which mountains were moved,<br> Or the earth were cloven asunder,<br> Or the dead were made to speak,<br> (This would be the one!)<br> But, truly, the Command is<br> With God in all things!'<br> Do not the Believers know,<br> That, had God (so) willed,<br> He could have guided<br> All mankind (to the Right)?<br>But the Unbelievers,—never<br> Will disaster cease to seize<br> Them for their (ill) deeds,<br> Or to settle close to their homes,<br> Until the promise of God<br> Come to pass, for, verily,<br> God will not fail<br> In His promise.</p> <div class="filenav">
