 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha f<u>a</u>liqu al<u>h</u>abbi wa<b>al</b>nnaw<u>a</u> yukhriju al<u>h</u>ayya mina almayyiti wamukhriju almayyiti mina al<u>h</u>ayyi <u>tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu faann<u>a</u> tu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  It is God Who causeth<br> The seed-grain<br> And the date-stone<br> To split and sprout.<br> He causeth the living<br> To issue from the dead,<br> And He is the One<br> To cause the dead<br> To issue from the living.<br> That is God: then how<br> Are ye deluded<br> Away from the truth?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  F<u>a</u>liqu al-i<u>s</u>b<u>ah</u>i wajaAAala allayla sakanan wa<b>al</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara <u>h</u>usb<u>a</u>nan <u>tha</u>lika taqdeeru alAAazeezi alAAaleem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  He it is that cleaveth<br> The day-break (from the dark):<br> He makes the night<br> For rest and tranquillity,<br> And the sun and moon<br> For the reckoning (of time):<br> Such is the judgment<br> And ordering of (Him),<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> The Omniscient.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu a<b>l</b>nnujooma litahtadoo bih<u>a</u> fee <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti albarri wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri qad fa<u>ss</u>aln<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  It is He Who maketh<br> The stars (as beacons) for you,<br> That ye may guide yourselves,<br> With their help,<br> Through the dark spaces<br> Of land and sea:<br> We detail Our Signs<br> For people who know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee anshaakum min nafsin w<u>ah</u>idatin famustaqarrun wamustawdaAAun qad fa<u>ss</u>aln<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  It is He Who hath<br> Produced you<br> From a single person:<br> Here is a place of sojourn<br> And a place of departure:<br> We detail Our signs<br> For people who understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee anzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faakhrajn<u>a</u> bihi nab<u>a</u>ta kulli shay-in faakhrajn<u>a</u> minhu kha<u>d</u>iran nukhriju minhu <u>h</u>abban mutar<u>a</u>kiban wamina a<b>l</b>nnakhli min <u>t</u>alAAih<u>a</u> qinw<u>a</u>nun d<u>a</u>niyatun wajann<u>a</u>tin min aAAn<u>a</u>bin wa<b>al</b>zzaytoona wa<b>al</b>rrumm<u>a</u>na mushtabihan waghayra mutash<u>a</u>bihin on<i><u>th</u></i>uroo il<u>a</u> thamarihi i<u>tha</u> athmara wayanAAihi inna fee <u>tha</u>likum la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  It is He Who sendeth down<br> Rain from the skies:<br> With it We produce<br> Vegetation of all kinds:<br> From some We produce<br> Green (crops), out of which<br> We produce grain,<br> Heaped up (at harvest);<br> Out of the date-palm<br> And its sheaths (or spathes)<br> (Come) clusters of dates<br> Hanging low and near:<br> And (then there are) gardens<br> Of grapes, and olives,<br> And pomegranates,<br> Each similar (in kind)<br> Yet different (in variety):<br> When they begin to bear fruit,<br> Feast your eyes with the fruit<br> And the ripeness thereof.<br> Behold! in these things<br> There are Signs for people<br> Who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  WajaAAaloo lill<u>a</u>hi shurak<u>a</u>a aljinna wakhalaqahum wakharaqoo lahu baneena waban<u>a</u>tin bighayri AAilmin sub<u>ha</u>nahu wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  Yet they make<br> The Jinns equals<br> With God, though God<br> Did create the Jinns;<br> And they falsely,<br> Having no knowledge,<br> Attribute to Him<br> Sons and daughters.<br> Praise and glory be<br> To Him! (for He is) above<br> What they attribute to Him!</p> <div class="filenav">
