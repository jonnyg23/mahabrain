 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la li-abeehi waqawmihi m<u>a</u> taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  And rehearse to them<br> (Something of) Abraham's story.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Q<u>a</u>loo naAAbudu a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>man fana<i><u>th</u></i>allu lah<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>kifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Behold, he said<br> To his father and his people:<br> "What worship ye?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Q<u>a</u>la hal yasmaAAoonakum i<u>th</u> tadAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  They said: "We worship<br> Idols, and we remain constantly<br> In attendance on them."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Aw yanfaAAoonakum aw ya<u>d</u>urroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  He said: "Do they listen<br> To you when ye call (on them),<br> Or do you good or harm?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Q<u>a</u>loo bal wajadn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  They said: "Nay, but we<br> Found our fathers doing<br> Thus (what we do)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Q<u>a</u>la afaraaytum m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  He said: "Do ye then<br> See whom ye have been<br> Worshipping,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Antum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okumu al-aqdamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  "Ye and your fathers before you?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Fa-innahum AAaduwwun lee ill<u>a</u> rabba alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  "For they are enemies to me;<br> Not so the Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Worlds;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqanee fahuwa yahdeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  "Who created me, and<br> It is He who guides me;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee huwa yu<u>t</u>AAimunee wayasqeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  "Who gives me food and drink,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> mari<u>d</u>tu fahuwa yashfeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  "And when I am ill,<br> It is He who cures me;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee yumeetunee thumma yu<u>h</u>yeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "Who will cause me to die,<br> And then to live (again);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee a<u>t</u>maAAu an yaghfira lee kha<u>t</u>ee-atee yawma a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  "And who, I hope,<br> Will forgive me my faults<br> On the Day of Judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Rabbi hab lee <u>h</u>ukman waal<u>h</u>iqnee bi<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  "O my Lord! bestow wisdom<br> On me, and join me<br> With the righteous;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wa<b>i</b>jAAal lee lis<u>a</u>na <u>s</u>idqin fee al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  "Grant me honourable mention<br> On the tongue of truth<br> Among the latest (generations);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wa<b>i</b>jAAalnee min warathati jannati a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  "Make me one of the inheritors<br> Of the Garden of Bliss;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wa<b>i</b>ghfir li-abee innahu k<u>a</u>na mina a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  "Forgive my father, for that<br> He is among those astray;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wal<u>a</u> tukhzinee yawma yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  "And let me not be<br> In disgrace on the Day<br> When (men) will be raised up;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Yawma l<u>a</u> yanfaAAu m<u>a</u>lun wal<u>a</u> banoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  "The Day whereon neither<br> Wealth nor sons will avail,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Ill<u>a</u> man at<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha biqalbin saleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  "But only he (will prosper)<br> That brings to God<br> A sound heart;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Waozlifati aljannatu lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  "To the righteous, the Garden<br> Will be brought near,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Waburrizati alja<u>h</u>eemu lilgh<u>a</u>ween<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  "And to those straying in Evil,<br> The Fire will be placed<br> In full view;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Waqeela lahum ayna m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  "And it shall be said<br> To them, 'Where are<br> The (gods) ye worshipped—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Min dooni All<u>a</u>hi hal yan<u>s</u>uroonakum aw yanta<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  "'Besides God? Can they<br> Help you or help themselves?'</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Fakubkiboo feeh<u>a</u> hum wa<b>a</b>lgh<u>a</u>woon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  "Then they will be thrown<br> Headlong into the (Fire),—<br> They and those straying<br> In Evil,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Wajunoodu ibleesa ajmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  "And the whole hosts<br> Of Iblīs together.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Q<u>a</u>loo wahum feeh<u>a</u> yakhta<u>s</u>imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  "They will say there<br> In their mutual bickerings:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi in kunn<u>a</u> lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  "'By God, we were truly<br> In an error manifest,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  I<u>th</u> nusawweekum birabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  "'When we held you as equals<br> With the Lord of the Worlds;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Wam<u>a</u> a<u>d</u>allan<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> almujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  "'And our seducers were<br> Only those who were<br> Steeped in guilt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Fam<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> min sh<u>a</u>fiAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  "'Now, then, we have none<br> To intercede (for us),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Wal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>adeeqin <u>h</u>ameem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  "'Nor a single friend<br> To feel (for us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Falaw anna lan<u>a</u> karratan fanakoona mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  "'Now if we only had<br> A chance of return,<br> We shall truly be<br> Of those who believe!'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  Verily in this is a Sign<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
