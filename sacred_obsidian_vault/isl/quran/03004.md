 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXX.: Rūm, or The Roman Empire.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  <u>D</u>araba lakum mathalan min anfusikum hal lakum mimm<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum min shurak<u>a</u>a fee m<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum faantum feehi saw<u>a</u>on takh<u>a</u>foonahum kakheefatikum anfusakum ka<u>tha</u>lika nufa<u>ss</u>ilu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  He does propound<br> To you a similitude<br> From your own (experience):<br> Do ye have partners<br> Among those whom your right hands<br> Possess, to share as equals<br> In the wealth We have<br>Bestowed on you? Do ye<br> Fear them as ye fear<br> Each other? Thus do We<br> Explain the Signs in detail<br> To a people that understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Bali ittabaAAa alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo ahw<u>a</u>ahum bighayri AAilmin faman yahdee man a<u>d</u>alla All<u>a</u>hu wam<u>a</u> lahum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Nay, the wrong-doers (merely)<br> Follow their own lusts,<br> Being devoid of knowledge.<br> But who will guide those<br> Whom God leaves astray?<br> To them there will be<br> No helpers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Faaqim wajhaka li<b>l</b>ddeeni <u>h</u>aneefan fi<u>t</u>rata All<u>a</u>hi allatee fa<u>t</u>ara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa AAalayh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tabdeela likhalqi All<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>lika a<b>l</b>ddeenu alqayyimu wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  So set thou thy face<br> Steadily and truly to the Faith:<br> (Establish) God's handiwork according<br> To the pattern on which<br> He has made mankind:<br> No change (let there be)<br> In the work (wrought)<br>By God: that is<br> The standard Religion<br> But most among mankind<br> Understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Muneebeena ilayhi wa<b>i</b>ttaqoohu waaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wal<u>a</u> takoonoo mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Turn ye back in repentance<br> To Him, and fear Him:<br> Establish regular prayers,<br> And be not ye among those<br> Who join gods with God,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Mina alla<u>th</u>eena farraqoo deenahum wak<u>a</u>noo shiyaAAan kullu <u>h</u>izbin bim<u>a</u> ladayhim fari<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Those who split up<br> Their Religion, and become<br> (Mere) Sects,—each party<br> Rejoicing in that which<br> Is with itself!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> massa a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa <u>d</u>urrun daAAaw rabbahum muneebeena ilayhi thumma i<u>tha</u> a<u>tha</u>qahum minhu ra<u>h</u>matan i<u>tha</u> fareequn minhum birabbihim yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  When trouble touches men,<br> They cry to their Lord,<br> Turning back to Him<br> In repentance: but when<br> He gives them a taste<br> Of Mercy as from Himself,<br> Behold, some of them<br> Pay part-worship to<br> Other god's besides their Lord,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Liyakfuroo bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum fatamattaAAoo fasawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  (As if) to show their ingratitude<br> For the (favours) We have<br> Bestowed on them! Then enjoy<br> (Your brief day); but soon<br>Will ye know (your folly).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Am anzaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim sul<u>ta</u>nan fahuwa yatakallamu bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Or have We sent down<br> Authority to them, which<br> Points out to them<br> The things to which<br> They pay part-worship?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa<b>i</b><u>tha</u> a<u>th</u>aqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa ra<u>h</u>matan fari<u>h</u>oo bih<u>a</u> wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibhum sayyi-atun bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeehim i<u>tha</u> hum yaqna<u>t</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  When We give men<br> A taste of Mercy,<br> They exult thereat:<br> And when-some evil<br> Afflicts them because of<br> What their (own) hands<br> Have sent forth, behold,<br> They are in despair!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Awa lam yaraw anna All<u>a</u>ha yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o wayaqdiru inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  See they not that God<br> Enlarges the provision and<br> Restricts it, to whomsoever<br> He pleases? Verily in that<br> Are Signs for those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Fa<u>a</u>ti <u>tha</u> alqurb<u>a</u> <u>h</u>aqqahu wa<b>a</b>lmiskeena wa<b>i</b>bna a<b>l</b>ssabeeli <u>tha</u>lika khayrun lilla<u>th</u>eena yureedoona wajha All<u>a</u>hi waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  So give what is due<br> To kindred, the needy,<br> And the wayfarer.<br> That is best for those<br>Who seek the Countenance,<br> Of God, and it is they<br> Who will prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytum min riban liyarbuwa fee amw<u>a</u>li a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fal<u>a</u> yarboo AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytum min zak<u>a</u>tin tureedoona wajha All<u>a</u>hi faol<u>a</u>-ika humu almu<u>d</u>AAifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  That which ye lay out<br> For increase through the property<br> Of (other) people, will have<br> No increase with God:<br> But that which ye lay out<br> For charity, seeking<br> The Countenance of God,<br> (Will increase): it is<br> These who twill get<br> A recompense multiplied.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum thumma razaqakum thumma yumeetukum thumma yu<u>h</u>yeekum hal min shurak<u>a</u>-ikum man yafAAalu min <u>tha</u>likum min shay-in sub<u>ha</u>nahu wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  It is God Who has<br> Created you: further, He has<br> Provided for your sustenance;<br> Then He will cause you<br> To die; and again He will<br> Give you life. Are there<br> Any of your (false) "Partners"<br> Who can do any single<br> One of these things?<br> Glory to Him! and High<br> Is He above the partners<br> They attribute (to Him)!</p> <div class="filenav">
