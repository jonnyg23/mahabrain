 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XX.: Ṭā-Hā. (Mystic Letters, Ṭ. H.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ishra<u>h</u> lee <u>s</u>adree   </p>
 <p>25.  (Moses) said: "O my Lord!<br> Expand me my breast;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wayassir lee amree   </p>
 <p>26.  "Ease my task for me;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa<b>o</b><u>h</u>lul AAuqdatan min lis<u>a</u>nee   </p>
 <p>27.  "And remove the impediment<br> From my speech,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Yafqahoo qawlee   </p>
 <p>28.  "So they may understand<br> What I say:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wa<b>i</b>jAAal lee wazeeran min ahlee   </p>
 <p>29.  "And give me a Minister<br> From my family,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  H<u>a</u>roona akhee   </p>
 <p>30.  "Aaron, my brother;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Oshdud bihi azree   </p>
 <p>31.  "Add to my strength<br> Through him,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Waashrik-hu fee amree   </p>
 <p>32.  "And make him share<br> My task:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Kay nusabbi<u>h</u>aka katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "That we may celebrate<br> Thy praise without stint,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wana<u>th</u>kuraka katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "And remember Thee<br> Without stint:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Innaka kunta bin<u>a</u> ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "For Thou art He<br> That (ever) regardeth us."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Q<u>a</u>la qad ooteeta su/laka y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>36.  (God) said: "Granted<br> Is thy prayer, O Moses!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Walaqad manann<u>a</u> AAalayka marratan okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>37.  "And indeed We conferred<br> A favour on thee<br> Another time (before).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  I<u>th</u> aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ommika m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u>   </p>
 <p>38.  "Behold! We sent<br> To thy mother, by inspiration,<br> The message:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Ani iq<u>th</u>ifeehi fee a<b>l</b>tt<u>a</u>booti fa<b>i</b>q<u>th</u>ifeehi fee alyammi falyulqihi alyammu bi<b>al</b>ss<u>ah</u>ili ya/khu<u>th</u>hu AAaduwwun lee waAAaduwwun lahu waalqaytu AAalayka ma<u>h</u>abbatan minnee walitu<u>s</u>naAAa AAal<u>a</u> AAaynee   </p>
 <p>39.  "Throw (the child)<br> Into the chest, and throw<br> (The chest) into the river:<br> The river will cast him<br> Up on the bank, and he<br> Will be taken up by one<br> Who is an enemy to Me<br> And an enemy to him<br> But I cast (the garment<br> Of) love over thee from Me<br> And (this) in order that<br> Thou mayest be reared<br> Under Mine eye.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  I<u>th</u> tamshee okhtuka fataqoolu hal adullukum AAal<u>a</u> man yakfuluhu farajaAAn<u>a</u>ka il<u>a</u> ommika kay taqarra AAaynuh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zana waqatalta nafsan fanajjayn<u>a</u>ka mina alghammi wafatann<u>a</u>ka futoonan falabithta sineena fee ahli madyana thumma ji/ta AAal<u>a</u> qadarin y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>40.  "Behold! thy sister goeth forth<br> And saith, "Shall I show you<br>One who will nurse<br> And rear the (child)?<br> So We brought thee back<br> To thy mother, that her eye<br> Might be cooled and she<br> Should not grieve.<br> Then thou didst slay<br> A man, but We saved thee<br> From trouble, and We tried<br> Thee in various ways.<br> Then didst thou tarry<br> A number of years<br> With the people of Midian.<br> Then didst thou come hither<br> As ordained, O Moses!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<b>i</b><u>st</u>anaAAtuka linafsee   </p>
 <p>41.  "And I have prepared thee<br> For Myself (for service)"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  I<u>th</u>hab anta waakhooka bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee wal<u>a</u> taniy<u>a</u> fee <u>th</u>ikree   </p>
 <p>42.  "Go, thou and thy brother,<br> With My Signs,<br>And slacken not,<br> Either of you, in keeping<br> Me in remembrance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  I<u>th</u>hab<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> firAAawna innahu <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>43.  "Go, both of you, to Pharaoh,<br> For he has indeed<br> Transgressed all bounds;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Faqool<u>a</u> lahu qawlan layyinan laAAallahu yata<u>th</u>akkaru aw yakhsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>44.  "But speak to him mildly;<br> Perchance he may take<br> Warning or fear (God)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Q<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> innan<u>a</u> nakh<u>a</u>fu an yafru<u>t</u>a AAalayn<u>a</u> aw an ya<u>t</u>gh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>45.  They (Moses and Aaron) said:<br> "Our Lord! We fear lest<br> He hasten with insolence<br> Against us, or lest he<br> Transgress all bounds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>f<u>a</u> innanee maAAakum<u>a</u> asmaAAu waar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>46.  He said: "Fear not:<br> For I am with you:<br> I hear and see (everything).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Fa/tiy<u>a</u>hu faqool<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> rasool<u>a</u> rabbika faarsil maAAan<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela wal<u>a</u> tuAAa<u>thth</u>ibhum qad ji/n<u>a</u>ka bi-<u>a</u>yatin min rabbika wa<b>al</b>ssal<u>a</u>mu AAal<u>a</u> mani ittabaAAa alhud<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>47.  "So go ye both to him,<br> And say, "Verily we are<br> Apostles sent by thy Lord:<br> Send forth, therefore, the Children<br> Of Israel with us, and<br> Afflict them not:<br> With a Sign, indeed,<br> Have we come from thy Lord!<br>And Peace to all<br> Who follow guidance!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Inn<u>a</u> qad oo<u>h</u>iya ilayn<u>a</u> anna alAAa<u>tha</u>ba AAal<u>a</u> man ka<u>thth</u>aba watawall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>48.  "Verily it has been revealed<br> To us that the Penalty<br> (Awaits) those who reject<br> And turn away."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Q<u>a</u>la faman rabbukum<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>49.  (When this message was delivered),<br> (Pharaoh) said: "Who, then,<br> O Moses, is the Lord<br> Of you two?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbun<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee aAA<u>ta</u> kulla shay-in khalqahu thumma had<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>50.  He said: "Our Lord is<br> He Who gave to each<br> (Created) thing its form<br> And nature, and further,<br> Gave (it) guidance."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Q<u>a</u>la fam<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>lu alqurooni al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>51.  (Pharaoh) said: "What then<br> Is the condition<br> Of previous generations?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Q<u>a</u>la AAilmuh<u>a</u> AAinda rabbee fee kit<u>a</u>bin l<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>illu rabbee wal<u>a</u> yans<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>52.  He replied: "The knowledge<br> Of that is with my Lord,<br> Duly recorded: my Lord<br> Never errs, nor forgets,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu al-ar<u>d</u>a mahdan wasalaka lakum feeh<u>a</u> subulan waanzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faakhrajn<u>a</u> bihi azw<u>a</u>jan min nab<u>a</u>tin shatt<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>53.  "He Who has made for you<br> The earth like a carpet<br> Spread out; has enabled you<br> To go about therein by roads<br> (And channels); and has sent<br> Down water from the sky."<br> With it have We produced<br> Divers pairs of plants<br> Each separate front the others.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Kuloo wa<b>i</b>rAAaw anAA<u>a</u>makum inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin li-olee a<b>l</b>nnuh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>54.  Eat (for yourselves) and pasture<br> Your cattle: verily, in this<br> Are Signs for men<br> Endued with understanding.</p> <div class="filenav">
