 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLII.: Shūrā, or Consultation.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wam<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bakum min mu<u>s</u>eebatin fabim<u>a</u> kasabat aydeekum wayaAAfoo AAan katheer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Whatever misfortune<br> Happens to you, is because<br>Of the things your hands<br> Have wrought, and for many<br> (Of them) He grants forgiveness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> lakum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Nor can ye frustrate (aught),<br> (Fleeing) through the earth;<br> Nor have ye, besides God,<br> Any one to protect<br> Or to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi aljaw<u>a</u>ri fee alba<u>h</u>ri ka<b>a</b>l-aAAl<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And among His Signs<br> Are the ships, smooth-running<br> Through the ocean, (tall)<br> As mountains.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  In yasha/ yuskini a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>a faya<i><u>th</u></i>lalna raw<u>a</u>kida AAal<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ahrihi inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin likulli <u>s</u>abb<u>a</u>rin shakoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  If it be His Will,<br> He can still the Wind:<br> Then would they become<br> Motionless on the back<br> Of the (ocean). Verily<br> In this are Signs<br> For everyone who patiently<br> Perseveres and is grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Aw yoobiqhunna bim<u>a</u> kasaboo wayaAAfu AAan katheer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Or He can cause them<br> To perish because of<br> The (evil) which (the men)<br> Have earned; but much<br> Doth He forgive.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  WayaAAlama alla<u>th</u>eena yuj<u>a</u>diloona fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lahum min ma<u>h</u>ee<u>s</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  But let those know, who<br> Dispute about Our Signs,<br> That there is for them<br> No way of escape.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fam<u>a</u> ooteetum min shay-in famat<u>a</u>AAu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi khayrun waabq<u>a</u> lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAal<u>a</u> rabbihim yatawakkaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Whatever ye are given (here)<br> Is (but) a convenience<br> Of this Life: but that<br> Which is with God<br> Is better and more lasting:<br> (It is) for those who believe<br> And put their trust<br> In their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yajtaniboona kab<u>a</u>-ira al-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lfaw<u>ah</u>isha wa-i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> gha<u>d</u>iboo hum yaghfiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Those who avoid the greater<br> Crimes and shameful deeds,<br> And, when they are angry<br> Even then forgive;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena istaj<u>a</u>boo lirabbihim waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta waamruhum shoor<u>a</u> baynahum wamimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum yunfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Those who hearken<br> To their Lord, and establish<br>Regular prayer; who (conduct)<br> Their affairs by mutual<br> Consultation;<br> Who spend out of what<br> We bestow on them<br> For Sustenance;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> a<u>sa</u>bahumu albaghyu hum yanta<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  And those who, when<br> An oppressive wrong is inflicted<br> On them, (are not cowed<br> But) help and defend themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wajaz<u>a</u>o sayyi-atin sayyi-atun mithluh<u>a</u> faman AAaf<u>a</u> waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a faajruhu AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innahu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  The recompense for an injury<br> Is an injury equal thereto<br> (In degree): but if a person<br> Forgives and makes reconciliation,<br>His reward is due<br> From God: for (God)<br> Loveth not those who<br> Do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Walamani inta<u>s</u>ara baAAda <i><u>th</u></i>ulmihi faol<u>a</u>-ika m<u>a</u> AAalayhim min sabeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  But indeed if any do help<br> And defend themselves<br> After a wrong (done)<br> To them, against such<br> There is no cause<br> Of blame.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Innam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssabeelu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa wayabghoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  The blame is only<br> Against those who oppress<br> Men with wrong-doing<br> And insolently transgress<br> Beyond bounds through the land,<br> Defying right and justice:<br> For such there will be<br> A Penalty grievous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Walaman <u>s</u>abara waghafara inna <u>tha</u>lika lamin AAazmi al-omoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  But indeed if any<br> Show patience and forgive,<br> That would truly be<br> An exercise of courageous will<br> And resolution in the conduct<br> Of affairs.</p> <div class="filenav">
