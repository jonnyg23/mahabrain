 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Y<u>a</u> banee <u>a</u>dama qad anzaln<u>a</u> AAalaykum lib<u>a</u>san yuw<u>a</u>ree saw-<u>a</u>tikum wareeshan walib<u>a</u>su a<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika khayrun <u>tha</u>lika min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi laAAallahum ya<u>thth</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  O ye Children of Adam!<br> We have bestowed raiment<br> Upon you to cover<br> Your shame, as well as<br> To be an adornment to you.<br> But the raiment of righteousness,—<br> That is the best.<br> Such are among the Signs<br> Of God, that they<br> May receive admonition!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Y<u>a</u> banee <u>a</u>dama l<u>a</u> yaftinannakumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu kam<u>a</u> akhraja abawaykum mina aljannati yanziAAu AAanhum<u>a</u> lib<u>a</u>sahum<u>a</u> liyuriyahum<u>a</u> saw-<u>a</u>tihim<u>a</u> innahu yar<u>a</u>kum huwa waqabeeluhu min <u>h</u>aythu l<u>a</u> tarawnahum inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena awliy<u>a</u>a lilla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  O ye Children of Adam!<br> Let not Satan seduce you,<br> In the same manner as<br> He got your parents out<br> Of the Garden, stripping them<br> Of their raiment, to expose<br> Their shame: for he<br> And his tribe watch you<br> From a position where ye<br> Cannot see them: We made<br> The Evil Ones friends<br> (Only) to those without Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> faAAaloo f<u>ah</u>ishatan q<u>a</u>loo wajadn<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu amaran<u>a</u> bih<u>a</u> qul inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> ya/muru bi<b>a</b>lfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>-i ataqooloona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  When they do aught<br> That is shameful, they say:<br> "We found our fathers<br> Doing so"; and "God<br> Commanded us thus":<br> Say: "Nay, God never<br>Commands what is shameful:<br> Do ye say of God<br> What ye know not?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Qul amara rabbee bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i waaqeemoo wujoohakum AAinda kulli masjidin wa<b>o</b>dAAoohu mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a<b>l</b>ddeena kam<u>a</u> badaakum taAAoodoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Say: "My Lord hath commanded<br> Justice; and that ye set<br> Your whole selves (to Him)<br> At every time and place<br> Of prayer, and call upon Him,<br> Making your devotion sincere<br> As in His sight:<br> Such as He created you<br> In the beginning, so<br> Shall ye return."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Fareeqan had<u>a</u> wafareeqan <u>h</u>aqqa AAalayhimu a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>latu innahumu ittakha<u>th</u>oo a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena awliy<u>a</u>a min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waya<u>h</u>saboona annahum muhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Some He hath guided:<br> Others have (by their choice)<br> Deserved the loss of their way;<br> In that they took<br> The Evil Ones, in preference<br> To God, for their friends<br> And protectors, and think<br> That they receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Y<u>a</u> banee <u>a</u>dama khu<u>th</u>oo zeenatakum AAinda kulli masjidin wakuloo wa<b>i</b>shraboo wal<u>a</u> tusrifoo innahu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu almusrifeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>31.  O Children of Adam!<br> Wear your beautiful apparel<br> At every time and place<br> Of prayer: eat and drink:<br> But waste not by excess,<br> For God loveth not the wasters.</p> <div class="filenav">
