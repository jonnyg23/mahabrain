 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVI.: Yā-Sīn (being Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa<b>i</b><u>d</u>rib lahum mathalan a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba alqaryati i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ah<u>a</u> almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Set forth to them,<br> By way of a parable,<br> The (story of) the Companions<br> Of the City. Behold,<br> There came apostles to it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  I<u>th</u> arsaln<u>a</u> ilayhimu ithnayni faka<u>thth</u>aboohum<u>a</u> faAAazzazn<u>a</u> bith<u>a</u>lithin faq<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> ilaykum mursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  When We (first) sent<br> To them two apostles,<br> They rejected them:<br> But We strengthened them<br> With a third: they said,<br> "Truly, we have been sent<br> On a mission to you."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Q<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> antum ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlun<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> anzala a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu min shay-in in antum ill<u>a</u> tak<u>th</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  The (people) said: "Ye are<br> Only men like ourselves<br> And (God) Most Gracious<br> Sends no sort of revelation:<br> Ye do nothing but lie."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Q<u>a</u>loo rabbun<u>a</u> yaAAlamu inn<u>a</u> ilaykum lamursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  They said: "Our Lord doth<br> Know that we have been sent<br> On a mission to you:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wam<u>a</u> AAalayn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "And our duty is only<br> To proclaim the clear Message.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> ta<u>t</u>ayyarn<u>a</u> bikum la-in lam tantahoo lanarjumannakum walayamassannakum minn<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  The (people) said: "For us,<br> We augur an evil omen<br>From you: if ye desist not,<br> We will certainly stone you.<br> And a grievous punishment<br> Indeed will be inflicted<br> On you by us."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Q<u>a</u>loo <u>ta</u>-irukum maAAakum a-in <u>th</u>ukkirtum bal antum qawmun musrifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  They said: "Your evil omens<br> Are with yourselves:<br> (Deem ye this an evil omen).<br> If ye are admonished?<br> Nay, but ye are a people<br> Transgressing all bounds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Waj<u>a</u>a min aq<u>sa</u> almadeenati rajulun yasAA<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi ittabiAAoo almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Then there came running,<br> From the farthest part<br> Of the City, a man,<br> Saying, "O my People!<br> Obey the apostles:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  IttabiAAoo man l<u>a</u> yas-alukum ajran wahum muhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  "Obey those who ask<br> No reward of you<br> (For themselves), and who have<br> Themselves received Guidance</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wam<u>a</u> liya l<u>a</u> aAAbudu alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>aranee wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Aattakhi<u>th</u>u min doonihi <u>a</u>lihatan in yuridni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu bi<u>d</u>urrin l<u>a </u>tughni AAannee shaf<u>a</u>AAatuhum shay-an wal<u>a</u> yunqi<u>th</u>oon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "Shall I take (other) gods<br> Besides Him? If (God)<br> Most Gracious should<br> Intend some adversity for me,<br> Of no use whatever<br> Will be their intercession<br> For me, nor can they<br> Deliver me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Innee i<u>th</u>an lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "I would indeed,<br> If I were to do so,<br> Be in manifest Error.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Innee <u>a</u>mantu birabbikum fa<b>i</b>smaAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "For me, I have faith<br> In the Lord of you (all):<br> Listen, then, to me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Qeela odkhuli aljannata q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> layta qawmee yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  It was said: "Enter thou<br> The Garden." He said:<br> "Ah me! Would that<br> My People knew (what I know)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Bim<u>a</u> ghafara lee rabbee wajaAAalanee mina almukrameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "For that my Lord<br> Has granted me Forgiveness<br> And has enrolled me<br> Among those held in honour!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wam<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> qawmihi min baAAdihi min jundin mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> munzileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And We sent not down<br> Against his People, after him,<br> Any hosts from heaven,<br> Nor was it needful<br> For Us so to do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  In k<u>a</u>nat ill<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atan w<u>ah</u>idatan fa-i<u>tha</u> hum kh<u>a</u>midoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  It was no more than<br> A single mighty Blast,<br> And behold! they were (like ashes)<br> Quenched and silent.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Y<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asratan AAal<u>a</u> alAAib<u>a</u>di m<u>a</u> ya/teehim min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Ah! alas for (My) servants!<br> There comes not an apostle<br> To them but they mock him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Alam yaraw kam ahlakn<u>a</u> qablahum mina alqurooni annahum ilayhim l<u>a</u> yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  See they not how many<br> Generations before them<br> We destroyed? Not to them<br> Will they return:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa-in kullun lamm<u>a</u> jameeAAun ladayn<u>a</u> mu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  But each one of them<br> All—will be brought<br> Before Us (for judgment).</p> <div class="filenav">
