 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXV.: Burūj, or The Zodiacal Signs.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i <u>tha</u>ti alburooj<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Sky, (displaying)<br> The Zodiacal Signs;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>a</b>lyawmi almawAAood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  By the promised Day<br> (Of Judgment);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wash<u>a</u>hidin wamashhood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  By one that witnesses,<br> And the subject of the witness;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Qutila a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alukhdood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Woe to the makers<br> Of the pit (of fire),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  A<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri <u>tha</u>ti alwaqood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Fire supplied (abundantly)<br> With Fuel:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  I<u>th</u> hum AAalayh<u>a</u> quAAood<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Behold! they sat<br> Over against the (fire),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wahum AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yafAAaloona bi<b>a</b>lmu/mineena shuhood<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And they witnessed<br> (All) that they were doing<br> Against the Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wam<u>a</u> naqamoo minhum ill<u>a</u> an yu/minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>ameed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And they ill-treated them<br> For no other reason than<br> That they believed in God,<br> Exalted in Power,<br> Worthy of all Praise!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Alla<u>th</u>ee lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena fatanoo almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti thumma lam yatooboo falahum AAa<u>tha</u>bu jahannama walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bu al<u>h</u>areeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Those who persecute (or draw<br> into temptation)<br> The Believers, men and women,<br> And do not turn<br> In repentance, will have<br> The Penalty of Hell:<br> They will have the Penalty<br> Of the Burning Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum jann<u>a</u>tun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru <u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alkabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  For those who believe<br> And do righteous deeds,<br> Will be Gardens,<br> Beneath which Rivers flow:<br> That is the great Salvation,<br> (The fulfilment of all desires),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Inna ba<u>t</u>sha rabbika lashadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Truly strong is the Grip<br> (And Power) of thy Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Innahu huwa yubdi-o wayuAAeed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  It is He Who creates<br> From the very beginning,<br> And He can restore (life).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wahuwa alghafooru alwadood<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And He is the Oft-Forgiving,<br> Full of loving-kindness,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  <u>Th</u>oo alAAarshi almajeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Lord of the Throne of Glory,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  FaAAAA<u>a</u>lun lim<u>a</u> yureed<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>16.  Doer (without let)<br> Of all that He intends.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Hal at<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>adeethu aljunood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Has the story<br> Reached thee,<br> Of the Forces—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  FirAAawna wathamood<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Of Pharaoh<br> And the <u>Th</u>amūd?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Bali alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fee tak<u>th</u>eeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And yet the Unbelievers<br> (Persist) in rejecting<br> (The Truth)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu min war<u>a</u>-ihim mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  But God doth<br> Encompass them<br> From behind!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Bal huwa qur-<u>a</u>nun majeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Nay, this is<br> A Glorious Qur-ān,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/085/085_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Fee law<u>h</u>in ma<u>h</u>foo<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  (Inscribed) in<br> A Tablet Preserved!</p>  <div class="filenav">
