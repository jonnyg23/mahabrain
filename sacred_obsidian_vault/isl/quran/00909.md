 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Almun<u>a</u>fiqoona wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiq<u>a</u>tu baAA<u>d</u>uhum min baAA<u>d</u>in ya/muroona bi<b>a</b>lmunkari wayanhawna AAani almaAAroofi wayaqbi<u>d</u>oona aydiyahum nasoo All<u>a</u>ha fanasiyahum inna almun<u>a</u>fiqeena humu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  The Hypocrites, men and women,<br> (Have an understanding) with each other:<br>They enjoin evil, and forbid<br> What is just, and are close<br> With their hands. They have<br> Forgotten God; so He<br> Hath forgotten them. Verily<br> The Hypocrites are rebellious<br> And perverse.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  WaAAada All<u>a</u>hu almun<u>a</u>fiqeena wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lkuff<u>a</u>ra n<u>a</u>ra jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> hiya <u>h</u>asbuhum walaAAanahumu All<u>a</u>hu walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  God hath promised the Hypocrites<br> Men and women, and the rejecters,<br> Of Faith, the fire of Hell:<br> Therein shall they dwell:<br> Sufficient is it for them:<br> For them is the curse of God,<br> And an enduring punishment,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum k<u>a</u>noo ashadda minkum quwwatan waakthara amw<u>a</u>lan waawl<u>a</u>dan fa<b>i</b>stamtaAAoo bikhal<u>a</u>qihim fa<b>i</b>stamtaAAtum bikhal<u>a</u>qikum kam<u>a</u> istamtaAAa alla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum bikhal<u>a</u>qihim wakhu<u>d</u>tum ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee kh<u>ad</u>oo ol<u>a</u>-ika <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati waol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  As in the case of those<br> Before you: they were<br> Mightier than you in power,<br> And more flourishing in wealth<br> And children. They had<br> Their enjoyment of their portion:<br> And ye have of yours, as did<br> Those before you; and ye<br> Indulge in idle talk<br> As they did. They!—<br> Their works are fruitless<br> In this world and in the Hereafter,<br> And they will lose<br> (All spiritual good).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Alam ya/tihim nabao alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim qawmi noo<u>h</u>in waAA<u>a</u>din wathamooda waqawmi ibr<u>a</u>heema waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi madyana wa<b>a</b>lmu/tafik<u>a</u>ti atat-hum rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liya<i><u>th</u></i>limahum wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Hath not the story reached them<br> Of those before them?—<br> The people of Noah, and ’Ād,<br> And <u>Th</u>amūd; the people<br>Of Abraham, the men<br> Of Midian, and the Cities overthrown.<br> To them came their apostles<br> With Clear Signs. It is<br> Not God Who wrongs them,<br> But they wrong their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa<b>a</b>lmu/minoona wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>tu baAA<u>d</u>uhum awliy<u>a</u>o baAA<u>d</u>in ya/muroona bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wayanhawna AAani almunkari wayuqeemoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wayu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wayu<u>t</u>eeAAoona All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu ol<u>a</u>-ika sayar<u>h</u>amuhumu All<u>a</u>hu inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  The Believers, men<br> And women, are protectors,<br> One of another: they enjoin<br> What is just, and forbid<br> What is evil: they observe<br> Regular prayers, practise<br> Regular charity, and obey<br> God and His Apostle.<br> On them will God pour<br> His mercy: for God<br> Is Exalted in power, Wise:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  WaAAada All<u>a</u>hu almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> wamas<u>a</u>kina <u>t</u>ayyibatan fee jann<u>a</u>ti AAadnin wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nun mina All<u>a</u>hi akbaru <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  God hath promised to Believers,<br> Men and women, Gardens<br> Under which rivers flow,<br> To dwell therein,<br> And beautiful mansions<br> In Gardens of everlasting bliss.<br> But the greatest bliss<br> Is the Good Pleasure of God:<br> That is the supreme felicity.</p> <div class="filenav">
