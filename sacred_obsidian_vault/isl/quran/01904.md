 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIX.: Maryam, or Mary.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur fee alkit<u>a</u>bi moos<u>a</u> innahu k<u>a</u>na mukhla<u>s</u>an wak<u>a</u>na rasoolan nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Also mention in the Book<br> (The story of) Moses:<br> For he was specially chosen,<br> And he was an apostle<br> (And) a prophet.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wan<u>a</u>dayn<u>a</u>hu min j<u>a</u>nibi a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oori al-aymani waqarrabn<u>a</u>hu najiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  And We called him<br> From the right side<br> Of Mount (Sinai), and made<br> Him draw near to Us,<br> For mystic (converse).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu min ra<u>h</u>matin<u>a</u> akh<u>a</u>hu h<u>a</u>roona nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  And, out of Our Mercy,<br> We gave him his brother<br> Aaron, (also.) a prophet.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur fee alkit<u>a</u>bi ism<u>a</u>AAeela innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>sa</u>diqa alwaAAdi wak<u>a</u>na rasoolan nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Also mention in the Book<br> (The story of) Ismā‘īl<br> He was (strictly) true<br> To what he promised,<br> And he was an apostle<br> (And) a prophet.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wak<u>a</u>na ya/muru ahlahu bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b>zzak<u>a</u>ti wak<u>a</u>na AAinda rabbihi mar<u>d</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  He used to enjoin<br> On his people Prayer<br> And Charity, and he was<br> Most acceptable in the sight<br> Of his Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur fee alkit<u>a</u>bi idreesa innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>s</u>iddeeqan nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Also mention in the Book<br> The case of Idrīs<br> He was a man of truth<br> (And sincerity), (and) a prophet:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  WarafaAAn<u>a</u>hu mak<u>a</u>nan AAaliyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  And We raised him<br> To a lofty station.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena anAAama All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim mina a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena min <u>th</u>urriyyati <u>a</u>dama wamimman <u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u> maAAa noo<u>h</u>in wamin <u>th</u>urriyyati ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-isr<u>a</u>-eela wamimman hadayn<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>jtabayn<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni kharroo sujjadan wabukiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Those were some<br> Of the prophets on whom<br> God did bestow His Grace,—<br> Of the posterity of Adam,<br> And of those whom We<br> Carried (in the Ark)<br> With Noah, and of<br> The posterity of Abraham<br> And Israel—of those<br> Whom We guided and chose.<br>Whenever the Signs<br> Of (God) Most Gracious<br> Were rehearsed to them,<br> They would fall down<br> In prostrate adoration<br> And in tears.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Fakhalafa min baAAdihim khalfun a<u>da</u>AAoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo a<b>l</b>shshahaw<u>a</u>ti fasawfa yalqawna ghayy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  But after them there followed<br> A posterity who missed<br> Prayers and followed after lusts<br> Soon, then, will they<br> Face Destruction,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Ill<u>a</u> man t<u>a</u>ba wa<u>a</u>mana waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an faol<u>a</u>-ika yadkhuloona aljannata wal<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoona shay-<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Except those who repent<br> And believe, and work<br> Righteousness: for these<br> Will enter the Garden<br> And will not be wronged<br> In the least,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Jann<u>a</u>ti AAadnin allatee waAAada a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu AAib<u>a</u>dahu bi<b>al</b>ghaybi innahu k<u>a</u>na waAAduhu ma/tiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Gardens of Eternity, those<br> Which (God) Most Gracious<br> Has promised to His servants<br> In the Unseen: for His promise<br> Must (necessarily) come to pass.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  L<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> laghwan ill<u>a</u> sal<u>a</u>man walahum rizquhum feeh<u>a</u> bukratan waAAashiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  They will not there hear<br> Any vain discourse, but<br> Only salutations of Peace:<br> And they will have therein<br> Their sustenance, morning<br> And evening.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Tilka aljannatu allatee noorithu min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> man k<u>a</u>na taqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Such is the Garden which<br> We give as an inheritance<br> To those of Our servants<br> Who guard against evil.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Wam<u>a</u> natanazzalu ill<u>a</u> bi-amri rabbika lahu m<u>a</u> bayna aydeen<u>a</u> wam<u>a </u>khalfan<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> bayna <u>tha</u>lika wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na rabbuka nasiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  (The angels say:)<br> "We descend not but<br> By command of thy Lord:<br> To Him belongeth what is<br> Before us and what is<br> Behind us, and what is<br> Between: and thy Lord<br> Never doth forget,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>AAbudhu wa<b>i</b><u>st</u>abir liAAib<u>a</u>datihi hal taAAlamu lahu samiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  "Lord of the heavens<br> And of the earth,<br> And of all that is<br> Between them: so worship Him,<br> And be constant and patient<br> In His worship: knowest thou<br> Of any who is worthy<br> Of the same Name as He?"</p> <div class="filenav">
