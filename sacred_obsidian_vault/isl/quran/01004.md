 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Qul man yarzuqukum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i amman yamliku a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>ra waman yukhriju al<u>h</u>ayya mina almayyiti wayukhriju almayyita mina al<u>h</u>ayyi waman yudabbiru al-amra fasayaqooloona All<u>a</u>hu faqul afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>31.  Say: "Who is it that<br> Sustains you (in life)<br> From the sky and from the earth?<br>Or who is it that<br> Has power over hearing<br> And sight? And who<br> Is it that brings out<br> The living from the dead<br> And the dead from the living?<br> And who is it that<br> Rules and regulates all affairs?"<br> They will soon say, "God".<br> Say, "Will ye not then<br> Show piety (to Him)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Fa<u>tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu rabbukumu al<u>h</u>aqqu fam<u>atha</u> baAAda al<u>h</u>aqqi ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lu faann<u>a</u> tu<u>s</u>rafoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>32.  Such is God, your real<br> Cherisher and Sustainer:<br> Apart from Truth,<br> What (remains) but error?<br> How then are ye turned away?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika <u>h</u>aqqat kalimatu rabbika AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena fasaqoo annahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>33.  Thus is the Word<br> Of thy Lord proved true<br> Against those who rebel:<br> Verily they will not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Qul hal min shurak<u>a</u>-ikum man yabdao alkhalqa thumma yuAAeeduhu quli All<u>a</u>hu yabdao alkhalqa thumma yuAAeeduhu faann<u>a</u> tu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Say: "Of your 'partners',<br> Can any originate creation<br> And repeat it?" Say:<br> "It is God Who originates<br> Creation and repeats it:<br> Then how are ye deluded<br> Away (from the truth)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Qul hal min shurak<u>a</u>-ikum man yahdee il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqi quli All<u>a</u>hu yahdee lil<u>h</u>aqqi afaman yahdee il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqi a<u>h</u>aqqu an yuttabaAAa amman l<u>a</u> yahiddee ill<u>a</u> an yuhd<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u> lakum kayfa ta<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>35.  Say: "Of your "partners"<br> Is there any that<br> Can give any guidance<br> Towards Truth?" Say: "It is God<br> Who gives guidance<br> Towards Truth. Is then He<br> Who gives guidance to Truth<br> More worthy to be followed,<br> Or he who finds not guidance<br> (Himself) unless he is guided?<br> What then is the matter<br> With you? How judge ye?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wam<u>a</u> yattabiAAu aktharuhum ill<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>annan inna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anna l<u>a</u> yughnee mina al<u>h</u>aqqi shay-an inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bim<u>a</u> yafAAaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>36.  But most of them follow<br> Nothing but fancy: truly<br> Fancy can be of no avail<br> Against Truth. Verily God<br> Is well aware of all<br> That they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>nu an yuftar<u>a</u> min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u>kin ta<u>s</u>deeqa alla<u>th</u>ee bayna yadayhi wataf<u>s</u>eela alkit<u>a</u>bi l<u>a</u> rayba feehi min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>37.  This Qur-ān is not such<br> As can be produced<br> By other than God;<br> On the contrary it is<br> A confirmation of (revelations)<br> That went before it,<br> And a fuller explanation<br> Of the Book—wherein<br> There is no doubt<br> From the Lord of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Am yaqooloona iftar<u>a</u>hu qul fa/too bisooratin mithlihi wa<b>o</b>dAAoo mani ista<u>t</u>aAAtum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>38.  Or do they say,<br> "He forged it"?<br> Say: "Bring then<br> A Sūra like unto it,<br> And call (to your aid)<br> Anyone you can,<br> Besides God, if it be<br> Ye speak the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Bal ka<u>thth</u>aboo bim<u>a</u> lam yu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>oo biAAilmihi walamm<u>a</u> ya/tihim ta/weeluhu ka<u>tha</u>lika ka<u>thth</u>aba alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>39.  Nay, they charge with falsehood<br> That whose knowledge they<br> Cannot compass, even before<br> The elucidation thereof"<br> Hath reached them: thus<br> Did those before them<br> Make charges of falsehood:<br> But see what was the end<br> Of those who did wrong!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Waminhum man yu/minu bihi waminhum man l<u>a</u> yu/minu bihi warabbuka aAAlamu bi<b>a</b>lmufsideen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>40.  Of them there are some<br> Who believe therein,<br> And some who do not:<br> And thy Lord knoweth best<br> Those who are out for mischief.</p> <div class="filenav">
