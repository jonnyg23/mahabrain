 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIV.: Nūr, or Light.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha yusabbi<u>h</u>u lahu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayru <u>sa</u>ff<u>a</u>tin kullun qad AAalima <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tahu watasbee<u>h</u>ahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bim<u>a</u> yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Seest thou not that it is<br> God Whose praises all beings<br> In the heavens and on earth<br> Do celebrate, and the birds<br>(Of the air) with wings<br> Outspread? Each one knows<br> Its own (mode of) prayer<br> And praise. And God<br> Knows well all that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Yea, to God belongs<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth; and to God<br> Is the final goal (of all).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha yuzjee sa<u>ha</u>ban thumma yu-allifu baynahu thumma yajAAaluhu ruk<u>a</u>man fatar<u>a</u> alwadqa yakhruju min khil<u>a</u>lihi wayunazzilu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i min jib<u>a</u>lin feeh<u>a</u> min baradin fayu<u>s</u>eebu bihi man yash<u>a</u>o waya<u>s</u>rifuhu AAan man yash<u>a</u>o yak<u>a</u>du san<u>a</u> barqihi ya<u>th</u>habu bi<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Seest thou not that God<br> Makes the clouds move<br> Gently, then joins them<br> Together, then makes them<br> Into a heap?—then wilt thou<br> See rain issue forth<br> From their midst. And He<br> Sends down from the sky<br> Mountain masses (of clouds)<br> Wherein is hail: He strikes<br> Therewith whom He pleases<br> And He turns it away<br> From whom He pleases.<br> The vivid flash of His lightning<br> Well-nigh blinds the sight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Yuqallibu All<u>a</u>hu allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra inna fee <u>tha</u>lika laAAibratan li-olee al-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  It is God Who alternates<br> The Night and the Day:<br>Verily in these things<br> Is an instructive example<br> For those who have vision!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khalaqa kulla d<u>a</u>bbatin min m<u>a</u>-in faminhum man yamshee AAal<u>a</u> ba<u>t</u>nihi waminhum man yamshee AAal<u>a</u> rijlayni waminhum man yamshee AAal<u>a</u> arbaAAin yakhluqu All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  And God has created<br> Every animal from water: sort<br> Of them there are some<br> That creep on their bellies;<br> Some that walk on two legs;<br> And some that walk on four.<br> God creates what He wills;<br> For verily God has power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Laqad anzaln<u>a</u> <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin mubayyin<u>a</u>tin wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yahdee man yash<u>a</u>o il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  We have indeed sent down<br> Signs that make things manifest:<br> And God guides whom He wills<br> To a Way that is straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wayaqooloona <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wabi<b>al</b>rrasooli waa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> thumma yatawall<u>a</u> fareequn minhum min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika wam<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika bi<b>a</b>lmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  They say, "We believe<br> In God and in the Apostle,<br> And we obey": but<br> Even after that, some of them<br> Turn away: they are not<br> (Really) Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> duAAoo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi liya<u>h</u>kuma baynahum i<u>tha</u> fareequn minhum muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  When they are summoned<br> To God and His Apostle,<br> In order that he may judge<br> Between them, behold, some<br> Of them decline (to come).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wa-in yakun lahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu ya/too ilayhi mu<u>th</u>AAineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  But if the right is<br> On their side, they come<br> To him with all submission</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Afee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un ami irt<u>a</u>boo am yakh<u>a</u>foona an ya<u>h</u>eefa All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim warasooluhu bal ol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Is it that there is<br> A disease in their hearts<br> Or do they doubt,<br> Or are they in fear,<br> That God and His Apostle<br> Will deal unjustly with them?<br> Nay, it is they themselves<br> Who do wrong.</p> <div class="filenav">
