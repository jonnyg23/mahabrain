 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LI.: Zāriyāt, or the Winds That Scatter.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>a banayn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> bi-aydin wa-inn<u>a</u> lamoosiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  With power and skill<br> Did We construct<br> The Firmament:<br> For it is We Who create<br> The vastness of Space.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a farashn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> faniAAma alm<u>a</u>hidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And We have spread out<br> The (spacious) earth:<br> How excellently<br> We do spread out!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wamin kulli shay-in khalaqn<u>a</u> zawjayni laAAallakum ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And of every thing<br> We have created pairs<br> That ye may receive<br> Instruction.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fafirroo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innee lakum minhu na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Hasten ye then (at once)<br> To God: I am from Him<br> A Warner to you,<br> Clear and open!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wal<u>a</u> tajAAaloo maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara innee lakum minhu na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And make not another<br> An object of worship<br> With God:<br> I am from Him<br> A Warner to you,<br> Clear and open!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika m<u>a</u> at<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo s<u>ah</u>irun aw majnoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Similarly, no apostle came<br> To the Peoples before them,<br> But they said (of him)<br> In like manner,<br> "A sorcerer, or<br> One possessed"!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Ataw<u>as</u>aw bihi bal hum qawmun <u>ta</u>ghoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Is this the legacy<br> They have transmitted,<br>One to another<br> Nay, they are themselves<br> A people transgressing<br> Beyond bounds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Fatawalla AAanhum fam<u>a</u> anta bimaloom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  So turn away<br> From them: not thine<br> Is the blame.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wa<u>th</u>akkir fa-inna a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u> tanfaAAu almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  But teach (thy Message):<br> For teaching benefits<br> The Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Wam<u>a</u> khalaqtu aljinna wa<b>a</b>l-insa ill<u>a</u> liyaAAbudoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  I have only created<br> Jinns and men, that<br> They may serve Me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  M<u>a</u> oreedu minhum min rizqin wam<u>a</u> oreedu an yu<u>t</u>AAimoon<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>57.  No Sustenance do I require<br> Of them, nor do I<br> Require that they should<br> Feed Me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa a<b>l</b>rrazz<u>a</u>qu <u>th</u>oo alquwwati almateen<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>58.  For God is He Who<br> Gives (all) Sustenance,—<br> Lord of Power,—<br> Steadfast (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Fa-inna lilla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo <u>th</u>anooban mithla <u>th</u>anoobi a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bihim fal<u>a</u> yastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  For the wrong-doers,<br> Their portion is like<br>Unto the portion of their<br> Fellows (of earlier generations):<br> Then let them not ask Me<br> To hasten (that portion)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Fawaylun lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min yawmihimu alla<u>th</u>ee yooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Woe, then, to the Unbelievers,<br> On account of that Day<br> Of theirs which they<br> Have been promised!</p>  <div class="filenav">
