 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVI.: Aḥqāf, or Winding Sand-tracts.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Walaqad ahlakn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>awlakum mina alqur<u>a</u> wa<u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  We destroyed aforetime<br> Populations round about you;<br> And We have shown<br> The Signs in various ways,<br> That they may turn (to Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Falawl<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>arahumu alla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi qurb<u>a</u>nan <u>a</u>lihatan bal <u>d</u>alloo AAanhum wa<u>tha</u>lika ifkuhum wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Why then was no help<br> Forthcoming to them from those<br> Whom they worshipped as gods,<br> Besides God, as a means<br> Of access (to God)? Nay,<br> They left them in the lurch:<br> But that was their<br> Falsehood and their invention.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wa-i<u>th</u> <u>s</u>arafn<u>a</u> ilayka nafaran mina aljinni yastamiAAoona alqur-<u>a</u>na falamm<u>a</u> <u>h</u>a<u>d</u>aroohu q<u>a</u>loo an<u>s</u>itoo falamm<u>a</u> qu<u>d</u>iya wallaw il<u>a</u> qawmihim mun<u>th</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> qawman<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>ban onzila min baAAdi moos<u>a</u> mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi yahdee il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqi wa-il<u>a</u> <u>t</u>areeqin mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  They said, "O our people!<br> We have heard a Book<br> Revealed after Moses,<br> Confirming what came<br> Before it: it guides (men)<br> To the Truth and<br> To a Straight Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Y<u>a</u> qawman<u>a</u> ajeeboo d<u>a</u>AAiya All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>a</u>minoo bihi yaghfir lakum min <u>th</u>unoobikum wayujirkum min AAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "O our people, hearken<br> To the one who invites<br> (You) to God, and believe<br> In him: He will forgive<br> You your faults,<br> And deliver you from<br> A Penalty Grievous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Waman l<u>a</u> yujib d<u>a</u>AAiya All<u>a</u>hi falaysa bimuAAjizin fee al-ar<u>d</u>i walaysa lahu min doonihi awliy<u>a</u>a ol<u>a</u>-ika fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "If any does not hearken<br> To the one who invites<br> (Us) to God, he cannot<br> Frustrate (God's Plan) on earth,<br> And no protectors can he have<br> Besides God: such men<br> (Wander) in manifest error."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Awa lam yaraw anna All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a walam yaAAya bikhalqihinna biq<u>a</u>dirin AAal<u>a</u> an yu<u>h</u>yiya almawt<u>a</u> bal<u>a</u> innahu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  See they not that<br> God, Who created the heavens<br> And the earth, and never<br> Wearied with their creation,<br> Is able to give life<br> To the dead? Yea, verily<br> He has power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wayawma yuAAra<u>d</u>u alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri alaysa h<u>atha</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> warabbin<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la fa<u>th</u>ooqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bim<u>a</u> kuntum takfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And on the Day that<br> The Unbelievers will be<br> Placed before the Fire,<br> (They will be asked,)<br> Is this not the Truth?"<br> They will say, "Yea,<br> By our Lord!"<br> (One will say:)<br> "Then taste ye<br> The Penalty, for that ye<br> Were wont to deny (Truth)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir kam<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abara oloo alAAazmi mina a<b>l</b>rrusuli wal<u>a</u> tastaAAjil lahum kaannahum yawma yarawna m<u>a</u> yooAAadoona lam yalbathoo ill<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>AAatan min nah<u>a</u>rin bal<u>a</u>ghun fahal yuhlaku ill<u>a</u> alqawmu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Therefore patiently persevere,<br> As did (all) apostles<br> Of inflexible purpose;<br> And be in no haste<br> About the (Unbelievers). On the Day<br> That they see the (Punishment)<br> Promised them, (it will be)<br> As if they had not<br> Tarried more than an hour<br> In a single day. (Thine<br> But) to proclaim the Message:<br> But shall any be destroyed<br> Except those who transgress?</p>  <div class="filenav">
