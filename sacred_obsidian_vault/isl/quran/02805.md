 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba min baAAdi m<u>a</u> ahlakn<u>a</u> alquroona al-ool<u>a</u> ba<u>sa</u>-ira li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wahudan wara<u>h</u>matan laAAallahum yata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  We did reveal to Moses<br> The Book after We had<br> Destroyed the earlier generations,<br> (To give) Insight to men,<br> And Guidance and Mercy,<br> That they might receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wam<u>a</u> kunta bij<u>a</u>nibi algharbiyyi i<u>th</u> qa<u>d</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> al-amra wam<u>a</u> kunta mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Thou wast not on the Western<br> Side when We decreed<br> The Commission to Moses,<br> Nor wast thou a witness<br> (Of those events).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wal<u>a</u>kinn<u>a</u> ansha-n<u>a</u> quroonan fata<u>ta</u>wala AAalayhimu alAAumuru wam<u>a</u> kunta th<u>a</u>wiyan fee ahli madyana tatloo AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> mursileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  But We raised up (new)<br> Generations, and long were the ages<br> That passed over them;<br> But thou vast not a dweller<br> Among the people of Madyan,<br> Rehearsing Our Signs to them;<br> But it is We Who send<br> Apostles (with inspiration).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wam<u>a</u> kunta bij<u>a</u>nibi a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oori i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>dayn<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin ra<u>h</u>matan min rabbika litun<u>th</u>ira qawman m<u>a</u> at<u>a</u>hum min na<u>th</u>eerin min qablika laAAallahum yata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Nor wast thou at the side<br> Of (the Mountain of) Ṭūr<br> When We called (to Moses).<br> Yet (art thou sent)<br> As a Mercy from thy Lord,<br> To give warning to a people<br> To whom no warner had come<br> Before thee: in order that<br> They may receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Walawl<u>a</u> an tu<u>s</u>eebahum mu<u>s</u>eebatun bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeehim fayaqooloo rabban<u>a</u> lawl<u>a</u> arsalta ilayn<u>a</u> rasoolan fanattabiAAa <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tika wanakoona mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  If (We had) not (sent thee<br> To the Quraish),—in case<br> A calamity should seize them<br> For (the deeds) that their hands<br> Have sent forth, they might say:<br> "Our Lord! why didst Thou not<br>Send us an apostle? We<br> Should then have followed<br> Thy Signs and been amongst<br> Those who believe!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu min AAindin<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> ootiya mithla m<u>a</u> ootiya moos<u>a</u> awa lam yakfuroo bim<u>a</u> ootiya moos<u>a</u> min qablu q<u>a</u>loo si<u>h</u>r<u>a</u>ni ta<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>har<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> bikullin k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  But (now), when the Truth<br> Has come to them from Ourselves,<br> They say, "Why are not<br> (Signs) sent to him, like<br> Those which were sent to Moses?"<br> Do they not then reject<br> (The Signs) which were formerly<br> Sent to Moses? They say:<br> "Two kinds of sorcery,<br> Each assisting the other .<br> And they say: "For us,<br> We reject all (such things)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Qul fa/too bikit<u>a</u>bin min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi huwa ahd<u>a</u> minhum<u>a</u> attabiAAhu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Say: "Then bring ye<br> A Book from God,<br> Which is a better Guide<br> Than either of them,<br> That I may follow it!<br> (Do), if ye are truthful!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fa-in lam yastajeeboo laka fa<b>i</b>AAlam annam<u>a</u> yattabiAAoona ahw<u>a</u>ahum waman a<u>d</u>allu mimmani ittabaAAa haw<u>a</u>hu bighayri hudan mina All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  But if they hearken not<br> To thee, know that they<br> Only follow their own lusts:<br> And who is more astray<br> Than one who follows his own<br> Lusts, devoid of guidance<br> From God? For God guides not<br> People given to wrong-doing.</p> <div class="filenav">
