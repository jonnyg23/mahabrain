 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an il<u>a</u> qawmihi faq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  (Further, We sent a long line<br> Of prophets for your instruction).<br> We sent Noah to his people:<br> He said, "O my people!<br> Worship God! Ye have<br> No other god but Him.<br> Will ye not fear (Him)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Faq<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qawmihi m<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum yureedu an yatafa<u>dd</u>ala AAalaykum walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu laanzala mal<u>a</u>-ikatan m<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> bih<u>atha</u> fee <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-in<u>a</u> al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  The chiefs of the Unbelievers<br> Among his people said:<br> "He is no more than a man<br> Like yourselves: his wish is<br> To assert his superiority<br> Over you: if God had wished<br> (To send messengers),<br> He could have sent down<br> Angels: never did we hear<br> Such a thing (as he says),<br> Among our ancestors of old."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  In huwa ill<u>a</u> rajulun bihi jinnatun fatarabba<u>s</u>oo bihi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>25.  (And some said): "He is<br> Only a man possessed:<br> Wait (and have patience)<br> With him for a time."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi on<u>s</u>urnee bim<u>a</u> ka<u>thth</u>aboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  (Noah) said: "O my Lord!<br> Help me: for that they<br> Accuse me of falsehood!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Faaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayhi ani i<u>s</u>naAAi alfulka bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u> wawa<u>h</u>yin<u>a</u> fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> waf<u>a</u>ra a<b>l</b>ttannooru fa<b>o</b>sluk feeh<u>a</u> min kullin zawjayni ithnayni waahlaka ill<u>a</u> man sabaqa AAalayhi alqawlu minhum wal<u>a</u> tukh<u>at</u>ibnee fee alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo innahum mughraqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  So We inspired him<br> (With this message): "Construct<br> The Ark within Our sight<br> And under Our guidance: then<br> When comes Our command,<br> And the fountains of the earth<br> Gush forth, take thou on board<br> Pairs of every species, male<br> And female, and thy family—<br> Except those of them<br> Against whom the Word<br> Has already gone forth:<br> And address Me not<br> In favour of the wrong-doers;<br> For they shall be drowned<br> (In the Flood).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> istawayta anta waman maAAaka AAal<u>a</u> alfulki faquli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee najj<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> mina alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And when thou hast embarked<br> On the Ark—thou and those<br> With thee,—say: "Praise be<br> To God, Who has saved us<br> From the people who do wrong."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Waqul rabbi anzilnee munzalan mub<u>a</u>rakan waanta khayru almunzileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And say: "O my Lord!<br> Enable me to disembark<br> With Thy blessing: for Thou<br> Art the Best to enable (us)<br> To disembark."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin wa-in kunn<u>a</u> lamubtaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Verily in this there are<br> Signs (for men to understand);<br> (Thus) do We try (men)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Thumma ansha/n<u>a</u> min baAAdihim qarnan <u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Then We raised after them<br> Another generation.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Faarsaln<u>a</u> feehim rasoolan minhum ani oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And We sent to them<br> An apostle from among themselves,<br> (Saying), "Worship God!<br> Ye have no other god<br> But Him. Will ye not<br> Fear (Him)?"</p> <div class="filenav">
