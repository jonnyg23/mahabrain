 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wa-i<u>th</u> quln<u>a</u> lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati osjudoo li-<u>a</u>dama fasajadoo ill<u>a</u> ibleesa q<u>a</u>la aasjudu liman khalaqta <u>t</u>een<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Behold! We said to the angels:<br> "Bow down unto Adam":<br> They bowed down except Iblīs<br> He said, "Shall I bow down<br> To one whom Thou didst create<br> From clay?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Q<u>a</u>la araaytaka h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee karramta AAalayya la-in akhkhartani il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati laa<u>h</u>tanikanna <u>th</u>urriyyatahu ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  He said, "Seest Thou? This is<br> The one whom Thou hast honoured<br> Above me! If Thou wilt but<br> Respite me to the Day<br> Of Judgment, I will surely<br> Bring his descendants<br> Under my sway<br> All but a few!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>la i<u>th</u>hab faman tabiAAaka minhum fa-inna jahannama jaz<u>a</u>okum jaz<u>a</u>an mawfoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  (God) said: "Go thy way;<br> If any of them follow thee,<br> Verily Hell will be<br> The recompense of you (all)—<br> An ample recompense.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Wa<b>i</b>stafziz mani ista<u>t</u>aAAta minhum bi<u>s</u>awtika waajlib AAalayhim bikhaylika warajlika wash<u>a</u>rik-hum fee al-amw<u>a</u>li wa<b>a</b>l-awl<u>a</u>di waAAidhum wam<u>a</u> yaAAiduhumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu ill<u>a</u> ghuroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  "Lead to destruction those<br> Whom thou canst among them,<br> With thy (seductive) voice;<br> Make assaults on them<br> With thy cavalry and thy<br> Infantry; mutually share<br> With them wealth and children;<br> And make promises to them."<br> But Satan promises them<br> Nothing but deceit.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Inna AAib<u>a</u>dee laysa laka AAalayhim sul<u>ta</u>nun wakaf<u>a</u> birabbika wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  "As for My servants,<br> No authority shalt thou<br> Have over them:"<br> Enough is thy Lord<br> For a Disposer of affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Rabbukumu alla<u>th</u>ee yuzjee lakumu alfulka fee alba<u>h</u>ri litabtaghoo min fa<u>d</u>lihi innahu k<u>a</u>na bikum ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Your Lord is He<br> That maketh the Ship<br> Go smoothly for you<br> Through the sea, in order that<br> Ye may seek of His Bounty.<br> For He is unto you<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> massakumu a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>urru fee alba<u>h</u>ri <u>d</u>alla man tadAAoona ill<u>a</u> iyy<u>a</u>hu falamm<u>a</u> najj<u>a</u>kum il<u>a</u> albarri aAAra<u>d</u>tum wak<u>a</u>na al-ins<u>a</u>nu kafoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  When distress seizes you<br> At sea, those that ye<br> Call upon—besides Himself—<br> Leave you in the lurch!<br> But when He brings you back<br> Safe to land, ye turn<br>Away (from Him). Most ungrateful<br> Is man!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Afaamintum an yakhsifa bikum j<u>a</u>niba albarri aw yursila AAalaykum <u>has</u>iban thumma l<u>a</u> tajidoo lakum wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Do ye then feel secure<br> That He will not cause you<br> To be swallowed up<br> Beneath the earth<br> When ye are on land,<br> Or that He will not send<br> Against you a violent tornado<br> (With showers of stones)<br> So that ye shall find<br> No one to carry out<br> Your affairs for you?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Am amintum an yuAAeedakum feehi t<u>a</u>ratan okhr<u>a</u> fayursila AAalaykum q<u>as</u>ifan mina a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>i fayughriqakum bim<u>a</u> kafartum thumma l<u>a</u> tajidoo lakum AAalayn<u>a</u> bihi tabeeAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Or do ye feel secure<br> That He will not send you<br> Back a second time<br> To sea and send against you<br> A heavy gale to drown you<br> Because of your ingratitude,<br> So that ye find no helper<br> Therein against Us?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Walaqad karramn<u>a</u> banee <u>a</u>dama wa<u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u>hum fee albarri wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri warazaqn<u>a</u>hum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti wafa<u>dd</u>aln<u>a</u>hum AAal<u>a</u> katheerin mimman khalaqn<u>a</u> taf<u>d</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  We have honoured the sons<br> Of Adam; provided them<br> With transport on land and sea;<br> Given them for sustenance things<br> Good and pure; and conferred<br> On them special favours,<br> Above a great part<br> Of Our Creation.</p> <div class="filenav">
