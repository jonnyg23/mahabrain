 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ta</u>-seen-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Tā. Sīn. Mīm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alkit<u>a</u>bi almubeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  These are Verses of the Book<br> That makes (things) clear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Natloo AAalayka min naba-i moos<u>a</u> wafirAAawna bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  We rehearse to thee some<br> Of the story of Moses<br> And Pharaoh in Truth,<br> For people who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inna firAAawna AAal<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wajaAAala ahlah<u>a</u> shiyaAAan yasta<u>d</u>AAifu <u>ta</u>-ifatan minhum yu<u>th</u>abbi<u>h</u>u abn<u>a</u>ahum wayasta<u>h</u>yee nis<u>a</u>ahum innahu k<u>a</u>na mina almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Truly Pharaoh elated himself<br> In the land and broke up<br> Its people into sections,<br> Depressing a small group<br> Among them: their sons he slew,<br> But he kept alive their females:<br> For he was indeed<br> A maker of mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wanureedu an namunna AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena istu<u>d</u>AAifoo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wanajAAalahum a-immatan wanajAAalahumu alw<u>a</u>ritheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And We wished to be<br> Gracious to those who were<br> Being depressed in the land,<br> To make them leaders (in faith)<br> And make them heirs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wanumakkina lahum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wanuriya firAAawna wah<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>na wajunoodahum<u>a</u> minhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo ya<u>hth</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  To establish a firm place<br> For them in the land,<br> And to show Pharaoh, Haman,<br> And their hosts, at their hands,<br> The very things against which<br> They were taking precautions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ommi moos<u>a</u> an ar<u>d</u>iAAeehi fa-i<u>tha</u> khifti AAalayhi faalqeehi fee alyammi wal<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>fee wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanee inn<u>a</u> r<u>a</u>ddoohu ilayki waj<u>a</u>AAiloohu mina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  So We sent this inspiration<br> To the mother of Moses:<br> "Suckle (thy child), but when<br> Thou hast fears about him,<br> Cast him into the river,<br> But fear not nor grieve:<br> For We shall restore him<br> To thee, and We shall make<br> Him one of Our apostles."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fa<b>i</b>ltaqa<u>t</u>ahu <u>a</u>lu firAAawna liyakoona lahum AAaduwwan wa<u>h</u>azanan inna firAAawna wah<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>na wajunoodahum<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo kh<u>at</u>i-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Then the people of Pharaoh<br> Picked him up (from the river):<br> (It was intended) that (Moses)<br> Should be to them an adversary<br> And a cause of sorrow:<br> For Pharaoh and Haman<br> And (all) their hosts were<br> Men of sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waq<u>a</u>lati imraatu firAAawna qurratu AAaynin lee walaka l<u>a</u> taqtuloohu AAas<u>a</u> an yanfaAAan<u>a</u> aw nattakhi<u>th</u>ahu waladan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  The wife of Pharaoh said:<br> "(Here is) a joy of the eye,'<br> For me and for thee:<br> Slay him not. It may be<br>That he will be of use<br> To us, or we may adopt<br> Him as a son." And they<br> Perceived not (what they<br> Were doing)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a fu-<u>a</u>du ommi moos<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>righan in k<u>a</u>dat latubdee bihi lawl<u>a</u> an raba<u>t</u>n<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> qalbih<u>a</u> litakoona mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  But there came to be<br> A void in the heart<br> Of the mother of Moses:<br> She was going almost to<br> Disclose his (case), had We<br> Not strengthened her heart<br> (With faith), so that she<br> Might remain a (firm) believer.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Waq<u>a</u>lat li-okhtihi qu<u>ss</u>eehi faba<u>s</u>urat bihi AAan junubin wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And she said to the sister<br> Of (Moses), "Follow him".<br> So she (the sister) watched him<br> In the character of a stranger.<br> And they knew not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wa<u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> AAalayhi almar<u>ad</u>iAAa min qablu faq<u>a</u>lat hal adullukum AAal<u>a</u> ahli baytin yakfuloonahu lakum wahum lahu n<u>as</u>i<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And We ordained that he<br> Refused suck at first, until<br> (His sister came up<br> And) said: "Shall I<br> Point out to you the people<br> Of a house that will nourish<br> And bring him up for you'"<br> And be sincerely attached<br> To him?"...</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Faradadn<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> ommihi kay taqarra AAaynuh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zana walitaAAlama anna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Thus did We restore him<br> To his mother, that her eye<br> Might be comforted, that she<br> Might not grieve, and that<br> She might know that the promise<br> Of God is true: but<br> Most of them do not understand.</p> <div class="filenav">
