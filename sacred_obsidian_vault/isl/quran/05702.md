 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVII.: Ḥadīd, or Iron.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Man <u>tha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee yuqri<u>d</u>u All<u>a</u>ha qar<u>d</u>an <u>h</u>asanan fayu<u>da</u>AAifahu lahu walahu ajrun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Who is he that will<br> Loan to God a beautiful<br> Loan? For (God) will<br> Increase it manifold<br> To his credit,<br> And he will have (besides)<br> A liberal reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Yawma tar<u>a</u> almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti yasAA<u>a</u> nooruhum bayna aydeehim wabi-aym<u>a</u>nihim bushr<u>a</u>kumu alyawma jann<u>a</u>tun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  One Day shalt thou see<br> The believing men and<br> The believing women—<br> How their Light runs<br> Forward before them<br> And by their right hands:<br> (Their greeting will be):<br> "Good News for you this Day!<br> Gardens beneath which How rivers!<br> To dwell therein for aye!<br> This is indeed<br> The highest Achievement!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Yawma yaqoolu almun<u>a</u>fiqoona wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiq<u>a</u>tu lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo on<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<u>a</u> naqtabis min noorikum qeela irjiAAoo war<u>a</u>akum fa<b>i</b>ltamisoo nooran fa<u>d</u>uriba baynahum bisoorin lahu b<u>a</u>bun b<u>at</u>inuhu feehi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>matu wa<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiruhu min qibalihi alAAa<u>tha</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  One Day will the Hypocrites<br> Men and women—say<br> To the Believers: "Wait<br> For us! Let us borrow<br> (A light) from your Light!"<br> It will be said: "Turn<br> Ye back to your rear!<br> Then seek a light (where<br> Ye can)!" So a wall<br> Will be put up betwixt them,<br>With a gate therein.<br> Within it will be Mercy<br> Throughout, and without it,<br> All alongside, will be<br> (Wrath and) Punishment!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Yun<u>a</u>doonahum alam nakun maAAakum q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinnakum fatantum anfusakum watarabba<u>s</u>tum wa<b>i</b>rtabtum wagharratkumu al-am<u>a</u>niyyu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amru All<u>a</u>hi wagharrakum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi algharoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  (Those without) will call out,<br> "Were we not with you?<br> (The others) will reply, "True!<br> But ye led yourselves<br> Into temptation; ye looked forward<br> (To our ruin); ye doubted<br> (God's Promise); and (your false)<br> Desires deceived you; until<br> There issued the Command<br> Of God. And the Deceiver<br> Deceived you in respect of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Fa<b>a</b>lyawma l<u>a</u> yu/kha<u>th</u>u minkum fidyatun wal<u>a</u> mina alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo ma/w<u>a</u>kumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru hiya mawl<u>a</u>kum wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  "This Day shall no ransom<br> Be accepted of you, nor<br> Of those who rejected God.<br> Your abode is the Fire:<br> That is the proper place .<br> To claim you: and an evil<br> Refuge it is!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Alam ya/ni lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo an takhshaAAa quloobuhum li<u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> nazala mina al<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u> yakoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablu fa<u>ta</u>la AAalayhimu al-amadu faqasat quloobuhum wakatheerun minhum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Has not the time arrived<br> For the Believers that<br> Their hearts in all humility<br> Should engage in the remembrance<br>Of God and of the Truth<br> Which has been revealed (to them),<br> And that they should not<br> Become like those to whom<br> Was given Revelation aforetime,<br> But long ages passed over them<br> And their hearts grew hard?<br> For many among them<br> Are rebellious transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  IAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>yee al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> qad bayyann<u>a</u> lakumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti laAAallakum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Know ye (all) that<br> God giveth life<br> To the earth after its death<br> Already have We shown<br> The Signs plainly to you,<br> That ye may learn wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Inna almu<u>ss</u>addiqeena wa<b>a</b>lmu<u>ss</u>addiq<u>a</u>ti waaqra<u>d</u>oo All<u>a</u>ha qar<u>d</u>an <u>h</u>asanan yu<u>da</u>AAafu lahum walahum ajrun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  For those who give<br> In Charity, men and women,<br> And loan to God<br> A Beautiful Loan,<br> It shall be increased manifold<br> (To their credit),<br> And they shall have (besides)<br> A liberal reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warusulihi ol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>iddeeqoona wa<b>al</b>shshuhad<u>a</u>o AAinda rabbihim lahum ajruhum wanooruhum wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And those who believe<br> In God and His apostles—<br> They are the Sincere<br> (Lovers of Truth), and<br>The Witnesses (who testify),<br> In the eyes of their Lord:<br> They shall have their Reward<br> And their Light,<br> But those who reject God<br> And deny Our Signs,—<br> They are the Companions<br> Of Hell-Fire.</p> <div class="filenav">
