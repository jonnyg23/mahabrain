 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Waq<u>a</u>lati alyahoodu AAuzayrun ibnu All<u>a</u>hi waq<u>a</u>lati a<b>l</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> almasee<u>h</u>u ibnu All<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>lika qawluhum bi-afw<u>a</u>hihim yu<u>da</u>hi-oona qawla alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qablu q<u>a</u>talahumu All<u>a</u>hu ann<u>a</u> yu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  The Jews call ’Uzair a son<br> Of God, and the Christians<br> Call Christ the Son of God.<br> That is a saying from their mouth;<br> (In this) they but imitate<br> What the Unbelievers of old<br> Used to say. God's curse<br> Be on them: how they are deluded<br> Away from the Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Ittakha<u>th</u>oo a<u>h</u>b<u>a</u>rahum waruhb<u>a</u>nahum arb<u>a</u>ban min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lmasee<u>h</u>a ibna maryama wam<u>a</u> omiroo ill<u>a</u> liyaAAbudoo il<u>a</u>han w<u>ah</u>idan l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa sub<u>ha</u>nahu AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  They take their priests<br> And their anchorites to be<br> Their lords in derogation of God,<br> And (they take as their Lord)<br> Christ the son of Mary;<br> Yet they were commanded<br> To worship but One God:<br> There is no god but He.<br> Praise and glory to Him<br> (Far is He) from having<br> The partners they associate<br> (With Him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Yureedoona an yu<u>t</u>fi-oo noora All<u>a</u>hi bi-afw<u>a</u>hihim waya/b<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> an yutimma noorahu walaw kariha alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Fain would they extinguish<br> God's Light with their mouths,<br> But God will not allow<br> But that His Light should be<br> Perfected, even though the Unbelievers<br> May detest (it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee arsala rasoolahu bi<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> wadeeni al<u>h</u>aqqi liyu<i><u>th</u></i>hirahu AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ddeeni kullihi walaw kariha almushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  It is He Who hath sent<br> His Apostle with Guidance<br> And the Religion of Truth,<br> To proclaim it<br> Over all religion,<br> Even though the Pagans<br> May detest (it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo inna katheeran mina al-a<u>h</u>b<u>a</u>ri wa<b>al</b>rruhb<u>a</u>ni laya/kuloona amw<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili waya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yaknizoona a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ahaba wa<b>a</b>lfi<u>dd</u>ata wal<u>a</u> yunfiqoonah<u>a</u> fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi fabashshirhum biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  O ye who believe! There are<br> Indeed many among the priests<br> And anchorites, who in falsehood<br> Devour the substance of men<br> And hinder (them) from the Way<br> Of God. And there are those<br> Who bury gold and silver<br> And spend it not in the Way.<br> Of God: announce unto them<br> A most grievous penalty—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Yawma yu<u>h</u>m<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> fee n<u>a</u>ri jahannama fatukw<u>a</u> bih<u>a</u> jib<u>a</u>huhum wajunoobuhum wa<i><u>th</u></i>uhooruhum h<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> kanaztum li-anfusikum fa<u>th</u>ooqoo m<u>a</u> kuntum taknizoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  On the Day when heat<br> Will be produced out of<br> That (wealth) in the fire<br> Of Hell, and with it will be<br> Branded their foreheads,<br> Their flanks, and their backs.<br> —"This is the (treasure) which ye<br> Buried for yourselves: taste ye,<br> Then, the (treasures) ye buried!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Inna AAiddata a<b>l</b>shshuhoori AAinda All<u>a</u>hi ithn<u>a</u> AAashara shahran fee kit<u>a</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi yawma khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a minh<u>a</u> arbaAAatun <u>h</u>urumun <u>tha</u>lika a<b>l</b>ddeenu alqayyimu fal<u>a</u> ta<i><u>th</u></i>limoo feehinna anfusakum waq<u>a</u>tiloo almushrikeena k<u>a</u>ffatan kam<u>a</u> yuq<u>a</u>tiloonakum k<u>a</u>ffatan wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  The number of months<br> In the sight of God<br> Is twelve (in a year)<br> So ordained by Him<br> The day He created<br> The heavens and the earth;<br> Of them four are sacred:<br> That is the straight usage.<br> So wrong not yourselves<br> Therein, and fight the Pagans<br>All together as they<br> Fight you all together.<br> But know that God<br> Is with those who restrain<br> Themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Innam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnasee-o ziy<u>a</u>datun fee alkufri yu<u>d</u>allu bihi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yu<u>h</u>illoonahu AA<u>a</u>man wayu<u>h</u>arrimoonahu AA<u>a</u>man liyuw<u>at</u>i-oo AAiddata m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama All<u>a</u>hu fayu<u>h</u>illoo m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama All<u>a</u>hu zuyyina lahum soo-o aAAm<u>a</u>lihim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Verily the transposing<br> (Of a prohibited month)<br> Is an addition to Unbelief:<br> The Unbelievers are led<br> To wrong thereby: for they make<br> It lawful one year,<br> And forbidden another year,<br> In order to adjust the number<br> Of months forbidden by God<br> And make such forbidden ones<br> Lawful. The evil of their course<br> Seems pleasing to them.<br> But God guideth not<br> Those who reject Faith.</p> <div class="filenav">
