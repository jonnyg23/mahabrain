 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIV.: Sabā, or the City of Sabā</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>wooda minn<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lan y<u>a</u> jib<u>a</u>lu awwibee maAAahu wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayra waalann<u>a</u> lahu al<u>h</u>adeed<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  We bestowed Grace aforetime<br> On David from Ourselves:<br> "O ye Mountains! sing ye<br> Back the Praises of God<br> With him! and ye birds<br> (Also)! And We made<br> The iron soft for him;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Ani iAAmal s<u>a</u>bigh<u>a</u>tin waqaddir fee a<b>l</b>ssardi wa<b>i</b>AAmaloo <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an innee bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  (Commanding), "Make thou<br> Coats of mail, balancing well<br> The rings of chain armour,<br> And work ye righteousness;<br> For be sure I see<br> (Clearly) all that ye do."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Walisulaym<u>a</u>na a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>a ghuduwwuh<u>a</u> shahrun waraw<u>ah</u>uh<u>a</u> shahrun waasaln<u>a</u> lahu AAayna alqi<u>t</u>ri wamina aljinni man yaAAmalu bayna yadayhi bi-i<u>th</u>ni rabbihi waman yazigh minhum AAan amrin<u>a</u> nu<u>th</u>iqhu min AAa<u>tha</u>bi a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And to Solomon (We<br> Made) the Wind (obedient):<br> Its early morning (stride)<br> Was a month's (journey),<br> And its evening (stride)<br> Was a month's (journey);<br> And We made a Font<br> Of molten brass to flow<br>For him; and there were<br> Jinns that worked in front<br> Of him, by the leave<br> Of his Lord, and if any<br> Of them turned aside<br> From Our command, We<br> Made him taste<br> Of the Penalty<br> Of the Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  YaAAmaloona lahu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o min ma<u>ha</u>reeba watam<u>a</u>theela wajif<u>a</u>nin ka<b>a</b>ljaw<u>a</u>bi waqudoorin r<u>a</u>siy<u>a</u>tin iAAmaloo <u>a</u>la d<u>a</u>wooda shukran waqaleelun min AAib<u>a</u>diya a<b>l</b>shshakoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  They worked for him<br> As he desired, (making) Arches,<br> Images, Basons<br> As large as Reservoirs,<br> And (cooking) Cauldrons fixed<br> (In their places): "Work ye,<br> Sons of David, with thanks!<br> But few of My servants<br> Are grateful!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Falamm<u>a</u> qa<u>d</u>ayn<u>a</u> AAalayhi almawta m<u>a</u> dallahum AAal<u>a</u> mawtihi ill<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>bbatu al-ar<u>d</u>i ta/kulu minsaatahu falamm<u>a</u> kharra tabayyanati aljinnu an law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoona alghayba m<u>a</u> labithoo fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi almuheen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Then, when We decreed<br> (Solomon's) death, nothing showed them<br> His death except a little<br> Worm of the earth, which<br>Kept (slowly) gnawing away<br> At his staff: so when he<br> Fell down, the Jinns saw<br> Plainly that if they had<br> Known the unseen, they<br> Would not have tarried<br> In the humiliating Penalty<br> (Of their Task).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Laqad k<u>a</u>na lisaba-in fee maskanihim <u>a</u>yatun jannat<u>a</u>ni AAan yameenin washim<u>a</u>lin kuloo min rizqi rabbikum wa<b>o</b>shkuroo lahu baldatun <u>t</u>ayyibatun warabbun ghafoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  There was, for Sabā,<br> Aforetime, a Sign in their<br> Home-land—two Gardens<br> To the right and to the left.<br> "Eat of the Sustenance (provided)<br> By your Lord, and be grateful<br> To Him: a territory fair and happy,<br> And a Lord Oft-Forgiving!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  FaaAAra<u>d</u>oo faarsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim sayla alAAarimi wabaddaln<u>a</u>hum bijannatayhim jannatayni <u>th</u>aw<u>a</u>tay okulin kham<u>t</u>in waathlin washay-in min sidrin qaleel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  But they turned away<br> (From God), and We sent<br> Against them the flood<br> (Released) from the Dams,<br>And We converted their two<br> Garden (rows) into "gardens"<br> Producing bitter fruit,<br> And tamarisks, and some few<br> (Stunted) Lote-trees.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  <u>Tha</u>lika jazayn<u>a</u>hum bim<u>a</u> kafaroo wahal nuj<u>a</u>zee ill<u>a</u> alkafoor<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  That was the Requital<br> We gave them because<br> They ungratefully rejected Faith:<br> And never do We give<br> (Such) requital except to such<br> As are ungrateful rejecters.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> baynahum wabayna alqur<u>a</u> allatee b<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> quran <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiratan waqaddarn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssayra seeroo feeh<u>a</u> lay<u>a</u>liya waayy<u>a</u>man <u>a</u>mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Between them and the Cities<br> On which We had poured<br> Our blessings, We had placed<br> Cities in prominent positions,<br> And between them We had<br> Appointed stages of journey<br> In due proportion: "Travel therein,<br> Secure, by night and by day."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Faq<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>AAid bayna asf<u>a</u>rin<u>a</u> wa<i><u>th</u></i>alamoo anfusahum fajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum a<u>ha</u>deetha wamazzaqn<u>a</u>hum kulla mumazzaqin inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin likulli <u>s</u>abb<u>a</u>rin shakoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  But they said: "Our Lord!<br> Place longer distances<br>Between our journey-stages":<br> But they wronged themselves (therein).<br> At length We made them<br> As a tale (that is told),<br> And We dispersed them<br> All in scattered fragments.<br> Verily in this are Signs<br> For every (soul that is)<br> Patiently constant and grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Walaqad <u>s</u>addaqa AAalayhim ibleesu <i><u>th</u></i>annahu fa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoohu ill<u>a</u> fareeqan mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And on them did Satan<br> Prove true his idea,<br> And they followed him, all<br> But a Party that believed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahu AAalayhim min sul<u>ta</u>nin ill<u>a</u> linaAAlama man yu/minu bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati mimman huwa minh<u>a</u> fee shakkin warabbuka AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  But he had no authority<br> Over them,—except that We<br> Might test the man who<br> Believes in the Hereafter<br> From him who is in doubt<br> Concerning it: and thy Lord<br> Doth watch over all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
