 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  A<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>lu qaww<u>a</u>moona AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i bim<u>a</u> fa<u>dd</u>ala All<u>a</u>hu baAA<u>d</u>ahum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in wabim<u>a</u> anfaqoo min amw<u>a</u>lihim fa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>tu q<u>a</u>nit<u>a</u>tun <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>tun lilghaybi bim<u>a</u> <u>h</u>afi<i><u>th</u></i>a All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>a</b>ll<u>a</u>tee takh<u>a</u>foona nushoozahunna faAAi<i><u>th</u></i>oohunna wa<b>o</b>hjuroohunna fee alma<u>da</u>jiAAi wa<b>i</b><u>d</u>riboohunna fa-in a<u>t</u>aAAnakum fal<u>a</u> tabghoo AAalayhinna sabeelan inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAaliyyan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Men are the protectors<br> And maintainers of women,<br> Because God has given<br> The one more (strength)<br> Than the other, and because<br> They support them<br> From their means.<br> Therefore the righteous women<br> Are devoutly obedient, and guard<br> In (the husband's) absence<br> What God would have them guard.<br> As to those women<br> On whose part ye fear<br> Disloyalty and ill-conduct,<br> Admonish them (first),<br> (Next), refuse to share their beds,<br> (And last) beat them (lightly);<br> But if they return to obedience,<br> Seek not against them<br> Means (of annoyance):<br>For God is Most High,<br> Great (above you all).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wa-in khiftum shiq<u>a</u>qa baynihim<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>bAAathoo <u>h</u>akaman min ahlihi wa<u>h</u>akaman min ahlih<u>a</u> in yureed<u>a</u> i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>an yuwaffiqi All<u>a</u>hu baynahum<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAaleeman khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  If ye fear a breach<br> Between them twain,<br> Appoint (two) arbiters,<br> One from his family,<br> And the other from hers;<br> If they wish for peace,<br> God will cause<br> Their reconciliation:<br> For God hath full knowledge,<br> And is acquainted<br> With all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa<b>o</b>AAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha wal<u>a</u> tushrikoo bihi shay-an wabi<b>a</b>lw<u>a</u>lidayni i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>nan wabi<u>th</u>ee alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keeni wa<b>a</b>lj<u>a</u>ri <u>th</u>ee alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lj<u>a</u>ri aljunubi wa<b>al</b><u>ssah</u>ibi bi<b>a</b>ljanbi wa<b>i</b>bni a<b>l</b>ssabeeli wam<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu man k<u>a</u>na mukht<u>a</u>lan fakhoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  serve God, and join not<br> Any partners with Him;<br> And do good<br> To parents, kinsfolk,<br> Orphans, those in need,<br> Neighbours who are near,<br> Neighbours who are strangers,<br> The Companion by your side,<br> The way-farer (ye meet),<br> And what your right hands possess:<br> For God loveth not<br> The arrogant, the vainglorious;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yabkhaloona waya/muroona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa bi<b>a</b>lbukhli wayaktumoona m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi waaAAtadn<u>a</u> lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>ban muheen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  (Nor) those who are niggardly<br> Or enjoin niggardliness on others,<br> Or hide the bounties<br> Which God hath bestowed<br> On them; for We have prepared,<br> For those who resist Faith,<br> A Punishment that steeps<br> Them in contempt;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yunfiqoona amw<u>a</u>lahum ri-<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waman yakuni a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu lahu qareenan fas<u>a</u>a qareen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Not those who spend<br> Of their substance, to be seen<br> Of men, but have no faith<br> In God and the Last Day:<br> If any take the Evil One<br> For their intimate,<br> What a dreadful intimate he is!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wam<u>atha</u> AAalayhim law <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waanfaqoo mimm<u>a</u> razaqahumu All<u>a</u>hu wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bihim AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  And what burden<br> Were it on them if they<br> Had faith in God<br> And in the Last Day,<br> And they spent<br> Out of what God hath<br> Given them for sustenance?<br> For God hath full<br> Knowledge of them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>limu mithq<u>a</u>la <u>th</u>arratin wa-in taku <u>h</u>asanatan yu<u>da</u>AAifh<u>a</u> wayu/ti min ladunhu ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  God is never unjust<br> In the least degree:<br> If there is any good (done),<br> He doubleth it,<br> And giveth from His own<br> Presence a great reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Fakayfa i<u>tha</u> ji/n<u>a</u> min kulli ommatin bishaheedin waji/n<u>a</u> bika AAal<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  How then if We brought<br> From each People a witness,<br>And We brought thee<br> As a witness against<br> These People!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in yawaddu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waAAa<u>s</u>awoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola law tusaww<u>a</u> bihimu al-ar<u>d</u>u wal<u>a</u> yaktumoona All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>adeeth<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  On that day<br> Those who reject Faith<br> And disobey the Apostle<br> Will wish that the earth<br> Were made one with them:<br> But never will they hide<br> A single fact from God!</p> <div class="filenav">
