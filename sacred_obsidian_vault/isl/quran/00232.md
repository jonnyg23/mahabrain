 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_243.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>243.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kharajoo min diy<u>a</u>rihim wahum oloofun <u>h</u>a<u>th</u>ara almawti faq<u>a</u>la lahumu All<u>a</u>hu mootoo thumma a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>hum inna All<u>a</u>ha la<u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>243.  Didst thou not<br> Turn by vision to those<br> Who abandoned their homes,<br> Though they were thousands<br> (In number), for fear of death?<br> God said to them: "Die":<br> Then He restored them to life.<br> For God is full of bounty<br> To mankind, but<br> Most of them are ungrateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_244.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>244.  Waq<u>a</u>tiloo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>244.  Then fight in the cause<br> Of God, and know that God<br> Heareth and knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_245.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>245.  Man <u>tha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee yuqri<u>d</u>u All<u>a</u>ha qar<u>d</u>an <u>h</u>asanan fayu<u>da</u>AAifahu lahu a<u>d</u>AA<u>a</u>fan katheeratan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaqbi<u>d</u>u wayabsu<u>t</u>u wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>245.  Who is he<br> That will loan to God<br> A beautiful loan, which God<br> Will double unto his credit<br> And multiply many times?<br> It is God that giveth (you)<br> Want or Plenty,<br> And to Him shall be<br> Your return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_246.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>246.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> almala-i min banee isr<u>a</u>-eela min baAAdi moos<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>loo linabiyyin lahumu ibAAath lan<u>a</u> malikan nuq<u>a</u>til fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>la hal AAasaytum in kutiba AAalaykumu alqit<u>a</u>lu all<u>a</u> tuq<u>a</u>tiloo q<u>a</u>loo wam<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> nuq<u>a</u>tila fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi waqad okhrijn<u>a</u> min diy<u>a</u>rin<u>a</u> waabn<u>a</u>-in<u>a</u> falamm<u>a</u> kutiba AAalayhimu alqit<u>a</u>lu tawallaw ill<u>a</u> qaleelan minhum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>246.  Hast thou not<br> Turned thy vision to the Chiefs<br> Of the Children of Israel<br> After (the time of) Moses?<br>They said to a Prophet<br> (That was) among them:<br> "Appoint for us a King, that we<br> May fight in the cause of God."<br> He said: "Is it not possible,<br> If ye were commanded<br> To fight, that that ye<br> Will not fight?" They said:<br> "How could we refuse<br> To fight in the cause of God,<br> Seeing that we were turned out<br> Of our homes and our families?"<br> But when they were commanded<br> To fight, they turned back,<br> Except a small band<br> Among them. But God<br> Has full knowledge of those<br> Who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_247.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>247.  Waq<u>a</u>la lahum nabiyyuhum inna All<u>a</u>ha qad baAAatha lakum <u>ta</u>loota malikan q<u>a</u>loo ann<u>a</u> yakoonu lahu almulku AAalayn<u>a</u> wana<u>h</u>nu a<u>h</u>aqqu bi<b>a</b>lmulki minhu walam yu/ta saAAatan mina alm<u>a</u>li q<u>a</u>la inna All<u>a</u>ha i<u>st</u>af<u>a</u>hu AAalaykum waz<u>a</u>dahu bas<u>t</u>atan fee alAAilmi wa<b>a</b>ljismi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu/tee mulkahu man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu w<u>a</u>siAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>247.  Their Prophet said to them:<br> "God hath appointed<br> Talut as king over you."<br> They said: "How can he<br> Exercise authority over us<br> When we are better fitted<br> Than he to exercise authority,<br> And he is not even gifted,<br> With wealth in abundance?"<br> He said: "God hath<br> Chosen him above you,<br> And hath gifted him<br> Abundantly with knowledge<br> And bodily prowess: God<br>Granteth His authority to whom<br> He pleaseth. God careth<br> For all, and He knoweth<br> All things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_248.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>248.  Waq<u>a</u>la lahum nabiyyuhum inna <u>a</u>yata mulkihi an ya/tiyakumu a<b>l</b>tt<u>a</u>bootu feehi sakeenatun min rabbikum wabaqiyyatun mimm<u>a</u> taraka <u>a</u>lu moos<u>a</u> wa<u>a</u>lu h<u>a</u>roona ta<u>h</u>miluhu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan lakum in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>248.  And (further) their Prophet<br> Said to them: "A Sign<br> Of his authority<br> Is that there shall come<br> To you the Ark of the Covenant,<br> With (an assurance) therein<br> Of security from your Lord,<br> And the relics left<br> By the family of Moses<br> And the family of Aaron,<br> Carried by angels.<br> In this is a Symbol<br> For you if ye indeed<br> Have faith."</p> <div class="filenav">
