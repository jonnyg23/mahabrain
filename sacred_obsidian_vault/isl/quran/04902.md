 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIX.: Ḥujurāt, or the Inner Apartments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> yaskhar qawmun min qawmin AAas<u>a</u> an yakoonoo khayran minhum wal<u>a</u> nis<u>a</u>on min nis<u>a</u>-in AAas<u>a</u> an yakunna khayran minhunna wal<u>a</u> talmizoo anfusakum wal<u>a</u> tan<u>a</u>bazoo bi<b>a</b>l-alq<u>a</u>bi bi/sa al-ismu alfusooqu baAAda al-eem<u>a</u>ni waman lam yatub faol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  O ye who believe!<br> Let not some men<br> Among you laugh at others:<br> It may be that<br> The (latter) are better<br> Than the (former):<br> Nor let some women<br> Laugh at others:<br>It may be that<br> The (latter) are better<br> Than the (former):<br> Nor defame nor be<br> Sarcastic to each other,<br> Nor call each other<br> By (offensive) nicknames:<br> Ill-seeming is a name<br> Connoting wickedness,<br> (To be used of one)<br> After he has believed:<br> And those who<br> Do not desist are<br> (Indeed) doing wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ijtaniboo katheeran mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anni inna baAA<u>d</u>a a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anni ithmun wal<u>a</u> tajassasoo wal<u>a</u> yaghtab baAA<u>d</u>ukum baAA<u>d</u>an ayu<u>h</u>ibbu a<u>h</u>adukum an ya/kula la<u>h</u>ma akheehi maytan fakarihtumoohu wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha taww<u>a</u>bun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  O ye who believe!<br> Avoid suspicion as much<br> (As possible): for suspicion<br> In some cases is a sin:<br> And spy not on each other,<br> Nor speak ill of each other<br> Behind their backs. Would any<br> Of you like to eat<br> The flesh of his dead<br> Brother? Nay, ye would<br> Abhor it… But fear God:<br> For God is Oft-Returning,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su inn<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>kum min <u>th</u>akarin waonth<u>a</u> wajaAAaln<u>a</u>kum shuAAooban waqab<u>a</u>-ila litaAA<u>a</u>rafoo inna akramakum AAinda All<u>a</u>hi atq<u>a</u>kum inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  O mankind! We created<br> You from a single (pair)<br> Of a male and a female,<br> And made you into<br> Nations and tribes, that<br> Ye may know each other<br> (Not that ye may despise<br> (Each other). Verily<br> The most honoured of you<br> In the sight of God<br> Is (he who is) the most<br> Righteous of you.<br> And God has full knowledge<br> And is well acquainted<br> (With all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Q<u>a</u>lati al-aAAr<u>a</u>bu <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> qul lam tu/minoo wal<u>a</u>kin qooloo aslamn<u>a</u> walamm<u>a</u> yadkhuli al-eem<u>a</u>nu fee quloobikum wa-in tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu l<u>a</u> yalitkum min aAAm<u>a</u>likum shay-an inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  The desert Arabs say,<br> "We believe." Say, ''Ye<br> Have no faith; but ye<br> (Only) say, "We have submitted<br> Our wills to God,"<br> For not yet has Faith<br> Entered your hearts.<br> But if ye obey God<br> And His Apostle, He<br> Will not belittle aught<br> Of your deeds: for God<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Innam<u>a</u> almu/minoona alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi thumma lam yart<u>a</u>boo waj<u>a</u>hadoo bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi ol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Only those are Believers<br> Who have believed in God<br> And His Apostle, and have<br> Never since doubted, but<br> Have striven with their<br> Belongings and their persons<br> In the Cause of God:<br> Such are the sincere ones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Qul atuAAallimoona All<u>a</u>ha bideenikum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Say: "What! Will ye<br> Instruct God about your<br> Religion? But God knows<br> All that is in the heavens<br> And on earth: He has<br> Full knowledge of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Yamunnoona AAalayka an aslamoo qul l<u>a</u> tamunnoo AAalayya isl<u>a</u>makum bali All<u>a</u>hu yamunnu AAalaykum an had<u>a</u>kum lil-eem<u>a</u>ni in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  They impress on thee<br> As favour that they<br> Have embraced Islam.<br> Say, "Count not your Islam<br> As a favour upon me:<br> Nay, God has conferred<br> A favour upon you<br> That He has guided you<br> To the Faith, if ye<br> Be true and sincere.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu ghayba a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ba<u>s</u>eerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  "Verily God knows<br> The secrets of the heavens<br> And the earth: and God<br> Sees well all<br> That ye do."</p>  <div class="filenav">
