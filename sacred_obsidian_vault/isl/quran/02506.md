 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXV.: Furqān, or The Criterion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Tab<u>a</u>raka alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i buroojan wajaAAala feeh<u>a</u> sir<u>a</u>jan waqamaran muneer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Blessed is He Who made<br> Constellations in the skies,<br> And placed therein a Lamp<br> And a Moon giving light;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra khilfatan liman ar<u>a</u>da an ya<u>thth</u>akkara aw ar<u>a</u>da shukoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  And it is He Who made<br> The Night and the Day<br> To follow each other:<br> For such as have the will<br> To celebrate His praises<br> Or to show their gratitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  WaAAib<u>a</u>du a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni alla<u>th</u>eena yamshoona AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i hawnan wa-i<u>tha</u> kh<u>at</u>abahumu alj<u>a</u>hiloona q<u>a</u>loo sal<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  And the servants of (God)<br> Most Gracious are those<br> Who walk on the earth<br> In humility, and when the ignorant<br> Address them, they say,<br> "Peace!";</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yabeetoona lirabbihim sujjadan waqiy<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Those who spend the night<br> In adoration of their Lord<br> Prostrate and standing;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> i<u>s</u>rif AAann<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ba jahannama inna AAa<u>tha</u>bah<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na ghar<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Those who say, "Our Lord!<br> Avert from us the Wrath<br> Of Hell, for its Wrath<br> Is indeed an affliction grievous,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Innah<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>at mustaqarran wamuq<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  "Evil indeed is it<br> As an abode, and as<br> A place to rest in";</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> anfaqoo lam yusrifoo walam yaqturoo wak<u>a</u>na bayna <u>tha</u>lika qaw<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Those who, when they spend,<br> Are not extravagant and not<br> Niggardly, but hold a just (balance)<br> Between those (extremes);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yadAAoona maAA<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara wal<u>a</u> yaqtuloona a<b>l</b>nnafsa allatee <u>h</u>arrama All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u> yaznoona waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika yalqa ath<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Those who invoke not,<br> With God, any other god,<br> Nor slay such life as God<br> Has made sacred, except<br> For just cause, nor commit<br> Fornication;—and any that does<br> This (not only) meets punishment</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Yu<u>da</u>AAaf lahu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wayakhlud feehi muh<u>a</u>n<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  (But) the Penalty on the Day<br> Of Judgment will be doubled<br> To him, and he will dwell<br> Therein in ignominy,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Ill<u>a</u> man t<u>a</u>ba wa<u>a</u>mana waAAamila AAamalan <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an faol<u>a</u>-ika yubaddilu All<u>a</u>hu sayyi-<u>a</u>tihim <u>h</u>asan<u>a</u>tin wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Unless he repents, believes,<br> And works righteous deeds,<br> For God will change<br> The evil of such persons<br> Into good, and God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Waman t<u>a</u>ba waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an fa-innahu yatoobu il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi mat<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  And whoever repents and does good<br> Has truly turned to God<br> With an (acceptable) conversion;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yashhadoona a<b>l</b>zzoora wa-i<u>tha</u> marroo bi<b>a</b>llaghwi marroo kir<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Those who witness no falsehood;<br> And, if they pass by futility,<br> They pass by it<br> With honourable (avoidance);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim lam yakhirroo AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>s</u>umman waAAumy<u>a</u>n<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  Those who, when they are<br> Admonished with the Signs<br> Of their Lord, droop not down<br> At them as if they were<br> Deaf or blind;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> hab lan<u>a</u> min azw<u>a</u>jin<u>a</u> wa<u>th</u>urriyy<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> qurrata aAAyunin wa<b>i</b>jAAaln<u>a</u> lilmuttaqeena im<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  And these who pray,<br> "Our Lord! Grant unto us<br> Wives and offspring who will be<br> The comfort of our eyes,<br>And give us (the grace)<br> To lead the righteous."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika yujzawna alghurfata bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo wayulaqqawna feeh<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>iyyatan wasal<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Those are the ones who<br> Will be rewarded with<br> The highest place in heaven,<br> Because of their patient constancy:<br> Therein shall they be met<br> With salutations and peace,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asunat mustaqarran wamuq<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Dwelling therein;—how beautiful<br> An abode and place of rest!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Qul m<u>a</u> yaAAbao bikum rabbee lawl<u>a</u> duAA<u>a</u>okum faqad ka<u>thth</u>abtum fasawfa yakoonu liz<u>a</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Say (to the Rejecters):<br> "My Lord is not uneasy<br> Because of you if ye call not on Him<br> But ye have indeed rejected<br> (Him), and soon will come<br> The inevitable (punishment)!"</p>  <div class="filenav">
