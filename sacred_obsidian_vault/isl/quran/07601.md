 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sura LXXVI.: Dahr, or Time, or Insān, or Man.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Hal at<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>ni <u>h</u>eenun mina a<b>l</b>ddahri lam yakun shay-an ma<u>th</u>koor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Has there not been<br> Over Man a long period<br> Of Time, when he was<br> Nothing—(not even) mentioned?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Inn<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na min nu<u>t</u>fatin amsh<u>a</u>jin nabtaleehi fajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu sameeAAan ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Verily We created<br> Man from a drop<br> Of mingled sperm,<br> In order to try him:<br> So We gave him (the gifts).<br> Of Hearing and Sight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Inn<u>a</u> hadayn<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>ssabeela imm<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>kiran wa-imm<u>a</u> kafoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  We showed him, the Way:<br> Whether he be grateful<br> Or ungrateful (rests<br> On his will).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inn<u>a</u> aAAtadn<u>a</u> lilk<u>a</u>fireena sal<u>a</u>sila waaghl<u>a</u>lan wasaAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  For the Rejecters<br> We have prepared<br>Chains, Yokes, and<br> A Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Inna al-abr<u>a</u>ra yashraboona min ka/sin k<u>a</u>na miz<u>a</u>juh<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>foor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  As to the Righteous,<br> They shall drink<br> Of a Cup (of Wine)<br> Mixed with Kāfūr,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  AAaynan yashrabu bih<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>du All<u>a</u>hi yufajjiroonah<u>a</u> tafjeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  A Fountain where<br> The Devotees of God<br> Do drink, making it<br> Flow in unstinted abundance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Yoofoona bi<b>al</b>nna<u>th</u>ri wayakh<u>a</u>foona yawman k<u>a</u>na sharruhu musta<u>t</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  They perform (their) vows,<br> And they fear a Day<br> Whose evil flies far and wide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wayu<u>t</u>AAimona a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>aAA<u>a</u>ma AAal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ubbihi miskeenan wayateeman waaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And they feed, for the love<br> Of God, the indigent,<br> The orphan, and the captive,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Innam<u>a</u> nu<u>t</u>AAimukum liwajhi All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> nureedu minkum jaz<u>a</u>an wal<u>a</u> shukoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  (Saying), "We feed you<br> For the sake of God alone:<br> No reward do we desire<br> From you, nor thanks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Inn<u>a</u> nakh<u>a</u>fu min rabbin<u>a</u> yawman AAaboosan qam<u>t</u>areer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  "We only fear a Day<br> Of distressful Wrath<br> From the side of our Lord."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fawaq<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu sharra <u>tha</u>lika alyawmi walaqq<u>a</u>hum na<u>d</u>ratan wasuroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  But God will deliver<br> Them from the evil<br> Of that Day, and will<br> Shed over them a Light<br> Of Beauty and<br> A (blissful) Joy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wajaz<u>a</u>hum bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo jannatan wa<u>h</u>areer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And because they were<br> Patient and constant, He will<br> Reward them with a Garden<br> And (garments of) silk.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Muttaki-eena feeh<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>a</u>-iki l<u>a</u> yarawna feeh<u>a</u> shamsan wal<u>a</u> zamhareer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Reclining in the (Garden)<br> On raised thrones,<br> They will see there neither<br> The sun's (excessive heat)<br> Nor (the moon's) excessive cold.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wad<u>a</u>niyatan AAalayhim <i><u>th</u></i>il<u>a</u>luh<u>a</u> wa<u>th</u>ullilat qu<u>t</u>oofuh<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>leel<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>14.  And the shades of the (Garden)<br> Will come low over them,<br> And the bunches (of fruit),<br> There, will hang low<br> In humility.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wayu<u>ta</u>fu AAalayhim bi-<u>a</u>niyatin min fi<u>dd</u>atin waakw<u>a</u>bin k<u>a</u>nat qaw<u>a</u>reer<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  And amongst them will be<br> Passed round vessels of silver<br> And goblets of crystal,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Qaw<u>a</u>reera min fi<u>dd</u>atin qaddarooh<u>a</u> taqdeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Crystal-clear, made of silver:<br> They will determine<br> The measure thereof<br> (According to their wishes).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wayusqawna feeh<u>a</u> ka/san k<u>a</u>na miz<u>a</u>juh<u>a</u> zanjabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And they will be given<br> To drink there of a Cup<br> (Of Wine) mixed<br> With Zanjaḅīl,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  AAaynan feeh<u>a</u> tusamm<u>a</u> salsabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  A fountain there,<br> Called Salsabīl.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waya<u>t</u>oofu AAalayhim wild<u>a</u>nun mukhalladoona i<u>tha</u> raaytahum <u>h</u>asibtahum lu/lu-an manthoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And round about them<br> Will (serve) youths<br> Of perpetual (freshness):<br> If thou seest them,<br> Thou wouldst think them<br> Scattered Pearls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> raayta thamma raayta naAAeeman wamulkan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And when thou lookest,<br> It is there thou wilt see<br>A Bliss and<br> A Realm Magnificent.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  AA<u>a</u>liyahum thiy<u>a</u>bu sundusin khu<u>d</u>run wa-istabraqun wa<u>h</u>ulloo as<u>a</u>wira min fi<u>dd</u>atin wasaq<u>a</u>hum rabbuhum shar<u>a</u>ban <u>t</u>ahoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>21.  Upon them will be<br> Green Garments of fine silk<br> And heavy brocade,<br> And they will be adorned<br> With Bracelets of silver;<br> And their Lord will<br> Give to them to drink<br> Of a Wine<br> Pure and Holy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Inna h<u>atha</u> k<u>a</u>na lakum jaz<u>a</u>an wak<u>a</u>na saAAyukum mashkoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  "Verily this is a Reward<br> For you, and your Endeavour<br> Is accepted and recognised."</p> <div class="filenav">
