 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXIII.: Taṭfīf, or Dealing in Fraud.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Waylun lilmu<u>t</u>affifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Woe to those<br> That deal in fraud,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> ikt<u>a</u>loo AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si yastawfoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Those who, when they<br> Have to receive by measure<br> From men, exact full measure,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> k<u>a</u>loohum aw wazanoohum yukhsiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  But when they have<br> To give by measure<br> Or weight to men,<br> Give less than due.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Al<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnu ol<u>a</u>-ika annahum mabAAoothoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Do they not think<br> That they will be called<br> To account?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Liyawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  On a Mighty Day,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yawma yaqoomu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su lirabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  A Day when (all) mankind<br> Will stand before<br> The Lord of the Worlds?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Kall<u>a</u> inna kit<u>a</u>ba alfujj<u>a</u>ri lafee sijjeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Nay! Surely the Record<br> Of the Wicked is<br>(Preserved) in Sijjīn.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> sijjeen<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>8.  And what will explain<br> To thee what Sijjīn is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Kit<u>a</u>bun marqoom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  (There is) a Register<br> (Fully) inscribed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Woe, that Day, to those<br> That deny—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yuka<u>thth</u>iboona biyawmi a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Those that deny<br> The Day of Judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wam<u>a</u> yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bihi ill<u>a</u> kullu muAAtadin atheem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And none can deny it<br> But the Transgressor<br> Beyond bounds,<br> The Sinner!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  I<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  When Our Signs are rehearsed<br> To him, he says,<br> "Tales of the Ancients!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Kall<u>a</u> bal r<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  By no means!<br> But on their hearts<br> Is the stain of the (ill)<br> Which they do!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Kall<u>a</u> innahum AAan rabbihim yawma-i<u>th</u>in lama<u>h</u>jooboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Verily, from (the Light<br> Of) their Lord, that Day,<br> Will they be veiled.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Thumma innahum la<u>sa</u>loo alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Further, they will enter<br> The Fire of Hell.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Thumma yuq<u>a</u>lu h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee kuntum bihi tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Further, it will be said<br> To them: "This is<br> The (reality) which ye<br> Rejected as false!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Kall<u>a</u> inna kit<u>a</u>ba al-abr<u>a</u>ri lafee AAilliyyeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Nay, verily the Record<br> Of the Righteous is<br> (Preserved) in ‘Illīyīn.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> AAilliyyoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And what will explain<br> To thee what ‘Illīyīn is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Kit<u>a</u>bun marqoom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  (There is) a Register<br> (Fully) inscribed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Yashhaduhu almuqarraboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  To which bear witness<br> Those Nearest (to God),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Inna al-abr<u>a</u>ra lafee naAAeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Truly the Righteous<br> Will be in Bliss:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>a</u>-iki yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>23.  On Thrones (of Dignity)<br> Will they command a sight<br> (Of all things):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  TaAArifu fee wujoohihim na<u>d</u>rata a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Thou wilt recognise<br> In their Faces<br> The beaming brightness of Bliss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Yusqawna min ra<u>h</u>eeqin makhtoom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Their thirst will be slaked<br> With Pure Wine sealed:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Khit<u>a</u>muhu miskun wafee <u>tha</u>lika falyatan<u>a</u>fasi almutan<u>a</u>fisoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  The seal thereof will be<br> Musk: and for this<br> Let those aspire,<br> Who have aspirations:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wamiz<u>a</u>juhu min tasneem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  With it will be (given)<br> A mixture of Tasnīm:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  AAaynan yashrabu bih<u>a</u> almuqarraboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  A spring, from (the waters)<br> Whereof drink<br> Those Nearest to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena ajramoo k<u>a</u>noo mina alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ya<u>dh</u>akoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Those in sin used<br> To laugh at those<br> Who believed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> marroo bihim yatagh<u>a</u>mazoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  And whenever they passed<br> By them, used to wink<br> At each other (in mockery);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> inqalaboo il<u>a</u> ahlihimu inqalaboo fakiheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  And when they returned<br> To their own people,<br> They would return jesting;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> raawhum q<u>a</u>loo inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i la<u>da</u>lloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And whenever they saw them,<br> They would say, "Behold!<br> These are the people<br> Truly astray!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wam<u>a</u> orsiloo AAalayhim <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  But they had not been<br> Sent as Keepers over them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Fa<b>a</b>lyawma alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo mina alkuff<u>a</u>ri ya<u>dh</u>akoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  But on this Day<br> The Believers will laugh<br> At the Unbelievers:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>a</u>-iki yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>35.  On Thrones (of Dignity)<br> They will command (a sight)<br> (Of all things),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/083/083_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Hal thuwwiba alkuff<u>a</u>ru m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Will not the Unbelievers<br> Have been paid back<br> For what they did?</p>  <div class="filenav">
