 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLII.: Shūrā, or Consultation.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wam<u>a</u> ikhtalaftum feehi min shay-in fa<u>h</u>ukmuhu il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu rabbee AAalayhi tawakkaltu wa-ilayhi oneeb<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Whatever it be wherein<br> Ye differ, the decision<br> Thereof is with God:<br> Such is God my Lord:<br> In Him I trust,<br> And to Him I turn.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  F<u>at</u>iru a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i jaAAala lakum min anfusikum azw<u>a</u>jan wamina al-anAA<u>a</u>mi azw<u>a</u>jan ya<u>th</u>raokum feehi laysa kamithlihi shay-on wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alba<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  (He is) the Creator<br> Of the heavens and<br> The earth: He has made<br> For you pairs<br> From among yourselves,<br> And pairs among cattle:<br> By this means does He<br> Multiply you: there is nothing<br> Whatever like unto Him,<br> And He is the One<br> That hears and sees (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Lahu maq<u>a</u>leedu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o wayaqdiru innahu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  To Him belong the keys<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br>He enlarges and restricts.<br> The Sustenance to whom<br> He will: for He knows<br> Full well all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  SharaAAa lakum mina a<b>l</b>ddeeni m<u>a</u> wa<u>ssa</u> bihi noo<u>h</u>an wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka wam<u>a</u> wa<u>ss</u>ayn<u>a</u> bihi ibr<u>a</u>heema wamoos<u>a</u> waAAees<u>a</u> an aqeemoo a<b>l</b>ddeena wal<u>a</u> tatafarraqoo feehi kabura AAal<u>a</u> almushrikeena m<u>a</u> tadAAoohum ilayhi All<u>a</u>hu yajtabee ilayhi man yash<u>a</u>o wayahdee ilayhi man yuneeb<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  The same religion has He<br> Established for you as that<br> Which He enjoined on Noah—<br> The which We have sent<br> By inspiration to thee—<br> And that which We enjoined<br> On Abraham, Moses, and Jesus:<br> Namely, that ye should remain<br> Steadfast in Religion, and make<br> No divisions therein:<br> To those who worship<br> Other things than God,<br> Hard is the (way)<br> To which thou callest them,<br> God chooses to Himself<br> Those whom He pleases,<br> And guides to Himself<br> Those who turn (to Him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wam<u>a</u> tafarraqoo ill<u>a</u> min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu alAAilmu baghyan baynahum walawl<u>a</u> kalimatun sabaqat min rabbika il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman laqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum wa-inna alla<u>th</u>eena oorithoo alkit<u>a</u>ba min baAAdihim lafee shakkin minhu mureeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And they became divided<br> Only after knowledge<br> Reached them,—through selfish<br> Envy as between themselves.<br> Had it not been<br> For a Word that<br>Went forth before<br> From thy Lord,<br> (Tending) to a Term appointed,<br> The matter would have<br> Been settled between them:<br> But truly those who have<br> Inherited the Book after them<br> Are in suspicious (disquieting)<br> Doubt concerning it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Fali<u>tha</u>lika fa<b>o</b>dAAu wa<b>i</b>staqim kam<u>a</u> omirta wal<u>a</u> tattabiAA ahw<u>a</u>ahum waqul <u>a</u>mantu bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu min kit<u>a</u>bin waomirtu li-aAAdila baynakum All<u>a</u>hu rabbun<u>a</u> warabbukum lan<u>a</u> aAAm<u>a</u>lun<u>a</u> walakum aAAm<u>a</u>lukum l<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ujjata baynan<u>a</u> wabaynakumu All<u>a</u>hu yajmaAAu baynan<u>a</u> wa-ilayhi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Now then, for that (reason),<br> Call (them to the Faith),<br> And stand steadfast<br> As thou art commanded,<br> Nor follow thou their vain<br> Desires; but say: "I believe<br> In the Book which<br> God has sent down;<br> And I am commanded<br> To judge justly between you.<br> God is our Lord<br> And your Lord. For us<br> (Is the responsibility for)<br> Our deeds, and for you<br> For your deeds. There is<br> No contention between us<br> And you. God will<br> Bring us together,<br> And to Him is<br> (Our) final goal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>ha</u>jjoona fee All<u>a</u>hi min baAAdi m<u>a</u> istujeeba lahu <u>h</u>ujjatuhum d<u>ah</u>i<u>d</u>atun AAinda rabbihim waAAalayhim gha<u>d</u>abun walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  But those who dispute<br> Concerning God after He<br> Has been accepted,—<br> Futile is their dispute<br> In the sight of<br> Their Lord: on them<br> Is Wrath, and for them<br> Will be a Penalty<br> Terrible.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee anzala alkit<u>a</u>ba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa<b>a</b>lmeez<u>a</u>ni wam<u>a</u> yudreeka laAAalla a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata qareeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  It is God Who has<br> Sent down the Book in truth,<br> And the Balance<br> (By which to weigh conduct).<br> And what will make thee<br> Realise that perhaps the Hour<br> Is close at hand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  YastaAAjilu bih<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bih<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo mushfiqoona minh<u>a</u> wayaAAlamoona annah<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqu al<u>a</u> inna alla<u>th</u>eena yum<u>a</u>roona fee a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Only those wish to<br> Hasten it who believe not<br> In it: those who believe<br> Hold it in awe,<br> And know that it is<br> The Truth. Behold, verily<br> Those that dispute concerning<br> The Hour are far astray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  All<u>a</u>hu la<u>t</u>eefun biAAib<u>a</u>dihi yarzuqu man yash<u>a</u>o wahuwa alqawiyyu alAAazeez<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Gracious is God<br> To His servants:<br>He gives Sustenance<br> To whom He pleases:<br> And He has Power<br> And can carry out<br> His Will.</p> <div class="filenav">
