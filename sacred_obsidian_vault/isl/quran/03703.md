 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVII.: Ṣāffāt, or Those Ranged in Ranks.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Walaqad n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>un falaniAAma almujeeboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  (In the days of old),<br> Noah cried to Us,<br> And We are the Best<br> To hear prayer.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wanajjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu mina alkarbi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  And We delivered him<br> And his people from<br> The Great Calamity,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> <u>th</u>urriyyatahu humu alb<u>a</u>qeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  And made his progeny<br> To endure (on this earth);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Watarakn<u>a</u> AAalayhi fee al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And We left (this blessing)<br> For him among generations<br> To come in later times:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Sal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>in fee alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  "Peace and salutation to Noah<br> Among the nations!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Thus indeed do We reward<br> Those who do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Innahu min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  For he was one<br> Of Our believing Servants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Thumma aghraqn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Then the rest We overwhelmed<br> In the Flood.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wa-inna min sheeAAatihi la-ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Verily among those<br> Who followed his Way<br> Was Abraham.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  I<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>a rabbahu biqalbin saleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Behold, he approached his Lord<br> With a sound heart.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la li-abeehi waqawmihi m<u>atha</u> taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  Behold, he said to his father<br> And to his people, "What<br> Is that which ye worship?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  A-ifkan <u>a</u>lihatan doona All<u>a</u>hi tureedoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  "Is it a Falsehood—<br> Gods other than God—<br> That ye desire?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Fam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>annukum birabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  "Then what is your idea<br> About the Lord of the<br> Worlds?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Fana<i><u>th</u></i>ara na<i><u>th</u></i>ratan fee a<b>l</b>nnujoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Then did he cast<br> A glance at the Stars,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Faq<u>a</u>la innee saqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  And he said, "I am<br> Indeed sick (at heart)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Fatawallaw AAanhu mudbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  So they turned away<br> From him, and departed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Far<u>a</u>gha il<u>a</u> <u>a</u>lihatihim faq<u>a</u>la al<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  When did he turn<br> To their gods and said,<br> "Will ye not eat<br> (Of the offerings before you)?…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  M<u>a</u> lakum l<u>a</u> tan<u>t</u>iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  "What is the matter<br> With you that ye<br> Speak not (intelligently)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Far<u>a</u>gha AAalayhim <u>d</u>arban bi<b>a</b>lyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  Then did he turn<br> Upon them, striking (them)<br> With the right hand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Faaqbaloo ilayhi yaziffoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  Then came (the worshippers)<br> With hurried steps,<br> And faced (him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Q<u>a</u>la ataAAbudoona m<u>a</u> tan<u>h</u>itoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  He said: "Worship ye<br> That which ye have<br> (Yourselves) carved?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khalaqakum wam<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  "But God has created you<br> And your handiwork!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Q<u>a</u>loo ibnoo lahu buny<u>a</u>nan faalqoohu fee alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  They said, "Build him<br> A furnace, and throw him<br> Into the blazing fire!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Faar<u>a</u>doo bihi kaydan fajaAAaln<u>a</u>humu al-asfaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  (This failing), they then<br> Sought a stratagem against him,<br>But We made them the ones<br> Most humiliated!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Waq<u>a</u>la innee <u>tha</u>hibun il<u>a</u> rabbee sayahdeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  He said: "I will go<br> To my Lord! He<br> Will surely guide me!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Rabbi hab lee mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  "O my Lord! grant me<br> A righteous (son)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Fabashsharn<u>a</u>hu bighul<u>a</u>min <u>h</u>aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  So We gave him<br> The good news<br> Of a boy ready<br> To suffer and forbear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Falamm<u>a</u> balagha maAAahu a<b>l</b>ssaAAya q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> bunayya innee ar<u>a</u> fee alman<u>a</u>mi annee a<u>th</u>ba<u>h</u>uka fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur m<u>atha</u> tar<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> abati ifAAal m<u>a</u> tu/maru satajidunee in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Then, when (the son)<br> Reached (the age of)<br> (Serious) work with him,<br> He said: "O my son<br> I see in vision<br> That I offer thee in sacrifice:<br> Now see what is<br> Thy view!" (The son) said:<br> "O my father! Do<br> As thou art commanded:<br>Thou will find me,<br> If God so wills one<br> Practising Patience and Constancy!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Falamm<u>a</u> aslam<u>a</u> watallahu liljabeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  So when they had both<br> Submitted their wills (to God),<br> And he had laid him<br> Prostrate on his forehead<br> (For sacrifice),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Wan<u>a</u>dayn<u>a</u>hu an y<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  We called out to him,<br> "O Abraham!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Qad <u>s</u>addaqta a<b>l</b>rru/y<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  "Thou hast already fulfilled<br> The vision!"—thus indeed<br> Do We reward<br> Those who do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Inna h<u>atha</u> lahuwa albal<u>a</u>o almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  For this was obviously<br> A trial—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Wafadayn<u>a</u>hu bi<u>th</u>ib<u>h</u>in AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  And We ransomed him<br> With a momentous sacrifice:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Watarakn<u>a</u> AAalayhi fee al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  And We left (this blessing)<br> For him among generations<br> (To come) in later times:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Sal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  "Peace and salutation<br> To Abraham!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  Thus indeed do We reward<br> Those who do right</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Innahu min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  For he was one<br> Of Our believing Servants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Wabashsharn<u>a</u>hu bi-is<u>ha</u>qa nabiyyan mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  And We gave him<br> The good news<br> Of Isaac—a prophet,—<br> One of the Righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Wab<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> AAalayhi waAAal<u>a</u> is<u>ha</u>qa wamin <u>th</u>urriyyatihim<u>a</u> mu<u>h</u>sinun wa<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limun linafsihi mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  We blessed him and Isaac:<br> But of their progeny<br> Are (some) that do right,<br> And (some) that obviously<br> Do wrong, to their own souls.</p> <div class="filenav">
