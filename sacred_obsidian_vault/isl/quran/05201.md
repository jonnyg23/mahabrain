 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LII.: Ṭūr, or the Mount.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>oor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Mount (of Revelation);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wakit<u>a</u>bin mas<u>t</u>oor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  By a Decree Inscribed</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Fee raqqin manshoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  In a Scroll unfolded;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>lbayti almaAAmoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  By the much-frequented Fane;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa<b>al</b>ssaqfi almarfooAA<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  By the Canopy Raised High;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri almasjoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And by the Ocean<br> Filled with Swell;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Inna AAa<u>tha</u>ba rabbika law<u>a</u>qiAA<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Verily, the Doom of thy Lord<br> Will indeed come to pass;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  M<u>a</u> lahu min d<u>a</u>fiAA<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  There is none<br> Can avert it;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Yawma tamooru a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o mawr<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>9.  On the Day when<br> The firmament will be<br> In dreadful commotion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wataseeru aljib<u>a</u>lu sayr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And the mountains will fly<br> Hither and thither.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fawaylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Then woe that Day<br> To those that treat<br> (Truth) as Falsehood;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Alla<u>th</u>eena hum fee khaw<u>d</u>in yalAAaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  That play (and paddle)<br> In shallow trifles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Yawma yudaAAAAoona il<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ri jahannama daAAAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  That Day shall they be<br> Thrust down to the Fire<br> Of Hell, irresistibly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  H<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru allatee kuntum bih<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "This", it will be said,<br> "Is the Fire,—which ye<br> Were wont to deny!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Afasi<u>h</u>run h<u>atha</u> am antum l<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  "Is this then a fake,<br> Or is it ye that<br> Do not see?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  I<u>s</u>lawh<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>biroo aw l<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>biroo saw<u>a</u>on AAalaykum innam<u>a</u> tujzawna m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "Burn ye therein:<br> The same is it to you<br> Whether ye bear it<br> With patience, or not:<br> Ye but receive the recompense<br> Of your (own) deeds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Inna almuttaqeena fee jann<u>a</u>tin wanaAAeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  As to the Righteous,<br> They will be in Gardens,<br> And in Happiness,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  F<u>a</u>kiheena bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum rabbuhum wawaq<u>a</u>hum rabbuhum AAa<u>tha</u>ba alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Enjoying the (Bliss) which<br> Their Lord hath bestowed<br> On them, and their Lord<br> Shall deliver them from<br> The Penalty of the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Kuloo wa<b>i</b>shraboo hanee-an bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  (To them will be said:)<br> "Eat and drink ye,<br> With profit and health,<br> Because of your (good) deeds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Muttaki-eena AAal<u>a</u> sururin ma<u>s</u>foofatin wazawwajn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>h</u>oorin AAeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  They will recline (with ease)<br> On Thrones (of dignity)<br> Arranged in ranks;<br> And We shall join them<br> To Companions, with beautiful<br> Big and lustrous eyes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAat-hum <u>th</u>urriyyatuhum bi-eem<u>a</u>nin al<u>h</u>aqn<u>a</u> bihim <u>th</u>urriyyatahum wam<u>a</u> alatn<u>a</u>hum min AAamalihim min shay-in kullu imri-in bim<u>a</u> kasaba raheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And those who believe<br> And whose families follow<br> Them in Faith,— to them<br> Shall We join their families:<br> Nor shall We deprive them<br> (Of the fruit) of aught<br> Of their works:<br> (Yet) is each individual<br> In pledge for his deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Waamdadn<u>a</u>hum bif<u>a</u>kihatin wala<u>h</u>min mimm<u>a</u> yashtahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And We shall bestow<br> On them, of fruit and meat,<br> Anything they shall desire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Yatan<u>a</u>zaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> ka/san l<u>a</u> laghwun feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta/theem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  They shall there exchange,<br> One with another,<br> A (loving) cup<br> Free of frivolity,<br> Free of all taint<br> Of ill.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Waya<u>t</u>oofu AAalayhim ghilm<u>a</u>nun lahum kaannahum lu/luon maknoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Round about them will serve,<br> (Devoted) to them,<br> Youths (handsome) as Pearls<br> Well-guarded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waaqbala baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  They will advance<br> To each other, engaging<br> In mutual enquiry.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> qablu fee ahlin<u>a</u> mushfiqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  They will say: "Aforetime,<br> We were not without fear<br> For the sake of our people.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Famanna All<u>a</u>hu AAalayn<u>a</u> wawaq<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>ssamoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "But God has been good<br> To us, and has delivered us<br> From the Penalty<br> Of the Scorching Wind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> min qablu nadAAoohu innahu huwa albarru a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "Truly, we did call<br> Unto Him from of old:<br> Truly it is He,<br> The Beneficent, the Merciful!</p> <div class="filenav">
