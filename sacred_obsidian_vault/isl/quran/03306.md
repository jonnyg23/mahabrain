 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo o<u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha <u>th</u>ikran katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  O ye who believe!<br> Celebrate the praises of God,<br> And do this often;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wasabbi<u>h</u>oohu bukratan waa<u>s</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  And glorify Him<br> Morning and evening.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee yu<u>s</u>allee AAalaykum wamal<u>a</u>-ikatuhu liyukhrijakum mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori wak<u>a</u>na bi<b>a</b>lmu/mineena ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  He it is Who sends<br> Blessings on you, as do<br> His angels, that He may<br> Bring you out from the depths<br> Of Darkness into Light:<br> And He is Full of Mercy<br> To the Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Ta<u>h</u>iyyatuhum yawma yalqawnahu sal<u>a</u>mun waaAAadda lahum ajran kareem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Their salutation on the Day<br> They meet Him will be<br> "Peace!"; and He has<br> Prepared for them<br> A generous Reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka sh<u>a</u>hidan wamubashshiran wana<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  O Prophet! Truly We<br> Have sent thee as<br>A Witness, a Bearer<br> Of Glad Tidings,<br> And a Warner,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wad<u>a</u>AAiyan il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi bi-i<u>th</u>nihi wasir<u>a</u>jan muneer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And as one who invites<br> To God's (Grace) by His leave,<br> And as a Lamp<br> Spreading Light.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wabashshiri almu/mineena bi-anna lahum mina All<u>a</u>hi fa<u>d</u>lan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Then give the glad tidings<br> To the Believers, that<br> They shall have from God<br> A very great Bounty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAi alk<u>a</u>fireena wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiqeena wadaAA a<u>tha</u>hum watawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And obey not (the behests)<br> Of the Unbelievers<br> And the Hypocrites,<br> And heed not their annoyances,<br> But put thy trust in God.<br> For enough is God<br> As a Disposer of affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> naka<u>h</u>tumu almu/min<u>a</u>ti thumma <u>t</u>allaqtumoohunna min qabli an tamassoohunna fam<u>a</u> lakum AAalayhinna min AAiddatin taAAtaddoonah<u>a</u> famattiAAoohunna wasarri<u>h</u>oohunna sar<u>ah</u>an jameel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye marry believing women,<br> And then divorce them<br> Before ye have touched them,<br>No period of ’Iddat<br> Have ye to count<br> In respect of them:<br> So give them a present,<br> And set them free<br> In a handsome manner.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu inn<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>laln<u>a</u> laka azw<u>a</u>jaka all<u>a</u>tee <u>a</u>tayta ojoorahunna wam<u>a</u> malakat yameenuka mimm<u>a</u> af<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu AAalayka waban<u>a</u>ti AAammika waban<u>a</u>ti AAamm<u>a</u>tika waban<u>a</u>ti kh<u>a</u>lika waban<u>a</u>ti kh<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>tika all<u>a</u>tee h<u>a</u>jarna maAAaka wa<b>i</b>mraatan mu/minatan in wahabat nafsah<u>a</u> li<b>l</b>nnabiyyi in ar<u>a</u>da a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu an yastanki<u>h</u>ah<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>li<u>s</u>atan laka min dooni almu/mineena qad AAalimn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> fara<u>d</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayhim fee azw<u>a</u>jihim wam<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nuhum likayl<u>a</u> yakoona AAalayka <u>h</u>arajun wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  O Prophet! We have<br> Made lawful to thee<br> Thy wives to whom thou<br> Hast paid their dowers;<br> And those whom thy<br> Right hand possesses out of<br> The prisoners of war whom<br> God has assigned to thee;<br> And daughters of thy paternal<br> Uncles and aunts, and daughters<br> Of thy maternal uncles<br> And aunts, who migrated<br> (From Mecca) with thee;<br> And any believing woman<br> Who dedicates her soul<br>To the Prophet if the Prophet<br> Wishes to wed her;—this<br> Only for thee, and not<br> For the Believers (at large);<br> We know what We have<br> Appointed for them as to<br> Their wives and the captives<br> Whom their right hands<br> Possess;—in order that<br> There should be no difficulty<br> For thee. And God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Turjee man tash<u>a</u>o minhunna watu/wee ilayka man tash<u>a</u>o wamani ibtaghayta mimman AAazalta fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayka <u>tha</u>lika adn<u>a</u> an taqarra aAAyunuhunna wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>zanna wayar<u>d</u>ayna bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytahunna kulluhunna wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee quloobikum wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAaleeman <u>h</u>aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Thou mayest defer (the turn<br> Of) any of them that thou<br> Pleasest, and thou mayest receive<br> Any thou pleasest: and there<br> Is no blame on thee if<br> Thou invite one whose (turn)<br>Thou hadst set aside.<br> This were nigher to<br> The cooling of their eyes,<br> The prevention of their grief,<br> And their satisfaction—<br> That of all of them—<br> With that which thou<br> Hast to give them:<br> And God knows (all)<br> That is in your hearts:<br> And God is All-Knowing,<br> Most Forbearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  L<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>illu laka a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>o min baAAdu wal<u>a</u> an tabaddala bihinna min azw<u>a</u>jin walaw aAAjabaka <u>h</u>usnuhunna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> malakat yameenuka wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in raqeeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  It is not lawful for thee<br> (To marry more) women<br> After this, nor to change<br> Them for (other) wives,<br> Even though their beauty<br> Attract thee, except any<br> Thy right hand should<br> Possess (as handmaidens):<br> And God doth watch<br> Over all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
