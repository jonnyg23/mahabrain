 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIV.: Nūr, or Light.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena j<u>a</u>oo bi<b>a</b>l-ifki AAu<u>s</u>batun minkum l<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>saboohu sharran lakum bal huwa khayrun lakum likulli imri-in minhum m<u>a</u> iktasaba mina al-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee tawall<u>a</u> kibrahu minhum lahu AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Those who brought forward<br> The lie are a body<br>Among yourselves: think it not<br> To be an evil to you;<br> On the contrary it is good<br> For you: to every man<br> Among them (will come<br> The punishment) of the sin<br> That he earned, and to him<br> Who took on himself the lead<br> Among them, will be<br> A Penalty grievous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Lawl<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> samiAAtumoohu <i><u>th</u></i>anna almu/minoona wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>tu bi-anfusihim khayran waq<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> ifkun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Why did not the Believers<br> Men and women—when ye<br> Heard of the affair,—put<br> The best construction on it<br> In their own minds<br> And say, "This (charge)<br> Is an obvious lie"?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Lawl<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>oo AAalayhi bi-arbaAAati shuhad<u>a</u>a fa-i<u>th</u> lam ya/too bi<b>al</b>shshuhad<u>a</u>-i faol<u>a</u>-ika AAinda All<u>a</u>hi humu alk<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Why did they not bring<br> Four witnesses to prove it?<br> When they have not brought<br> The witnesses, such men,<br> In the sight of God,<br> (Stand forth) themselves as liars!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wara<u>h</u>matuhu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati lamassakum fee m<u>a</u> afa<u>d</u>tum feehi AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Were it not for the grace<br> And mercy of God on you,<br> In this world and the Hereafter,<br> A grievous penalty would have<br> Seized you in that ye rushed<br> Glibly into this affair.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  I<u>th</u> talaqqawnahu bi-alsinatikum wataqooloona bi-afw<u>a</u>hikum m<u>a</u> laysa lakum bihi AAilmun wata<u>h</u>saboonahu hayyinan wahuwa AAinda All<u>a</u>hi AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Behold, ye received it<br> On your tongues,<br>And said out of your mouths<br> Things of which ye had<br> No knowledge; and ye thought<br> It to be a light matter,<br> While it was most serious<br> In the sight of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Walawl<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> samiAAtumoohu qultum m<u>a</u> yakoonu lan<u>a</u> an natakallama bih<u>atha</u> sub<u>ha</u>naka h<u>atha</u> buht<u>a</u>nun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And why did ye not,<br> When ye heard it, say?—<br> "It is not right of us<br> To speak of this:<br> Glory to God! this is<br> A most serious slander!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  YaAAi<i><u>th</u></i>ukumu All<u>a</u>hu an taAAoodoo limithlihi abadan in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  God doth admonish you,<br> That ye may never repeat<br> Such (conduct), if ye<br> Are (true) Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wayubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  And God makes the Signs<br> Plain to you: for God<br> Is full of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>h</u>ibboona an tasheeAAa alf<u>ah</u>ishatu fee alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleemun fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu waantum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Those who love (to see)<br> Scandal published broadcast<br> Among the Believers, will have<br> A grievous Penalty in this life<br> And in the Hereafter: God<br> Knows, and ye know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Walawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wara<u>h</u>matuhu waanna All<u>a</u>ha raoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Were it not for the grace<br> And mercy of God on you,<br>And that God is<br> Full of kindness and mercy,<br> (Ye would be ruined indeed).</p> <div class="filenav">
