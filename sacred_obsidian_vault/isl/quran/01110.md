 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba fa<b>i</b>khtulifa feehi walawl<u>a</u> kalimatun sabaqat min rabbika laqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum wa-innahum lafee shakkin minhu mureeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  We certainly gave the Book<br> To Moses, but differences<br> Arose therein: had it not been<br> That a Word had gone forth<br> Before from thy Lord, the matter<br> Would have been decided<br> Between them: but they<br> Are in suspicious doubt<br> Concerning it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Wa-inna kullan lamm<u>a</u> layuwaffiyannahum rabbuka aAAm<u>a</u>lahum innahu bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  And, of a surety, to all<br> Will your Lord pay back<br> (In full the recompense)<br> Of their deeds: for He<br> Knoweth well all that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Fa<b>i</b>staqim kam<u>a</u> omirta waman t<u>a</u>ba maAAaka wal<u>a</u> ta<u>t</u>ghaw innahu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  Therefore stand firm (in the straight<br> Path) as thou art commanded,—<br>Thou and those who with thee<br> Turn (unto God); and transgress not<br> (From the Path): for He seeth<br> Well all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Wal<u>a</u> tarkanoo il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo fatamassakumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wam<u>a</u> lakum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min awliy<u>a</u>a thumma l<u>a</u> tun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  And incline not to those<br> Who do wrong, or the Fire<br> Will seize you; and ye have<br> No protectors other than God,<br> Nor shall ye be helped.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Waaqimi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta <u>t</u>arafayi a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wazulafan mina allayli inna al<u>h</u>asan<u>a</u>ti yu<u>th</u>hibna a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti <u>tha</u>lika <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><u>ththa</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  And establish regular prayers<br> At the two ends of the day<br> And at the approaches of the night:<br> For those things that are good<br> Remove those that are evil:<br> Be that the word of remembrance<br> To those who remember (their Lord):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  And be steadfast in patience;<br> For verily God will not suffer<br> The reward of the righteous<br> To perish.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Falawl<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina alqurooni min qablikum oloo baqiyyatin yanhawna AAani alfas<u>a</u>di fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> qaleelan mimman anjayn<u>a</u> minhum wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAa alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo m<u>a</u> otrifoo feehi wak<u>a</u>noo mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  Why were there not,<br> Among the generations before you,<br> Persons possessed of balanced<br> Good sense, prohibiting (men)<br>From mischief in the earth—<br> Except a few among them<br> Whom We saved (from harm)?<br> But the wrong-doers pursued<br> The enjoyment of the good things<br> Of life which were given them,<br> And persisted in sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na rabbuka liyuhlika alqur<u>a</u> bi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmin waahluh<u>a</u> mu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  Nor would thy Lord be<br> The One to destroy<br> Communities for a single wrong-doing,<br> If its members were likely<br> To mend.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Walaw sh<u>a</u>a rabbuka lajaAAala a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan wal<u>a</u> yaz<u>a</u>loona mukhtalifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  If thy Lord had so willed,<br> He could have made mankind<br> One People: but they<br> Will not cease to dispute,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Ill<u>a</u> man ra<u>h</u>ima rabbuka wali<u>tha</u>lika khalaqahum watammat kalimatu rabbika laamlaanna jahannama mina aljinnati wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  Except those on whom thy Lord<br> Hath bestowed His Mercy:<br> And for this did He create<br> Them: and the Word<br> Of thy Lord shall be fulfilled:<br> "I will fill Hell with jinns<br> And men all together."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Wakullan naqu<u>ss</u>u AAalayka min anb<u>a</u>-i a<b>l</b>rrusuli m<u>a</u> nuthabbitu bihi fu-<u>a</u>daka waj<u>a</u>aka fee h<u>ath</u>ihi al<u>h</u>aqqu wamawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atun wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  All that we relate to thee<br> Of the stories of the apostles,—<br> With it We make firm<br> Thy heart: in them there cometh<br> To thee the Truth, as well as<br> An exhortation and a message<br> Of remembrance to those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Waqul lilla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona iAAmaloo AAal<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>natikum inn<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>miloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Say to those who do not<br> Believe: "Do whatever ye can:<br> We shall do our part;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Wa<b>i</b>nta<i><u>th</u></i>iroo inn<u>a</u> munta<i><u>th</u></i>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  "And wait ye!<br> We too shall wait."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Walill<u>a</u>hi ghaybu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa-ilayhi yurjaAAu al-amru kulluhu fa<b>o</b>AAbudhu watawakkal AAalayhi wam<u>a</u> rabbuka bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  To God do belong<br> The unseen (secrets)<br> Of the heavens and the earth,<br> And to Him goeth back<br> Every affair (for decision)<br> Then worship Him,<br> And put thy trust in Him .<br> And thy Lord is not<br> Unmindful of aught<br> That ye do.</p>  <div class="filenav">
