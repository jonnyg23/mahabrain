 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLI.: Ḥā-mīm (Abbreviated Letters), or Ḥā-Mīm Sajda, or Fuṣṣilat</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba fa<b>i</b>khtulifa feehi walawl<u>a</u> kalimatun sabaqat min rabbika laqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum wa-innahum lafee shakkin minhu mureeb<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>45.  We certainly gave Moses<br> The Book aforetime: but disputes<br> Arose therein. Had it not<br> Been for a Word<br> That went forth before<br> From thy Lord, (their differences)<br> Would have been settled<br> Between them: but they<br> Remained in suspicious<br> Disquieting doubt thereon.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Man AAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an falinafsihi waman as<u>a</u>a faAAalayh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> rabbuka bi<i><u>th</u></i>all<u>a</u>min lilAAabeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Whoever works righteousness<br> Benefits his own soul;<br> Whoever works evil, it is<br> Against his own soul:<br> Nor is thy Lord ever<br> Unjust (in the least)<br> To His servants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Ilayhi yuraddu AAilmu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati wam<u>a</u> takhruju min thamar<u>a</u>tin min akm<u>a</u>mih<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>milu min onth<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>aAAu ill<u>a</u> biAAilmihi wayawma yun<u>a</u>deehim ayna shurak<u>a</u>-ee q<u>a</u>loo <u>ath</u>ann<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> minn<u>a</u> min shaheed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  To Him is referred<br> The Knowledge of the Hour<br> (Of Judgment: He knows all):<br> No date-fruit comes out<br> Of its sheath, nor does<br> A female conceive (within<br> Her womb) nor bring forth<br> (Young), but by His Knowledge.<br> The Day that (God) will propound<br> To them the (question),<br> "Where are the Partners<br> (Ye attributed) to Me?"<br> They will say, "We do<br> Assure Thee not one<br> Of us can bear witness!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa<u>d</u>alla AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yadAAoona min qablu wa<i><u>th</u></i>annoo m<u>a</u> lahum min ma<u>h</u>ee<u>s</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  The (deities) they used to invoke<br> Aforetime will leave them<br> In the lurch, and they<br> Will perceive that they<br> Have no way of escape.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  L<u>a</u> yas-amu al-ins<u>a</u>nu min duAA<u>a</u>-i alkhayri wa-in massahu a<b>l</b>shsharru fayaoosun qanoo<u>t</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Man does not weary<br> Of asking for good (things),<br> But if ill touches him,<br> He gives up all hope<br> (And) is lost in despair.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wala-in a<u>th</u>aqn<u>a</u>hu ra<u>h</u>matan minn<u>a</u> min baAAdi <u>d</u>arr<u>a</u>a massat-hu layaqoolanna h<u>atha</u> lee wam<u>a</u> a<i><u>th</u></i>unnu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata q<u>a</u>-imatan wala-in rujiAAtu il<u>a</u> rabbee inna lee AAindahu lal<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> falanunabbi-anna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo walanu<u>th</u>eeqannahum min AAa<u>tha</u>bin ghalee<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  When We give him a taste<br> Of some mercy from Ourselves.<br>After some adversity has<br> Touched him, he is sure<br> To say, "This is due<br> To my (merit): I think not<br> That the Hour (of Judgment)<br> Will (ever) be established;<br> But if I am brought back<br> To my Lord, I have<br> (Much) good (stored) in His sight!"<br> But We will show<br> The Unbelievers the truth<br> Of all that they did,<br> And We shall give them<br> The taste of a severe<br> Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> anAAamn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>ni aAAra<u>d</u>a wana<u>a</u> bij<u>a</u>nibihi wa-i<u>tha</u> massahu a<b>l</b>shsharru fa<u>th</u>oo duAA<u>a</u>-in AAaree<u>d</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  When We bestow favours<br> On man, he turns away,<br> And gets himself remote<br> On his side (instead of'<br> Coming to Us); and when<br> Evil seizes him, (he comes)<br> Full of prolonged prayer!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Qul araaytum in k<u>a</u>na min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi thumma kafartum bihi man a<u>d</u>allu mimman huwa fee shiq<u>a</u>qin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Say: "See ye if<br> The (Revelation) is (really)<br> From God, and yet do ye<br> Reject it? Who is more<br> Astray than one who<br> Is in a schism<br> Far (from any purpose)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Sanureehim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fee al-<u>a</u>f<u>a</u>qi wafee anfusihim <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yatabayyana lahum annahu al<u>h</u>aqqu awa lam yakfi birabbika annahu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Soon will We show them<br> Our Signs in the (furthest)<br>Regions (of the earth), and<br> In their own souls, until<br> It becomes manifest to them<br> That this is the Truth.<br> Is it not enough that<br> Thy Lord doth witness<br> All things?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Al<u>a</u> innahum fee miryatin min liq<u>a</u>-i rabbihim al<u>a</u> innahu bikulli shay-in mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Ah indeed! are they<br> In doubt concerning<br> The Meeting with their Lord?<br> Ah indeed! it is He<br> That doth encompass<br> All things!</p>  <div class="filenav">
