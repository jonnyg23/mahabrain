 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXV.: Fāṭir, or The Originator of Creation; or Malāïka, or The Angels.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha anzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faakhrajn<u>a</u> bihi thamar<u>a</u>tin mukhtalifan alw<u>a</u>nuh<u>a</u> wamina aljib<u>a</u>li judadun bee<u>d</u>un wa<u>h</u>umrun mukhtalifun alw<u>a</u>nuh<u>a</u> waghar<u>a</u>beebu sood<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Seest thou not that<br> God sends down rain<br> From the sky? With it<br> We then bring out produce<br> Of various colours<br> And in the mountains<br>Are tracts white and red,<br> Of various shades of colour,<br> And black intense in hue.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>al</b>ddaw<u>a</u>bbi wa<b>a</b>l-anAA<u>a</u>mi mukhtalifun alw<u>a</u>nuhu ka<u>tha</u>lika innam<u>a</u> yakhsh<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha min AAib<u>a</u>dihi alAAulam<u>a</u>o inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun ghafoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And so amongst men<br> And crawling creatures and cattle,<br> Are they of various colours.<br> Those truly fear God,<br> Among His Servants,<br> Who have knowledge:<br> For God is Exalted in Might,<br> Oft-Forgiving.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yatloona kit<u>a</u>ba All<u>a</u>hi waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta waanfaqoo mimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum sirran waAAal<u>a</u>niyatan yarjoona tij<u>a</u>ratan lan taboor<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Those who rehearse the Book<br> Of God, establish regular Prayer,<br> And spend (in Charity)<br> Out of what We have provided<br> For them, secretly and openly,<br> Hope for a Commerce<br> That will never fail:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Liyuwaffiyahum ojoorahum wayazeedahum min fa<u>d</u>lihi innahu ghafoorun shakoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  For He will pay them<br> Their meed, nay, He will<br> Give them (even) more<br> Out of His Bounty:<br> For He is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Ready to appreciate (service).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka mina alkit<u>a</u>bi huwa al<u>h</u>aqqu mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi inna All<u>a</u>ha biAAib<u>a</u>dihi lakhabeerun ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  That which We have revealed<br> To thee of the Book<br> Is the Truth,—confirming<br> What was (revealed) before it:<br> For God is assuredly—<br> With respect to His servants—<br> Well acquainted and<br> Fully Observant.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Thumma awrathn<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>st</u>afayn<u>a</u> min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> faminhum <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limun linafsihi waminhum muqta<u>s</u>idun waminhum s<u>a</u>biqun bi<b>a</b>lkhayr<u>a</u>ti bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfa<u>d</u>lu alkabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Then We have given<br> The Book for inheritance<br> To such of Our servants<br> As We have chosen:<br> But there are among them<br> Some who wrong their own<br>Souls; some who follow<br> A middle course; and some<br> Who are, by God's leave,<br> Foremost in good deeds;<br> That is the highest Grace.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Jann<u>a</u>tu AAadnin yadkhuloonah<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>allawna feeh<u>a</u> min as<u>a</u>wira min <u>th</u>ahabin walu/lu-an walib<u>a</u>suhum feeh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>areer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Gardens of Eternity will they<br> Enter: therein will they<br> Be adorned with bracelets<br> Of gold and pearls;<br> And their garments there<br> Will be of silk.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Waq<u>a</u>loo al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee a<u>th</u>haba AAann<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>azana inna rabban<u>a</u> laghafoorun shakoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And they will say:<br> "Praise be to God,<br> Who has removed from us<br> (All) sorrow: for our Lord<br> Is indeed Oft-Forgiving<br> Ready to appreciate (service):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Alla<u>th</u>ee a<u>h</u>allan<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>ra almuq<u>a</u>mati min fa<u>d</u>lihi l<u>a</u> yamassun<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>abun wal<u>a</u> yamassun<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> lughoob<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "Who has, out of His Bounty,<br> Settled us in a Home<br> That will last: no toil<br> Nor sense of weariness<br> Shall touch us therein."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lahum n<u>a</u>ru jahannama l<u>a</u> yuq<u>da</u> AAalayhim fayamootoo wal<u>a</u> yukhaffafu AAanhum min AAa<u>tha</u>bih<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee kulla kafoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  But those who reject (God)—<br> For them will be<br> The Fire of Hell:<br> No term shall be determined<br>For them, so they should die,<br> Nor shall its Penalty<br> Be lightened for them.<br> Thus do We reward<br> Every ungrateful one!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wahum ya<u>st</u>arikhoona feeh<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> akhrijn<u>a</u> naAAmal <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an ghayra alla<u>th</u>ee kunn<u>a</u> naAAmalu awa lam nuAAammirkum m<u>a</u> yata<u>th</u>akkaru feehi man ta<u>th</u>akkara waj<u>a</u>akumu a<b>l</b>nna<u>th</u>eeru fa<u>th</u>ooqoo fam<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena min na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Therein will they cry<br> Aloud (for assistance):<br> "Our Lord! Bring us out:<br> We shall work righteousness,<br> Not the (deeds) we used<br> To do"—"Did We not<br> Give you long enough life<br> So that he that would<br> Should receive admonition?<br> And (moreover) the warner<br> Came to you. So taste ye<br> (The fruits of your deeds):<br> For the Wrong-doers<br> There is no helper."</p> <div class="filenav">
