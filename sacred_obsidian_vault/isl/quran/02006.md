 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XX.: Ṭā-Hā. (Mystic Letters, Ṭ. H.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Wayas-aloonaka AAani aljib<u>a</u>li faqul yansifuh<u>a</u> rabbee nasf<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  They ask thee concerning<br> The Mountains: say, "My Lord<br> Will uproot them and scatter<br> Them as dust;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Faya<u>th</u>aruh<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>AAan <u>s</u>af<u>s</u>af<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  "He will leave them as plains<br> Smooth and level;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  L<u>a</u> tar<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> AAiwajan wal<u>a</u> amt<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  "Nothing crooked or curved<br> Wilt thou see in their place."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in yattabiAAoona a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>AAiya l<u>a</u> AAiwaja lahu wakhashaAAati al-a<u>s</u>w<u>a</u>tu li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni fal<u>a</u> tasmaAAu ill<u>a</u> hams<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  On that Day will they follow<br> The Caller (straight): no crookedness<br>(Can they show) him: all sounds<br> Shall humble themselves in<br> The Presence of (God) Most Gracious:<br> Nothing shalt thou hear<br> But the tramp of their feet<br> (As they march).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in l<u>a</u> tanfaAAu a<b>l</b>shshaf<u>a</u>AAatu ill<u>a</u> man a<u>th</u>ina lahu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu wara<u>d</u>iya lahu qawl<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  On that Day shall no<br> Intercession avail<br> Except for those for whom<br> Permission has been granted<br> By (God) Most Gracious<br> And whose word is<br> Acceptable to Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  YaAAlamu m<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wam<u>a</u> khalfahum wal<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>oona bihi AAilm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  He knows what (appears<br> To His creatures as) before<br> Or after or behind them:<br> But they shall not compass it<br> With their knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  WaAAanati alwujoohu lil<u>h</u>ayyi alqayyoomi waqad kh<u>a</u>ba man <u>h</u>amala <i><u>th</u></i>ulm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  (All) faces shall be humbled<br> Before (Him)—the Living,<br> The Self-Subsisting, Eternal:<br> Hopeless indeed will be<br> The man that carries<br> Iniquity (on his back).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Waman yaAAmal mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti wahuwa mu/minun fal<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>fu <i><u>th</u></i>ulman wal<u>a</u> ha<u>d</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  But he who works deeds<br> Of righteousness, and has faith,<br> Will have no fear of harm<br> Nor of any curtailment<br> (Of what is his due).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika anzaln<u>a</u>hu qur-<u>a</u>nan AAarabiyyan wa<u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> feehi mina alwaAAeedi laAAallahum yattaqoona aw yu<u>h</u>dithu lahum <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  Thus have We sent this<br> Down—an Arabic Qur-ān—<br> And explained therein in detail<br> Some of the warnings,<br> In order that they may<br> Fear God, or that it may<br> Cause their remembrance (of Him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  FataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu almaliku al<u>h</u>aqqu wal<u>a</u> taAAjal bi<b>a</b>lqur-<u>a</u>ni min qabli an yuq<u>da</u> ilayka wa<u>h</u>yuhu waqul rabbi zidnee AAilm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  High above all is God,<br> The King, the Truth!<br> Be not in haste<br> With the Qur-ān before<br> Its revelation to thee<br> Is completed, but say,<br> "O my Lord! advance me<br> In knowledge."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Walaqad AAahidn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> <u>a</u>dama min qablu fanasiya walam najid lahu AAazm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  We had already, beforehand,<br> Taken the covenant of Adam,<br>But he forgot: and We found<br> On his part no firm resolve.</p> <div class="filenav">
