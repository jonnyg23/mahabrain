 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su ittaqoo rabbakum inna zalzalata a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati shay-on AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O mankind! Fear your Lord!<br> For the convulsion of the Hour<br> (Of Judgment) will be<br> A thing terrible!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Yawma tarawnah<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>halu kullu mur<u>d</u>iAAatin AAamm<u>a</u> ar<u>d</u>aAAat wata<u>d</u>aAAu kullu <u>tha</u>ti <u>h</u>amlin <u>h</u>amlah<u>a</u> watar<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa suk<u>a</u>r<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> hum bisuk<u>a</u>r<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinna AAa<u>tha</u>ba All<u>a</u>hi shadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The Day ye shall see it,<br> Every mother giving suck<br> Shall forget her suckling-babe,<br> And every pregnant female<br> Shall drop her load (unformed):<br> Thou shalt see mankind<br> As in a drunken riot,<br> Yet not drunk: but dreadful<br> Will be the Wrath of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yuj<u>a</u>dilu fee All<u>a</u>hi bighayri AAilmin wayattabiAAu kulla shay<u>ta</u>nin mareed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And yet among men<br> There are such as dispute<br> About God, without knowledge,<br> And follow every evil one<br> Obstinate in rebellion!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Kutiba AAalayhi annahu man tawall<u>a</u>hu faannahu yu<u>d</u>illuhu wayahdeehi il<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bi a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  About the (Evil One)<br> It is decreed that whoever<br> Turns to him for friendship,<br> Him will he lead astray,<br> And he will guide him<br> To the Penalty of the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su in kuntum fee raybin mina albaAAthi fa-inn<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>kum min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma min nu<u>t</u>fatin thumma min AAalaqatin thumma min mu<u>d</u>ghatin mukhallaqatin waghayri mukhallaqatin linubayyina lakum wanuqirru fee al-ar<u>ha</u>mi m<u>a</u> nash<u>a</u>o il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman thumma nukhrijukum <u>t</u>iflan thumma litablughoo ashuddakum waminkum man yutawaff<u>a</u> waminkum man yuraddu il<u>a</u> ar<u>th</u>ali alAAumuri likayl<u>a</u> yaAAlama min baAAdi AAilmin shay-an watar<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>a h<u>a</u>midatan fa-i<u>tha</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> alm<u>a</u>a ihtazzat warabat waanbatat min kulli zawjin baheej<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  O mankind! if ye have<br> A doubt about the Resurrection,<br> (Consider) that We created you<br> Out of dust, then out of<br> Sperm, then out of a leech-like<br> Clot, then out of a morsel<br> Of flesh, partly formed<br> And partly unformed, in order<br> That We may manifest<br> (Our power) to you;<br> And We cause whom We will<br> To rest in the wombs<br> For an appointed term,<br> Then do We bring you out<br> As babes, then (foster you)<br> That ye may reach your age<br> Of full strength; and some<br> Of you are called to die,<br> And some are sent back<br> To the feeblest old age,<br> So that they know nothing<br> After having known (much),<br> And (further), thou seest<br> The earth barren and lifeless,<br> But when We pour down<br> Rain on it, it is stirred<br> (To life), it swells,<br> And it puts forth every kind<br> Of beautiful growth (in pairs).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha huwa al<u>h</u>aqqu waannahu yu<u>h</u>yee almawt<u>a</u> waannahu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  This is so, because God<br> Is the Reality: it is He<br> Who gives life to the dead,<br> And it is He Who has<br> Power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waanna a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata <u>a</u>tiyatun l<u>a</u> rayba feeh<u>a</u> waanna All<u>a</u>ha yabAAathu man fee alquboor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And verily the Hour will come:<br> There can be no doubt<br> About it, or about (the fact)<br> That God will raise up<br> All who are in the graves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yuj<u>a</u>dilu fee All<u>a</u>hi bighayri AAilmin wal<u>a</u> hudan wal<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bin muneer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Yet there is among men<br> Such a one as disputes<br> About God, without knowledge,<br> Without guidance, and without<br> A Book of Enlightenment,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Th<u>a</u>niya AAi<u>t</u>fihi liyu<u>d</u>illa AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi lahu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> khizyun wanu<u>th</u>eequhu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati AAa<u>tha</u>ba al<u>h</u>areeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  (Disdainfully) bending his side,<br> In order to lead (men) astray<br> From the Path of God:<br> For him there is disgrace<br> In this life, and on the Day<br> Of Judgment We shall<br> Make him taste the Penalty<br> Of burning (Fire).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  <u>Tha</u>lika bim<u>a</u> qaddamat yad<u>a</u>ka waanna All<u>a</u>ha laysa bi<i><u>th</u></i>all<u>a</u>min lilAAabeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  (It will be said): "This is<br> Because of the deeds which<br> Thy hands sent forth,<br> For verily God is not<br> Unjust to His servants."</p> <div class="filenav">
