 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLII.: Shūrā, or Consultation.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min waliyyin min baAAdihi watar<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena lamm<u>a</u> raawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba yaqooloona hal il<u>a</u> maraddin min sabeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  For any whom God<br> Leaves astray, there is<br> No protector thereafter.<br> And thou wilt see<br> The wrong-doers, when<br> In sight of the Penalty,<br> Say: "Is there any way<br> (To effect) a return?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Watar<u>a</u>hum yuAAra<u>d</u>oona AAalayh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>shiAAeena mina a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ulli yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona min <u>t</u>arfin khafiyyin waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo inna alkh<u>a</u>sireena alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum waahleehim yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati al<u>a</u> inna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena fee AAa<u>tha</u>bin muqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  And thou wilt see them<br> Brought forward to the (Penalty),<br> In a humble frame of mind<br> Because of (their) disgrace,<br> (And) looking with a stealthy<br> Glance. And the Believers<br> Will say: "Those are indeed.<br> In loss. who have given<br> To perdition their own selves<br> And those belonging to them<br> On the Day of Judgment.<br> Behold! Truly the wrong-doers<br> Are in a lasting Penalty!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahum min awliy<u>a</u>a yan<u>s</u>uroonahum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min sabeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And no protectors have they<br> To help them,<br> Other than God.<br> And for any whom God<br> Leaves to stray, there is<br> No way (to the Goal).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Istajeeboo lirabbikum min qabli an ya/tiya yawmun l<u>a</u> maradda lahu mina All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> lakum min malja-in yawma-i<u>th</u>in wam<u>a</u> lakum min nakeer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Hearken ye to your Lord,<br> Before there come a Day<br> Which there will be<br> No putting back, because<br> Of (the ordainment of) God!<br> That Day there will be<br> For you no place of refuge<br> Nor will there be for you<br> Any room for denial<br> (Of your sins)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Fa-in aAAra<u>d</u>oo fam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka AAalayhim <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i>an in AAalayka ill<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu wa-inn<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> a<u>th</u>aqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na minn<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan fari<u>h</u>a bih<u>a</u> wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibhum sayyi-atun bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeehim fa-inna al-ins<u>a</u>na kafoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  If then they turn away,<br> We have not sent thee<br> As a guard over them.<br> Thy duty is but to convey<br> (The Message). And truly,<br> When We give man<br> A taste of a Mercy<br> From ourselves, he doth<br> Exult thereat, but<br> When some ill happens<br> To him, on account<br> Of the deeds which<br> His hands have sent forth,<br> Truly then is man ungrateful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Lill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yakhluqu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o yahabu liman yash<u>a</u>o in<u>a</u>than wayahabu liman yash<u>a</u>o a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ukoor<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  To God belongs the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth.<br> He creates what He wills<br> (And plans). He bestows<br>(Children) male or female<br> According to His Will (and Plan),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Aw yuzawwijuhum <u>th</u>ukr<u>a</u>nan wa-in<u>a</u>than wayajAAalu man yash<u>a</u>o AAaqeeman innahu AAaleemun qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Or He bestows both males<br> And females, and He leaves<br> Barren whom He will:<br> For He is full<br> Of knowledge and power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na libasharin an yukallimahu All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>yan aw min war<u>a</u>-i <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>bin aw yursila rasoolan fayoo<u>h</u>iya bi-i<u>th</u>nihi m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o innahu AAaliyyun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  It is not fitting<br> For a man that God<br> Should speak to him<br> Except by inspiration,<br> Or from behind a veil,<br> Or by the sending<br> Of a Messenger<br> To reveal, with God's permission,<br> What God wills: for He<br> Is Most High, Most Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka roo<u>h</u>an min amrin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kunta tadree m<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>bu wal<u>a</u> al-eem<u>a</u>nu wal<u>a</u>kin jaAAaln<u>a</u>hu nooran nahdee bihi man nash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> wa-innaka latahdee il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  And thus have We,<br> By Our command, sent<br>Inspiration to thee:<br> Thou knewest not (before)<br> What was Revelation, and<br> What was Faith; but We<br> Have made the (Qur-ān)<br> A Light, wherewith We<br> Guide such of Our servants<br> As We will; and verily<br> Thou dost guide (men)<br> To the Straight Way,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  <u>S</u>ir<u>at</u>i All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i al<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ta<u>s</u>eeru al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  The Way of God,<br> To Whom belongs<br> Whatever is in the heavens<br> And whatever is on earth.<br> Behold (how) all affairs<br> Tend towards God!</p>  <div class="filenav">
