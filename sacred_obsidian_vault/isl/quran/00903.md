 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lilmushrikeena an yaAAmuroo mas<u>a</u>jida All<u>a</u>hi sh<u>a</u>hideena AAal<u>a</u> anfusihim bi<b>a</b>lkufri ol<u>a</u>-ika <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum wafee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  It is not for such<br> As join gods with God,<br> To visit or maintain<br> The mosques of God<br> While they witness<br> Against their own souls<br> To infidelity. The works<br> Of such bear no fruit:<br> In Fire shall they dwell.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Innam<u>a</u> yaAAmuru mas<u>a</u>jida All<u>a</u>hi man <u>a</u>mana bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waaq<u>a</u>ma a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta walam yakhsha ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha faAAas<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika an yakoonoo mina almuhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  The mosques of God<br> Shall be visited and maintained<br> By such as believe in God<br> And the Last Day, establish<br> Regular prayers, and practise<br> Regular charity, and fear<br> None (at all) except God.<br> It is they who are expected<br> To be on true guidance!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  AjaAAaltum siq<u>a</u>yata al<u>ha</u>jji waAAim<u>a</u>rata almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi kaman <u>a</u>mana bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waj<u>a</u>hada fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yastawoona AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Do ye make the giving<br> Of drink to pilgrims,<br>Or the maintenance of<br> The Sacred Mosque, equal<br> To (the pious service of) those<br> Who believe in God<br> And the Last Day, and strive<br> With might and main<br> In the cause of God?<br> They are not comparable<br> In the sight of God:<br> And God guides not<br> Those who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wah<u>a</u>jaroo waj<u>a</u>hadoo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim aAA<i><u>th</u></i>amu darajatan AAinda All<u>a</u>hi waol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>-izoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Those who believe, and suffer<br> Exile and strive with might<br> And main, in God's cause,<br> With their goods and their persons,<br> Have the highest rank<br> In the sight of God:<br> They are the people<br> Who will achieve (salvation).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Yubashshiruhum rabbuhum bira<u>h</u>matin minhu wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nin wajann<u>a</u>tin lahum feeh<u>a</u> naAAeemun muqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Their Lord doth give them<br> Glad tidings of a Mercy<br> From Himself, of His good pleasure,<br> And of Gardens for them,<br> Wherein are delights<br> That endure:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan inna All<u>a</u>ha AAindahu ajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  They will dwell therein<br> For ever. Verily in God's presence<br> Is a reward, the greatest (of all).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>akum wa-ikhw<u>a</u>nakum awliy<u>a</u>a ini ista<u>h</u>abboo alkufra AAal<u>a</u> al-eem<u>a</u>ni waman yatawallahum minkum faol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  O ye who believe! Take not<br> For protectors your fathers<br> And your brothers if they love<br> Infidelity above Faith:<br>If any of you do so,<br> They do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Qul in k<u>a</u>na <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum waabn<u>a</u>okum wa-ikhw<u>a</u>nukum waazw<u>a</u>jukum waAAasheeratukum waamw<u>a</u>lun iqtaraftumooh<u>a</u> watij<u>a</u>ratun takhshawna kas<u>a</u>dah<u>a</u> wamas<u>a</u>kinu tar<u>d</u>awnah<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>abba ilaykum mina All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wajih<u>a</u>din fee sabeelihi fatarabba<u>s</u>oo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/tiya All<u>a</u>hu bi-amrihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Say; If it be that your fathers,<br> Your sons, your brothers,<br> Your mates, or your kindred;<br> The wealth that ye have gained;<br> The commerce in which ye fear<br> A decline: or the dwellings<br> In which ye delight—<br> Are dearer to you than God,<br> Or His Apostle, or the striving<br> In His cause;—then wait<br> Until God brings about<br> His Decision: and God<br> Guides not the rebellious.</p> <div class="filenav">
