 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa-il<u>a</u> thamooda akh<u>a</u>hum <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu qad j<u>a</u>atkum bayyinatun min rabbikum h<u>ath</u>ihi n<u>a</u>qatu All<u>a</u>hi lakum <u>a</u>yatan fa<u>th</u>arooh<u>a</u> ta/kul fee ar<u>d</u>i All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> tamassooh<u>a</u> bisoo-in faya/khu<u>th</u>akum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  To the Thamūd people<br> (We sent) Ṣaliḥ, one<br>Of their own brethren:<br> He said: "O my people!<br> Worship God; ye have<br> No other god but Him.<br> Now hath come unto you<br> A clear (Sign) from your Lord!<br> This she-camel of God<br> Is a Sign unto you:<br> So leave her to graze<br> In God's earth, and let her<br> Come to no harm,<br> Or ye shall be seized<br> With a grievous punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo i<u>th</u> jaAAalakum khulaf<u>a</u>a min baAAdi AA<u>a</u>din wabawwaakum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i tattakhi<u>th</u>oona min suhoolih<u>a</u> qu<u>s</u>ooran watan<u>h</u>itoona aljib<u>a</u>la buyootan fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> taAAthaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  "And remember how He<br> Made you inheritors<br> After the ’Ad people<br> And gave you habitations<br> In the land: ye build<br> For yourselves palaces and castles<br> In (open) plains, and carve out<br> Homes in the mountains;<br> So bring to remembrance<br> The benefits (ye have received)<br> From God, and refrain<br> From evil and mischief<br> On the earth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Q<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo min qawmihi lilla<u>th</u>eena istu<u>d</u>AAifoo liman <u>a</u>mana minhum ataAAlamoona anna <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an mursalun min rabbihi q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> orsila bihi mu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  The leaders of the arrogant<br> Party among his people said<br>To those who were reckoned<br> Powerless—those among them<br> Who believed: "Know ye<br> Indeed that Ṣaliḥ is<br> An apostle from his Lord?"<br> They said: "We do indeed<br> Believe in the revelation<br> Which hath been sent<br> Through him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo inn<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee <u>a</u>mantum bihi k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  The arrogant party said:<br> "For our part, we reject<br> What ye believe in."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  FaAAaqaroo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>qata waAAataw AAan amri rabbihim waq<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>u i/tin<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> taAAidun<u>a</u> in kunta mina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Then they ham-strung<br> The she-camel, and insolently<br> Defied the order of their Lord,<br> Saying: "O Ṣaliḥ! bring about<br> Thy threats, if thou art<br> An apostle (of God)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b>rrajfatu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo fee d<u>a</u>rihim j<u>a</u>thimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  So the earthquake took them<br> Unawares, and they lay<br> Prostrate in their homes<br> In the morning!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Fatawall<u>a</u> AAanhum waq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi laqad ablaghtukum ris<u>a</u>lata rabbee wana<u>s</u>a<u>h</u>tu lakum wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>ibboona a<b>l</b>nn<u>as</u>i<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  So Ṣaliḥ left them,<br> Saying: "O my people!<br> I did indeed convey to you<br> The message for which<br> I was sent by my Lord:<br> I gave you good counsel,<br> But ye love not good counsellors!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Waloo<u>t</u>an i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi ata/toona alf<u>ah</u>ishata m<u>a</u> sabaqakum bih<u>a</u> min a<u>h</u>adin mina alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>80.  We also (sent) Lūṭ:<br> He said to his people:<br> "Do ye commit lewdness<br> Such as no people<br> In creation (ever) committed<br> Before you?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Innakum lata/toona a<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>la shahwatan min dooni a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i bal antum qawmun musrifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "For ye practise your lusts<br> On men in preference<br> To women: ye are indeed<br> A people transgressing<br> Beyond bounds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na jaw<u>a</u>ba qawmihi ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo akhrijoohum min qaryatikum innahum on<u>a</u>sun yata<u>t</u>ahharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  And his people gave<br> No answer but this:<br> They said, "Drive them out<br> Of your city: these are<br>Indeed men who want<br> To be clean and pure!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Faanjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu ill<u>a</u> imraatahu k<u>a</u>nat mina algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>83.  But we saved him<br> And his family, except<br> His wife: she was<br> Of those who lagged behind</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Waam<u>t</u>arn<u>a</u> AAalayhim ma<u>t</u>aran fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  And we rained down on them<br> A shower (of brimstone):<br> Then see what was the end<br> Of those who indulged<br> In sin and crime!</p> <div class="filenav">
