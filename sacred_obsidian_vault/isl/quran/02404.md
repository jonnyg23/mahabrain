 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIV.: Nūr, or Light.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tadkhuloo buyootan ghayra buyootikum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tasta/nisoo watusallimoo AAal<u>a</u> ahlih<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum laAAallakum ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  O ye who believe!<br> Enter not houses other than<br> Your own, until ye have<br> Asked permission and saluted<br> Those in them: that is<br> Best for you, in order that<br> Ye may heed (what is seemly).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Fa-in lam tajidoo feeh<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>adan fal<u>a</u> tadkhulooh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yu/<u>th</u>ana lakum wa-in qeela lakumu irjiAAoo fa<b>i</b>rjiAAoo huwa azk<u>a</u> lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  If ye find no one<br> In the house, enter not<br> Until permission is given<br> To you: if ye are asked<br> To go back, go back:<br> That makes for greater purity<br>For yourselves: and God<br> Knows well all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Laysa AAalaykum jun<u>ah</u>un an tadkhuloo buyootan ghayra maskoonatin feeh<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAun lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tubdoona wam<u>a</u> taktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  It is no fault on your part<br> To enter houses not used<br> For living in, which serve<br> Some (other) use for you:<br> And God has knowledge<br> Of what ye reveal<br> And what ye conceal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Qul lilmu/mineena yaghu<u>dd</u>oo min ab<u>sa</u>rihum waya<u>h</u>fa<i><u>th</u></i>oo furoojahum <u>tha</u>lika azk<u>a</u> lahum inna All<u>a</u>ha khabeerun bim<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>naAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Say to the believing men<br> That they should lower<br> Their gaze and guard<br> Their modesty: that will make<br> For greater purity for them:<br> And God is well acquainted<br> With all that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waqul lilmu/min<u>a</u>ti yagh<u>d</u>u<u>d</u>na min ab<u>sa</u>rihinna waya<u>h</u>fa<i><u>th</u></i>na furoojahunna wal<u>a</u> yubdeena zeenatahunna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ahara minh<u>a</u> walya<u>d</u>ribna bikhumurihinna AAal<u>a</u> juyoobihinna wal<u>a</u> yubdeena zeenatahunna ill<u>a</u> libuAAoolatihinna aw <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihinna aw <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-i buAAoolatihinna aw abn<u>a</u>-ihinna aw abn<u>a</u>-i buAAoolatihinna aw ikhw<u>a</u>nihinna aw banee ikhw<u>a</u>nihinna aw banee akhaw<u>a</u>tihinna aw nis<u>a</u>-ihinna aw m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nuhunna awi a<b>l</b>tt<u>a</u>biAAeena ghayri olee al-irbati mina a<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>li awi a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ifli alla<u>th</u>eena lam ya<i><u>th</u></i>haroo AAal<u>a</u> AAawr<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i wal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>ribna bi-arjulihinna liyuAAlama m<u>a</u> yukhfeena min zeenatihinna watooboo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi jameeAAan ayyuh<u>a</u> almu/minoona laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  And say to the believing women<br> That they should lower<br> Their gaze and guard<br> Their modesty; that they<br> Should not display their<br> Beauty and ornaments except<br> What (must ordinarily) appear<br> Thereof; that they should<br>Draw their veils over<br> Their bosoms and not display<br> Their beauty except<br> To their husbands, their fathers,<br> Their husbands’ fathers, their sons,<br> Their husbands’ sons,<br> Their brothers or their brothers’ sons,<br> Or their sisters’ sons,<br> Or their women, or the slaves<br> Whom their right hands<br> Possess, or male servants<br> Free of physical needs,<br> Or small children who<br> Have no sense of the shame<br> Of sex; and that they<br> Should not strike their feet<br> In order to draw attention<br> To their hidden ornaments.<br> And O ye Believers!<br> Turn ye all together<br> Towards God, that ye<br> May attain Bliss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Waanki<u>h</u>oo al-ay<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> minkum wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>eena min AAib<u>a</u>dikum wa-im<u>a</u>-ikum in yakoonoo fuqar<u>a</u>a yughnihimu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu w<u>a</u>siAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Marry those among you<br> Who are single, or<br> The virtuous ones among<br> Your slaves, male or female:<br> If they are in poverty,<br> God will give them<br> Means out of His grace:<br> For God encompasseth all,<br> And He knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  WalyastaAAfifi alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yajidoona nik<u>ah</u>an <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yughniyahumu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yabtaghoona alkit<u>a</u>ba mimm<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum fak<u>a</u>tiboohum in AAalimtum feehim khayran wa<u>a</u>toohum min m<u>a</u>li All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum wal<u>a</u> tukrihoo fatay<u>a</u>tikum AAal<u>a</u> albigh<u>a</u>-i in aradna ta<u>h</u>a<u>ss</u>unan litabtaghoo AAara<u>d</u>a al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> waman yukrihhunna fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha min baAAdi ikr<u>a</u>hihinna ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Let those who find not<br> The wherewithal for marriage<br> Keep themselves chaste, until<br> God gives them means<br> Out of His grace.<br> And if any of your slaves<br> Ask for a deed in writing<br> (To enable them to earn<br> Their freedom for a certain sum),<br> Give them such a deed<br> If ye know any good<br> In them; yea, give them<br> Something yourselves<br> Out of the means which<br> God has given to you.<br> But force not your maids<br> To prostitution when they desire<br> Chastity, in order that ye<br> May make a gain<br> In the goods of this life.<br> But if anyone compels them,<br> Yet, after such compulsion,<br> Is God Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful (to them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Walaqad anzaln<u>a</u> ilaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin mubayyin<u>a</u>tin wamathalan mina alla<u>th</u>eena khalaw min qablikum wamawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atan lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  We have already sent down<br> To you verses making things<br> Clear, an illustration from (the story<br> Of) people who passed away<br> Before you, and an admonition<br> For those who fear (God).</p> <div class="filenav">
