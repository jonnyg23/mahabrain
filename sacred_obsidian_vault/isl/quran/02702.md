 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>wooda wasulaym<u>a</u>na AAilman waq<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>dd</u>alan<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> katheerin min AAib<u>a</u>dihi almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  We gave (in the past)<br> Knowledge to David and Solomon<br> And they both said:<br> "Praise be to God, Who<br> Has favoured us above many<br> Of His servants who believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wawaritha sulaym<u>a</u>nu d<u>a</u>wooda waq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su AAullimn<u>a</u> man<u>t</u>iqa a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayri waooteen<u>a</u> min kulli shay-in inna h<u>atha</u> lahuwa alfa<u>d</u>lu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And Solomon was David's heir.<br> He said: "O ye people!<br> We have been taught the speech"<br> Of Birds, and on us<br> Has been bestowed (a little)<br> Of all things: this is<br> Indeed Grace manifest (from God.)"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<u>h</u>ushira lisulaym<u>a</u>na junooduhu mina aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayri fahum yoozaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And before Solomon were marshalled<br>His hosts,—of Jinns and men<br> And birds, and they were all<br> Kept in order and ranks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> ataw AAal<u>a</u> w<u>a</u>di a<b>l</b>nnamli q<u>a</u>lat namlatun y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnamlu odkhuloo mas<u>a</u>kinakum l<u>a</u> ya<u>ht</u>imannakum sulaym<u>a</u>nu wajunooduhu wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  At length, when they came<br> To a (lowly) valley of ants,<br> One of the ants said:<br> "O ye ants, get into<br> Your habitations, lest Solomon<br> And his hosts crush you<br> (Under foot) without knowing it."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Fatabassama <u>dah</u>ikan min qawlih<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>la rabbi awziAAnee an ashkura niAAmataka allatee anAAamta AAalayya waAAal<u>a</u> w<u>a</u>lidayya waan aAAmala <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an tar<u>da</u>hu waadkhilnee bira<u>h</u>matika fee AAib<u>a</u>dika a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  So he smiled, amused<br> At her speech; and he said:<br> "O my Lord! so order me<br> That I may be grateful<br> For Thy favours, which Thou<br> Hast bestowed on me and<br> On my parents, and that<br> I may work the righteousness<br> That will please Thee:<br> And admit me, by Thy Grace,<br> To the ranks of Thy<br> Righteous Servants."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Watafaqqada a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayra faq<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> liya l<u>a</u> ar<u>a</u> alhudhuda am k<u>a</u>na mina algh<u>a</u>-ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And he took a muster<br> Of the Birds; and he said:<br> "Why is it I see not<br> The Hoopoe? Or is he<br> Among the absentees?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  LaoAAa<u>thth</u>ibannahu AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan aw laa<u>th</u>ba<u>h</u>annahu aw laya/tiyannee bisul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  "I will certainly punish him<br> With a severe penalty,<br> Or execute him, unless he<br> Bring me a clear reason<br> (For absence)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Famakatha ghayra baAAeedin faq<u>a</u>la a<u>hat</u>tu bim<u>a</u> lam tu<u>h</u>i<u>t</u> bihi waji/tuka min saba-in binaba-in yaqeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  But the Hoopoe tarried not<br> Far: he (came up and) said:<br> "I have compassed (territory)<br> Which thou hast not compassed,<br> And I have come to thee<br> From Saba with tidings true.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Innee wajadtu imraatan tamlikuhum waootiyat min kulli shay-in walah<u>a</u> AAarshun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "I found (there) a woman<br> Ruling over them and provided<br> With every requisite; and she<br> Has a magnificent throne.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wajadtuh<u>a</u> waqawmah<u>a</u> yasjudoona li<b>l</b>shshamsi min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wazayyana lahumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu aAAm<u>a</u>lahum fa<u>s</u>addahum AAani a<b>l</b>ssabeeli fahum l<u>a</u> yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "I found her and her people<br> Worshipping the sun besides God:<br> Satan has made their deeds<br> Seem pleasing in their eyes,<br> And has kept them away<br> From the Path,—so<br> They receive no guidance,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  All<u>a</u> yasjudoo lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee yukhriju alkhabaa fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tukhfoona wam<u>a</u> tuAAlinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "(Kept them away from the Path),<br> That they should not worship<br> God, Who brings to light<br> What is hidden in the heavens<br> And the earth, and knows<br> What ye hide and what<br> Ye reveal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa rabbu alAAarshi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  "God!—there is no god<br> But He!—Lord of the Throne's<br> Supreme!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Q<u>a</u>la sanan<i><u>th</u></i>uru a<u>s</u>adaqta am kunta mina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  (Solomon) said: "Soon shall we<br> See whether thou hast told<br> The truth or lied!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  I<u>th</u>hab bikit<u>a</u>bee h<u>atha</u> faalqih ilayhim thumma tawalla AAanhum fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur m<u>atha</u> yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "Go thou, with this letter<br> Of mine, and deliver it<br> To them: then draw back<br> From them, and (wait to) see<br> What answer they return"...</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Q<u>a</u>lat y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> almalao innee olqiya ilayya kit<u>a</u>bun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  (The Queen) said: "Ye chiefs!<br> Here is—delivered to me—<br> A letter worthy of respect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Innahu min sulaym<u>a</u>na wa-innahu bismi All<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  "It is from Solomon, and is<br> (As follows), 'In the name<br> Of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  All<u>a</u> taAAloo AAalayya wa/toonee muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "'Be ye not arrogant<br> Against me, but come<br> To me in submission<br> (To the true Religion).'"</p> <div class="filenav">
