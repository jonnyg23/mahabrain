 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXVII.: Mursalāt, or Those Sent Forth.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>a</b>lmursal<u>a</u>ti AAurf<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the (Winds) Sent Forth<br> One after another<br> (To man's profit);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fa<b>a</b>lAA<u>as</u>if<u>a</u>ti AAa<u>s</u>f<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Which then blow violently<br> In tempestuous Gusts,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>shir<u>a</u>ti nashr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And scatter (things)<br> Far and wide;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Fa<b>a</b>lf<u>a</u>riq<u>a</u>ti farq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Then separate them,<br> One from another,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fa<b>a</b>lmulqiy<u>a</u>ti <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Then spread abroad<br> A Message,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  AAu<u>th</u>ran aw nu<u>th</u>ra<b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>6.  Whether of Justification<br> Or of Warning;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Innam<u>a</u> tooAAadoona law<u>a</u>qiAA<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Assuredly, what ye are<br> Promised must come to pass.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>nnujoomu <u>t</u>umisat   </p>
 <p>8.  Then when the stars<br> Become dim;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o furijat   </p>
 <p>9.  When the heaven<br> Is cleft asunder;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> aljib<u>a</u>lu nusifat   </p>
 <p>10.  When the mountains are<br> Scattered (to the winds) as dust;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>rrusulu oqqitat   </p>
 <p>11.  And when the apostles<br> Are (all) appointed a time<br> (To collect);—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Li-ayyi yawmin ojjilat   </p>
 <p>12.  For what Day are these<br> (Portents) deferred?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Liyawmi alfa<u>s</u>l<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  For the Day of Sorting out.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> yawmu alfa<u>s</u>l<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And what will explain<br> To thee what is<br> The Day of Sorting out?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Alam nuhliki al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Did We not destroy<br> The men of old<br> (For their evil)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Thumma nutbiAAuhumu al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  So shall We make<br> Later (generations)<br> Follow them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika nafAAalu bi<b>a</b>lmujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Thus do We deal<br> With men of sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Alam nakhluqkum min m<u>a</u>-in maheen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Have We not created<br> You from a fluid<br> (Held) despicable?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  FajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu fee qar<u>a</u>rin makeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  The which We placed<br> In a place of rest,<br> Firmly fixed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Il<u>a</u> qadarin maAAloom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  For a period (of gestation),<br> Determined (according to need)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Faqadarn<u>a</u> faniAAma alq<u>a</u>diroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  For We do determine<br> (According to need); for We<br> Are the Best to determine (things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Ah woe, that Day!<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Alam najAAali al-ar<u>d</u>a kif<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Have We not made<br> The earth (as a place)<br> To draw together</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  A<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>an waamw<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  The living and the dead,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> raw<u>a</u>siya sh<u>a</u>mikh<u>a</u>tin waasqayn<u>a</u>kum m<u>a</u>an fur<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And made therein<br> Mountains standing firm,<br> Lofty (in stature);<br> And provided for you<br> Water sweet (and wholesome)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  In<u>t</u>aliqoo il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum bihi tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  (It will be said:)<br> "Depart ye to that<br> Which ye used to reject<br> As false!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  In<u>t</u>aliqoo il<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>illin <u>th</u>ee thal<u>a</u>thi shuAAab<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  "Depart ye to a Shadow<br> (Of smoke ascending)<br> In three columns,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  L<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>aleelin wal<u>a</u> yughnee mina allahab<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "(Which yields) no shade<br> Of coolness, and is<br> Of no use against<br> The fierce Blaze.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Innah<u>a</u> tarmee bishararin ka<b>a</b>lqa<u>s</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "Indeed it throws about<br> Sparks (huge) as Forts,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Kaannahu jim<u>a</u>latun <u>s</u>ufr<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "As if there were<br> (A string of) yellow camels<br> (Marching swiftly).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  H<u>atha</u> yawmu l<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  That will be a Day<br> When they shall not<br>Be able to speak,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wal<u>a</u> yu/<u>th</u>anu lahum fayaAAta<u>th</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Nor will it be<br> Open to them<br> To put forth pleas.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  H<u>atha</u> yawmu alfa<u>s</u>li jamaAAn<u>a</u>kum wa<b>a</b>l-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  That will be a Day<br> Of Sorting out! We shall<br> Gather you together<br> And those before (you)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fa-in k<u>a</u>na lakum kaydun fakeedoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Now, if ye have<br> A trick (or plot),<br> Use it against Me!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <div class="filenav">
