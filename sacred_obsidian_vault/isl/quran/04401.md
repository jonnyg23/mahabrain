 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIV.: Du<u>kh</u>ān, or Smoke (or Mist.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ha</u>-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Hā-Mīm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi almubeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  By the Book that<br> Makes things clear;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Inn<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u>hu fee laylatin mub<u>a</u>rakatin inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> mun<u>th</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  We sent it down<br> During a blessed night:<br> For We (ever) wish<br> To warn (against Evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Feeh<u>a</u> yufraqu kullu amrin <u>h</u>akeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  In that (night) is made<br> Distinct every affair<br> Of wisdom,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Amran min AAindin<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> mursileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  By command, from Our<br> Presence. For We (ever)<br> Send (revelations),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Ra<u>h</u>matan min rabbika innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  As a Mercy<br> From thy Lord:<br> For He hears and knows<br> (All things);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Rabbi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> in kuntum mooqineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  The Lord of the heavens<br> And the earth and all<br>Between them, if ye (but)<br> Have an assured faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  L<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu rabbukum warabbu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ikumu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  There is no god but He:<br> It is He Who gives life<br> And gives death,<br> The Lord and Cherisher<br> To you and your earliest<br> Ancestors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Bal hum fee shakkin yalAAaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Yet they play about<br> In doubt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Fa<b>i</b>rtaqib yawma ta/tee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o bidukh<u>a</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Then watch thou<br> For the Day<br> That the sky will<br> Bring forth a kind<br> Of smoke (or mist)<br> Plainly visible,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Yaghsh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa h<u>atha</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Enveloping the people:<br> This will be a Penalty<br> Grievous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Rabban<u>a</u> ikshif AAann<u>a</u> alAAa<u>tha</u>ba inn<u>a</u> mu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  (They will say:)<br> "Our Lord! Remove<br> The Penalty from us,<br> For we do really believe!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Ann<u>a</u> lahumu a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u> waqad j<u>a</u>ahum rasoolun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  How shall the Message<br> Be (effectual) for them,<br> Seeing that an Apostle<br> Explaining things clearly<br> Has (already) come to them,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Thumma tawallaw AAanhu waq<u>a</u>loo muAAallamun majnoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Yet they turn away<br> From him and say: "Tutored<br> (By others), a man possessed!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Inn<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>shifoo alAAa<u>tha</u>bi qaleelan innakum AA<u>a</u>-idoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  We shall indeed remove<br> The Penalty for a while,<br> (But) truly ye will revert<br> (To your ways).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Yawma nab<u>t</u>ishu alba<u>t</u>shata alkubr<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> muntaqimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  One day We shall seize<br> You with a mighty onslaught:<br> We will indeed (then)<br> Exact Retribution!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Walaqad fatann<u>a</u> qablahum qawma firAAawna waj<u>a</u>ahum rasoolun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  We did, before them,<br> Try the people of Pharaoh:<br>There came to them<br> An apostle most honourable,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  An addoo ilayya AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi innee lakum rasoolun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Saying: "Restore to me<br> The servants of God:<br> I am to you an apostle<br> Worthy of all trust;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waan l<u>a</u> taAAloo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innee <u>a</u>teekum bisul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "And be not arrogant<br> As against God:<br> For I come to you<br> With authority manifest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wa-innee AAu<u>th</u>tu birabbee warabbikum an tarjumoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  "For me, I have sought<br> Safety with my Lord<br> And your Lord, against<br> Your injuring me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa-in lam tu/minoo lee fa<b>i</b>AAtaziloon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  "If ye believe me not,<br> At least keep yourselves<br> Away from me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  FadaAA<u>a</u> rabbahu anna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i qawmun mujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  (But they were aggressive:)<br> Then he cried<br>To his Lord:<br> "These are indeed<br> A people given to sin."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Faasri biAAib<u>a</u>dee laylan innakum muttabaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  (The reply came:)<br> "March forth with my servants<br> By night: for ye are<br> Sure to be pursued.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wa<b>o</b>truki alba<u>h</u>ra rahwan innahum jundun mughraqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "And leave the sea<br> As a furrow (divided):<br> For they are a host<br> (Destined) to be drowned."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Kam tarakoo min jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  How many were the gardens<br> And springs they left behind,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  WazurooAAin wamaq<u>a</u>min kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And corn-fields<br> And noble buildings,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  WanaAAmatin k<u>a</u>noo feeh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kiheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And wealth (and conveniences<br> Of life), wherein they<br> Had taken such delight!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika waawrathn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> qawman <u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Thus (was their end)!<br> And We made other people<br> Inherit (those things)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Fam<u>a</u> bakat AAalayhimu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo mun<i><u>th</u></i>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And neither heaven<br> Nor earth shed a tear<br> Over them: nor were<br> They given a respite (again).</p> <div class="filenav">
