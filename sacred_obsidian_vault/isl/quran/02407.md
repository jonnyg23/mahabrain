 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIV.: Nūr, or Light.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Innam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na qawla almu/mineena i<u>tha</u> duAAoo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi liya<u>h</u>kuma baynahum an yaqooloo samiAAn<u>a</u> waa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  The answer of the Believers,<br> When summoned to God<br> And His Apostle, in order<br> That he may judge between them,<br> Is no other than this:<br> They say, "We hear and we obey":<br> It is such as these<br> That will attain felicity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Waman yu<u>t</u>iAAi All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wayakhsha All<u>a</u>ha wayattaqhi faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>-izoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  It is such as obey<br> God and His Apostle,<br> And fear God and do<br> Right, that will win<br> (In the end).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Waaqsamoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi jahda aym<u>a</u>nihim la-in amartahum layakhrujunna qul l<u>a</u> tuqsimoo <u>ta</u>AAatun maAAroofatun inna All<u>a</u>ha khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  They swear their strongest oaths<br> By God that, if only thou<br>Wouldst command them, they<br> Would leave (their homes).<br> Say: "Swear ye not;<br> Obedience is (more) reasonable;<br> Verily, God is well acquainted<br> With all that ye do."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Qul a<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola fa-in tawallaw fa-innam<u>a</u> AAalayhi m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ummila waAAalaykum m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ummiltum wa-in tu<u>t</u>eeAAoohu tahtadoo wam<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli ill<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Say: "Obey God, and obey<br> The Apostle: but if ye turn<br> Away, he is only responsible<br> For the duty placed on him<br> And ye for that placed<br> On you. If ye obey him,<br> Ye shall be on right guidance.<br> The Apostle's duty is only<br> To preach the clear (Message).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  WaAAada All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo minkum waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti layastakhlifannahum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i kam<u>a</u> istakhlafa alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim walayumakkinanna lahum deenahumu alla<u>th</u>ee irta<u>da</u> lahum walayubaddilannahum min baAAdi khawfihim amnan yaAAbudoonanee l<u>a</u> yushrikoona bee shay-an waman kafara baAAda <u>tha</u>lika faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  God has promised, to those<br> Among you who believe<br> And work righteous deeds, that He<br> Will, of a surety, grant them<br> In the land, inheritance<br> (Of power), as He granted it<br> To those before them; that<br> He will establish in authority<br> Their religion—the one<br> Which He has chosen for them;<br> And that He will change<br> (Their state), after the fear<br> In which they (lived), to one<br>Of security and peace:<br> 'They will worship Me (alone)<br> And not associate aught with Me.'<br> If any do reject Faith<br> After this, they are<br> Rebellious and wicked.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Waaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  So establish regular Prayer<br> And give regular Charity;<br> And obey the Apostle;<br> That ye may receive mercy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  L<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>sabanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo muAAjizeena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wama/w<u>a</u>humu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru walabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Never think thou<br> That the Unbelievers<br> Are going to frustrate<br> (God's Plan) on earth:<br> Their abode is the Fire,—<br> And it is indeed<br> An evil refuge!</p> <div class="filenav">
