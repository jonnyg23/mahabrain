 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Kuntum khayra ommatin okhrijat li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si ta/muroona bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi watanhawna AAani almunkari watu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi walaw <u>a</u>mana ahlu alkit<u>a</u>bi lak<u>a</u>na khayran lahum minhumu almu/minoona waaktharuhumu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  Ye are the best<br> Of Peoples, evolved<br> For mankind,<br> Enjoining what is right,<br> Forbidding what is wrong,<br> And believing in God.<br> If only the People of the Book<br> Had faith, it were best<br> For them: among them<br> Are some who have faith,<br> But most of them<br> Are perverted transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Lan ya<u>d</u>urrookum ill<u>a</u> a<u>th</u>an wa-in yuq<u>a</u>tilookum yuwallookumu al-adb<u>a</u>ra thumma l<u>a</u> yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  <u>D</u>uribat AAalayhimu a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>illatu ayna m<u>a</u> thuqifoo ill<u>a</u> bi<u>h</u>ablin mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>h</u>ablin mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wab<u>a</u>oo bigha<u>d</u>abin mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>d</u>uribat AAalayhimu almaskanatu <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum k<u>a</u>noo yakfuroona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wayaqtuloona al-anbiy<u>a</u>a bighayri <u>h</u>aqqin <u>tha</u>lika bim<u>a</u> AAa<u>s</u>aw wak<u>a</u>noo yaAAtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  Shame is pitched over them<br> (Like a tent) wherever<br> They are found,<br> Except when under a covenant<br> (Of protection) from God<br> And from men; they draw<br> On themselves wrath from God,<br> And pitched over them<br> Is (the tent of) destitution.<br> This because they rejected<br> The Signs of God, and slew<br> The Prophets in defiance of right;<br>This because they rebelled<br> And transgressed beyond bounds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Laysoo saw<u>a</u>an min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi ommatun q<u>a</u>-imatun yatloona <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi <u>a</u>n<u>a</u>a allayli wahum yasjudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  Not all of them are alike:<br> Of the People of the Book<br> Are a portion that stand<br> (For the right); they rehearse<br> The Signs of God all night long,<br> And they prostrate themselves<br> In adoration.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waya/muroona bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wayanhawna AAani almunkari wayus<u>a</u>riAAoona fee alkhayr<u>a</u>ti waol<u>a</u>-ika mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>114.  They believe in God<br> And the Last Day;<br> They enjoin what is right,<br> And forbid what is wrong;<br> And they hasten (in emulation)<br> In (all) good works:<br> They are in the ranks<br> Of the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Wam<u>a</u> yafAAaloo min khayrin falan yukfaroohu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<b>a</b>lmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  Of the good that they do,<br> Nothing will be rejected<br> Of them; for God knoweth well<br> Those that do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lan tughniya AAanhum amw<u>a</u>luhum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>duhum mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an waol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  Those who reject Faith,—<br> Neither their possessions<br> Nor their (numerous) progeny<br> Will avail them aught against God:<br> They will be Companions<br> Of the Fire,—dwelling<br> Therein (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Mathalu m<u>a</u> yunfiqoona fee h<u>ath</u>ihi al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> kamathali ree<u>h</u>in feeh<u>a</u> <u>s</u>irrun a<u>sa</u>bat <u>h</u>artha qawmin <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo anfusahum faahlakat-hu wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamahumu All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u>kin anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  What they spend<br> In the life<br> Of this (material) world<br> May be likened to a Wind<br> Which brings a nipping frost:<br> It strikes and destroys the harvest<br> Of men who have wronged<br>Their own souls: it is not God<br> That hath wronged them, but<br> They wrong themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo bi<u>ta</u>natan min doonikum l<u>a</u> ya/loonakum khab<u>a</u>lan waddoo m<u>a</u> AAanittum qad badati albagh<u>da</u>o min afw<u>a</u>hihim wam<u>a</u> tukhfee <u>s</u>udooruhum akbaru qad bayyann<u>a</u> lakumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti in kuntum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  O ye who believe!<br> Take not into your intimacy<br> Those outside your ranks:<br> They will not fail<br> To corrupt you. They<br> Only desire your ruin:<br> Rank hatred has already<br> Appeared from their mouths:<br> What their hearts conceal<br> Is far worse.<br> We have made plain<br> To you the Signs,<br> If ye have wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  H<u>a</u> antum ol<u>a</u>-i tu<u>h</u>ibboonahum wal<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibboonakum watu/minoona bi<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi kullihi wa-i<u>tha</u> laqookum q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> wa-i<u>tha</u> khalaw AAa<u>dd</u>oo AAalaykumu al-an<u>a</u>mila mina alghay<i><u>th</u></i>i qul mootoo bighay<i><u>th</u></i>ikum inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  Ah! ye are those<br> Who love them,<br> But they love you not,<br> Though ye believe<br> In the whole of the Book,<br> When they meet you,<br> They say, "We believe":<br> But when they are alone,<br> They bite off the very tips<br> Of their fingers at you<br> In their rage. Say:<br> "Perish in your rage;<br> God knoweth well<br> All the secrets of the heart."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  In tamsaskum <u>h</u>asanatun tasu/hum wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibkum sayyi-atun yafra<u>h</u>oo bih<u>a</u> wa-in ta<u>s</u>biroo watattaqoo l<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urrukum kayduhum shay-an inna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloona mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  If aught that is good<br> Befalls you, it grieves them;<br> But if some misfortune<br> Overtakes you, they rejoice<br>At it. But if ye are constant<br> And do right,<br> Not the least harm<br> Will their cunning<br> Do to you; for God<br> Compasseth round about<br> All that they do.</p> <div class="filenav">
