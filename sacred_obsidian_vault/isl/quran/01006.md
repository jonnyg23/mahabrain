 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Walaw anna likulli nafsin <i><u>th</u></i>alamat m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i la<b>i</b>ftadat bihi waasarroo a<b>l</b>nnad<u>a</u>mata lamm<u>a</u> raawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba waqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Every soul that hath sinned,<br> If it possessed all<br> That is on earth,<br> Would fain give it in ransom:<br>They would declare (their) repentance<br> When they see the Penalty:<br> But the judgment between them<br> Will be with justice,<br> And no wrong will be done<br> Unto them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Al<u>a</u> inna lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i al<u>a</u> inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>55.  Is it not (the case) that to God<br> Belongeth whatever is<br> In the heavens and on earth?<br> Is it not (the case) that<br> God's promise is assuredly true?<br> Yet most of them understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Huwa yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>56.  It is He who giveth life<br> And who taketh it,<br> And to Him shall ye<br> All be brought back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su qad j<u>a</u>atkum mawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atun min rabbikum washif<u>a</u>on lim<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoori wahudan wara<u>h</u>matun lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>57.  O mankind! there hath come<br> To you a direction from your Lord<br> And a healing for the (diseases)<br> In your hearts,—and for those<br> Who believe, a Guidance<br> And a Mercy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Qul bifa<u>d</u>li All<u>a</u>hi wabira<u>h</u>matihi fabi<u>tha</u>lika falyafra<u>h</u>oo huwa khayrun mimm<u>a</u> yajmaAAoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>58.  Say: "In the Bounty of God.<br> And in His Mercy,—in that<br> Let them rejoice": that is better<br> Than the (wealth) they hoard.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Qul araaytum m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu lakum min rizqin fajaAAaltum minhu <u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>man wa<u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lan qul <u>a</u>ll<u>a</u>hu a<u>th</u>ina lakum am AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi taftaroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>59.  Say: "See ye what things<br> God hath sent down to you<br>For sustenance? Yet ye<br> Hold forbidden some things<br> Thereof and (some things) lawful."<br> Say: "Hath God indeed<br> Permitted you, or do ye invent<br> (Things) to attribute to God?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>annu alla<u>th</u>eena yaftaroona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati inna All<u>a</u>ha la<u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>60.  And what think those<br> Who invent lies against God,<br> Of the Day of Judgment?<br> Verily God is full of Bounty<br> To mankind, but most<br> Of them are ungrateful!"</p> <div class="filenav">
