 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Awa lam yar<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo anna a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a k<u>a</u>nat<u>a</u> ratqan fafataqn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> wajaAAaln<u>a</u> mina alm<u>a</u>-i kulla shay-in <u>h</u>ayyin afal<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Do not the Unbelievers see<br> That the heavens and the earth<br> Were joined together (as one<br> Unit of Creation), before<br> We clove them asunder?<br> We made from water<br> Every living thing. Will they<br> Not then believe?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i raw<u>a</u>siya an tameeda bihim wajaAAaln<u>a</u> feeh<u>a </u>fij<u>a</u>jan subulan laAAallahum yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  And We have set on the earth<br> Mountains standing firm,<br>Lest it should shake with them,<br> And We have made therein<br> Broad highways<br> (between mountains)<br> For them to pass through:<br> That they may receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a saqfan ma<u>h</u>foo<i><u>th</u></i>an wahum AAan <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tih<u>a</u> muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And We have made<br> The heavens as a canopy<br> Well guarded: yet do they<br> Turn away from the Signs<br> Which these things (point to)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra wa<b>al</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara kullun fee falakin yasba<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  It is He Who created<br> The Night and the Day,<br> And the sun and the moon:<br> All (the celestial bodies)<br> Swim along, each in its<br> Rounded course.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wam<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> libasharin min qablika alkhulda afa-in mitta fahumu alkh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  We granted not to any man<br> Before thee permanent life<br> (Here): If then thou shouldst die,<br> Would they live permanently?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Kullu nafsin <u>tha</u>-iqatu almawti wanablookum bi<b>al</b>shsharri wa<b>a</b>lkhayri fitnatan wa-ilayn<u>a</u> turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Every soul shall have<br> A taste of death:<br> And We test you<br> By evil and by good<br>By way of trial.<br> To Us must ye return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> ra<u>a</u>ka alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in yattakhi<u>th</u>oonaka ill<u>a</u> huzuwan ah<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee ya<u>th</u>kuru <u>a</u>lihatakum wahum bi<u>th</u>ikri a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni hum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>36.  When the Unbelievers see thee,<br> They treat thee not except<br> With ridicule. "Is this,"<br> (They say), "the one who talks<br> Of your gods?" And they<br> Blaspheme at the mention<br> Of (God) Most Gracious!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Khuliqa al-ins<u>a</u>nu min AAajalin saoreekum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee fal<u>a</u> tastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Man is a creature of haste:<br> Soon (enough) will I show<br> You My Signs; then<br> Ye will not ask Me<br> To hasten them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  They say: "When will this<br> Promise come to pass,<br> If ye are telling the truth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Law yaAAlamu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo <u>h</u>eena l<u>a</u> yakuffoona AAan wujoohihimu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra wal<u>a</u> AAan <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorihim wal<u>a</u> hum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  If only the Unbelievers<br> Knew (the time) when they<br> Will not be able<br> To ward off the Fire<br> From their faces, nor yet<br> From their backs, and (when)<br> No help can reach them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Bal ta/teehim baghtatan fatabhatuhum fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona raddah<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> hum yun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Nay, it may come to them<br> All of a sudden and confound<br> Them: no power will they<br> Have then to avert it,<br> Nor will they (then)<br> Get respite.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Walaqadi istuhzi-a birusulin min qablika fa<u>ha</u>qa bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena sakhiroo minhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Mocked were (many)<br> Apostles before thee;<br> But their scoffers<br> Were hemmed in<br> By the thing that they mocked.</p> <div class="filenav">
