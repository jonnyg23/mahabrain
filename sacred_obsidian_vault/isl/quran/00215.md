 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 15</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Y<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmatiya allatee anAAamtu AAalaykum waannee fa<u>dd</u>altukum AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  O Children of Israel! call to mind<br> The special favour which I bestowed<br> Upon you, and that I preferred you<br> To all others (for My Message).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo yawman l<u>a</u> tajzee nafsun AAan nafsin shay-an wal<u>a</u> yuqbalu minh<u>a</u> AAadlun wal<u>a</u> tanfaAAuh<u>a</u> shaf<u>a</u>AAatun wal<u>a</u> hum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  Then guard yourselves against a Day<br>When one soul shall not avail another,<br> Nor shall compensation be<br> accepted from her<br> Nor shall intercession profit her<br> Nor shall anyone be helped (from outside).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Wa-i<u>th</u>i ibtal<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema rabbuhu bikalim<u>a</u>tin faatammahunna q<u>a</u>la innee j<u>a</u>AAiluka li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si im<u>a</u>man q<u>a</u>la wamin <u>th</u>urriyyatee q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> yan<u>a</u>lu AAahdee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  And remember that Abraham<br> Was tried by his Lord<br> With certain Commands,<br> Which he fulfilled:<br> He said: "I will make thee<br> An Imām to the Nations."<br> He pleaded: "And also<br> (Imāms) from my offspring!"<br> He answered: "But My Promise<br> Is not within the reach<br> Of evil-doers."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Wa-i<u>th</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> albayta math<u>a</u>batan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waamnan wa<b>i</b>ttakhi<u>th</u>oo min maq<u>a</u>mi ibr<u>a</u>heema mu<u>s</u>allan waAAahidn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela an <u>t</u>ahhir<u>a</u> baytiya li<b>l</b><u>tta</u>-ifeena wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>kifeena wa<b>al</b>rrukkaAAi a<b>l</b>ssujood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  Remember We made the House<br> A place of assembly for men<br> And a place of safety;<br> And take ye the Station<br> Of Abraham as a place<br> Of prayer; and We covenanted<br> With Abraham and lsmā’īl,<br> That they should sanctify<br> My House for those who<br> Compass it round, or use it<br>As a retreat, or bow, or<br> Prostrate themselves (therein<br> In prayer).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu rabbi ijAAal h<u>atha</u> baladan <u>a</u>minan wa<b>o</b>rzuq ahlahu mina a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti man <u>a</u>mana minhum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri q<u>a</u>la waman kafara faomattiAAuhu qaleelan thumma a<u>dt</u>arruhu il<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  And remember Abraham said:<br> "My Lord, make this a City<br> Of Peace, and feed its People<br> With fruits,—such of them<br> As believe in God and the Last Day."<br> He said: "(Yea), and such as<br> Reject Faith,—for a while<br> Will I grant them their pleasure,<br> But will soon drive them<br> To the torment of Fire,—<br> An evil destination (indeed)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Wa-i<u>th</u> yarfaAAu ibr<u>a</u>heemu alqaw<u>a</u>AAida mina albayti wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeelu rabban<u>a</u> taqabbal minn<u>a</u> innaka anta a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  And remember Abraham<br> And Ismā’īl raised<br> The foundations of the House<br> (With this prayer): "Our Lord!<br> Accept (this service) from us:<br> For Thou art the All-Hearing,<br> The All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Rabban<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>jAAaln<u>a</u> muslimayni laka wamin <u>th</u>urriyyatin<u>a</u> ommatan muslimatan laka waarin<u>a</u> man<u>a</u>sikan<u>a</u> watub AAalayn<u>a</u> innaka anta a<b>l</b>ttaww<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  "Our Lord! make of us<br> Muslims, bowing to Thy (Will),<br> And of our progeny a people<br> Muslim, bowing to Thy (Will);<br> And show us our places for<br> The celebration of (due) rites;<br> And turn unto us (in Mercy);<br> For Thou art the Oft-Returning,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Rabban<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>bAAath feehim rasoolan minhum yatloo AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tika wayuAAallimuhumu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wayuzakkeehim innaka anta alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  "Our Lord! send amongst them<br> An Apostle of their own,<br> Who shall rehearse Thy Signs<br> To them and instruct them<br>In Scripture and Wisdom,<br> And sanctify them:<br> For Thou art the Exalted in Might,<br> The Wise."</p> <div class="filenav">
