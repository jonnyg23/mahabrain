 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 24</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_189.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>189.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum min nafsin w<u>ah</u>idatin wajaAAala minh<u>a</u> zawjah<u>a</u> liyaskuna ilayh<u>a</u> falamm<u>a</u> taghashsh<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> <u>h</u>amalat <u>h</u>amlan khafeefan famarrat bihi falamm<u>a</u> athqalat daAAaw<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha rabbahum<u>a</u> la-in <u>a</u>taytan<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an lanakoonanna mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>189.  It is He Who created<br> You from a single person,<br> And made his mate<br> Of like nature, in order<br> That he might dwell with her<br> (In love). When they are<br> United, she bears a light<br> Burden and carries it about<br> (Unnoticed). When she grows<br> Heavy, they both pray<br> To God their Lord, (saying):<br> "If Thou givest us<br> A goodly child,<br>We vow we shall<br> (Ever) be grateful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_190.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>190.  Falamm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an jaAAal<u>a</u> lahu shurak<u>a</u>a feem<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> fataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>190.  But when He giveth them<br> A goodly child, they ascribe<br> To others a share in the gift<br> They have received:<br> But God is exalted<br> High above the partners<br> They ascribe to Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_191.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>191.  Ayushrikoona m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yakhluqu shay-an wahum yukhlaqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>191.  Do they indeed ascribe<br> To Him as partners things<br> That can create nothing,<br> But are themselves created?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_192.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>192.  Wal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona lahum na<u>s</u>ran wal<u>a</u> anfusahum yan<u>s</u>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>192.  No aid can they give them,<br> Nor can they aid themselves!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_193.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>193.  Wa-in tadAAoohum il<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yattabiAAookum saw<u>a</u>on AAalaykum adaAAawtumoohum am antum <u>sa</u>mitoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>193.  If ye call them to guidance,<br> They will not obey:<br> For you it is the same<br> Whether ye call them<br> Or ye hold your peace!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_194.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>194.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi AAib<u>a</u>dun amth<u>a</u>lukum fa<b>o</b>dAAoohum falyastajeeboo lakum in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>194.  Verily those whom ye<br> Call upon besides God<br> Are servants like unto you:<br> Call upon them, and let them<br> Listen to your prayer,<br> If ye are (indeed) truthful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_195.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>195.  Alahum arjulun yamshoona bih<u>a</u> am lahum aydin yab<u>t</u>ishoona bih<u>a</u> am lahum aAAyunun yub<u>s</u>iroona bih<u>a</u> am lahum <u>atha</u>nun yasmaAAoona bih<u>a</u> quli odAAoo shurak<u>a</u>akum thumma keedooni fal<u>a</u> tun<i><u>th</u></i>iroon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>195.  Have they feet to walk with?<br> Or hands to lay hold with?<br>Or eyes to see with?<br> Or ears to hear with?<br> Say: "Call your 'god-partners',<br> Scheme (your worst) against me,<br> And give me no respite!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_196.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>196.  Inna waliyyiya All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee nazzala alkit<u>a</u>ba wahuwa yatawall<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>196.  "For my Protector is God,<br> Who revealed the Book<br> (From time to time),<br> And He will choose<br> And befriend the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_197.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>197.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min doonihi l<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona na<u>s</u>rakum wal<u>a</u> anfusahum yan<u>s</u>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>197.  "But those ye call upon<br> Besides Him, are unable<br> To help you, and indeed<br> To help themselves."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_198.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>198.  Wa-in tadAAoohum il<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoo watar<u>a</u>hum yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ilayka wahum l<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>198.  If thou callest them<br> To guidance, they hear not.<br> Thou wilt see them<br> Looking at thee, but<br> They see not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_199.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>199.  Khu<u>th</u>i alAAafwa wa/mur bi<b>a</b>lAAurfi waaAAri<u>d</u> AAani alj<u>a</u>hileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>199.  Hold to forgiveness;<br> Command what is right;<br> But turn away from the ignorant.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_200.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>200.  Wa-imm<u>a</u> yanzaghannaka mina a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni nazghun fa<b>i</b>staAAi<u>th</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi innahu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>200.  If a suggestion from Satan<br> Assail thy (mind)<br>Seek refuge with God;<br> For He heareth and knoweth<br> (All things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_201.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>201.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw i<u>tha</u> massahum <u>ta</u>-ifun mina a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni ta<u>th</u>akkaroo fa-i<u>tha</u> hum mub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>201.  Those who fear God,<br> When a thought of evil<br> From Satan assaults them,<br> Bring God to remembrance,<br> When lo! they see (aright)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_202.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>202.  Wa-ikhw<u>a</u>nuhum yamuddoonahum fee alghayyi thumma l<u>a</u> yuq<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>202.  But their brethren (the evil ones)<br> Plunge them deeper into error,<br> And never relax (their efforts).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_203.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>203.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> lam ta/tihim bi-<u>a</u>yatin q<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> ijtabaytah<u>a</u> qul innam<u>a</u> attabiAAu m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya min rabbee h<u>atha</u> ba<u>sa</u>-iru min rabbikum wahudan wara<u>h</u>matun liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>203.  If thou bring them not<br> A revelation, they say:<br> "Why hast thou not<br> Got it together?"<br> Say: "I but follow<br> What is revealed to me<br> From my Lord:<br> This is (nothing but)<br> Lights from your Lord,<br> And Guidance, and Mercy,<br> For any who have Faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_204.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>204.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> quri-a alqur-<u>a</u>nu fa<b>i</b>stamiAAoo lahu waan<u>s</u>itoo laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>204.  When the Qur-ān is read,<br> Listen to it with attention,<br>And hold your peace:<br> That ye may receive Mercy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_205.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>205.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur rabbaka fee nafsika ta<u>d</u>arruAAan wakheefatan wadoona aljahri mina alqawli bi<b>a</b>lghuduwwi wa<b>a</b>l-<u>asa</u>li wal<u>a</u> takun mina algh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>205.  And do thou (O reader!)<br> Bring thy Lord to remembrance<br> In thy (very) soul,<br> With humility and in reverence,<br> Without loudness in words,<br> In the mornings and evenings;<br> And be not thou<br> Of those who are unheedful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_206.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>206.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena AAinda rabbika l<u>a</u> yastakbiroona AAan AAib<u>a</u>datihi wayusabbi<u>h</u>oonahu walahu yasjudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>206.  Those who are near<br> To thy Lord, disdain not<br> To do Him worship:<br> They celebrate His praises,<br> And bow down before Him.</p>  <div class="filenav">
