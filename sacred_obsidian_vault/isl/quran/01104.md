 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Waoo<u>h</u>iya il<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>in annahu lan yu/mina min qawmika ill<u>a</u> man qad <u>a</u>mana fal<u>a</u> tabta-is bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafAAaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>36.  It was revealed to Noah:<br> "None of thy People will believe<br> Except those who have believed<br> Already! So grieve no longer<br> Over their (evil) deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>naAAi alfulka bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u> wawa<u>h</u>yin<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tukh<u>at</u>ibnee fee alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo innahum mughraqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "But construct an Ark<br> Under Our eyes and Our<br> Inspiration, and address Me<br> No (further) on behalf<br> Of those who are in sin:<br> For they are about to be<br> Overwhelmed (in the Flood)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Waya<u>s</u>naAAu alfulka wakullam<u>a</u> marra AAalayhi malaon min qawmihi sakhiroo minhu q<u>a</u>la in taskharoo minn<u>a</u> fa-inn<u>a</u> naskharu minkum kam<u>a</u> taskharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Forthwith he (starts)<br> Constructing the Ark:<br> Every time that the Chiefs<br> Of his People passed by him,<br> They threw ridicule on him.<br> He said: "If ye ridicule<br> Us now, we (in our turn)<br> Can look down on you<br> With ridicule likewise!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fasawfa taAAlamoona man ya/teehi AAa<u>tha</u>bun yukhzeehi waya<u>h</u>illu AAalayhi AAa<u>tha</u>bun muqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "But soon will ye know<br> Who it is on whom<br> Will descend a Penalty<br> That will cover them<br>With shame,—on whom will be<br> Unloosed a Penalty lasting:"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> waf<u>a</u>ra a<b>l</b>ttannooru quln<u>a</u> i<u>h</u>mil feeh<u>a</u> min kullin zawjayni ithnayni waahlaka ill<u>a</u> man sabaqa AAalayhi alqawlu waman <u>a</u>mana wam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mana maAAahu ill<u>a</u> qaleel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  At length, behold!<br> There came Our Command,<br> And the fountains of the earth<br> Gushed forth! We said:<br> "Embark therein, of each kind<br> Two, male and female,<br> And your family—except<br> Those against whom the Word<br> Has already gone forth,—<br> And the Believers."<br> But only a few<br> Believed with him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Waq<u>a</u>la irkaboo feeh<u>a</u> bismi All<u>a</u>hi majr<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wamurs<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> inna rabbee laghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  So he said: "Embark ye<br> On the Ark,<br> In the name of God,<br> Whether it move<br> Or be at rest!<br> For my Lord is, be sure,<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wahiya tajree bihim fee mawjin ka<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>li wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>unu ibnahu wak<u>a</u>na fee maAAzilin y<u>a</u> bunayya irkab maAAan<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> takun maAAa alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  So the Ark floated<br> With them on the waves<br> (Towering) like mountains,<br> And Noah called out<br> To his son, who had<br> Separated himself (from the rest):<br> "O my son! embark<br> With us, and be not<br> With the Unbelievers!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Q<u>a</u>la sa<u>a</u>wee il<u>a</u> jabalin yaAA<u>s</u>imunee mina alm<u>a</u>-i q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> AA<u>as</u>ima alyawma min amri All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> man ra<u>h</u>ima wa<u>ha</u>la baynahum<u>a</u> almawju fak<u>a</u>na mina almughraqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  The son replied: "I will<br> Betake myself to some mountain:<br> It will save me from<br> The water." Noah said:<br> "This day nothing can save,<br> From the Command of God,<br> Any but those on whom<br> He hath mercy!"—<br> And the waves came<br> Between them, and the son<br> Was among those<br> Overwhelmed in the Flood.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Waqeela y<u>a</u> ar<u>d</u>u iblaAAee m<u>a</u>aki way<u>a</u> sam<u>a</u>o aqliAAee waghee<u>d</u>a alm<u>a</u>o waqu<u>d</u>iya al-amru wa<b>i</b>stawat AAal<u>a</u> aljoodiyyi waqeela buAAdan lilqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Then the word went forth:<br> "O earth! swallow up<br> Thy water, and O sky!<br> Withhold (thy rain)!"<br> And the water abated,<br> And the matter was ended.<br> The Ark rested on Mount<br> Jūdī, and the word<br> Went forth: "Away<br> With those who do wrong!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>un rabbahu faq<u>a</u>la rabbi inna ibnee min ahlee wa-inna waAAdaka al<u>h</u>aqqu waanta a<u>h</u>kamu al<u>ha</u>kimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  And Noah called upon<br> His Lord, and said:<br> "O my Lord! surely<br> My son is of my family!<br> And Thy promise is true,<br> And Thou art<br> The Justest of Judges!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>u innahu laysa min ahlika innahu AAamalun ghayru <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>in fal<u>a</u> tas-alni m<u>a</u> laysa laka bihi AAilmun innee aAAi<i><u>th</u></i>uka an takoona mina alj<u>a</u>hileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  He said: "O Noah!<br> He is not of thy family:<br> For his conduct is unrighteous.<br> So ask not of Me<br> That of which thou<br> Hast no knowledge!<br> I give thee counsel, lest<br> Thou act like the ignorant!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi innee aAAoo<u>th</u>u bika an as-alaka m<u>a</u> laysa lee bihi AAilmun wa-ill<u>a</u> taghfir lee watar<u>h</u>amnee akun mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Noah said: "O my Lord!<br> I do seek refuge with Thee,<br> Lest I ask Thee for that<br> Of which I have no knowledge.<br> And unless Thou forgive me<br> And have Mercy on me,<br> I should indeed be lost!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Qeela y<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>u ihbi<u>t</u> bisal<u>a</u>min minn<u>a</u> wabarak<u>a</u>tin AAalayka waAAal<u>a</u> omamin mimman maAAaka waomamun sanumattiAAuhum thumma yamassuhum minn<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  The word came: "O Noah!<br> Come down (from the Ark)<br> With Peace from Us,<br> And Blessing on thee<br> And on some of the Peoples<br> (Who will spring) from those<br> With thee: but (there will be<br> Other) Peoples to whom We<br> Shall grant their pleasures<br> (For a time), but in the end<br> Will a grievous Penalty<br> Reach them from Us."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Tilka min anb<u>a</u>-i alghaybi noo<u>h</u>eeh<u>a</u> ilayka m<u>a</u> kunta taAAlamuh<u>a</u> anta wal<u>a</u> qawmuka min qabli h<u>atha</u> fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir inna alAA<u>a</u>qibata lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Such are some of the stories<br> Of the Unseen, which We<br> Have revealed unto thee:<br> Before this, neither thou<br> Nor thy People knew them.<br> So persevere patiently:<br> For the End is for those<br> Who are righteous.</p> <div class="filenav">
