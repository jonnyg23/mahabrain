 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 34</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_254.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>254.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo anfiqoo mimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum min qabli an ya/tiya yawmun l<u>a</u> bayAAun feehi wal<u>a</u> khullatun wal<u>a</u> shaf<u>a</u>AAatun wa<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u>firoona humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>254.  O ye who believe!<br> Spend out of (the bounties)<br> We have provided for you,<br> Before the Day comes<br> When no bargaining<br> (Will avail), nor friendship<br> Nor intercession.<br> Those who reject Faith—they<br> Are the wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_255.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>255.  All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa al<u>h</u>ayyu alqayyoomu l<u>a</u> ta/khu<u>th</u>uhu sinatun wal<u>a</u> nawmun lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i man <u>tha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee yashfaAAu AAindahu ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>nihi yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wam<u>a</u> khalfahum wal<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>oona bishay-in min AAilmihi ill<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a wasiAAa kursiyyuhu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wal<u>a</u> yaooduhu <u>h</u>if<i><u>th</u></i>uhum<u>a</u> wahuwa alAAaliyyu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>255.  God! There is no god<br> But He,—the Living,<br> The Self-subsisting, Eternal.<br> No slumber can seize Him<br> Nor sleep. His are all things<br> In the heavens and on earth.<br> Who is there can intercede<br> In His presence except<br> As He permitteth? He knoweth<br> What (appeareth to His creatures<br>As) Before or After<br> Or Behind them.<br> Nor shall they compass<br> Aught of His knowledge<br> Except as He willeth.<br> His Throne doth extend<br> Over the heavens<br> And the earth, and He feeleth<br> No fatigue in guarding,<br> And preserving them<br> For He is the Most High,<br> The Supreme (in glory).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_256.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>256.  L<u>a</u> ikr<u>a</u>ha fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni qad tabayyana a<b>l</b>rrushdu mina alghayyi faman yakfur bi<b>al</b><u>tta</u>ghooti wayu/min bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi faqadi istamsaka bi<b>a</b>lAAurwati alwuthq<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> infi<u>sa</u>ma lah<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>256.  Let there be no compulsion<br> In religion: Truth stands out<br> Clear from Error: whoever<br> Rejects Evil and believes<br> In God hath grasped<br> The most trustworthy<br> Hand-hold, that never breaks.<br> And God heareth<br> And knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_257.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>257.  All<u>a</u>hu waliyyu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo yukhrijuhum mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo awliy<u>a</u>ohumu a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>ghootu yukhrijoonahum mina a<b>l</b>nnoori il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>257.  God is the Protector<br> Of those who have faith:<br>From the depths of darkness<br> He will lead them forth<br> Into light. Of those<br> Who reject faith the patrons<br> Are the Evil Ones: from light<br> They will lead them forth<br> Into the depths of darkness.<br> They will be Companions<br> Of the fire, to dwell therein<br> (For ever).</p> <div class="filenav">
