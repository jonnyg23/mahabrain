 <p>(142)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for the nineteenth of the</p>  
 <p>month</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>All praise be to Allah in the manner He has praised His own Self, the</p>  
 <p>praise of his Oneness in the words. "There is no god but Allah" as He is</p>  
 <p>praise for His Oneness by his Throne, His Chair and the things</p>  
 <p>underneath it. And His greatness is magnified in the words, "Allah is the</p>  
 <p>Great" as magnified by His Throne, Chair and things underneath it. And</p>  
 <p>He is praised in the words "All praise be for Allah" as He is praised by</p>  
 <p>His creation. And the praise of his Oneness in the words, "There is no</p>  
 <p>god but Allah" as he is praise for His Oneness by His Creation. And the</p>  
 <p>praise of His glory in the words "Glory be to Him" as He is glorified by</p>  
 <p>His Angels. And His greatness is magnified in the words "Allah is the</p>  
 <p>Great" as He is magnified by His Angels. And He is praised in the words</p>  
 <p>"All praise be to Allah" as He is praise by His Heavens and His Earth.</p>  
 <p>And the praise of His Oneness in the words "There is no god but Allah"</p>  
 <p>as He is praised for His Oneness by His Heavens and His Earth. And</p>  
 <p>the praise of His glory in the words "Glory be to Him" as He is glorified</p>  
 <p>by His Heavens and His Earth. And His greatness is magnified in the</p>  
 <p>words "Allah is the Great" as is magnified by His Heavens and the Earth.</p>  
 <p>And He is praised in the words "All praise be to Allah" as He is praised</p>  
 <p>by the thunder, lightning and rain. And the praise of His glory in the</p>  
 <p>words "Glory be to Allah" as He is glorified by the thunder, lightning and</p>  
 <p>the rain. And His greatness is magnified in the words "Allah is the Great"</p>  
 <p>as is magnified by the thunder, the lightning and rain. And He is praised</p>  
 <p>in the words "All praise be to Allah" as he praised by His throne and the</p>  
 <p>things encompassed by His knowledge. And the praise of His Oneness</p>  
 <p>in the words "There is no god but Allah" as He is praised for His</p>  
 <p>Oneness by His throne and the things encompassed by His Knowledge.</p>  
 <p>And the praise of His glory in the words "Glory be to Allah" as He is</p>  
 <p>glorified by His throne and the things encompassed by His knowledge.</p>  
 <p>And His greatness is magnified in the words "Allah is the Great" as</p>  
 <p>magnified by His throne and things encompassed by His Knowledge.</p>  
 <p>And He is praised in the words "All praise be to Allah" as He is praised</p>  
 <p>by the Oceans and by whatever is in them. And the praise of His</p>  
 <p>Oneness in the words "There is no god but Allah" as He is praised for</p>  
 <p>His Oneness by the Oceans and whatever is in them. And His greatness</p>  
 <p>is magnified in the words "Allah is the Great" as is magnified by the</p>  
 <p>Oceans and whatever is in them. And all praise be to Allah till it reaches</p>  
 <p>the height of His Knowledge and perfection of His pleasure the limit</p>  
 <p>thereof unknown. And the praise of His Oneness in the words "There is</p>  
 <p>no god but Allah" according to the height of His knowledge and till it</p>  
 <p>reaches the perfection for His pleasure, the limit thereof is unknown. O</p>  
 <p>Allah! Bless Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and His Progeny, be merciful to</p>  
 <p>Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and his Progeny and favour Muhammad (s.a.w.a.)</p>  
 <p>and his Progeny with Your bounties in the manner You blessed, showed</p>  
 <p>mercy and favoured Ibrahim and his progeny with Your bounties. Verily</p>  
 <p>You are Praiseworthy and Glorious. O Allah! After praising You,</p>  
 <p>confessing Your Oneness, glorifying You and seeking Your blessings to</p>  
 <p>bestow on Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and his progeny, I beseech You to</p>  
 <p>forgive all my sins, minor, major, committed secretly or openly whether in</p>  
 <p>my knowledge or not which You have reckoned and preserved the</p>  
 <p>account thereof but I have personally forgotten. O Allah! Allah! O</p>  
 <p>Beneficent! O Beneficent! O Merciful! O Merciful! Ameen! O the Lord of</p>  
 <p>Worlds! O Allah! Send Your Blessings on Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and his</p>  
 <p>Progeny. Ameen.</p>
