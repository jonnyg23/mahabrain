 <p>(127)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for the fourth of the month</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Praise be to You that Your religion appeared in this world, Your</p>  
 <p>representative Messengers and Imams did come. Your realm is strong,</p>  
 <p>Your power is great, Your promise is true. Your Heavenly Throne is at</p>  
 <p>the highest pitch and Your have sent Your messenger with the guidance</p>  
 <p>and the true religion so that he may make it triumphant over all other</p>  
 <p>religions, though averse may be polytheists. O Allah! You have</p>  
 <p>perfected Your religion, completed Your Light. You are Holy in respect</p>  
 <p>of Your promise. You have established manifest signs for mankind and</p>  
 <p>perfected became Your Words with truth and justice. O Allah! All praise</p>  
 <p>be to You. It is Your blessing that You remove the difficulties, bestow the</p>  
 <p>happiness and ease, decree with truth decide with justice and guide to</p>  
 <p>the right path. Hallowed is Your face. Glorified praise be to You. There</p>  
 <p>is no god but You the Lord of the Heavens and the Lord of the Earth and</p>  
 <p>of all that is in them, the Lord of the great Arsh (Throne). O Allah!</p>  
 <p>Praised are You in Torah, praised are You in the Evangel, praised are</p>  
 <p>You in the scriptures of the past, praised are You in Surah-e-Hamd and</p>  
 <p>in the glorious Quran, praised amongst the Angels that enjoy nearness</p>  
 <p>to You, praised are You amongst the Prophet-messengers, praised are</p>  
 <p>You by the Honourable Recorders of men's deeds. Praise is befitting</p>  
 <p>You only. All praised be to You and the praise is Your glory. Your testing</p>  
 <p>is by a gracious trial. Your decree is just, the Earth is within Your control</p>  
 <p>and so is the sky. All praise be to You. You keep the scale of justice in</p>  
 <p>balance. You are Lofty, Provider of manifest proofs, Truthful, Glorious</p>  
 <p>and Glorified. O Allah! You are praised for You are the Revealer of the</p>  
 <p>Holy Verses, Grantor of the prayers, Remover of the difficulties,</p>  
 <p>Bestower of the blessings; life and death are under Your control. O God!</p>  
 <p>All praise is for You although You need it not. Sincere obedience is for</p>  
 <p>You. The vastness of "Arsh" is for You, everlasting praise is for You. All</p>  
 <p>praise as justice demands is for You. Praise be to You as You have</p>  
 <p>praised Yourself and in a manner which meets Your likeness and</p>  
 <p>pleasure, through praise, worships, and thanks to You. O my Allah! Your</p>  
 <p>praiseworthiness is Most Exalted and You are Most Merciful. O Lord!</p>  
 <p>Praise be to You during night when it enshrouds. Praise be to You</p>  
 <p>during the day when the sun shines. Praise be to You in this world and</p>  
 <p>in the Hereafter. Praise be to You. How Excellent and Exalted are You in</p>  
 <p>Your attributes. Praise be to You. How Generous and how Great are</p>  
 <p>You. Glory be to You. What an Excellence and Glory You possess.</p>  
 <p>Praise be to You for the destinies and decrees whether Your creatures</p>  
 <p>like them or not. Praise be to You in all conditions of this world and</p>  
 <p>those of the world Hereafter, O the most Merciful!</p>
