 <p><a name="page_357"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 357</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SPIRIT OF GOD WITHIN</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>ARISE, MY SPIRIT</b></p>  
 <p>Arise, my Spirit, arise and shine.<br> Let Thy light illumine my path, and let Thy wisdom direct my way.<br> Compel my will to do Thy bidding, and command my Soul to look to Thee.<br> I will follow Thee, my Spirit, and will learn of Thee all that I need to know.<br> I will sit in the Silence and listen and watch, and<br> I will see Thy light and hear Thy voice.<br> II will follow Thee and will not depart from Thee,<br> for in Thee alone is Peace.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Arise and shine</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>BIRTHLESS</b><b> AND DEATHLESS</b></p>  
 <p>The Spirit within me is Birthless and deathless;<br> It was not born and It cannot die.<br> I have no fear of death, for I perceive that<br> Death is an illusion and not the Truth.<br> I was born of the Spirit, and I live in the Spirit,<br> And shall continue to live in and by the Spirit.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Spirit within me lives forever</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>COMMAND MY SOUL</b></p>  
 <p>Spirit within me, command my Soul to do Thy bidding;<br> Compel me to follow the course of Truth and Wisdom.<br> Control my inward thoughts and my outward ways,<br> And make me to understand Thy Laws.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_358"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 358</font></a></p>  
 <p>Command my Soul to turn to Thee for guidance and light;<br> To turn to Thee for wisdom and knowledge.<br> Let the paths of my Life be made straight and sure;<br> Let the Journey of my Soul find its completion in Thee.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Command my Soul to do Thy bidding</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>DESPAIR GIVES WAY TO JOY</b></p>  
 <p>Despair gives way to joy at the thought of Thee, Indwelling Good.<br> I cannot be sad when I think of Thee.<br> My sorrow is turned to gladness and my shame to rejoicing.<br> My tears are wiped away and the sunlight of the Spirit shines through the clouds of depression and lights the way to Heaven.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Thy Joy has made me glad</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>FREE SPIRIT WITHIN ME</b></p>  
 <p>Free Spirit within me, Unbound and Perfect, teach me Thy ways and make known to me Thy Limitless Completion.<br> O Spirit of Life, control my every action and thought.<br> Compel me to follow Thy light that I too may be free and complete.<br> I will follow Thy footsteps and learn of Thee all the wondrous secrets of Life.<br> I will follow Thy light into the Perfect Day.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Free spirit within me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>FULLNESS OF LIGHT</b></p>  
 <p>The Light of Life is full within me and around me.<br> It shines forth into the Perfect Day.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_359"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 359</font></a></p>  
 <p>O Light within, lighting my path to peace,<br> I adore and love You and I let You shine.<br> Go forth and bless all who come to You, Light within.<br> My Light radiates to all and through all.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My light has come</i>.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>HE WHO INHABITS ETERNITY</b></p>  
 <p>He Who inhabits Eternity keeps watch over me and mine.<br> "He Who neither slumbers nor sleeps" forever keeps watch over all.<br> I will rest in the assurance of Love and Protection.<br> O Thou Great Overshadowing Presence,</p>  
 <p>I am conscious of Thy care; I am aware of Thy loving kindness. I rest in Thee.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Be still and know</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I LISTEN</b></p>  
 <p>I will listen for Thy voice, Inner Presence.<br> It will guide me and acquaint me with all knowledge.<br> Thy voice is sweet and tender; it is always kind and gentle.<br> O Lover of my Soul, how I adore Thee! How I love Thee!<br> How I love Thy voice; it thrills me with gladness and joy.<br> It fills me with peace and calm, and it soothes me.<br> It quiets me and gives me wonderful rest.<br> I listen, O Divine Speaker, I listen to Thee alone.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I listen for Thy voice</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>JOY HAS COME TO LIVE WITH ME</b></p>  
 <p>Joy has come to live with me. How can I be sad?<br> I do so love Thy presence, which is Joy within me.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_360"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 360</font></a></p>  
 <p>It makes me glad, and I sing, for I am so filled with Thy Spirit that I cannot be depressed nor unhappy.<br> I am filled with the joy of the Spirit, and I overflow with the gladness of life.<br> Thou art a Happy Companion to travel with me through Life, wonderful Joy, Thou art so radiant and beaming.<br> It is impossible to be sad in Thy presence.<br> I shall give myself to Thee and remain with Thee, for Thou art complete and satisfying.<br> I find fulfillment in Thee and joy forevermore.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am filled with the Spirit of Joy</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>KNOWLEDGE AND WISDOM</b></p>  
 <p>All Wisdom and Knowledge is from within.<br> And my God, Who is All-Knowing, is also within.<br> I am guided and guarded along Life's road into the All Good.<br> My Mind is instructed from On High,<br> And my Wisdom cometh from afar.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Spirit within me is All Wisdom</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY THOUGHT IS IN THEE</b></p>  
 <p>My thought is in Thee, Inner Light.<br> My words are from Thee, Inner Wisdom.<br> My understanding is of Thee, Inner God.<br> I cannot be hid from Thee, my Inspiration and my Life.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My thought is in Thee</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>O LOVE DIVINE</b></p>  
 <p>O Love Divine within me, I am overpowered by Thy Presence.<br> I am speechless, for words cannot utter the things that Thou hast revealed to me.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_361"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 361</font></a></p>  
 <p>Why dost Thou love me so, and why clasp me so close to Thy Eternal Heart?<br> O Blessed Presence, I know, for Thou hast claimed me as Thine own.<br> I shall nevermore walk apart from Thee.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The love of God is within me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>PEACE STEALS THROUGH THE SOUL</b></p>  
 <p>Peace steals through the waiting Soul, and the comfort of the Spirit comes into the stillness of the heart.<br> Peace, like an ocean of Infinite Life, reflects itself through me and calms every turbulent feeling.<br> I am at peace and rest in the knowledge of the All Good which is at hand.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I rest in peace</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>STAND FORTH AND SPEAK</b></p>  
 <p>Stand forth and speak, Spirit within me.<br> Proclaim Thy presence, announce Thy course.<br> Declare through me Thy wondrous works and<br> Let the children of men hear Thy voice.<br> Behold, He maketh all things new.<br> The Spirit within speaks words of Truth and Life to all.<br> The Spirit within me is God.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I speak the Truth</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>SUBTLE ESSENCE OF SPIRIT WITHIN ME</b></p>  
 <p>Subtle Essence of Spirit within me, flowing through me;<br> Elixir of Life in my veins purifying me with Thy marvelous Life,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_362"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 362</font></a></p>  
 <p>I let Thy Spirit cleanse me from all false thought and ideas;<br> I let Thy Life flow through me in a complete and Perfect Whole.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I feel the presence of Spirit within me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE CHRIST WITHIN</b></p>  
 <p>My Life is "hid with Christ in God";<br> The Inner Man is the image and likeness of the Spirit of God.<br> I let that Inner Man come forth into expression in my life;<br> He will guide my feet into all Truth and Wisdom.<br> The Christ within me is free from all worry, and cannot be hindered by any opposing force.<br> My Christ within me is now perfect.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My Christ is within me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE EVERLASTING ARMS</b></p>  
 <p>His Arms enfold me, His Strength upholds me,<br> His Presence fills me with Life and Joy.<br> I shall nevermore be sad nor depressed, for I know that I do not walk Life's path alone.<br> There is One Who goes with me and tells me all the things that I should know.<br> There is a Presence with me guiding me into the Perfect Way.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I rejoice in knowing that I am not alone</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE MANTLE OF LOVE</b></p>  
 <p>Like a cloak His Love is wrapped around me. Like a warm garment It shelters me from the storms of life.<br> I feel and know that an Almighty Love envelops me in Its close embrace.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_363"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 363</font></a></p>  
 <p>O Love Divine, My Love, how wonderful Thou art. I am open to receive Thy great blessing.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Love envelops me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE VOICE OF TRUTH</b></p>  
 <p>The Voice of Truth speaks to me and through me.<br> The Voice of Truth guides me and keeps me on the Path of the Perfect Day.<br> I will listen to the Inner Voice and It will tell me what to do in the hour of need.<br> I shall be told everything that I ought to know when the time of need arrives, and I shall not be misled.<br> The Voice of Truth cannot lie, but always speaks to me from On High.<br> Nothing enters but This Voice, for it is the Voice of God.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>God speaks to me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE WITNESS OF TRUTH</b></p>  
 <p>There is a Witness within me who knows the Truth and who will not let me enter into falsehood.<br> My Inner Guide keeps me on the Pathway of Life and directs me at all times to that which is right and best.<br> I shall never be without this witness of the Spirit, for I believe in It and accept It as the Great Companion of the Soul.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Spirit within me is perfect now</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THROUGH THE LONG NIGHT WATCHES</b></p>  
 <p>Through the long night watches Thou hast been with me.<br> In the dark places of human ignorance Thy hand hath guided me,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_364"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 364</font></a></p>  
 <p>Thy light hath lighted the pathway of desolation to a land of plenty.<br> I have perceived Thee from afar, and my soul hath yearned to Thee, O Thou Mighty One!<br> The Spirit within me hath urged me on to the goal, and I have not been misled.<br> I have been guided and guarded through the long journey, and Thy Presence hath been made known to me.<br> I awake from the dream and reenter the house of my Lord clothed with Peace and robed in colors of Light.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Spirit of Truth watches over me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THY STRENGTH IS SUFFICIENT</b></p>  
 <p>O Spirit of man and God within me, Thy Power is great, and Thy Knowledge goes beyond the range of human experience.<br> Thy Wisdom excels that of all else, and beside Thee there is none other.<br> In Thy Strength do I daily walk and live;<br> In Thy Presence do I always rest in peace and joy.<br> Spirit within me and without, Powerful Thou art, and Great;<br> Wonderful is Thy Might, and Complete is Thy Understanding.<br> I let Thy Mighty Strength flow through me,<br> And out into all the paths of my human endeavors.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Life from within expresses through me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WAITING ON THEE</b></p>  
 <p>In waiting on Thee there is fulness of Life.<br> I wait on Thee, my Inner Lord; I listen for Thy voice.<br> I hear Thy word; I do Thy will; again I wait on Thee.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_365"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 365</font></a></p>  
 <p>And listening, I hear Thee say: "Be perfect, be complete; live, love, be glad."</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Sit thou in the stillness and let thy Lord speak</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WHOSE RIGHT IT IS TO COME</b></p>  
 <p>He has come Whose right it is.<br> He has made His home within me, and will nevermore depart from me.<br> I shall walk no more alone, for One walks with me<br> Who knows the path of Life, and Whose feet will never falter nor fail.<br> My Inner Light shines through the mist of human beliefs<br> And frees me from the bondage of fear and limitation.<br> I shall walk with You, my Friend, and shall learn of You the ways of Life and Freedom.<br> We shall travel together from this day, and none can part us,<br> For we are united in the perfect bonds of an everlasting unity.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I walk with Thee</i>.</p>
